INGRAM RIDGE is an unincorporated community in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Ingram Ridge derives its name from G. A. Ingram, the original owner of the town site.[2] The small community once had a church,[3] and a cemetery.[4] Its elevation above sea level is .[5] It is located in the eastern part of the Pascola township.
