RIVERVIEW is an unincorporated community in southwestern Morgan County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

The community sits above the Lake of the Ozarks at the confluence of Big Buffalo Creek with the Osage River.[2]


History

A post office called Riverview was established in 1877, and remained in operation until 1906.[3] The community was named so named on account of scenic views over the nearby Osage River.[4]
