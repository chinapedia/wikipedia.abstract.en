WEST BARATANG GROUP are an island group of the Andaman Islands, located west of Baratang Island.


Administration

Politically, the Baratang Islands, are part of Rangat Taluk.[1] The islands are also known as Port Anson Islands.


Fauna

Parrot island is a famous tourist spot.[2][3] It is a small uninhabited island located near Baratang Jetty. What makes it attractive for tourists is the mystery that this island holds which calls thousands of parrots every evening.
