_CEROSTERNA STOLZI_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Coenraad Ritsema in 1911. It is known from Malaysia, Borneo and Sumatra.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Cerosterna stolzi stolzi_ Ritsema, 1911
-   _Cerosterna stolzi brunnea_ (Fisher, 1935)
