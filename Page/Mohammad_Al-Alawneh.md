MOHAMMAD WALEED YASEEN AL-ALAWNEH () (born 18 June 1988) is a Jordanian football player, who currently plays as a midfielder for Al-Faisaly and Jordan.


International career

Al-Alawneh played his first international match against Egypt in an international friendly on 27 January 2016, which Jordan won 1-0.
