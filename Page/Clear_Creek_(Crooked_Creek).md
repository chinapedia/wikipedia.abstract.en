CLEAR CREEK is a stream in Monroe and Shelby counties in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Crooked Creek.

The stream headwaters are in southern Shelby County west of Shelbina at and the confluence with Crooked Creek is just upstream from the Mark Twain Lake in northern Monroe County at .[2][3]

Clear Creek was so named on account of the clarity of the water it contains.[4]
