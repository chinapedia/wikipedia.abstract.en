_INTOXICATED LOVE_ (German: _LIEBE IM RAUSCH_) is a 1927 German silent film directed by Georg Jacoby and starring Elga Brink, Stewart Rome and Georg Alexander.[1]

The film's art direction was by Franz Schroedter and Hermann Warm.
