GEORGE BRUCE CUNNINGHAM was a Scottish amateur football forward who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

As of 1911, Cunningham was working as a clerk in a hide leather factory in Glasgow.[2] Cunningham served as a private in the Royal Northumberland Fusiliers during the First World War.[3]
