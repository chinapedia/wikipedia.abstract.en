PABLO SÁNCHEZ NIÑO (born 20 October 1995) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper.[1]


Club career

On 21 February 2019, he was released from his contract with Giana Erminio by mutual consent.[2]


Personal life

Pablo was born in London, England. He then moved to Milan, Italy where he attended the Sir James British School of Milan.
