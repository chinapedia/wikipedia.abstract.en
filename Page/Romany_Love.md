_ROMANY LOVE_ is a 1931 British musical film directed by Fred Paul and starring Esmond Knight, Florence McHugh and Roy Travers. It was made at Isleworth Studios as a quota quickie.[1]


Cast

-   Esmond Knight as Davy Summers
-   Florence McHugh as Taraline
-   Roy Travers as Joe Cayson
-   Rita Cave
-   Rick Barnes
