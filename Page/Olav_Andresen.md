OLAV ANDRESEN (born 15 April 1877) was a Norwegian politician.

He was born in Heddal to Andres Johannessen and Ragnhild Amundsdatter. He served as mayor of Notodden 1922–1924, 1928–1931 and 1934–1940. He was elected representative to the Storting for the period 1925–1927, for the Labour Party.[1]
