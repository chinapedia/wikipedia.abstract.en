KILCASHEL STONE FORT is a double court cairn and National Monument located in County Mayo, Ireland.[1][2]


Location

Kilcashel Stone Fort is located 800 m (½ mile) southeast of Kilmovee.


History

Kilcashel Stone Fort was possibly constructed c. 2500–500 BC.


Description

Kilcashel Stone Fort is in diameter; the stone wall is thick and in height.[3] A souterrain is located inside. There is also a bullaun.[4][5]
