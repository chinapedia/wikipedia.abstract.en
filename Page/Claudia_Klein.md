'''Claudia Klein ''' (born 24 September 1971) is a German women's international footballer who plays as a defender. She is a member of the Germany women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1995 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she plays for Grün-Weiß Brauweiler in Germany.[1]
