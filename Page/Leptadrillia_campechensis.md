_LEPTADRILLIA CAMPECHENSIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 6 mm and 14 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the Campeche Bank, Yucatán, Mexico
