ISTVÁN ÁGH (born _István Nagy_; 24 March 1938 in Iszkáz) is a Hungarian poet.[1][2] He is the younger brother of László Nagy who was also a poet.


Awards

-   Kossuth Prize (1992)
-   Attila József Prize (1969) and (1980)
