MILOŠ ZLIČIĆ (; born 29 December 1999) is a Serbian football forward who last played for Vojvodina. He is a younger brother of Lazar Zličić.


Club career

Vojvodina

Born in Novi Sad, Zličić passed Vojvodina youth school and joined the first team at the age of 16. Previously, he was nominated for the best player of the "Tournament of Friendship", played in 2015.[1] He made his senior debut in a friendly match against Bačka Bačka Palanka during the spring half of the 2015–16 season, along with a year younger Mihajlo Nešković.[2] Zličić made an official debut for Vojvodina in the 16th fixture of the 2016–17 Serbian SuperLiga season, played on 19 November 2016 against Novi Pazar.[3]

Loan to Cement

In July 2018, Zličić joined the Serbian League Vojvodina side Cement Beočin on half-year loan deal.[4][5] Zličić made his debut in an official match for Cement on 18 August, in the first round of the new season of the League Vojvodina, in a defeat against Omladinac.[6] He scored his first senior goal on 25 August, in victory against Radnički.[7]


International career

Zličić was called in Serbia U15 national team squad during the 2014,[8] and he also appeared for under-16 national team between 2014 and 2015.[9][10] He was also member of a U17 level later.[11] After that, he was member of a U18 level, and scored goal against Slovenia U18.[12]
