The UNITED STATES WOMEN'S NATIONAL UNDER-18 VOLLEYBALL TEAM represents the United States in international women's volleyball competitions and friendly matches under the age 18 and it is ruled by the American Volleyball Federation USAV body That is an affiliate of Federation of International Volleyball FIVB and also a part of North, Central America and Caribbean Volleyball Confederation NORCECA.
