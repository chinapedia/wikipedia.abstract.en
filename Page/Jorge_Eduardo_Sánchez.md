JORGE EDUARDO SÁNCHEZ RAMOS (born 10 December 1997) is a Mexican professional footballer who plays as a defender for Liga MX club América.


Club career

Santos Laguna

América


International career

Mexico U-21

Sánchez was included in the final roster that participated at the 2018 Toulon Tournament.[1] He would appear in four matches as he went on to be included in the Best XI of the tournament as Mexico finished runner-up.[2]

Sánchez was included in the final roster that participated in the 2018 Central American and Caribbean Games.[3] He appeared in two group stage matches as Mexico finished last in their group with one point.

Mexico

He made his debut for the Mexico national football team on 26 March 2019 in a friendly against Paraguay, as a starter.[4]
