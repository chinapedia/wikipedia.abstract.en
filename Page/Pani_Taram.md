PANI TARAM is an Indian politician,an M.L.A of Koloriang 21, District Kurung Kumey, from the state of Arunachal Pradesh. He has served, as parliament secretary, Agriculture.

Presently, he is the parliament secretary, Home and education . .[1]

Shri Pani Taram was elected, by winning, against two(2) times M.L.A, Shri. Lokam Tassar, from Koloriang seat in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election. He resigned from his former government service, as the District Fisheries Development Officer (D.F.D.O) . Standing as a People's Party of Arunachal candidate.[2][3][4] Graduated from Jawahar lal Nehru College, Pasighat, Arunachal Pradesh . He is a Science Graduate , B.Sc(Hons) by Qualification.
