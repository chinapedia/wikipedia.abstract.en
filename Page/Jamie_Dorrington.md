JAMIE DORRINGTON (born 1 November 1984) is an English actor, known for his roles as Tommy in the Sky drama _The Endz_ and Gaz in the ITV soap opera _Emmerdale_.[1]


Career

In 2011, Dorrington made his professional acting debut as Officer Johnson in the short film _Summers and Winters_. Later that year, he joined the cast of the Sky drama _The Endz_ as Tommy.[2][3] In 2012, Dorrington appeared In _Airborne_, a horror movie, as a security guard.[4]

From 2014 to 2016, Dorrington appeared in three episodes of the ITV drama _Happy Valley._[5] In 2017, he appeared in two episodes of the Channel 4 drama _Ackley Bridge_ as Jack Murgatroyd.[6] In 2018, Dorrington appeared in ten episodes of the ITV soap opera _Emmerdale_ as Gaz.[7][8]
