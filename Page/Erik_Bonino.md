ERIK BONINO (born July 1963)[1] is a former Chairman of Shell UK.[2]


Career

Previous to Shell he worked in the oil industry for eighteen years.

Shell UK

He joined Shell in 2002.[3] Shell UK is headquartered at the Shell Centre. He is a trustee of EngineeringUK. He became Chairman of Shell UK on 1 April 2014.
