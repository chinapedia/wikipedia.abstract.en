VÁCLAV DUDL (born 22 September 1999) is a Czech footballer who plays for Sparta Prague as a left back.


Club career

He made his senior league debut for Sparta Prague on 16 October 2016 in a Czech First League 3–0 home win against Jihlava at the age of 17 years and 24 days.[1]


Honours

SPARTAK TRNAVA

-   Slovak Cup: 2018–19
