"MOVE IN A LITTLE CLOSER, BABY" is a pop song, first recorded by Harmony Grass as "Move in a Little Closer." The song became an international hit for Cass Elliot in the spring of 1969.

Grass had recorded the song in 1968, and released it as a single. The song reached number 24 in the UK Singles Chart in January 1969.[1] It was also released in Germany, Spain and Australia.[2]

Cass Elliott cover

"Move in a Little Closer, Baby" was the final single from Elliott's album _Bubblegum, Lemonade, and... Something for Mama_. Her cover of the song peaked at number 58 on the U.S. _Billboard_ Hot 100. It was a bigger hit in Canada, where it reached number 55 on the Pop chart and number 19 on the Adult Contemporary chart. It also reached number 34 in Australia.

The song was included on her 1970 compilation LP, _Mama's Big Ones_.
