_THE CENTER_ was an afternoon music program that aired on BET. It debuted on March 3, 2003, replacing _Hits from the Street_. The program focused on music, entertainment and lifestyle.

The program was originally hosted by R&B singer Amerie, but in October 2003 she was replaced by Young Sir. On March 8, 2004, Tiffany Withers, host of _BET.com Countdown_, switched places. She would host the program until November 5, 2004, when Julissa Bermudez took over. Occasionally, special guests, such as R&B/hip-hop artists, would host the program.

The last show aired on January 25, 2007.
