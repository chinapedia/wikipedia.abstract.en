SWING FLUGSPORTGERÄTE GMBH () is a German aircraft manufacturer based in Landsberied, west of Munich, near the Bavarian Alps.[1]

The company specializes in the design and manufacture of paragliders and paramotor wings in the form of ready-to-fly aircraft, as well as paragliding harnesses, reserve parachutes and accessories.[2]

The company is organized as a Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung (GmbH), a German limited liability company.[3]

Founded in 1986, Swing is one of the oldest paraglider manufacturers and has a network of 170 distributors in 48 countries. The company's Arcus beginner level paraglider is one of the best-selling designs in the history of paragliding, with more than 8,500 sold.[4]
