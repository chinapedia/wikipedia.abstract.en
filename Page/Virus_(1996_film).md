_VIRUS_ is a 1996 bio-hazard thriller starring ex-Seattle Seahawks linebacker Brian Bosworth.[1] It was directed by Allan A. Goldstein, best known for _Death Wish V: The Face of Death_.


Synopsis

Bosworth stars as Secret Service agent Ken Fairchild, who is compelled to spring into action when a truck containing a biological weapon, crashes into a gorge. The pathogen then begins to spread throughout a town in the Pacific Northwest.


Cast

-   Brian Bosworth as Ken Fairchild
-   Leah Pinsent as Larraine Keller


Reception

The film currently has a 21% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes.[2]
