BAY CREEK is a stream in Oregon County in the Ozarks of southern Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Warm Fork Spring River.

The stream headwaters are at and its confluence with the Warm Fork Spring River is at .[2] The stream source area lies adjacent to the eastern Koshkonong city limits and the stream meanders east to join the Warm Fork Spring River at Wiggs Ford.[3]

Bay Creek was so named on account of a small inlet near its mouth.[4]
