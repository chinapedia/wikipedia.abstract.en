POTLAPAHAD is a village in Penpahad mandal of Suryapet district in Telangana, India.[1]It is located 22km from district headquarters, Suryapet.


Geography

It is in the 152 m elevation(altitude) .


Demographics

Potlapahad has population of 1812 of which 900 are males while 912 are females as per Population Census 2011. The literacy rate of village was 64.87 % where Male literacy stands at 74.37 % while female literacy rate was 55.70 %. [2]
