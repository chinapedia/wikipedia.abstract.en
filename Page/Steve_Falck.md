STEVEN L. FALCK (born April 26, 1958) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Falck was born in Manchester, Iowa and attended Upper Iowa University. A Democrat, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1997 to 2002 (28th district).[1]
