RICHARD E. MYERS (born October 29, 1934) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Myers was born in Iowa City, Iowa. He attended Iowa State University, the University of Iowa, and Kirkwood Community College and is a businessman. A Democrat, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1993 to 2005 (49th district from 1995 to 2003 and 30th district from 1993 to 1995 and in 2003).[1]
