JOHANNES WALAEUS (JAN DE WALE, JOHANNES DE WALE) (1604-1649) was a Dutch physician and illustrious professor at the Faculty of Medicine in Leiden University. He was graduated Doctor of Medicine in 1631, when he defended his dissertation, entitled _Disputiatio medica de febribus_ at Leiden University. Two years after that, he was nominated Professor extraordinarius. In 1648, he was offered full professorship at Leiden University.
