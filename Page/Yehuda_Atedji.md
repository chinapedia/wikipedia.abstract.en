YEHUDA ATEDJI (יהודה אטדגי; born June 14, 1961) is an Israeli former Olympic windsurfer.[1]

He was born in Israel, and is Jewish.[2]


Windsurfing career

He competed for Israel at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles (at Long Beach, California) at the age of 23.[3][4][5] In Men's Windsurfer/Windglider (mistral) he came in 14th out of 38 competitors.[6][7][8] His best result was his 5th place finish in Race 4.[9] When he competed in the Olympics, he was 5-8.5 (174 cm) tall and weighed 150 lbs (68 kg).[10]
