SIGNEASY is a cloud-based solution to sign and fill documents or get documents signed from phone, tablet or computer. Signatures done with SignEasy are compliant with the ESIGN Act as well as the eIDAS and the European Directive (EC/1999/93). [1][2][3][4]

SignEasy has been Featured on TNW, LifeHacker, Inc., Engadget, Forbes, Mashable & many more [5][6]


History and Achievements

SignEasy was founded in 2010 by Sunil Patro and has been featured as a Best Business App by Apple consecutively in 2014 and 2015 and has also been pre-installed by Apple on its demo devices. Apple also featured SignEasy in its iPad campaign “Everything Changes with iPad”, as a part of the ‘Small Business Collection’.[7] SignEasy was also among the apps which appeared in the iPhone 6s commercial in April 2016 [8]

SignEasy is headquartered in San Francisco with an office in Bengaluru, India.

SignEasy is available on the Google Playstore[9][10] with over 3.5 million downloads and over 5 million documents processed and used across 150+ countries and Apple platform as well as the iOS 8 and Touch ID (fingerprint scanner) [11][12][13][14]

SignEasy allows users to send documents to others for signatures.[15][16] SignEasy was Featured among Best Business Apps in App Store 2014 & 2015. And the only eSignature app featured in Apple's global TV commercials.

SignEasy has been Featured on TNW, LifeHacker, Inc., Engadget, Forbes, Mashable & many more [17][18]

SignEasy works with Google Drive, Gmail, Zoho CRM, Dropbox, Evernote, among others. Users can sign documents without leaving the applications they are working in.
