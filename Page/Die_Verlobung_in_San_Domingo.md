_DIE VERLOBUNG IN SAN DOMINGO_ (The Engagement in San Domingo) is a 1960 opera by Werner Egk after Heinrich von Kleist, premiered on 22 November 1963 at the Bavarian State Opera.[1]


Recording

-   _Die Verlobung in San Domingo_ – Evelyn Lear, Margarethe Bence, Hans Günter Nöcker, Hamid Nasseri, Fritz Wunderlich, Richard Holm, Bayerisches Staatsorchester, Werner Egk
