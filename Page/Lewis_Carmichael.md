LEWIS CARMICHAEL (born 1995) is a Scotland international rugby union player. He plays for Edinburgh in the Pro14. His position of choice is Lock.


Rugby Union career

Amateur career

He won the 2014 RBS Premiership title with Melrose.

Professional career

He plays for Edinburgh in the Pro14.

International career

Carmichael received his first senior cap against Canada on the 9 June 2018.[1] He scored a try on his debut.[2]
