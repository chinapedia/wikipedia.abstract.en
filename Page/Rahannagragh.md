RAHANNAGRAGH is a ringfort and National Monument located in County Galway, Ireland.[1]


Location

Rahannagragh is located 4.2 km (2.6 miles) southeast of Loughrea.[2]


History and description

Rahannagragh is surrounded by a ditch and a bank.[3][4]
