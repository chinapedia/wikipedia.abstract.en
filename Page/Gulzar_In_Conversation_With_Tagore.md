GULZAR IN CONVERSATION WITH TAGORE is a 2016 album of Tagore's 6 poems and 1 song translated into Hindi by Gulzar with music by Shantanu Moitra and sung by Shaan (singer) and Shreya Ghoshal.[1][2]
