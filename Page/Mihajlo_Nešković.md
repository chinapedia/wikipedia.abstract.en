MIHAJLO NEŠKOVIĆ (, born 6 October 2000 in Šid) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays for Serbian club Vojvodina.


Club career

Vojvodina

On 21 October 2016 Nešković made his debut for Vojvodina, in 3:1 home win against Voždovac.[1] In June 2017, Nešković signed his first professional contract, penning a three-year-deal with the club.[2]
