BEN FREDERICK (born July 8, 1982) is a Republican member of the Michigan House of Representatives and the former mayor of Owosso.[1]


Biography

An Owosso native, Frederick was elected to the city council in 2007 and was selected by the council as mayor in 2009.

Prior to his election to the House, Frederick worked for three members of the Michigan Legislature.[2]
