ROODLANDS GENERAL HOSPITAL is a hospital located on Station Road, Haddington, East Lothian. The hospital is operated by NHS Lothian.[1]


History

The hospital was built as an infectious diseases hospital for the local area and opened around 1906.[2] It became the County Infectious Diseases Hospital in 1930 and joined the National Health Service as Roodlands General Hospital in 1948.[3]
