NUNO ALVES (born 8 February 1973) is a Portuguese male weightlifter, competing in the 62 kg category and representing Portugal at international competitions. He participated at the 1996 Summer Olympics in the 59 kg event.[1] He competed at world championships, most recently at the 1998 World Weightlifting Championships.[2]
