NIELS ENGBERG served as the International Commissioner of the Fællesrådet for Danmarks Drengespejdere, as well as a member of the World Scout Committee.[1]

In 1969, Engberg was awarded the 52nd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2]
