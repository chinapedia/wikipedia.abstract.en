JAKUB MORDEC is a Polish footballer who usually plays as a midfielder for his current team, Wisła Kraków.[1][2] Has vital Poland U19 experience, playing against the Montenegro U-19.[3]


Youth

Attending university at Polish college Prokocim Kraków, he was also part of the youth system and reserves of Wisła Kraków.[4]


Club career

The Polish midfielder made his debut for the first team as a substitute subbed on versus Pogoń Szczecin.[5]
