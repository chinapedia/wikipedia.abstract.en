_ATAGEMA ALBATA_ is a species of sea slug or dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Discodorididae.


Distribution

This species was described from Victoria, Australia. It has also been reported from the Sunshine Coast, Queensland, Australia.[1]
