RICK DOERR (born 21 November 1960) is a U.S. Paralympic sailor. He won a silver medal in the 2016 Summer Paralympics in the Three-Person Keelboat (Sonar).[1] Rick was formerly a lacrosse player until his career was cut short by a car accident.
