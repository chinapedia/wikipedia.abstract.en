_OH ANDHA NAATKAL_ () is an unreleased Tamil drama film written and directed by James Vasanthan. The film features actresses Raadhika, Kushboo, Suhasini and Urvashi in the leading roles.[1]


Cast

-   Urvashi as Thirupurasundari
-   Raadhika as Radha
-   Suhasini as Sindhu
-   Kushboo as Meena
-   Latha Hegde
-   Y. Gee. Mahendra
-   Jithin


Production

In September 2016, James Vasanthan announced that he would make a film featuring actresses Raadhika, Kushboo, Suhasini and Urvashi in the leading roles. The film began its shoot during September 2016 in Melbourne, Australia.[2][3] Latha Hegde, previously seen in Kannada films, also joined the cast and shot for the film in Melbourne during October 2016.[4] Certain scenes of the film required to be shot in and around Avalon Airport, while the film would feature the first Australian Rules Football scenes in an Indian film for the first time. In November 2016, James Vasanthan revealed that the four actresses would play characters from their yesteryear films, who would re-embark on a trip to Australia. Suhasini's Sindhu from _Sindhu Bhairavi_ (1985), Raadhika's Radha from _Rettai Vaal Kuruvi_ (1987), Urvashi's Thiripurasundari from _Michael Madana Kama Rajan_ (1990) and Kushboo's Meena from _Mannan_ (1992) would be the characters reprised.[5]
