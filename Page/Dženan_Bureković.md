DŽENAN BUREKOVIĆ (born 29 May 1995) is a Bosnian-Herzegovinian defender who play for Újpest.[1] [2]

He received a call for the Bosnia and Herzegovina U21 team for their game against Switzerland played on 30 June 2016, but stayed on the bench.[3]
