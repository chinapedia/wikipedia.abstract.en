BOOMERANG is a Turkish-language children's television channel broadcasting to Turkey.


History

On 13 April 2016, Turner Broadcasting System Europe announced the launch of Boomerang in Turkey on 23 April 2016, which is Turkey's National Children's Day; this makes it the first channel in Turkey to launch on that date.[1] The HD channel was launched exclusively on the Tivibu and D-Smart operators in Turkey.[2]
