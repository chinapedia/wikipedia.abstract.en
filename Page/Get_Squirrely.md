_GET SQUIRRELY_ (also distributed as _A.C.O.R.N.S.: OPERATION CRACK DOWN_ and _VOLL AUF DIE NUSS_) is an American animated film produced by John H. Williams (through Vanguard Animation[1]) and Dan Krech[2] and directed by Ross Venokur.[3][4] Released on November 4, 2016, the film is Vanguard Animation's first theatrically released feature film since 2010's _Space Chimps 2: Zartog Strikes Back_.


Plot

Frankie (Jason Jones), a smooth-talking squirrel, finds out that acorns are being stolen by the evil A.C.O.R.N.S. Corporation. Now he must assemble a rag-tag team of forest animals with only three days to pull off their biggest heist yet.
