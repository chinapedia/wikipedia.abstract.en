The NINGXIA MUSEUM () is a museum in Jinfeng District, Yinchuan, Ningxia, China.[1][2]


History

The original museum building was prepared in 1959 and built in 1973. Construction of a new museum building started in November 2006. The museum was re-opened on 28 August 2008 and the old site was closed.[3][4]


Architecture

The museum building is a three-story building with a total floor area of 30,258 mÂ². It consists of eight exhibition halls.[5] The building was constructed resembling the Hui () character.


Exhibitions

The museum displays more than 10,000 cultural relics.


Opening time

The museum opens from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. It is free of charge.
