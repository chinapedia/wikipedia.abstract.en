MUHAMMAD ZARIF IRFAN BIN HASHIMUDDIN[1] (born 21 February 1995)[2][3] is a Malaysian footballer who is currently playing for PKNS FC in the Malaysia Super League.


Club career

AirAsia

Zarif began his professional career with AirAsia Allstars FC in 2015 playing in third-tier Malaysia FAM League.

Selangor

After his contract with AirAsia expired, Zarif signed with Malaysia Super League side Selangor on December 2015. Zarif has not made any appearances for Selangor in 2016 season. On 21 January 2017, he made his league debut for Selangor in a 2–0 win over Penang in MP Selayang Stadium.

PKNS

On December 2017, Zarif signed a contract with Malaysia Super League club PKNS FC.[4] He made his debut for the club on 14 July 2018 in a 4–0 win over Terengganu.
