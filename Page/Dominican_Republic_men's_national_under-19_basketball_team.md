The DOMINICAN REPUBLIC NATIONAL U-19 BASKETBALL TEAM _(Dominican Republic Youth national basketball team)_, is the representative for the Dominican Republic in international basketball competitions, and it is organized and run by the Federación Dominicana de Baloncesto. The DOMINICAN REPUBLIC NATIONAL U-19 BASKETBALL TEAM represents the Dominican Republic at the FIBA Under-19 World Championship.
