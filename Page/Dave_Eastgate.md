DAVE EASTGATE born 31 January 1979 is an Australian actor, stand-up comedian, writer and musician.[1]

Eastgate has performed his stand-up comedy routines extensively around Australia and internationally in New Zealand, UK, USA and Singapore. He played sold-out shows at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival in 2011 and 2012.[2]
