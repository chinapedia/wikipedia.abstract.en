MATTHEW OWEN "MATT" MURRAY (born September 18, 1989) is an American actor.


Early life

The youngest of eight siblings, Murray was born in Detroit, Michigan.[1] After graduating from high school, he moved to Toronto, and then Los Angeles, to pursue an acting career.[2]


Career

Murray began his career in 2012 with guest roles in television series such as _The Firm_ and _Suits_.[3] He subsequently played the recurring role of Officer Duncan Moore in the police drama series _Rookie Blue_ (2014â€“2015).[4] In 2015, Murray played Brian in the short-lived television series _Kevin from Work_.[5] In 2016, Murray starred in the recurring role of Tony in the USA Network series _Eyewitness_.[6] On March 1, 2017, the series was canceled after one season.[7] In 2017, Murray played a supporting role in the CBS sitcom _9JKL_; the series received negative reviews from critics.[8] On May 12, 2018, the series was canceled after one season.[9]

In 2017, Murray was featured prominently in the third season of the web series _Teenagers_.[10] As part of an ensemble cast that includes Emmanuel Kabongo, Chloe Rose, and Raymond Ablack, he won the award for Best Ensemble at the 2018 HollyWeb Festival,[11] and also received a nomination for Best Ensemble at the 9th annual Indie Series Awards.[12]
