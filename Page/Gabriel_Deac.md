GABRIEL DEAC (born 26 April 1995) is a Romanian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Voluntari.[1]


Honours

FC Voluntari

-   Liga II: 2014–15
-   Romanian Cup: 2016–17
-   Romanian Supercup: 2017
