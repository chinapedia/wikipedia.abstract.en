DANNY LANGTREE (born 18 February 1991) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a forward for Oldham in Betfred League 1.

Daniel Langtree played his junior rugby league for the Blackbrook Royals and was a product of the St. Helens Academy system.[1][2] In 2009 he was sacked by the Saints and banned from rugby for two years for failing a drugs test after being found to have traces of cocaine in his system.[3]

Langtree previously played for Hull FC in the Super League until his contract was terminated in April 2019[4], and Oldham in the Kingstone Press Championship and Betfred League 1.
