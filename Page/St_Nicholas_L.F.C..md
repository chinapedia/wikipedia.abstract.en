ST NICHOLAS FOOTBALL CLUB LADIES are a women's association football team based in South Gloucestershire, England. They are currently members of the .

The club's senior women's team was founded in 2008. Up until that point they had girls' teams up to under-18 level, the addition of the women's team enabled the girls to continue playing at the club beyond that age. Over all, St Nicholas Football club run 21 teams in boys', girls', men's and women's football.[1]


Team colours

The home kit consists of black and green shirts and socks, with black shorts, while their away kit is yellow with black trim. Both are manufactured by Zapkam.[2]
