SUN FAJING (; ; born 3 October 1996) is a Chinese tennis player.

Sun has a career high ATP singles ranking of 402 achieved on 31 July 2017. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 322 achieved on 25 December 2017. Sun has won 1 ITF doubles title.

Sun made his ATP main draw debut at the 2016 Chengdu Open, in the doubles draw partnering He Yecong.
