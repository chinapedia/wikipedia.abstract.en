HUBBARD is an unincorporated community in Olive Township, St. Joseph County, in the U.S. state of Indiana.[1][2]

The community is part of the South Bend–Mishawaka IN-MI, Metropolitan Statistical Area.

Hubbard was a stop on the Chicago South Shore and South Bend Railroad.[3]
