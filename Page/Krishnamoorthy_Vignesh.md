KRISHNAMOORTHY VIGNESH (born 9 September 1994) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Tamil Nadu in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 6 October 2016.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Tamil Nadu in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 29 January 2017.[3]

He was the leading wicket-taker for Tamil Nadu in the 2017–18 Ranji Trophy, with 24 dismissals in six matches.[4] In July 2018, he was named in the squad for India Green for the 2018–19 Duleep Trophy.[5] He was the leading wicket-taker for India Green in the tournament, with eight dismissals in two matches.[6]
