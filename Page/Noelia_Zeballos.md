NOELIA ZEBALLOS MELGAR (; born 2 May 1994) is a Bolivian tennis player.

Zeballos has a career high WTA singles ranking of 675, achieved on 5 March 2018. She also has a career high WTA doubles ranking of 411, achieved on 21 August 2017. Zeballos has won three ITF doubles titles.

Playing for Bolivia in Fed Cup, Zeballos has a win-loss record of 13–25.

Her brother Federico is also a tennis player.
