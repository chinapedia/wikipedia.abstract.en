V√çA ARGENTINA is a Panama Metro station on Line 1.[1] It was one of the metro network's first 11 stations, opened on 5 April 2014 and commencing operations the following day.[2]

The station serves the neighbourhoods of Obarrio and El Cangrejo, providing access to the city's financial and banking district.[3]

In its first year of operations, it was the sixth most used station of the twelve on the network at that time, carrying 18% of the system's users at peak times.[4]
