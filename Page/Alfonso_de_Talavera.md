ALFONSO DE TALAVERA, O.S.H. or ALFONSO DE GUZMAN was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as the first Bishop of Comayagua (1531â€“1540).[1]


Biography

Alfonso de Talavera was ordained a priest in the Order of Saint Jerome.[2] In September 1531, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VII as Bishop of Comayagua.[3] He served as Bishop of Comayagua until his resignation in 1540.[4]
