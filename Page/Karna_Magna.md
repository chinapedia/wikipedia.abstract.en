KARNA MANGA is a village in the commune of Mbe in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon. It is located on the road from Ngaoundéré to Karna and Garoua[1]


Population

In 1967, the settlement contained 571 inhabitants, mostly [2] At the time of the 2005 census, there were 848 people in the village.[3]


Infrastructure

Karna Manga has a school, a health centre, and a Catholic mission.[4]
