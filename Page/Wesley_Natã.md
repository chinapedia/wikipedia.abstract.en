WESLEY NATÃ WACHHOLZ (born 18 April 1995), known as WESLEY NATÃ or simply WESLEY, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Bulgarian First League club Tsarsko Selo Sofia.


Club career

Born in Porto Alegre, Rio Grande do Sul, Wesley was a Chapecoense youth graduate. He made his senior debut on 24 April 2016, coming on as a late substitute for fellow youth graduate Hyoran in a 2–3 Campeonato Catarinense away loss against Criciúma.

In July 2016, Wesley was loaned to Concórdia. On 13 September, he moved to Série B side Bahia, also in a temporary deal.[1] He made his professional debut on 9 October, starting and scoring the second in a 4–0 home routing of Tupi.
