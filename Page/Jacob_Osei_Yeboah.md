JACOB OSEI YEBOAH is a politician who contested as an independent candidate for Ghana's presidential elections in 2012 and 2016.[1][2][3] He was one of the four candidates out of seventeen, to have survived the Electoral Commission's controversial disqualification of candidates ahead of the 2016 general elections in Ghana. He is an old student of Opoku Ware School (Santasi-Kumasi).
