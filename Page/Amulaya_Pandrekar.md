AMULAYA PANDREKAR (born 31 March 1996) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Goa in the 2014–15 Ranji Trophy on 21 January 2015.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Goa in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 3 February 2017.[3] He made his List A debut for Goa in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 28 February 2017.[4]
