FLUCOM is an unincorporated community in Jefferson County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Flucom was established in 1891, and remained in operation until 1921.[2] According to tradition, the name Flucom is a transfer from a similarly-named place in Germany.[3]
