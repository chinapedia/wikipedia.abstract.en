_MORE RUBBISH_ is the sixth studio album by Michael Carr's comedy character Buddy Goode. It was released on 16 December 2016 both digitally and on CD.[1]

The album was launched at the Rooty Hill RSL on 17 December 2016.[2]

On 4 October 2017, it was announced that the album had been nominated for the Best Comedy Release category at the 2017 ARIA Awards.[3] However, it lost to John Clarke's album _Clarke's Classics_.
