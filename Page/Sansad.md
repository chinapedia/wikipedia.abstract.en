_SANSAD_ or _SANGSAD_ is the word for "Assembly", association, council or "Parliament" in several Indo-Aryan languages, derived from a Sanskrit root. It may also mean:


National Parliament

-   Jatiya Sangsad ("National Parliament" of Bangladesh; _Jatiyô Sôngsôd_)
    -   Jatiya Sangsad Bhaban, National Parliament House of Bangladesh
    -   Speaker of the Jatiya Sangsad
    -   Sangsad Television
-   Parliament of India (, _Bhāratīya sansad_)
    -   Sansad Bhavan, Parliament House of India
    -   Sansad Marg
    -   Sansad Ratna Award
    -   Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana
-   Federal Parliament of Nepal (, _saṅghīya sansad Nēpāl_)


Associations

-   Muktijoddha Sangsad, a non-political welfare association of the combatants during the Bangladesh Liberation war formed on 13 February 1972
-   Padatik Nattya Sangsad, a Dhaka based Bengali theatre group
-   Muktijoddha Sangsad KC, a football club in Bangladesh
-   Kendriya Muslim Sahitya Sangsad, a literary organisation located in Sylhet, Bangladesh
-   Narayanganj Suktara Sangsad, a football team of Narayanganj, Bangladesh
