_DRILLIA RUFESCENS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell attains 8 mm, its diameter 3.5 mm.


Distribution

This species occurs in the Pacific Ocean off Samoa..
