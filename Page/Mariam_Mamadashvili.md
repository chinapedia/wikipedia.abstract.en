MARIAM MAMADASHVILI (, born 16 November 2005)[1] is a Georgian child singer. She won the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016 where she represented Georgia with the song "Mzeo".[2] She has been living in the United States since 2015.[3]

Mamadashvili has been performing since she was four years old. She studied at the Bzikebistudio in Georgia and also the Evgeni Mikeladze State Central Music School.[4] Since moving to the United States, she has been enrolled at Tomlinson Middle School and Broadway Method Academy, and resides in Fairfield, Connecticut[5]
