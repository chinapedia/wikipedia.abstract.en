PARTY OF NATIONALISTS or NATIONALISTS’ PARTY[1] (, _Ḥezb-e Melliun_) was a conservative[2] political party in Pahlavi era Iran, and government majority party from 1957 to 1960. The party was founded in 1957 by Manouchehr Eghbal on orders directed by Shah to initiate a two-party system.[3] Its opposition was liberal People's Party.[4]
