AKRON is an unincorporated community in Harrison County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Akron was platted in 1858, and named after Akron, Ohio, the native home of a share of the first settlers.[2] A post office called Akron was established in 1861, and remained in operation until 1908.[3]
