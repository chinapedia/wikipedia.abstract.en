HANS TRYGVE HELMER LÜTKEN (born 6 April 1882) was a Norwegian jurist and state auditor.

He was born in Christiania to Harald Lütken and Martha Larsen, and graduated as cand.jur. in 1906.[1] He served as Auditor General from 1926 to 1949.[2]
