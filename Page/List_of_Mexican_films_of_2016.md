This is a list of MEXICAN FILMS RELEASED IN 2016.


Box office records

-   On May 30, _¿Qué Culpa Tiene el Niño?_, starred by Karla Souza and Ricardo Abarca became the third highest-grossing film of all time in Mexico, only surpassed by _No Se Aceptan Devoluciones_ and _Nosotros Los Nobles_ (both 2013).[1] The film grossed MXN$192 million in its first three weeks of release.[2]
