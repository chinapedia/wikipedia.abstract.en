HOLNEIKER MENDES MARREIROS (born April 25, 1995) is a Brazilian football player for Tochigi SC.[1] He plays for Zweigen Kanazawa.


Playing career

Mendes joined a J2 League club, Zweigen Kanazawa, in 2015; he then moved to Tochigi SC during 2017 season.[2]
