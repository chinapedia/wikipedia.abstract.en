CLASSIC CHER is a concert residency by American singer-actress Cher. The show is performed in Las Vegas, Oxon Hill,[1] and Atlantic City.[2]


Set list

This set list is representative of the performance of the opening night.[3]

1.  "Woman's World"
2.  "Strong Enough"
3.  "All or Nothing"
4.  "The Beat Goes On"
5.  "All I Really Want to Do"
6.  "I Got You Babe"
7.  "Gypsys, Tramps & Thieves"
8.  "Dark Lady"
9.  "Half-Breed"
10. "Welcome to Burlesque"
11. "Take Me Home"
12. "After All"
13. "Walking in Memphis"
14. "The Shoop Shoop Song (It's in His Kiss)"
15. "I Found Someone"
16. "If I Could Turn Back Time"

Encore

1.  "Believe"
