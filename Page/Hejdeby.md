HEJDEBY is a populated area, a socken (not to be confused with parish), on the Swedish island of Gotland.[1] It comprises the same area as the administrative Hejdeby District, established on 1January 2016.[2]


Geography

Hejdeby is situated in the northwest part of Gotland, just east of Visby.[3] The medieval Hejdeby Church is located in the socken.[4] , Hejdeby Church belongs to Hejdeby parish in Romaklosters pastorat.[5][6]
