THOMAS JACK WINDLE (born 1998/1999)[1] is an English footballer who plays college soccer for the Tyler Junior College Apaches, as a defender.


Early and personal life

Windle attended Water Street Primary School in Skipton and Settle College.[2]


Career

He joined Bradford City at the age of 11, after being spotted playing for local side LMS in Skipton.[3] He made 3 senior appearances for the club in the EFL Trophy during the 2016–17 season.[4] He left the club after seven years, and in 2018 obtained a scholarship for Tyler Junior College.[5][6]
