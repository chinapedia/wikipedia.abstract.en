CAMILLE FOURNIER is known for being the former chief technology officer of Rent The Runway, former vice president of technology at Goldman Sachs, and author of _The Manager's Path: A Guide for Tech Leaders Navigating Growth and Change_.[1] She is a managing director at the hedge fund Two Sigma.[2]


Career

Fournier earned her bachelor's degree in computer science from Carnegie Mellon University. After graduating, she worked at Microsoft for 18 months. In 2005, she graduated from the University of Wisconsin–Madison with a master's degree in computer science.[3] She moved on to work at Goldman Sachs in New York for over six years, originally as an engineer working on credit risk software and eventually as a vice president of technology.[4]

In 2011, Fournier joined the startup Rent the Runway as the director of engineering, and in 2014 she became the company's chief technology officer.[5][6] She resigned from her position in 2015.[7][8] Fournier is a managing director at New York City hedge fund Two Sigma.[9]

Fournier writes the _Ask The CTO_ column for O'Reilly Media.[10] In 2017, she published a book titled _The Manager's Path: A Guide for Tech Leaders Navigating Growth and Change_.[11]
