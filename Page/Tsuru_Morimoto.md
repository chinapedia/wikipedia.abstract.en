is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Her father Mamoru Morimoto is former middle distance runner.


Club career

Morimoto was born on November 9, 1970. She played for Nissan FC Ladies. However, the club was disbanded in 1993. After she left the club, she played for Nikko Securities Dream Ladies and Italian Serie A club Lazio.


National team career

On August 21, 1994, Morimoto debuted and scored a goal for Japan national team against Austria. She was a member of Japan for 1995 World Cup. She also played at 1994 Asian Games and 1995 AFC Championship. She played 5 games and scored 1 goal for Japan until 1995.[1]
