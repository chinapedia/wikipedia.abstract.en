LOUSE CREEK is a stream in southern Oregon County in the Ozarks of southern Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Frederick Creek.

The stream headwaters are at and the confluence with Frederick Creek is at .[2] The stream source area lies north of Missouri Route 142 and it flows northeast crossing under Missouri Route E and joins Frederick Creek east of Garfield.[3][4]

Louse Creek was so named on account of the poor hygiene of the locals near its course.[5]
