DANDOT RS DALMIAN (DRS) (Urdu ڈنڈوت آر ایس ڈالیمان) is a village, union council, and administrative subdivision of Jhelum District (Urdu جہلم) in the Punjab Province of Pakistan. It is part of Pind Dadan Khan Tehsil.[1]
