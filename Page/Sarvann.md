_SARVANN_ is a 2017 Indian Punjabi language thriller film directed by Karan R Guliani, written by Amberdeep Singh and produced by Priyanka Chopra under her production company Purple Pebble Pictures. It stars Amrinder Gill, Simi Chahal & Ranjit Bawa as the main protagonists of the film and was released worldwide on 13 January, 2017.[1] It is an action, drama film about an NRI boy Mithu (Amrinder Gill), coming to India to connect to his true roots.[2]
