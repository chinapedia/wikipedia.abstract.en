GABRIELA VELASCO ANDREU (born 1 April 1985) is a former professional Spanish female tennis player.

Velasco Andreu has won 12 doubles titles on the ITF tour in her career. On 20 June 2005, she reached her best singles ranking of world number 369. On 17 July 2006, she peaked at world number 229 in the doubles rankings. Velasco Andreu made her WTA tour debut at the 2003 Copa Colsanitas.[1] She retired from professional tennis in 2007.
