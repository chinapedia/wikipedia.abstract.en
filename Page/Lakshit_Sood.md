LAKSHIT SOOD (born 20 February 1991) is an Indian tennis player.

Sood has a career high ATP singles ranking of 1075 achieved on 20 July 2015. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 282 achieved on 13 November 2017.

Sood made his ATP main draw debut at the 2015 Aircel Chennai Open in the doubles draw partnering his twin brother Chandril Sood.
