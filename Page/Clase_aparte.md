_CLASE APARTE_ is a 1980 studio album by Colombian group Binomio de Oro.[1] The album contains the hits "Dime pajarito", "Voz de acordeones" - dedicated like the album to the murdered writer Octavio Daza,[2][3] and "Habíamos terminado".
