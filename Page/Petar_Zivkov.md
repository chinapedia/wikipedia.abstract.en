PETAR ZIVKOV (born 26 January 1995) is an Austrian football forward of Serbian descent playing in Italy for Serie C club Rende on loan from Reggina.


Career

Born in Vienna,[1] he played with SV Wienerberg, SK Rapid Wien, FK Austria Wien, SC Team Wiener and Trenkwalder Admira in Vienna during his youth career,[2] besides OFK Beograd in Serbia as well. In 2011, he moves to Italy, and after playing in the youth teams of Chievo Verona and Carpi 1909, he debuted as senior playing with lower-league Italian clubs such as Civitanovese and Fermana.[3] During 2014 he spent some time back in Serbia with OFK Beograd bur without debuting in the league.[4] Then he returned to Italy, and, after a spell with Matelica, he spends a season with RapalloBogliasco.[5] His regular performances earned him a contract with Vicenza.[6][7] He made a debut in the 2016–17 Serie B on October 9, 2016, in a home game against Cesena, a 0–0 draw.[8]

On 10 July 2018, he signed with Reggina. The contract is for one year with another one-year extension option.[9] On 21 January 2019, he moved on loan to Rende.[10]
