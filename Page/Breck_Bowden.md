WILLIAM BRECKENRIDGE "BRECK" BOWDEN (; born 1951) is an American environmental scientist, currently the Robert and Genevieve Patrick Professor of Watershed Science & Planning and Director of the Vermont Water Resources and Lake Science Center, Rubenstein School of Environment and Natural Resources, University of Vermont,[1][2] and also a published author.[3][4]
