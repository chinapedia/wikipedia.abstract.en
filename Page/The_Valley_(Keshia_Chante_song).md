"THE VALLEY" is the first single[1] by Canadian R&B Keshia Chanté[2] from her currently unnamed album due in 2017.[3] This is the first release from Chante since her 2011 album Night & Day.[4]

On November 10, 2016, BET premiered the song worldwide.[5]
