_WEAVERS OF FORTUNE_ is a 1922 British silent sports film directed by Arthur Rooke and starring Henry Vibart, Dacia and Derek Glynne.[1]


Cast

-   Henry Vibart as Jackson
-   Dacia as Minna Vandyck
-   Derek Glynne as Tom Winter
-   Myrtle Vibart as Molly Jackson
