IONUȚ CIPRIAN IANCU (born 18 February 1994)[1] is a Romanian handballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Tatabánya KC and the Romania national team.


Achievements

-   LIGA NAȚIONALĂ:
    -   _Gold Medalist_: 2013
    -   _Silver Medalist_: 2015, 2016, 2017
-   CUPA ROMÂNIEI:
    -   _Winner_: 2013, 2016
-   NEMZETI BAJNOKSÁG I:
    -   _Bronze Medalist_: 2018


Individual awards

-   World University Championship Best Goalkeeper: 2016[2]
-   Gala Premiilor Handbalului Românesc Liga Națională Goalkeeper of the Season: 2019[3]
