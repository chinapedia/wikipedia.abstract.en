The CHIESA DELLA MADONNA DEL VALLONE is a Roman Catholic church or sanctuary located in a rural site in the frazione of Piedicolle in Acquacanina, province of Macerata, in the region of Marche, Italy.[1] It is located within the Parco Nazionale dei Monti Sibillini.


History

This marian shrine was completed in 1747, likely at the site of a chapel or aedicule. The main altarpiece depicts the _Madonna and Child_. Documents recall a church at the site by 1624. The church also has paintings both from the 20th century and a _Deposition_ attributed to Lorenzo Garbieri.[2]
