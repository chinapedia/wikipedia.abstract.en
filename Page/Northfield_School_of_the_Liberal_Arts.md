NORTHFIELD SCHOOL OF THE LIBERAL ARTS is a private classical Christian school in Wichita, Kansas. It was established in 1993, and operates from grades 6 to 12. It has its roots in the Liberal Arts, but also puts a large emphasis on The Great Books; a collection of 150 books that are important to the development of western thought.[1]
