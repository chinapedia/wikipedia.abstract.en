MARIO DIMITROV (; born 25 October 1994, in Pernik) is a Bulgarian footballer who plays as a defender for FC Minyor Radnevo


Club career

On 9 August 2016, Dimitrov joined Bansko as free agent after his contract with Spartak Pleven has expired.[1] He was released in December.[2]
