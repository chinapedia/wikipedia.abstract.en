_THE HOUSE WITHOUT MEN_ (German: _DAS HAUS OHNE MÄNNER_) is a 1928 German silent film directed by Rolf Randolf and starring Ossi Oswalda, Iwa Wanja and Ida Renard.[1]

The film's art direction was by Heinrich Richter.
