GUADELOUPE has a population of 403,977 (2012).[1]


Population

According to INSEE Guadeloupe has an estimated population of 403,977 on January 1, 2012.[2] Life expectancy at birth is 77.0 years for males, and 83.5 for females (figures for 2011).[3]


Languages

French is the official language, taught in the school system. Antillean Creole French is spoken by a large part of the population, understood by nearly all, and taught in some schools. A 2007 document issued by the Organisation internationale de la Francophonie estimated the population to be 80.2% "francophone" and 14.9% "partially francophone".[4]
