The C.P. SHIPLEY HOUSE was a historic building located in Mason City, Iowa, United States. Clinton Pardes Shipley (1851-1936) was a native of Baltimore County, Maryland. He married Margaret A. McMillin (1853-1940) in Mason City on November 17, 1875.[1] The two-story Prairie School house, completed in 1913, had a stucco exterior, a broad hip roof and overhanging eaves.[2] It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.[3] The house has subsequently been torn down. The _Globe Gazette_ building is now on the property.
