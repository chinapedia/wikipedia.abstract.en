_MIMOPHIS_ is a genus of snakes in the family Lamprophiidae found in Madagascar.[1] Prior to 2017, the genus comprised only a single species, _Mimophis mahfalensis_. A second species, _Mimophis occultus_, was described in 2017.[2]

-   _Mimophis mahfalensis_
-   _Mimophis occultus_
