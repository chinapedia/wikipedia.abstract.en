HAAPITI RAHI, also known as Motu Haapiti Rahi, is a private island in the lagoon of Bora Bora in French Polynesia. It is the located between Krisu, and Tevairoa, near Haapiti Iti.


Administration

The island is part of Bora Bora Commune.
