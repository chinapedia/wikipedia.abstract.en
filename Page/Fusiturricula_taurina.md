_FUSITURRICULA TAURINA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The size of an adult shell varies between 9 mm and 13 mm.


Distribution

This species occurs in the demersal zone of Brazil.
