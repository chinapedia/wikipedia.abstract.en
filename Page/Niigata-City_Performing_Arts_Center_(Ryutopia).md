The is a large multi-purpose public cultural facility in Chūō-ku, Niigata, Japan, which opened on 22 October 1998.[1]


Facilities

The main concert hall seats 1,890, and the theater seats 903.[2]


Access

Train / Car

The complex is located within Hakusan Park, and the nearest station to the facility is Hakusan Station on the JR Echigo Line, 20 minutes' walk away. It is 15 minutes by car from the main Niigata Station.[3]

Teansit bus

There is a BRT "Bandai-bashi Line" bus stop 'Shiyakusyo-mae' (Stop No.08) near the facilitiy. Also, Niigata City Loop Bus has a stop 'Hakusan Koen mae'.[4][5]
