DOUG LAMBERT is a former Scottish international lawn and indoor bowls player.[1]

Lambert (born 1947) won a silver medal in the triples and a bronze medal in the fours at the 1984 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Aberdeen.[2]

He played for the Bridge of Earn club in Perthshire.[3]
