ALESSIO CELADONI DI CELADONIA (died 1517) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Molfetta (1508–1517) and Bishop of Gallipoli (1494–1508).[1][2]


Biography

In 1494, Alessio Celadoni di Celadonia was appointed during the papacy of Pope Alexander VI as Bishop of Gallipoli.[3][4] On 7 June 1508, he was transferred by Pope Julius II to the diocese of Molfetta.[5] He served as Bishop of Molfetta until his death in 1517.[6][7]
