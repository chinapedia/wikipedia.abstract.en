DANIL VLADISLAVOVICH KRUGOVOY (; born 28 May 1998) is a Russian football player who plays for FC Ufa and its professional farm-club FC Ufa-2.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Zenit-2 St. Petersburg on 9 November 2016 in a game against FC Tyumen.[1]

On 18 June 2018, he signed a 4-year contract with FC Ufa.[2]


International

He was a late substitute in one game for Russia national under-17 football team at the 2015 UEFA European Under-17 Championship. Later he started all four games the team played at the 2015 FIFA U-17 World Cup.
