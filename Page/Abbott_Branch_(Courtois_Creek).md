ABBOTT BRANCH is a stream in northern Iron County, Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Courtois Creek.[2]

The stream headwaters arise at and the confluence with Courtois Creek is at .[3] The community of Good Water lies on Courtois Creek about 2000 feet north of the confluence and Missouri Route Z crosses the stream about 1000 feet east of the confluence. The headwaters of the stream arise just north of Missouri Route 32 about east-southeast of the confluence.[4] Viburnum lies about west of the confluence.

Abbott Branch has the name of Robert Abbott, an early settler.[5]
