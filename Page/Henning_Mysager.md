Dr. HENNING MYSAGER, mayor of Brovst municipality, Denmark representing Venstre from 1986 to 1998, served as the Vice-Chairman of the World Scout Committee.

In 1983, Mysager was awarded the 168th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
