JITESH DONGA (born 23 August 1991) is a Gujarati language novelist and writer of fiction. His novels are _Vishwamanav_ (2014) and _North Pole_ (2017).


Life

Jitesh Donga is born in Sarambhada, a small village in Amreli district, Gujarat, India. He spent most of his childhood in his village helping his farmer parents. He completed his primary education in his village and secondary schooling from Science School, Rajkot. He studied B. Tech. (Electrical Engineering) at Charotar University of Science and Technology, Anand, Gujarat. While doing several odd jobs, he completed his first novel which became successful.[1][2]


Works

_Vishwamanav_ (2014) is a Gujarati novel written by Jitesh kalubhai Donga. The book was inspired by four true stories.[3] _Antar Valonu_ (2016) is his collection of adapted stories. _North Pole_ (2017) is his second novel.[4][5]
