The LIUZHOU INTERNATIONAL CHALLENGER is a professional tennis tournament played on hard courts. It is currently part of the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) Challenger Tour and the International Tennis Federation (ITF) Women's Circuit. It has been held annually in Liuzhou, China since 2016.
