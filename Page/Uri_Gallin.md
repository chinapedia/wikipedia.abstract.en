URI GALLIN (also "Orion" Gallin; אורי גלין; born June 29, 1928) is an Israeli former Olympic discus thrower.[1]


Discus throw career

Gallin's personal best in the discus throw is 48.02, in 1956. He competed for Israel at the 1952 Summer Olympics in Helsinki at the age of 24.[2] He participated in the men's discus throw, coming in 32nd with a best distance of 40.76.[3]
