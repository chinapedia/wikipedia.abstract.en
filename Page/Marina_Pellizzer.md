'''Marina Pellizzer ''' (born 8 February 1972) is an Italian women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Italy women's national football team. She was part of the team at the UEFA Women's Euro 2001. On club level she plays for Foroni Verona in Italy.[1]
