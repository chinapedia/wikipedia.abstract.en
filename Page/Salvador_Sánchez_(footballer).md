SALVADOR SÁNCHEZ (born 31 July 1995) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a defender for Olimpo.[1][2]


Career

Sánchez's youth career began with Tres Algarrobos in 2008, he remained there for two years before joining River Plate's youth ranks.[3][4][5] Five years later, he left to join Argentine Primera División club Arsenal de Sarandí.[6][7] His first mark on Arsenal's first-team came during the 2015 Copa Sudamericana when he was on the bench twice for a tie against Independiente.[8] He made his professional football debut on 20 September 2015 in a league win against Olimpo, he made a further two appearances in the 2015 campaign.[9] Sanchez joined Primera B Nacional side Santamarina in September 2017 following a trial period during pre-season.[10][11]

His Santamarina debut came on 16 September versus Aldosivi.[12] Eighteen further appearances followed, prior to a move to fellow Primera B Nacional outfit Olimpo on 4 July 2018.[13]
