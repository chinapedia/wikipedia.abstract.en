'''Stacey Naris ''' (born 24 February 1991) is a Namibian women's international footballer who plays as a defender. She is a member of the Namibia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship. On club level she played for TuS Lipperode in Germany.[1][2]
