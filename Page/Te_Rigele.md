TE RIGELE (born 9 October 1997) is a Chinese tennis player.

On the junior tour, Te has a career-high ranking of No. 48, achieved on January 2015.

Te has a career-high ATP singles ranking of No. 566, achieved on 21 May 2018. On 21 May 2018, he achieved No. 525 in ATP doubles ranking. Te has won three ITF doubles titles and four ITF doubles titles.

Te made his ATP main draw debut at the 2014 ATP Shenzhen Open, in the doubles draw partnering Qiu Zhuoyang.
