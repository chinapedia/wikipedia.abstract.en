TIVIM VIDHAN SABHA CONSTITUENCY is one of the 40 legislative assembly constituencies of the state of Goa in southern India. Tivim is also one of the 20 constituencies falling under the North Goa Lok Sabha constituency.


Members of Legislative Assembly

-   1967: J. Rane, Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party
-   1972: Punaji Achrekar, Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party
-   1977: Dayanand Narvekar, Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party
-   1980: Dayanand Narvekar, Indian National Congress (Urs)
-   1984: Dayanand Narvekar, Indian National Congress
-   1989: Vinayak Naik, Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party
-   1994: Dayanand Narvekar, Indian National Congress
-   1999: Dayanand Narvekar, Indian National Congress
-   2002: Sadanand Shet, Bharatiya Janata Party
-   2007: Nilkanth Halarnkar, Nationalist Congress Party
-   2012: Kiran Kandolkar, Bharatiya Janata Party
-   2017: Nilkanth Ramnath Halarnkar, Indian National Congress
