NIZAM UDDIN HAZARI is a Bangladesh Awami League politician. He became the Member of Parliament for Feni-2 in 2014, being re-elected in 2018.[1] He is a former mayor of Feni.[2] He served more than 5 years in jail on an arms case.[3] He started politics while he was a student of Chittagong Govt. Commerce College.[4]
