Media

Manga

_Sakura Maimai_ is a four-panel manga series by Oshioshio. It began serialization in _Comic Cune_{{'s}} October 2014 issue released on August 27, 2014; At first, _Comic Cune_ was a "magazine in magazine" placed in _Monthly Comic Alive_,[1] later it became independent of _Comic Alive_ and changed to a formal magazine on August 27, 2015.[2] _Sakura Maimai_ is also available on Kadokawa Corporation's _ComicWalker_ website.[3] As of October 2015, it has been collected in a single _tankōbon_ volume.[4]
