WILL FLETCHER (born 24 December 1989) is a British rower. He and Richard Chambers placed seventh in the men's lightweight double sculls event at the 2016 Summer Olympics.[1][2]

Fletcher studied at Durham University as a member of Hild Bede, graduating with a Modern Languages degree in 2011.[3]
