The PINEAPPLE MUSEUM () is a pineapple museum in Pekan Nanas, Pontian District, Johor, Malaysia.[1]


History

The museum was inaugurated by Johor Chief Minister Abdul Ghani Othman in 2002.[2]


Architecture

The museum is divided into several sections, which are history, introductory, agronomy and processing.


Exhibitions

The museum exhibits collections, history and information on pineapple plantation industry, which includes products and tools for pineapple processing.[3]


Opening time

The museum opens everyday from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
