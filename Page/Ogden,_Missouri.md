OGDEN is an extinct town in New Madrid County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. The GNIS classifies it as a populated place.[1]

A post office called Ogden was established in 1826, and remained in operation until 1893.[2] The community has the name of the local Ogden family of pioneer citizens.[3]
