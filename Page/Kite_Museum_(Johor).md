The KITE MUSEUM (Malay: _Muzium Layang-Layang_) is a museum about kites in Pasir Gudang, Johor Bahru District, Johor, Malaysia.[1]


History

The museum was established in February 2002 during the 7th Pasir Gudang International Kite Festsival.[2]


Architecture

The museum is housed in a two-story building. It features a small windmill to generate its own electricity.[3]


Exhibitions

The museum showcases various types of kites, posters and videos on Pasir Gudang International Kite Festival.


Opening time

The museum opens everyday from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.[4]
