_GUDEODISCUS DAUTZENBERGI_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Plectopylidae.

The specific name is in honour of Belgian malacologist Philippe Dautzenberg.


Distribution

The distribution of _Gudeodiscus dautzenbergi_ includes Vietnam.[1]


Ecology

It is a ground-dwelling species as all other plectopylid snails in Vietnam.[2]

_Gudeodiscus phlyarius_ live at geographically close sites to _Gudeodiscus dautzenbergi_.[3]
