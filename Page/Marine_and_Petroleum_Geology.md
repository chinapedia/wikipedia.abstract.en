_MARINE AND PETROLEUM GEOLOGY_ is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering marine and petroleum geology. It was established in 1984 and is published by Elsevier. The editor-in-chief is Massimo Zecchin (Istituto Nazionale di Oceanografia e di Geofisica Sperimentale and (Max) Qinhong Hu (The University of Texas at Arlington).
