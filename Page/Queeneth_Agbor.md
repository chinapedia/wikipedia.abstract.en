QUEENETH AGBOR (born in Calabar) is a Nigerian actress and media personality. Raised in Malabar, she graduated in microbiology from the University of Calabar.[1] She relocated to Lagos,[2] after feeling that people were "pressurising" her to become an actress.[3] She has since become known for her roles in Nollywood films such as _Painful Kingdom_ with Olu Jacobs, _Hooked_ with Francis Duru and _The Movement_.[4]
