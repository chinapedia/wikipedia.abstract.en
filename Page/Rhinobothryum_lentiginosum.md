_RHINOBOTHRYUM LENTIGINOSUM_, commonly known as the AMAZON BANDED SNAKE, is a species of snake in the family Colubridae. The species is endemic to South America.


Geographic range

_R. lentiginosum_ is found in Bolivia, Brazil, Colombia, Ecuador, French Guiana, Guyana, Paraguay, Peru, and Venezuela.[1]
