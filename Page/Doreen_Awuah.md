'''Doreen Awuah ''' (born 12 December 1989) is a Ghanaian women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Ghana women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2007 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she plays for Ghatel Ladies in Ghana.[1] She made her debut for Ghana in a group match against Norway.[2]
