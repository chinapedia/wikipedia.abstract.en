_AHUA_ is a genus of South Pacific funnel weavers first described by Raymond Robert Forster & C. L. Wilton in 1973.[1]


Species

it contains four species:[2]

-   _Ahua dentata_ Forster & Wilton, 1973 — New Zealand
-   _Ahua insula_ Forster & Wilton, 1973 — New Zealand
-   _Ahua kaituna_ Forster & Wilton, 1973 — New Zealand
-   _Ahua vulgaris_ Forster & Wilton, 1973 — New Zealand
