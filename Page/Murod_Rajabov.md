MUROD RAJABOV (, born 5 July 1995) is an Uzbek footballer playing with Navbahor Namangan on loan from Olmaliq FK in the Uzbek League. He previously played with FK Novi Pazar in the Serbian SuperLiga.


Club career

Born in G‘uzor,[1] Rajabov played with FC Shurtan Guzar in the 2015 Uzbek League, and with Olmaliq FK in the first half of the 2016 Uzbek League.[2]

In summer 2016, he moved abroad and signed with Serbian top-tier side FK Novi Pazar.[3] He made 3 appearances in the 2016–17 Serbian SuperLiga and during winter-break he returned to his former club, Olmaliq FK.[4]


International career

He was called for the Uzbekistani Olympic team training camp that took place from 11 to 16 July 2015.[5]
