VICKSBURG is an unincorporated community in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Vicksburg was established in 1913, and remained in operation until 1917.[2] The community was named after J. P. Vickery, a local schoolteacher.[3]
