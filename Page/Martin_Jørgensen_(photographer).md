MARTIN JØRGENSEN (born March 2, 1978 in Valby) is a Danish cinematographer and entrepreneur.[1]

On 3 March 2007 he married Alexandra, Countess of Frederiksborg, ex-wife of Prince Joachim of Denmark[2] in a private ceremony at Øster Egede Church near Fakse.[3] In September 2015, it was announced that Jørgensen and the Countess were getting a divorce after 8 and a half years of marriage.[4] The Countess cited differences in values as the reason for the divorce.[5] According to _Royal Central_, the divorce was finalized in 2015.[6]
