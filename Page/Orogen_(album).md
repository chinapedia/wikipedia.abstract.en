_OROGEN_ is a solo piano album by American pianist John Burke. The album was inspired by the tectonic creation of mountains and utilizes melodic development to illustrate this creation.[1] A self-released title,[2] _Orogen_ earned Burke a Grammy Award nomination for Best New Age Album.[3]
