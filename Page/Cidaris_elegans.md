_CIDARIS ELEGANS_ is a fossil species of pencil sea urchins in the family Cidaridae. It is known from the Oxfordian stage (Jurassic) of France.[1]

Note that _Cidaris elegans_ (A. Agassiz, 1879) is a synonym for _Histocidaris elegans_.
