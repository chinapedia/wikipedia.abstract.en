_SCANDAL MAKER_ is a 2016 Chinese comedy-drama film directed by Ahn Byeong-ki, starring Tong Dawei and Michelle Chen and a remake of the 2008 South Korean film _Scandal Makers_.[1][2] It was released in China on November 11, 2016.[3]
