The VANCOUVER SHOWCASE is a new college basketball tournament that will be first played in November 18 through 20, 2018 at the Vancouver Convention Centre in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.[1] Eight men’s and eight women’s basketball teams will play in the tournament annually.[2] Each tournament (men’s and women’s) will be played over a three-day period with four games each day.
