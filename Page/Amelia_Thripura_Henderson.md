AMELIA THRIPURA HENDERSON (born October 20, 1995), better known as AMELIA HENDERSON is a Malaysian actress, model and television presenter. She was the host and face of the GOASEAN channel, a joint-venture of Astro and Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN).


Early life

Henderson was born in Klang, Selangor, Malaysia and is the daughter of an Indian/Sri Lankan dentist mother and a Scottish architect father.[1] She is a member of Mensa International, with a certified IQ of 180.[2]


Personal life

Henderson wed a member of the Kedah royal family, Tunku Dato' Harrunnarasheed Putra Tunku Annuar in 2015. Following the wedding, Henderson was conferred official mention, "Yang Mulia Datin Che Puan Amelia Thripura Henderson".[3] The couple divorced in 2018.[4]
