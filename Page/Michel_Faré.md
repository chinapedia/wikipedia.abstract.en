MICHEL FARÉ (1913–1985) was a French writer[1] and art historian, author of two seminal works on French still life.


Publications

-   _Le Grand siècle de la nature morte le XVIIe_, Paris, Fribourg, Office du livre, 1974.
-   _La Vie silencieuse en France, la nature morte au XVIIIe_, Paris Fribourg, Office du livre, 1976. In collaboration with his son, Fabrice Faré.
