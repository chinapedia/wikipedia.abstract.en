ABBEYFEALE AERODROME is an airport serving Abbeyfeale, a town in County Limerick in Ireland. The airport is west-northwest of Abbeyfeale.

The runway has a displaced threshold on each end. The Foynes non-directional beacon (Ident: FOY) is located north-northeast of the airport. The Kerry VOR-DME (Ident: SHA) is located northeast of the runway.[1][2]

Abbeyfeale is not a licensed airfield.[3]
