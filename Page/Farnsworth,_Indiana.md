FARNSWORTH was a former town in Cass Township, Sullivan County, in the U.S. state of Indiana.


History

Extensive strip mining in the area caused the town of Farnsworth to become extinct.[1]

A post office was established at Farnsworth in 1886, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1913.[2]
