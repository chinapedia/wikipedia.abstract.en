ANANDAPUR is a village, with a police station, in Keshpur CD Block in Medinipur Sadar subdivision of Paschim Medinipur district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Anandapur had a total population of 11,461 of which 5,741 (50%) were males and 5,720 (50%) were females. Population below 6 years was 1,337. The total number of literates in Anandapur was 7,826 (68.28% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Police station

Anandapur police station has jurisdiction over part of Keshpur CD Block.[2][3]
