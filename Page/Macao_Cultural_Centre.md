The MACAO CULTURAL CENTRE (CCM; ; ) is a cultural center in Sé, Macau, China.[1]


History

The cultural center was founded in 1999 with a cost of US$100 million.[2] In August 2017, the roof of the cultural center was damaged due to Typhoon Hato.[3]


Architecture

The cultural center is housed in a five-story building with a total area of 45,000 m².


Activities

The cultural center regularly hold musical performances.[4][5]
