MARCUS ERSSON (born 27 September 1997) is a Swedish professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with Brynäs IF of the Swedish Swedish Hockey League (SHL).[1]


Playing career

Ersson began playing hockey as a youth for Brynäs IF in the 2012–13 season where he played in their under-18 and under-20 teams. In the 2015–16 season, Ersson made his debut in professional hockey, playing 16 games for Brynäs IF of the Swedish Hockey League.[2]
