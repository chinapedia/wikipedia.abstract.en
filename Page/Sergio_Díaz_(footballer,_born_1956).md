SERGIO CARLOS DÍAZ LAZARTE also known as SERGIO DÍAZ (born October 4, 1956 in Buenos Aires, Argentina) is a former Argentine footballer who played for clubs in Argentina, Chile, Colombia and Mexico.


Career

Offensive wheel of excellent technique, displayed his talent in several clubs at the professional level. Among those who have Independiente Medellín of Colombia; Huachipato, Cobreloa and Colo-Colo of Chile.

In Colo-Colo was instrumental in obtaining Chilean Primera División Championship 1989, playing 28 games, completing 2520 minutes, scoring 13 goals.[1] also part of the squad Champion of Chilean Primera División Championship 1990, playing 6 games, 515 minutes scoring 1 goal [2]


Teams

-   San Lorenzo 1984

-   Independiente Medellín 1988

Titles
