MYSTERY is an EP by South African indie rock band Blk Jks. It was released on March 10, 2009 on Secretly Canadian Records, and is the band's debut release.[1]


Critical reception

_Mystery_ received generally favorable reviews from music critics. According to Metacritic, based on 7 reviews, the EP has a score of 80 out of 100, indicating "generally favorable reviews".[2]
