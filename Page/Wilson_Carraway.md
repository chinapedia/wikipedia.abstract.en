FRANKLIN WILSON CARRAWAY (October 16, 1904 – November 7, 1972) was an American politician in the state of Florida.

He served in the Florida State Senate from 1955 to 1965 as a Democratic member for the 8th district. From 1962–1963, he was president of the Senate.[1] He also served briefly in the Florida House of Representatives, from 1945 to 1950.[2]
