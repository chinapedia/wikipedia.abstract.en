K. S. HARISANKAR is an Indian playback singer who works in Malayalam cinema. He made his debut at the age of five, along with K. J. Yesudas, in the film _Saaphalyam_.[1][2][3]

He sang _Payye payye_ from _Ormayundo Ee Mukham_[4] and VidyaSagar compositions; _Nilaavum Maayunnu_ and _Vaanam Chaayum_ from films _Ennum Eppozhum_[5] and _Anarkali_. The song "Jeevamshamayi" from the movie _theevandi_ made a breakthrough to his career. His upcoming project is a music video named "Ekamaay".[6]


Personal life

Harisankar was born on November 18, 1993, at Haripad, Alappuzha district, Kerala as the elder son of Dr. Alappuzha Sreekumar who worked as Principal of Swathi Tirunal Music College, Trivandrum and Veena Vidooshi exponent Kamala Lakshmi. He is the grandson of M.P.Gopalan Nair who worked as Chief Engineer in All India Radio & singer K. Omanakutty. He is the great-nephew of Singer and Music director M. G. Radhakrishnan (late) and M.G.Sreekumar.[7] His younger brother, K.S. Ravisankar is a violinist.

On 21 May 2017, he got married to Dr. Gadha Sidharthan.


Biography

Harisankar is a trained classical singer under his father Aleppey Sreekumar and K. Omanakutty from Swathi Thirunal College of Music. He has won several awards and has appeared as a Carnatic vocalist at Music Academy, Chennai and several other venues in Kerala. He also own _Pragathi_, an independent fusion band.[8] He grew up under the tutelage of Dr.Omanakutty and her brothers M. G. Radhakrishnan and M. G. Sreekumar.[9]


Singing career

He sang his first song in GS Vijayan's film “Saphalyam” with Yesudas. Later he sung with KS Chithra for the film “Rishivamsam”, directed by Rajeev Anchal


Awards and honors

-   2019 Vayalar Award - Jeevamshamayi, Pavizha mazhaye
-   2019 Mazhavil Entertainment Award Best Singer - Jeevamshamaayi
-   2019 Asiavision Award for best singer - Jeevamshamayi (Movie : Theevandi)
-   2017 MS Subbalakshmi Fellowship 2017-19
-   2015 Asiavision Awards for new promising singer - Nilavum Mayunnu (Movie : Ennum Epozhum)[10][11]
