TOM MARRIOTT is an English youth footballer who plays as a midfielder for League Two side Mansfield Town.


Playing career

Marriott made his debut for Mansfield Town in a 1–0 win over Port Vale on 4 October 2016, in an EFL Trophy group stage game at Vale Park.[1]


Style of play

In 2015, the Mansfield Town website described Marriott as a right-sided attacking midfielder who was "a creative player with an eye for goal" with good intelligence.[2]
