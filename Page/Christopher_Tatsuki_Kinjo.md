is a former Japanese football player.[1]


Playing career

Christopher Tatsuki Kinjo played for J2 League club; Avispa Fukuoka from 2013 to 2014. In 2015, he moved to J3 League club; FC Ryukyu.


Personal life

Christopher Tatsuki Kinjo is the older brother of Justin Toshiki Kinjo. He is born in Japan, and is of American descent.[2]
