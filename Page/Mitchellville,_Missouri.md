MITCHELLVILLE is an unincorporated community in Harrison County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

An early variant name was "Woodbine".[2] A post office called Woodbine was established in 1857, the name was changed to Mitchellville in 1865, and the post office closed in 1888.[3] The present name is after James Mitchell, a pioneer citizen.[4]
