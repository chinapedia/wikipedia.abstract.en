GIACOMO PORFIDA or GIACOMO PURFIDA (died 1480) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Ariano (1463–1480)[1] and Bishop of Lacedonia (1452–1463).[2]


Biography

On 11 August 1452, Giacomo Porfida was appointed during the papacy of Pope Nicholas V as Bishop of Lacedonia.[3][4][5] On 8 April 1463, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Pius II as Bishop of Ariano.[6][7][8] He served as Bishop of Ariano until his death in 1480.[9][10][11]
