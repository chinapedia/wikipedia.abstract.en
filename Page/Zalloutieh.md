ZALLOUTIEH () is a local authority in Southern Lebanon, located in Tyre District, Governorate of South Lebanon.


Name

According to E. H. Palmer, _Kh. Zallûtiyeh_ means _the ruin of pebbles_.[1]


History

In 1881, the Palestine Exploration Fund's _Survey of Western Palestine_ (SWP) found "large heaps of small stones" at _Kh. Zallûtiyeh_.[2]

Presently, _Zalloutieh_ is listed as a Lebanese local authority in the Tyre District[3]
