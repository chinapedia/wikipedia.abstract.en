JADWIGA VUYK, née JADWIGA REICH ROSENBLATT (1886 – 1950) was a Polish-Dutch art dealer and art historian.

Vuyk was born in Łódź and married the Dutch banker Pieter Vuyk in Edmonton in 1912, who she divorced in 1930. She had already moved to Amsterdam with her daughter Margueritha Vuyk in 1927.[1] She is known for publications on Dutch masters, most notably Anthonie Blocklandt van Montfoort and Jacobus Storck.

Vuyk died in Amsterdam.
