THIBAULT VLIETINCK (born 19 August 1997 in Knokke) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays for Club Brugge in Belgian Pro League.[1]


Club career

Vlietinck is a youth exponent from Club Brugge. He made his senior debut on 14 October 2016 in the Belgian Pro League against Charleroi. He replaced Jelle Vossen after 89 minutes.[2]
