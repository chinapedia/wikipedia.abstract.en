MILL POND, also known as TOLIVER POND, is a historic natural pond located at Grandin, Carter County, Missouri. It is approximately 180 meters east of Grandin city limits at 3rd Street. The pond covers about 3 1/2 acres, is spring fed, and is about 60 feet deep.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.[2]
