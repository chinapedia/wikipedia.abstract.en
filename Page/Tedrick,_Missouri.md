TEDRICK is an historic town in Douglas County, Missouri, United States.[1] The location is on a ridge east of Fox Creek and approximately one mile south-southeast of Champion.[2]

A post office called Tedrick was established in 1898, and remained in operation until 1901.[3] The community has the name of one Mr. Tedrick, the proprietor of a local mill.[4]
