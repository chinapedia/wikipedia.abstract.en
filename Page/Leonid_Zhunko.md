LEONID MYKHAILOVYCH ZHUNKO () (born 6 June 1951, in Krasne Pole, Markivka Raion, Ukraine), is a Ukrainian politician.

In 2000s he served as a Presidential representative in Crimea as well as Governor of Sevastopol.[1]
