JULIA VIKTOROVNA GUZIEVA (; born 18 December 1988) is a Russian curler from Kaliningrad.[1] She played lead for the Russia women's national curling team at the 2016 European Curling Championships.


Awards

-   Russian Women's Curling Championship: Silver (2014, 2016), Bronze (2012, 2013, 2015)
-   Russian Women's SuperCup: Gold (2016)
-   Russian Mixed Doubles Curling Cup: Bronze (2011)
-   Master of Sports of Russia, International Class (2016)[2]
