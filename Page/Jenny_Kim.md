JENNY KIM (, born 22 November 1994) is a South Korean model and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Miss Supranational 2017. She was previously appointed Miss Universe Korea 2016, and competed in Miss Universe 2016.[1][2]


Personal life

Kim currently studies at Ewha Womans University double majoring in international office administration and English language and literature.[3] She also volunteers to be an interpreter at the National Museum of Indonesia in Jakarta.[4]


Miss World Korea 2015

Kim competed Miss World Korea 2015, became 1st runner-up.


Miss Universe 2016

Kim was appointed Miss Universe Korea 2016 by Park Jeong-ah, national director of Miss Universe Korea. Kim competed with 85 other contestants at the 65th Miss Universe 2016, where she was awarded Miss Congeniality.[5]
