The _JOURNAL OF DIABETES INVESTIGATION_ is a bimonthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering the study of diabetes. It was established in 2010 and is published by John Wiley & Sons on behalf of the Asian Association for the Study of Diabetes, of which it is the official journal. The editor-in-chief is Nigishi Hotta (Chubu Rosai Hospital). In 2014, the journal became open-access.[1] According to the _Journal Citation Reports_, the journal has a 2015 impact factor of 2.294.[2]
