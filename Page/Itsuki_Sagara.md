[1] is a Japanese actress.


Biography

Sagara was scouted in Takeshita street by Itoh Company during her third year junior high school's summer vacation in 2009.[2]

In 2010 she had started her entertainment activities. Sagara's acting debut was in the drama _Atami no Sōsa-kan_ in July.[3] Later in the December 14 she released the photo album _Hajimete no Suki_.
