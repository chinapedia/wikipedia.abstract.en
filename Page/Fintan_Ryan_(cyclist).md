FINTAN RYAN is an Irish male(?) track cyclist, representing Ireland at international competitions. He competed at the 2015 UEC European Track Championships in the team pursuit event and at the 2016 UEC European Track Championships in the elimination race event.[1] He was also 2013 national junior road race champion of Ireland. In the year following this he raced for the Nicolas Roche Performance Team in the 2013-2014 seasons.
