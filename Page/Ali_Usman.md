ALI USMAN (born 6 June 1993) is a Pakistani cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Pakistan International Airlines in the 2016–17 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy on 22 October 2016.[2]

He was the joint-leading wicket-taker for Multan in the 2018–19 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy, with twenty-two dismissals in five matches.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Multan in the 2018–19 National T20 Cup on 15 December 2018.[4]
