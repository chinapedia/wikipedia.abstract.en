_PARADISE_ (Italian: _PARADISO_) is a 1932 Italian comedy film directed by Guido Brignone and starring Nino Besozzi, Sandra Ravel and Lamberto Picasso. It was part of a group of "White Telephone" films made during the decade.[1] It was produced by Cines, the largest Italian film studio at the time.
