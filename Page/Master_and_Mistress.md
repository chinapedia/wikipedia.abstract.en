_MASTER AND MISTRESS_ (German: _HERR MEISTER UND FRAU MEISTERIN_) is a 1928 German silent film directed by Alfred Theodor Mann and starring Hans Albers, Maly Delschaft and Carl de Vogt.[1]

The film's art direction was by Heinrich Richter.
