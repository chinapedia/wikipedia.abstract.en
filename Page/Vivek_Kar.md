VIVEK KAR is an Indian bollywood film music director and score composer.


Career

Working as a composer, Kar debuted as a film composer in the year 2013 in the film _Zindagi 50-50_[1] where he composed four original songs.

Kar composed the songs for the film _Meeruthiya Gangsters_ in 2015,[2] _One Night Stand_ in 2016[3] , _Gun & Goal_ in 2015, _Care of Footpath 2_ in 2015, and _Direct Ishq_ in 2016.[4]

Vivek's movie Care of Footpath 2 was a lateral entry to Oscars as well [5]
