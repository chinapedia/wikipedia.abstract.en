_APQ_ (subtitled _THE MAIDEN VOYAGE SESSIONS, VOL. 3_) is a live album by saxophonist Art Pepper's Quartet recorded in 1981 at the Maiden Voyage nightclub in Los Angeles and released on the Galaxy label.[1][2][3]


Reception

The AllMusic review by Ron Wynn stated "Hardly vital, but contains some solid solos".[4]


Track listing

All compositions by Art Pepper except where noted.

1.  "Mambo Koyama" - 11:36
2.  "Valse Triste" - 9:21
3.  "What's New?" (Bob Haggart, Johnny Burke) - 9:58
4.  "Landscape" - 11:04

-   Recorded at the Maiden Voyage, in Los Angeles CA on August 13, 1981 (tracks 2-4) and August 15, 1981 (track 1)


Personnel

-   Art Pepper - alto saxophone
-   George Cables - piano
-   David Williams - bass
-   Carl Burnett - drums
