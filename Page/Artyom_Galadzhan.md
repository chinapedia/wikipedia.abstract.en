ARTYOM SERGEYEVICH GALADZHAN (; born 22 May 1998) is a Russian football player who plays as a forward for FC Lokomotiv Moscow.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Football Premier League for FC Lokomotiv Moscow on 26 November 2016 in a game against FC Ural Yekaterinburg.[1]

On 28 August 2018, he joined FC Orenburg on loan for the 2018–19 season.[2]


International

He represented Russia national under-17 football team at the 2015 UEFA European Under-17 Championship.
