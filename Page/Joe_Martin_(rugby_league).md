JOSEPH MARTIN (born 28 March 1995) is an English rugby league footballer who plays for Oldham in Betfred League 1. He plays as a fullback.[1]


Background

Martin was born in Halifax, West Yorkshire, England.


Career

Martin has previously spent time on loan at the Gloucestershire All Golds.[2]
