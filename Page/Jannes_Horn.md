JANNES-KILIAN HORN (born 6 February 1997) is a German footballer who plays as a defender for 1. FC Köln[1][2] and the German national under-20 squad.[3]


Career

Horn spent his youth career at VFL Wolfsburg, where he was promoted to the first team during the 2016-17 season.[4] In June 2017, Horn was transferred to 1.FC Köln who had recently qualified for the Europa League.[5]
