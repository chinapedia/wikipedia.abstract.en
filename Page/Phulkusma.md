PHULKUSMA (also spelled Fulkusma) is a village and a gram panchayat in Raipur CD Block in Khatra subdivision of Bankura district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Phulkusma had a total population of 4,003 of which 1,994 (50%) were males and 2,009 (50%) were females. Population below 6 years was 446. The total number of literates in Phulkusma was 2,744 (77.14% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Transport

SH 9 from Durgapur (in Paschim Bardhaman district) to Nayagram (in Jhargram district) passes through Phulkusma.[2]Barikul-Phulkusma Road meets SH 9 at Phulkusma.[3]
