The SPANISH NETWORK OF EXCELLENCE ON CYBERSECURITY RESEARCH (RENIC), is a research initiative to promote cybersecurity interests in Spain.

Initiatives and Participations

-   RENIC is ECSO member, and is also a member of its board of directors.[7]
-   A collaboration agreement between RENIC and the Innovative Business Cluster on Cybersecurity (AEI Cybersecurity) has been signed.[8]
-   RENIC is pleased to sponsor the Cybersecurity Research National Conferences (JNIC) JNIC2017 edition, organized by Universidad Rey Juan Carlos.[9][10]
-   RENIC is pleased to announce the publication of the online version of the Catalog and knowledge map of cybersecurity research[11]
