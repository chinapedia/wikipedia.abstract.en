SHANE O'DONOGHUE (born 24 November 1992) is an Irish field hockey player who plas as an forward for Belgian club KHC Dragons and the Irish national team.

He competed for the Ireland men's national field hockey team at the 2016 Summer Olympics, where they finished ninth.[1] At the 2018 World Cup, he became Ireland's all time topscorer with 94 goals.[2]

He currently plays club hockey for KHC Dragons in Belgium, which he rejoined in 2018 after he also played for them between 2014 and 2016.[3]
