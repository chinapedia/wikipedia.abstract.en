The CAMENCA is a long left tributary of the river Prut in western Moldova.[1] It flows through the villages Borosenii Noi, Alexăndrești, Camenca, Cobani, Balatina, Cuhnești, Chetriș and Călinești, and it discharges into the Prut near the village Pruteni.
