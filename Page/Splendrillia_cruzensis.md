_SPLENDRILLIA CRUZENSIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 10 mm and 13 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the Virgin Islands.
