JAMES MCBRYER was a Scottish amateur football left back who appeared in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.


Personal life

McBryer studied to be an engineer.[1] He was a volunteer with the British Red Cross during the First World War and also served as a motor boat engineer in Basra, Iraq.[2]
