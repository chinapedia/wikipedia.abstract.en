The ATLANTA DIVISION is a railroad division operated by CSX Transportation in the U.S. states of Alabama, Georgia, Kentucky, Louisiana, Mississippi, South Carolina, Florida & Tennessee. The ATLANTA DIVISION has 18 subdivisions.[1]

The subdivisions within the ATLANTA DIVISION are as follows:

-   Atlanta Terminal Subdivision
-   Abbeville Subdivision
-   A&WP Subdivision
-   Birmingham Mineral Subdivision
-   Boyles Terminal Subdivision
-   Camak Subdivision
-   Cartersville Subdivision
-   Etowah Subdivision
-   Georgia Subdivision
-   Gainesville Midland Subdivision
-   KD Subdivision
-   Lineville Subdivision
-   M&M Subdivision
-   Manchester Subdivision
-   NO&M Subdivision
-   PD Subdivision
-   S&NA South Subdivision
-   W&A Subdivision
