TARINER (Տարիներ "years") is a classic Armenian song by Harout Pamboukjian.


Cover version

The 2014 song by Sirusho is the remake version of Armenian singer Sirusho released it in November 2014.[1] The original song was released by Harout Pambukjian in the 70's. Produced by Sirusho, both the song and the music video have a modern feel to them and the song features Harout Pambukjian himself.[2] The music video was shot in Los Angeles.[3][4][5][6][7]
