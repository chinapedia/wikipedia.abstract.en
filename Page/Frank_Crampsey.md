FRANK C. CRAMPSEY (born 3 May 1932) is a Scottish retired amateur football goalkeeper who made over 110 appearances in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1][2]


Personal life

Crampsey's older brother, Bob, was a football historian, author and broadcaster.[3]
