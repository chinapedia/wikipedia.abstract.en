GEE KYUNG-HUN (Hangul: 지경훈, ; born 5 June 1990 in Seoul) is a South Korean footballer who is currently a free agent .

He is a versatile midfielder and can play as a defensive midfielder or a central defender.[1]


Club career

Gee spent two seasons between 2015-2017 at Hong Kong Premier League club Hong Kong Rangers.[2]
