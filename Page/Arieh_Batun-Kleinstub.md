ARIEH BATUN-KLEINSTUB (also "-Naveh"; אריה קלינשטוב-נווה; born January 26, 1933) is an Israeli former Olympic high jumper.[1]


High jump career

He competed for Israel at the 1952 Summer Olympics in Helsinki, at the age of 19. He came in 35th in the Men's High Jump with a jump of 1.70 metres.[2]

His personal best in the high jump was 1.88 metres in 1953.[3]
