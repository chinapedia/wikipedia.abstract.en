FREDA SLUTZKIN (died 1999[1]) was the first female lawyer in Mandatory Palestine.[2][3] She was born in Australia and studied law in Palestine before becoming the first woman in Mandatory Palestine to take and then to pass the bar examination in 1930.[4]
