ISHWAR CHAUDHARY (born 10 July 1988) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Gujarat in the 2008–09 Ranji Trophy on 23 November 2008.[2] Ahead of the 2018–19 Ranji Trophy, he transferred from Gujarat to Sikkim.[3] He was the leading wicket-taker for Sikkim in the tournament, with 51 dismissals in eight matches.[4]
