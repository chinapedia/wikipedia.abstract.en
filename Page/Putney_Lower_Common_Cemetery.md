PUTNEY LOWER COMMON CEMETERY is a cemetery on Putney Lower Common, Lower Richmond Road, London SW15.[1]

It opened in 1855, is closed to new burials, and is the smallest in the London Borough of Wandsworth.[2]

The cemetery contains the Commonwealth war graves of five British services personnel of World War I.[3]
