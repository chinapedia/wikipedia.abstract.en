MARCH is an unincorporated community in southeast Dallas County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] The community is located at the intersection of Missouri routes 38 and F.[2]


History

A post office called March was established in 1888, and remained in operation until 1915.[3] The community secured a post office in the month of March, hence the name.[4]
