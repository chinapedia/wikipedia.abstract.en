DANVILLE is a census-designated places in Montgomery County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Danville was laid out in 1834, and named after Danville, Virginia, the native home of an early settler.[2] A post office called Danville was established in 1834, and remained in operation until 1942.[3]
