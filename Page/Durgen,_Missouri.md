DURGEN is an unincorporated community in Lewis County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Durgen was platted in 1860.[2] The community took its name from nearby Durgens Creek.[3] A post office called Durgen's Creek was established in 1855, the name was changed to Durgen in 1895, and the post office closed in 1905.[4]
