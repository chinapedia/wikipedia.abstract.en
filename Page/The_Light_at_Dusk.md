_THE LIGHT AT DUSK_ is a lost 1916 silent[1] film drama directed by Edgar Lewis and produced by the Lubin Manufacturing Company.[2][3]


Cast

-   Sally Crute - Mrs. Krest
-   Hedda Kuszewski - Olga
-   Robert Frazer - Nicholas (*Robert W. Frazer)
-   Evelyn Terrill - Frances Farrell
