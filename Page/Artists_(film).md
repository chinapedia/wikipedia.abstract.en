_ARTISTS_ (German: _ARTISTEN_) is a 1928 German silent film directed by Géza von Bolváry and starring Gyula Szőreghy, Anton Edthofer and John Mylong.[1]


Cast

-   Gyula Szőreghy as Derr Zirkusdirektor Brasco
-   Anton Edthofer as Der Todesfahrer Henrik Elcot
-   John Mylong as Sein Kelly
-   Ernst Deutsch as Der Illusionist Maranoff
-   Dillo Lombardi as Der alte Artist Ettore Manelli
-   Mary Johnson as Seine Ziehtochter Helga Hardt
-   Werner Fuetterer as Fred Milson
-   Karl Harbacher as Hans, Milsons Freund
-   Teddy Bill as Ralph, Milsons Freund
-   Carl von Barany as Willy, Milsons Freund
