'''Ri Jin-sim ''' (born 29 May 1991) is a North Korean footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for the North Korea women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level, she plays for Wolmido in North Korea.[1]
