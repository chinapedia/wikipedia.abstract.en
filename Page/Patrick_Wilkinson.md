PATRICK WILKINSON (born May 19, 1999) is an American professional soccer player who plays for Saint Louis Billikens.[1]


Career

Wilkinson signed with United Soccer League side Swope Park Rangers on August 18, 2016.[2] He made his debut on August 22, 2016, as a 68th-minute substitute in a 1-1 draw against Saint Louis FC.[3]
