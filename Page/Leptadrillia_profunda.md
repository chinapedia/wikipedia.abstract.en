_LEPTADRILLIA PROFUNDA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 6.5 mm and 8.5 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off Florida.
