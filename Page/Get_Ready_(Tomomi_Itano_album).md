GET READY (stylized as GET READYâ™¡) is the second studio album released by Tomomi Itano. It was released in Japan on King Records on November 2, 2016.

It was released in three versions: a limited CD+DVD edition (Type-A), a limited CD+PHOTOBOOK edition (Type-B), and a regular CD edition.[1]

It included title tracks from all six Tomomi Itano's CD singles (from the sixth single "Come Party!" to the eighth single "Hide & Seek") and four new songs.
