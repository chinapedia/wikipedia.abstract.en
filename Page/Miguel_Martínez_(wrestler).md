Wrestling_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics,_Chunayev_vs_Martínez_8.jpg MIGUEL MARTÍNEZ (born March 23, 1991) is a Cuban Greco-Roman wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 66 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated in the round of 32 by Rasul Chunayev.[1][2]
