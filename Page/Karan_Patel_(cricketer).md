KARAN PATEL (born 30 September 1994) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Gujarat in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 20 October 2016.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Gujarat in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 29 January 2017.[3]
