MAHMUT AKAN (born 14 July 1994) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for MKE Ankaragücü.[1]


Professional career

Mahmut made his professional debut for Karabükspor in a 3-2 Süper Lig victory over Akhisar Belediyespor on 2 June 2017.[2]
