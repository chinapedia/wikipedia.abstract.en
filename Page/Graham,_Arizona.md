GRAHAM is a populated place situated in Graham County, Arizona.[1] It has an estimated elevation of above sea level.[2]

The first settlement at Graham was made in 1880 by a colony of Mormons.[3] A post office called Graham was established in 1882, and remained in operation until 1885.[4] The community takes its name from Graham County.[5]
