DAVE HEWITT (born 4 November 1995) is an English rugby league footballer who plays for Oldham in Betfred League 1. He plays as a scrum-half or stand-off.[1]


Background

Hewitt was born in St Helens, Merseyside, England.


Career

Hewitt is an England Schoolboys and England Academy international.[2][3][4]

He was in the Saints Academy system and played his junior rugby league with the Bold Miners club.[5][6][7] He has previously played on loan from St. Helens at Oldham in 2015.[8][9]

In 2016 Hewitt joined the Sheffield Eagles on a full-time deal.[10][11][12] In July 2016 he joined Oldham on loan from the Eagles.[13]

In October 2016 he joined the Roughyeds on a permanent deal.[14]
