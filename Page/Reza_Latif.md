REZA LATIF is a Bangladeshi film director, producer and cinematographer. He won Bangladesh National Film Award for Best Cinematography of the films Ononto Prem (1977) and Goriber Bou (1990).[1] He made 150 films since the early 1970s.[2]


Career

Latif first worked in the film Nacher Putul.[3]

Latif made his directorial debut in 2010 by the film _Ma Babar Shopno_.[4] His last directed film was _Bhalobashar Shesh Nai_.
