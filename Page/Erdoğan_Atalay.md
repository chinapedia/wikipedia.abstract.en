ERDOĞAN ATALAY (born September 22, 1966 in Hanover, Germany)[1] is a Turkish-German actor.[2]

In 2017 he married his girlfriend and manager Katja Ohneck.[3]


Biography

Having already made his first appearance as a minor actor in "Aladdin and the miracle lamp" at the National Theatre of Hanover before studying acting at the Hochschule für Musik und Theater Hamburg. Afterwards he took on first parts in several German television series such as _Music Groschenweise_, _Employment for Lohbeck_, _Double Employment_ and _The Guard_.[4] In 1996 Action Concept[5] engaged him for the role he has played successfully up to now: Semir Gerkhan in the German television series _Alarm für Cobra 11 – Die Autobahnpolizei_.[6]
