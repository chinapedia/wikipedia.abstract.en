VLADISLAV ANDREYEVICH SHADRIN (; born 11 June 1997) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Zorky Krasnogorsk.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Avangard Kursk on 25 September 2016 in a game against FC Torpedo Moscow.[1]
