HAARIG COMMERCIAL HISTORIC DISTRICT is a national historic district located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. In 2000, the area listed was and included 13 contributing buildings. It developed between about 1875 and 1950, and includes representative examples of Italianate and Tudor Revival architecture. They are predominantly two- and three-story brick commercial buildings.[1][2]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2000.[3]
