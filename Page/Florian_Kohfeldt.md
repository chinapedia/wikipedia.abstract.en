FLORIAN KOHFELDT (born 5 October 1982) is a German football manager, currently managing Werder Bremen.


Managerial career

Werder Bremen

Since 2006, he has coached various youth teams of Werder Bremen. From the 2014–15 season, Kohfeldt worked as assistant manager of Bremen, under head coach Viktor Skrypnyk. On 2 October 2016, it was announced that Kohfeldt would become head coach of Werder Bremen II, effective as of the following day.[1][2] Kohfeldt was appointed as the new manager of Werder Bremen after initially being the club's interim manager following Alexander Nouri's dismissal.[3] He finished with a record of 12 wins, 14 draws, and 16 losses in 42 matches with the reserve team.[4]

He was appointed manager of Werder Bremen on 30 October 2017.[5] In April 2018, he agreed a contract extension with the club until summer 2021.[6]
