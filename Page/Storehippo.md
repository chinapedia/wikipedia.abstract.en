STOREHIPPO is a SaaS based E-commerce platform.[1][2] StoreHippo platform creates websites that are automatically ready to be viewed on a mobile devices and hence ready for mobile commerce.[3]


History

StoreHippo was founded in January 2014 by Rajiv Kumar. It is headquartered in Gurgaon, Delhi- NCR, India.[4] It is a product of Hippo Innovations, a mobile e-commerce player, funded by esteemed investors from New York and Hong Kong.[5]


Product

StoreHippo is a DIY(Do It Yourself) platform to build eCommerce stores for B2B and B2C businesses.


NASSCOM - InnoTrek 2016

StoreHippo was amongst the 39 tech startups from India to participate in the NASSCOM InnoTrek 2016.[6]
