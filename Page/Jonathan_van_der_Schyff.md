JONATHAN VAN DER SCHYFF (born 23 May 1983 in Carletonville, South Africa) is a former South Africa Under-19 international rugby union player who played as a Flanker and lock for Glasgow Warriors and Currie.


Rugby Union career

Amateur career

Van der Schyff played for Currie.[1][2]

Provincial and professional career

Van der Schyff played for Blue Bulls U19 and Blue Bulls U20[3] in the Currie Cup. He also played for Sharks in the same competition.[4]

He played for Glasgow Warriors in the 2004â€“05 season.

He made one appearance in the Celtic League, playing against Newport Gwent Dragons.[5][6]

He made another single appearance in that season's Heineken Cup, playing against Scarlets in Wales.[7][8]

Back to South Africa for the 2006-07 season, he played for the Pumas.[9][10]

International career

Van der Schyff played for South Africa U18 and South Africa U19 age grades.[11]
