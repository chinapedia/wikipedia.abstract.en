STEPHANE LEONARD (born April 19, 1979) is a German artist. Leonard works in various different medias. His oeuvre spans from drawing and painting to sound installation, music, video installation, music videos and film.

StephaneLeonard_OT_Trauma_installationview1_Porto_2015.jpg


Education

Stephane Leonard studied philosophy and art history at the Humboldt University of Berlin from 2001 to 2002. From 2002 to 2007 he studied fine arts at the University of the Arts Bremen. His main focus was on drawing, studying in the class of professor Paco Knöller. Leonard was also a frequent visitor of the video art class under Professor Jean-François Guiton and participated in various courses about Electroacoustic music and Elektronic music and composition. From 2007 to 2008 Stephane Leonard became a master student (honours student) under Paco Knöller. Since 2014 Leonard is a guest lecturer at the Hochschule für Technik und Wirtschaft Berlin, where he teaches drawing and sound design at the department of Industrial Design.
