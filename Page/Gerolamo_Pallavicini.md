Biography

On 18 April 1485, Gerolamo Pallavicini was appointed during the papacy of Pope Innocent VIII as Bishop of Novara.[2][3] He served as Bishop of Novara until his death on 18 August 1503.[4][5] While bishop, he was the principal co-consecrator of Nikolaus Schinner, Bishop of Sion (1498).[6]
