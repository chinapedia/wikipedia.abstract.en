NIVENIOIDEAE is a subfamily of flowering plants and one of the two main subfamilies in the popular Iridaceae family. It contains seven genera,[1] from South Africa, Australia and Madagascar, including the only true shrubs in the family (_Klattia_, _Nivenia_ and _Witsenia_),[2] It also includes _Aristea_, _Cleanthe_, _Patersonia_ and also _Geosiris_, which was in its own family until being re-assessed.[3]
