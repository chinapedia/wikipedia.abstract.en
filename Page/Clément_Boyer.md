CLEMENT BOYER (born 27 July 1994) is a French rugby league footballer who plays as a or as a forward for Toulouse Olympique in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

Boyer was born in Tarn, France.


Career

Boyer is a French international.[2]

Boyer has previously played for Toulouse Olympique and Halifax.[3]

In 2016, he was nominated for the Kingstone Press League 1 Young Player of the Year.[4]
