_LEGAL EAGLES_ (Chinese: 法网天后) is a Singaporean drama produced by Mediacorp Studios Malaysia and telecast on Mediacorp Channel 8. The show aired at 9pm on weekdays and had a repeat telecast at 8am the following day. The series consists of 30 episodes. The Series is repeated at 5.30pm on Channel 8 on weekdays after _Good Luck_. It stars Felicia Chin , Zhang Zhenhuan , Hong Huifang , Rayson Tan , Wee Kheng Ming & Phyllis Quek as the casts of this series.
