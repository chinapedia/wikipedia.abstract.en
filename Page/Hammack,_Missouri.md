HAMMACK is an unincorporated community in Macon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Hammack was established in 1896, and remained in operation until 1904.[2] The community was named after one Mr. Hammack, the proprietor of a nearby mill.[3]
