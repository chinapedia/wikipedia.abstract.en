GÖKHAN KARDEŞ (15 May 1997) is a Belgian-born Turkish professional football player who currently plays for Afjet Afyonspor, on loan from Beerschot Wilrijk as a defender.[1]


Career

Kardes made his professional debut as Jong PSV player in the second division on 8 August 2016 against FC Den Bosch.[2]
