KNIGHTS (also known as KNIGHT) is an unincorporated community in Jasper County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Knight was established in 1881, and remained in operation until 1888.[2] The community was named after the proprietor of a local trading post.[3]
