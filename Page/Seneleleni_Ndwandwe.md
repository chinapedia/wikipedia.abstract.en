SENELELENI NDWANDWE (a.k.a. Seneleni Nxumalo) (died 1980) was Ndlovukati (Queen mother) of Swaziland during the reign of Sobhuza II.[1][2] She was full sister and co-wife to her predecessor, Zihlati Ndwandwe.[3]
