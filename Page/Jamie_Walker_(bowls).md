JAMIE WALKER (born 20 December 1991) is an English international bowls player.[1]

He was named as the 2015 Thomas Taylor 'Bowler of the Year'[2] after securing a National title in 2015. In 2016 he won two more National titles, the Junior singles and Mixed fours at Leamington Spa.[3]

In December 2016 he was part of the triples team with Andy Knapper and Robert Paxton who won the gold medal at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch.[4]

He has won six National Championship titles in 2011, 2013, 2015 (x 2), 2016 and 2017.
