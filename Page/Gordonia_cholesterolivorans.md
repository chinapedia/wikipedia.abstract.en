_GORDONIA CHOLESTEROLIVORANS_ is a bacterium from the genus of Gordonia which has been isolated from sewage sludge from a sewage treatment plant in Ciudad Real in Spain.[1][2][3] _Gordonia cholesterolivorans_ has the ability to degrade cholesterol.[4]
