_ACULOPS LYCOPERSICI_, also known as the TOMATO RUSSET MITE, is a species of mite that belongs to the family Eriophydae.

_Aculops lycopersici_ is an important pest in tomato plants.[1] It was first described by Massee[2][3] in 1937 in Australia, but is now common around the world.
