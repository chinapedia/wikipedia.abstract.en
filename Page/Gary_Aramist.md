GARY ARAMIST (גדי ארמיסט; born July 24, 1958) is an Israeli former Olympic sport shooter.[1][2]

He is Jewish.[3]


Shooting career

Aramist competed for Israel at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, California, at the age of 26 in sport shooting.[4] In Men's Free Pistol, 50 metres, he came in tied for 36th out of 56 competitors.[5] When he competed in the Olympics, he was 5-5.5 (167 cm) tall and weighed 139 lbs (63 kg).[6]
