EINAR HANSSEN (24 August 1874 – ) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Nesodden to Carl Julius Hanssen and Marie Elisabeth Giverholt. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1898, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1922 to 1946. He was decorated as Commander of the Swedish Order of the Polar Star, and Commander of the Danish Order of the Dannebrog.[1]
