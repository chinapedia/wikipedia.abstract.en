_CEREOPSIUS PRAETORIUS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Erichson in 1842, originally under the genus _Lamia_.It is known from the Philippines.[1]


Varietas

-   _Cereopsius praetorius var. elpenor_ Pascoe, 1862
-   _Cereopsius praetorius var. flavescens_ Breuning, 1944
-   _Cereopsius praetorius var. transitivus_ Breuning, 1944
