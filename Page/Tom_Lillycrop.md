TOM LILLYCROP (born 29 November 1990) is a rugby league footballer who plays for the Batley Bulldogs in the Betfred Championship. He plays as a prop.[1]

Lillycrop has previously played for the Dewsbury Rams and Sheffield Eagles in the Championship.[2]
