PORTOBELLO GAA is a Gaelic Athletic Association club in based in the Rathmines area on the south side of Dublin city.

The club has an adult men's football team which in 2016 played in the Dublin AFL Division 8 and won the Dublin Junior "C" Football Championship. The team is managed by Thomas Brennan.

The club also has camogie and ladies Gaelic football teams and a handball team.
