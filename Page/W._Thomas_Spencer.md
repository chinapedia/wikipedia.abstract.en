W. THOMAS SPENCER (born August 6, 1928) was an American politician in the state of Florida.[1]

He served in the Florida State Senate in 1967 as a Democratic member for the 45th district.[2] He also in the Florida House of Representatives, from 1963 to 1967.[3]
