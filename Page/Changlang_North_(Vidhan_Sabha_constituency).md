CHANGLANG NORTH is a location in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh, found in the district of Changlang, and home to its administrative headquarters.

It is located 237Â km west of the state capital of Itanagar.[1] It is one of the 60 constituencies of Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh. The current MLA (October 2016) of this constituency is TESAM PONGTE.

The highlight of Changlang tourism is the Namdapha National Park. Namdapha National Park is home to approximately 96 species of mammals, 453 avian species, and 50 reptilian species.
