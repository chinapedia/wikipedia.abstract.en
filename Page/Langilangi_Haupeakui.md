LANGILANGI HAUPEAKUI (born 3 July 1989) is a United States international rugby union player. His regular playing position is either No. 8 or Flanker.[1]


Amateur career

Haupeakui was born in San Mateo, California and played American football before playing rugby union at college. He played with Folau Niua at East Palo Alto Razorbacks.[2]

When not on Warriors duty, Haupeakui turned out for Stirling County.[3]


Professional career

He was picked up by Sacramento Express for the inaugural PRO Rugby league in the United States.[4]

It was announced on 28 September 2016 that Haupeakui signed for Glasgow Warriors on a two-year deal, subject to visa and medical.[5]

On 25 January 2017 it was announced that Haupeakui was released from Glasgow Warriors to return home to the USA for family reasons.[6]


International career

He was called up to the USA team in the summer of 2016.[7]
