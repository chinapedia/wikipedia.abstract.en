VLADISLAV MALKEVICH (; ; born 4 December 1999) is a Belarusian professional footballer. As of 2018, he plays for BATE Borisov.[1]


Honours

_'BATE Borisov_

-   Belarusian Premier League champion: 2016, 2018
-   Belarusian Super Cup winner: 2017
