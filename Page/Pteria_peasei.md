_PTERIA PEASEI_, common name SWIFT WING OYSTER, is a species of bivalve mollusc in the family Pteriidae.[1][2]

Distribution

This species is present in the Indo-West Pacific from Myanmar to the Philippines; north to Japan and south to northern Australia.[3]
