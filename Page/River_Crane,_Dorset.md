Cranborne,_River_Crane_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1330796.jpg The RIVER CRANE is a river in Dorset. It flows past Cranborne Manor.[1][2] Near Verwood it becomes the Moors River.

The name is a shortening of _Cranbourne_, meaning a stream frequented by cranes (or herons).[3]
