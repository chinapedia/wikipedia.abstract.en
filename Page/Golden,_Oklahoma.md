GOLDEN is an unincorporated community in McCurtain County, Oklahoma, United States.[1] The community is west of Broken Bow. Golden has a post office with ZIP code 74737, which opened on March 13, 1911.[2][3] The community was named for its first postmaster, James M. Golden.[4]
