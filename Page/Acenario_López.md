ACENARIO LÓPEZ or AGENNATIUS LÓPEZ (died 1521) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Santorini (1516–1521).[1][2][3]


Biography

On 31 Mar 1516, Acenario López was appointed during the papacy of Pope Leo X as Bishop of Santorini.[4][5] He served as Bishop of Santorini until his death in 1521.[6]
