ANTÓNIO ALFREDO BARJONA DE FREITAS (11 April 1860 – 8 May 1923)[1] was a Portuguese colonial administrator, military officer and politician.[2]

He was governor general of Cape Verde from 12 August 1903 until 29 December 1904.[3] He was Minister of Public Works from 14 May to 22 December 1909 in the government of Venceslau de Lima.[4]
