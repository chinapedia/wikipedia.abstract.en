NITURIA (also spelled Neturia) is a village, with a police station, in Neturia CD Block in Raghunathpur subdivision of Purulia district in West Bengal, India.

Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Nituria had a total population of 1,144 of which 580 (51%) were males and 564 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 149. The total number of literates in Nituria was 689 (69.25% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Police station

Neturia police station has jurisdiction over Neturia CD Block. The area covered is 203.65 km² and the population covered is 101,922.[2][3]
