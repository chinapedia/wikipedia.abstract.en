MALVIKA RAAJ (born 18 September 1993) is an Indian actress and model. She played a debut role in the Bollywood film _Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham_ in 2001, as Pooja the young Kareena Kapoor.[1][2][3]

She is now going to been seen as the leading lady opposite Rinzing Denzongpa (Danny Denzongpa’s son) in the movie called “squad” She appeared in a film called _Jayadev_ in 2017. Raaj will be next seen as the lead actress in Emraan Hashmi's _Captain Nawab_.[4]


Early Life

Malvika Raaj born in 18 September, 1993 at Mumbai. She is granddaughter of Jagdish Raj, daughter of Bobby Raj and niece of Anita Raj.[5]
