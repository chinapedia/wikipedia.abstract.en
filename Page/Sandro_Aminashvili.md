SANDRO AMINASHVILI (born February 21, 1992) is a Georgian freestyle wrestler. He competed in the men's freestyle 86 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated in the round of 16 by Zbigniew Baranowski.[1][2] At the 2018 European Wrestling Championships, Aminashvili won bronze medal in men's freestyle 86 kg event.
