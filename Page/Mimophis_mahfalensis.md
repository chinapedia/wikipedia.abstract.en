_MIMOPHIS MAHFALENSIS_ is a species of snake in the family Lamprophiidae. It is endemic to Madagascar[1][2] and occurs in the central and southern parts of the island; the northern population has been splitt off as a separate species, _Mimophis occultus_.[3]
