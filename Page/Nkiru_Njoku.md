NKIRU NJOKU (born ) is a Nigerian screenwriter, writer and video producer. She is the head writer of M-net's _Tinsel_, a Nigerian soap that started airing in 2008.[1] Njoku presently serves as the head content producer of _Project Fame West Africa_.[2]
