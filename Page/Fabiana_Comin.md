'''Fabiana Comin ''' (born 21 March 1970 in Fonte) is an Italian football coach[1] and former footballer who played as a goalkeeper for the Italy women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup and UEFA Women's Euro 2001.[2]
