ILYAZ ALIMOV (born 20 August 1990) is a Kyrgyzstani footballer who plays for FC Alay in the Kyrgyzstan League, as well as the Kyrgyzstan national football team.He is Uyghur


International career

Ilyaz Alimov made his international debut against China, in a 4–0 loss.[1] It took him almost two years to receive his second cap, with it coming on 6 June in a 6–0 loss to the hands of Iran at the Enghelab Stadium, coming on as a substitute.[2] He played in his first international victory in a 2–0 win over Kazakhstan, less than a week after his 26th birthday. He has gone on to play in a further two more national games, but is yet to score a goal.
