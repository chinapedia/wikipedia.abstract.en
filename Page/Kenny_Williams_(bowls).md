KENNY WILLIAMS was a former Australian international lawn and indoor bowler.[1]

Williams won a bronze medal in the 1984 World Outdoor Championships in Aberdeen with bowls partner Bob Middleton.[2] He played for Culburra Bowls Club in New South Wales.[3]

He was inducted into the Australian Hall of Fame.[4]
