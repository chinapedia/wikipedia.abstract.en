_UROMACER_ is a genus of snakes in the family Colubridae.


Geographic range

The genus _Uromacer_ is endemic to the island of Hispaniola.[1]


Species

The following three species are recognized as being valid.[2]

-   _Uromacer catesbyi_
-   _Uromacer frenatus_
-   _Uromacer oxyrhynchus_

_Nota bene_: A binomial authority in parentheses indicates that the species was originally described in a genus other than _Uromacer_.
