_OFFICE UPRISING_ is an American horror comedy directed by Lin Oeding and written by Peter Gamble Robinson and Ian Shorr that premiered on July 19, 2018, on Sony Crackle. The film stars Brenton Thwaites, Jane Levy, Alan Ritchson, Zachary Levi, Karan Soni and Ian Harding.


Plot

Desmond is a slacker who works at a corporate office and factory for Ammotech, one of the world's leading weapons manufacturers. When an energy drink created by the military turns his fellow coworkers into violent psychopaths, Desmond, his crush Samantha and his best friend Mourad must rise to the challenge and survive an office full of homicidal coworkers armed with the latest weaponry.

Production

Development

On October 26, 2016, it was announced that stunt performer Lin Oeding would direct a zombie film _Office Uprising._[1]

Filming

Principal photography on the film began in early-December 2016 in Alabama.[2]

Marketing
