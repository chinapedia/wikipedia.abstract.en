'''Ragy Abdel Hady ''' (راجي عبد الهادي, born 28 January 1974) is an Egyptian male water polo player. He was a member of the Egypt men's national water polo team, playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played for Al Ahly in Egypt.
