CHERIE JONES (also known by her stage name JONES) is a British-based alternative pop singer and songwriter. She came into the music scene in 2015, with her debut extended play, entitled _Indulge_, and was so called after her debut single of the same name. Her debut album, entitled _New Skin_, was released on 7 October 2016. She said in a recent interview that she recognises herself to be similar to musicians Lykke Li and Yukimi Nagano.[1]
