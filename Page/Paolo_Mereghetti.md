PAOLO MEREGHETTI (born 28 September 1949) is an Italian film critic.

Born in Milan, Mereghetti graduated in philosophy with a thesis about Orson Welles.[1][2] Currently the film critic of the newspaper _Corriere della Sera_, he collaborated with various magazines, including _Positif, Linus, SegnoCinema, Ombre Rosse_.[3]

Mereghetti is best known for the book collection of film reviews _Il Mereghetti_, he published in various editions starting from 1993.[4][5][6] In 2001 he was awarded a Flaiano Prize for film criticism.[7]
