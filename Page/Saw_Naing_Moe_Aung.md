SAW NAING MOE AUNG (; born 2 November 1994 in Myanmar) is a footballer from Burma, and a striker of Yangon United. He promoted from Yangon Youth Team to Yangon United Senior Team. Saw Naing Moe Aung was born in Kawthaung, Tanintharyi Division. He is Kayin and Christian.


Club career

Early career

Saw Naing Moe Aung began his football career playing for Yangon United Youth team in 2014. He scored 18 goals in 2014 MNL Youth League. He won Golden Boot and Best Youth Player of 2014 MNL. But he got knee injury and out of game for two years.

Yangon United

After his injury problem, Yangon United senior team called him. His contract will end in 2017.
