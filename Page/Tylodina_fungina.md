_TYLODINA FUNGINA_ is a species of sea snail or false limpet, a marine opisthobranch gastropod mollusk in the family Tylodinidae.


Ecology

_Tylodina fungina_ feeds only on _Aplysina gerardogreeni_ according to the _in situ_ observations on the Pacific coast of Mexico.[1] It is specialized on this sponge.[2]
