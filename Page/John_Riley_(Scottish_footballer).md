JOHN J. RILEY was a Scottish amateur football wing half who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park, Morton and Hamilton Academical.[1][2][3]


Personal life

Riley served as a private in the motor transport section of the Royal Army Service Corps during the First World War.[4]
