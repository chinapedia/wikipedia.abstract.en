The school has a capacity of 100 teachers and 900 students. The school understands itself as a _Eliteschule des Sports_. One out of four of the students is trained for national and international Sport Competitions. The school has a calisthenics parc, a beach volleyball court and a 40 meter long boulder wall. The School hosts two artworks from Bruno Wank and Stefan Wischnewski.

The school is located at the former Alabama Depot area.
