_MELGHIRIMYCES THERMOHALOPHILUS_ is a Gram-positive, halophilic, thermotolerant and aerobic bacterium from the genus of Melghirimyces which has been isolated from the salt lake Chott Melghir in Algeria.[1][2][3][4]
