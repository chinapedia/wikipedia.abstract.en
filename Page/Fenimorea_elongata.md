_FENIMOREA ELONGATA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of this marine shell varies between 18 mm and 25 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs in the Gulf of Mexico off Florida.
