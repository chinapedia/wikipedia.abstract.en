Born in Islington, London, to geologist Samuel Pickworth Woodward, Bernard came to Western Australia in 1889.[1] He is commemorated in the scientific names of several organisms, including the birds _Amytornis woodwardi_, _Colluricincla woodwardi_, and the tree _Eucalyptus woodwardii_.[2][3]
