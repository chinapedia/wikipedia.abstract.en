MOSTAGANEM () is a province (_wilaya_) of Algeria. The capital is Mostaganem. Other localities include Ain Nouissi, Ain Tadles, Tazgait and Stidia.


Administrative divisions

The province is divided into 10 districts (_daïras_), which are further divided into 32 _communes_ or municipalities.
