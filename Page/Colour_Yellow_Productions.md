COLOUR YELLOW PRODUCTIONS is an Indian motion picture production company founded by Bollywood director and Producer Anand L. Rai.[1]

Colour Yellow Productions has produced successful films that include Tanu Weds Manu franchise, Happy Bhag Jayegi franchise, Raanjhanaa, Nil Battey Sannata, Manmarziyaan and Zero.[2]
