JANINA MADELEINE MINGE (born 11 June 1999) is a German footballer who plays as a midfielder for Frauen-Bundesliga club SC Freiburg and the Germany women's national under-20 football team.


Club career

In 2015, Minge joined Bundesliga team SC Freiburg for the 2015–16 season at the age of 16 years.[1]


International career

She made her debut for the U-15 national team in April 2013,[2] in an 8–0 win over the Netherlands. She made three appearances for the under-16 national team in 2014.[3] In 2015, she was the second youngest player in the U-17 national team squad for the European Championship in Iceland where the team reached the semi-finals but were defeated 0–1 by the Swiss selection.[4] In May 2016, the team won the 2016 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship after a penalty shootout against Spain in Belarus.[5] The four Freiburg players in the squad contributed seven of Germany's 10 goals at the tournament and two of them, including Minge, successfully converted their kicks in the shootout.[6]
