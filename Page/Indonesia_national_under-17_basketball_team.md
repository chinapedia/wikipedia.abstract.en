The INDONESIA NATIONAL UNDER-16 AND UNDER-17 BASKETBALL TEAM is a national basketball team of Indonesia, administered by the Indonesian Basketball Association "PERBASI".[1]

It represents the country in international under-16 and under-17 (under age 16 and under age 17) basketball competitions.
