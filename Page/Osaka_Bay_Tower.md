The , also known as ORC 1,[1] is a mixed-use, mainly hotel and office, skyscraper in the Osaka Resort City 200 building complex located in the Minato-ku ward of Osaka, Japan. Completed in March 1993, it stands at 200 m (656 ft) tall,[2][3][4][5] with the top floor located at 188.7 m (619 ft).[6] It is the 5th tallest building in Osaka Prefecture[7] and the 36th tallest building in Japan.[8]
