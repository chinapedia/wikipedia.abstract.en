EMRE AKBABA (born 4 October 1992) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Süper Lig side Galatasaray.[1]

Emre was born in France to parents of Turkish descent, and made one appearance for the Turkey national football B team in a 2–2 (3–5) win over the Italy U21s.[2]
