The _DICCIONARIO ENCICLOPÉDICO HISPANO-AMERICANO DE LITERATURA, CIENCIAS Y ARTES_ (1887–99) was a Spanish language general encyclopedia produced by Montaner y Simón in Barcelona, Spain.[1]
