GROUP B OF THE 2017 AFRICA CUP OF NATIONS was played from 15 to 23 January 2017 in Gabon. The group consisted of Algeria, Tunisia, Senegal, and Zimbabwe.[1]

NOTES


Standings

In the quarter-finals:

-   The group winners, Senegal, advanced to play the runners-up of Group A, Cameroon.
-   The group runners-up, Tunisia, advanced to play the winners of Group A, Burkina Faso.
