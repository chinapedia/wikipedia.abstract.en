JAN SCHINDELMEISER (born 17 December 1963 in Flensburg) is a German director of football who last worked as the executive board representative for sport of VfB Stuttgart.


Career

In July 2016 Schindelmeiser became the sporting director and member of the executive board of VfB Stuttgart.[7]. However, after just a year, VfB Stuttgart announced that they have relieved Schindelmeiser of his duties as the club's executive board representative for sport.[8]
