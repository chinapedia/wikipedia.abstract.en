_MICROBACTERIUM LACUS_ is a bacterium from the genus of Microbacterium which has been isolated from sediments from the Shinji lake from the Shimane Prefecture in Japan.[1][2][3] _Microbacterium lacus_ has the ability to degrade sulfadiazine.[4]
