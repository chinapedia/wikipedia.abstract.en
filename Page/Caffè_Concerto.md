CAFFÈ CONCERTO is a European style restaurant chain with its headquarters in London, England. It was founded in 1996[1], and the original branch was on Regent Street.[2] The company runs 18 restaurants in London, and it is established in two countries, the UK and the UAE. In 2013, Caffè Concerto was voted "fastest growing company" by Real Business Inside.[3]


Products

Caffè Concerto serves breakfast, lunch, dinner, and afternoon tea.[4] It is known for its opulent window displays of wedding and celebration cakes,[5] as well as live music events at its branches in Westfield and Mayfair.[6]
