The _INFORMATION RESOURCES MANAGEMENT JOURNAL_ (IRMJ) is a quarterly peer-reviewed applied research academic journal which focuses on information technology management.[1] It is published by IGI Global. The journal was established in 1988.

The journal is published in association with the Information Resources Management Association.[2]


Abstracting and indexing

The journal is abstracted and indexed by the following, among others:[3]

-   ACM Digital Library[4]
-   Compendex
-   DBLP[5]
-   EBSCO
-   Emerald Abstracts
-   INSPEC
-   Scopus
-   Web of Science: Emerging Sources Citation Index (ESCI)
