is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team.


Club career

Takeoka was born on May 1, 1971. She played for Nikko Securities Dream Ladies. The club won L.League championship for 3 years in a row (1996-1998). However, the club was disbanded in 1998 due to financial strain and she retired.


National team career

On August 21, 1994, Takeoka debuted for Japan national team against Austria. She was a member of Japan for 1995 World Cup. She also played at 1995 AFC Championship. She played 3 games and scored 3 goals for Japan until 1995.[1]
