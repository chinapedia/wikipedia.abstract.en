RATNAJIT TAMANG (born 1 January 1993) is a Nepalese badminton player.[1] He was born in badminton family. His father Dil Bahadur Tamang was a former national player, and his sister Nangsal and Sara Devi also play badminton in the international event.[2] In 2016, he became the first Nepalese that won the international tournament in Pakistan together with Nangsal in the mixed doubles event.[3]
