WOMEN'S FOOTBALL IN BELGIUM is a growing sport in Belgium.


National Competition

Super League Vrouwenvoetbal is the top tier league of Women's football in Belgium. [1]


National Football Team

The Belgium women's national football team was founded and played their first match in the 1976. Since then, Belgium has never been to the FIFA Women's World Cup or the UEFA Women's Championship, although they qualified for the upcoming 2017 UEFA Women's Championship. [2]
