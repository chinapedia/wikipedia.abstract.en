WIN ZIN OO (; born 1 May 1991) is a footballer from Burma who plays as a defender for Southern Myanmar. He played 2015 SEA Games Myanmar national under-23 team.[1]

He played for Zeyar Shwe Myay in the Myanmar National League in 2016.[2][3]


Honor

-   Silver Medal = Football at the 2015 Southeast Asian Games
