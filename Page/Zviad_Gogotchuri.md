ZVIAD GOGOTCHURI (, born 30 October 1986) is a visually impaired Georgian Paralympic judoka. He won the gold medal in the 2016 Summer Paralympics in the Men's 90kg Category. This marked the first Paralympic medal of any colour for Georgia in its history.[1]
