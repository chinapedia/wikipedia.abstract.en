ARMANDO OBISPO (5 March 1999) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as a centre back for Vitesse on loan from PSV Eindhoven.[1]


Career

Obispo made his professional debut as Jong PSV player in the second division on 8 August 2016 against FC Den Bosch.[2]


Personal life

Obispo was born in the Netherlands and is of Curaçao descent.[3]
