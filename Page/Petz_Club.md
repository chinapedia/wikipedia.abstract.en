_PETZ CLUB_ (fully known as _PETZ CLUB, SOS ANIMAUX DISPARUS_[1]) is a French/Monegasque animated children's television series created by Dominique Amouyal and Hadrien Soulez Larivière. The series debuted on France 5's Zouzous block on June 30, 2014.[2]


Plot

_Petz Club_ is about a group of three young kids and their dog who find lost pets.


Characters

-   NINA is an 8-year-old who created the Petz Club.[3]
-   OSCAR is a 7-and-a-half-year-old, and is the detective of the group.[4]
-   TIM is a 7-year-old, and is the sportiest of the group.[5]
-   MARLOU is the Petz Club's dog.[6]
-   GILOU is a vet who helps the Petz Club with their investigations.[7]
