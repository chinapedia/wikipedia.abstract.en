XISHAN COAL ELECTRICITY (GROUP) CO., LTD. is a subsidiary of state-owned coal and coke conglomerate Shanxi Coking Coal Group (for 52.34% stake). The subsidiary is based in Taiyuan, Shanxi.

The company was the parent company of listed company Xishan Coal and Electricity Power. However, in 2001 all the stake were transferred to its parent company Shanxi Coking Coal Group.[1]

Claimed by the subsidiary itself, the subsidiary is China's largest and the world's second largest coking coal production enterprise; It involves coal mining and processing, coal mining machine manufacturing, and electricity equipment repair.[2]

In 2015 Xishan Coal Electricity Group produced 47.50 million tons of coal, 5.471 million tons of coke and 12.09 billion kWh of electricity.[3]


History

XISHAN COAL ELECTRICITY (GROUP) CO., LTD. was founded in 1956 as XISHAN MINING BUREAU (). In 1998 the organization was incorporated as a limited company.[4] In 1999 a subsidiary Xishan Coal and Electricity Power was incorporated and floated on Shenzhen Stock Exchange in 2000.[5][6] The listed subsidiary received 3 mines in Gujiao from the group () as well as three corresponding coal preparation plants ().[7] The capital raised from the IPO was used to acquire 1 additional mine () and two factories from Xishan Coal Electricity Group.[8]

Xishan Coal Electricity Group became a subsidiary of Shanxi Coking Coal Group in 2001. The stake in the listed company was also transferred from Xishan Coal Electricity Group to the parent company that year.[9]

In 2009, one of the mine () of Xishan Coal Electricity Group in Gujiao had a pre-dawn explosion.[10] In the same year, a coking factory () which produce coke and coal gas was sold to the listed company. The factory was the only provider of coal gas in Gujiao.[11]
