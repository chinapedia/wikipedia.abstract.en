CLÉMENT JEROME MICHELIN (born 11 May 1997) is a French footballer who currently plays for Lens. He has also represented the France national youth teams starting from the France national under-17 football team.


Club career

Toulouse

Michelin made his debut for Ligue 1 side Toulouse on 20 September 2016 against Lille, replacing Issiaga Sylla after 61 minutes in a 2–1 away win.[1]

Lens

On 11 June 2019, Michelin signed a three-year contract with Ligue 2 club Lens.[2]
