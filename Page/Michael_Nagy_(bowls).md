MICHAEL NAGY (born 29 November 1980) is a New Zealand international lawn and indoor bowler.[1]


Bowls career

Nagy has won two Asia Pacific Bowls Championships and won a bronze medal at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch in the triples with Ali Forsyth and Blake Signal before winning a gold medal in the fours with Forsyth, Signal and Mike Kernaghan.[2]

He was selected as part of the New Zealand team for the 2018 Commonwealth Games on the Gold Coast in Queensland.[3]

He won the 2013/14 pairs title and 2013/14 fours title at the New Zealand National Bowls Championships when bowling for the Taren Point Bowls Club.[4]
