DIEGO ANDRÉS CERÓN SILVA (born September 15, 1991 in Chile), known as DIEGO CERÓN, is a Chilean footballer who currently plays for Santiago Morning of the Primera División B in Chile.


Teams

-   Cobresal 2010–2016

-   San Antonio Unido 2011 (loan)

-   Malleco Unido 2013 (loan)

-   Santiago Morning 2016–present


Titles

-   Cobresal 2015 (Torneo Clausura Chilean Primera División Championship)
