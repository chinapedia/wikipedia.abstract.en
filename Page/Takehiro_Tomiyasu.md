is a Japanese professional footballer who plays as a defender for Bologna.


International career

In May 2017, Tomiyasu was elected Japan U-20 national team for 2017 U-20 World Cup. At this tournament, he played full time in all 4 matches as center back.

On 30 August 2018, Tomiyasu received his first international call-up from the Japan national football team for the Kirin Challenge Cup 2018.[1]

In December 2018, Tomiyasu was re-called to the Japanese squad for the 2019 AFC Asian Cup.[2]
