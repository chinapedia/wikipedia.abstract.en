OLEG VLADIMIROVICH TARABANOV (; born 1 August 1997) is a Russian football player who plays for FC Irtysh Omsk.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Irtysh Omsk on 2 October 2016 in a game against FC Dynamo Barnaul.[1]
