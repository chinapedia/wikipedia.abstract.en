WERNER HOLL (born 28 January 1970) is a retired German pole vaulter.

He finished sixth at the 1993 World Indoor Championships[1] and won the bronze medal at the 1997 Summer Universiade. He no-heighted at the 1992 European Indoor Championships,[2] and competed at the 1993 World Championships without reaching the final.[3]

Holl became German indoor champion in 1994, won a silver in 1993 and bronze in 1995. At the main German championships, he won a bronze medal in 1991 and silver medals in 1993, 1994 and 1997.
