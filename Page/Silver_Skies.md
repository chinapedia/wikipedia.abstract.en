_SILVER SKIES_ is the fourth studio album by Australian country music singer Sara Storer. It was released in November 2007 and peaked at number 60 on the ARIA Charts.

At the ARIA Music Awards of 2008, the album was nominated for ARIA Award for Best Country Album, losing to _Rattlin' Bones_ by Kasey Chambers and Shane Nicholson .[1]
