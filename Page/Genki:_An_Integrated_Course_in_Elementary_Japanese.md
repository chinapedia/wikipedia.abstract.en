_GENKI: AN INTEGRATED COURSE IN ELEMENTARY JAPANESE_ is a textbook for learners of Japanese language that starts at an absolute beginner level.[1][2] The textbook is divided into two volumes, containing 23 lessons focusing on Japanese grammar, vocabulary, and kanji.[3] It is used in many universities throughout the United States and Canada and also is often used as a self-study text.[4]
