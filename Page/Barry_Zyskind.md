BARRY ZYSKIND is a businessman in the insurance industry, and is the head of AmTrust Financial Services Inc.[1] In the September 2016 edition of Best's Review, an A.M. Best publication, AmTrust was ranked the 11th largest commercial insurer in the U.S., up from the 128th position in 2005.[2] In the interview, CEO Barry Zyskind attributed AmTrust's growth to its 'focus on small commercial customers, its proprietary technology platform, and its extensive database of loss history to help appropriately price and structure policies.'[3]


AmTrust

In 1998, when AmTrust was founded, Barry Zyskind held the position of Director of the Board. As of 2016, he is Chairman of the Board, Chief Executive Officer and President. He also holds key executive positions at the firm's many wholly owned subsidiaries such as AMT Warranty Corporation.[4]


Non-executive roles

Zyskind has been non-executive Chairman at National General Holdings Corp. since 2016.[5] He holds the same position at Maiden Holdings Ltd[6] and sits on the board of American Stock Transfer & Trust Co.[7]


Employment history

Before joining the ranks of AmTrust, Zyskind was an Investment Banker at Janney Montgomery Scott LLC.[8]


Education

Zyskind has an MBA from New York University Stern School of Business.[9]
