_ZEUCTOBOARMIA HYRAX_, is a species of moth of the family Geometridae. It is found in the Afrotropic ecozone, where it has been recorded from Gambia, Ghana, Kenya, Malawi, Mali, Sierra Leone, South Africa, Sudan, Tanzania, Yemen and Zimbabwe. The larvae feed on the pepper-tree _Schinus molle_.[1]
