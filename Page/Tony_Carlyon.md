TONY CARLYON (16 April 1970) is a former professional cricketer who played for Jersey and is the current team coach.[1]

In the final match for Jersey in the 2016 ICC World Cricket League Division Four tournament, the fifth-place playoff against Italy, he played alongside his son, Harrison Carlyon.[2] Injuries in the Jersey team forced him to play, becoming the first father and son to play together in an international match for Jersey.[3][4] He last played for Jersey in 2011.[5]
