CHARITON COUNTY JAIL AND SHERIFF'S RESIDENCE is a historic combined sheriffs residence and jail located at Keytesville, Chariton County, Missouri. It was built in 1906-1907, and is a 2 1/2-story, red brick building with Queen Anne style detailing. It has a high hipped roof with lower cross gables. At the rear is the two story, rectangular cell block. A one-story block addition was built on the jail in 1970.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1997.[2]
