_WHEN THE MOTHER AND THE DAUGHTER_ (German: _WENN DIE MUTTER UND DIE TOCHTER..._) is a 1928 German silent film directed by Carl Boese and starring Fritz Spira, Trude Hesterberg and Vera Schmiterlöw.[1]
