ISIDRO ROS RÍOS (born 6 November 1995), commonly known as ISI, is a Spanish footballer who plays for AD Alcorcón as a winger.


Club career

Born in Las Torres de Cotillas, Murcia, Isi joined CD Plus Ultra's youth setup, from AD Cotillas CF. He made his senior debut in the 2012–13 campaign, while on loan at CD Beniel.[1]

In January 2014 Isi was loaned to Elche CF, returning to youth football. He returned to Plus Ultra in June, and scored a career-best 15 goals during the season.

On 9 July 2015, Isi signed for UCAM Murcia CF in Segunda División B.[2] He contributed with 18 appearances and one goal, as his side achieved promotion to Segunda División.

Isi made his professional debut on 12 October 2016, coming on as a late substitute for Juan José Collantes in a 2–1 Copa del Rey away win against RCD Mallorca.[3]

On 5 January 2017, Isi was loaned to FC Cartagena in the third level, until June.[4] Upon returning, he became a regular starter for UCAM, with the club now also in the third division.

On 5 July 2019, Isi joined second division side AD Alcorcón on a two-year deal.[5]
