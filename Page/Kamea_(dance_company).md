Kamea}} KAMEA DANCE COMPANY is a dance company based in Beer Sheva, Israel.[1]


History

Kamea dance company was established in Beer Sheva in 2002 by Daniella Schapira and Tamir Ginz. The company is affiliated with Beer-Sheva Municipal Dance Center. The company performs works by Ginz and by guest choreographers.[2] Company regularly performs in Suzanne Dellal Center for Dance and Theater in Tel Aviv. Kamea dance company worked with Nacho Duato. [3] In 2017, company created dance version of Johann Sebastian Bach oratorio St Matthew Passion. [4]


Notable performances

-   _Bamidbar Devarim_. Choreography by Tamir Ginz. Premiered in Suzanne Dellal Center for Dance and Theater. [5]
-   _Gnawa_. Choreography by Nacho Duato. [6]
-   _St Matthew Passion_. Choreography by Tamir Ginz. Premiered in Erholungshaus Leverkusen. [7]
