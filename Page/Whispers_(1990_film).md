_WHISPERS_ is a 1990 Canadian horror film directed by Douglas Jackson and starring Victoria Tennant, Jean LeClerc, and Chris Sarandon. It is based on the novel of the same name by Dean Koontz.

The film has an Audience Score of 13% on Rotten Tomatoes.[1]

Film critic Leonard Maltin gave the film two stars.[2]
