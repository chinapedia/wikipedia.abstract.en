_RAISING RACE QUESTIONS: WHITENESS AND INQUIRY IN EDUCATION: RAISING RACE QUESTIONS_ is a 2014 book about race and ethnicity in the United States by Ali Michael.[1][2][3]


Overview

_Raising Race Questions_ explores challenges and opportunities that arise when White teachers deal with race and how it functions in their classrooms.
