_PHYLLIDIOPSIS BAYI_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusk in the family Phyllidiidae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from 38 m depth at Point La Revellata, Punta Bianca, Corsica .[2][3]


Description

This nudibranch has a translucent white dorsum with brown flecks of pigment. It is a small Phyllidiid, growing to 8Â mm in length, although the specimens were immature and the maximum size is unknown.[4]


Diet

This species feeds on a sponge.
