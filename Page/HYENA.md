HYENA (also known as ANDREAS SÖDERLUND) is a gold record-selling[1][2] Swedish producer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist.[3]

HYENA'S track record contains acts such as Elias (Swedish Grammy nominated[4]),[5] Erik Rapp,[6] Grace Carter, Jarryd James, Shout Out Louds, Janice (Swedish Grammy nominated[7]) [8][9][10] (who he had one of the most played Swedish songs with on P3 during 2016),[11] and Saint to name a few. He has also produced Hello Saferide's debut album, "Introducing Hello Saferide".

Andreas (vocals, guitar) was also a member of Niccokick, a guitar-driven Swedish indie rock group, formed 2001 in Båstad, Sweden.[12]
