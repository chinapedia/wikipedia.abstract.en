_ACAVUS SUPERBUS_ is a species of air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Acavidae.

Three subspecies recognized.[1]


Description

_Acavus superbus_ has green colored shell and rose brown body.[2]


Distribution

This species is endemic to Sri Lanka. It is confined to wet zone of the country.


Subspecies

-   _Acavus superbus grevillei_
-   _Acavus superbus roseolabiatus_
-   _Acavus superbus superbus_
