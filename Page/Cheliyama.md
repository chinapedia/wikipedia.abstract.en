CHELIYAMA (also referred to as Cheyama) is a village in Raghunathpur II CD Block in Raghunathpur subdivision of Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Cheyama had a total population of 7,413 of which 3,809 (51%) were males and 3,604 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 853. The total number of literates in Cheyama was 4,758 (72.53% of the population over 6 years).[1]


CD Block HQ

The headquarters of Raghunathpur II CD Block are located at Chelyama.[2]


Transport

State Highway 8 (West Bengal) running from Santaldih (in Purulia district) to Majhdia (in Nadia district) passes through Chelyama.[3]


Healthcare

Banda (Cheliyama) Block Primary Health Centre functions with 15 beds.[4]
