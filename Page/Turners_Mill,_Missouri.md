TURNERS MILL is an extinct town in eastern Oregon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. The GNIS classifies it as a populated place.[1] The mill was adjacent to Turners Spring on the north bank of the Eleven Point River.[2]

The community has the name of Jesse L. Clay Turner, the proprietor of a local mill.[3] A variant name was "Surprise".[4] A post office called Surprise was established in 1895, and remained in operation until 1925.[5]
