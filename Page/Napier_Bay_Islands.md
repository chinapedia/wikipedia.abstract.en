NAPIER BAY ISLANDS is a group of islands of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the South Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] The islands are north of Port Blair.


Geography

The islands are situated north of Shoal Bay. The northern islands of this group form part of a natural harbour called Port Meadows.
