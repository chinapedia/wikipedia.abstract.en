_JANAKPUR TODAY_ (Nepali: जनकपुर टुडे) is a Nepali language national daily, published from Janakpurdham. It is owned by Janakpur Today Media Group. The chairman of the media group, Arun Singhaniya, was murdered in 2010, reportedly over content published by Janakpur Today.[1][2]
