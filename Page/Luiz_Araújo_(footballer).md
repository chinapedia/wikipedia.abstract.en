LUIZ DE ARAÚJO GUIMARÃES NETO (born 2 June 1996), known as LUIZ ARAÚJO, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays for Lille OSC as a Winger.


Club career

Born in Taquaritinga, São Paulo, Luiz Araújo represented Mirassol as a youth, being promoted to the first team in January 2013.[1] In March, he moved to São Paulo, returning to youth football.[2]

On 26 February 2016, after being the top goalscorer of that year's U-20 Copa Libertadores, Luiz Araújo was loaned to Grêmio Novorizontino until the end of 2016 Campeonato Paulista.[3] He made his professional debut on 19 March, coming on as a second-half substitute in a 1–1 home draw against XV de Piracicaba.

Returning to _Tricolor_ in April 2016, Luiz Araújo was immediately included in the first team and made his Série A debut on 5 June, replacing Ytalo in a 1–0 away win against Cruzeiro.

On June 4th 2017, Araújo played his last match for São Paulo FC, in a lost for 1-0 against AA Ponte Preta. Two days later, he did not train with the team, to make the last details for his transfer to French side Lille OSC.[4][5]
