OYBEK ABDUGAFFOROV (30, 1995) is a Tajik footballer who currently plays for FK Khujand and the Tajikistan national football team.[1]


Career

Club

In August 2017, Abdugafforov signed for FC Istiklol.[2]

On 22 February 2019, FK Khujand announced the signing of Abdugafforov.[3]

International

Abdugafforov was chosen for the 2015 Commonwealth of Independent States Cup squad for the Tajikistan national under-21 football team[4]

Abdugafforov made his senior debut in a friendly testimonial match against Syria commemorating the 20th anniversary of the independence of Tajikistan,[5] with his second appearing coming against Palestine.[6]
