_THE ENGLISH ROSE_ is a 1920 British silent drama film directed by Fred Paul and starring Humberston Wright.[1]


Cast

-   Fred Paul as Father Michael
-   Humberston Wright as Captain MacDonnell
-   Sydney Folker as Harry O'Malley
-   Mary Morton
-   Jack Raymond
-   Amy Brandon Thomas
-   George Turner
-   Clifford Desborough
