IVAN SERGEYEVICH ZLOBIN (; born 7 March 1997) is a Russian professional footballer who plays for Portuguese club Benfica as a goalkeeper.[1]


Club career

Born in Tyumen, Zlobin spent three years with CSKA Moscow's youth system before joining Portuguese club União de Leiria in October 2015, aged 18.[2] In January 2016, he was loaned out to Benfica, where he played for their under-19 team before signing a permanent five-year contract with the Lisbon side on 25 June that year.[3][4]

On 28 November 2016, Zlobin made his professional debut with Benfica's reserve team as a starter in a 1–0 home loss to AD Fafe in LigaPro.[5][6] On 1 February 2019, he was promoted to the club's first team, alongside three other Benfica B players.[7]
