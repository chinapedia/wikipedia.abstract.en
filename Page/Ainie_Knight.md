AINIE KNIGHT (born 24 May 1986) is a Philippines international lawn and indoor bowler.[1]

Knight from Sagay, Negros Occidental, in the Philippines won a bronze medal in the pairs at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Adelaide.[2]
