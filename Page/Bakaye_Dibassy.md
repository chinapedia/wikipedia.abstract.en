BAKAYE DIBASSY (born 11 August 1989) is a professional footballer who plays for Amiens and the Mali national football team.[1]


International career

Dibassy was born in France and is of Malian descent.[2] Dibassy made his debt for the Mali national football team in a 6–0 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification loss to Morocco on 1 September 2017.[3]
