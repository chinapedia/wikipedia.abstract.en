NEAL SCHUERER (born November 16, 1954) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Schuerer was born in Amana, Iowa. He attended Central College and is a restaurateur. A Republican, he served in the Iowa State Senate from 1997 to 2005 (30th district from 1997 to 2003 and 38th district from 2003 to 2005).[1]
