VAUGHN REGINALD WADDELL (September 29, 1910 – March 7, 1980) was an American professional basketball player.[1][2] He played in the National Basketball League for the Detroit Gems for eight games early in the 1946–47 season. He averaged 3.1 points per contest.[3]
