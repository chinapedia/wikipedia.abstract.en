SHANE LEE LINDSTROM (born February 11, 1994), known professionally as MURDA BEATZ, is a Canadian record producer and songwriter. He produced hit singles like "No Frauds" by Trinidadian-born American rapper Nicki Minaj, "Back on Road" by American rapper Gucci Mane, "Nice for What" by Canadian rapper Drake, "Motorsport" by American rap trio Migos and "Fefe" by American rapper 6ix9ine alongside frequent collaborators German production duo Cubeatz.

The producer makes use of popular production software FL Studio.[1]
