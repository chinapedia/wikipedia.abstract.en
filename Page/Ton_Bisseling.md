TON BISSELING (born 1952)[1] is a Dutch molecular biologist. He is a professor at Wageningen University and Research Centre.

In 1975 Bisseling obtained a degree in biology from the Radboud University Nijmegen.[2] Five years later he earned his PhD in molecular biology at Wageningen University. In 1998 Bisseling became professor and head of the molecular biology laboratory at the same institution.[3][4] During his career Bisseling has done research on the symbiosis between root nodules and rhizobia bacteria.[5][6]

Bisseling was elected a member of the Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences in 2010.[7]
