FRISBEE is an unincorporated community in Dunklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A variant name was "Manley".[2] A post office called Frisbee was established in 1907, the name was changed to Manley in 1908, and the post office closed in 1918.[3] The present name is apparently after one Mr. Frisbee, a railroad worker.[4]
