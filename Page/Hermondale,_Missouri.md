HERMONDALE is an unincorporated community in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Hermondale was established in 1919, and remained in operation until 1955.[2] The community was named after Hermon G. Reynolds, the original owner of the town site.[3]
