VICENTE PÉREZ SELFA (born 21 October 1997) is a Spanish motorcycle racer.[1] He races in the FIM CEV Moto3 Junior World Championship aboard a KTM RC 250 GP.

Following the death of fellow Avintia Academy rider Andreas Pérez[2] he switched his bike number to #77 as a tribute while also replacing Livio Loi in the Moto3 category.[3]
