VLAD SLAVNIC (born 10 September 1958) is a retired Australian discus thrower.

He finished seventh at the 1986 Commonwealth Games. Slavnic also became Australian champion in 1982.[1] His personal best throw was 64.10 metres, achieved in January 1988 in Melbourne.[2]
