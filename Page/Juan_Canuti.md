JUAN CANUTI (died 1545) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Cariati e Cerenzia (1535–1545) and Bishop of Carinola (1530–1535).[1]


Biography

On 21 October 1530, Juan Canuti was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VII as Bishop of Carinola.[2] On 15 January 1535, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul III as Bishop of Cariati e Cerenzia.[3] He served as Bishop of Cariati e Cerenzia until his death in 1545.[4]
