'''Yada Sengyong ''' (born 10 September 1993) is a Thai footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for the Thailand women's national football team.[1] She was part of the team at the 2015 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she plays for North Bangkok College in Thailand.[2]
