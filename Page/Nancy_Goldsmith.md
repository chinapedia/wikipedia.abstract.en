NANCY GOLDSMITH (נאנסי גולדשמידט; born August 4, 1966) is an Israeli former Olympic gymnast.[1]


Early life and gymnastics career

Goldsmith is Jewish.[2] Much of her training was at the National Academy of Artistic Gymnastics in Eugene, Oregon.[3]

She attended Stanford University starting in January 1984, and competed on the gymnastics team while a member of the Israel national team.[4][5] In 1984, Goldsmith was named All-Western Collegiate Athletic Association.[6] In 1987, she was named Stanford's Most Outstanding Gymnast.[7]

Goldsmith competed for Israel at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, California, at the age of 17 in gymnastics.[8] In the Women's Individual All-Around she came in 31st out of 65 competitors.[9][10] When she competed in the Olympics she was 5-2.5 (160 cm) tall and weighed 99 lbs (45 kg).[11]
