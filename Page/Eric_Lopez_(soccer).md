ERIC LOPEZ (born March 5, 1999) is an American soccer player who plays as a goalkeeper for LA Galaxy II of the United Soccer League.


Career

Youth

Lopez began playing with the LA Galaxy academy in 2012.[1]

Professional

Lopez signed with LA Galaxy II ahead of their 2015 season.[2] He was the youngest player ever to sign a United Soccer League contract.[3]

Lopez made his professional debut for LA Galaxy II as a 20th-minute substitute against Orange County Blues on August 20, 2016.[4] He then became the starting goalkeeper for _Los Dos_ in the middle of the 2017 USL season.

International

Lopez has played regularly with United States youth teams. He was the youngest player to be named to the United States under-17 final squad for the 2015 FIFA U-17 World Cup.
