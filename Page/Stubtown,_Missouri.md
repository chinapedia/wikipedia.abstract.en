STUBTOWN is an unincorporated community in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A variant name was "Game".[2] A post office called Game was established in 1898, and remained in operation until 1911.[3] The present name honors the local Stubbs family.[4]
