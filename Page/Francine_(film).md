_FRANCINE_ is a 2012 American-Canadian[1] drama film written and directed by Brian M. Cassidy and Melanie Shatzky and starring Melissa Leo.[2][3][4] It is Cassidy and Shatzky's directorial debut.[5]

Production

The film was shot in the Hudson Valley of New York.[6]


Reception

The film has a 60% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.[7] Roger Ebert gave the film three and a half stars out of four.[8] Michael Phillips of the _Chicago Tribune_ gave the film three stars.[9] Keith Uhlich of _Time Out_ gave the film four stars out of five.[10] Eric Kohn of IndieWire gave the film a B-.[11]
