XIX GMBH was a Swiss aircraft manufacturer based in Kronbühl, founded by Michi Kobler. The company specialized in the design and manufacture of paragliders in the form of ready-to-fly aircraft.[1]

The company seems to have been founded before 1998 and gone out of business in about 2009.[2]

The company was organized as a Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung, a Swiss limited liability company.[3]

In 2003 the company had a wide range of paragliders in their line, including the beginner Smile, the beginner to intermediate Art, the intermediate Form and Inter, the performance Top and the Sens competition wing series.[4]
