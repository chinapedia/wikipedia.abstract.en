KISBÉR () is a district in south-western part of Komárom-Esztergom County. _Kisbér_ is also the name of the town where the district seat is found. The district is located in the Central Transdanubia Statistical Region.


Geography

Kisbér District borders with Komárom District to the north, Oroszlány District to the east, Mór District _(Fejér County)_ to the southeast, Zirc District _(Veszprém County)_ to the southwest, Pannonhalma District and Győr District _(Győr-Moson-Sopron County)_ to the west. The number of the inhabited places in Kisbér District is 17.

Ethnicity

Besides the Hungarian majority, the main minorities are the Roma (approx. 350) and German (200).

Total population (2011 census): 20,284
Ethnic groups (2011 census):[5] Identified themselves: 18,120 persons:

-   Hungarians: 17,378 (95.91%)
-   Gypsies: 353 (1.95%)
-   Germans: 199 (1.10%)
-   Others and indefinable: 190 (1.05%)

Approx. 2,000 persons in Kisbér District did not declare their ethnic group at the 2011 census.
