Demographics

Cheedella is the most populated village in Penpahad mandal . It has population of 5943 of which 2986 are males while 2957 are females as per Population Census 2011. The literacy rate of village was 59.34 % where Male literacy stands at 69.72 % while female literacy rate was 48.78 %. [2]


Politics

It falls under Suryapet Assembly constituency and the village is administrated by Sarpanch , who is elected representative of village.
