SHARP is an unincorporated community in Ozark County, Missouri.[1] The village location is a quarter mile south of US 160 between Caulfield and Tecumseh[2][3] at the head of Hickory Stump Hollow.[4]


History

A post office called Sharp was established in 1890, and remained in operation until 1915.[5] According to tradition, the community was so named on account of a certain settler being considered "sharp".[6]
