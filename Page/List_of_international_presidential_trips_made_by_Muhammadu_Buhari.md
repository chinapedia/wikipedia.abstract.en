The following is a LIST OF INTERNATIONAL PRESIDENTIAL TRIPS MADE BY MUHAMMADU BUHARI as the 15th President of Nigeria.


Summary of International Trips

Here is a summary of the countries President Muhammadu Buhari of Nigeria has visited as President. There are two trips that he made to the United Kingdom, one on his official vacation and one for medical treatment that are included in this list.[1]

  1 visit            Cameroon, China, Egypt, Equatorial Guinea, Ethiopia, India, Iran, Mali, Malta, Morocco, Niger, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Senegal, Sudan, UAE

  3+ visits          Benin, France, United Kingdom, United States
