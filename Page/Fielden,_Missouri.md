FIELDEN is an unincorporated community in south central Douglas County, Missouri.[1] Fielden is located on the floodplain of Fox Creek along County Road 14-343 south of Missouri Route 14 and west of Gentryville.[2] The townsite is at an elevation of .[3]


History

A post office called Fielden was established in 1894, and remained in operation until 1909.[4] The community took its name from a local sawmill of the same name.[5]
