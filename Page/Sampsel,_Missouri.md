SAMPSEL (also spelled SAMPSELL) is an unincorporated community in Livingston County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Sampsel was originally spelled Sampsell, and under the latter name was platted in 1870 when the railroad was extended to that point.[2] The community has the name of J. B. F. Sampsell, a railroad man.[3] A post office called Sampsell Station was established in 1871, the name was changed to Sampsell in 1906, and the post office closed in 1953.[4]
