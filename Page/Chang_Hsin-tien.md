CHANG HSIN-TIEN (; born 11 April 1996) is a Taiwanese badminton player.[1][2]


Career

She plays mixed doubles with Chang Ko-chi. Together they were the runner up at the 2015 Chinese Taipei Masters Grand Prix losing there to the Indonesian pair Ronald Alexander and Melati Daeva Oktaviani in the finals.


Achievements

BWF Grand Prix

The BWF Grand Prix has two levels, the Grand Prix and Grand Prix Gold. It is a series of badminton tournaments sanctioned by the Badminton World Federation (BWF) since 2007.
