STENBOLONE ACETATE () (brand name STENOBOLONE, ANATROFIN; former developmental code names RS-2106, S-3760), also known as 2-METHYL-4,5Α-DIHYDRO-Δ¹-TESTOSTERONE 17Β-ACETATE (2-METHYL-Δ¹-DHT 17Β-ACETATE) or as 2-METHYL-5Α-ANDROST-1-EN-17Β-OL-3-ONE 17Β-ACETATE, is a synthetic, injected anabolic–androgenic steroid (AAS) and derivative of dihydrotestosterone (DHT) which has been marketed in Spain.[1][2][3] It is the C17β acetate ester of stenbolone, which is structurally related to 1-testosterone (Δ¹-DHT; dihydroboldenone) and to drostanolone (2α-methyl-DHT).[4][5][6]
