ROMAN SHANDRUK (; born 29 May 1998 in Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder who plays for FC Volyn Lutsk in the Ukrainian Premier League.

Shandruk is a product of the FC Volyn Youth Sportive School System. Then he signed a professional contract with FC Volyn Lutsk in the Ukrainian Premier League.

He made his debut in the Ukrainian Premier League for FC Volyn on 20 November 2016, playing in the match against FC Zirka Kropyvnytskyi.[1]
