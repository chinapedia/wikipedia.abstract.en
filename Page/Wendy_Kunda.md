'''Wendy Kunda ''' (born 23 October 1997) is a Zambian footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for the Zambia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship.[1] On club level she played for Mooba Queens in Zambia.[2]
