REECE THOMAS FIELDING (born 23 October 1998) is an English professional footballer who plays as a defender for Ossett United.


Playing career

Fielding came through the Doncaster Rovers youth team to sign a two-year scholarship contract in May 2015.[1] He made his first-team debut in a 2–0 win over Mansfield Town in an EFL Trophy group stage game at Field Mill on 30 August 2016.[2]
