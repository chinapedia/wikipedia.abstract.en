HIMACHAL PRADESH NATIONAL LAW UNIVERSITY SHIMLA (HPNLU SHIMLA) is a National Law University located at Shimla, Himachal Pradesh, India. It is the 20th National Law University established in India.National Law University, is set up with the vision to provide affordable quality legal education with an aim to educate and cater the needs of students.NLU Shimla is governed by High Court of Himachal Pradesh[1]
