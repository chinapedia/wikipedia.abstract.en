JOACHIM WAROI (born September 20, 1988) is an association football attacker from the Solomon Islands who plays for Western United and the Solomon Islands national football team.[1][2]


Club career

Signed for Lae City Dwellers FC in 2016 for an anonymous fee to enhance their attacking options.

Former Hekari United forward was given a one-match ban when celebrating a goal scored against his former club in an impudent manner by taking his jersey off and covering his head with it while running around which did not conform to the guidelines.[3]
