JOHN ROBERT BAYLY A'DEANE (21 March 1865 – 14 March 1924) was a New Zealand first-class cricketer active 1893–94 who played for Hawke's Bay. He was born and died in Takapau, Hawke's Bay. A'Deane's batting and bowling styles are unrecorded. He played in three first-class matches, scoring 33 runs with a highest score of 17 and held two catches. He took three wickets with a best innings return of two for 31.[1][2]
