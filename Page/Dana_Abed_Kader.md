DANA ABED KADER (born 1996) is a Romanian handballer who plays as left wing for HC Zalău.[1]

After the first four rounds of the 2016–17 Liga Națională season, Abed Kader leads most goals list.


Achievements

-   IHF YOUTH WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP:
    -   _Gold Medalist_: 2014


Personal life

She is of Syrian descent through her father. Her father lives in Syria. Dana lives with her sister, mother and grandmother in Bucharest. Her sister, Sara, is also a handballer.[2]
