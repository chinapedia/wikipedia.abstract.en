BANGLADESH COMMERCE BANK LIMITED is a private sector commercial bank in Bangladesh.


History

The Bank can trace its origins to the former Bangladesh Commerce and Investment Limited was set up on 27 January 1986.[1] It was a non-bank financial institution. In April 1992 its activities were suspended by Bangladesh Bank due to a liquidity crises.[2] To protect the banking sector, the employees and customers of the firm it was changed into a bank by Bangladesh Bank.[3] Bangladesh Commerce Bank Limited was incorporated on 1 June 1998.[4]
