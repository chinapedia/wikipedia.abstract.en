MARIA MARELLO (born 11 March 1961) is a retired Italian discus thrower. She is the coach of the Italian female discus thrower Daisy Osakue,[1] and javelin thrower Zahra Bani.


Biography

She won the gold medal at the 1987 Mediterranean Games[2] and finished fifth at the 1991 Mediterranean Games. She also competed at the 1987 World Championships without reaching the final.[3]

Marello became Italian champion twice, in 1987 and 1988.[4] Her personal best time was 57.54 metres, achieved in June 1986 in Verona.[5]
