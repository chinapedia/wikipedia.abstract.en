DEMIS COSMIN GRIGORAȘ (born 30 June 1993)[1] is a Romanian handballer who plays for Tatabánya KC and the Romania national team.

In December 2018, it was announced Chambéry Savoie Handball signed Grigoraș for summer 2019.[2]
