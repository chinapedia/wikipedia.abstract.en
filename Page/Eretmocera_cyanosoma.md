_ERETMOCERA CYANOSOMA_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Edward Meyrick in 1910. It is found on Sumba[1] and Java.

The wingspan is about 12Â mm. The forewings are indigo-blackish with a pale yellow spot in the disc at one-third, one on the dorsum towards the tornus, and one on the costa at three-fourths. The hindwings are dark purple-fuscous.[2]

The larvae have been recorded feeding on _Amarantus_ species.[3]
