JAMES GLOVER is a rugby league footballer who plays as a for the Sheffield Eagles in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Glover has previously played for the Dewsbury Rams[2] and spent time on loan at the Hemel Stags in Kingstone Press League 1.[3]

In October 2018 Glover joined the Sheffield Eagles on a two-year deal.[4]
