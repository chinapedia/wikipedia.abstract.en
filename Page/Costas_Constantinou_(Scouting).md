COSTAS CONSTANTINOU served as the Chief Commissioner of the Cyprus Scouts Association.

In 1994, Constantinou was awarded the 233rd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.
