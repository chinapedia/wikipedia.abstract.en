The TRUD STADIUM is a multi-purpose stadium in Podolsk, Russia. It is currently used mostly for football matches and was the home ground of FC Vityaz Podolsk from 2009 to present time, FC Avangard Podolsk in 2009 PFC CSKA Moscow in 2014 due to disqualificaton of Arena Khimki for single match of the Russian Cup. and women's football club Podolchanka. The stadium holds 11,962.
