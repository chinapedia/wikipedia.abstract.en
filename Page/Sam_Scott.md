SAMUEL JOHN WILLIAM SCOTT (born 5 June 1990) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a second row forward for the York City Knights in Betfred Championship.[1][2]

Scott has previously played for the Batley Bulldogs and Sheffield Eagles, in England,[3] and has played cricket for Shelley in the Drakes league.[4]
