ROSS ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] the island is lying north from Port Blair.


Geography

The island is located in Aerial Bay, near Diglipur, and is part of Aerial Bay Islands.


Administration

Ross Island, along neighbouring Aerial Bay Islands, is part of Diglipur Taluk.[2]
