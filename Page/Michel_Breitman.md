MICHEL BREITMAN (10 August 1926 – 16 May 2009) was a French writer and translator. He won the 1986 edition of the Prix des Deux Magots with is novel _Le Témoin de poussière_.

A translator of Dino Buzzati, Breitman also published numerous novels, short stories and radio plays.

He wrote his first novel, _Vétrino ou le bonhomme de verre_ directly in Italian, and when he returned to France wrote all his other works in his native tongue.

The film _Fortunat_, with Bourvil and Michèle Morgan was an adaptation of his eponymous novel published in 1955.
