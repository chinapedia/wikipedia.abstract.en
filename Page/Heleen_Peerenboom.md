ANNA HÉLÈNE WILHELMINA "HELEEN" PEERENBOOM (born 4 February 1980) is a Dutch female former water polo player. She was a member of the Netherlands women's national water polo team.

She competed with the team at the 2000 Summer Olympics.[1] She was also part of the national team at the 2001 Women's European Water Polo Championship and 2003 World Aquatics Championships.
