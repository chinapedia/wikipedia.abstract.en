'''Steffen Dierolf ''' (born 5 May 1976) is a German male water polo player. He was a member of the Germany men's national water polo team, playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played for SV Cannstatt in Germany.
