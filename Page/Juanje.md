JUAN JESÚS ARGÜEZ RUIZ (born 4 April 1995), commonly known as JUANJE, is a Spanish footballer who plays for Deportivo Fabril as a central midfielder.


Club career

Born in La Línea de la Concepción, Cádiz, Andalusia, Juanje joined Sevilla FC's youth setup in 2007, after starting it out at Atlético Zabal Linense.[1] He made his debut for the former's reserves on 22 February 2014, coming on as a second-half substitute in a 0–1 home loss against Real Balompédica Linense.

Juanje scored his first goal as a senior on 9 March 2014, netting the winner in a 2–1 home success over Granada CF B.[2] On 4 May, he scored a brace in a 3–1 win at Écija Balompié.[3]

On 6 May 2016, Juanje renewed his contract until 2018.[4] He finished the campaign with 34 appearances and three goals, as his side achieved promotion to Segunda División.

Juanje made his professional debut on 21 September 2016, coming on as a late substitute for Bernardo in a 0–1 away loss against Levante UD.[5] He featured rarely in the following years, suffering relegation in 2018.

On 31 August 2018, Juanje signed a one-year contract with another reserve team, Deportivo Fabril in the third division.[6]
