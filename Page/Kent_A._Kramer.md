KENT A. KRAMER (born November 21, 1961) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Kramer was born in Newton, Iowa. He attended the University of Iowa and is a financial planner. A Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 2003 to 2005 (69th district).[1][2]
