_TU JE SEI_ is a 2016 Indian Odia-language drama film directed by Ashok Pati.[1] It stars Riya Dey, Shital Patra and Babushan in lead roles.[2] The movie is a remake of 2013 Tamil movie _Raja Rani_.


Plot

The genre of the movie is romantic drama.[3]
