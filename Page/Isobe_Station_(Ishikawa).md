is a railway station on the Hokuriku Railroad Asanogawa Line in the city of Kanazawa, Ishikawa Prefecture, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Hokuriku Railroad (Hokutetsu).


Lines

Isobe Station is served by the 6.7 km Hokuriku Railroad Asanogawa Line between and , and is located 2.2 kilometers from Kanazawa Station.


Station layout

The station consists of one side platform serving a single bi-directional track. The station is unattended.
