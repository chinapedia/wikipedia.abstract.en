is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team.


Club career

Isaka was born in Saitama Prefecture on January 25, 1976. She joined L.League club Urawa Reds in 1994. She was selected Best Young Player awards in 1994 season. In 1995, she moved to Prima Ham FC Kunoichi (later _Iga FC Kunoichi_). In 1999 season, she scored 21 goals and became top scorer and she was elected MVP awards. She was also elected Best Eleven 3 times (1999, 2000 and 2003).


National team career

On June 15, 1997, Isaka debuted for Japan national team against China. She played at 1998, 2002 Asian Games, 1999 and 2001 AFC Championship. She was also a member of Japan for 1999 World Cup. She played 46 games and scored 15 goals for Japan until 2002.[1]
