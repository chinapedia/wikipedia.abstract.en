_TRUMPED UP CARDS_ is a party game developed by Reid Hoffman to poke fun at then presidential candidate Donald Trump. It was modeled after the popular _Cards Against Humanity_ card game and sold online. The game was featured on an episode of _The Daily Show with Trevor Noah_ in which Hoffman was a guest.[1] The game has also been covered by a large number of mainstream media outlets, including The New York Times[2] and USA Today.[3] The game is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License.
