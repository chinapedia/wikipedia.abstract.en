'''Omar El Sammany ''' (عمر السماني, born 22 August 1978) is an Egyptian male water polo player. He was a member of the Egypt men's national water polo team, playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played for Al Ahly in Egypt.
