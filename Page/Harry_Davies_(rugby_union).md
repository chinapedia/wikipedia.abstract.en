HARRY DAVIES is a Welsh professional Rugby Union player currently playing at Wing for Bath Rugby.


Early life

Davies started playing Rugby at Llantrisant RFC before becoming a part of the Cardiff Blues academy at the age of 17.[1]


Club Rugby

Whilst part of the Blues academy Davies played on loan for Cardiff RFC.[2] Davies also trained with the Welsh Sevens squad and represented the Blues in the World Club Sevens.[3]

Davies made his first team debut for the Blues in November 2014 scoring a try in the LV Cup match against Newcastle Falcons.[4]

In May 2016 it was announced that Davies had signed with Aviva Premiership side Bath for the 2016/17 season.[5]

Harry joined English championship side Bedford blues at the start of the 2018/19 season.
