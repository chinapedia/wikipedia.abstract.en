RENÉ BASSET (24 July 1855 – 4 January 1924) was a French orientalist, specialist of Arabic and Berber languages.


Biography

René Basset was the first director of the "École des lettres d'Alger"[1] created in 1879[2] during the French colonisation of Algeria.

A member of the société Asiatique of Paris as well as those of Leipzig and Florence, he collaborated with the _Journal Asiatique_ and studied Chinese Islam.

André Basset and Henri Basset were his sons.
