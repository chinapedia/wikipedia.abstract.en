_RHODODENDRON SIKAYOTAIZANENSE_ (志佳阳杜鹃) is a rhododendron species native to Taiwan. It is a shrub, with small, leathery leaves that are oblong to ovate-oblong, 0.4–1.4 × 0.2–0.6 cm in size. Flowers are red.


Synonyms

-   None recorded.
