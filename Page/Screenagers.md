_SCREENAGERS_ is a documentary created and directed by Delaney Ruston, a physician and film director, to describe growing up in a tech saturated world where it is her opinion that balance needs to be drawn between screen time and screen free time.[1][2][3] In the documentary, filmmaker Delaney Ruston must decide if she should give an iPhone to her teenage daughter.
