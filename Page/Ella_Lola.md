ELLA LOLA (born 2 September 1883)[1] was a vaudeville dancer at the end of the 19th century in America.

She was born in Boston and, besides her stage appearances, was filmed in short Kinetoscope productions such as _Ella Lola, a la Trilby_ (1898) which was an example of Trilbyana.[2][3][4]
