TAHA CENGIZ DEMIRTAŞ (born 15 May 1994) is a Turkish professional footballer who currently plays as a goalkeeper for Gençlerbirliği in the Süper Lig.


Professional career

Demirtaş made his debut for Gençlerbirliği in a 1-0 Süper Lig win over Bursaspor on 18 May 2018.[1]


International career

Demirtaş has represented the Turkish Football Federation at the U15 and U16 levels.
