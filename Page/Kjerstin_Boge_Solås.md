KJERSTIN BOGE SOLÅS (born 31 December 1997) is a Norwegian handball player for Siófok KC and the Norway women's national handball team.[1]

She also represented Norway in the 2016 Women's Junior World Handball Championship, placing 5th[2] and at the 2014 Women's Youth World Handball Championship, placing 13th.[3]
