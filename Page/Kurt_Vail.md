KURT VAIL is an American politician and member of the Republican party. He is a current member of the Connecticut House of Representatives representing Connecticut's 52nd assembly district in the General Assembly. The district is made up of all of Stafford and Somers. Vail was first elected in 2014, and re-elected in 2016.

Vail serves on the Insurance and Real Estate Committee. He also sits on the Labor and Public Employees and Public Safety and Security Committees.

Vail also served in the community as a member of the Stafford Board of Education and the Downtown Revitalization Committee. He previously was a candidate for Selectman in Stafford in 2001.
