UNION AND PROGRESS PARTY[1] or UNITY AND PROGRESS PARTY[2] () was a political party in constitutional period Persia.[3]

In the 1909 Persian legislative election, they won 4 seats and allied with the Moderate Socialists Party against Democrat Party.[4]

The party's line had little resemblance to that of the Young Turks and contained some socialist demands. Most of the party's supporters were active in the countrysides.[5]
