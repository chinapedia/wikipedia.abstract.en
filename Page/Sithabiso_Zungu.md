SITHABISO ZUNGU (born 31 August 1997) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for KwaZulu-Natal in the 2016–17 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 28 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for KwaZulu-Natal in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 31 October 2016.[3]

In September 2018, he was named in KwaZulu-Natal Inland's squad for the 2018 Africa T20 Cup.[4] He made his Twenty20 debut for KwaZulu-Natal Inland in the 2018 Africa T20 Cup on 14 September 2018.[5]
