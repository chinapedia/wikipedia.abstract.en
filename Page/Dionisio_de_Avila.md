Biography

Dionisio de Avilawas ordained a priest in the Order of the Blessed Virgin Mary of Mercy.[4][5] On 29 Oct 1539, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul III as Bishop of Santorini.[6][7] He served as Bishop of Santorini until his death in 1552.[8]
