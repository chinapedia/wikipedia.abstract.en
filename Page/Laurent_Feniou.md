LAURENT EUGENE FENIOU (born. June 1971) is the current Managing Director of Cartier.[1] He succeeded Arnaud Bamberger in 2015.


Career

In 2013, Feniou moved from Rothschild in where he had been managing director since May 2001.[2] Prior to Rothschild, Feniou was a Navy Officer working for the French embassy in Madrid, Spain before becoming an Associate of Credit Suisse First Bourbon.[3] Feniou is tri-lingual; he speaks English, French and Spanish.[4] He is married with two sons.[5]
