The JINDE BRIDGE () is a bridge connecting Donggang Township and Xinyuan Township in Pingtung County, Taiwan.


History

The bridge was opened for traffic in October 2002.[1] On 27 April 2014, the Pingtung County Government organized the bridge lighting ceremony for the bridge.[2]


Technical specification

The bridge crosses the Donggang River for a length of 440 meters and a width of 21.3 meters. Its main span spans over 155 meters and it has a 30-meter high arch.[3]
