UNIKRN INC. (styled as "UNIKRN") is an esports betting and news media company, based in Las Vegas, Nevada with offices in Seattle USA, Sydney Australia and Berlin, Germany.[1]


Overview

Founded in November 2014, Unikrn primarily offers real money [2] and token-based wagering on competitive video game tournaments, otherwise known as esports.[3] Unikrn creates betting markets on esports (organized video game competitions) matches akin to traditional betting.

Unikrn is licensed to provide real-money betting on esports in the UK[4] and Australia.[5] As of October 2018, the company has received its gambling license from the Isle of Man, allowing them to operate online esports gambling within countries with allowance for legalized gambling, which will include the United States, South Korea, and parts of Europe, Asia, and Latin America.[6]


Origins and Development

The company was founded by Rahul Sood and Karl Flores in November 2014, after Sood finished his role as General Manager at Microsoft Ventures.[7]

Unikrn raised $10 million[8][9] in funding from several high-profile investors, including Mark Cuban, Ashton Kutcher,[10] Binary Capital[11] and Tabcorp,[12] its main partner for betting operations,[13] as well as Elisabeth Murdoch and Shari Redstone. It also has partnerships with HP and Razer.[14]

In 2016, Unikrn became an investor in BIG (Berlin International Gaming),[15] a professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive team. It has also fielded an all-female CS:GO team, Team Unikrn.[16] Unikrn also has an investment in MAINGEAR,[17] a high-end PC manufacturer.

In the second quarter of 2017, Unikrn acquired LEET,[18] an esports startup based in Las Vegas, Nevada, who specialize in integrating esports into land-based casinos. Unikrn also owns an Indian-based esports company, DotaProHub.[19]

In June 2017, the company created UnikoinGold is an ERC-20 cryptocurrency token created by Unikrn, running on the Ethereum blockchain. It was released worldwide on 6 November 2017, following a month-long token sale that started on 22 September 2017 and ended on 22 October 2017. The token sale attracted investment from ConsenSys Ventures,[20] Mark Cuban,[21] and Ethereum co-founder Anthony Diiorio.[22]

Unikrn endorse the launch of The Esports Integrity Coalition (ESIC), along with Betway, ESL and Dreamhack, which aims to curb corruption and enforce ethical standards in the esports industry.[23]

In December 2017, Unikrn announced a partnership with MGM Resorts International to host events in Las Vegas.[24]
