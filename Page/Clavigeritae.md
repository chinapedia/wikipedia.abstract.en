The CLAVIGERITAE form a supertribe of beetles belonging to the family Staphylinidae.[1]


Systematics

The Clavigeritae comprise the following tribes:

-   Clavigerini
-   Colilodionini
-   Tiracerini
-   Protoclavigerini
