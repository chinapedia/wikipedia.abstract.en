_LEPTADRILLIA VIOLACEA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 9 mm and 11.5 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs in the Caribbean Sea off Guadeloupe.
