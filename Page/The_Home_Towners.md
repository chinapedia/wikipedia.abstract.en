_THE HOME TOWNERS_ is a 1928 American comedy film directed by Bryan Foy and starring Richard Bennett, Doris Kenyon, and Robert McWade.[1] The film had part-talking sequences in addition to the mostly silent with music and sound effects.


Cast

-   Richard Bennett as Vic Arnold
-   Doris Kenyon as Beth Calhoun
-   Robert McWade as P. H. Bancroft
-   Robert Edeson as Mr. Calhoun
-   Gladys Brockwell as Lottie Bancroft
-   John Miljan as Joe Roberts
-   Vera Lewis as Mrs. Calhoun
-   Stanley Taylor as Wally Calhoun
-   James T. Mack as Casey
-   Patricia Caron as Maid


Preservation status

_The Home Towners_ is now lost.[2][3]
