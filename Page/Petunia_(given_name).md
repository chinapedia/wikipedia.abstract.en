PETUNIA is an Italian, English, Irish, Scottish, French, Dutch, and Scandinavian feminine given name. It originated in the English language as after a flower.[1]


Usage

This name is given to fictional characters such as Petunia from Happy Tree Friends, Petunia Rhubarb from Veggie Tales, Petunia from The Angry Birds Movie, and even Petunia Dursley from Harry Potter. It is also a common pet name.[2]
