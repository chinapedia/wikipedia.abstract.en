THE GREEN HORNET is a 1994 Hong Kong action film directed by Lam Ching Ying and starring Chin Ka-lok. Based on the American Green Hornet series the film focuses on the character of Kato.[1]


Plot

The film follows the story of a Chinese reporter (Esther Kwan) as she seek to find out the identity of a masked crime-fighter.[2]


Cast

-   Kar Lok Chin as Dong The Green Hornet (Dressed as Kato)
-   Esther Kwan as Tom
-   Yu Rongguang as the police captain
