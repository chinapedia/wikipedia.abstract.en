ALVARADO is an unincorporated community in Richland Township, Steuben County, in the U.S. state of Indiana.[1]


History

A post office was established at Alvarado in 1855, and remained in operation until 1904.[2] An old variant name of the community was called Richland Center.[3]

In 1849, the Alvarado United Methodist Church was built, and is still in service today.[4]
