[1] is a Japanese actor, singer, and model. He is represented with Stardust Promotion's Section 3.[2] He is the leader of Stardust's music collective Ebidan unit Dish.[3] As a member of Dish he is nicknamed as TAKUMI, stylized as TAKUMI.


Biography

Kitamura was scouted by Stardust Promotion when he was in third grade of elementary school.[4]

His first singing debut was "Risu ni Koi Shita Shōnen" in the NHK series _Minna no Uta_ in February and March 2008. Later in June Kitamura's first film appearance was _Dive!!_.[5] He won the 2009 Reader Model Award of the magazine _Shōgaku Rokunensei_ in 2009.[6]

In Summer 2010 Kitamura started joining the actor unit Ebidan.[7] He later joined Ebidan's dance rock band Dish in December 2011.[8] Kitamura is the leader of the band[9] as its lead vocalist and guitarist.[10]
