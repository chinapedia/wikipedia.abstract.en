SHOEBURYNESS CARRIAGE SERVICING DEPOT is an Electric Traction Depot located in Shoeburyness, Essex, England. The depot is situated on the London, Tilbury and Southend Railway and is near Shoeburyness station. To the right of the depot is MoD Shoeburyness.[1]


Allocation

As of 2016, the depot's allocation consists of c2c Class 357 EMUs.[2]
