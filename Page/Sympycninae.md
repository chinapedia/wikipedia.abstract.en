SYMPYCNINAE is a subfamily of flies in the family Dolichopodidae.[1]


Genera

-   _Anepsiomyia_ Bezzi, 1902[2] (Diaphorinae or Sympycninae)
-   _Brevimyia_ Miller, 1945[3][4]
-   _Calyxochaetus_ Bigot, 1888[5]
-   _Campsicnemus_ Haliday in Walker, 1851[6]
-   _Ceratopos_ Vaillant, 1952
-   _Chaetogonopteron_ De Meijere, 1913
-   _Colobocerus_ Parent, 1933[7]
-   _Emperoptera_ Grimshaw, 1902 (synonym of _Campsicnemus_?)
-   _Erebomyia_ Runyon & Hurley, 2004[8]
-   _Filatopus_ Robinson, 1970
-   _Hercostomoides_ Meuffels & Grootaert, 1997
-   _Humongochela_ Evenhuis, 2004[9]
-   _Hyptiocheta_ Becker, 1922[10]
-   _Ischiochaetus_ Bickel & Dyte, 1989[11]
-   _Lamprochromus_ Mik, 1878[12]
-   _Liparomyia_ White, 1916[13]
-   _Micropygus_ Bickel & Dyte, 1989[14]
-   _Neoparentia_ Robinson, 1967[15]
-   _Nothorhaphium_ Bickel, 1999
-   _Nurteria_ Dyte & Smith, 1980[16]
-   _Olegonegrobovia_ Grichanov, 1995 (possible synonym of _Teuchophorus_?)
-   _Parasyntormon_ Wheeler, 1899[17]
-   _Paresus_ Wei, 2006
-   _Phrudoneura_ Meuffels & Grootaert, 1987 (_incertae sedis_)
-   _Pinacocerus_ Van Duzee, 1930[18]
-   _Scelloides_ Bickel & Dyte, 1989[19]
-   _Scotiomyia_ Meuffels & Grootaert, 1997
-   _Suschania_ Negrobov, 2003
-   _Sympycnus_ Loew, 1857[20]
-   _Syntormon_ Loew, 1857[21]
-   _Telmaturgus_ Mik, 1874
-   _Tetrachaetus_ Bickel & Dyte, 1989[22]
-   _Teuchophorus_ Loew, 1857[23]
-   _Yumbera_ Bickel, 1992[24]

The following genera are part of the _Eurynogaster_ complex, a monophyletic clade of flies endemic to Hawaii:[25]

-   _Adachia_ Evenhuis, 2005[26]
-   _Arciellia_ Evenhuis, 2005[27]
-   _Elmoia_ Evenhuis, 2005[28]
-   _Eurynogaster_ Van Duzee, 1933[29]
-   _Major_ Evenhuis, 2005[30]
-   _Sigmatineurum_ Parent, 1938[31]
-   _Sweziella_ Van Duzee, 1933
-   _Uropachys_ Parent, 1935
