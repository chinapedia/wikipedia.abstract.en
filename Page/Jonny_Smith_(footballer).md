JONATHAN GARY SMITH (born 28 July 1997) is an English professional footballer who plays as a winger for Bristol City.


Career

Wrexham

Born in Liverpool, England, Smith joined the Wrexham academy at the age of 10.[1] On 16 January 2016, Smith made his Wrexham debut in their FA Trophy second round defeat against Torquay United.[2]

Bristol City

In May 2016, Smith signed for Championship side Bristol City for an "undisclosed" compensation fee, agreeing terms on a two-year contract.[3][4]

On 19 August 2016, Smith joined League Two side Cheltenham Town on loan until January 2017.[5] Smith made his English Football League debut the following day in a 1–0 defeat against Doncaster Rovers.[6]

He moved on loan to Tranmere Rovers in July 2018, and signed a new 1yr contract at Bristol City which now takes him to June 2020.[7]
