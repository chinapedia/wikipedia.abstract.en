Stapleton Houses, Staten Island

The STAPLETON HOUSES are a housing project located in Staten Island, New York. The eight-story building is one of the largest housing developments in Staten Island.[1] The building was completed in 1960.

Notable residents have included Ghostface Killah and Tristan Wilds.[2]
