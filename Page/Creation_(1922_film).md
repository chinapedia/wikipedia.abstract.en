_CREATION_ is a 1922 British silent drama film directed by Humberston Wright and starring Dorothy Fane, Frank Dane and Simeon Stuart.[1]


Cast

-   Dorothy Fane as Zena Hammond
-   Frank Dane as Faux Evermore
-   Simeon Stuart as Dr. Ganally
-   William Freshman
-   Kate Gurney
-   Raleigh King
-   Thelma Murray
-   Beryl Norton
