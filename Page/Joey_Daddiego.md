JOEY DADDIEGO is an American former professional wrestler best known for his time in Ring of Honor.[1]

On April 8, 2014, Daddiego made his debut at Ring Of Honor under the name Jay Diesel at the event ROH Future Of Honor #1, in a losing effort to Jay Lethal in a Proving Ground match.[2] On November 22, 2014 Diesel grouped the House of Truth by teaming with Jay Lethal as they were defeated by The Briscoes in the Semi Finals of Tag Wars.[3] On January 3, 2015 Diesel was defeated by Will Ferrara in the First Round of the Top Prospect Tournament.[4] On February 11, 2017, Joey Daddiego turned face as he teamed with Will Ferrara and Cheeseburger as they defeated Brian Johnson, Matt Sells & Plunkett The Ogre.
