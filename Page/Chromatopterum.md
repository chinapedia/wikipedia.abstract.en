_CHROMATOPTERUM_ is a genus of flies in the family Chloropidae (insects commonly known as fruit flies).


Species

-   _Chromatopterum delicatum_ (Becker, 1910)
-   _Chromatopterum dimidiatum_ (Deeming, 1981)
-   _Chromatopterum ghanaensis_ (Deeming, 1981)
-   _Chromatopterum linguatum_ (Seguy, 1957)
-   _Chromatopterum mirabilis_ (Deeming, 1981)
-   _Chromatopterum nusantarum_ (Kanmiya & Yukawa, 1985)
-   _Chromatopterum pubescens_ (Becker, 1911)
-   _Chromatopterum suffusum_ (Sabrosky, 1951)
