World Series

The following world series listing is accurate through the end of the 2018 World Series. The New York Yankees have the most world series wins (134) and the most world series games played (225). The Toronto Blue Jays have the highest world series winning percentage (.667). Neither the Seattle Mariners nor Washington Nationals have played in a World Series. Excluding those two teams, the Colorado Rockies have the lowest world series winning percentage (.000).
