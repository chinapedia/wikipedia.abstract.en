is the second studio album of Japanese singer Shiori Niiyama. [1] It was released on 17 June 2015 under Being label. Album includes previous 2 released singles. The album reached #32 rank first week. Album charted for 3 weeks.[2]


Track listing

All songs were written by Shiori Niiyama[3]


In media

-   KIRA KIRA was used as insert song for movie _Yorisou_
-   ZETTAI was used as ending theme for TBS Television program "King's Brunch" (Oujisama no Brunch)
-   WAKATTERU YO was used as theme song for short movie _Yorisou_
-   ARIGATOU was used as theme song for Chiba TV program "Music Launcher"
