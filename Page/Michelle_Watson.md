'''Michelle Watson ''' (born 17 June 1976) is an Australian former footballer who played as a forward for the Australia women's national soccer team. She was part of the team at the 1994 OFC Women's Championship and 1995 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level, she played for Marconi Stallions in Australia.[1]
