_VARANASI_ is an upcoming Indian thriller Bollywood film, written and directed by Aryaman Keshu.[1]

Raima Sen will be seen playing some role in the film.[2]


Plot

The film is partially inspired from the terror attacks that shook the holy city of Varanasi in 2006.[3]


Cast

-   Raima Sen
-   Om Puri
-   Ravi Kishan
-   Rahul Dev
-   Ankit Vyas


Production

Development

The official announcement of the film was announced in the second half of September 2016. The title of the film was said to be _Varanasi_.[4]

Casting

The makers of the film have decided to cast Raima Sen, along with Om Puri, Ravi Kishan and Rahul Dev in the film.[5]

Filming

The principal photography of the film will commence sometime in October 2016.[6]
