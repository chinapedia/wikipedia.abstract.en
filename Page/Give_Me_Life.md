_GIVE ME LIFE_ (German: _SCHENK MIR DAS LEBEN_) is a 1928 German silent drama film directed by Klaus Fery and starring Grete Reinwald, Henry Stuart and Elizza La Porta.[1]

The film's sets were designed by the art director Willi Herrmann.
