NIEVE BERÓNICA VIBIECA AQUINO (born 6 January 1990) is a Dominican Republic female badminton player.[1][2]


Career

In 2013, she won the Guatemala International tournament in women's singles events, and became the runner-up in women's and mixed doubles.[3] She also won the Carebaco International tournament in the women's doubles event partnered with Daigenis Saturria.[4]
