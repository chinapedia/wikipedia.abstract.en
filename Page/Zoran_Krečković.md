ZORAN KREČKOVIĆ (, born April 17, 1959) is a Serbian professional basketball coach and former player. He is a current head coach of Al Jahraa in the Qatari League.[1]


Coaching career

Krečković began his coaching career in 1997 with IMT Beopetrol in Belgrade. In February 2000 he got fired.[2] He had a stint at Crvena zvezda in 2001-02 before moving on to Yamolgaz '92 Yambol in the Bulgarian League from 2002-03.[3][4]

Krečković coached the Kuwait national team from 2004–06 and in 2008-09, including appearances in the 2005 FIBA Asia Championship in Doha and the 2009 Asia Championship in Tianjin, China.[5]

He guided Al Sadd in the Qatari League 2011-12 season and got second position in the league and Emir Cup title in that season.[6] Krečković had a stint at Osaka Evessa of Japan League in 2012.[7] After that he coached Al Jahraa in Kuwaiti League for two seasons.[8]

In 2014, Krečković became a head coach of Al Sadd for second time.[9] In July 2018, he became a head coach of Al Jahraa for second time.[10]
