DOMINICAN COMMERCIAL HIGH SCHOOL is a former Catholic High School located in Jamaica, Queens, New York.[1] An all-girls school,[2]it was located at 161-06 89th Avenue.[3] The school first opened in 1936, and moved to its new building on 161st and 89th Avenue in 1938 (leaving the previous building to be converted into a convent).[4][5] The school closed in 1998.[6][7] The building later became Wellington Hall, an education facility for Association for the Advancement of the Blind and Retarded.[8]

As of 1941, Dominican Commercial was one of the two largest parochial schools in Jamaica, along with the Mary Louis Academy.[9] Dominican Commercial focused on preparing students for careers directly out of high school.[10] The school was initially not focused on college preparation.[11]
