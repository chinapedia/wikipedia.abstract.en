TRANSPORTATION IN NAURU includes pedestrian, bicycle, automobile, train, and airplane.


Air

Nauru has one airport, Nauru International Airport. Nauru Airlines, which flies to Brisbane, Australia; Majuro, Marshall Islands; Nadi, Fiji; and Tarawa, Kiribati, is the only airline to fly to the airport.[1] There are five airplanes in service.[2]
