COOPER HILL is an unincorporated community in Osage County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Cooper's Hill was established in 1860, the name was changed to Cooper Hill in 1893, and the post office closed in 1957.[2] The community has the name of J. M. Cooper, an early settler.[3]
