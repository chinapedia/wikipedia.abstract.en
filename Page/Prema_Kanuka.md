_PREMA KANUKA_ (English: LOVE GIFT) is a 1981 Telugu drama film, produced by Venkat Akkineni, Nagarjuna Akkineni under Annapurna Studios banner[1] and directed by K. Raghavendra Rao.[2] It stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Sridevi in the lead roles and music composed by Chakravarthy.[3]


Plot

The film begins on Raja Shekaram (Akkineni Nageswara Rao) an artist, reaching an estate for his portrayal and settles in a dock bungalow which is known as devil's house. One night, he observes something suspicious but finds it as a beautiful village girl Gowri (Madhu Malini) who stays there on nights, due to fear of her drunkard father Veeraiah (Prabhakar Reddy). Raja praises her beauty and paints her picture when she starts loving him. Being aware of it, Sandhya (Sridevi), daughter of Janthuvula Jaganatham (Rao Gopal Rao), a devious & materialistic person irks and destroys the paintings as she feels herself a most beautiful girl. Angered Raja moves to shut her down when astonishingly, he gets a remembrance of reincarnation and also makes Sandhya believe that they are lovers in a previous life. On the other side, Mohan (Mohan Babu) a vicious person, is the nephew of Jaganatham, performs anti-social & illegal activities in the area, has an intention to marry Sandhya, keeps an evil eye on Gowri but Raja always puts a check on him. Meanwhile, knowing about Raja & Sandhya love affair Jaganatham gives approval assuming Raja as a wealthy person. Parallelly, Raja convinces Gowri, performs her marriage with a guy Kumar (Chalapathi Rao), unfortunately, he is henchmen of Mohan who tries to surrender when Raja rescues and gives her shelter.

Exploiting it, Mohan denounces Raja when Jaganatham eagerly queries him then he replies that he is not going to marry Sandhya. All this was his play to take revenge against him as he is the son of the bungalow's previous owner Bhushaiah (Satyanarayana). Immediately, frightened Jaganatham runs away, informs Sandhya that Raja is a defrauder, at that moment, Sandhya reveals she is pregnant. Thereupon, Jaganatham suggests her to get aborted though she refuses in the beginning, after spotting Gowri with Raja, she decides to do so. Be cognizant of it, Gowri rushes to Raja, on the way, Mohan molests her but somehow she reaches Raja reveals regarding Mohan's barbarous deed even Sandhya too and dies. Now Raja moves to the hospital and enlightens Sandhya. Once upon a time, Jaganatham deceived his innocent sister Seeta (Pushpalatha) which has ruined their family and also shows his sister who is suffering in a mental hospital from years together. Listening to it, Sandhya understands Raja's virtue, right now, both of them make Jaganatham repent. At the point in time, surprisingly, Raja turns as CBI Officer who is appointed to dig out Mohan's illegal activities, so, Mohan kidnaps Seeta as well as Jaganatham. Here spotting Jaganatham, Seeta regains her memory. At last, Raja sees the end of Mohan and Jaganatham also pleads pardon for his deeds. Finally, the movie ends on a happy note with the marriage of Raja & Sandhya.
