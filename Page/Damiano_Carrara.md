DAMIANO CARRARA (born 22 September 1985)[1][2] is an Italian-born American chef, restaurateur and cookbook author who resides in Moorpark, California.[3]


Early life

Carrara was born in Lucca, Tuscany.[4] He served as a bartender at age 19 in his hometown before he continued bartending across Dublin, Ireland.[5] By 2012, he and his brother Massimiliano had relocated to Moorpark, California, where they opened their bakery Carrara Pastries.[6] In 2013, they opened a second location in Agoura Hills, California.[7]


Career

Carrara has made television appearances on several Food Network series. He first came to prominence as a contestant in the premier season of _Spring Baking Championship_ in 2015, where he finished in second place.[8][9][10] Later that year, he went on to win the "Chocotage XXL" episode of _Cutthroat Kitchen_.[11]

Carrara also infrequently serves as a judge on _Bakers vs. Fakers_ and _Winner Cake All_.[12]

_Food Network Star_ and _Halloween Baking Championship_

Damiano_Carrara_cooking_demo_01.jpg In May 2016,[13][14] Carrara was announced as a contestant for the twelfth season of _Food Network Star_. He survived until midway through the season finale, finishing in third place.[15] In October 2016, he served as one of the judges in the second season of _Halloween Baking Championship_.[16]
