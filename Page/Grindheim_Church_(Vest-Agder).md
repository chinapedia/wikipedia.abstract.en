GRINDHEIM CHURCH () is a parish church in Audnedal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Grindheim. The church is part of the Grindheim parish in the Mandal deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden, cruciform church was built in 1783. The church seats about 250 people.[1][2]
