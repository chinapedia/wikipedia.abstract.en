AUSTIN TAM-GEORGE is an academic who served as the Rivers State Commissioner of Information and Communications from 21 December 2015 to 23 June 2017 when he voluntarily resigned from his post in Governor Wike's Cabinet. Before his appointment, Tam-George was the Executive Director of the Institute of Communication and Corporate Studies.[1]


Education

Tam-George received his PhD from the University of the Witwatersrand, South Africa and attended the IESE Business School in Barcelona, where he studied communication and institutional leadership.[2]


Career

As an academic, he taught at Pan-African University in Nigeria and at University of Cape Town where he was an Andrew W. Mellon Postdoctoral Research Fellow.[3]
