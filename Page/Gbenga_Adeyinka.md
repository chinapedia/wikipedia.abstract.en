GBENGA ADEYINKA is a multi-award-winning Nigerian actor, comedian, radio and TV presenter, writer, and MC. He hails from Abeokuta in Ogun State and he usually refers to himself as CFR (Comedian of the Federal Republic) and now GCON (Grand Comedian of Nigeria).[1]


Education

Gbenga Adeyinka attended University of Lagos, where he studied English.[2]


Career

Gbenga worked as a corporate affairs manager in the engineering firm. He became popular with his _shine shine bobo promo_ for 'Star Game Show', he has also anchored Television programmes for AIT, Galaxy TV, MBI, and Africa Magic on DSTV. He speaks various Nigerian languages.[3]


Personal life

Gbenga Adeyinka is married to Mrs Abiola Adeyinka, and they have three children.[4]


Professional life

Gbenga has anchored many wedding programmes and events in and outside Nigeria. He is also the first to publish comedy magazine in Nigeria known as _Laffmattazz_.[5]
