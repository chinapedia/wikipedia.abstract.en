PETRONAS OFFICE TOWER is the office building for Petronas in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia.[1]

Construction of a second 11-storey tower which will be located beside the building was started in 2016 with a cost of RM136.9 million and is set to be complete in August 2018.[2][3][4][5]
