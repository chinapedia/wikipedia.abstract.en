GABRIELE REISMÜLLER (30 November 1920 – 24 November 1969) was a German actress.[1] She appeared in twenty-seven films between 1941 and 1969.


Selected filmography

-   _Venus on Trial_ (1941)
-   _The Millionaire_ (1947)
-   _Insolent and in Love_ (1948)
