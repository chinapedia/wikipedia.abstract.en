_HYMENOBACTER PSYCHROPHILUS_ is a Gram-negative, psychrophilic, aerobic, rod-shaped and non-motile bacterium from the genus of Hymenobacter which has been isolated from soil from an industrial site in Bozen in Italy.[1][2][3][4]
