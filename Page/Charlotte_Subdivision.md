The CHARLOTTE SUBDIVISION is a railroad line owned by CSX Transportation in the U.S. State of North Carolina. The line runs from Monroe, North Carolina, milepost SF307.7, to Bostic, North Carolina, milepost 404.9, for a total of 97.2 miles. At its south end the line continues north from the Monroe Subdivision and at its north end it continues north as the Blue Ridge Subdivision.[1][2] Csx_stanley_nc.jpg
