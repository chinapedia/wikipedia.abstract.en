LEIGH ALLEN REKOW (born June 26, 1934) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Rekow was born in the Luana/Postville, Iowa area and is a farmer. As a Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 2001 to 2003 (32nd district).[1]
