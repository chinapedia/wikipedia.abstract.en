MOUNT ZION is an unincorporated community in Henry County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Mount Zion was established in 1873, and remained in operation until 1910.[2] The community took its name from a nearby Methodist Episcopal church of the same name.[3]
