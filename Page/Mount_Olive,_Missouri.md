MOUNT OLIVE is an unincorporated community in Johnson County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A variant name was "Priest".[2] Priest was the name of F.T. Priest, an early settler.[3] A post office called Priest was established in 1899, and remained in operation until 1902.[4]
