SALMAN AL-AZAMI is a British-Bangladeshi author and academic at Liverpool Hope University, where he is a seniorlecturer in English language. He obtained his BA, MA, and PhD at Aligarh Muslim University in India.[1]


Publications

Dr Al-Azami is the author a number of works including _Religion in the Media: A Linguistic Analysis_, published by Palgrave Macmillan.[2]

He frequently publishes articles on matters pertaining to religion, media, and his native Bangladesh, in a variety of online media outlets including _The Huffington Post_, _OpenDemocracy_ and _Anadolu Agency_.[3][4][5] He has also published several academic articles.[6]
