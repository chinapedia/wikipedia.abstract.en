_THE SWINGIN' NUTCRACKER_ is a 1960 RCA Victor album by American jazz trumpeter and arranger Shorty Rogers performing compositions adapted from _The Nutcracker_ by Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky.[1][2][3]


Reception

Allmusic awarded the album 3 stars.[4]
