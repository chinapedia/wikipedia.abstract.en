J HECTOR MCKENZIE was a Scottish amateur football centre half who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1] He captained the club and was appointed secretary in 1919.[2]


Personal life

McKenzie served as a sergeant in the Scottish Horse and then as a private in the Coldstream Guards during the First World War.[3]
