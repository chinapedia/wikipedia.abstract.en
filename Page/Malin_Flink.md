'''Malin Flink ''' (born 4 September 1974) is a Swedish footballer who played as a midfielder for the Sweden women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1995 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she played for Gideonsbergs IF in Sweden.[1]
