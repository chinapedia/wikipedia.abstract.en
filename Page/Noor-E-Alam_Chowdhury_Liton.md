NOOR-E-ALAM CHOWDHURY LITON () is a Bangladesh Awami League politician and Member of Parliament from Madaripur-1 and whip of Jatiya Sangsad.


Early life

Chowdhury was born on 1 June 1964. He completed an HSC or High School graduation degree.[1] He is the nephew of father of nation Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.[2] His father, Ilias Ahmed Chowdhury, is a paternal cousin of Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina.[3]


Career

Chowdhury was elected to Parliament in 2014 from Madaripur-1 as a Bangladesh Awami League Candidate.[4] He is the Whip of the Parliament. He is also a member of the parliamentary standing committee on Ministry of Housing and Public Works.[5] He was the Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Ministry of Shipping.[6]
