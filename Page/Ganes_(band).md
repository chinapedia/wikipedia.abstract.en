GANES is a female pop trio from La Val, Italy founded in 2009 which sings in the Ladin language.[1] The band consists of sisters Elisabeth Schuen, Marlene Schuen and their cousin Maria Moling.

On 14 August 2015, they performed together with Corin Curschellas, as well as Patricia Draeger and Barbara Gisler.


Discography
