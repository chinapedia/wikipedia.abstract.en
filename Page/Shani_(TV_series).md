_KARMAPHAL DAATA SHANI_ ( Shani, the divine judge of deeds) is an Indian Hindi-language mythological television series, which aired from 7 November 2016 to 9 March 2018 on Colors TV. The series was produced by Swastik Productions of Siddharth Kumar Tewary.[1].


Summary

The story of the series is based on the life of God Shani, who is known for his wrath. The serial also shows Vishnu and Shiva as Shani's mentors. It also shows Shani's tough childhood, getting the title of _karmaphaldaata_, his downfall, his return to Suryalok, marriage, getting his wife's curse and finally his return as _Karmphaldaata_.[2]
