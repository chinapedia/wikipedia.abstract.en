'''Hami Syahin ''' (born 16 December 1998) is a Singaporean footballer who plays for Garena Young Lions as a forward.


Club career

Young Lions

Hami began his club career with S.League club Young Lions in 2015. However, he made zero appearance for the club. In 2016, Hami earned his first cap and played almost all matches throughout the 2016 S.League season. He scored his first goal against Warriors.[1]


International career

Senior

In the 2016 AFF Championship rules, each team must submit the team's initial 40 players as the provisional squad.[2] Hami was included in the 40 players provisional squad for the Singapore squad by head coach V. Sundramoorthy. However, Hami was not in the final 23 man squad to take part in the competition.[3]

He was called up officially in June 2017 for the closed door friendly against Myanmar on 6 June 2017, the 2019 Asian Cup Qualifiers against Chinese Taipei on 10 June 2017 and the friendly against Argentina on 13 June 2017.[4]


Others

Singapore Selection Squad

He was selected as part of the Singapore Selection squad for The Sultan of Selangor's Cup to be held on 6 May 2017.[5]
