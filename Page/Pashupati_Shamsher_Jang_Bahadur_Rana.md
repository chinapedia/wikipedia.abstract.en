PASHUPATI SHAMSHER JANG BAHADUR RANA () is a politician from Rana dynasty. He served as Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Water Resources and Communication (1990-1994), Minister of Water Resources (1995-97&1997-1998) after Panchayat era. He served as Minister of Panchayat and Local Development (1986-1988), Ministry of Transport and Tourism (1978 -1979) and various other full ministers and state ministers during Panchayat era.[1]


Family

Pashupati Shamsher Jang Bahadur Rana was born to Lgt. Gen. Bijaya Shamsher Jang Bahadur Rana, son of Rana PM Mohan Shamsher Jang Bahadur Rana.[2] He was married to _Rani_ Usha Raje Scindia, daughter of Maratha Scindia, King of the Gwalior State, Jivajirao Scindia and Queen Vijayaraje Scindia in 1967 A.D.[3]
