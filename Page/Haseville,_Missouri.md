HASEVILLE (also spelled HAYSVILLE) is an unincorporated community in Linn County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Haseville was established in 1879, and remained in operation until 1908.[2] The community derives its name from John Hays, a local merchant.[3]
