ALEKSANDR SERGEYEVICH BUTENKO (; born 20 July 1998) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Volgar Astrakhan on loan from FC Krasnodar.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Krasnodar-2 on 4 August 2016 in a game against FC Armavir.[1]

On 13 June 2019, he joined FC Volgar Astrakhan on loan.[2]
