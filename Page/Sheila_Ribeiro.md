''' Sheila Canevacci Ribeiro ''' (born March 25, 1973) is an artist working on digital[1] and Body arts[2] as well as on conceptual Fashion. She currently lives between São Paulo and Rome and is married to the Italian anthropologist Massimo Canevacci.


Career

Sheila Ribeiro is best known by her delusional artwork crossing contemporary power dynamics, codes migration, digital cognition and colored pop fashion aesthetics. She is also known for her multipurpose non-disciplinary[3] way of working through installations, performances, films, fashion shootings, dance pieces on stage, on camera, on the street.[4]
