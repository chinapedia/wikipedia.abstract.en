MELISSA MORALES (born 4 December 1990) is a Guatemalan tennis player.

Morales On 12 November 2018, she reached her best singles ranking of world number 780. On 17 September 2018, she peaked at world number 608 in the doubles rankings.

Since her debut for the Guatemala Fed Cup team in 2008, Morales has a 9–5 record in win-loss.[1]
