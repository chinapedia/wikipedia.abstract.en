SOLOMON ALAIMALO (born 27 December 1995) is a New Zealand rugby union player who currently plays as an outside back for in New Zealand's domestic Mitre 10 Cup and for the in the international Super Rugby competition.[1][2][3]


Senior career

Alaimalo debuted for Northland during the 2016 Mitre 10 Cup and immediately became a key figure for them, starting all 10 of their championship games, scoring 2 tries in the process.[4]


Super Rugby

Alaimalo was named in the squad ahead of the 2017 Super Rugby season.[5]
