Eintrittskarte_documenta_III_29_08_1964.jpg DOCUMENTA III was the third edition of documenta, a quinquennial contemporary art exhibition. It was held between 27 June and 5 October 1964 in Kassel, Germany. The artistic director was Arnold Bode in collaboration with Werner Haftmann. The title of the exhibition was: _Internationale Ausstellung – international exhibition_.[1]
