KWAME OWUSU BOATENG (born 21 November 1998) is an English professional footballer who plays as a defender.


Career

Born in London,[1] Boateng spent four years with Bradford City,[2] making two appearances in the Football League Trophy during the 2016–17 season.[3] By February 2018 he was playing for Whirkirk Wanderers in the Leeds Combination League.[4] He began the 2018–19 season with Harrogate Town, before moving to Guiseley in February 2019.[5] On 9 March 2019, he was loaned out for one month to Farsley Celtic and helped the team gain promotion.[6] He left Guiseley in May 2019.[7]
