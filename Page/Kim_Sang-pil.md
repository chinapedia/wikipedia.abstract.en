KIM SANG-PIL (; born 26 April 1989) is a South Korean footballer who plays as centre-back for Asan Mugunghwa in K League 2.[1]


Career

Kim joined K League Classic side FC Seoul in 2013, but made no appearance in his first club.

He moved to Daejeon Citizen in January 2014.[2]

He signed with Chungju Hummel before the 2016 season starts.[3]
