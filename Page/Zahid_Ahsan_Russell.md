ZAHID AHSAN RUSSELL (born 1 January 1978) is a Bangladesh Awami League politician and a present Member of Parliament from Gazipur-2.[1] He is the Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Youth and Sports Ministry.[2] His father was Ahsanullah Master, a former member of Parliament.[3]
