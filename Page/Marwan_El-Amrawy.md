MARWAN EL-AMRAWY (born April 14, 1995) in Alexandria, Egypt, is an Egyptian marathon swimmer. He placed 20th in the men's marathon 10 kilometre event at the 2016 Summer Olympics after placing 1st in Africa.[1][2]
