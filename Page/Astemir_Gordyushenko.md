ASTEMIR ALEKSANDROVICH GORDYUSHENKO (; born 30 March 1997) is a Russian football player who plays as a central midfielder for PFC CSKA Moscow.


Career

Club

Gordyushenko made his debut for the main squad of PFC CSKA Moscow in the Russian Cup game against FC Yenisey Krasnoyarsk on 21 September 2016,[1] with his Russian Premier League debut for CSKA Moscow coming on 2 October 2016 against FC Rostov.[2]

On 21 November 2016, Gordyushenko extended his contract with CSKA Moscow until the end of the 2019–20 season.[3]

On 25 January 2019, he joined Tyumen on loan until the end of the 2018–19 season.[4]
