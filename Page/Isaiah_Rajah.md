ISAIAH RAJAH (born 16 October 1993) is a Trinidadian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Trinidad and Tobago in the 2016–17 Regional Four Day Competition on 25 November 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Trinidad and Tobago in the 2017–18 Regional Super50 on 11 February 2018.[3]
