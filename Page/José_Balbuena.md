JOSÉ BALBUENA RODRÍGUEZ (born October 3, 1917, Lima, Peru - June 23, 2009, Lima, Peru) is a Peruvian-Chilean footballer who played for clubs in Peru and Chile.


Teams

-   Universitario 1936-1937

-   Deportivo Municipal 1937-1938

-   Universidad de Chile 1939-1950[1]

-   Ferrobádminton 1951


Titles

-   Deportivo Municipal 1938 (Peruvian Primera División Championship)

-   Universidad de Chile 1940 (Chilean Primera División Championship)
