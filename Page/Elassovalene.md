ELASSOVALENE (2A,8B-DIHYDRO-CYCLOPENT[_CD_]AZULENE) is a polycyclic hydrocarbon with chemical formula C₁₂H₁₀, composed of one cycloheptatriene ring and two fused cyclopentene rings.[1]
