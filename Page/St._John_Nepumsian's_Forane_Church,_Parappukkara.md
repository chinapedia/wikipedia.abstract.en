[1]ST. JOHN NEPOMUCENE FORANE CHURCH is a parish of the Syro-Malabar Catholic Church located at Parappukkara in the Thrissur district of Kerala, India, in the Eparchy of Irinjalakuda. It is dedicated to Saint John of Nepomuk.

There are roughly 750 Christian families in the parish. There are religious, educational, medical, social-service, and secular organizations and institution under this church. It is a forane church, overseeing seven congregations.

The church is constructed on a highest location in the area, and surrounded by a huge protective compound wall called Aana-Mathil, or Elephant(ine) wall.
