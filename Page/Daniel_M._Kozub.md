DANIEL M. KOZUB is a Democrat US politician from Hamburg, New York. He represented the 1st District in the Erie County Legislature from 2006-2011.[1]

Kozub graduated from Lackawanna High School and then served in the U.S. Navy. For over two decades, he has been a bus driver for NFTA Metro. Kozub also served as a member of the Lackawanna City Council for eight years. During his tenure in the Erie County Legislature, he was the chair of the public safety and energy and environment committees.[2]
