XEHIT-AM/XHHIT-FM is a radio station on 1310 AM and 95.5 FM in San Bernardino Tlaxcalancingo, Puebla. It carries Grupo ACIR's Radio Felicidad format.


History

XEHIT received its concession on September 3, 1968. It was later acquired by Grupo ACIR. The FM station signed on November 15, 2018.
