UBOVIĆA BRDO is a village in the Mrkonjić Grad municipality, Republic of Srpska, Bosnia and Herzegovina. The village census shows that it had 593 inhabitants in 1953,[1] reduced to only 81 in 2013.[2] The village is known for their yearly rock climbing competition and festivity called "Pecka Rock Climbing Festival".[3]


Famous people

The village is the birthplace of

-   Stevo Todorčević, a world-renowned mathematician
-   Tode Nikoletić, children's poet and writer
