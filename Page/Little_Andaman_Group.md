LITTLE ANDAMAN GROUP is a group of islands of the Andaman Islands, which include all islands south of Duncan Passage. It belongs to the South Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1]


Geography

The group includes The Brothers (Andaman), and Little Andaman Island.[2]


Administration

Politically, Little Andaman group is also a tehsil of Andaman.[3]


Demographics

The main village of Kwate-tu-Kwage is located on Hut Bay. only Little Andaman island is inhabited.
