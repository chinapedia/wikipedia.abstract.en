JEAN-PAUL GOUJON (born 1949) is a French university professor and writer.


Biography

An honorary professor of French literature at the University of Seville, "specialist of 20th-century writers"[1] and in particular of Pierre Louÿs, Jean-Paul Goujon is the author of several biographies and anthologies.
