DUBAI MAJESTY (foaled March 19, 2005) is a retired filly best known for winning the Breeders' Cup Filly & Mare Sprint and being the only two-time winner of the Winning Colors Stakes.


background

Dubai Majesty is a dark bay nearly black filly sired by Essence of Dubai, whose sire was Pulpit. Her dam Great Majesty's descendents include Hall of Fame inductee Ta Wee.


Career

2-3-year-old-season

Dubai Majesty only had 1 victory as a 2-year-old.But as a 3 year old she began to run in stakes company starting with a 2nd in the azeala stakes. She then contuined with a 3rd in the Raven Run Stakes and the Indiana Oaks.

4-year-old-season

Quickly after a 2nd and a 1st to start the season Dubai Majesty began running in G1 with a 4th in the Vinery Madison Stakes and a 3rd in the Humana Distaff Handicap. After that she won her first of 2 victorys in the winning colors stakes. after only 1 win in another 4 races the 4-year-old season was over for Dubai Majesty

5-year-old season

After 4 straight defeats Dubai Majesty went on to repeat the winning colors before a 3rd in the princess rooney. She then won a non graded stakes race following a 2nd in the G3 Presque Isle Downs Masters Stakes. Her last 2 races would be her 2 best wins as she won the raven run. But then in her last start the Breeders' Cup Filly & Mare Sprint she blazed ahead of everyone to win. After that she retired where she was bought at the Fasig-Tipton stock sale for 1.1 million dollars
