MOHAMED MARA (born 12 December 1996) is a Guinean international footballer who plays as a forward for French Ligue 1 club FC Lorient. Mara also holds French citizenship.


Career

Born in Conakry, Guinea, Mara was in FC Lorient's youth teams since 2010,[1] and made his professional debut for Lorient in a 1–0 win over Lille in September 2016.[2]


International career

Mara moved to France from Guinea when he was 13 years old.[3] He debuted for the Guinea national football team in a 2–1 2018 World Cup qualification loss to the DR Congo on 13 November 2016.[4]
