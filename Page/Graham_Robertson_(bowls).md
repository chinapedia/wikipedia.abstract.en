GRAHAM ROBERTSON is a former Scottish international indoor and lawn bowler.[1] He is the second most capped Scottish international of all time.


Bowls career

He won a gold medal in the fours with Angus Blair, Willie Wood and Alex Marshall at the 1992 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Worthing. Robertson won the men's pairs at the 1998 World Indoor Bowls Championship with bowls partner Richard Corsie.[2]

He has represented Scotland three times at the Commonwealth Games from 1986-1994.[3]


Personal life

He is a Managing Director of a Sports Surfaces company called Greengauge.[4]
