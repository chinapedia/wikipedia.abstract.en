_SPLENDRILLIA KORUAHINENSIS_ is an extinct species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the holotype (apex broken) attains 23.7 mm, its diameter 8.8 mm.


Distribution

This extinct marine species was endemic to New Zealand
