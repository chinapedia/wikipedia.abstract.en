RENFRO is an unincorporated community in Moniteau County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Renfro was established in 1899, and remained in operation until 1907.[2] The community derives its name from James P. Renfrow, the proprietor of a local mill.[3]
