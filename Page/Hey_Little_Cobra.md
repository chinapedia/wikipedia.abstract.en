"HEY LITTLE COBRA" is a song released in 1963 by the Rip Chords. The song was produced by Terry Melcher and Bruce Johnston, who also sang vocals.[1]

The song spent 14 weeks on the _Billboard_ Hot 100 chart, peaking at No. 4,[2] while reaching No. 5 on Canada's CHUM Hit Parade,[3] and No. 3 on New Zealand's "Lever Hit Parade".[4]
