ADRIANA BURKE (n√©e VIOLA; born 26 December 1966), commonly known as ADRIANA, is a Brazilian former footballer who played as a forward for the Brazil women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup. At club level she played for EC Radar in Brazil.[1]

While playing college soccer for St. John's Red Storm, Adriana set goal (40) and points (91) records which were not broken until 2015 by Rachel Daly.[2] She was inducted into the program's Hall of Fame in 2014.[3]

At the 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup, Adriana started two of three group games as Brazil were eliminated in the first round.[4]
