BEND is an unincorporated community in Maries County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Bend was established in 1900, and remained in operation until 1917.[2] The community derives its name from nearby Sinful Bend, a meander on the Gasconade River known for its rough characters.[3]
