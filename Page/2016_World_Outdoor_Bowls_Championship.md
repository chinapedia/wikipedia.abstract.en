The 2016 WORLD OUTDOOR BOWLS CHAMPIONSHIP was held at the Burnside Bowling Club in Avonhead, Christchurch, New Zealand from 29 November to 11 December 2016.[1]

There were eight events that determined the 2016 world champions, the men's singles, doubles, triples and fours and the women's singles, doubles, triples and fours.[2]
