AARON TEROI (born 2 October 1995) is a Cook Islands international rugby league footballer who plays for the Central Queensland Capras in Intrust Super Cup. He plays at and .


Background

Born in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. Teroi was educated at Craigieburn Secondary College.

He played his junior rugby league for the Northern Thunder RLFC in Broadmeadows before being signed by the Melbourne Storm.[1]


Playing career

In 2012 and 2013, Teroi played for Melbourne Storm's SG Ball Cup side.[2][3] In 2014 and 2015, he played for Melbourne's NYC side, being awarded the Greg Brentnall Victorian Young Achievers award at the end of the 2015 season.[4]
