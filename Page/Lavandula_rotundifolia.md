_LAVANDULA ROTUNDIFOLIA_ is a species of flowering plants of the Lamiaceae family. The species is endemic to Cape Verde.[1] The species was named by George Bentham in 1833. Its local name is _aipo_. The plant is used in traditional medicine for treating stomach aches.[2]


Distribution and ecology

_Lavandula rotundifolia_ occurs on the islands of Santo Antão, São Vicente, São Nicolau, Santiago and Fogo.[3][4]
