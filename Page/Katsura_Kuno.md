Kuno was a Varsity Scout, a Keio University graduate, and a member of Keio Scout _Mita-kai_ alumni association.[1]

In 2010, Kuno was awarded the 324th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2]
