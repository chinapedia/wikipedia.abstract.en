CALEDONIA was a former town in Cass Township, Sullivan County, in the U.S. state of Indiana.


History

Extensive strip mining in the area caused the town of Caledonia to become extinct.[1]

A post office was established at Caledonia in 1902, and operated until it was discontinued in 1909.[2]
