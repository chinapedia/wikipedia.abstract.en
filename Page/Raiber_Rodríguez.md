Wrestling_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics,_Bayramov_vs_Rodríguez_12.jpg RAIBER RODRÍGUEZ (born December 28, 1990) is a Venezuelan Greco-Roman wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 59 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated by Rovshan Bayramov.[1][2]
