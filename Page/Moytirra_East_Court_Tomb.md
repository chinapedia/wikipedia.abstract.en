MOYTIRRA EAST COURT TOMB, commonly called the GIANT'S GRAVE, is a court cairn and National Monument located in County Sligo, Ireland.[1][2]


Location

Moytirra East court tomb is located west of Ballyfarnon, to the east of Lough Arrow.[3][4]


History

Moytirra East Court Tomb was built c. 4000â€“2500 BC, in the Neolithic.

It was locally known as the Giant's Grave, and was said to hold the remains of a giant killed in the Battle of Moytura.[5]


Description

Moytirra East Court Tomb has the remains of a U-shaped court leading to a four-chamber gallery.[6][7] These would have been covered by a cairn, but these stones seem to have been removed.[8] [9]
