BALDO DE' SEROFINI (active middle 15th century) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance period, active in the Marche and Umbria.

Little is known of his biography. He is said to have been born in Perugia. He painted at least two canvases depicting a _Madonna del Soccorso_, one found in the Pinacoteca Civica Marco Moretti in Civitanova,[1] and the other for the Museo-Pinacoteca di Palazzo Lazzarini in Morrovalle in the Marche.[2] He painted a _Madonna and Child with Angels_ for the church of Santa Chiara in Cagli.[3] He also painted devotional works found now in the Galleria Nazionale delle Marche in Urbino.[4]
