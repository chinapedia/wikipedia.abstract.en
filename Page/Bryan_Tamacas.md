BRYAN ALEXANDER TAMACAS LÓPEZ (born 21 February 1995) is a Salvadoran professional footballer, who plays as a defender.[1][2][3][4]


International career

Youth

Tamacas has played for El Salvador at the under-17, under-20 and under-21 levels.

Senior

Tamacas made his international debut for El Salvador he started and played 86 minute in a friendly match against the Armenia on 1 June 2016.


Honours

-   Santa Tecla F.C.
    -   PRIMERA DIVISIÓN (3): Clausura 2015, Apertura 2016, Clausura 2017
