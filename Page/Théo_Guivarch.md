THÉO GUIVARCH (born 17 November 1995) is a French footballer who plays for SO Cholet on loan from Guingamp.[1][2]

Born in Lorient, Guivarch began playing football with local side FC Lorient. He did not make any Ligue 1 appearances for the club, and moved to Guingamp.[3]


Personal

Guivarch is not related to former France international footballer Stéphane Guivarc'h.[4]
