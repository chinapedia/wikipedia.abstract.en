PIETRO CECCARELLI (born 16 February 1992) is an Italian rugby union player. Usually positioned as a prop, he currently plays for Edinburgh Rugby in the pro 14. For the 2018/19 season Ceccarelli will join Pro14 side Edinburgh Rugby.

Ceccarelli linked up with the Italian national team in 2016 after appearances for Zebre and La Rochelle, respectively.
