SEOK-MIN HAM is a footballer who plays for Gangwon FC on loan from Suwon Bluewings.[1] In 2013, he was called onto the South Korea national under-20 football team.[2]

Finishing high school, he went to Soongsil University where he won the Goalkeeper award in 2013. Afterwards, he would head to the Korea National League.[3] An ardent supporter of Suwon Samsung Bluewings since 2005, he revealed that 'Suwon was always the No.1 club in his mind' after joining them.[4]


Trophies

Ham has received 1x K League Classic Runners-Up medal.[5]
