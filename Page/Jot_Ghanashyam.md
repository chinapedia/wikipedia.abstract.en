JOT GHANASHYAM is a village and a gram panchayat in Daspur II CD Block in Ghatal subdivision of Paschim Medinipur district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Geography

There are six villages under Jot Ghnashyam gram panchayat: Joteghanashyam, Satpota, Gourichak, Donabhanga, Gomokpota and Narayanchak.[1]


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Jot Ghanashaym had a total population of 15,200 of which 7,833 (52%) were males and 7,367 (48%) were females. Population below 6 years was 1,616. The total number of literates in Jot Ghanashyam was 11,851 (77.97% of the population over 6 years).[2]


Education

Nilmoni High School was established in 1938.
