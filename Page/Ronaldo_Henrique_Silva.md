RONALDO HENRIQUE SILVA (born April 10, 1991) is a Brazilian football player who plays for Perak FA in the Malaysia Super League


Playing career

Yokohama FC

Ronaldo played for J2 League club; Yokohama FC in 2014 season.

Perak FA

He is currently playing for Perak FA in 2019 season. He scored 2 goals in his debut against PKNP FC in the 2019 Malaysia FA Cup Quarterfinal 2nd Leg. Perak won the game 2-1. Three days later, he scored again in his first game in the Malaysia Super League against the same team, PKNP FC. The match ended 3-1 in favour of Perak.
