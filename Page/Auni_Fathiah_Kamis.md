AUNI FATHIAH BINTI KAMIS is a Malaysian international lawn bowler.[1] [2]


Bowls career

World Championship

Kamis won a silver medal in the fours at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Adelaide.[3]

Asia Pacific Championships

Kamis won double silver at the 2019 Asia Pacific Bowls Championships in the Gold Coast, Queensland in the triples and fours.[4][5]
