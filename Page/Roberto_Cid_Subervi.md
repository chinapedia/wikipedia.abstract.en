ROBERTO CID SUBERVI (; born 30 August 1993) is a Dominican–American tennis player.

Cid Subervi has a career high ATP singles ranking of 226 achieved on 23 July 2018. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 537, achieved on 30 July 2018. Cid Subervi has won 4 ITF singles titles and 1 ITF doubles title.

Cid Subervi has represented Dominican Republic at Davis Cup, where he has a win-loss record of 3–9.
