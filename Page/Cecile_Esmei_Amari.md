'''Cecile Esmei Amari ''' (born 20 November 1991) is an Ivorian footballer who plays as a midfielder for the Ivory Coast women's national football team.[1] She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship and 2015 FIFA Women's World Cup.[2] On club level she played for Subotika in Morocco.
