JOHN TROYER (born August 13, 1985) is a mixed martial artist from Louisville, Kentucky.


Bellator Fighting Championships

Troyer fought three times in the Bellator Fighting Championships. He lost to Justin Edwards at Bellator 5,[1] lost to Brent Weedman at Bellator 23[2] and defeated Josh Clark at Bellator 30.[3]


Other promotions

Troyer previously fought for Super Fight League[4] and XFC.[5]
