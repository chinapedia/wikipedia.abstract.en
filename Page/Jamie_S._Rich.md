JAMIE S. RICH is an American comic book editor and writer. He is the Group Editor of Batman-related titles at DC Comics. Previously, he was an editor at DC's Vertigo imprint.[1]

He was editor-in-chief at Oni Press from 1998 to 2004, and was an editor for Dark Horse Comics before that.[2] He has written graphic novels including _12 Reasons Why I Love Her_ and _You Have Killed Me_, and the prose novel _Cut My Hair_.[3]
