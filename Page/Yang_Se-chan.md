YANG SE-CHAN (born December 8, 1986), is a South Korean singer and comedian.[1][2] As of April 2017, Yang, along with Jeon So-min, was added as a regular cast member of South Korean variety show _Running Man_.[3]


Personal life

He is also famous for the nicknames of Dongdukchon Talai brothers, Gangsu, MC Ssep Ssep, and Yang Ax. His group debut "Uwonye" and "Triple Axel" debuted in 2003 as musical actor. Two years later, he made his debut with the album 'Only One' in 2008 as a singer.[4] His older brother, Yang Se-hyung, is also a comedian who was a cast member for _Infinite Challenge_ until March 31, 2018.[5]
