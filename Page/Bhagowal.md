BHAGOWAL () refers to two different villages located in the district of Gujrat. Each of these villages is independently referred to as Bhagowal.

-   The relatively larger village is Bhagowal Kalan that is located in the north
-   The other village, Bhagowal Khurd, is located in the south of Jalalpur Jattan city.
