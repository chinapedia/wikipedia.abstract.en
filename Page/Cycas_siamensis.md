_CYCAS SIAMENSIS_ is a species of Cycad endemic to Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam.


Synonyms

-   _Cycas baguanheensis_ L.K.Fu & S.Z.Cheng
-   _Cycas immersa_ Craib
-   _Cycas intermedia_ B.S.Williams
-   _Cycas panzhihuaensis_ L.Zhou & S.Y.Yang
-   _Cycas siamensis_ subsp. _siamensis_
-   _Epicycas siamensis_ (Miq.) de Laub.
