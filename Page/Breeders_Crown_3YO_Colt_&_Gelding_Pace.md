The BREEDERS CROWN 3YO COLT & GELDING PACE is a harness racing event for three-year-old Standardbred pacers. It is one part of the Breeders Crown annual series of twelve races for both Standardbred trotters and trotters. First run in 1985, it is contested over a distance of one mile. Race organizers have awarded the event to various racetracks across North America.[1] The 2017 race will be held at Hoosier Park in Anderson, Indiana, United States.[2]


Historical race events

In 2010, Pocono Downs became the first venue to host all 12 events on a single night.[3]
