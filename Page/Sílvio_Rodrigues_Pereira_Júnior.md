SÍLVIO RODRIGUES PEREIRA JÚNIOR (born 4 May 1994), commonly known as SÍLVIO, is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a forward for Vllaznia Shkodër.

In February 2016 he signed a contract with the Ukrainian Premier League FC Chornomorets.[1]
