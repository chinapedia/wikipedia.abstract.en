MILL CREEK is a stream in Morgan County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary to the Gravois Creek arm of the Lake of the Ozarks.[2]

The stream headwaters arise at [3] just northeast of Missouri Route 135 at an elevation of approximately .[4] The stream meanders northeast and east passing under Missouri Route 5 and enters the Lake of the Ozarks at at an elevation of .[5] Previous to the impoundment of the lake the confluence of Mill Creek with Gravois Creek was located 2.3 miles to the northeast at and an elevation of [6] and about one mile north of the community of Gladstone.[7]

Mill Creek was so named on account of watermills near its course.[8]
