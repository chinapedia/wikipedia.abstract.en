ALESSANDRO EGGER (born September 6, 1991) is an Italian-Serbian male model and actor.[1] He is recognised as Nick in the teen-drama situation comedy called _The band_.[2][3] He is known for being present in most of the Dolce Gabbana fashion shows since the beginning of his career.[4]


Personal life

Egger was born in Belgrade, Republic of Serbia, and moved to Como, Italy, when he was 6 years old.[5] He lives in Belgrade.[6][7]


Career

Egger's career started at age 13 with a TV commercial.[8] Throughout his modelling career, Egger has worked with in series like "Un medico in famiglia" with Lino Banfi and "Shades of Truth" with Christopher Lamber and Giancarlo Giannini.[9] In 2017 he is joining the Adventure game "Pechino Express [10]1" hosted by Costantino della Gherardesca.
