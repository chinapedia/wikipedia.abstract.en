SALEEM BURKI (born 14 April 1991) is a Pakistani cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Federally Administered Tribal Areas in the 2016–17 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy on 7 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut on 20 January 2017 for Federally Administered Tribal Areas in the 2016–17 Regional One Day Cup.[3]
