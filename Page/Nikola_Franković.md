'''Nikola Frankovic ''' (born 9 November 1982) is a Croatian male water polo player. He was a member of the Croatia men's national water polo team, playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played for VK Primorje in Croatia.
