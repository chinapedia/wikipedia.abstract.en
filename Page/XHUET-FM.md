XHUET-FM is a noncommercial radio station on 103.5 FM in Huetamo, Michoacán, Mexico. It is owned by Rey Tariacuri, A.C. and known as La Guacha.


History

XHUET received its concessions on February 12, 2016 and came to air seven months later. The station is run by Adolfo Arias Castro and broadcasts from Cerro del Algodón (Cotton Hill).
