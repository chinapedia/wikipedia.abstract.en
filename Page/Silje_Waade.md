SILJE KATRINE WAADE (born 20 March 1994) is a Norwegian handball player for Vipers Kristiansand and the Norwegian national team.[1][2]

She was an active track athlete until 17 years of age.[3]

She also represented Norway in the 2013 Women's Junior European Handball Championship, placing 4th.[4]
