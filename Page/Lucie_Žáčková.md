LUCIE ŽÁČKOVÁ (born 16 February 1978) is a Czech film and stage actress. She won the Young Talent award at the 2003 Thalia Awards and 2003 Alfréd Radok Awards.[1] She won the Czech Lion award for Best Supporting Actress in 2015 for her role in the film _The Snake Brothers_.[2]


Selected filmography

-   _The City of the Sun_ (2005)
-   _Zoufalci_ (2009)
-   _Revival_ (2013)
-   _The Snake Brothers_ (2015)
