'''Emelda Musonda ''' (born 29 November 1994) is a Zambian footballer who plays as a defender for the Zambia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship.[1][2][3] On club level she played for Red Arrows F.C. in Zambia.[4]
