_CNEMASPIS KOTTIYOORENSIS_, or the KOTTIYOOR DAY GECKO, is a species of diurnal gecko endemic to the Western Ghats in Kerala, India.[1]


Distribution

This species is endemic to Kerala, India. In 2014 it was reported that this species is found in Perumalmudi, in the proposed Kottiyoor Wildlife Sanctuary, Kannur District, Kerala.[2]
