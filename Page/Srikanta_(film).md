_SRIKANTA_ is a 2017 Indian Kannada-language romantic action thriller film written and directed by Manju Swaraj.[1] The film, which was released on 6 January 2017, was produced by M. S. Manu Gowda under the banner Mahashaila Cinebandha. Based on the 2-page short story _Skhalana_ by Nagathihalli Chandrashekar which was published in 1985 in the weekly Kannada magazine _Taranga_,[2] the film features Shiva Rajkumar and Chandini Sreedharan in the lead roles along with Vijay Raghavendra in a key supporting role. The principal photography of the film began on 16 October 2015.[3]

The film was released on 6 January 2017.[4]

Soundtrack

The background score and soundtrack of the film was composed by B. Ajaneesh Loknath. The soundtrack album consists of five tracks.[5] It has lyrics penned by Prof. Krishne Gowda, Pradyumna Narahalli, Chethan Kumar and V. Nagendra Prasad. The album was released on 17 December 2017.[6]
