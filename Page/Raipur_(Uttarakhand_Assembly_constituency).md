19-RAIPUR LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY () is one of the seventy electoral Uttarakhand Legislative Assembly constituencies of Uttarakhand state in India. It includes Dehradun Cantonment area.

Raipur Legislative Assembly constituency is a part of Tehri Garhwal (Lok Sabha constituency).
