SVENN ERIK MEDHUS (born 22 June 1982) is a Norwegian handball player.[1]

He made his debut on the Norwegian national team in 2007, and played 81 matches for the national team between 2007 and 2012.[2] He competed at the 2009 World Men's Handball Championship in Croatia.
