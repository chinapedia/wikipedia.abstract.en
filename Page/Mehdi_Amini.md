MEHDI AMINI is an Iranian Football Goalkeeper who currently plays for Iranian football club Paykan in the Persian Gulf Pro League.[1][2]


Club career

Sepahan

Amini started in the Sepahan youth system and signed a professional contract with the club in the summer of 2016. Amini made his professional debut in 2016 in a Hazfi Cup match. He made his Persian Gulf Pro League debut on 24 November 2016 in a 1–1 draw against Foolad.
