CALLUM MAYCOCK (born 23 December 1997) is an English footballer. He is a Midfielder who plays for League Two team Macclesfield Town, on loan from Coventry City.[1]


Career

Maycock made his professional debut coming on as a substitute on 6 November 2016 in a 1–1 FA Cup draw with Morecambe, coming on to replace Andre Wright.[2]

On 30 July 2018, Maycock was loaned out to Macclesfield Town until January 2019.[3] The deal was later extended for the remainder of the season.[4]
