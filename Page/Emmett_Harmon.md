Hon. EMMETT HARMON of Liberia served as a member of the World Scout Committee. In 1975, Harmon was awarded the 99th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting, at the 25th World Scout Conference.[1]
