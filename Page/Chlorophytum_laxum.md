_CHLOROPHYTUM LAXUM_ (BICHETII GRASS, SIAM LILY, FALSE LILY TURF, WHEAT PLANT) is a flowering plant species in the genus _Chlorophytum_, widespread through tropical Africa, Asia, and Australia.
