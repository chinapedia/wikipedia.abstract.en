NOAH AL-KHULAIFI (, __; born on 10 May 1999) is a Qatari swimmer. He competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics in the men's 100 metre backstroke event; his time of 1:07.47 in the heats did not qualify him for the semifinals.[1][2]
