The SANREMO MUSIC FESTIVAL 1962 was the 12th annual Sanremo Music Festival, held at the Sanremo Casino in Sanremo, province of Imperia between 8 and 18 February 1962.[1]

The show was presented by Renato Tagliani, assisted by actresses Laura Efrikian and Vicky Ludovisi. Gianni Ravera served as artistic director.[2]

According to the rules of this edition every song was performed in a double performance by a couple of singers or groups.[3] The winners of the Festival were Domenico Modugno and Claudio Villa with the song "Addio, addio".[4]
