PORT HUDSON is an unincorporated community in Franklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Port Hudson was established in 1859, and remained in operation until 1909.[2] The community's name most likely is a transfer from Port Hudson, Louisiana.[3]
