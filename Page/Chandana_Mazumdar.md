CHANDANA MAZUMDAR () is a Bangladeshi female singer.[1][2][3] She won Bangladesh National Film Award for the Best Female Playback Singer for the film Monpura, share with Krishnokoli in 2009.[4][5] She also known for the Lalon giti.[6]
