KAMER KRASNIQI (born 11 January 1996) is a Kosovo Albanian footballer who plays as a midfielder for VfL Osnabrück.


International career

On 5 June 2017. Krasniqi received a call-up from Kosovo U21 for a 2019 UEFA European Under-21 Championship qualification match against Norway U21[1] and making his debut after being named in the starting line-up.
