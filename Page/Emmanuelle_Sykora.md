'''Emmanuelle Sykora ''' (born 21 February 1976 in Pau) is a French women's international footballer who plays as a defender. She is a member of the France women's national football team. She was part of the team at the UEFA Women's Euro 1997, UEFA Women's Euro 2001 and 2003 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1]
