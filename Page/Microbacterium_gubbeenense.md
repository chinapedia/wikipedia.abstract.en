_MICROBACTERIUM GUBBEENENSE_ is a Gram-positive, facultatively anaerobic, non-spore-forming and non-motile bacterium from the genus of Microbacterium which has been isolated from the surface of a smear-ripened cheese in Ireland.[1][2][3][4]
