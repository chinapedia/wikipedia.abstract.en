'''Christopher B. Quinn ''' (born October 11, 1967) is a Republican member of the Pennsylvania House of Representatives, representing the 168th legislative district.[1][2] He was first elected in a special election held on July 12, 2016.[3]

Quinn holds a B.A. in Economics from the University of South Florida,[4] and started an insurance agency in Delaware County in 2002.[5] Prior to being elected state representative, Quinn served as a Middletown Township Councilman and as Vice Treasurer of the Delaware County Industrial Development Authority.[6]
