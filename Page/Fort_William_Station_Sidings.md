FORT WILLIAM STATION SIDINGS is a stabling point located in Fort William, Scotland. The depot is situated on the West Highland Line and is near Fort William station.

The depot code is FW.


Present

As of 2016, the depot has no allocation. It is, instead, a stabling point for Caledonian Sleeper Class 73/9 diesel-electric locomotives.
