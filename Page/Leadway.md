LEADWAY ASSURANCE COMPANY, also known as LEADWAY, is a Nigerian insurance corporation headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria. It is one of Nigeria's largest insurance companies.[1] Leadway provides commercial and personal property and casualty insurance, travel insurance and life insurance.


History

Leadway was established in 1970 by Sir Hassan O. Odukale. It commenced business in 1971 and started out as a direct motor insurer. It expanded into other areas of general business until it became a composite company underwriting both life and general insurance business. The company is now being run by the Oye Hassan-Odukale and Tunde Hassan-Odukale, sons of Sir Hassan O. Odukale.
