JORGE MARIA CUI served as the Secretary General of the Boy Scouts of the Philippines from 1975 to 1980, and as a member of the Asia-Pacific Scout Committee.

In 1979, he was awarded the 134th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting, at the 25th World Scout Conference.[1]
