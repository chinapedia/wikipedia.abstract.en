_ESCAPE DANGEROUS_ is a 1947 British drama film directed by Digby Smith.[1] It was made as a supporting feature for release on the lower half of a double-bill.

-   Jack Faint as First Tribunal Judge
-   Cyril Conway as Paul Bonnet
