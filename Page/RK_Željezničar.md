RUKOMETNI KLUB ŽELJEZNIČAR is a handball club from Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina.


Accomplishments

-   Yugoslav Handball Championship
    -   Winners: 1978
-   Handball Championship of Bosnia and Herzegovina
    -   Winners: 2000
-   IHF Cup
    -   Runners-up: 1982
