DODDS is an unincorporated community in New Madrid County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Variant names were Dodds Spur", "Sky" and "Warrington".[2] The community has the name of E. M. Dodd, a railroad official.[3] The local post office was called "Spurdod".[4] The Spurdod post office opened and closed in 1918.[5]
