SVETOSLAV CHITAKOV (; born 10 January 1992) is a Bulgarian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Gigant Saedinenie.


Career

On 21 January 2017, Chitakov signed with Oborishte Panagyurishte.[1]

On 14 June 2017 he joined Litex Lovech in Bulgarian Second League[2] but was released before the beginning of the season and actually signed with Maritsa Plovdiv in July.[3]

In July 2018, Chitakov joined Gigant Saedinenie.[4]
