IRVIN STUDIN is a Canadian former soccer player who played in the USL A-League, and the Canadian Professional Soccer League.


Playing career

Studin played in 1997 with the Toronto Lynx in the USL A-League.[1][2] He made his debut on May 23, 1997 in a match against Charleston Battery.[3] In 1998, he signed with Glen Shields S.C. in the newly formed Canadian Professional Soccer League. He made his debut on June 3, 1998 against Toronto Croatia.[4] He helped the club secure a postseason berth by finishing third in the standings. The following season, he signed with rivals York Region Shooters, but failed to secure a playoff berth by finishing seventh in the standings.[5]
