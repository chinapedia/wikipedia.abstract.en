She played Isabel Rockmetteller in "The Full House", the second episode of the third series of the 1990s British comedy television series _Jeeves and Wooster_.

Canning was born in Epsom, Surrey. She was married to Leon Lissek. She also appeared in the television soap opera _Crossroads_ (1964), playing Shirley Coniston, and in _Morning Story_ (1970). She was also known for '' Miss Julie'' (1972) and _A Village Affair_ (1995). She died, aged 63, in Oxford.
