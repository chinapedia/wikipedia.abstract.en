JKFZ CAMBRIDGE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL is an international school in Qingshanhu, Nanchang, Jiangxi Province, China.

JKFZ CIS will accept its first cohort of students in August 2017, offering students the Cambridge curriculum with a view to preparing students for Western university education. The school is affiliated with the Jiangxi University of Technology High School, a private non-profit high school founded in 2015.
