CHOKKALINGA BHAGAVATHAR, also known as K. A. Chokkalinga Bhagavathar or M. A. Chokkalinga Bhagavathar (1907[1] - 21 January 2002[2]) is an Indian stage and film artiste who acted mostly in Tamil language films. Though he began his film career in 1938, he acted only in two films _Thukkaram_ and _Rambaiyin Kaadhal_ in 1938 and 1939 respectively. After a lapse of almost 50 years he was brought back to film by Balu Mahendra for his film _Veedu_ in 1988. Since then he appeared in about 10 films during the following decade. He acted in an Italian film _Branchie_ also in 1999.
