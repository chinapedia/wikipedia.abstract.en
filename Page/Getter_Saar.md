GETTER SAAR (born 6 June 1992) is an Estonian female badminton player, currently residing in Finland.

Getter Saar plays mainly in the women's singles. As of 2016 she has participated, as a member of the national team, in the 2016 European Men's & Women's Team Championships.

As of 2016 she has started the 2008 BWF World Junior Championships, 2012 Korea Open Grand Prix Gold, 2013 Bitburger Open Grand Prix Gold, 2013 Scottish Open Grand Prix, 2016 Dutch Open Grand Prix, and the 2016 Bitburger Open Grand Prix Gold.

As a single player she was the third on the pedestal at the Estonian Championships in 2010, 2011, 2013 and 2015. Besides that, in 2010 and 2011 she was the Estonian junior champion in the women's singles, and in 2011 in the mixed doubles.[1]
