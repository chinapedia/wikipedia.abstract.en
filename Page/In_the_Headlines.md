_IN THE HEADLINES_ is a 1929 crime drama film directed by John G. Adolfi and starring Grant Withers and Marian Nixon. It was produced and distributed by the Warner Brothers.[1]

-   Robert Ober - Parker
-   Ben Hall - Cub Reporter
-   Spec O'Donnell - Johnny
-   Jack Wise - Levine


Preservation status

-   This film is considered to be lost.[2][3]
