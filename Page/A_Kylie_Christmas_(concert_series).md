A KYLIE CHRISTMAS is a series of Christmas concerts performed by Australian singer-songwriter Kylie Minogue at the Royal Albert Hall in London, in support of her album _Kylie Christmas_ (2015). In 2015, the show was held on 11 December, and two dates were held on 9 December 2016 and 10 December 2016. Minogue performed mainly songs from her album _Kylie Christmas_ and its reissue, but included some of her previous hits.[1][2][3][4][5][6]

_A Kylie Christmas – Live from the Royal Albert Hall 2015_

_A KYLIE CHRISTMAS – LIVE FROM THE ROYAL ALBERT HALL 2015_ is a concert film of its corresponding tour. The show was filmed at Royal Albert Hall in London and directed by William Baker. It was released via iTunes and Apple Music on 25 November 2016 to coincide with the release of _Kylie Christmas: Snow Queen Edition_.[7]
