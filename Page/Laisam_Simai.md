LAISAM SIMAI is an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Simai was elected from the Nampong constituency in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election, standing as an BJP candidate.[2][3][4]

Laisam Simai is one of the youngest MLA, in Arunachal Pradesh. He comes from a remote village known as New Kamlao which is under Manmao Circle in the eastern part of Arunachal Pradesh.
