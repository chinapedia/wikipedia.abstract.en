The WUKOU VILLAGE LIOU FAMILY ANCESTRAL HALL () is an ancestral shrine in Wanluan Township, Pingtung County, Taiwan.


Architecture

The Hakka-style hall spans over an area of 1 hectare. It was built with two internal wings, two external wings and Baroque-style walls. Most of the building material were brought from Guangdong. There are two lion stones placed in front of the main entrance.[1]
