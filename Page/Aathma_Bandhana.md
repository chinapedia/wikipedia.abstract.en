_AATHMA BANDHANA_ is a 1992 Indian Kannada horror drama film directed by Srikanth Nahata [1] and produced by M. Rajagopal. The film features Shashikumar, Jayapradha and Vaishnavi in the lead roles.[2]

The film is a revenge drama of a woman, played by Vaishnavi, who is killed by her in-laws mercilessly and her spirit entering into a doll. The doll takes up the revenge against each of them who was involved in her murder. The film was a remake of Tamil film _Vaa Arugil Vaa_ (1991) which itself was loosely inspired from 1988 American film _Child's Play_. The film's music was composed by Rajan-Nagendra and cinematography is by D. V. Ramana.
