SLUTIŠKI is a village in Latvia, located on the shore of the river Daugava.


History

The village is known to have existed at least since 1785.[1]


Old Believers

Slutišķi village is home to a community of Old Believers, whose craftsmanship has put a distinct imprint on the village architecture with its elaborate woodwork influenced by traditional Slavic aesthetics. The village contains an ethnographic museum and a rural exhibition. The entire village is considered an ethnographic monument.[2][3]
