NATIONAL MEDIA ANALYTICS CENTRE is a proposed special media cell of the Indian National Security Council Secretariat tasked to track content online to counter news and comments deemed negative or provocative.[1] The centre uses software designed by Ponnurangam Kumaraguru, an Assistant Professor at Delhi-based Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, to comb posts and comments and classifies them into relevant categories.[2]
