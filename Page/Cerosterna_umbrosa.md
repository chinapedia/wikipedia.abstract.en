_CEROSTERNA UMBROSA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by James Thomson in 1865. It is known from Sumatra.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Cerosterna umbrosa umbrosa_ Thomson, 1865
-   _Cerosterna umbrosa mixta_ Aurivillius, 1916
-   _Cerosterna umbrosa quadrifasciata_ Aurivillius, 1916
-   _Cerosterna umbrosa variefasciata_ Schwarzer, 1926
-   _Cerosterna umbrosa viduata_ Pascoe, 1868
