PABBAS is a well known Ice cream parlour in the city of Mangalore(Kudla), Karnataka, India.[1][2] Pabbas is an outlet of the parent brand Ideal Ice Cream.

Pabbas special, Tiramisu, Gadbad are few out of many rich flavoured ice creams.

Zee news lists visiting the Pabbas as one of the must thing to do when one is in or around Mangalore[3]

There is also a counter right outside where they provide take-aways.[4]
