TUKARAQ is a small village in the East of the Sool region disputed between the unrecognized states of Somaliland and Puntland.[1] The town is located on the road between Las Anod and Garowe in Las Anod District.[2]


Overview

Somaliland captured Tukaraq from the loyal khaatumo forces that fled to Ethiopia.[3]

On 15 May 2018 intense increase in military activity occurred between rival Puntland and Somaliland armed forces in the surrounding area of Tukaraq after Puntland launched multiple minor offensives, whereafter the Somaliland forces launched a major counteroffensive[4]

On 24 May 2018 both rival groups between Somaliland and Puntland both participating in a ongoing conflict in the Sool region released press statements claiming that the opposing side launched a predawn offensive around 05:30 AM local time.[5]


Demographics

Tukaraq is inhabited by the Dhulbahante, Darod clan and various Dhulbahante sub-clans are represented .
