LORRAINE TWOHILL is an Irish marketer who works as the head of marketing at Google.


Early life

Lorraine Twohill is originally from Ireland. She attended Dublin City University and graduated with a joint honors degree in International Marketing and Languages.[1] She currently lives in Palo Alto, California.


Career

After graduation, Twohill worked as a brand manager for Burns Philp from 1992 to 1995.[2] In 2000, she became the head of marketing at Dreamticket.com in London,[3] and in 2001 she joined the European travel site Opodo also in the same role.[4] In 2003, Twohill was hired as Google's first marketer located outside the United States. She held various roles in the company, including running Google's marketing efforts in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa. She ultimately was promoted to become head of global marketing in 2009.[5]
