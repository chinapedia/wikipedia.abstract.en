HYER BRANCH is a stream in Dent and Phelps counties in the Ozarks of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary to the Dry Fork of the Meramec River.

Hyer Branch or Hyer Valley Branch derives its name from John Hyer, a pioneer citizen.[3]
