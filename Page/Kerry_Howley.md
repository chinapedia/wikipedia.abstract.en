KERRY HOWLEY (born 1981) is an American writer, and professor at the University of Iowa's Nonfiction Writing Program.[1]


Life

She graduated from Georgetown University, and University of Iowa. She was an editor at _Reason magazine_.[2]

Her work has appeared in _New York magazine,_[3] _Paris Review,_[4] and The New Yorker,[5] and she is a regular contributor to Bookforum.[6]
