NICOS KALOGERAS () served as the Chief Commissioner of the Scouts of Greece,[1][2] served as the Chairman of the Kandersteg International Scout Centre Committee, and as a member of the European Scout Committee.

In 1992, Kalogeras was awarded the 217th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[3]
