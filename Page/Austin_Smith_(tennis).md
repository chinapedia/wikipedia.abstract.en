AUSTIN SMITH (born 25 November 1993) is an American tennis player.

Smith has a career high ATP singles ranking of 771 achieved on 26 June 2017. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 705 achieved on 3 April 2017.

Smith made his ATP main draw debut at the 2016 BB&T Atlanta Open in the singles draw. Smith plays college tennis at the University of Georgia.[1]
