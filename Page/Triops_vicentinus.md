_TRIOPS VINCENTINUS_ is a species of freshwater crustacean tadpole shrimps.

It is endemic to Portugal, and only found within the Faro District.[1]

It diverged from _Triops cancriformis_ about 180 million years ago during the Middle Jurassic Epoch.
