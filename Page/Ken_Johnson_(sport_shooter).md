KEN JOHNSON (born November 24, 1968 in Marshfield, Massachusetts) is an American sport shooter. He tied for 38th place in the men's 10 metre air rifle event at the 2000 Summer Olympics. He is the husband of Olympic gold medalist Nancy Johnson.[1]
