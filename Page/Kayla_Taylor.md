'''Kayla Taylor ''' (born 27 October 1994) is a Trinidad and Tobago women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She was a member of the Trinidad and Tobago women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2016 CONCACAF Women's Olympic Qualifying Tournament.[1] At the collegiate level, she plays for Wiley College in the United States.
