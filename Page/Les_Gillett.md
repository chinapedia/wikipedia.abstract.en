LES GILLETT (born 17 November 1970) is an English international indoor and lawn bowls player.[1] He is nicknamed Razor.


Bowls career

He won the 2001 World Indoor Bowls Championship pairs title in Great Yarmouth with bowls partner Mark McMahon.[2]

Sixteen years later he won his second title when he won the Open Pairs with Jason Greenslade at the 2017 World Indoor Bowls Championship in Great Yarmouth.[3] [4]

Major wins include the 1997 International Open and 2002 Welsh Open and is also a winner of three National Titles in 1993 and 1994 when representing Cheltenham and Gloucestershire and 1998 when representing Oxfordshire and Banbury Borough.[5] He now represents the Leicester indoor club.[6]


Personal Life

He is an estimator/buyer by trade and is married to Sadie Gillett with three children.[7]
