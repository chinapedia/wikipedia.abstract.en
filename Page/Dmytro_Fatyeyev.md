DMYTRO FATYEYEV (; born 21 June 1994) is a professional Ukrainian football defender who plays for Inhulets Petrove.

Fatyeyev is a product of Youth Sportive School #2 in Kirovohrad. His first trainer was Vadym Bondar. After spending a short time in the Ukrainian Second League, he in 2012 signed a contract with his native city FC Zirka in the Ukrainian First League.[1]
