_' Woodlands Waterfront Park_' is a park located at Admiralty Road West in Singapore and overlooks the sea from the north. The 11 Hectares park houses a large playground with a wide range of equipment and a 1.5-kilometre-long waterfront promenade. Central Spine and Green Lawn provides a spacious spots to hold events and picnics. Vantage points located along the trail gives visitors a panoramic view of the entire coastal park and the Straits of Johor. A 25km Northern explorer loop connects Woodlands waterfront park to Admiralty Park along with other park connectors. [1]


History

The park was opened in 2010 at a cost of S$19 million.[2]
