_THE SPORTING INSTINCT_ is a 1922 British silent drama film directed by Arthur Rooke and starring Lilian Douglas, Joseph R. Tozer and Mickey Brantford.[1]


Cast

-   Lilian Douglas as June Crisp
-   Joseph R. Tozer as Jerry West
-   Mickey Brantford as Tony
-   Tom Coventry as The Burglar
-   Betty Chapman as His Wife
-   Somers Bellamy as The Captain
-   Hetty Chapman
-   Vivian Gosnell
-   Howard Symons
-   Billy Vernon
