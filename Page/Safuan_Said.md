SAFUAN SAID (born 1982) is a Malaysian international Lawn bowler.[1]


Bowls career

He won the lawn bowls gold medal in the singles competition at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch, New Zealand and a bronze medal in the pairs with Fairul Izwan Abd Muin.[2] [3]

He has also won five medals at the Asia Pacific Bowls Championships including two singles gold medals in 2005 and 2011.

Indoors he won the 2005 World Junior Cup and also won the gold medal at the 2008 World Cup Singles in Warilla, New South Wales, Australia.[4].
