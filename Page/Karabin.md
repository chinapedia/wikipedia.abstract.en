_KARABIN_ is the fourth studio album by Polish singer Maria Peszek, released in 2016. The album explored such topics as freedom, hatred and individualism. Although it did not match the critical success of its predecessors, having met with mixed reviews,[1][2] it went gold in Poland in just over a month.[3] To promote the album, Peszek performed concerts in Poland, Ireland, England and Czech Republic.
