PARK KUN-BAE (; born October 25, 1948) served as President of the Boy Scouts of Korea, and as a member of the World Scout Committee.

In 1999, Park was awarded the 277th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting,[1][2] as well as the highest distinction of the Scout Association of Japan, the Golden Pheasant Award.[3]
