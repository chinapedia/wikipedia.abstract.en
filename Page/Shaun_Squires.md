SHAUN SQUIRES (born 20 March 1990) is a rugby league footballer who plays for the Dewsbury Rams in the Kingstone Press Championship. He plays as a second row and centre.

Squires has previously played for the Batley Bulldogs and the Sheffield Eagles in the Championship.[1]
