ELIZIANE PEREIRA GAMA FERREIRA (born February 27, 1977) is a Brazilian journalist and politician. She was state deputy in the Legislative Assembly of Maranhão from 2007 to 2015 and federal deputy from 2015 to 2019.[1] Since 2019, Gama serves as a Senator for the state of Maranhão.[2]
