VITALY KOMOGOROV (born 28 August 1991) is a Russian handball player who plays for Tatabánya KC and the Russian national team.[1]

He represented Qatar at the 2019 World Men's Handball Championship.[2][3]


Achievements

-   LIGA NAȚIONALĂ:
    -   _Winner_: 2018, 2019
-   SUPERCUPA ROMÂNIEI:
    -   _Winner_: 2018
-   LNH DIVISION 1:
    -   _Bronze Medalist_: 2016
-   EHF CUP:
    -   _Finalist_: 2016


Individual awards

-   Prosport All-Star Left Back of the Romanian Liga Națională: 2018
-   Gala Premiilor Handbalului Românesc Liga Națională Left Back of the Season: 2019[4]
