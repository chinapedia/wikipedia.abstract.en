_CHIONINIA DELALANDII_ (English: DELALANDE'S SKINK) is a species of skink, a lizard in the family Scincidae. The species is endemic to the Cape Verde Islands.[1][2]


Etymology

The species is named after French naturalist Pierre Antoine Delalande.[3]
