The TAMPERE TUNNEL (also the RANTAVÄYLÄ TUNNEL, ) is a 2,3 kilometre-long road tunnel in Tampere, Finland. It was opened 15 November 2016, six months before scheduled. The Tampere Tunnel is the longest road tunnel in Finland.[1]
