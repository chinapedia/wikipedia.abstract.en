STEPHEN GARETH YOUNG (born May 1955)[1] is a British businessman. He has been the chief executive (CEO) of Meggitt plc, a British engineering business specialising in aerospace equipment, since May 2013.


Early life

Young is a chartered management accountant.[2]


Career

Young was finance director of Meggitt from 2004 to 2013, and became CEO in May 2013.[3][4] In January 2018, he retired and was succeeded by Tony Wood.[5]

Young is a non-executive director of Derwent London.[6][7]
