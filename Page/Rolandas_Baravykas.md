ROLANDAS BARAVYKAS (born 23 August 1995) is a Lithuanian footballer who plays as a defender for A Lyga club FK Žalgiris.


Club career

On 2013 Baravykas moved to FK Atlantas and signed 4 years deal. In this period, Rolandas managed to establish himself as a starting 11 player and appeared more than 100 times for his club. He also was invited into Lithuania national team and made his debut in a friendly match against Malta. He also played in various youth teams. After 2016 season he joined FK Žalgiris.[1]


International career

In May 2015, Baravykas received his first call-up to the senior Lithuania squad for two matches against Hungary and Malta.[2]


Style of play

Baravykas is an universal full-back. His primary position is on the right side, but he also can play on the left.
