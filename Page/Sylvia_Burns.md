SYLVIA BURNS is a South African international lawn bowler.[1]


Bowls career

She won a gold medal in the Women's Triples at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch.[2]

In 2016, she won a bronze medal with Susan Nel and Elma Davis in the triples at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch.[3]

She won the 2017 pairs title at the National Championships bowling for the Edgemead Bowls Club.[4]
