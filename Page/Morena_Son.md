MORENA SON are a Cuban son _septeto_ from Santiago de Cuba, Cuba.[1] They have toured South America and Europe (Italy, England, Germany, Switzerland, Spain and France).[2]


Members

-   Aimeé Campos – tres
-   Aylen Guevara – vocals
-   María Mercedes Soto – percussion, vocals
-   Juana Filiú – bass
-   Niurka Cardona – vocals, maracas
-   Orialis Luna – guitar
-   Deisy Estrada – bongos
