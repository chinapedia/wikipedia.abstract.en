MOHAMED ZAGHLOUL (born August 31, 1993) is an Egyptian freestyle wrestler. He competed in the men's freestyle 86 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated in the round of 16 by Pedro Ceballos.[1][2]
