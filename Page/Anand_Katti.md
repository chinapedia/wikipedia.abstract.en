ANAND KATTI (born 11 July 1972) is a first-class cricketer who played for Karnataka in the Ranji Trophy. He was born in Belgaum, Karnataka, India.[1]

Anand is a right-hand batsman and Slow Left-arm orthodox bowler. He took a hat-trick in the 1988-89 Ranji Trophy playing for Karnataka against Kerala.[2]
