'''Diana Esther Nwaiwu ''' (born 10 October 1973) is a Nigerian footballer who played as a goalkeeper for the Nigeria women's national football team. She was part of the team at the inaugural 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup as well as the 1995 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1] At the club level, she played for Kakanfo Babes in Nigeria.[2]
