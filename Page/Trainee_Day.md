_TRAINEE DAY_ (original title: _MAMAN A TORT_) is a 2016 French-Belgian comedy-drama film written and directed by Marc Fitoussi. It stars Émilie Dequenne and Jeanne Jestin.[1][2][3]


Plot

A 14-year-old discovers a different side of her mother and the workings of the corporate world when she interns at her mother's insurance workplace.
