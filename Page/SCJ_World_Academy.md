_GD Goenka Signature School_ is a modern new school in Gurgaon, India. It is located on Sohna Road, Gurgaon. The school campus is on a 20-acre plot close to the GD Goenka University. The school offers day, weekly and full boarding options for students in the ages 2.5 years to 18 years of age. It was under construction for two years and the first session started in April 2016. The school will be affiliated to CBSE and it offers the Real World Curriculum.[1]
