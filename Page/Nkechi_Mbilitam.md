'''Nkechi Mbilitam ''' (born 5 April 1974) is a Nigerian footballer who played as a midfielder for the Nigeria women's national football team. She was part of the team at the inaugural 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup as well as the 1995 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1]
