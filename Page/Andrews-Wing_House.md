The ANDREWS-WING HOUSE is a historic house located at 733 Main Street in Boonville, Cooper County, Missouri.


Description and history

It was built around 1855 for David Andrews, and is a one-story, vernacular brick dwelling with a variation of a hall and parlor plan. It has a brick ell and two-story frame addition. Also on the property is a contributing rusticated stone garage (c. 1920).[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on March 16, 1990.[2]
