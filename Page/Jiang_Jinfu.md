JIANG JINFU (, born 2 September 1991) is a Chinese actor and model.


Life and career

Jiang was born in Changsha, Hunan, China on 2 September 1991. After graduating from Yali High School, he attended Shanghai Theatre Academy in 2009 and graduated in 2013.[1] Jiang worked as a print model before entering entertainment industry, and one of his most well-known modeling works included the Chinese fiction _The Left Ear_.[2] In 2011, Jiang signed a contract with Tangren Media, and gained massive attention after starring as the leading actor in the 2012 drama _Xuan-Yuan Sword: Scar of Sky_.[3]

In 2015, Jiang embarked a conflict with his agent company Tangren Media and set up his own studio.[4] In 2018, Jiang decided to study abroad in Japan, and suspended his acting career.[5]


Controversy

On November 20, 2018, Jiang was revealed to have engaged in domestic abuse toward his ex-girlfriend through a self-admittance post on Weibo.[6][7]

On November 28, police in Japan issued an arrest warrant for Jiang, who was suspected of crime of intentional injury and domestic violence.[8] On November 30, Jiang turned himself in to the police.[9]

On January 9, 2019, Jiang was released from police custody in Japan, and the domestic violence charges against him were dropped.[10]
