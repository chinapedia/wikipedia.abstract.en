CUMMEEN COURT CAIRN is a court cairn and National Monument located in County Sligo, Ireland.[1][2]


Location

Cummeen Court Cairn is located west of Sligo town centre, to the south of Cummeen Strand.[3]


History

This court cairn was built c. 4000â€“2500 BC, in the Neolithic.[4][5]


Description

Cummeen Court Cairn is a central court tomb with the court removed, standing on a raised earth platform up to above field level. Two galleries, aligned east-west, face each other apart. The east gallery was divided into two chambers and is long. The west gallery is long.[6][7]
