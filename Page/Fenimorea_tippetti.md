_FENIMOREA TIPPETTI_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of this marine shell varies between 18 mm and 22 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs in the Caribbean Sea off Quintana Roo, Yucátan Peninsula, Mexico
