Dr. FAYEQ HAMDI TAHBOUB () serves as the International Commissioner of the Palestinian Scout Association.[1][2][3]

In 1998, Tahboub was awarded the 267th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.
