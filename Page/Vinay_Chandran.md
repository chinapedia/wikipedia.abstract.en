VINAY CHANDRAN is a gay rights and human rights activist, a counselor, a writer based in Bangalore. Vinay Chandran had also given a TED Talk on Homosexuality in NIT Trichy.[1] He also runs a free helpline for counseling to LGBTQ individuals called Sahaya Helpline. He has written a book from his experience as a counselor and many articles as well in the areas of Queer and Human rights.[2][3][4][5][6][7][8]


Activism

Chandran continues to serve as the Director of the Bangalore Queer Film Festival (BQFF)[9] since its inception in 2008.
