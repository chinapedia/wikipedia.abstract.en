KOREAN INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL IN HANOI (KISH; ; ) is a Korean international school in Cầu Giấy District, Hanoi, Vietnam.[1]

it had about 1,000 students.[2] It serves elementary school, junior school, and senior school.[3]


History

It opened in 2006. Originally it had 52 students, all in the elementary level.[4]
