_HELLO, MY NAME IS FRANK_ is a 2014 American comedy drama film written and directed by Dale Peterson and starring Garrett M. Brown and Rachel DiPillo.[1]


Cast

-   Garrett M. Brown as Frank Brown
-   Rachel DiPillo as Laura
-   Mary Kate Wiles as Kim
-   Hayley Kiyoko as Alisa
-   Travis Caldwell as Vinnie
-   Kitty Swink as Louise
-   Ray Xifo as Ray
-   James DuMont as Dad in Park
-   Nate Hartley as John
-   Wayne Duvall as Preacher
-   Brent Briscoe as Puggis
-   Tess Harper as Aunt Flossie


Reception

The film has an Audience Score of 91% on Rotten Tomatoes.[2]


Accolades

At the Manhattan Film Festival, the film won the Best Dramatic Feature Award and Brown garnered the Best Actor Award.[3]
