SEYYED MAHMOUD ALIZADEH-TABATABAEI () is an Iranian lawyer. According to the _New York Times_, he is "one of the most influential" and "well connected" lawyers to highest leaders in Iran.[1] He was a member of City Council of Tehran fRom 1999 to 2003.

He is well known as one of the attorneies for the Hashemi Rafsanjani family.[2] Alizadeh-Tabatabaei has also represented Mir-Hossein Mousavi,[3] Zahra Rahnavard,[4] Amir Hekmati,[5] Saeed Malekpour,[6] Ghoncheh Ghavami[7] and Mohammad Ali Taheri[8] among others.
