_' Mesa del Caballo_' is a census-designated place (CDP) in Gila County, Arizona, United States. The population was 765 at the 2010 census.[1]


Geography

The CDP is located in northern Gila County atop Houston Mesa, a low ridge north of Payson. The center of Payson is south via Houston Mesa Road and Arizona State Route 87. According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of , all of it land.[2]
