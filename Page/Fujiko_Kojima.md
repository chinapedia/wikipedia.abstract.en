[1] is a Japanese actress, fashion model, and _tarento_.

Kojima is represented with Asia Business Partners.


Biography

-   While at sixth-grade elementary school, Kojima was scouted in a rental video store by her agency while selecting an animated video with her father.[2]
-   In September 2006 she became the exclusive model for the _Nico Puchi_'s first issue. Kojima later moved to _Love Berry_.
-   In April 2007 she made regular appearances as an Oha Girl in _Oha Suta_ along with Nozomi Maeda and Serina Nagano.
-   In April 2008 Kojima made her acting debut in the drama _Kimi Hannin janai yo ne?_ as the sister of Shihori Kanjiya's character.[3]
-   In 2009 her first lead role in a film was _Oppai Valley_. Kojima later played the sub-protagonist Maria in the drama _Shōkōjo Seira_.
-   In 2010 she made leading roles in the films _Tomehane! Suzuri Kōkō Shodōbu_ and _Shodo Girls_ and continue to use her calligraphy skills.
-   In 2011 Kojima's first leading role in a television drama was _Asu no Hikari o Tsukame 2_.[4]
