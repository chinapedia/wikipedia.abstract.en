This is a '''list of international co-produced Philippine films and television series '''.

The first film co-produced by a Philippine film studio with a studio based outside the Philippines is _Rodrigo de Villa_. The film was produced with Indonesian film outfit Persari.[1]

Excluded are:

-   Sole Philippine productions
    -   Such as works with a foreign cast such as _Ignacio de Loyola_ which had primarily Spanish actors but was produced only by a Philippine-based studio.
    -   Works which were adaptation of foreign media.
-   Media produced solely by foreign production companies such as _Metro Manila_ set in the Philippines and included Filipinos in its cast but was solely by British Studio, Chocolate Frog Films.
