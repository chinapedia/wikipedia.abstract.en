ORIANA SCHEUSS (born February 7, 1972 in Teufen, Appenzell Ausserrhoden) is a Swiss sport shooter. She competed at the 2000 Summer Olympics in the women's 50 metre rifle three positions event, in which she tied for 17th place, and the women's 10 metre air rifle event, in which she tied for 36th place.[1]
