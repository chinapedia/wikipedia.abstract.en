_ANGOPHORA MELANOXYLON_ is a tree species that is native to eastern Australia.[1]

The multi-stemmed shrub to medium-sized tree typically grows to a height of and forms a lignotuber. It has fibrous, grey to grey-brown bark that persists to the smaller branches. Adult leaves are opposite and a discolorous dull grey green to green.[2]
