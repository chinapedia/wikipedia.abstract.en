Lillehammer_2016_-_Speed_skating_Ladies'_500m_race_1_-_Mei_Han.jpg HAN MEI (; ; born 27 January 1998)is a Chinese speed skater.


Biography

At the 2016 Youth Olympic Games in Lillehammer, Han won three silver medals.[1] She competed at the ISU Junior Speed Skating World Cup and won the silver medal in the Women's 1000m category.[2] She competed in the 2018 Winter Olympics in the Women's team pursuit.
