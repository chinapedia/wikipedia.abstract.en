MARKO STOUT is an American contemporary artist known for his gritty industrial pop style with a focus on New York City and modern urban life. Stout produces work in a variety of media including print, painting, sculpture, film, video, photography and new media.[1][2]

Mark Stout is also known for the INTERNATIONAL "EDIBLE APPLE FILM FESTIVAL" in downtown NYC with the sole aim of promoting innovative avant-garde art works and experimental film which was founded in 2015 by Stout.[3][4]

Stout was featured on The Huffington Post after his tour with his art installation _Chelsea Girls_.[5][6]
