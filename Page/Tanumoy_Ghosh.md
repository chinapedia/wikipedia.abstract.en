TANUMOY GHOSH (born 10 December 1987) is a Bangladeshi first-class cricketer who plays for Rajshahi Division.[1] In 2017, he was given a five-year ban by the Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB), in his role of a team losing a match on purpose in the Dhaka Second Division Cricket League.[2]
