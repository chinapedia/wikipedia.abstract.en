"BUCK WILD" is a 1989 single released by the Washington, D.C.-based go-go band Experience Unlimited.[1] The song was released as the first single from their 1989 album _Livin' Large_.[2] The single peaked at #7 on Billboard's "Hot R&B/Hip-Hop songs" chart, on May 13, 1989.[3]
