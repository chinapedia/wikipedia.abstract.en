CABRAGH WEDGE TOMB, also called CABRAGH I or the GIANT'S GRAVE, is a wedge-shaped gallery grave and National Monument located in County Sligo, Ireland.[1][2]


Location

Cabragh Wedge Tomb is located west of Coolaney in the foothills of the Ox Mountains. A tributary of the Owenbeg River flows to the west.[3][4][5]

This wedge tomb was built c. 2500â€“2000 BC, in the Copper or Bronze Age. The entrance faces southwest, towards the setting sun at the winter solstice.[6][7]


Description

The tomb is long with double outer walling and entrance stones. There are two burial chambers and long, with the roofstones collapsed inward.[8]
