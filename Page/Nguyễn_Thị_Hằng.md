NGUYỄN THỊ HUYÊN (, 1441 - 1505) was a queen consort of Later Lê dynasty. She was the wife of emperor Lê Thánh Tông and mother of emperor Lê Hiến Tông[1].


Biography

Empress Nguyễn Thị Huyên was born in 1441 at Gia Miêu outer village, Tống Sơn district, Hà Trung local government, Thanh Hoa town. She was the daughter of the Duke Nguyễn Công Lộ and foster daughter of the officer Nguyễn Đức Trung. She has entered Lê dynasty's palace on July 1460 with rank _Sung-nghi_ (充儀), lived at the palace of Vĩnh Ninh.

By one researching of scholar Trần Quang Đức on 3 March 2016, emperor Lê Thánh Tông has been died by empress Nguyễn Thị Huyên in the handjob.[2]
