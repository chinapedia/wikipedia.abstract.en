SUN CLUB was an American rock band from Baltimore, Maryland. They were signed to ATO Records.[1][2]


History

Sun Club formed in 2015, releasing their debut full-length album on October 30th of that year, titled _The Dongo Durango_.[3] [4][5][6][7][8][9] In April 2017, Sun Club announced via Facebook that they were breaking up.[10]


Band members

-   Mikey Powers – vocals/guitar[11]
-   Adam Shane – bass guitar[12]
-   Devin McCord – drum/snare&toms[13]
-   Kory Albert Johnson – floor toms/percussion/keyboard/vocal[14]
-   Shane Justice McCord – keyboard/glockenspiel/guitar/vocals[15]
