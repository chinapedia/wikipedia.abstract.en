is a greatest hits album by Japanese pop band Dreams Come True. It was released on July 7, 2016.[1] It was number-one on the _Oricon_ weekly Albums Chart on its release,[2] with 108,797 copies sold.[3] It was the third best-selling album in Japan in July 2016, with 162,968 copies sold.[4] It was also number-one on the _Billboard Japan_ Top Album Sales chart[5] and number-two on the _Billboard Japan_ Hot Albums chart.[6]
