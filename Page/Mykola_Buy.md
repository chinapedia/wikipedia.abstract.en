MYKOLA BUY (; born 22 May 1996 in Lviv Oblast, Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder.


Career

Buy is a product of the FC Lviv youth sportive school system. He played in the different amateur teams in Lviv Oblast, but in July 2016 signed a contract with the Ukrainian First League club FC Illichivets.[1]

He made his debut for FC Illichivets in a game against FC Obolon-Brovar Kyiv on 26 August 2016 in the Ukrainian First League.[2]
