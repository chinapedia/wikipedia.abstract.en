Victoria Justice is an American singer and actress. Her music career started in 2011 when she contributed vocals to the soundtrack albums for the American TV sitcom, _Victorious_, in which she also starred from 2010 to 2013. Three soundtrack albums were released between 2011 and 2012. In 2007 Justice also began to work as an independent artist outside of _Victorious_. Her debut single "A Thousand Miles", a pop song, was released the same year.
