MARIE-CHARLOTTE LÉGER (born 13 March 1996) is a French women's footballer who plays for Montpellier in the Division 1 Féminine[1] and France national team.


Career

International career

Marie-Charlotte Léger has represented France at youth levels since 2012.[2] She made her international debut on 19 September 2015 in a friendly match against Brazil as a substitute in the 92nd minute.[3]


Honours

Hénin-Beaumont

-   Division 2 Féminine Winner: 2013[4]
