SEAN GOLDBERG(born 13 June 1995) is an Israeli football Defender who plays for Hapoel Be'er Sheva and a member of the Israel national under-21 football team.[1]


Club career

Born in 1995, Goldberg started his football career with Maccabi Tel Aviv youth team. On 1 July 2014, he was called up for Maccabi Tel Aviv football team.[2] On 26 October 2014, Goldberg made his professional league debut against Hapoel Acre at Acre Municipal Stadium, replacing Sheran Yeini at the 75th minute.[3] On 3 February 2015, Goldberg was loaned to Hapoel TA.[4] In the team, Goldberg played nine games. In the 2014/2015 season, Maccabi Tel Aviv achieved double : league and league cup. On 1 September 2015, Goldberg moved to Bnei Yehuda on loan until the end of 2016-17 season.[5]

On May 22, 2018 Goldberg left Beitar.


International

Goldberg has represented his country at various age groups. Since March 2015, he has been member of Israel national under-21 football team.
