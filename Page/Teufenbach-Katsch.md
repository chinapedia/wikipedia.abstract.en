TEUFENBACH-KATSCH is a municipality since 2015 in the Murau District of Styria, Austria.

It was created as part of the Styria municipal structural reform,[1] at the end of 2014, by merging the former towns Teufenbach and Frojach-Katsch.[2]


Geography

Municipality arrangement

The municipality territory includes the following three hamlets (populations as of January 2015[3]):

-   Frojach (572)
-   Katsch an der Mur (593)
-   Teufenbach (692)

The municipality consists of the three Katastralgemeinden: Frojach, Katsch und Teufenbach.
