DONALD RAPO (born 4 October 1990) is an Albanian football player who plays as a defender for Luftëtari Gjirokastër in Albanian Superliga.[1][2][3]


Club career

Burreli

In August 2014, Rapo moved to Albanian First Division club KS Burreli. He made his league debut for the club on 27 September 2014 in a 1-0 away loss to Besëlidhja Lezhë. He played all ninety minutes of the match, receiving a yellow card in the 77th minute.[4] He scored his first league goal for the club on 13 December 2014 in a match against the same side he made his debut against, Besëlidhja Lezhë. The match ended 2-1 in favor of Burreli. His goal, scored in the 85th minute, made the score 2-1. He played all ninety minutes of the match.[5]

Luftëtari Gjirokastër

In Summer 2015, Rapo moved to then Albanian First Division side Luftëtari Gjirokastër. He made his league debut for the club on 12 September 2015 in a 2-0 away victory over FK Dinamo Tirana. He was subbed on for Mustafa Agastra in the 67th minute.[6] He scored his first league goal for the club came after their promotion to the Albanian Superliga. It came on 22 December 2016 in a 2-2 home draw with FK Kukësi. His goal, scored in the 88th minute, made the score 2-2. He was subbed off three minutes later, being replaced by Brixhild Brahimaj.[7]
