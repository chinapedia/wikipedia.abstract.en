_MY SECRET APP_ () is a Singaporean Chinese drama series which is telecast on Singapore's free-to-air channel, Starhub TV. It stars DAMIEN TEO, Fann Wong , Darren Lim & Hayley Woo as the main casts in the series. It is shown in 都会 Channel.
