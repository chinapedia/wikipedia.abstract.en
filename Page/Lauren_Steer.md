LAUREN STEER (born 17 March 1997) is an Australian soccer player who last played for Adelaide United in the Australian W-League. She previously played for Metro United and scored the game-winning goal at the 2015 Women's Premier Cup Final.[1]


Playing career

Club

Adelaide United, 2014–2016

Steer signed with Adelaide United in September 2014 at the age of 17.[2][3] She made her debut on 14 September 2014 in a match against Sydney FC.[4] She made six appearances for the team during the 2014–15 W-League season. Adelaide finished in seventh place during the regular season with a record.[5][6] She returned to the squad for the 2015–16 W-League season and helped the team finish in fifth place during the regular season with a record. Steer made twelve appearances during the season.[7]
