The UNITED ARAB EMIRATES NATIONAL UNDER-19 BASKETBALL TEAM is a national basketball team of the United Arab Emirates, administered by the United Arab Emirates Basketball Association.[1] ()

It represents the country in international under-18 and under-19 (under age 18 and under age 19) basketball competitions.
