SELAHATTIN ÇOBANOĞLU (born 20 August 1985 in Mersin) is a Turkish sprinter and middle distance runner. He usually competed at the 800 metres events.

Çobanoglu represented his country at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece. He was eliminated in the first round of at the 800 metres competition.[1] He won a silver medal at the 2005 Summer Universiade held in their country.[2]

Çobanoğlu is the owner of Turkey record at the 600 metres with 1:17.23.[3]

His brother Utku Çobanoglu is the sprint and middle distance runner like himself.[4]
