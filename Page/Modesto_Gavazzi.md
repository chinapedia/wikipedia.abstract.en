Biography

Modesto Gavazzi was ordained a priest in the Order of Friars Minor Conventual.[2][3] On 7 August 1598, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VIII as Bishop of Alife.[4][5] He served as Bishop of Alife until his death in 1608.[6][7]
