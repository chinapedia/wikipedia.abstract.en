SLAUGHTER BRANCH is a stream in northern Franklin County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary to Brushy Creek.[2]

The headwaters of the stream are at and the confluence with Brushy Creek is at .[3] The source area for the stream lies just south of Campbellton and Missouri Route 185.[4]

Slaughter Branch most likely derives its name from George Slaughterback, a pioneer citizen.[5]
