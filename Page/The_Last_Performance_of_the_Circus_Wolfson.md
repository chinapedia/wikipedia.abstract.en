_THE LAST PERFORMANCE OF THE CIRCUS WOLFSON_ (German: _DIE LETZTE GALAVORSTELLUNG DES ZIRKUS WOLFSON_) is a 1928 German silent film directed by Domenico Gambino and starring Hermann Vallentin.[1]

The film's art direction was by Willi Herrmann and Fritz Willi Krohn.
