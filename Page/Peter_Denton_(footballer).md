PETER ROBERT DENTON (1 March 1946 – 6 October 2016)[1] was an English footballer who played as a winger. Denton played in the Football League for Coventry City and Luton Town.[2]

Denton died on 6 October 2016 after a sudden worsening from a heart condition.[3]
