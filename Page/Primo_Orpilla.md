Career

Primo Orpilla is the interior designer of the offices of large technology companies in Silicon Valley including Facebook, Microsoft, AOL, Samsung, Uber, Yelp, and Giant Pixel. Other companies including Alibaba Group, TATA, Levi Strauss, Evernote and Cambridge Associates have also used his services.[4]

In 2015, he extended his activities to finance and contracting firms looking for improvement of their work amenities.[5]
