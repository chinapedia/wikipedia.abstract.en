NIDIO RICARDO FERREIRA ALVES (born 17 June 1994 ) or RICKY is a football player who currently plays for Timor-Leste national football team as a forward.[1][2]


International career

Ricky made his senior international debut is in 2-0 lost against Cambodia national football team in the friendly match on 29 May 2016.
