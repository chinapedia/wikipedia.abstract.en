_GUDEODISCUS FRANCOISI_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Plectopylidae.

This species was described by French zoologist Pierre Marie Henri Fischer (1865-1916) (son of Paul-Henri Fischer) in 1898.


Distribution

The distribution of _Gudeodiscus francoisi_ includes Vietnam.[1]


Ecology

It is a ground-dwelling species as all other plectopylid snails in Vietnam.[2]

It co-occur with other plectopylids in Vietnam: with _Gudeodiscus anceyi_, _Gudeodiscus giardi_.[3] _Gudeodiscus phlyarius_ and _Gudeodiscus suprafilaris_ live at geographically close sites to _Gudeodiscus francoisi_.[4]
