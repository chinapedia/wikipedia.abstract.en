OLAF BERTRAM LØHRE (born 31 December 1877) was a Norwegian politician.

He was born in Strinda to farmer and teacher Johan Peter Eriksen Løhre and Karen Nicoline Nilsdatter. He was elected representative to the Storting for the period 1922–1924, for the Labour Party, and the period 1928–1930, for the Communist Party.[1]
