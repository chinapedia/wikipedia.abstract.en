_MIKOSCH COMES IN_ (German: _MIKOSCH RÜCKT EIN_) is a 1952 West German comedy film directed by Johann Alexander Hübler-Kahla and starring Georg Thomalla, Willy Fritsch and Paul Hörbiger.[1]
