GOH YEA CHING (born 19 June 1996) is a Malaysian badminton player.[1][2]


Career

In 2014, she won Romanian International tournament in women's doubles event.[3] In 2016, she won Portugal International and became the runner-up of Romanian International tournament.[4][5]
