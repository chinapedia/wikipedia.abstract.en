MILAN KVOCERA (born 1 January 1998) is a Slovak football forward who currently plays for Fortuna Liga club AS Trenčín.


Club career

AS Trenčín

Kvocera made his professional Fortuna Liga debut for AS Trenčín against Slovan Bratislava on 24 September 2016. Kvocera came on in the 46th minute, replacing Aliko Bala. Trenčín won the game 2:1.[1]
