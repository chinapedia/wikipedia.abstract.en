SUSHIL SINGH () is an Indian politician and a member of the 17th Legislative Assembly of Uttar Pradesh of India. He represents the Saiyad Raja Assembly constituency in Chandauli district of Uttar Pradesh and is a member of the Bhartiya Janta Party.[1][2] [3][4]


Early life and education

Sushil Singh was born in Varanasi in 1976. Prior to entering politics, he was a Bank Manager by profession.


Political career

Sushil Singh has been MLA for three terms. He is a member of the Bhartiya Janta Party . During his previous terms, he represented Dhanapur and Sakaldiha assembly constituency respectively, [5]
