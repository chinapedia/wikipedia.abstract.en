CLINTON is an unincorporated community in Phelps County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Variant names were "Clinton Bank" and "Clinton Mine".[2] The community was named after Jake Clinton, the proprietor of a local mine.[3] The community once contained the Clinton Bank School, now defunct.[4]
