DIBYENDU ADHIKARI is an Indian politician from the state of West Bengal.

In 2016, he was elected to Lok Sabha from Tamluk parliamentary constituency of West Bengal on TMC ticket in a bye-election when the seat fell vacant due to the resignation of the sitting MP Suvendu Adhikari. He was the Member of Legislative Assembly(MLA) from Dakhin Kanthi constituency of West Bengal from 2009-2016. He is also councillor of Contai Municipality. [1][2]
