SOHUM SHAH (b. [1]) is an author, blogger and entrepreneur. He is a University of Arizona alumnus, where he studied finance and information systems.[2][3]

In 2014, he started Cannabis Commodities Exchange, a commodities exchange, in Colorado.[4][5][6][7]

he contributed cannabis strain reviews to _The Cannabist_, a specialist outlet operated by _The Denver Post_.[8][9]
