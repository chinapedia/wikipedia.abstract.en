"CATCH 22" is a song by Australian rapper Illy featuring English singer, Anne-Marie. The song was released on 10 October 2016, alongside the announcement of his forthcoming fifth studio album, _Two Degrees_.

Music video

The "Catch 22" video was released on 6 November 2016.[4] It is set in a previously dead video game arcade where a group of friends find a game called _Catch 22_, allowing them to test their fate.[5]
