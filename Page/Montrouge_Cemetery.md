The MONTROUGE CEMETERY is a cemetery belonging to the town of Montrouge, but geographically located in the south of the 14th arrondissement of Paris, bordering the boulevard périphérique.

While the most visited grave is that of Coluche, there are many other graves of interest[1] and a crypt.
