MICHAEL BOSO (born 3 September 1991) is a Solomon Islands footballer who plays as a defender for Suva F.C. in Fiji. He made his debut for the national team on October 5, 2016, in a 3-0 loss against New Caledonia.


International

Boso was named in the Solomon Islands national squad for the first time for the 2016 OFC Nations Cup but he didn't made the final squad. He made his debut four months later in match against New Caledonia.
