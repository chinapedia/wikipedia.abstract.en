MOREAU DRIVE HISTORIC DISTRICT is a national historic district located at Jefferson City, Cole County, Missouri. It encompasses 252 contributing buildings and 3 contributing structures in a predominantly residential section of Jefferson City. The district developed between about 1847 and 1950, and includes representative examples of Tudor Revival, Colonial Revival, Bungalow / American Craftsman, and Modern Movement style architecture.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2013.[2]
