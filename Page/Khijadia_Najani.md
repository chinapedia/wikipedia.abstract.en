KHIJADIA(-)NAJANI is a village and former non-salute princely state on Saurashtra peninsula in Gujarat, western India.


History

The petty princely state, in Sorath _prant_, was ruled by Kathi Chieftains.

In 1901 it comprised a single village, with a population of 156, yielding 2,800 Rupees state revenue (1903-4, mostly from land), paying 52 tribute to the Gaikwar Baroda State. [1]
