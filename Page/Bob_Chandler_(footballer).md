ROBERT WALTER CHANDLER (September 1894 – 1964) was an English professional football goalkeeper who appeared in the Football League for Aston Villa.[1]


Personal life

Chandler attested as an Air Mechanic 2nd Class in the Royal Air Force during the latter months of the First World War.[2] He worked as a fitter.[3]
