Arbeiterwille_Banner_1890-11-26.png _ARBEITERWILLE_ was social democratic paper published in Graz, Styria from 1890 to 1934.

_ARBEITERWILLE_ first appeared on 9 July 1890 and continued to be published until 12 February 1934 when the defeat of the Schutzbund in the Austrian Civil War led to its suppression.
