DISNAKA MANOJ (born 13 January 1997) is a Sri Lankan cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Tamil Union Cricket and Athletic Club in the 2016–17 Premier League Tournament on 2 December 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Jaffna District in the 2016–17 Districts One Day Tournament on 25 March 2017.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Tamil Union Cricket and Athletic Club in the 2017–18 SLC Twenty20 Tournament on 27 February 2018.[4]
