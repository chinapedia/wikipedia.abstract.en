ERNEST MEHINTO (-‡2002 to 2005[1]) was a veterinarian and a leader of Scoutisme Dahoméen who served as the Chairman of the Africa Scout Committee.

In 1977, Mehinto was awarded the 109th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2]
