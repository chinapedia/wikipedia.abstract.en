_ERIC BENÉT_ is the eponymous seventh studio album by American R&B recording artist Eric Benét. It was released on October 7, 2016[1] and is Benét's first major album release in four years following the release of the compilations "The Other One" and the Japan exclusive album "From E to U: Volume 1". The project features guest appearances from Tamia, Arturo Sandoval and MC Lyte. The album was supported by the single "Sunshine" and the remix of the track featuring Tamia.

The album debuted on the Billboard charts at #7 on R&B, and #12 on R&B/Hip Hop Albums.[2]
