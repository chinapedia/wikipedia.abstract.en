TOMÁS DE MONTERROSO, O.P. (died 1678) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Antequera, Oaxaca (1664–1678).


Biography

Tomás de Monterroso was ordained a priest in the Order of Preachers.[1] On 23 June 1664, Tomás de Monterroso was appointed during the papacy of Pope Alexander VII as Bishop of Antequera, Oaxaca.[2] He served as Bishop of Antequera, Oaxaca until his death on 26 January 1678.[3]
