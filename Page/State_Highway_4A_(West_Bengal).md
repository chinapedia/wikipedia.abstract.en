STATE HIGHWAY 4A (WEST BENGAL) is a state highway in West Bengal, India.


Route

SH 4A originates from Tulin and passes through Jhalda and Kotshila and terminates at Chas Morh.[1]

The total length of SH 4A is 39 km.[2]

The only district traversed by SH 4A is:[3]
Purulia district (0 - 39 km)
