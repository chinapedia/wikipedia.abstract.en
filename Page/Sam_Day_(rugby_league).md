SAM DAY (born 12 June 1994) is an English rugby league footballer who plays as a for the Dewsbury Rams in the Betfred Championship.


Background

Day was born in Wakefield, West Yorkshire, England.


Career

He previously played for Featherstone Rovers,[1] but after a loan period with Dewsbury in 2017 he joined them on a permanent deal for the 2018 season.[2]
