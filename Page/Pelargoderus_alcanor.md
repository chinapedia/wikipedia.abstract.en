_PELARGODERUS ALCANOR_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It is known from Newman in 1842, originally under the genus _Monohammus_. It is known from Sulawesi and the Philippines.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Pelargoderus alcanor alcanor_ (Newman, 1842)
-   _Pelargoderus alcanor thomsoni_ Aurivillius, 1921
