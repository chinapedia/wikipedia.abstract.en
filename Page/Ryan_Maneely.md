RYAN MANEELY (born 19 October 1994) is a Scottish[1] rugby league footballer who plays as a for the Rochdale Hornets in the Betfred Championship.[2]

Maneely has previously played for Saddleworth Rangers and been in the systems of the Warrington Wolves, and spent time on loan at the Swinton Lions[3] and Rochdale Hornets.[4][5]

In October, Maneely was named in Scotland's 2016 Four Nations squad.[6]
