EDMUNDO FLORES MERCADO, JR.[1] is a Filipino footballer.

He previously played for the Philippines national team as a goalkeeper.[2] He was part of the squad that played at the 2002 ASEAN Football Championship.[3] In 2011, Mercado was playing as a goalkeeper for Philippine Air Force F.C. at the UFL Cup.[4]

Mercado was also part of the Philippines national beach soccer team that participated at the Asian qualiers of the 2013 FIFA Beach Soccer World Cup.[5]
