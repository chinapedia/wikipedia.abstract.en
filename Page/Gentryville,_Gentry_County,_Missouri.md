GENTRYVILLE is an unincorporated community in Gentry County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Gentry was platted in 1848, taking its name from Gentry County.[2] A post office called Gentryville was established in 1846, and remained in operation until 1938.[3]
