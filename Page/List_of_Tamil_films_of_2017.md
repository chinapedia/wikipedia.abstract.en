This is a LIST OF TAMIL LANGUAGE FILMS produced in the Tamil cinema in India that were released in 2017.


Box office collection prior to GST

Post-amendment to the Tamil Nadu Entertainment Tax Act 1939 on 27th September 2011, Gross jumped to 130 per cent of Nett for films with non-Tamil titles and U certificates as well.[1] Commercial Taxes Department disclosed 36.48 crore in entertainment tax revenue for the year[2].

Box office collection post-GST

Post-GST launch on 1st July 2017, Gross jumped to 137.44 per cent of Nett for all films[6][7]. State Legislature passed the _Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Entertainment Tax Act_ to repeal the Act of 1939. According to Ormax Media industry report, the Tamil film segment registered domestic nett box office receipts of ₹1,191 crore[8].
