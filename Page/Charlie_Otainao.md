CHARLIE OTAINAO (born 5 June 1992) is a Solomon Islander footballer who plays as a midfielder for Fijian Club Rewa F.C., He Moved to Rewa F.C. from Kossa F.C. of Solomon Islands S-League.[1][2] in 2018.
