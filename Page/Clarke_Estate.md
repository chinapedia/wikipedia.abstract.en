The CLARKE ESTATE is a historic mansion in Santa Fe Springs, California, U.S.. It was built from 1919 to 1921 for Chauncey Clarke and his wife, Marie Rankin Clarke.[1] It was designed by architect Irving Gill.[2] It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since January 4, 1990.[3]
