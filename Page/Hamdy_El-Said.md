HAMDY EL-SAID (born January 22, 1993) is an Egyptian Greco-Roman wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 98 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated in the round of 16 by Alin Alexuc-Ciurariu.[1][2]
