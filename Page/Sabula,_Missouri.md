SABULA is an unincorporated community in Iron County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Sabula was originally called Reynolds, and under the latter name was platted in 1873 when the railroad was extended to that point.[2] A post office called Sabula was established in 1884, and remained in operation until 1953.[3]
