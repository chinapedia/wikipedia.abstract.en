MANGALORE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (MUDA) is a city planning authority responsible for planning functions of the Indian city of Mangalore in Karnataka state.[1]

Deputy commissioner of Dakshina Kannada holds the chairman position of MUDA.[2]


History

The _"Mangalore Town Planning Authority"_ was established in 29 Nov 1965 , later the organization was reestablished with the present name in the year 1988 by the Government of Karnataka under the Karnataka Urban Development Authority.[3]
