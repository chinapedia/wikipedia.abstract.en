SYLPHIDE CHARITY VAIGNCOURT-STRALLEN (born 1 November 1990), known professionally as ZIZI STRALLEN, is a British actress, singer and dancer best known for playing Mary Poppins in the Cameron Mackintosh theatrical production of _Mary Poppins_. [1]


Personal life

She has three sisters, Scarlett Strallen, Summer Strallen and Saskia 'Sasi' Strallen, all of whom are actresses. Her sister Scarlett also previously played Mary Poppins during the Broadway, West End and Sydney production of _Mary Poppins_.[2] She is the daughter of Sandy Strallen and Cherida Langford.[3] She is also the niece of Broadway and television actress Bonnie Langford.[4]


Career

Strallen appeared Off West End in the Menier Chocolate Factory production of _Merrily We Roll Along_ as Meg Kincaid in 2013.[5]

Strallen played the role of Young Phyllis in _Follies_ in the West End at the National Theatre from September 2017 to November 2017.[6][7]

She appeared as Fran in the musical _Strictly Ballroom_ in the West End in March 2018 at the Piccadilly Theatre. The _Standard_ reviewer wrote: "The best news, by far, is Strallen, who exudes a lightness and brightness even when a mere face in the dance studio crowd early on. Her acting and dancing is impeccable..."[8][9]

She will star in the West End revival of _Mary Poppins_, which is scheduled to start in autumn of 2019 at the Prince Edward Theatre.[10] She had previously played the role of Mary Poppins in the UK and Ireland tour of the musical starting in October 2015.[11]

It has recently been announced that Zizi has spent the last 6 months filming for the new Universal Pictures and Working Title's film adaption of the musical 'Cats', playing the role of Tantomile. Cats is set for release December 2019 and stars Jennifer Hudson, Judi Dench, Francesca Hayward and Ian McKellen.[12]
