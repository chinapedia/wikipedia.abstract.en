KAMOUNIA (), sometimes spelled KAMOUNEYA,[1] is a beef and liver stew prepared with cumin.[2][3] It is a part of Sudanese cuisine and Tunisian cuisine.[4] Lamb is also sometimes used as a primary ingredient,[5] and additional spices are sometimes used.[6] It is sometimes served with or atop cooked rice.[7] Additional basic ingredients can include broth, garlic, olive oil and parsley.[8]
