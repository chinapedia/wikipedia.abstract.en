DUBOIS CREEK is a stream in Franklin County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary to the Missouri River.

The headwaters arise at and the confluence with the Missouri is at .[2] The stream source area is along the east side of Missouri Route A between Union and Krakow. The stream flows northeast passing under Route 47 and then north passing under Route 100 to enter the Missouri floodplain just east of Washington.[3]

Dubois Creek most likely was named after Louis Dubois, an early French frontiersman.[4][5]
