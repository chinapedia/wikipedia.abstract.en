LENKA KROBOTOVÁ (born 10 March 1977) is a Czech actress. She won the Czech Lion award for Best Supporting Actress in 2014 for her role in the film _Díra u Hanušovic_.[1] She is the daughter of actor and director Miroslav Krobot and the actress Hana Doulová.[2]
