THOMAS RICHARD LOWERY (born 1997) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Crewe Alexandra.


Career

A graduate of the Crewe Alexandra Academy, Lowery signed his first professional contract with the club in May 2016.[1] He made his Crewe debut on 4 October 2016, coming on as a second-half substitute in a Football League Trophy tie against Wolverhampton Wanderers U23s at Gresty Road.[2] He made his league debut four days later, coming on at half-time against Luton Town at Kenilworth Road on 8 October 2016,[3] and a week later was in Crewe's starting line-up at Notts County.

In January 2018, Lowery signed a new three-and-a-half year contract at Crewe.[4] He scored his first goal for Crewe in a 2-0 defeat of Yeovil Town at Gresty Road on 19 April 2019,[5] in his 50th League appearance.
