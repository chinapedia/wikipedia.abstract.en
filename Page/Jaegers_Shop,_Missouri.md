JAEGERS SHOP is an unincorporated community in Franklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. [1]


History

Variant names were "Cedar Fork", Cedarfork", and "Jaeger".[2] The name Cedar Fork was after nearby Cedar Fork creek, while Jaeger was the name of William F. Jaeger, a local blacksmith.[3] A post office called Cedar Fork was established in 1858, and remained in operation until 1907.[4]
