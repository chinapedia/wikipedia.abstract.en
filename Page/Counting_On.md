_COUNTING ON_ (formerly _JILL & JESSA: COUNTING ON_) is an American reality television show that has aired on the cable channel TLC since 2015. A spin-off show of _19 Kids and Counting_, it features the Duggar family: Jessa Seewald, Jinger Vuolo, Joy-Anna Forsyth, fourteen of their sixteen siblings (Josh Duggar and Jill Dillard are not featured on the show: Josh from the beginning and Jill since 2017) as well as parents Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar. The show was created in the wake of the Josh Duggar molestation controversy and subsequent cancellation of _19 Kids and Counting_.[1]

The show follows the lives of the older Duggar children, with the early episodes focusing specifically on Jill Dillard and Jessa Seewald. After its first season, the show expanded to include the rest of the Duggar children and their respective families. Notable events throughout the show have included the weddings of Jinger to Jeremy Vuolo, Joy-Anna to Austin Forsyth, Joseph to Kendra Caldwell, Josiah to Lauren Swanson, and John-David to Abbie Burnett as well as the pregnancy announcements of Jill, Jessa, Joy-Anna, Kendra, Jinger, and Lauren. The show has also featured the births of six grandchildren out of a total of thirteen; four births were featured on 19KAC, and three were not aired.

Josh Duggar + Jill and Derick Dillard controversies

During the run of _19 Kids and Counting_, the Josh Duggar controversies resulted in Josh not appearing in the spin-off show, _Counting On_. While Josh's wife, Anna, and their children have appeared in a couple of episodes, the producers exclude Josh on screen. The producers of _Counting On_ also confirmed that Jill Dillard’s husband Derick is no longer appearing in the series due to controversial comments made on social media in the fall of 2017 against transgender star Jazz Jennings who also has a show on TLC.[40] In addition, Derick announced on Twitter in December 2017 that he and Jill along with their two sons, Israel (and Samuel who did not make an appearance due to the timing of his birth and the episode not airing due to Derick’s participance), will no longer participate in future episodes of the series. However, despite this statement, Jill appeared in the birth episodes of Gideon Martyn Forsyth and Ivy Jane Seewald.
