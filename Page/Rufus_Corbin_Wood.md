RUFUS CORBIN WOOD (May 30, 1818-July 29, 1885) was sheriff of Norfolk County, Massachusetts from 1878 to 1885.[1]


Early life

Wood was born in Palmer, Massachusetts on May 30, 1818.[2]


Death and burial

Wood died on July 29, 1885. He was buried at the Canton Corner Cemetery in Canton, Massachusetts.[3]
