BENBOW is an unincorporated community in Marion County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Benbow was originally called Midway, and under the latter name was laid out in 1862.[2] A post office called Benbow was established in 1865, and remained in operation until 1906.[3] The identity of namesake Benbow has been lost.[4]
