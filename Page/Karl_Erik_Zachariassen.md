KARL ERIK ZACHARIASSEN (1942 â€“ 11 December 2009) was a Norwegian entomologist.

After taking his cand.real. degree at the University of Oslo with a thesis within zoophysiology, he studied further at the University of California, San Diego and took the dr.philos. degree in 1980. His doctoral thesis was titled _Mechanisms Involved in Tolerance to Freezing in Adult Beetles_. He became professor at the Norwegian Institute of Technology in 1988, later the Norwegian University of Science and Technology.[1]

He furthermore chaired the Norwegian Entomological Society for fourteen years, was a fellow of the Norwegian Academy of Science and Letters and the Royal Norwegian Society of Sciences and Letters.[2] He resided in Soknedal, and died in 2009.
