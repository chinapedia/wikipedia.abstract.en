DEFENCE ISLANDS are an island group of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the South Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] the islands are situated north from Port Blair.


Geography

The islands belongs to the Defence Group and lies in the sound of Jirkatang. at their southern islands, there is a natural harbour called Port Campbell.[2]


Administration

Politically, Defence Island, along neighboring islands, is part of Ferrargunj Taluk.[3]
