DON FAILLA is an American author best known for his work in multi-level marketing industry.[1]


Works

Failla has been working in MLM since the 1960s. As of 2011, he had sold over 4.5 million copies of his books in 23 languages worldwide.[2] His book, _10 Lessons on Napkins_, is the fundamental for MLM companies to based on.


Books

-   _10 Lessons on Napkins_ (1968)
-   _The 45 Second Presentation that Will Change Your Life_ (1984)[3]
-   _The Basics How to Build a Large Successful Multi-Level Marketing Organization_ (1996)
-   _The System: The 3 Steps to Building a Large, Successful Network Marketing Organization_ (2006)
-   _The System: And Other Tools for Rapid Success in Network Marketing_ (2009)
