HEJDE is a populated area, a socken (not to be confused with parish), on the Swedish island of Gotland.[1] It comprises the same area as the administrative Hejde District, established on 1January 2016.[2]


Geography

Hejde is situated in central Gotland.[3] The medieval Hejde Church is located in the socken.[4] , Hejde Church belongs to Hejde parish in Klinte pastorat.[5][6]
