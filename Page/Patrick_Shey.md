PATRICK S. SHEY (born January 22, 1959) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Shey was born in Algona, Iowa and attended the University of Iowa and DePaul University. A Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1999 to 2003 (52nd district), from Clinton County, Iowa.[1]
