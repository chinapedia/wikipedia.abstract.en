CHARLES SCOTT OGILVIE (December 1886 – 1939) was a Scottish amateur football outside left who appeared in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

Ogilvie emigrated to Canada in October 1912 and worked as a civil engineer.[2] After Canada's entry into the First World War in August 1914, Ogilvie joined the Canadian Expeditionary Force and became a corporal in a Canadian Highlander battalion.[3]
