_GRINGOS_ is a 1991 book by Charles Portis and the author's fifth novel.[1] It follows Jimmy Burns, an expatriate American, who during his adventures in Mexico encounters a female stalker, tomb-robbing archaeologists, UFO hunters, and a group of hippies.[2][3]
