PYAE MOE (; born 15 October 1992) is a footballer from Myanmar. He made his first appearance for the Myanmar national football team in 2007.[1] In 2015, he moved to Zeyar Shwe Myae for contract end. In 2016 MNL second half season,he returned to Rakhine United.
