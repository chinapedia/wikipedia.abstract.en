NOURIA YAMINA ZERHOUNI is an Algerian politician who is currently Governor of Boumerdès Province.


Career

Zerhouni was appointed Minister of Tourism by President Abdelaziz Bouteflika in September 2013.[1] In July 2015, she became governor of Bourmerdès.[2][3]
