_MICROBACTERIUM AQUIMARIS_ is a Gram-positive, non-spore-forming and non-motile bacterium from the genus of Microbacterium which has been isolated from seawater from Jeju in Korea.[1][2][3] the major menaquinones of _Microbacterium aquimaris_ are MK-11, MK-12 and MK-10.[4][5]
