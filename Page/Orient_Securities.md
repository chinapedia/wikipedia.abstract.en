ORIENT SECURITIES CO., LTD. also known as DFZQ is a Chinese investment bank and brokerage firm headquartered in Shanghai. Orient Securities is a constituent of SSE 50 Index, the blue chip index of Shanghai Stock Exchange.

It provided services in securities, futures, asset management, wealth management, investment banking, investment advisory, and securities research. It was successfully listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange on 23 March 2015[1] and on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in 2016.[2]
