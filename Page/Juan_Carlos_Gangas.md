JUAN CARLOS GANGAS LUBONES (born September 19, 1944 in Santiago, Chile) is a former Chilean footballer who played for clubs of Chile and Bolivia.


Teams

-   Universidad de Chile 1964-1967

-   Colo-Colo 1968-1970

-   Deportes Antofagasta 1971-1972

-   Jorge Wilstermann 1973

-   Bolívar 1974-1975

-   Deportes Antofagasta 1976

-   Ñublense 1977


Titles

-   Universidad de Chile 1964, 1965 and 1967 (Chilean Primera División Championship)

-   Colo-Colo 1970 (Chilean Primera División Championship)
