GIROLAMO NEGRI, also known as _il Boccia_ (circa 1648 - after 1718) was an Italian painter of the Baroque period.


Biography

Negri was born at Bologna. He initially trained with Domenico Maria Canuti, but then entered the studio of Lorenzo Pasinelli. He also painted in Modena, Mirandola, Faenza and Cesena. He painted an _Apparition of the Virgin and Child to St Peter_ for the Bologna Cathedral. He painted the _Denial by St Peter_ for the church of San Filippo Neri, Bologna.[1]
