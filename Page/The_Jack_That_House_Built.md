"THE JACK THAT HOUSE BUILT" is a song by a pioneering British house music act under the name of Jack 'N' Chill consisting of band members Edward "Ed" Stratton and Vlad Naslas. The title is a parody of the 1755 nursery rhyme This Is The House That Jack Built. In February 1988, it reached a peak of no. 6 in the UK, charting in the top 100 for a total of 18 weeks.
