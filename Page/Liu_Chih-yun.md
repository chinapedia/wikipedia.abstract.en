CHIH-YUN LIU () served as Deputy International Commissioner of the Boy Scouts of China.

In 1995, Liu was awarded the 242nd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
