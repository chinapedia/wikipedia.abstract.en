PUHAKKA is a Finnish language surname.[1]


Geographical distribution

As of 2014, 92.7% of all known bearers of the surname _Puhakka_ were residents of Finland (frequency 1:1,864), 3.6% of Sweden (1:85,624) and 1.0% of Canada (1:1,115,091).

In Finland, the frequency of the surname was higher than national average (1:1,864) in the following regions:[2]

-   1. North Karelia (1:307)
-   2. Kymenlaakso (1:446)
-   3. North Ostrobothnia (1:947)
-   4. Southern Savonia (1:1,445)


Notable people

Notable people with the name include:

-   Antti Puhakka (1816–1893), Finnish romantic poet
-   Joona Puhakka (born 1982), Finnish diver
-   Mirja Puhakka (fl. 1980–1984), Finnish ski-orienteering competitor
-   Olli Puhakka (1916–1989), Finnish Air Force ace
-   Osmo Puhakka (born 1948), Finnish Lutheran clergyman and politician
