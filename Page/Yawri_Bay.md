YAWRI BAY[1] is a bay in the coast of Sierra Leone on the Atlantic Ocean.


Geography

Yawri Bay opens to the southwest and is located about 25 km to the south of Freetown. The Banana Islands —including Dublin Island, Ricketts Island and Wolf Rock— as well as Thistle Island close to the headland, are located off the northwestern point enclosing the bay.[2]
