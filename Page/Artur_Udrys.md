ARTUR UDRYS (born ) is a Belarusian male volleyball player. On club level he plays for Fakel Novy Urengoy.[1]


Clubs

-   Stroitiel Mińsk (2009–2013)
-   Shahter Salihorsk (2012–2014)
-   Kristałł Woroneż (2015–2016)
-   Gumi KB Insurance Stars (2016–2017)[2]
-   Guangdong Shenzhen Volleyball (2017–2018)
-   Fakel Novy Urengoy (2018–present)
