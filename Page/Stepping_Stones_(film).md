_STEPPING STONES_ is a 1931 British musical film directed by Geoffrey Benstead. It was made at Isleworth Studios as a quota quickie.[1] It is a revue-style show featuring a number of music hall performers.


Cast

-   Jade Hales as Lady
-   George Bellamy as John
-   Ethel Lodge as Joan
-   Charles Paton as Suger Daddy
-   Henderson and Lennox as Cabaret Boys
-   Celia Bird as Romany Lady
-   Pearl Hay P as Crinoline Baby
-   Fred Rains
-   Marguerite Allan
-   Heather Thatcher
-   Alec Alexander as Bandleader
