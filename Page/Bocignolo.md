The BOCIGNOLO was a Ragusan noble family. It hailed from Chlieuno (Livno),[1] a town in Hum. In the 15th century they were one of the eleven smallest Ragusan houses. It was mentioned as living in the city of Ragusa (Dubrovnik) in 1588.[2] In 1535 a member was at Vienna in the service of Ferdinand Habsburg, the future emperor.[3]
