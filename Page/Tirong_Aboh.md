TIRONG ABOH (died 21 May 2019) was an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Aboh was elected from Khonsa West seat in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election, standing as a People's Party of Arunachal candidate.[2][3][4] He and his son were among 11 people shot dead by unknown militants, on 21 May 2019, in the Bogapani area of the Tirap district.[5]
