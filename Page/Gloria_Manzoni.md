GLORIA MANZONI (born 25 April 1998) is an Italian road and track cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Women's Team ,[1] and represents Italy at international competitions. She competed at the 2016 UEC European Track Championships in the 500m time trial event and team sprint event.[2]
