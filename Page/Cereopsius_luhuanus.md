_CEREOPSIUS LUHUANUS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Heller in 1896. It is known from Sulawesi and Moluccas.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Cereopsius luhuanus luhuanus_ Heller, 1896
-   _Cereopsius luhuanus ternatensis_ Breuning, 1956
