John Kelly}} JOHN GERALD KELLY (born 14 December 1935) is a Scottish former professional footballer who played as a right half.


Career

Born in Glasgow, Kelly played for Shettleston, Third Lanark, Crewe Alexandra, Celtic, Greenock Morton, Barnsley and Cork Hibernians.[1][2]
