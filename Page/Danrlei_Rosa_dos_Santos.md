DANRLEI ROSA DOS SANTOS (born 1 August 1994) is a footballer who hails from São Luiz Gonzaga, Brazil. He plays for Caxias and can play either as a defender or defensive midfielder.[1][2][3]

He became a professional in 2014 and played in the youth of Atletico Paranaense before joining Joinville.[4]
