REED N. WILCOX is the current president of Southern Virginia University, where he has served since 2014.

Previously Wilcox was involved in the pharmaceuticals industry. He has been involved in consulting in much of the US, and has also at times lived abroad in China, Japan, France and Ghana. He served as the president of the Toulouse Mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Wilcox has a bachelor's degree from Brigham Young University and a JD/MBA from Harvard University.

Wilcox also previously ran a company called the Lifelike Toy Company, based near Denver, Colorado, and with Mitt Romney on the board.[1]
