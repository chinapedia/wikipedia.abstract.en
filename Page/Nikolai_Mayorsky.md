NIKOLAI SERGEYEVICH MAYORSKY (; born 7 January 1997) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Energomash Belgorod on 30 October 2016 in a game against FC Arsenal-2 Tula.[1]
