VALERIY VOLODYMYROVYCH SARATOV () (born 6 June 1951, in Krasne Pole, Markivka Raion, Ukraine), is a Ukrainian politician who was under a suspicion of state treason in favor for the Russian Federation.

In 2010-11 he served as a Governor of Sevastopol.[1]
