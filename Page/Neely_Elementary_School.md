NEELY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, also known as NEELY SCHOOL BUILDING, is a historic school building located at St. Joseph, Missouri. The building was originally constructed in 1871, with its earliest extant portions to date from 1894. Additions and major renovations were completed in 1909 and 1916. The school is a two-story brick and limestone building on a raised basement with Classical Revival style design elements. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2014.[2]
