_RHOPALOCARPUS LUCIDUS_ is a tree in the family Sphaerosepalaceae. It is endemic to Madagascar.


Distribution and habitat

_Rhopalocarpus lucidus_ is a widespread species in Madagascar. Its habitat is forests and thickets from sea-level to altitude. Some populations are within protected areas.[1][2]


Threats

Because _Rhopalocarpus lucidus_ is used as firewood and for making tools and rope, subsistence harvesting is a threat.[3]
