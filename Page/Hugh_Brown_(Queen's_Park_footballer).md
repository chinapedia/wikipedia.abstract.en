HUGH BROWN was a Scottish amateur football right back who made one appearance in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

During the First World War, Brown served in the Lovat Scouts prior to being commissioned into the Royal Scots Fusiliers.[2] He was wounded on the Western Front in May 1917.[3]
