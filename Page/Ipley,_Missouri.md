IPLEY is an unincorporated community in Dunklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A variant name was "Shipley".[2] A post office called Shipley was established in 1903, and remained in operation until 1913.[3] The community derives its name from Hugh Shipley, a pioneer citizen.[4]
