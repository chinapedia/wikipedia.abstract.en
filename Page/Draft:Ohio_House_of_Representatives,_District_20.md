Ohio_House_Map,_District_20_(2012-2020).png The Ohio House of Representatives, District 20, is located in eastern Franklin County, Ohio, with the major population centers being Canal Winchester, Ohio, Groveport, Ohio, Obetz, Ohio, Reynoldsburg, Ohio and Whitehall, Ohio. District 20 has a diverse population and includes rural, suburban and urban cities, villages and townships. The district's boundaries were last redrawn in 2012 and the seat has been held by Democrat Heather Bishoff since its redistricting.[1]
