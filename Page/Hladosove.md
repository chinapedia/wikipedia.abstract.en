The settlement was taken under control of pro-Russian forces during the War in Donbass, that started in 2014.[1] Ukrainian troops took the settlement under their control in November 2017 (together with Travneve).[2][3] Power supply to Hladosove (and Travneve) was completely restored on 28 December 2017.[4]


Demographics

Native language as of the Ukrainian Census of 2001:[5]

-   Ukrainian 81.82%
-   Russian 18.18%
