RENATE FEYL (born 30 July 1944) is a Prague-born writer living in Germany.[1]

Born in Prague (at that time Protectorate of Bohemia and Moravia), she grew up in Jena and went on to study philosophy at Humboldt University. Since 1970, Feyl has lived in Berlin working as a freelance writer.[2][3][4]


Selected works[5][6]

-   _Bau mir eine Brücke_, novel (1972)
-   _Der lautlose Aufbruch_, essays (1981)
-   _Idylle mit Professor_, novel (1986)
-   _Sein ist das Weib, Denken der Mann_, essay (1991)
-   _Ausharren im Paradies_, novel (1992)
-   _Die profanen Stunden des Glücks_ (1996)
-   _Das sanfte Joch der Vortrefflichkeit_ (1999)
-   _Aussicht auf bleibende Helle_ (2006)
