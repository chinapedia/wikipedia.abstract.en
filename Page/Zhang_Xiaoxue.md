ZHANG XIAOXUE (born 13 December 1992) is a Chinese field hockey player.[1] She competed for the China women's national field hockey team at the 2016 Summer Olympics.[2]

She won a silver medal as a member of the Chinese team at 2014 Asian Games.[3]
