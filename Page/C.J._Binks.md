C.J. BINKS (Christopher John) is a Tasmanian writer.

He has lived in Tasmania since the 1940s [1][2]

His interest in Western Tasmania led him to write _Explorers of Western Tasmania_ a significant history of exploration of the region,[3] another _Pioneers of the West Coast_ [4] and a later work _Hills of the West Wind_ [5]

He has also written about Bass Strait [6] and Devonport [7][8][9]
