ELLIS LUKE HUDSON (born 4 February 1999) is an English professional footballer who plays as a left winger.


Early and personal life

Born in Bradford,[1] Hudson attended Bingley Grammar School.[2]


Career

Hudson joined Bradford City at the age of 8.[3] He made his senior debut on 30 August 2016, in a EFL Trophy game against Stoke under-23s.[4] He turned professional in September 2016, signing a two-year contract.[5][6][7] Hudson joined Harrogate Town on loan in November 2017, alongside teammate Lachlan Barr.[8] He then moved on loan to Guiseley in March 2018.[9]

He was offered a new contract by Bradford City at the end of the 2017–18 season.[10] He left the club in December 2018 by mutual consent.[11]
