'''Mahmoud Ahmed ''' (محمود احمد, born 1 March 1976) is an Egyptian male water polo player. He was a member of the Egypt men's national water polo team, playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played for Gezira Sporting Club in Egypt.
