GURVINDER SINGH (born 12 June 1983) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Himachal Pradesh in the 2010–11 Ranji Trophy on 24 November 2010.[2]

He was the leading wicket-taker for Himachal Pradesh in the 2017–18 Ranji Trophy, with 20 dismissals in six matches.[3]
