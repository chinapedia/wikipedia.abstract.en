SERGEY SEMENOV (born January 4, 1985) is a Ukrainian footballer.


Playing career

Semenov began his career with in 2004 SC Tavriya Simferopol in the Ukrainian Premier League appearing in 14 matches and recording 1 goal.[1][2] The following season he played in the Ukrainian Second League with MFC Zhytomyr, and played with NAPCS-Dynamo Simferopol in 2006. In 2016, he went abroad to play with FC Ukraine United in the Canadian Soccer League.[3]In his second season he assisted FC Ukraine in achieving a perfect season, and winning the Second Division Championship.[4]
