JAMES TALBOT is a Northern Irish international lawn and indoor bowler.[1]


Bowls career

Talbot is from County Antrim, in Northern Ireland and he won a bronze medal in the triples and fours at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship.[2]

He won the gold medal at the 2011 World Cup Singles in Warilla, New South Wales, Australia.[3]
