PENNSBORO is an unincorporated community in Dade County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Pennsboro was founded in the 1880s, and named after William Penn, a Dade County pioneer.[2] A post office was established at Pennsboro in 1895, and remained in operation until 1931.[3]
