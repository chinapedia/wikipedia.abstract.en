KSENIIA TSYMBALYUK (born 8 January 1997) is a Russian female road and track cyclist, representing Russia at international competitions. She competed at the 2016 UEC European Track Championships in the team pursuit event.[1] At the 2017 UCI Road World Championships she rode in the women's time trial event.[2]
