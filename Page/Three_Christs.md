_THREE CHRISTS_ is an American drama film directed, co-produced, and co-written by Jon Avnet, based on the book _The Three Christs of Ypsilanti_ by Milton Rokeach. It screened in the Gala Presentations section at the 2017 Toronto International Film Festival.[1][2][3]


Premise

The film is an adaptation of _The Three Christs of Ypsilanti_,[4] Rokeach's 1964 book-length psychiatric case study of three patients whose paranoid schizophrenic delusions cause them each to believe they are Jesus Christ.[5]


Cast

-   Richard Gere as Dr. Alan Stone
-   Julianna Margulies as Ruth
-   Peter Dinklage as Joseph
-   Walton Goggins as Leon
-   Bradley Whitford as Clyde
-   Kevin Pollak as Dr. Orbus
-   Stephen Root as Dr. Rogers
-   Jane Alexander as Dr. Abraham


Production

As of August 8, 2016, _Three Christs_ had begun filming in New York.[6]
