RAMESH KUMAR PANDEY is an Indian academic, and the current vice-chancellor of Ranchi University.[1][2][3]


Biography

Pandey was born and brought up in Jharkhand (then Bihar). He completed his Bachelor's, Master's and Ph.D. from Ranchi University. He was a post-doctoral fellow at University of Nottingham. In his 37 years teaching career he wrote more than 25 research papers.[4]

In 2015 he was appointed as vice-chancellor of Ranchi University.[5] At the time of joining he told about his priority:

  My priority is to organize students' union elections. It is only through students' representatives that we come to know about the problems of students. I will talk to the members of all student unions and will finalize a date for the elections as soon as possible.
