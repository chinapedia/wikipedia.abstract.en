_RASPUTIN_ (German: _DORNENWEG EINER FÜRSTIN_) is a 1928 German-Soviet drama film directed by Nikolai Larin and Boris Nevolin and starring Vladimir Gajdarov, Suzanne Delmas and Ernst Rückert.[1]

The film's sets were designed by Carl Ludwig Kirmse.
