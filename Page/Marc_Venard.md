MARC VENARD (11 July 1929 – 11 November 2014) was a French historian.


Biography

A student at the École Normale Supérieure, he was agrégé and doctor in history and a specialist of religious history of the 16th century. He was _emeritus_ professor of modern history at the Universities of Rouen and Paris West University Nanterre La Défense and member of the Académie des sciences, belles-lettres et arts de Rouen.
