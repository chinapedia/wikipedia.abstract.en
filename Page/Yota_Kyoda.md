Career

On 20 October 2016, Kyoda was selected as the 2nd draft pick for the Chunichi Dragons at the 2016 NPB Draft and on 26 November signed a provisional contract with a ¥85,000,000 sign-on bonus and a ¥12,000,000 yearly salary.[2][3]

On February 27, 2019, he was selected for Japan national baseball team at the 2019 exhibition games against Mexico.[4]
