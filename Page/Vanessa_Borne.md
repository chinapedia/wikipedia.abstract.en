DANIELLE SIERRA KAMELA (born March 18, 1988) is an American professional wrestler and actress currently signed to WWE, where she works in their developmental territory NXT under the ring name VANESSA BORNE.


Early life

Kamela was born to a Samoan mother and a European American father.[1] A graduate of Chaparral High School and Arizona State University, Kamela worked as a Fox Sports Arizona presenter, Phoenix Suns dancer, and Arizona Cardinals cheerleader prior to her wrestling career.[2]


Professional wrestling career

Knokx Pro Entertainment (2016)

Kamela trained with Rikishi and Gangrel at Knokx Pro Entertainment. She made her in-ring debut on March 26, 2016 using her real name Danielle. She was defeated by Pedro in the European Cruiserweight Championship Match.[3]

On April 12, 2016 it was announced that Kamela had signed with WWE, where she was assigned to the WWE Performance Center in Orlando, Florida.[4] She made her WWE NXT debut shortly after at a live event, under her real name.[5] Her television debut took place on the October 5, 2016, episode of _NXT_, working as an enhancement talent, where she was defeated by Peyton Royce.[6] She made another appearance on the October 19 episode of _NXT_, where she was defeated by Nikki Cross, but the decision was later reversed because Cross continued to attack Kamela.[7] On June 28, 2017, Kamela returned to television under the new ring name VANESSA BORNE, where she defeated Jayme Hachey in a Mae Young Classic qualifier match.[8] On July 13, she was eliminated from the tournament in the first round by Serena Deeb.[9] Towards the rest of the year and the beginning of 2018, Borne continued to compete against various competitors such as Liv Morgan,[10] Nikki Cross,[11] Kairi Sane,[12] and Dakota Kai, however, she ended up on the losing side against all of them.[13][14]

On February 13th 2019, Borne started an alliance with Aliyah, after she helped her defeat Taynara Conti.[15] In their first match together, Borne and Aliyah defeated Conti and Xia Li.[16]


Other media

Kamela was a guest on the professional cheerleading web series _Beyond The Pom Poms_ in 2014.[17]
