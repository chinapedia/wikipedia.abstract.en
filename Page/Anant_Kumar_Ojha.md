Picture_of_Anant_Ojha_in_Sahibganj.jpg ANANT KUMAR OJHA is currently MLA of Rajmahal Vidhan Sabha constituency of Jharkhand after winning the seat in the 2014 Jharkhand Legislative Assembly elections securing over 77,000 votes. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party.[1] He completed his graduation from Graduate Arts Sahebganj College, Sahebganj S.K.M.University, Dumka in 1995. There's no criminal case registered against him.[2]

Following the assembly election of 2014, Anant Ojha was one of the candidates for the post of next Chief Minister .[3][4][5] Anant Ojha is a former president of BJYM (Bhartiya Janata Yuva Morcha).[6] On 7 November, Anant Ojha was appointed as general secretary of the Jharkhand BJP.[7][8] He is also a member of the Jharkhand Academic Council.[9] Working for developing Rajmahal as the tourist destination of the country.He is called Gandhi of Rajmahal because of his honesty.Anant Ojha is also known as the Chanakya of Jharkhand's Politics because of his farsighted management skills.
