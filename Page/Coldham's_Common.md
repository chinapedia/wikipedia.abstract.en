COLDHAM'S COMMON is a 49.3 hectare Local Nature Reserve in Cambridge. It is owned and managed by Cambridge City Council.[1][2]

This site has areas of unimproved grassland, which have anthills of yellow meadow ants.[3] There is also scrub and woodland. Flora include spiny rest harrow, upright brome and bee orchid.[4]

There is access from Coldham's Lane.
