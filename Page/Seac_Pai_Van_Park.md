The SEAC PAI VAN PARK (; ) is a park in Coloane, Macau, China. It is the largest natural green area in Macau and is governed by the Civil and Municipal Affairs Bureau.[1]


History

The area was originally a farmland. It was then later converted into a multipurpose park. In 1981, it was listed as a protected area.[2]
