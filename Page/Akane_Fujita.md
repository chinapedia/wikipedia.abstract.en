is a Japanese voice actress from Shizuoka Prefecture. She is affiliated with Ken Production.[1] After passing an audition in 2011, she made her debut as a voice actress in 2012, and she played her first main role in 2016. She is known for her roles as Sagiri Izumi in _Eromanga Sensei_ and Sistine Fibel in _Akashic Records of Bastard Magic Instructor_.


Life and career

Fujita had an interest in voice acting since at least her junior high school years, since she had regularly been watching anime, and she had friends who were anime and voice actor fans. According to her, one reason she decided to become a voice actor was because she "wanted to do something that was fun".[2] During her high school years, she was a part of her school's broadcasting club. As a third-year student, she participated in a national broadcasting competition sponsored by NHK. In 2011, she decided to participate in an audition organized by the agency Ken Production. She passed the audition, and pursued studies at a voice acting training school while also pursuing university studies.[3]

Fujita made her voice acting debut in 2012, voicing the character Yuri in the video game _Generation of Chaos: Pandora’s Reflection_.[4] She would then play a number of supporting roles in anime series such as _Aikatsu!_, _Chihayafuru_, _Magical Warfare_, and _Pokémon XY & Z_.[5] In 2015, she was cast as Yukari Mizumoto in _The Idolmaster Cinderella Girls_ and Megumi Uda in _High School Fleet_.[6][7] She was also cast as the character Sagiri Izumi in an online radio drama adaptation of the light novel series _Eromanga Sensei_.[8]

In 2017, Fujita reprised the role of Sagiri in the anime television series adaptation of _Eromanga Sensei_.[9] She also performed the song , which was used as the ending theme for the series' eighth episode.[10] That same year, she was cast as the character Sistine Fibel in the anime series _Akashic Records of Bastard Magic Instructor_; she and co-stars Yume Miyamoto and Ari Ozawa performed the series' ending theme "Precious You".[11] She also played the roles of Leviathan in the anime series _Seven Mortal Sins_ and Kaiko Mikuniyama in the anime series _A Sister's All You Need_.[12][13] She performed the song , which was used as an insert song in the second episode of _Seven Mortal Sins_,[14] and the song , which was used as the ending theme to the ninth episode of _A Sister's All You Need_.[15]
