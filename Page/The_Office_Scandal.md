_THE OFFICE SCANDAL_ is a 1929 American drama film directed by Paul L. Stein and starring Phyllis Haver, Raymond Hatton and Margaret Livingston.[1] It was originally made as a silent, but some sound effects a musical score and talking sequences were added.
