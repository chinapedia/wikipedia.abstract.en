KREAM (stylized as KREAM) is a Norwegian DJ duo consisting of brothers Daniel Slettebakken (born 28 April 1989) and Markus Slettebakken (born 16 February 1994), from Bergen.[1][2][3]


Career

2016

Kream released the song that they co-wrote with Swedish songwriting duo SHY (SHY Martin and SHY Nodi) "Taped Up Heart" which features vocals from Clara Mae (Clara Hagman), via Big Beat Records.[4] The song has charted on Billboard's Hot/Dance Electronic Songs chart and peaked at 22.[5]
