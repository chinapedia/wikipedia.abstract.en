KALLUM MANTACK is an English professional footballer who plays as a winger for Ashton United. He previously played in the Football League for Oldham Athletic.


Playing career

Mantack came through the Oldham Athletic youth team to sign his first professional contract in April 2016.[1] He made his first team debut in a 2–1 victory over Wigan Athletic in an EFL Cup match at Boundary Park on 9 August 2016.[2] He joined Alfreton Town on an initial one-month loan on 16 September 2016.[3] After securing a place in the Alfreton first team, his loan spell was extended until 15 January.[4] In July 2018, Mantack was named in the 2018/19 Stockport County first team squad, having appeared for the club on loan from Oldham Athletic during the previous season.[5] He made four appearances before departing the club.[6]

In November 2018, he signed for Altrincham.[7] He made his debut on Saturday, 24th November in an FA Trophy match against Bradford Park Avenue, coming on as a substitute and scoring in a 4-0 win.[8]

In February 2019, Mantack joined Ashton United, making his debut in a 2-0 defeat to York City.[9]
