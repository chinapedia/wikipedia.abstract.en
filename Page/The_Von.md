THE VON is an American band based in South Florida.[1] The band comprises Luis Bonilla, Marek Schneider and Elisa Seda.[2][3][4]


History

The VON was formed in 2013 by the vocalist and bassist Luis Bonilla and the guitarist Marek Schneider. The VON has seen the majority of its career as a trio with the drummer Elisa Seda.[5] Elisa became the part of the band when she replaced their former drummer when the band's 2015 album was recorded.[6]

In 2015, THE VON released its album _Ei8ht_.[7][8] The album depicts musical influences how they came together with respect to their diverse cultures, yoga ideology, and the themes of fearlessly living the life.[9][10][11] The VON released their first single, _Nothing to Fear_, in the start of 2015.[12][13] This was followed by _Cry Of War_ in 2016.[14][15][16][17] The VON is now working on a National and European Tour.[18][19]
