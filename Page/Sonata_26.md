The SONATA 26 is a sailboat that was designed by American Gary Mull.[1][2]

The boat was built by Investigator Yachts in Australia, who completed 66 examples, starting in 1980. The design is out of production.[3]

The Sonata 26 is a development of the Sonata 8. The two boats share the same specifications.[4]


Design

The Sonata 26 is a small recreational keelboat, built predominantly of fiberglass. It has a fractional sloop rig, a transom-hung rudder and a lifting or optional fixed fin keel. It displaces and carries of ballast.[5]
