The ARUBA NATIONAL UNDER-16 AND UNDER-17 BASKETBALL TEAM is a national basketball team of Aruba, administered by the Aruba Basketball Bond.[1]

It represents the country in international under-16 and under-17 (under age 16 and under age 17) basketball competitions.

It appeared at the 2016 CBC U16 Championship.
