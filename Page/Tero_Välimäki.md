TERO VÄLIMÄKI (born March 21, 1982) is a Finnish Greco-Roman wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 66 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated in the round of 16 by Shmagi Bolkvadze.[1][2]
