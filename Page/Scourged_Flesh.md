SCOURGED FLESH is a Christian metal band that originated in Perth, Western Australia.[1]


Background

The band started in 2006 with brothers Todd (Guitar, Vocals) and Dave Kilgallon (Drums).[2] Scourged Flesh added on Bassist Simon Bracegirdle as an official member.[3] The band was later joined, after the recording of their first two albums, _Released From Damnation_ and _Bury the Lies_, the band added Guitarist Daniel Holmes and Bassist Simon Hoggett.[4] In 2008, Dave Kilgallon joined the fellow Australian band Mortification as their drummer.[5]
