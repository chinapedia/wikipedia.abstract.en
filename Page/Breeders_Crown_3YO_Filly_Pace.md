The BREEDERS CROWN 3YO FILLY PACE is a harness racing event for three-year-old Standardbred fillies pacers. It is one part of the Breeders Crown annual series of twelve races for both Standardbred trotters and trotters. First run in 1985, it is contested over a distance of one mile. Race organizers have awarded the event to various racetracks across North America.[1] The 2017 race will be held at Hoosier Park in Anderson, Indiana, United States.[2]


Historical race events

In 2010, Pocono Downs became the first venue to host all 12 events on a single night.[3]

Records

Most wins by a driver:

-   4 – John Campbell (1989, 1991, 1992, 1993)

Most wins by a trainer:

-   3 – Robert McIntosh (1992, 1993, 2005)

Stakes record:

-   1:49 3/5 – Monkey On My Wheel (2011)
