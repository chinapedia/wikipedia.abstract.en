MIKHAIL SHEMETAU (born 21 July 1994) is a Belarusian track cyclist, representing Belarus at international competitions. He competed at the 2016 UCI Track Cycling World Championships in the individual pursuit event and at the 2016 UEC European Track Championships in the team pursuit event.[1]
