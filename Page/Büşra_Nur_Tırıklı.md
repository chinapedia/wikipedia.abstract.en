BÜŞRA NUR TIRIKLI (born December 7, 1994) is a Turkish female Paralympian athlete competing in the F11 disability class of discus throw event.[1] She is a member of Gaziantep BB Disabled SK.[2]

She won the silver medal in the discus throw F11/12 event at the 2016 IPC Athletics European Championships held in Grosseto, Italy.[3]

She competed in the discus throw F11 at the 2016 Paralympics in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil after receiving a special invitation.[4]
