VLADIMIR IVANOVICH KUZYUTKIN (; born 20 February 1947)[1] is a Soviet and Russian volleyball coach,[2][3][4] who from 2009–2011 worked as head coach of the Russia women's national volleyball team and helped his team to win the 2010 World Cup. In 2011 he was named a Honored Coach of Russia. From February to November 2014 he coached the Bulgaria women's national volleyball team.[5]

On 2 February 2017, Kuzyutkin returned as head coach for the Russian women's national team.
