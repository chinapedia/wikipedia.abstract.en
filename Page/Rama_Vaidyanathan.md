Bharatanatyam_danseuse.jpg RAMA VAIDYANATHAN is an Indian bharatnatyam artist from Delhi. She is married to C. V. Kamesh, CXO at Hitachi India, and son of Saroja Vaidyanathan [1][2][3][4]

Rama Vaidyanathan is selected for Sangeet Natak Akademi Award for year 2017.[5]
