_MUQABLA_ () is 1979 Hindi action film directed by Rajkumar Kohli, starring Sunil Dutt, Vinod Mehra, Reena Roy, Bindiya Goswami, Rajesh Khanna and Rekha. The film was average in box office terms.[1] The song _Teen Batti Wala Govinda Aala_ remains popular and even today is played on the festival of Janmmashtami in India.[2]


Cast

-   Sunil Dutt as Havaldar Suraj
-   Vinod Mehra as traffic inspector Deepak
-   Reena Roy as Vandana
-   Bindiya Goswami as Karisma
-   Rajesh Khanna as Suraj's father
-   Rekha as Soni


Soundtrack

All music was composed by Laxmikant Pyarelal. Lyrics were by Verma Malik.

2. "Na Subah Meri Na Shaam" - Asha Bhosle

3. "Meri Rus Gayi Jhanjar Wali" - Kishore Kumar, Asha Bhosle

4. "Woh Mehfil Ki Shama" - Asha Bhosle, Mahendra Kapoor

5. "Dil Wale Yaar Sajna" - Asha Bhosle, Anwar
