SACHIDANAND PANDEY (born 1 July 1996) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Services in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 20 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Services in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 4 March 2017.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Services in the 2017–18 Zonal T20 League on 13 January 2018.[4]
