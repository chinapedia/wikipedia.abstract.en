_SIXTEEN DAUGHTERS AND NO FATHER_ (German: _SECHZEHN TÖCHTER UND KEIN PAPA_) is a 1928 German silent comedy film directed by Adolf Trotz and starring Paul Graetz and Camilla Spira.[1]

The film's art direction was by August Rinaldi.
