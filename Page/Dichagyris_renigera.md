_DICHAGYRIS RENIGERA_ is a moth of the family Noctuidae. It is found in South- and Southeast-Europe, Armenia, Caucasus and Turkey.

There is one generation per year. The moth occurs from early June to early August, visits sugar and comes to light.


Subspecies

-   _Dichagyris renigera renigera_ (Alps)
-   _Dichagyris renigera funetissima_ (Bubacek, 1926) (Spain, Pyrenees)
-   _Dichagyris renigera argentina_ (Caradja, 1930)
