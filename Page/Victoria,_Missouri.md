VICTORIA is an unincorporated community in Jefferson County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Victoria was platted in 1859.[2] A variant name was "Victoria Station".[3] A post office called Victoria Station was established in 1863, the name was changed to Victoria in 1885, and the post office closed in 1955.[4]
