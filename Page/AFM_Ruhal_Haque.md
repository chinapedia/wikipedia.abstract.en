AFM RUHAL HAQUE (born 11 February 1944) is a Bangladesh Awami League politician and the former Minister of Health and Family Welfare.[1][2]


Early life

Haque graduated with an MBBS and work as a doctor.[3]


Career

Haque was elected to Parliament in 2008 and 2014 from Satkhira-3 as a Bangladesh Awami League candidate.[4][5] He was made the president Swadhinata Chikitshak Parishad, a pro-Awami League doctors association, by Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina.[6] He served as the r Minister of Health and Family Welfare.[7]
