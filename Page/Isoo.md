'''Isoo ''' is a village in Anantnag Tehsils in Anantnag district in Jammu and Kashmir.


Demographics

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Isoo village is 003671. Isoo village is located in Anantnag Tehsil of Anantnag district in Jammu & Kashmir, India. It is situated 17 km away from Anantnag, which is both district & sub-district headquarter of Isoo village.

The total geographical area of village is 119.8 hectares. Isoo has a total population of 1,955 peoples. There are about 301 houses in Isoo village.[1] Achabal is nearest town to Isoo which is approximately 6 km away.


Transport

By Rail

Sadura Railway Station & Anantnag Railway Station are the very near by railway stations to Isoo.However ever Jammu Tawi Railway Station is major railway station 243 km near to Isoo.
