KF SHKODRA is a football club based in Shkodër, Albania.


History

Klubi i Futbollit "Shkodra" was founded on November 9, 1996 by Taip Piraniqi, a former footballer and manager of Vllaznia Shkodër. It was the second team within the city. The football club "Shkodra" created a system of youth teams composed of athletes from ages 7 to 19. It has six coaches., The process is followed and monitored by Piraniqi. During its 21-year duration, the club produced high-level players. The two best known players are Albania international footballers Elseid Hysaj and Armando Vajushi. Other players from the ranks of the club include Arsid Kruja, Antonio Marku, Sindri Guri, Rubin Xhepaj, Ervin Rexha and Elis Kabuni.[1]
