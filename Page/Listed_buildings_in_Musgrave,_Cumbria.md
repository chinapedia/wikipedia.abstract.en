Musgrave is a civil parish in the Eden District, Cumbria, England. It contains three listed buildings that are recorded in the National Heritage List for England. All the listed buildings are designated at GradeÂ II, the lowest of the three grades, which is applied to "buildings of national importance and special interest". The parish contains the villages of Great Musgrave and Little Musgrave and is otherwise rural. The listed buildings comprise a house with a schoolroom, and a house and its forecourt railings. __NOTOC__
