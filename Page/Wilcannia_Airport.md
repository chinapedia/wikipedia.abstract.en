WILCANNIA AIRPORT (IATA: WIO, ICAO: YWCA) is an Australian domestic airport located near the town of Wilcannia, New South Wales. The airport is 9 kilometers (6 miles) from the center of Wilcannia, and is the only airport serving it.[1] The airport does not service scheduled commercial traffic.
