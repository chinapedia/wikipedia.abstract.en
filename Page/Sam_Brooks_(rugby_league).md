SAMUEL BROOKS is a Scotland international rugby league footballer who plays as a for the Leigh Centurions in the Betfred Championship.[1]

He was in the Wigan Warriors Academy system.[2] He moved to Halifax for the 2014 season.[3] In 2015 he moved to the Rochdale Hornets,[4] before making a mid-season switch to play for Whitehaven in the Kingstone Press Championship.[5] He since returned to Whitehaven in the Kingstone Press Championship as a dual-registered player.[6]

Brooks has previously played for the Widnes Vikings in the Super League.

Brooks is a Scotland international.[7]
