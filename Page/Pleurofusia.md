_PLEUROFUSIA_ is an extinct genus of sea snails, marine gastropod molluscs in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Species

Species within the genus _Pleurofusia_ include:

-   † _Pleurofusia crassinoda_ (Des Moulins, 1842)
-   † _Pleurofusia dowlingi_ Petuch 1997
-   † _Pleurofusia feddeni_ Noetling 1895
-   † _Pleurofusia fluctuosa_ Harris 1937
-   † _Pleurofusia fusus_ Vredenburg 1921
-   † _Pleurofusia longirostropsis_ (de Gregorio, 1890)
-   † _Pleurofusia paulensis_ Lozouet, 2015
-   † _Pleurofusia phasma_ Vredenburg 1921
-   † _Pleurofusia pseudocrassinoda_ Lozouet, 2015
-   † _Pleurofusia pseudosubtilis_ (Peyrot, 1931)
-   † _Pleurofusia scala_ Vredenburg 1921
-   † _Pleurofusia tauzini_ Lozouet, 2015
