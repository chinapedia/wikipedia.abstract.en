Dr. MING-HUEY KAO () served as the International Commissioner of the Boy Scouts of China.

In 1987, Kao was awarded the 188th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
