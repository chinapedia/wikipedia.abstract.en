KATHERINE WYNTER (born 9 February 1996) is a Jamaican badminton player.[1][2] In 2016, she won the Jamaica International tournament in the women's doubles event partnered with Ruth Williams.[3] She also won the bronze medal at the 2016 Pan Am Badminton Championships in the mixed doubles event partnered with Dennis Coke.[4] She competed at the 2018 Commonwealth Games in Gold Coast.[5]

Wynter educated at the University of the West Indies in Mona, and was awarded University Sportswoman of the Year in 2015 and 2016.[6]
