NIKOLAI OLEGOVICH KOVALYOV (; born 22 May 1996) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Zenit-Izhevsk on 2 October 2016 in a game against FC KAMAZ Naberezhnye Chelny.[1]
