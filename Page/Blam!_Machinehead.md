_BLAM! MACHINEHEAD_ (released in the US as _MACHINE HEAD_) is a shoot 'em up developed by Core Design and published by Virgin Interactive and Eidos Interactive, released for Sega Saturn, DOS and PlayStation in 1996.


Reception

_Next Generation_ reviewed the Saturn version of the game, rating it three stars out of five, and stated that "Overall, the game has some high points and is an excellent title to show off Saturn's graphics capabilities. However, the control could have been tweaked better, and it needs additional options."[1]
