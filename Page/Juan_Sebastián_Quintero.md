JUAN SEBASTIÁN QUINTERO FLETCHER (born 23 March 1995) is a Colombian footballer who plays as a defender for Brazilian club Fortaleza.


Club career

On 31 July 2017, after spending four years at Deportivo Cali, the club announced his loan to Sporting de Gijón for one season.[1]


International career

He was included among the squad of Colombia Olympic football team at the 2016 Summer Olympics, but never played.[2]


Personal life

In November 2018, Quintero and his brother were shot six times in their car when they were driving around Cali. Finally, both were unharmed.[3]
