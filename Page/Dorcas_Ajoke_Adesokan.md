DORCAS AJOKE ADESOKAN (born 5 July 1998) is Nigerian badminton player.[1][2]


Career

In 2014, she won bronze medals at the African Badminton Championships in women's singles and mixed doubles event, and silver medal in mixed team event.[3] In June, she won Lagos International tournament in women's doubles events.[4]
