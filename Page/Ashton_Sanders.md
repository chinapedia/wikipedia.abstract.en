ASHTON DURRAND SANDERS (born October 24, 1995)[1] is an American actor. He is best known for his portrayal of Teen Chiron in the Academy Award-winning film _Moonlight_ (2016).


Early life

Sanders was born in Carson, California. He attended nc north Pitt Grand Arts High School in Downtown Los Angeles, from which he graduated in 2013.[2] He was studying towards a BFA at the Theatre School at DePaul University, before dropping out in 2016 to focus on his acting career.[3]


Career

Sanders made his film debut in _The Retrieval_, directed by Chris Eska.[4] The film had its world premiere at South by Southwest on March 11, 2013.[5] It was released in a limited release on April 2, 2014 by Variance Films.[6][7]

In 2015, Sanders had a small role in _Straight Outta Compton_.[8] In 2016, he appeared in an episode of Refinery29's web series _The Skinny_.[9]

The same year, Sanders appeared in the drama film _Moonlight_, directed by Barry Jenkins.[10] The film had its world premiere at the Telluride Film Festival on September 2, 2016, and began a limited release on October 21, 2016 by A24.[11][12] _Moonlight_ received massive critical acclaim as well dozens of accolades, including the Golden Globe Award for Best Picture – Drama.[13] The film also won three Academy Awards for Best Picture, Best Adapted Screenplay, and Best Supporting Actor for Mahershala Ali.[14] Sanders' role in the film was praised by critics;[15] Benjamin Lee of _The Guardian_ called his performance "powerful".[16]

Sanders co-starred in _The Equalizer 2_, a sequel to the hit action film _The Equalizer_, opposite Denzel Washington.[17] In 2019, Sanders starred in Rupert Wyatt's science fiction thriller film _Captive State_, opposite John Goodman and Vera Farmiga.[18] He also played the lead in HBO's adaptation of the novel _Native Son_.
