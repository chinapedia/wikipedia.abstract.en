_CLAVASCIDIUM UMBRINUM_, also known as _Catapyrenium umbrinum_,[1] is a lichen.[2] It is characteristically 2 to 4 millimeters wide and medium to dark brown found on soil.

Found in Europe and North America; rather rare in Arizona, California, Chihuahua and Baja California.
