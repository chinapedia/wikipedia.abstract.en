RICK KETTING (born 15 January 1996) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays for IFK Mariehamn, as a centre back.[1]


Career

Ketting joined Sparta Rotterdam at the age of 10 and went through almost the entire youth academy of the club. Almost… because before that, he played for VV Nieuwerkerk.[2]

On 1 February 2019, Ketting joined Finnish club IFK Mariehamn on a one-year contract.[3]
