Dynamiden-walzer.png DYNAMIDEN op. 173 is a Viennese Waltz composed by Josef Strauss in 1865. Its subtitle is _Geheime Anziehungskräfte_ ('Mysterious Powers of Magnetism').

Richard Strauss used this waltz to his comic opera _Der Rosenkavalier_.
