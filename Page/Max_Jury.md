MAX JURY is an American singer-songwriter from Des Moines, Iowa, USA, who now lives in Paris, France. In 2016, Max Jury released his eponymous debut album including his breakthrough hit 'Numb'. He toured[1] with Lana Del Rey and Rufus Wainwright.

Late 2018, Max released[2] four acoustic demos as part of an EP titled 'Notes from California'.

His new album 'Modern World'[3] to be released 31^(st) May 2019 by Marathon artists, was produced by Robin Hannibal, the four-time Grammy-nominated producer and songwriter, best known for his co-writing credit on Kendrick Lamar's 'Bitch Don't Kill My Vibe'.
