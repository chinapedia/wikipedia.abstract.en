VUK MILOŠEVIĆ (born 30 November 1987) is a Montenegrin handball player who plays for the Romanian club SCM Politehnica Timișoara.[1]


Individual awards

-   Serie A - 1ª Divisione Nazionale Top Scorer: 2013


Personal life

He is the son of Pero Milošević.
