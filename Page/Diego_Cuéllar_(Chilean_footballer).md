DIEGO IGNACIO CUÉLLAR VÁSQUEZ (born November 22, 1986 in Chile) is a Chilean footballer who currently plays for Deportes Vallenar of the Segunda División Profesional in Chile.[1][2][3][4]


Teams

-   Deportes Ovalle 2006-2009

-   Unión La Calera 2010-2011

-   San Luis de Quillota 2012

-   Deportes La Serena 2013

-   Naval 2013-2014

-   Deportes Ovalle 2014-2016

-   Deportes Vallenar 2016–present
