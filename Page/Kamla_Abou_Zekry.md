KAMLA ABOU ZEKRY (born January 8, 1974) is an Egyptian television and film director who directed well-known movies and TV series such as _Bent Esmaha Zaat_, _Segn El Nisaa_, and _Wahed Sefr_. She has taken part in many international and national film festivals, including the Cairo International Film Festival, the Dubai International Film Festival, and the Venice Film Festival. Her films have also been screened at the Cannes Film Festival.


Early life

Abou Zekry was born in Cairo, Egypt. She graduated from the Higher Institute of Cinema.
