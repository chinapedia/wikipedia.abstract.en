H. VINOTH is an Indian film writer and director who made his directorial debut with _Sathuranga Vettai_ (2014) and his next venture _Theeran Adhigaaram Ondru_ with actor Karthi was the biggest blockbuster of that year.[1] Vinoth's debut was produced by actor, director Manobala and was distributed by Thirupathi Brothers Film Media and he's currently working with Ajith kumar on Nerkonda Paarvai.[2]


Film career

He worked as an assistant director to Vijay Milton and R. Parthiepan.

_Sathuranga Vettai_, his first movie in 2014, was very well received by the audience. The film was said to be a "con film". Vinoth stated, "My film is about the pace at which we all want to make money in our lives. It's about the anger of the common man who thinks money is the solution to all his problems. When there's an imbalance in nature, we experience tsunami and earthquake. Likewise, my story is about the imbalance in the social morals of a common man".[3] Although it was a heist film, the director said that he had made the film "as funny as possible" and that it had been narrated ), was signed on as director.[4][5] The film subsequently began production in mid-October 2016.

In early 2016, Vinoth narrated a script to Karthi, who was very impressed and agreed to team up with Vinoth after his current commitments Kaashmora and Kaatru Veliyidai.[6][7] The movie is titled as _Dheeran Adhigaram Ondru_ and produced by S. R. Prakashbabu and S.R. Prabhu of Dream Warrior Pictures.[8]

_Theeran Adhigaaram Ondru_(English: Braveman - Chapter One) or simply Theeran is a 2017 Indian action thriller[1][2] film written and directed by H. Vinoth and produced by S. R. Prakashbabu and S. R. Prabhu of Dream Warrior Pictures. The story is based on true events from the Operation Bawaria case, which involved the nefarious activity of dacoits and its eventual containment by Tamil Nadu police. Karthi and Rakul Preet Singh play the lead roles in this movie, while Abhimanyu Singh played the prime antagonist. Along with the Telugu dubbed version titled Khakee - The Power of Police, the film was released on 17 November 2017. A Hindi dubbed version titled Theeran was released on YouTube by Goldmines Telefilms on 24 June 2018. Karthi received Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actor – South and nominated Filmfare Award for Best Actor – Tamil.
