KENNEDY is an extinct town in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. The GNIS classifies it as a populated place.[1]

A post office called Kennedy was established in 1890, and remained in operation until 1915.[2] The community's name honors the local Kennedy family.[3]
