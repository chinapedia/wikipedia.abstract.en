ALI ALI EL MORSI () served as the International Commissioner and Vice-President of the Egyptian Scout Federation.

In 2002, he was awarded the 290th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1][2]
