Virgil I. Robinson Jr. born November 2, 1947 in Inverness, Mississippi. Played for the New Orleans Saints as a RB from 1971-1975. He also played for the Houston Texans and the Shreveport Steamer. Robinson ran for 647 yards in his career on 189 attempts, scoring 3 rushing touchdowns. He also caught 27 passes for 156 yards and 1 receiving touchdowns.[1][2][3][4]
