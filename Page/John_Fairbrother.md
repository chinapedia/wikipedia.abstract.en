JOHN FAIRBROTHER (born 12 February 1941) is an English former footballer who played as a centre forward. He scored 153 goals from 352 appearances in the Football League playing for Watford, Peterborough Unitedfor whom he top-scored in the 1966–67 and 1967–68 seasons[1] Northampton Town, Mansfield Town and Torquay United.[2][3] He also played in the Southern League for Worcester City87 goals in two seasons in all competitions, of which 51 were scored in the league[4]Bath City, for whom he scored 60 goals in 108 appearances in all competitions,[5] and Barnet, and continued his non-league football career with Hemel Hempstead Town, Berkhamsted Town and Leverstock Green.[6]
