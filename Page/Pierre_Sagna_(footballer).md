PIERRE SAGNA (born 21 August 1990) is a Senegalese footballer who plays for Portuguese club Belenenses SAD as a full back.[1]


International career

Sagna was born in Senegal, and moved to the France at the age of 10 - he is eligible for both national teams, but prefers to aim for the Senegal national football team.[2]
