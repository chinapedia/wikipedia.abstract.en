GIULIA DILETTA LEOTTA (Catania, 16 August 1991)[1] is an Italian television presenter.[2][3][4]

She anchors Serie A broadcasts for DAZN in the 2018-2019 season.[5] Leotta had presented the channels broadcasts of Serie B games along on Sky Sport (Italy) with Gianluca Di Marzio and Luca Marchegiani.[6] Aída Yéspica in 2018 from April[7] to August[8] worked, with Leotta, as a radio host of _105 Take Away_, a program of Radio 105 Network that Leotta anchors since October 2017 with Daniele Battaglia e Alan Caligiuri. Leotta hosted[9] with Francesco Facchinetti[10] the 2018 ediction of Miss Italia[11] won by Carlotta Maggiorana.[12]

Leotta studied Law at the Luiss University in Rome and graduated in 2015.[13][14]
