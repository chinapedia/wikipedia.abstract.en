MARCO HARBOE RAMKILDE (born 9 May 1998) is a Danish football player who plays as centre-forward, most recently for Danish Superliga club AaB.[1]


Club career

AaB

In 2016, Ramklide was called up for AaB first team. On 29 May 2016, he made his Danish Superliga debut against OB at EWII Park, replacing Christian Bassogog at the 59th minute.[2]

Ramkilde left by the end of the 2018/19, where his contract expired.[3]


International career

Ramkilde has represented his country at various age groups.[4] He was a member of Denmark national under-17 football team in the 2015 UEFA European Under-17 Championship qualification. Since September 2016, he has been member of Denmark national under-19 football team and played in 2017 UEFA European Under-19 Championship qualification.[5]
