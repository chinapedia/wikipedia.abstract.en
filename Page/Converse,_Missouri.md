CONVERSE is an unincorporated community in Clinton County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Converse was laid out in 1870 when the railroad was extended to that point, and named for a railroad worker.[2] A post office was established at Converse in 1870, and remained in operation until 1951.[3]
