The NONSUCH 26 is a Canadian sailboat, that was designed by Mark Ellis and first built in 1982. It is one of the series of Nonsuch sailboats.[1][2]

The Nonsuch 26 is a development of the larger, 1978-designed Nonsuch 30.[3][4]


Production

The Nonsuch 26 design was built by Hinterhoeller Yachts in St. Catharines, Ontario, Canada, between 1982 and 1988.[5][6]
