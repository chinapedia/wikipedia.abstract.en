TREVOR BRYAN (born August 23, 1989) is an American professional boxer in the Heavyweight division. Bryan is the current Interim WBA World Heavyweight champion.


Professional Career

Bryan made his professional debut in May 2011 defeating Demarcus Young by RTD in the second round in Hollywood, Florida. In August 2015, Bryan beat Derric Rossy in Las Vegas, Nevada and won the Junior NABF Heavyweight title.[1] Bryan would go on to beat former cruiserweight title challenger BJ Flores to win the interim WBA Heavyweight title.[2]
