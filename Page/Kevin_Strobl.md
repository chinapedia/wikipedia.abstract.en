KEVIN STROBL (born 26 August 1997) is an Italian badminton player.[1][2] He competed at the 2018 Mediterranean Games, and won the men's doubles bronze medal partnered with Lukas Osele.[3][4]


Career

In 2016, he became the runner-up of Ethiopia International tournament in men's doubles event.[5][6]
