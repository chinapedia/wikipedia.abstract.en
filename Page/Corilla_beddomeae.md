_CORILLA BEDDOMEAE_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Corillidae.

The specific name _beddomeae_ is in honor of British naturalist Richard Henry Beddome.


Distribution

Distribution of _Corilla beddomeae_ includes Sri Lanka.
