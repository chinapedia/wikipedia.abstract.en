Milionia_fulgida.jpg, Java]] _MILIONIA FULGIDA_ is a species of day-flying moth in the family Geometridae. The species was first described by Vollenhoven in 1863. It is found on Java and Borneo.[1] Adults are distinguished by the lack of any orange marking on the hindwings.[2]
