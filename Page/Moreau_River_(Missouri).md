MOREAU RIVER is a stream in Cole County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Missouri River.

The stream headwaters occur at the confluence of North Moreau Creek with South Moreau Creek at [2] about ten miles southwest of Jefferson City. The stream meanders to the east passing under US Route 54 south of Jefferson City and US Route 50 east of Jefferson City to enter the Missouri River at .[3][4]

Moreau River most likely was named for its dark water, "Moreau" being a word derived from the French meaning "black".[5]
