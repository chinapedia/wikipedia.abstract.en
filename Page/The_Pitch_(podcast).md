_THE PITCH_ is an American podcast from Gimlet Media hosted by Josh Muccio.[1] The show features entrepreneurs in need of venture funding as they pitch to a live panel of investors,[2] similar to ABC's hit TV series _Shark Tank_.[3] Billed as, "A show where real entrepreneurs pitch to real investorsâ€”for real money", the podcast aims to capture the essence of what happens when entrepreneurs pitch investors.[4]

The first episode of _The Pitch_ premiered on June 21, 2016. In September 2016, it was announced that the second season of the podcast would begin airing in 2017 and feature a panel of investors who will be making investment decisions on the spot.[5]

In June 2017, it was announced that _The Pitch_ had joined Gimlet Media for _The Pitch_'s second season.[6]
