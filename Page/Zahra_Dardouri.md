ZAHRA DARDOURI is an Algerian politician who is currently Minister of Post, Information Technologies and Communication.


Career

Dardouri was previously head of the Regulatory Authority for Post and Telecommunications. She was appointed Minister of Post, Information Technologies and Communication by President Abdelaziz Bouteflika in September 2013.[1][2]
