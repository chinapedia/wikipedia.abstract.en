SUMIRAN AMONKAR (born 28 July 1991) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Goa in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 20 October 2016.[2] On his debut, he scored a century.[3] He made his List A debut for Goa in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 25 February 2017.[4]
