Biography

Girolamo Candido was ordained a priest in the Order of Friars Minor.[2] On 20 Nov 1500, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Alexander VI as Bishop of Cariati e Cerenzia.[3][4] He served as Bishop of Cariati e Cerenzia until his death on 1504.[5][6]
