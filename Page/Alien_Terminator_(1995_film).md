_ALIEN TERMINATOR_ is a 1995 American horror film, written and directed by Dave Payne.[1] It stars Cassandra Leigh[2][3] and Maria Ford.[4]


Cast

-   Lisa Boyle (Cassandra Leigh) as Rachel
-   Maria Ford as McKay
-   Rodger Halston as Dean Taylor
-   Emile Levisetti as Pete
-   Kevin Alber as Dr Newton Fuller
-   Bob McFarland as a coach
-   Betsy Baldwin as Kelly Hill, a reporter[5]
