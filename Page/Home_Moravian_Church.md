Moravian_Home_Church_(pre-1907).tif HOME MORAVIAN CHURCH is a Moravian church located in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. The church was founded on November 13, 1771.[1]

Located in Old Salem, the church is visited by tourists and historians. It conducts weekly services.
