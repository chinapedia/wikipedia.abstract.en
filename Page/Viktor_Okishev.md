VIKTOR OKISHEV (born 4 February 1994 in Petropavl) is a Kazakh cyclist.[1]

He was suspended for 2 years in 2014 when he tested positive for anabolic steroids.


Major results

2013
    4th National Time Trial Championships

2014
    1st Asian U23 Time Trial Championships
