Missouri_Route_5_Roadside_Park.jpg MILL HOLLOW is a valley in northern Douglas County the Ozarks of southern Missouri.[1]

The headwaters of the stream that occupies the hollow are at and the confluence with Bryant Creek is at .[2] The stream in the valley is fed by a perennial spring with its source near the upper reaches of the valley. Missouri Route 5 runs parallel to the valley on the southeast side.[3][4] The valley is the location of Ava Roadside Park which lies adjacent to Route 5 five miles north of Ava.

Mill Hollow was named for a mill which once stood in the valley in the 1830s.[5]
