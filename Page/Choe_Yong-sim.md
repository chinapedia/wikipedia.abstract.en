CHOE YONG-SIM (; born 13 October 1990 in Pyeongyang) is a North Korean women's international footballer who plays as a defender.

She is a member of the North Korea women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2010 Asian Games,[1] and 2012 Summer Olympics. On club level she played for Pyongyang City in North Korea.
