_CHI LA SOW SRAVANTHI_ is a long-running serial on Gemini TV in India. The show was re-telecast in 2015 on Vanitha TV under the title _Sathamanam Bhavathi_.


Cast

-   Nanda Kishore as Jagan
-   Meena as Sravanthi
-   Bharani as Mahesh
-   Lakshmi as Saranya & Sravanthi
