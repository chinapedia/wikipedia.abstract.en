IBRAHIM ZAKARIA () served as International Commissioner of the Scouts of Syria.

In 1980, he was awarded the 140th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
