BRUSH CREEK is a stream in Osage County in central Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Gasconade River.

The stream headwaters are located at and the confluence with the Gasconade is at .[2] The stream source arises adjacent to Missouri Route U and it flows southeast parallel to that road to cross under Route E just west of Rich Fountain. The stream turns east and follows just south of Route E to its confluence just south of the Missouri Route 89 bridge over the Gasconade.[3]

Brush Creek was so named due to the abundance of brush near its course.[4]
