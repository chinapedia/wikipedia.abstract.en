MARK ANTHONY NIPAYA GERALDO (born 12 July 1991)[1] is a Filipino professional boxer.

He lost to Takuma Inoue for the OPBF super flyweight title.[2][3]

He has a loss to former world champion McJoe Arroyo and victories over world champion Jerwin Ancajas and Tanawat Phonnaku.[4][5]

In 2017, Geraldo won his first regional title, the WBO Oriental bantamweight title.[6][7][8]

He has been ranked at least as high as 3rd by the WBO and 9th by the IBF.[9]
