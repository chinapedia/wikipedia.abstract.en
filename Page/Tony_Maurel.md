TONY MAUREL (born 21 April 1993) is a French rugby league footballer who plays as a or on the for Toulouse Olympique in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Maurel is a French international.[2]

Maurel has previously played for Ayguevives, Belberaud Ramonville and the Toulouse Olympique Broncos.[3]
