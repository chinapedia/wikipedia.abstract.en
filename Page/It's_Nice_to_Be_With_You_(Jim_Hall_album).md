_IT'S NICE TO BE WITH YOU_ (subtitled _JIM HALL IN BERLIN_) is an album by guitarist Jim Hall which was recorded in 1969 during a European tour and released on the MPS label.[1][2]


Reception

AllMusic awarded the album 3 stars and its review by Ken Dryden states "this collectible release is well worth acquiring".[3]
