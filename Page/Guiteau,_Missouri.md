GUITEAU is an unincorporated community in Oregon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

The community had its start as a country store named after presidential assassin Charles J. Guiteau, which was intended to rival nearby Garfield, Missouri.[2] Besides the store, the community had a church,[3] and a schoolhouse.[4]
