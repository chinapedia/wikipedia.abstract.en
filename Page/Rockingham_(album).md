_ROCKINGHAM_ is the fifth album by Nerf Herder, released in March 2016.

The album was crowdfunded using PledgeMusic.[1] It was released digitally, on CD, and on Vinyl. There was a release party April 8, 2016, at the Troubadour (West Hollywood, California),[2] Allie Goertz was a special guest.[3][4]
