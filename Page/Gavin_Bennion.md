GAVIN BENNION (born 31 December 1993) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a or second row forward for the Swinton Lions in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Bennion has previously played for the Mackay Cutters and spent time on loan from the Warrington Wolves at the Swinton Lions and Featherstone Rovers.[2][3]
