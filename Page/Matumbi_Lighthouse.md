The MATUMBI LIGHTHOUSE is located at the south western tip of Pemba, in Tanzania.[1] The lighthouse is on a small island called Matumbi Makubwa.[2] The lighthouse's construction history is unknown, however, the 31m square stone tower was renovated in 2002 by Salem Construction limited from Zanzibar.[3]
