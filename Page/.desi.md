.DESI is a top-level domain (TLD) used in the Domain Name System of the Internet. It is operated by Desi Networks LLC.[1][2]

The stated purpose of the domain is to promote domain name owners. The word "desi" means local or countryhood. The domain refers to websites of desis in the Indian sub-continent and to culturally Indian individuals around the world.[3]
