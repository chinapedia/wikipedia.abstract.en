MATTEO ORSINI or _' Matteo Mignano de Orsini_' (died 1512) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Calvi Risorta (1505–1512)[1] and Bishop of Città Ducale (1502–1505).[2]


Biography

On 24 January 1502, Matteo Orsini was appointed during the papacy of Pope Alexander VI as Bishop of Città Ducale.[3][4][5] On 8 November 1505, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Julius II as Bishop of Calvi Risorta.[6][7] He served as Bishop of Calvi Risorta until his death in 1512.[8][9]
