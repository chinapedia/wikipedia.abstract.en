EDUARDO ÁLVAREZ AZNAR (born 1 January 1984) is a Spanish equestrian. He competed in the individual jumping competition at the 2016 Summer Olympics.[1]

Eduardo is a son of six-time Olympian Luis Álvarez de Cervera.
