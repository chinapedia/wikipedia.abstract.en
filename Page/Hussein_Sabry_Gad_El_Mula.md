HUSSEIN SABRY GAD EL MULA () served as the Treasurer of the Arab Scout Region.

In 1990, he was awarded the 213th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.
