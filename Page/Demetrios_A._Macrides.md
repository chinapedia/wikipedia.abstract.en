DEMETRIOS A. MACRIDES () served as the Chief Commissioner of the Scouts of Greece.[1][2][3]

In 1967, Macrides was awarded the 45th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[4]
