Pinto was Chief Scout of Uganda and served as the Chairman of the Africa Scout Committee.

He died in Nsambya after a car accident.[1]

In 2003, Pinto was awarded the 297th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2]
