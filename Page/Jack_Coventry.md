JACK COVENTRY (born 5 March 1994) is a rugby league footballer who plays for Keighley Cougars in Betfred League One. He plays as a prop.[1]

Coventry has previously spent time on loan at the Hunslet in Betfred League One.[2]

He previously played for Featherstone in the Betfred Championship.[3]
