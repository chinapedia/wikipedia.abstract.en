_PLAGIOSTROPHA BICOLOR_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 9 mm and 14 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the Philippines and Japan.
