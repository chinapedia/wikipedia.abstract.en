The 2000 LIGA PERDANA 2 season is the third season of Liga Perdana 2. A total of ten teams participated in the season.[1]

Kedah was relegated from Liga Perdana 1 to join the Liga Perdana 2.[2]

The season kicked off on 14 April 2000.[3] Kelantan won the title and was promoted to Liga Perdana 1 alongside Malacca.


Teams

Ten teams competing in the third season of Liga Perdana 2.[4]

-   Kelantan (2000 Liga Perdana 2 champions)

-   Malacca

-   Kedah

-   Kelantan JKR

-   Kelantan TNB

-   ATM

-   Johor FC

-   TMFC

-   KL Malay Mail

-   NS Chempaka
