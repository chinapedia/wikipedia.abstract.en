_' Lars Schänzler_' (born 24 August 1995) is a badminton player from Germany.[1]


Career

Schänzler started playing badminton at the aged of eight.[2] In 2013, he became the boys' singles runner-up at the U-19 German Championships. At the same year, he competed at the European Junior Championships reaching the quarter finals.[3] Together with the national team, he won the bronze medals at the 2014, 2016, 2018 European Men's Team Championships and at the 2017 European Mixed Team Championships.
