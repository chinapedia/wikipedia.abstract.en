KathrinSchmidt.JPG KATHRIN SCHMIDT (born 12 March 1958 in Gotha, Bezirk Erfurt), is a German writer.[1] She is known both for her poetry and prose.[2]


Selected works

-   _Du stirbst nicht_, Kiepenheuer & Witsch, Köln 2009


Awards

-   1988 Anna Seghers-Preis
-   1993 Leonce-und-Lena-Preis
-   2009 German Book Prize
-   2017 Thüringer Literaturpreis[3]
