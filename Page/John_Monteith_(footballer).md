JOHN BUCHANAN MONTEITH (5 December 1883 – 27 September 1918) was a Scottish professional football right back who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

Monteith served as a gunner in the Royal Garrison Artillery during the First World War and was killed in action in Pas-de-Calais on 27 September 1918.[2][3] He was buried in Quéant Communal Cemetery British Extension.[4]
