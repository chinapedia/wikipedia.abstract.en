_ACAVUS PHOENIX_, the GIANT LAND SNAIL, is a species of air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Acavidae.

Two subspecies recognized.[1]


Description

Acavus_phoenix_(Pfeiffer,_1854)_(4540126345).jpg _Acavus phoenix_ is the most common _Acavus_ species on the island. Shell about 60mm in diameter[2] and is rose colored and body black colored.[3]


Distribution

This species is endemic to Sri Lanka. It is confined to wet zone of the country.


Subspecies

-   _Acavus phoenix phoenix_
-   _Acavus phoenix castaneus_
