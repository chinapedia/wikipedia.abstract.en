_ATAGEMA SCABRIUSCULA_ is a species of sea slug or dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Discodorididae.


Distribution

This species was described from the Hawaiian Islands. It is a fairly common species in the intertidal zone and shallow water.[1]
