LOCH BUIDHE is a fresh water loch on Rannoch Moor,Argyll and Bute within Highland council area, Scotland..[1] [2]

It is situated about north of Bridge of Orchy. It lies to the south of Lochan na Stainge, and to the west of Lochan na h-Achlaise. The A82 road crosses Rannoch Moor to the west of the loch, while the West Highland Way long-distance path passes by to the west.[3] The name is Gaelic for _yellow loch_.
