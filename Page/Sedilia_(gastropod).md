_SEDILIA_ is a genus of sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Species

Species within the genus _Sedilia_ include:

-   † _Sedilia aphanitoma_ Dall 1892
-   _Sedilia compacta_ Faber, 2011
-   † _Sedilia sedilia_ (Dall, 1890)

Species brought into synonymy:

-   _Sedilia melanacme_ (E. A. Smith, 1882): synonym of _Buchema melanacme_ (E. A. Smith, 1882)
