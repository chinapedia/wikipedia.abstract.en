"MOTHERLAND" is Crystal Kay's fourteenth single, released on May 12, 2004. It was used as the third ending theme for the anime adaption of _Fullmetal Alchemist_; it was the ending theme from episodes 26 to 41. The single debuted at number 9 on the Oricon chart, her second single to debut in the top ten.
