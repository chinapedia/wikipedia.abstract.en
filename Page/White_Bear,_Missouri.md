WHITE BEAR is an unincorporated community in Marion County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Variant names were Bear Creek, Bear Creek Station, and Whiteledge.[2] A post office called White Ledge was established in 1892, and remained in operation until 1904.[3] White Bear was named for a product made by a local lime kiln.[4]
