NAZAR LYTVYN (born March 22, 1983) is a Ukrainian footballer who played in the Ukrainian First League, Ukrainian Second League, and Canadian Soccer League


Playing career

Lytvyn began his career in 1998 with FC Karpaty-2 Lviv in the Ukrainian Second League, and also played with FC Karpaty-3 Lviv.[1] He later briefly played with FC Sokil Zolochiv before signing with FC Rava Rava-Ruska. During his tenure with Rava he captured the Druha Liha A championship in 2004. In 2006, he was transferred to FC Lviv of the Ukrainian First League, and signed with FC Halychyna Lviv in 2007. He later played with FC Arsenal-Kyivshchyna Bila Tserkva, and Karpaty Kamenka-Bug.

In 2016, he went overseas to Canada to sign with FC Ukraine United of the Canadian Soccer League.[2] In his second season he assisted FC Ukraine in achieving a perfect season, and winning the Second Division Championship.[3]
