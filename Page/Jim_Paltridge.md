JAMES PALTRIDGE (24 July 1891 – 14 June 1980) was an English professional football left half who played in the Football League for Chesterfield.[1][2][3]


Personal life

Paltridge served as a staff sergeant in the Royal Army Service Corps during the First World War.[4]
