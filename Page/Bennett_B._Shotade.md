BENNETT B. SHOTADE served as the Africa Regional Scout Commissioner of the World Scout Bureau.

In 1977, Shotade was awarded the 112th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1] He was also a recipient of the Silver World Award.[2]
