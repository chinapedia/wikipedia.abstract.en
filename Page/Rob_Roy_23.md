The ROB ROY 23 is an American sailboat, that was designed by Edward S. Brewer and first built in 1980. The design is out of production.[1][2][3]

The boat was built by Marine Concepts in Tarpon Springs, Florida, United States. The design is a cabin version of the Sun Seeker 23 daysailer, also built by Marine Concepts.[4][5]
