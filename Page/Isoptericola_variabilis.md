_ISOPTERICOLA VARIABILIS_ is a facultative anaerobic and nitrile-hydrolysing bacterium from the genus of _Isoptericola_ which has been isolated from the hindgut of the termite _Mastotermes darwiniensis_ in Germany.[1][2][3][4][5]
