_PARADISE HOTEL_ (also known as _Hotel Rai_, _Хотел Рай_) is a 2010 Bulgarian documentary film directed by Sophia Tzavella. It depicts the lives of Roma living in an apartment block in a village in Yambol Province, Bulgaria.

The film received the FIPRESCI award for a film in the International selection at the 13th Thessaloniki International Film Festival.
