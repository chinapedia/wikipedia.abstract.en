AGUSTÍN DOFFO (born 25 May 1995) is an Argentine footballer who plays for O'Higgins as a left winger.


Club career

Born in Oliva, Córdoba, Doffo joined Vélez Sarsfield's youth setup in 2010.[1] He made his first team debut on 23 May 2016, coming on as a late substitute for fellow youth graduate Nicolás Delgadillo in a 3–1 away win against Banfield for the Primera División championship; he also provided an assist for Mariano Pavone's last goal.[2]

In August 2016 Doffo moved to Villarreal CF, signing a one-year loan deal and being assigned to the reserves in Segunda División B.[3]
