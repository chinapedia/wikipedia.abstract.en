HANS BANG (11 August 1863 – 21 April 1945) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Risør to merchant Hans Bang and Adama Schanche. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1886, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1919. He participated in several governmental commissions, particularly on tax issues, and chaired _Trustkontrollrådet_ from 1926.[1]
