ANTONINA VITALYEVNA SKOROBOGATCHENKO (; born 14 February 1999) is a Russian handballer for HC Kuban Krasnodar and the Russian national team.[1]

In the summer of 2017, Skorobogatchenko tested positive for meldonium at the 2017 European Junior Championship.[2]


Individual awards

-   All-Star Right Back of the EHF U-17 European Championship: 2015
-   All-Star Right Back of the IHF Junior World Championship: 2016
-   All-Star Right Back of the IHF Youth World Championship: 2016
