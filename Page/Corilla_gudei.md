_CORILLA GUDEI_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Corillidae.

The specific name _gudei_ is in honour of malacologist Gerard Pierre Laurent Kalshoven Gude.


Distribution

Distribution of _Corilla gudei_ includes Sri Lanka.
