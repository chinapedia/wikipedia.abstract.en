HANNAH SMITH (born 1986) is a Welsh international lawn and indoor bowler.[1]

Smith from Carmarthen won a bronze medal in the pairs at the 2010 Commonwealth Games in Delhi and two years later won another bronze in the triples at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Adelaide.[2]
