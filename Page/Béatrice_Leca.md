BÉATRICE LECA (born 1970 in Paris) is a contemporary French writer, winner of the 1996 edition of the Prix Fénéon.


Publications

-   1996: _Technique du marbre_, Éditions du Seuil, Prix Fénéon
-   1999: _Des années encore_, Seuil
-   2004: _Aux bords des forêts_, Melville
