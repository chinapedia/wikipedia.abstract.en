ANIL YÜKSEL (born 19 May 1990) is a Dutch–born Turkish tennis player.

Yüksel has a career high ATP singles ranking of 499 achieved on 19 September 2016. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 528, achieved on 8 February 2016. Yüksel has won 4 ITF doubles titles.

Yüksel has represented Turkey at Davis Cup, where he has a win-loss record of 3–2.
