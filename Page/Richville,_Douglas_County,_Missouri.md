Old_store_building_at_Richville,_Missouri.jpg RICHVILLE is an unincorporated community in southeastern Douglas County, Missouri, United States.[1] Richville is located at the intersection of Missouri State Route 14 and State route W and a short distance west of the intersection of Route 14 and Missouri State Route 181. The site is located on a high ridge at an elevation of .[2] Sauls Knob at [3] lies just to the southeast on the south side of Route 14.[4]


History

A post office called Richville was established in 1870, and remained in operation until 1933.[5] The community was named after the local Rich family.[6]
