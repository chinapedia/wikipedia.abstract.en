SHAMIL MAGOMEDOVICH SAADUYEV (; born 10 November 1996) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Legion-Dynamo Makhachkala.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Legion-Dynamo Makhachkala on 3 October 2016 in a game against FC Kuban-2 Krasnodar.[1]
