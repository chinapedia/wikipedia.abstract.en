NADIGUDEM MANDAL is one of the 23 mandals in Suryapet district of the Indian state of Telangana.[1] It is under the administration of Kodad revenue division with its headquarters at Nadigudem. It is bounded by Munagala mandal towards South, Mothey mandal towards North, Kodad mandal towards East, Chilkur mandal towards South.


Demographics

Mothey mandal is having a population of 29,761 . Eklaskhan Peta is the smallest Village and Nadigudem is the biggest Village in the mandal.
