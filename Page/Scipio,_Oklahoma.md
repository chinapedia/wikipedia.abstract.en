SCIPIO is an unincorporated community in Pittsburg County, Oklahoma, United States.[1] The community is northwest of McAlester. A post office opened at Scipio on January 24, 1980. The community was named for nearby Scipio Creek, which was in turn named for the Roman general Scipio Africanus.[2]
