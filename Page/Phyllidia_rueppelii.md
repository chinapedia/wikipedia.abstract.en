_PHYLLIDIA RUEPPELII_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusk in the family Phyllidiidae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the Red Sea. It has been reported from the Gulf of Oman.[2]
