_DENDRONOTUS ARCTICUS_ is a species of sea slugs, a dendronotid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusc in the family Dendronotidae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from four specimens collected together at 15 m depth in the Laptev Sea, .[2]
