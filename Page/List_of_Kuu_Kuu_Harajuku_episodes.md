_Kuu Kuu Harajuku_ (originally titled _KooKoo Harajuku_[1]) is an animated children's television series created by Gwen Stefani for Network Ten,[2] based on her _Harajuku Lovers_ brand. The series debuted on Eleven in Australia on November 1, 2015.[3]
