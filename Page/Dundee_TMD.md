DUNDEE TMD is a Traction Maintenance Depot located in Dundee, Scotland. The depot is situated on the East Coast Main Line and is near Dundee station.

The depot code is DE.


Allocation

As of 2016, the depot's allocation consists of Abellio ScotRail Class 158 Express Sprinters.[1]
