ZACK KENNEDY (born 15 May 1995) is an American tennis player.

Kennedy has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 818 achieved on 12 September 2016.

Kennedy made his ATP main draw debut at the 2016 BB&T Atlanta Open in the doubles draw partnering Christopher Eubanks. Kennedy plays college tennis at Georgia State University.[1]
