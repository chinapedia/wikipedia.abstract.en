The 2002 LIGA PERDANA 2 season is the fifth season of Liga Perdana 2. A total of 12 teams participated in the season.[1]

Johor was relegated from Liga Perdana 1 to join the Liga Perdana 2.[2]

The season kicked off on 27 January 2002.[3] Kedah won the title and was promoted to Liga Perdana 1 alongside TM.


Teams

12 teams competing in the fifth season of Liga Perdana 2.[4]

-   Kedah (2002 Liga Perdana 2 champions)

-   TM

-   MPPJ

-   Selangor Public Bank FC

-   Brunei

-   Johor
