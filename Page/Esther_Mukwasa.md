'''Esther Mukwasa ''' (born 24 October 1996) is a Zambian footballer who plays as a midfielder for the Zambia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship.[1][2][3] On club level she played for Moba Queens in Zambia.[4]
