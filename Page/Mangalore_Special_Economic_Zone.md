MANGALORE SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE or MSEZ is a Special Economic Zone spread across 1600 acres in Mangalore, Karnataka, India

MSEZ is managed by _Mangalore Special Economic Zone Limited(MSEZL)_. The MSEZL is located 15 km from Mangalore city center, off Cochin-Mumbai NH 66, 5 km from Mangalore International Airport and 8 km from all-weather deep draft sea port, New Mangalore Port.

MSEZL is jointly promoted by Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC), a Fortune 500 Company and Infrastructure Leasing & Finance Services (IL&FS), one of India's leading infrastructure development and finance companies, Karnataka Industrial Area Development Board (KIADB) and Kanara Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KCCI). A unique combination of Government entities, a large financial institution and an apex chamber brings in the expertise to develop MSEZL with world-class industrial infrastructure.[1][2]
