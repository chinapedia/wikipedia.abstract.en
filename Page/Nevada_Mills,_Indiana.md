NEVADA MILLS is an unincorporated community in Jamestown Township, Steuben County, in the U.S. state of Indiana.[1]


History

A post office was established at Nevada Mills in 1867, and remained in operation until 1905.[2] An old variant name of the community was called Millville.[3]
