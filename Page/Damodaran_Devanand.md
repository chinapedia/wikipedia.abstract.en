DAMODARAN DEVANAND (born 31 July 1972) is a first-class cricketer who played for Tamil Nadu in the Ranji Trophy. He was born in Ranipettai, Tamil Nadu, India.[1]

Damodaran is a right-hand batsman and Right-arm Medium bowler. He took a hat-trick in the 2001-02 Ranji Trophy playing for Tamil Nadu against Orissa.[2]
