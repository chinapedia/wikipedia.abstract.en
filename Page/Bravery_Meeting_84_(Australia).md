The BRAVERY COUNCIL OF AUSTRALIA MEETING 84 HONOURS LIST was announced by the Governor General of Australia on 17 March 2016.[1]

Awards were announced for the Star of Courage,[2] Bar to the Bravery Medal,[3] the Bravery Medal,[4] Commendation for Brave Conduct[5] and Group Bravery Citation.[6]
