AURORA SPRINGS is an unincorporated community in northern Miller County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] The community is located on Saline Creek, just south of Eldon and west of U.S. Route 54.[2]


History

Aurora Springs was platted in 1880, and named for a mineral spring near the original town site.[3] A post office called Aurora Springs was established in 1882, and remained in operation until 1912.[4]
