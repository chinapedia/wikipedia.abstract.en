SAMUEL MUKOOZA (born December 4, 1989), is an Ugandan professional basketball player. In 2016/17, he was a Free agent. His last professional team was Trelleborg Basket of the Swedish Basketettan basketball league.

He represented Uganda's national basketball team at the 2015 AfroBasket, where he was his team's best passer.[1]
