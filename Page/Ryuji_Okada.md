Club career

Ryuji Okada was born in Shizuoka Prefecture on September 6, 1974. After graduating from high school, he joined Shimizu S-Pulse in 1993. He debuted in J.League Cup against Gamba Osaka on October 23, 1993.[1] However he could only play this match and retired end of 1994 season.
