FAISAL SHAIKH (born 2 December 1997) is a first-class cricketer who played for Goa and Mumbai in the Ranji Trophy. He was born in Bombay, Maharashtra, India.[1]

Faisal is a right-hand batsman and right-arm offbreak bowler. He took a 9/29 in the 2002-03 Ranji Trophy playing for Goa against Services[2] which is fifth best bowling figure in the Ranji trophy.[3]
