ROBERT SCOTT MILLS was Dean of Dunedin from 1973 until 1991.

Mills was educated at the University of New Zealand and ordained in 1960. He was a curate at St Stephen, Westminster[1] from 1955 to 1960. After a curacy at Whangarei he was Chaplain to the Bishop of Nelson. He was Vicar of Welling from 1960 to 1965; Dean of Dunedin from 1965 to 1973;[2] Vicar of Blockhouse Bay from 1969 to 1973.[3]
