'' TATHAGATA (HE XUNTIAN)'' _(如来如去 )_ is a music work[1][2][3] for Music Ceremony: _Eulogy of Lingshan Lucky_,[4] composed by He Xuntian.[5][6]


Summary

_Tathagata_ has eight movements:

1.  The Heaven Outside of Heaven 天外天
2.  Tathagata 达塔伽达
3.  Meditation 冥思
4.  The Heart of the Void 虚空之心
5.  The Realm of Forms 色界
6.  The Wind the Trees 树有风
7.  The Dance of the Four Dharmadhatus 四方之舞
8.  Tathagata 如来如去[7][8][9]


First performance

March 29, 2009
Lingshan Buddhist Palace, Wuxi[10][11]
