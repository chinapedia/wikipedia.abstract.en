'''Shirley Cloete ''' (born 13 January 1982) is a Namibian women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Namibia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship. On club level she played for Okahandja Beauties FC in Namibia.[1][2]
