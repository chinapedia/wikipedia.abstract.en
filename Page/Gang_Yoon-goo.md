GANG YOON-GOO (born February 8, 1993) is a South Korean football player who has played in the J2 League[1] and currently plays for Daegu FC in the K League 1.


Playing career

Gang Yoon-goo played for J2 League club; Vissel Kobe, Oita Trinita and Ehime FC from 2013 to 2015.
