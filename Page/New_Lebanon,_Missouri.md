New_Lebanon_Cemetery,_New_Lebanon,_Missouri.jpg NEW LEBANON is an unincorporated community in Cooper County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called New Lebanon was established in 1887, and remained in operation until 1906.[2] The community took its name from the local New Lebanon Church.[3]

New Lebanon Cumberland Presbyterian Church and School and New Lebanon Historic District are listed on the National Register of Historic Places.[4]
