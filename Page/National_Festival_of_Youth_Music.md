The NATIONAL FESTIVAL OF YOUTH MUSIC (),[1] also referred to as the YOUTH MUSIC FESTIVAL,[2] is a music festival in Iran that highlights Iranian music performed by young musicians.

The festival took place for the first time in December 1999.[3] Since then, it has been taking place seasonally at several halls in Tehran, including the Roudaki Hall,[4] organized by the Iran Music Association,[5] the House of Music, the Music Office of the Ministry of Ershad, and the Rudaki Foundation.[6]
