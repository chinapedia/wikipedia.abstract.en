_WESTERN BLOOD_ is a lost[1] 1918 silent western film directed by Lynn Reynolds and starring Tom Mix. It was produced and released by Fox Film Corporation.[2]


Cast

-   Tom Mix - Tex Wilson
-   Victoria Forde - Roberta Stephens
-   Frank Clark - Col. Stephens
-   Barney Furey - Wallace Payton
-   Pat Chrisman - Juan
-   Buck Jones - Cowboy (*as Buck Gebhardt)
