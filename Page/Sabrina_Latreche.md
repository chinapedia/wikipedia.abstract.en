SABRINA LATRECHE (born 30 July 1993) is an Algerian chess player.

She has been a Woman International Master (WIM) since 2011

She was joint winner of the 2014 and 2016 Women's [[Arab Chess Championship], and 2018 arab chess championdship bliz.

She won the Algerian Women's chess championship four times: in 2011 and three times in a row from 2015 to 2017.

She represented Algeria at seven Olympiads from 2006 to 2018.

She won a gold medal for the second board at Chess at the 2011 All-Africa Games, and was part of the Algerian team which won the team silver medal.

She played in the Women's World Chess Championship 2017, but went out in the first round to Alexandra Kosteniuk.
