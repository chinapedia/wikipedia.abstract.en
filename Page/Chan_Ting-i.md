CHAN TING-I () is a Taiwanese politician. She is also known by the name NICOLE CHAN.[1] Chan became the chairperson of the National Communications Commission on 1 August 2016.[2] She resigned the position on 3 April 2019.[3]


Early personal life

Chan's father Chan Yi-chang was a member of the Kuomintang and served on the Control Yuan.[4] Chan Tang-i obtained her bachelor's degree in law from National Taiwan University in 1990, master's degree in law from University of London in the United Kingdom in 1990 and master of business administration from National Chengchi University in 2008.[5][6]
