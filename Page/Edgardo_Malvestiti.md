EDGARDO MALVESTITI (born 02 May 1969) is a former Argentine professional football player and currently manager.


Coaching Career

Águila

In June 2016, Malvestiti signed with Águila of the Salvadoran Primera División, replacing Juan Ramón Sánchez. On November 2016, Malvestiti was replaced by Darío Larrosa. The main cause of his dismissal was the filtering of some audio where he referred to players and club directives in a personal way.
