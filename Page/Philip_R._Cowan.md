PHILIP R. COWAN served as the Secretary on Scouting with the Handicapped of the World Scout Bureau.

In 1967, Cowan was awarded the 42nd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
