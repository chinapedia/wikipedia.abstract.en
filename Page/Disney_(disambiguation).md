DISNEY or The Walt Disney Company is an American diversified media conglomerate.

DISNEY may also refer to:


Units of The Walt Disney Company

-   The Walt Disney Company, the publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange
    -   Disney Enterprises, Inc., the copyright holder and trademark owner of everything Disney-branded
    -   Walt Disney Studios (Burbank), the company headquarters and studio lot in Burbank, California
    -   Walt Disney Studios (division), the movie division
        -   Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, a theatrical distribution company
        -   Walt Disney Pictures, a film banner and production company
            -   Walt Disney Animation Studios, the main animation studio (formerly known as Walt Disney Feature Animation)
        -   Walt Disney Studios Home Entertainment, a home entertainment distribution company
        -   Disney Theatrical Group
            -   Disney Theatrical Productions, a stage production company
        -   Disney Music Group, the music publishing arm
            -   Walt Disney Records, Disney-branded music label
    -   Disney Parks, Experiences and Products, division which manages and builds the Disney theme parks and vacation resorts, Disney Stores, merchandising, publishing and digital media, games and interactive experiences
        -   Walt Disney World
        -   Disneyland Resort
            -   Disneyland
            -   Disney California Adventure
        -   Disneyland Paris
        -   Hong Kong Disneyland Resort
        -   Tokyo Disney Resort
        -   Shanghai Disney Resort
        -   Disney Signature Experiences, non-theme park travel units
            -   Adventures by Disney
            -   Disney Cruise Line
            -   Disney Vacation Club
        -   Walt Disney Imagineering, the research and development arm, responsible for the creation, design, and construction of Disney theme parks and attractions worldwide
        -   Disney Publishing Worldwide, publishing subsidiary
    -   Disney Media Networks
        -   Walt Disney Television
            -   Disney Channel, a cable TV network
            -   Walt Disney Television, a United States-based TV production company
            -   Disney Television Animation, a TV studio dedicated to animation
            -   Capital Disney, a Disney radio station in the United Kingdom
            -   Radio Disney, a Disney radio station in the United States
