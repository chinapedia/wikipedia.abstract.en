JEANNE KIRKTON (born October 11, 1953) is an American politician. She was a member of the Missouri House of Representatives from 2009 to 2017. She is a member of the Democratic party.[1]

She was a member of the Webster Groves City Council from 2006 to 2008.[2][3] She was a critical care nurse from 1973 to 1982 and a nurse anesthetist from 1982 to 1992.[4]

Kirkton is a 1971 graduate of St. Charles High School.[5] She has a degree in nursing from Maryville University (1973), a certificate from Barnes Hospital School of Nurse Anesthesia (1982), and a bachelor's degree in history and political science from Webster University (1996).[6][7]
