MARIANNA CARLEVARIJS (1703 – 1750) was an Italian painter, active mainly in Venice, creating pastel portraits.


Biography

Marianna was the daughter of the prominent Venetian painter of vedute, Luca Carlevarijs. She was a friend, and pupil in the style and subject matter, of Rosalba Carriera.[1]


Works

-   _Portrait of Girolamo Maria Balbi_, sala dei pastelli, Ca Rezzonico Museum, Venice
-   _Portrait of Cornelia Foscolo Balbi_, sala dei pastelli, Ca Rezzonico Museum, Venice
-   _Portrait of Marco e Caterina Balbi_, sala dei pastelli, Ca Rezzonico Museum, Venice[2]
-   _Portrait of a Young Woman_, Private Collection[3]
-   _Portrait of Unknown Gentleman_[4]
-   _Allegory of Peace_, Museo Civici of Vicenza[5]
