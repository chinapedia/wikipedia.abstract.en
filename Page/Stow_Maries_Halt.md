STOW MARIES HALT is a 2.2 hectare nature reserve south of Stow Maries in Essex. It is owned and managed by the Essex Wildlife Trust.[1]

The former Stow St Mary Halt railway station has marshes, a pond and scrub, together with adjoining pasture which is also part of the reserve. Butterflies include purple and white-letter hairstreaks, and there are flowers such as common fleabane and wild carrot.[2]

There is access from Church Lane.[3]
