WILLIAMS-GIERTH HOUSE, also known as THE CASTLE HOUSE is a historic home located at Poplar Bluff, Butler County, Missouri. It was built in 1892, and is a large 2 1/2-story, irregular plan, Shingle Style dwelling. It features a reconstructed wraparound porch, two turrets, and a hipped roof with polygonal dormer.[1]

It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2012.[2]
