ORCHARD CREEK is a stream in Oregon County in the Ozarks of southern Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Warm Fork Spring River.

The stream headwaters are at and the confluence with Warm Fork is at .[2]

Orchard Creek, historically called "Orchard Branch", was so named on account of orchards near its course.[3]
