MEIERHOFFER SAND COMPANY OFFICE BUILDING was a historic office building located at Boonville, Cooper County, Missouri. It was built about 1900, and was a one-story, rectangular vernacular brick building with a gable roof. An addition was constructed about 1910 which housed a wagon scale. By 1929, the building was used as a grocery store and was later converted to a dwelling.[1] It has apparently been demolished.

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1990.[2]
