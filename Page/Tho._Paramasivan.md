THO. PARAMASIVAN (simply called as THO PA, born 1950) is an Indian anthropologist and writer of Tamil ancestry. He was a professor in the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University. [1] [2]


Books

-   Alagar Kovil
-   Ariyapatatha Tamilagam
-   Panpaatu Asaivugal
