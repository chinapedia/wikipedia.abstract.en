CHRISTIAN FASSNACHT (born 11 November 1993) is a Swiss footballer who plays as a winger for Young Boys. [1]


Club career

Fassnacht was part of the Young Boys squad that won the 2017–18 Swiss Super League, their first league title in 32 years.[2] He helped winning it by scoring 11 goals during the season.[3]


International career

Fassnacht made his debut with the Swiss senior national team (he never represented Switzerland before at any level) in a 2–1 away loss against Belgium.[4]


Honours

YOUNG BOYS

-   Swiss Super League: 2017–18
