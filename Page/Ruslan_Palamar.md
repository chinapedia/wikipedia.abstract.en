RUSLAN PALAMAR (; born 9 August 1993 in Odessa, Ukraine) is a Ukrainian football midfielder who currently plays for Balkany Zorya in the Ukrainian First League.

Palamar is a product of FC Chornomorets Odessa youth sportive system and spent a time playing for FC Chornomorets Odessa in the Ukrainian Premier League Reserves as well few games for its senior team in the Premiers. His first coach was A.Baranov.[1]

In 2015, he joined a new amateur club FC Zhemchuzhyna Odessa.

On 11 June 2018 Palamar signed contract witch FC Balkany Zorya after his previous club Zhemchuzhyna was dissolved.[2]
