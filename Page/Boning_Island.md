BONING ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] The island lies north from Port Blair.


Geography

The island belongs to the West Baratang Group and east of Port Anson and Talakaicha Island.


Administration

Politically, Boning Island, along neighboring Baratang Islands, is part of Rangat Taluk.[2]
