_PARALEPRODERA INSIDIOSA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Francis Polkinghorne Pascoe in 1888, originally under the genus _Leprodera_. It is known from India, Myanmar, Malaysia, Laos, Taiwan, Cambodia, and Thailand. It contains the varietas _Paraleprodera insidiosa var. unimaculata_.[1]
