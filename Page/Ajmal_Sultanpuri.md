AJMAL SULTANPURI is an Indian poet in Urdu language. He is a native village of Harakhpur in Sultanpur District, Uttar Pradesh.[1]

He was awarded the Lifetime Achievement Award by Uttar Pradesh Urdu Academy in March 2016, for his contribution to Urdu poetry.[2][3]

_Kahan hai mera Hindustan_[4] and _Agar main tera Shahjahan_ are two of his popular poems, which he recites at _mushairas_ (poetry recitals).
