IBRAHIM EL KHALIL BEKAKCHI (born January 10, 1992 in Sétif) is an Algerian footballer who plays for JS Saoura in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1.


Club career

In July 2014, Bekakchi was loaned out to CA Bordj Bou Arréridj for two years.[1]


International

In 2009, Bekakchi was selected as a member of the Algeria under-17 team at the 2009 FIFA U-17 World Cup in Nigeria.[2]
