LA MESILLA is a census-designated place in Río Arriba County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 1,772 as of the 2010 census.[1]


Geography

La Mesilla is located at coordinates . According to the United States Census Bureau, La Mesilla has a total area of 17.67 square kilometers, of which 17.57 km² is land and (0.82%) 0.1 km² is water.[2]


Demographics

According to the 2010 census,[3] 1772 people were living in La Mesilla. The population density was 151.9 inhabitants per square kilometer. Of the 1772 inhabitants, La Mesilla was composed by 68.34% White, 0.34% were African American, 1.35% were Native American, 0.4% were Asian, 0% were Pacific Islanders, the 25.62% were of other races and 3.95% from two or more races. Of the total population 73.36% were Hispanic or Latino of any race[4]
