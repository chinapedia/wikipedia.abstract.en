MARY BETH BOWIE (born 27 October 1978)[1] is a Canadian women's international footballer who played as a midfielder.[2] She has competed for the Canada women's national soccer team. She was part of the team at the 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup.[3]

Born in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Bowie grew up in Dartmouth and attended Dalhousie University,[4] before playing for the Connecticut Huskies women's soccer program in the U.S.[5] Overall, Bowie made 13 appearances for the Canadian national team, including five starts.[6]

Bowie played for the Boston Renegades in the W League, and followed that up by playing locally with Scotia Soccer Club, Athens Soccer Club and Halifax City Soccer Club.[7]
