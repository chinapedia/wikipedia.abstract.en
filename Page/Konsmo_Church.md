KONSMO CHURCH () is a parish church in Audnedal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Konsmo. The church is part of the Konsmo parish in the Mandal deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden, cruciform church was built in 1802 by the architect Arnt Listad. The church seats about 400 people.[1][2]
