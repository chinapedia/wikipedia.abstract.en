DANIL IGOREVICH KOLESNIKOV (; born 24 February 1998) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Mashuk-KMV Pyatigorsk.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Dynamo Stavropol on 13 November 2016 in a game against FC SKA Rostov-on-Don.[1]
