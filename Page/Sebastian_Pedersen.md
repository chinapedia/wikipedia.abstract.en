SEBASTIAN PEDERSEN (born 8 June 1999) is a Norwegian football striker who currently plays for Eliteserien side Strømsgodset.[1]

Pedersen, who is a younger brother of Marcus Pedersen, started his career in HamKam. At the age of 16, he went from their junior team to that of Stabæk, and also started attending the Norwegian College of Elite Sport.[2] He made his first-team debut one year later, as a late-match substitute in August 2016 against Haugesund.[3] He scored his first goal in his third match as a substitute, against Lillestrøm.

He signed for Strømsgodset in January 2018.[4]
