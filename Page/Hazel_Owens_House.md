HAZEL OWENS HOUSE, also known as the COMPANY HOTEL, is a historic home located at Grandin, Carter County, Missouri. It was built between 1888 and 1909 by the Missouri Lumber and Mining Company. It is a two-story, "L"-plan, vernacular frame dwelling with a gable roof and weatherboard siding. There is a screened-in front porch. It retains its hotel-like appearance and still has 22 interior rooms.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.[2]
