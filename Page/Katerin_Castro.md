'''Katerin Castro ''' (born 21 November 1991) is a Colombian footballer who played as a forward for the Colombia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1] At the club level, she played for Club Deportivo Estudiantes F.C. in Colombia.[2]
