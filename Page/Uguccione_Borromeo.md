Biography

On 19 February 1304, Uguccione Borromeo was appointed during the papacy of Pope Benedict XI as Bishop of Novara.[2][3] He served as Bishop of Novara until his death in 1329.[4][5]

While bishop, he was the principal consecrator of Castone Torriani, Archbishop of Milan (1308).[6]
