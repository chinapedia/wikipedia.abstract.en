MORGAN WATKINS is a British film, television and stage actor from Camden, London. He trained at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art.

He played Norman Pike in the BBC television series _The Hour_ and Rottweiler in the 2014 film _Kingsman: The Secret Service_.[1]

He played Len in the revival of Edward Bond's _Saved_ at the Lyric Hammersmith.

He plays George Gissing in _The Limehouse Golem_ starring Bill Nighy, Olivia Cooke and Douglas Booth.[2]
