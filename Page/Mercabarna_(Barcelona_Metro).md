MERCABARNA is a Barcelona Metro station, located in the Mercabarna market complex of the Zona Franca in the Sants-Montju√Øc district of Barcelona. The station is served by line L9.[1][2]

The station is located underneath the western approach to the Mercabarna market, with its only entrance provided by a staircase and elevator at the intersection of _Carrer K_ and the _Carrer Transversal 6,_ with a ticket office below ground. The two long side platforms are at a lower level.[3][4]

The station was opened in 2016, at the time of the extension of line L9 from Zona Universitaria station to Aeroport T1 station.
