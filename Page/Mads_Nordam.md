MADS NORDAM (born 14 May 1995) is a Danish footballer who plays for the Danish 1st Division club Næstved BK.[1][2]


Career

Lyngby Boldklub

On 5 July 2014 it was announced, that Lyngby Boldklub had signed Nordam from the youth sector of F.C. Copenhagen.[3]

Fremad Amager

On 22 December 2016 Lyngby confirmed, that Nordam had signed a contract with Danish 1st Division-side Fremad Amager.[4]
