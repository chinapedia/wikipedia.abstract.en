The LLOYD WRIGHT HOME AND STUDIO is a historic two-story house in West Hollywood, California, U.S.. It was built in 1927. It was designed by architect Lloyd Wright a son of Frank Lloyd Wright. [1] It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since April 6, 1987.[2]
