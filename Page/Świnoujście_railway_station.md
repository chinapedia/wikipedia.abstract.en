ŚWINOUJŚCIE RAILWAY STATION is the principal railway station in the town of Świnoujście, in the West Pomeranian Voivodeship, Poland. The station and is located on the Szczecin Dąbie–Świnoujście Port railway. The train services are operated by PKP and Przewozy Regionalne.


Train services

The following trains run from this station:

-   Intercity: _Świnoujście - Szczecin - Stargard - Krzyz - Poznan - Kutno - Warsaw - Lublin_
-   Intercity: _Świnoujście - Szczecin - Stargard - Krzyz - Poznan - Kutno - Lodz - Krakow_
-   Intercity: _Świnoujście - Szczecin - Stargard - Krzyz - Poznan - Ostrow Wielkopolski - Katowice/Krakow_
-   Intercity: _Świnoujście - Szczecin - Stargard - Krzyz - Poznan - Leszno - Wroclaw - Opole - Katowice - Krakow - Rzeszow - Przemysl_
-   Intercity: _Świnoujście - Szczecin - Kostrzyn - Rzepin - Zielona Gora - Wroclaw_
-   Regional (R): _Świnoujście - Szczecin - Stargard - Dobiegniew - Krzyz - Wronki - Poznan_


Public transport

The coach station is located opposite the train station. The ferry to the main part of Świnoujście is also nearby, this operates every 20 minutes. Local bus services 1, 5, 7 and 10 serve the station.
