AUGUSTINE WILLIAMS (born 3 August 1997) is a Sierra Leonean footballer who currently plays for LA Galaxy II in the USL Championship.


Career

Williams joined United Soccer League side Portland Timbers 2 on 3 August 2016.[1]

After two seasons in Portland, Williams joined LA Galaxy II on 28 January 2019.[2]
