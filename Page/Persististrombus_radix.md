_PERSISTISTROMBUS RADIX_ is an extinct species of fossil sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Strombidae, the conchs.[1]


Fossil record

Fossils of _Persististrombus radix_ are found in marine strata of the Oligocene (age range: from 28.4 to 23.03 million years ago.). Fossils are known from Bulgaria, France, Greece, India, Iran and Somalia.[2]
