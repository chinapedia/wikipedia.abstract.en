KANWAR ABHINAY (born 16 June 1991) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Himachal Pradesh in the 2012–13 Ranji Trophy on 1 December 2012.[2] He made his List A debut for Himachal Pradesh in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 25 February 2017.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Himachal Pradesh in the 2017–18 Zonal T20 League on 8 January 2018.[4]
