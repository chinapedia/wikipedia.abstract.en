'''Gloria Foriwa ''' (born 11 May 1981) is a Ghanaian footballer who played as a forward for the Ghana women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2003 FIFA Women's World Cup and 2007 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she played for Ghatel Ladies in Ghana.[1][2]
