The collection includes 123 objects dating from the fifteenth to the seventeenth centuries that were excavated from the and have been designated a Prefectural Cultural Property.[2] The "Cultural Landscape along the Sarugawa River resulting from Ainu Tradition and Modern Settlement" has been designated an Important Cultural Landscape.[3][4]
