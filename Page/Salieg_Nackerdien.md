MUGAMMAD SALIEG NACKERDIEN (born 19 July 1963) is a South African cricketer from Paarl. He was a Left handed batsman,[1] and bowled Right Arm Medium.


First-class Career

He represented Boland in First-class and List-A cricket He made his First-class debut in 1983/84 and almost three years later he played List-A also for Boland.

He retired from all forms of cricket in 1996.
