BABOUA is a village in the commune of Belel in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon.


Population

In 1967, Baboua contained 523 people, principally Fula people.[1]

In the 2005 census, 846 people were counted there.[2]


Infrastructure

Baboua has a public school.[3]
