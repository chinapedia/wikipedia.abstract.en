GIUSEPPE DRAGO (29 September 1955 – 21 September 2016) was an Italian physician and politician who was President of Sicily from 20 January 1998 to 21 November 1998.


Biography

In May 2009 he was disqualified from holding public office due to misappropriating funds. As a result, he lost his seat in the Chamber of Deputies.[1][2]

He died in Modica of a lung infection on 21 September 2016.[3]
