'''Sherif Khalil ''' also written as '''Khalil Sherif '''(شريف خليل , born 18 August 1982) is an Egyptian male water polo player. He was a member of the Egypt men's national water polo team, playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played for Heliopolis in Egypt.
