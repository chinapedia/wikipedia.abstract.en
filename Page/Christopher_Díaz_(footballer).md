CHRISTOPHER FELIPE DÍAZ PEÑA (born 25 July 1995) is a Chilean footballer who currently plays for Cobreloa.


Career

Díaz began his career at his local side Curicó Unido in 2013, before moving to Canada with United Soccer League side Whitecaps FC 2.[1]

On 2 January 2019, Cobreloa announced the signing of Díaz.[2]
