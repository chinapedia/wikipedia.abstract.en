_RATED R: REPUBLICANS IN HOLLYWOOD_ is a 2004 American documentary about politically conservative members of Hollywood and explores whether they face discrimination.[1]

It includes interviews with such filmmakers as Drew Carey, Michael Medved, John Milius, Vincent Gallo, Patricia Heaton and Pat Sajak.
