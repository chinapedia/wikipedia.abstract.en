_MAHATMUDU_ (English: GREAT PERSON) is a 1976 Telugu drama film, produced and directed by M. S. Gopinath under the Rajeswari Chitra banner.[1] It stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Sharada in the lead roles and music composed by T. Chalapathi Rao.[2]


Plot

Zamindar Parvathamma (G.Varalakshmi) has two sons Venu Gopal (Akkineni Nageswara Rao) & Nanda Gopal (Satyanarayana) and elder one Nanda Gopal is foster but she loves the two equally that's why calls both of them as Gopal. Venu loves a beautiful girl Seeta (Sharada) before he reveals it unexpectedly, Nanda Gopal sees Seeta and starts liking her. Now Parvathamma moves with the marriage proposal when Seeta too accepts as confused with names. Knowing it, Venu stupefies but immediately retrieves, also convinces Seeta and performs the marriage. Soon after, Nanda Gopal learns regarding the love affair Venu & Seeta when sly Manager Basavaiah (Allu Ramalingaiah) provokes his suspension. So, Venu gets away from home, on his way, gets acquainted with a girl Radha (Prabha) daughter of Zamindar Ranganatham (Kanta Rao) who tries to commit suicide as she is pregnant before marriage. To protect honor, Venu Gopal pretends to act as the father of her child. On the other side, Nanda Gopal goes into the clutches of a vicious person Giri (Giri Babu) and becomes debauchery which suffers Parvathamma & Seeta. Time passes, Seeta & Radha give birth to babies and Parvathamma passes away. Eventually, Venu improves the living condition of the destitute and also reforms several dacoits into respectable citizens for which everyone in that area adores him as a deity. Here Giri keeps an evil eye on Seeta, out of humiliation she leaves the house and fortunately, reaches Venu. Parallelly, Giri & Basavaiah swindle Nanda Gopal and necks him out. Right now, Giri shows his presence at Radha's residence for her wealth as he is the same who deceived her when spotting Seeta, he tries to molest. At present, the wheel of fortune brings Nanda Gopal to the same place and resues her. At that moment, engaged Radha seeks to kill Giri when Venu obstructs her way. At last, Giri realizes his mistake and accepts Radha. Finally, the movie ends Venu dedicating his life to human welfare.
