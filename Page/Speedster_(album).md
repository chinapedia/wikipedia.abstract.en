_SPEEDSTER_ (styled _SPEEDSTER_) is the third studio album by Japanese boy band Generations from Exile Tribe. It was released on March 2, 2016.[1] It was number one on the _Oricon_ weekly Albums Chart on its release,[2] with 71,380 copies sold.[3] It was also number one on the _Billboard Japan_ Hot Albums chart[4] and also on the _Billboard Japan_ Top Album Sales chart.[5]
