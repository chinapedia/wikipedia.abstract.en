The _INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF INTELLIGENT INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES_ is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering intelligent information technologies, especially agent-based systems. It was established in 2005 and is published by IGI Global. The editor-in-chief is Vijayan Sugumaran (Oakland University).
