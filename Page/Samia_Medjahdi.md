SAMIA MEDJAHDI (; born 6 January 1985) is a retired Algerian tennis player.

Medjahdi has a WTA singles career high ranking of 730 achieved on 7 August 2006. She also has a WTA doubles career high ranking of 505 achieved on 29 August 2005. Medjahdi has won one ITF doubles title.

Playing for Algeria in Fed Cup, Medjahdi has a W/L record of 8–7.
