The 1950 MIAMI HURRICANES FOOTBALL TEAM represented the University of Miami for the 1950 college football season. The Hurricanes played their home games at the Burdine Stadium in Miami. The team was coached by Andy Gustafson, in his third year as head coach for the Hurricanes.
