MENTOR is an unincorporated community in southern Greene County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] The community is located southeast of Springfield, south of U.S. Route 60, west of Missouri Route NN and one mile north of the Greene-Christian county line.[2]


History

The first settlement at Mentor was made in the 1880s.[3] A post office called Mentor was in operation from 1896 until 1906.[4]
