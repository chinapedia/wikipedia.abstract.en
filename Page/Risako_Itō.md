[1] is a Japanese actress, fashion model, and gravure model.

Itō was once represented with Ever Green Entertainment.[2]

She gained notable fame near the end of 2017 for her involvement in the commercials produced by UHA for their Sageru Gummy commercials.


Biography

-   By the time she was in fourth grade, Itō started working as magazine model.[3] In 2005 she joined Ever Green Entertainment.
-   Itō made regular appearances in "Oha Girl" of _Oha Suta_ along with Mayu Matsuoka and Hidemi Hikita from the 7 April 2008 until the 26 March 2010.
-   In November 2011 she was chosen as the Miss Weekly Young Jump of Gravure Japan co-hosted by _Weekly Young Jump_ and _Weekly Playboy_.
-   Itō left Ever Green Entertainment in April 2016.[4]
-   In June 2016 she later joined the talent agency Cube.
