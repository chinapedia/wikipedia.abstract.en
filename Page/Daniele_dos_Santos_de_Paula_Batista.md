DANIELE DOS SANTOS DE PAULA BATISTA known as just DANIELE (born 2 April 1983) is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a forward for the Brazil women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1] At the club level, she plays for Vasco da Gama in Brazil.[2]
