ZAKIR KATHRADA (born 17 August 1991) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Western Province in the 2011–12 CSA Provincial Three-Day Challenge on 1 March 2012.[2]

He was the leading run-scorer in the 2017–18 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge tournament for Northern Cape, with 250 runs in eight matches.[3]
