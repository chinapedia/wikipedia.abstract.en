_MELLETES_, the BUTTERFLY SCULPINS, is a genus of sculpins native mostly to the northern Pacific Ocean, Sea of Okhotsk, Bering Sea, and the Arctic Ocean.


Species

There is currently only one recognized species in this genus:[1]

-   _Melletes papilio_ T. H. Bean, 1880 (butterfly sculpin)
