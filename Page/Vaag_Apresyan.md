VAAG VAGARSHAKOVICH APRESYAN (; born 29 January 1998) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Mashuk-KMV Pyatigorsk on 20 November 2016 in a game against FC SKA Rostov-on-Don.[1]
