PHUMZILE MAJAJA (born 29 November 1990) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Border in the 2016–17 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 10 November 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Border in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 18 December 2016.[3]
