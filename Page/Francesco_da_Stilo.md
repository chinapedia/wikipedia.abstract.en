Biography

Francesco da Stilo was born ordained a priest in the Order of Preachers.[2] On 19 June 1461, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Pius II as Bishop of Lipari.[3] He served as Bishop of Lipari until his death in 1489.[4] While bishop, he was the principal co-consecrator of Dalmazio Gabrielli, Bishop of Siracusa (1469).[5]
