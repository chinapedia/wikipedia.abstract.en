MEGA LONG MALL is a shopping centre located in Donggongon, Penampang, Sabah, Malaysia, about 10 kilometres from the Kota Kinabalu city centre. It is the longest shopping mall in Sabah.[1]


Lawsuit

In early 2015, the mall was sued by 102 shop owners from the mall itself for allegedly breaching their sales and purchase agreement.[2]
