ROLANDAS BERNATONIS (born 22 January 1987) is a Lithuanian handball player who currently plays for DVTK-Eger and the Lithuania national team.[1]

He was one of the best foreign scorers in the 2014–15 and 2015–16 seasons of Liga ASOBAL, scoring a total of 240 goals in 48 games.[2]
