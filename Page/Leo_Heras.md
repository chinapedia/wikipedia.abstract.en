LEONARDO HERAS ARIPEZ (born May 29, 1990) is a Mexican professional baseball outfielder for the Leones de Yucatán of the Mexican League.


Career

Heras played in the Mexican League for the Broncos de Reynosa and the Diablos Rojos del México. The Houston Astros signed Heras in 2013, and he reported to the Corpus Christi Hooks of the Class AA Texas League.[1] He played in an exhibition game in Mexico in 2016.[2][3] The Astros released Heras after the 2016 season, and he signed with the Leones de Yucatán.[4][5]
