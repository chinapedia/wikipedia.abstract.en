_SHE STOOPS TO CONQUER_ is a 1914 British silent historical comedy film directed by George Loane Tucker and starring Henry Ainley, Jane Gail and Gregory Scott. It is an adaptation of Oliver Goldsmith's play _She Stoops to Conquer_.[1]
