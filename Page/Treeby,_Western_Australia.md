TREEBY is a southern suburb of Perth, falling within the City of Cockburn local government area. It is named after early settlers of the area, Joseph and Emma Treeby.[1][2] The suburb was created in 2016 from the northern portion of the suburb of Banjup (extending above Armadale Road).[3]
