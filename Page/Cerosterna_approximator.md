_CEROSTERNA APPROXIMATOR_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by James Thomson in 1865. It is known from Thailand and the Malay Peninsula.[1][2]


Subspecies

-   _Cerosterna approximator approximator_ Thomson 1865
-   _Cerosterna approximator birmanica_ Gahan 1895
