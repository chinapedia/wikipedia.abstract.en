JASON PIERCE is a Canadian drummer.


Career

During the 2000s, Pierce was a member of the Canadian band The Weekend.[1]

In 2012, Pierce performed drums on the Evans Blue album _Graveyard of Empires_.[2] He was also the drummer for Treble Charger for a time.[3]

In 2014, while in search of a replacement drummer, Our Lady Peace enlisted Pierce to perform during live shows.[4][5] In 2016, he officially joined the band as their full-time drummer.[6]

He has also toured with Carly Rae Jepsen, Justin Bieber, Paramore, Dallas Green,[7] and Chantal Kreviazuk,[8] among others.
