TONENKE (; ) is a village in Yasynuvata Raion (district) in Donetsk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, at 23.5 km NW from the centre of Donetsk city.

The War in Donbass, that started in mid-April 2014, has brought along both civilian and military casualties. One civilian was killed by shelling on 12 November 2016.[1] One Ukrainian serviceman was killed on 31 January 2017.[2]
