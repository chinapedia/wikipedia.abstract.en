ROBBINS is an unincorporated community in Johnson County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. [1]


History

A variant spelling was "Robins".[2] A post office called Robins was established in 1892, and remained in operation until 1904.[3] The community has the name of Henry Clay Robbins, an early settler.[4]
