EVITA LETER (born July 5, 1995) is a Surinamese swimmer. She competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics in the women's 100 metre breaststroke event; her time of 1:14.96 in the heats did not qualify her for the semifinals.[1][2] She hails from Paramaribo.[3]
