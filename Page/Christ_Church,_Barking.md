Christ_Church,_Bastable_Avenue,_Barking_(geograph_2376511).jpg CHRIST CHURCH, BARKING or CHRIST CHURCH, THAMES VIEW is a Church of England church on the Thames View estate in Barking, Greater London. Built between 1958 and 1959 as a chapel of ease to St Patrick's,[1] it now forms a team parish with St Patrick's and St Margaret's.
