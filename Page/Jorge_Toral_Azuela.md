JORGE TORAL AZUELA served as the International Commissioner of the Asociación de Scouts de México, Asociación Civil.

In 1975, he was awarded the 103rd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting, at the 25th World Scout Conference.[1] He was also a recipient of the Silver World Award.
