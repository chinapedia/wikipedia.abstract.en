MATEJ SLÁVIK (born 5 August 1994) is a Slovak football goalkeeper who currently plays for KFC Komárno on loan from Fortuna Liga club FC DAC 1904 Dunajská Streda.


Club career

DAC Dunajská Streda

Slávik made his professional Fortuna Liga debut for FC DAC 1904 Dunajská Streda[1] against FC Spartak Trnava on 23 October 2016.
