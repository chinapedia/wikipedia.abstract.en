BRYCE TREGGS (born April 30, 1994) is an American football wide receiver who is currently a free agent. He played college football at California, and was signed by the San Francisco 49ers as an undrafted free agent after the 2016 NFL Draft.


Professional career

San Francisco 49ers

Treggs signed with the San Francisco 49ers as an undrafted free agent on May 6, 2016.[1] He was waived on September 3, 2016.[2]

Philadelphia Eagles

Treggs was claimed off waivers by the Philadelphia Eagles on September 4, 2016.[3]

On September 2, 2017, Treggs was waived by the Eagles and was signed to the practice squad the next day.[4][5]

Cleveland Browns

On October 4, 2017, Treggs was signed by the Cleveland Browns off the Eagles' practice squad.[6] He was waived on December 13, 2017.[7]

Philadelphia Eagles (second stint)

On December 14, 2017, Treggs was signed to the Eagles' practice squad.[8] While Treggs was on their practice squad, the Eagles defeated the New England Patriots in Super Bowl LII.[9] He signed a reserve/future contract with the Eagles on February 7, 2018.[10]

On September 1, 2018, Treggs was waived by the Eagles.[11]


Personal life

His father, Brian Treggs, also played college football at Cal and in the NFL.[12]
