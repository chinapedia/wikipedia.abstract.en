CAPUCINE MOTTE (born 1971) is a Belgian born French woman of letters. A former lawyer (New York and Paris) and galierist, she won the 2013 edition of the Roger Nimier Prize.


Works

-   _La Vraie Vie des jolies filles_, Éditions Jean-Claude Lattès, Paris, 2010, 301 p. [1][2] - Prix Contrepoint - 2011
-   _Apollinaria : une passion russe_, Éditions Jean-Claude Lattès, Paris, 2013, 296 p. [3] - Prix Roger Nimier 2013[4]
