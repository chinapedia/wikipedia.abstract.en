_SCYTHRIS MARIANNAE_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Bengt Å. Bengtsson in 1997. It is found in Spain and France.[1]


Etymology

The species name is dedicated to the wife of the author, Mari-Ann.[2]
