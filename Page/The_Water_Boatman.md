_THE WATER BOATMAN_ is a 2016 British horror and thriller film directed by Chris R Wright.[1] The film stars Charlie Mills as failed musician, Neil, along with Rachel Teate as Liv and the voice of _Big Brother_, Marcus Bentley.[2]


Plot

The British chilling story is set in present times. Having recently moved into their first home together, Neil, a failed musician turned florist returns to the house to find his girlfriend acting strange. The night slowly takes an unexpected turn, as Neil's world is turned upside down, as he struggles to keep both Liv and his sanity.[3]
