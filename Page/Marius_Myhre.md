MARIUS MYHRE (born 21 November 1991) is a Norwegian male badminton player.[1][2]


Career

He plays in the men's singles. He participated in the 2013 BWF World Championships, but lost in the first round to Henri Hurskainen. He participated in the 2015 Russia Open Grand Prix where he reached the second round, but lost to Henri Hurskainen. At the 2016 Dutch Open Grand Prix, he reached the third round before losing to the number one seed, Ajay Jayaram.
