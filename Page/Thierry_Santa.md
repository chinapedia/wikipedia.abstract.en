THIERRY SANTA is a French politician in New Caledonia.[1]

He served as President of the Congress of New Caledonia from July 2015 to July 2018.[2][3][4] Santa is also secretary general of The Rally political party, since 16 May 2013.


Personal life

Santa married Sabine Di Russo (born 1 February 1974) on 3 September 2010,[5] the Human Resources Director of the company _Nouméa Casinos_.[6]
