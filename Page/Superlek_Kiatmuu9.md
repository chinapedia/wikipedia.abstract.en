SUPERLEK KIATMUU9 is a Thai Muay Thai fighter, originally from Buriram, in the northeast of Thailand,Superlek is from the same Muay Thai gym of his uncle and multiple time Lumpinee Stadium champion Singdam Kiatmuu9[1]

Superlek is a teammate of another well known Muay Thai fighter, Petpanomrung Kiatmuu9.[2]

On July 2016, Superlek Kiatmuu9 was ranked the #1 Super-Feather weight ranked on Lumpinee Stadium by muaythai2000.com,[3] also ranked #1 Super-Feather weight on Rajadamnern Stadium by muaythai2000.com.[4]


Titles and accomplishments

-   2016 WBC 130lbs. World Champion[5]
-   2013 Thailand 126lbs. Champion
-   2013 Lumpinee Stadium 118lbs. Champion[6]
-   2012 Sports Authority of Thailand Muay Thai Fighter of the Year[7]
-   2012 Lumpinee Stadium 114lbs. Champion[8](One Title Defense)
-   2012 Thailand 112lbs. Champion
-   2011 Thailand 105lbs. Champion
