The INDONESIA WOMEN'S NATIONAL UNDER-18 AND UNDER-19 BASKETBALL TEAM is a national basketball team of Indonesia, administered by the Indonesian Basketball Association "PERBASI".[1]

It represents the country in international under-18 and under-19 (under age 18 and under age 19) women's basketball competitions.
