STEWART ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] the island is lying north from Port Blair. Its population only consists of two people.


Geography

Administration

Politically, Stewart Island, along neighboring Stewart Sound Group Islands, is part of Diglipur Taluk.[2]
