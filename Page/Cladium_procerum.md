_CLADIUM PROCERUM_, known commonly as the LEAFY TWIGRUSH,[1] is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family. It is found mostly in swampland and on the margins of lakes. It grows up to tall.[2] It was described by Stanley Thatcher Blake in 1943.[3]
