_NERVOUS LIKE ME_ is the debut studio album by American rock band Cayetana, released in 2014.


Track listing


Critical reception

_Nervous Like Me_ was released to positive reviews. Philip Cosores of Consequence of Sound gave the album a B+ rating and praised the album as one of the best debut albums from a Philadelphia band.[1]
