IN SODAVID (born 2 July 1998 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia) is a Cambodian footballer who plays for Phnom Penh Crown in the Cambodian League and the Cambodia national football team.


Club career

Sodavid signed his first professional contract with Phnom Penh Crown in 2015 and made his first appearance on 26 September.[1] During his first professional season, he made 6 appearances with 2 goals and helped his club to win sixth title.


International career

He made his international debut in a Friendly Match against Sri Lanka on 9 October 2016.[2]
