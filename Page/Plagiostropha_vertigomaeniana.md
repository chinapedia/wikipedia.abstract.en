_PLAGIOSTROPHA VERTIGOMAENIANA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 6 mm and 11 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off Balicasag Island, Panglao, Bohol, the Philippines.
