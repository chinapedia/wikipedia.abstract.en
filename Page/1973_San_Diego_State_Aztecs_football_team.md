The 1973 SAN DIEGO STATE AZTECS FOOTBALL TEAM represented California State University San Diego[1] during the 1973 NCAA Division I football season as a member of the Pacific Coast Athletic Association.[2]

The team was led by head coach Claude Gilbert, in his first year, and played home games at San Diego Stadium[3] in San Diego, California. They finished the season as Conference Champion for the second consecutive year, with a record of nine wins, one loss and one tie (9–1–1, 3–0–1 PCAA).
