BASTIAN KARSTEN AYONG[1] (born 20 January 1998) is a Cameroon-born Czech professional football forward.

He made his senior league debut for Příbram on 4 December 2016 in a Czech First League 2–2 away draw at Mladá Boleslav.[2]
