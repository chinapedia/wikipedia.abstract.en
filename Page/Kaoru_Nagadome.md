is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team.


Club career

Nagadome was born on May 7, 1973. She played for Prima Ham FC Kunoichi. She was elected Best Eleven in 1995 season.


National team career

On June 15, 1997, Nagadome debuted for Japan national team against China. She was a member of Japan for 1999 World Cup. She played 4 games for Japan until 1999.[1]
