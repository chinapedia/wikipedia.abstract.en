THE KNAVE OF CLUBS is a former pub at 25 Bethnal Green Road, Shoreditch, London E1. It closed in July 1994, and since 2001 has been _Les Trois Garçons_, a restaurant.[1]

It is a Grade II listed building, built in 1880.[2]
