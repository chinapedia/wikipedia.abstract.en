EMILY JOANNA CAREY[1] (born 30 April 2003[2]) is a British actress and model. She is perhaps best known for her role of Grace Beauchamp in BBC One's _Casualty_, which she played from 2014 to 2017.[3][4]

Carey also played the young Diana Prince (Wonder Woman), portrayed by Gal Gadot as an adult, in the Warner Brothers movie _Wonder Woman_ (2017).[5][6][7][8][9] She appeared as a young Lara Croft in the _Tomb Raider_ reboot film, released in March 2018.[10]

Other notable work includes Mary Conan Doyle in the series _Houdini and Doyle_ on FOX/ITV,[11][12] and West End Theatre roles in _Shrek The Musical_ at the Theatre Royal, Drury Lane, and the Olivier Award-nominated _The Sound of Music_ at Regents Park Open Air Theatre. Carey is listed in a Huffington Post article as one of the top five child stars of 2016.[13]

Carey has signed to play the title role of Anastasia in a live action _Anastasia_ film for Netflix.[14][15]

Carey signed to IMG Models in 2019.[16]
