PAVEL DÕMOV (born 31 December 1993) is an Estonian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Estonian Meistriliiga club Paide Linnameeskond .


Club career

FC Flora

Dõmov joined FC Flora on 1 January 2019, after signing for the club on 14 December 2018 until 2020.[1]


International career

Dõmov started his international youth career in 2011 with the under-19 team. He made his international debut for Estonia on 19 November 2016, in a 1–1 away draw against Saint Kitts and Nevis in a friendly.[2]
