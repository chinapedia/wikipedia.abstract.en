_MAY LAMOK SA LOOB NG KULAMBO_ is a 1984 Philippine comedy film directed by, and produced by Hirene Lopez and Danny L. Zialcita as the film's executive producer. The film stars Gloria Diaz, Eddie Garcia, Amy Austria, and Tommy Abuel.

The film was released in 1984 by Essex Films.

Another related film called, _May Daga sa Labas ng Lungga_, was released earlier in the same year.


Synopsis

Lauro (Eddie Garcia) is a married man who just loves women. His wife Elena (Gloria Diaz), a beautiful and strong-willed woman is dead set in keeping him from womanizing.
