LAWRENCE KIMBERLEY (born 1962) has been Dean of Christchurch since 2015.[1]

Kimberley was previously an accountant. He was Curate at Church of St Michael and All Angels, Christchurch then Vicar of Heathcote- Mount Pleasant. He was Archdeacon of Pegasus[2] from 2006 to 2014. He was then Vicar of St Martin, Opawa until his appointment to the Deanery.[3]
