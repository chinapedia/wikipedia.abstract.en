CAROLINE TAYLOR (born 20 August 1973) is a Welsh international lawn and indoor bowler.[1]

Taylor from Newtown, Powys won a bronze medal in the triples at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Adelaide.[2]

She was selected as part of the Welsh team for the 2018 Commonwealth Games on the Gold Coast in Queensland[3]
