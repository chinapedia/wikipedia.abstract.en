FERIEL ESSEGHIR (born 29 October 1983) is an Algerian retired tennis player.

Esseghir has a WTA singles career high ranking of 381 achieved on 8 April 2002. She also has a WTA doubles career high ranking of 534 achieved on 5 August 2002.

Playing for Algeria in Fed Cup, Esseghir has a W/L record of 8–17.[1]

She made her WTA Tour singles main draw debut at the 2002 Morocco Open. İn the doubles event partnering Meryem El Haddad.[2]
