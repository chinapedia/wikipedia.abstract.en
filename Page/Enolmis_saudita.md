_ENOLMIS SAUDITA_ is a moth of the family Scythrididae. It was described by Pietro Passerin d'Entrèves in 1986. It is found in south-western Saudi Arabia[1] and Yemen.[2]

The wingspan is about 17 mm, making it one of the largest species in the genus _Enolmis_. There is a dark brown, broad central fascia covering almost a third of the wing area of the forewings.[3]
