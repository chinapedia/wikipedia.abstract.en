HARRISON MILLS is an unincorporated community in Crawford County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Harrison's Mills was established in 1832, and remained in operation until 1880.[2] B. Harrison, an early postmaster, gave the community his last name.[3]
