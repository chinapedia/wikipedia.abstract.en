ADELCHI VIRGILI (born 10 March 1990) is an Italian tennis player.

Virgili has a career high ATP singles ranking of 494 achieved on 12 December 2016. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 378 achieved on 9 September 2013.

Virgili made his ATP main draw debut at the 2016 Delray Beach International Tennis Championships in the doubles draw partnering Sander Groen.[1]
