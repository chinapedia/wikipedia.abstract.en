MACHO is a 2016 Mexican film starring Miguel Rodarte, Cecilia Suárez, Aislinn Derbez and Renato López, who was murdered less than two weeks after the November 11, 2016, box-office debut.[1]

In the first three weeks of its release up to 27 November it grossed 32 million Mexican pesos (ca. 1.6 million U.S. dollars) and 740,000 viewers.[2]
