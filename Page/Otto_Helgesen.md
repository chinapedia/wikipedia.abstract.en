OTTO HELGESEN (4 July 1898  – 19 June 1973) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Fredrikstad to Helge Helgesen and Karen Elisabeth Nielsen. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1920, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1950 to 1968. He was a member of the Labour Court of Norway from 1960, and chaired _Prisrådet_ from 1962.[1] He was decorated as a Commander of the Order of St. Olav. He died in 1973.[2]
