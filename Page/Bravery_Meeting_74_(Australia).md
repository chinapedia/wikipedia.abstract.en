The BRAVERY COUNCIL OF AUSTRALIA MEETING 74 HONOURS LIST was announced by the Governor General of Australia on 21 March 2011.[1]

Awards were announced for the Bravery Medal,[2] Commendation for Brave Conduct[3] and Group Bravery Citation.[4]
