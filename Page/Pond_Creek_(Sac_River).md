POND CREEK is a stream in Greene County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Sac River.

The headwaters are just north of U.S. Route 60 and Republic at and the confluence with the Sac is north of I-44 and just south of Missouri Route 266 two mile east of Plano at .[2][3]

Pond Creek flows near ponds, hence the name.[4]
