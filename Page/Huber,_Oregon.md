HUBER is an unincorporated community in Washington County, Oregon, United States, now mostly located within Aloha near the Tualatin Hills Nature Park.


History

Huber was established in 1910. It is named for Jacob Huber, an early resident of the area. The Huber post office opened in 1916 and was closed on December 31, 1953.[1]
