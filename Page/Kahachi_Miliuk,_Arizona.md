KAHACHI MILIUK is a populated place situated in Pima County, Arizona.[1] It is located in the Fresnal Canyon on the Tohono O'odham Indian Reservation, and its name is derived from the three O'odham words: _ge_, _aji_, and _meliwkud_, meaning "big", "skinny" and "place where runners end a race" (or "finish line").[2][3] It has an estimated elevation of above sea level.[4]
