Biography

Lelio Veterano was born in 1580.[5] On 5 December 1616, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul V as Bishop of Fondi.[6][7] On 13 December 1616, he was consecrated bishop by Giovanni Garzia Mellini, Cardinal-Priest of Santi Quattro Coronati, with Francesco Sacrati (cardinal), Titular Archbishop of _Damascus_, and Vincenzo Landinelli, Bishop of Albenga, serving as co-consecrators.[8] He served as Bishop of Fondi until his death in 1619.[9]
