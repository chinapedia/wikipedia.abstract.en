_HETERELMIS_ is a genus of beetles in the riffle beetle family Elmidae.[1]

Species include:

-   _Heterelmis comalensis_
-   _Heterelmis glabra_
-   _Heterelmis obesa_
-   _Heterelmis obscura_
-   _Heterelmis stephani_
-   _Heterelmis tarsalis_
-   _Heterelmis vulnerata_
