The BRAVERY COUNCIL OF AUSTRALIA MEETING 72 HONOURS LIST was announced by the Governor General of Australia, Quentin Bryce on 15 March 2010.[1]

Awards were announced for the Star of Courage,[2] the Bravery Medal,[3] Commendation for Brave Conduct[4] and Group Bravery Citation.[5]
