SOFIANE ALAKOUCH (born 29 July 1998) is a French-Moroccan footballer who plays for Nîmes as a defender.[1]


International career

Alakouch is of Moroccan descent and represented France at the U21 level. He received a call-up to represent the Morocco national football team on August 2017.[2]

He later received a call-up to represent the France national under-20 football team for the 2018 Toulon Tournament on May 17, 2018.[3]
