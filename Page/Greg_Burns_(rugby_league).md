GREGORY 'GREG' BURNS (born 5 March 1995) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a for the Sheffield Eagles in the Betfred Championship.[1][2]

Burns is a graduate of the Eagles Academy system[3] and has played in Sheffield's reserve team.[4]

His brothers Jonathan and Paddy came through the Eagles' youth system.[5] Paddy signed a professional contract with them in 2018 and Jonathan plays with the Hemel Stags.

On 14 April 2017, Greg scored his first professional try for the Eagles. It was against Rochdale Hornets in an 18-42 victory.
