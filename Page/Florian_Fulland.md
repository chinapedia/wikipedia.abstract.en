FLORIAN FULLAND (born 15 September 1984) is a German former footballer and football manager.[1]

On 21 November 2016, Fulland was announced as the interim replacement for René Müller at SC Paderborn, as the club were struggling.[2] He managed two 3. Liga matches, with a record of one draw and one loss and was replaced on 6 December 2016.[3][4]
