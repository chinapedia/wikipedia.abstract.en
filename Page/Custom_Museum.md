The CUSTOM MUSEUM (Malay: _Muzium Adat_) is a museum in Kuala Klawang, Jelebu District, Negeri Sembilan, Malaysia.[1][2]


History

The museum was originally constructed in 2005 and officially opened on 2 February 2008 by Negeri Sembilan Chief Minister Mohamad Hasan and Minister for Culture, Arts and Heritage Rais Yatim.[3]


Architecture

The museum houses four galleries in a four-story building. There is a giant replica of Malay headdress at the top of the museum entrance.[4]


Exhibitions

The museum exhibits facets and lifestyle of the Malaysian people and identity of Negeri Sembilan.[5]

-   Introduction of custom
-   Life cycle
-   Intellectual tradition, government and power
-   Pepatih custom[6]


Opening time

The museum opens everyday from 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. free of charge.
