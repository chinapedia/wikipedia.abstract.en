RISTINE is an unincorporated community in New Madrid County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Ristine was laid out in 1882, when the railroad was extended to that point.[2] A post office called Ristine was established in 1888, and remained in operation until 1894.[3] The community has the name of one Mr. Ristine, a railroad official.[4]
