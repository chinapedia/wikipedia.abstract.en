TALLAHASSEE NATIONAL CEMETERY is a United States National Cemetery located in Tallahassee, Florida, Leon County, Florida. Administered by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs.


History

Tallahassee National Cemetery was dedicated May 22, 2015. The Veterans Administration purchased the 250-acre parcel of land August 2012 for $6.8 million. This cemetery will serve over 83,000 Veterans in Tallahassee and the surrounding area.
