THILOGNE is a small town in north-east Senegal with the status of a commune. It lies in the Matam Department of Matam Region near the border with Mauretania and connected to Dakar via the N2 road.

In 2013 it had a population of just over 10,000.[1]

Thilogne is twinned with Trappes in France.
