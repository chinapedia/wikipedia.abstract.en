_AGELESCAPE_ is a genus of funnel weavers first described by G. Levy in 1996.[1]


Species

it contains seven species:[2]

-   _Agelescape affinis_ (Kulczyński, 1911) — Turkey, Syria
-   _Agelescape caucasica_ Guseinov, Marusik & Koponen, 2005 — Greece, Azerbaijan
-   _Agelescape dunini_ Guseinov, Marusik & Koponen, 2005 — Azerbaijan
-   _Agelescape gideoni_ Levy, 1996 — Turkey to Israel, Iran
-   _Agelescape levyi_ Guseinov, Marusik & Koponen, 2005 — Azerbaijan
-   _Agelescape livida_ (Simon, 1875) — Mediterranean
-   _Agelescape talyshica_ Guseinov, Marusik & Koponen, 2005 — Azerbaijan
