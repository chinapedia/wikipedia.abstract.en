ASKHAT TAGYBERGEN (, , born 9 August 1990) is a Kazakh footballer who plays for FC Kaisar in the Kazakhstan Premier League.[1][2]


Career

Club

Tagybergen signed for FC Astana in February 2016.[3]

On 15 June 2017, Tagybergen returned to FC Astana after a loan spell with FC Tobol, where he made desultory appearances.[4]

On 9 January 2018, FC Kaisar announced the signing of Tagybergen.[5]

International

Askhat Tagybergen was named on the Kazakhstan national football team squad.[6]
