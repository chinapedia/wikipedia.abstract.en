PUNGENIN is a phenolic compound found in the needles of Blue Spruce (_Picea pungens_).[1] It is the glucoside of 3,4-dihydroxyacetophenone.


Chemical Ecology

The compound serves a feeding deterrent against Spruce Budworm larvae.[2]
