WILLIAM "JUNIOR" OMAND (14 September 1931 – 2005) was Scottish amateur football outside left who made over 250 appearances in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1][2] He later became president of the club.[3] Omand represented Scotland at amateur level.[4]


Honours

QUEEN'S PARK

-   Scottish League Second Division: 1955–56[5][6]
