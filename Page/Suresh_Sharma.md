SURESH KUMAR SHARMA (born 10 June 1944) was an Indian cricketer from Northern Punjab. He was a right-handed batsman and played first-class cricket for only one season.[1]


First-class career

Sharma made his first-class cricket debut in 1963-64 season and did not play again in future. Post retirement, he also played the role of referee in a few matches.
