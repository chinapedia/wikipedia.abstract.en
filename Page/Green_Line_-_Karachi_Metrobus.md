The GREEN LINE () is a bus rapid transit line under construction in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.[1] The busway is the first phase of the Karachi Metrobus network.[2][3]


Route

The Green Line will extend from Karachi City railway station to Surjani Town. The route will take the green line from:

-   Railway Road
-   Moulvi Tamizuddin Road
-   M.A Jinnah Road
-   Business Recorder Road
-   Nawab Siddique Ali Khan Road
-   Shahrah-e-Sher Shah Suri
-   Shahrah-e-Usma
-   Khawaja Shamsuddin Azeemi Road


Financing & construction

The Government of Pakistan is financing the majority of the project.[4] Construction of the Green Line began on 26 February 2016. The line will have 37 bus stations when completed.[5] _Engineering Associates_ were contracted as the designers and supervision consultants for Green Line while _Think Transportation_ was contracted as urban engineering consultants.[6][7][8]
