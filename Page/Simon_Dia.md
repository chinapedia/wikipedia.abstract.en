OUSMANE SIMON DIA (born 17 July 1992 in Saint-Quentin, France), is a footballer who plays as a forward for Police Tero.


Career

Dia began his career at Valenciennes. In October 2012, Dia signed on loan for Qatari club Lekhwiya.[1] During his time at the club, Dia made four first team appearances for the club in the Qatar Stars League.

In January 2014, Dia signed for Amiens, making his debut against Dunkerque.[2]

On 14 August 2015, Dia signed for his hometown club of Saint-Quentin, after rejecting an offer from Belgian club Seraing.[3]


Personal life

Dia's father is former footballer Ali Dia.[4]
