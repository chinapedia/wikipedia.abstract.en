_PLEUROFUSIA SCALA_ is an extinct species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description


Distribution

This extinct marine species was found in Miocene strata of Myanmar, age range 23.03 to 20.43 Ma.
