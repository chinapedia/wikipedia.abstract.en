MARI BURELLE-VALENCIA (born April 4, 1985, as MARI JACQUELINE BURELLE), who goes by the stage name stylized as MARI, is an American Christian musician and pianist, who primarily plays a style of Christian EDM music. She has released one musical work, _Treasure_ (2016).


Early life

She was born, Mari Jacqueline Burelle,[1] on April 4,[2][3] 1985,[4] in Boston, Massachusetts,[5] while she moved to Queens, New York, in 2002, and was raised by adoptive parents, while she started playing the piano when she was young.[6] She is of Puerto Rican and Cuban descent.[7]


Music career

Her music recording career started in 2016, with the studio album, _Treasure_,[8] that was released on May 27, 2016,[9] from Elevate Entertainment.[10]


Personal life

She got married to Ivan Valencia in 2005.[11]
