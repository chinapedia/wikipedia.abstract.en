YANGEL CLEMENTE HERRERA RAVELO (born 7 January 1998) is a Venezuelan professional footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for Manchester City.[1]


Club career

Herrera started his club career at Monagas SC before moving to Atlético Venezuela in 2016.[2]

Manchester City

Loan to New York City

On 31 January 2017 it was confirmed that Herrera had signed for Manchester City.[3] Herrera was immediately sent to New York City on a two-year loan.[4][5] He made his debut on 12 March, as a substitute in a 4–0 win over D.C. United.[6]

Loan to Huesca

Following the conclusion of his two year stay at New York City, Herrera was loaned to La Liga side Huesca for the last six months of the 2018–19 season.[7]


International career

Herrera made his international debut for the Venezuela national team on 10 October 2016, coming on as a substitute in a 0–2 loss to Brazil.[8][9]

Herrera was named captain of the Venezuela U20 team for the 2017 U-20 World Cup. He scored the only goal, in the 108th minute of their Round of 16 win over Japan.[10]
