SAMUELA TUIKILIGANA (born 1958) is a Fijian international lawn bowler.[1]

In 2008 he won the silver medal in the triples at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch along with Curtis Mar and Keshwa Goundar.[2]
