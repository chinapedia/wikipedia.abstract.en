JEAN SALVAJ served as the International Commissioner of the Swiss Scout Federation, as well as a member of the International Scout Committee.

In 1953, Salvaj was awarded the 7th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
