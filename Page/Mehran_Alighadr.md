MEHRAN ALIGHADR (, born 24 May 1989) is an Iranian futsal player. He is a striker, and currently a player of _Tasisat Daryaei_ and Iran national futsal team.


Honours

Country

-   FIFA Futsal World Cup
    -   Third place (1): 2016
-   AFC Futsal Championship
    -   Champion (2): 2016 - 2018
-   Grand Prix
    -   Runner-Up (1): 2015

Club

-   Iranian Futsal Super League
    -   Champion (1): 2016-17 (Giti Pasand)
    -   Runner-Up (2): 2017-18 (Tasisat Daryaei) - 2018–19 (Giti Pasand)
