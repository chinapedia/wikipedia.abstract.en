_CEREOPSIUS VITTIPENNIS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Warren Samuel Fisher based on specimen(s) from Mount Kinabalu in 1935, originally under the genus _Epepeotes_. It is known from Borneo.[1][2]
