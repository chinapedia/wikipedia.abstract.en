BLESSING NNABUGWU DIALA (born 8 December 1989) is a footballer who plays as a forward for Equatorial Guinean women's league club Deportivo Evinayong.

Born in Nigeria, she is a member, as a naturalized citizen, of the Equatorial Guinea women's national team. She was part of the team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1]
