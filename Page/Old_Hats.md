_OLD HATS_ is a 2013 comedy sketch, revue, musical and clown show written and performed by David Shiner and Bill Irwin, the show debuted in New York City.[1] Musician Nellie McKay also performed in the 2013 show.[2]

The original 2013 Off-Broadway was a combination of "clowning revue-with-music".[3] _Old Hats_ won the 2013 Drama Desk Award for Outstanding Revue.[4]


Revival

The show was revived in New York City in 2016 with Shiner and Irwin returning and a new third performer, musician Shaina Taub, in between sketches Taub performed original songs with a band.[5] The revival was filmed for WNET's _Theater Close-up_ and BroadwayHD.
