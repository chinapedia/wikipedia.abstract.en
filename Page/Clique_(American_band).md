CLIQUE (stylized as CLIQUE), is an American emo band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.


History

Clique began in 2014 with the release of a self-titled album in 2014 on Broken World Media.[1][2] In 2016, Clique signed to Topshelf Records, where they released their second full-length album titled _Burden Piece_.[3][4][5][6]


Band members

-   PJ Carroll (guitar/vox)
-   Brandon Shipp (guitar/vox)
-   Travis Arterburn (bass)
-   Tom Anthony (drums)
