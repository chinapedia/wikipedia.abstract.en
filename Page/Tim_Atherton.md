TIMOTHY IAN ATHERTON (born November 7, 1989) is an Australian professional baseball player for the Brisbane Bandits of the Australian Baseball League.

He was a member of the Australia national baseball team in the Australia Series, Australian Challenge,[1] 2016 Haarlem Baseball Week,[2] 2017 World Baseball Classic and 2018 exhibition series against Japan.[3]


Australian Baseball League

Atherton has played four seasons with the Canberra Cavalry, one season for the Sydney Blue Sox and the two most recent seasons with the Brisbane Bandits.

He was a part of the Bandits 2017-18 and 2018-19 championships, becoming the first player to be named twice ABLCS MVP.
