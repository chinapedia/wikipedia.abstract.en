MARIANNWOOD NATURE RESERVE is a 12 hectare protected area in Pinetown, Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.[1] The park consists of scarp and riverine forest, as well as grasslands. The grasslands are noted for their flowers during the spring months.[2]

The park has self-guided trails and picnic facilities.
