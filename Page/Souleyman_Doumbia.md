SOULEYMAN KELI DOUMBIA (born 24 September 1996) is an Ivorian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Rennes.[1]


Club career

Doumbia was part of the Paris Saint-Germain youth academy from 2009 to 2016.[2] In the summer of 2016, he was transferred to Serie B club Bari, where he made his professional debut on 22 October versus Trapani.[3] On 31 January 2017, Doumbia joined fellow Serie B side Vicenza on loan.[4][5]

In January 2019, he signed a three-year contract with Stade Rennais.[6]


International career

Doumbia was born in France to parents of Ivorian descent. He was first called up to represent the Ivory Coast U20s for the 2015 Toulon Tournament.[7] He also started for the Ivory Coast U23s in a 5–1 friendly loss to the France U21s.[8] He made his Ivory Coast national football team debut on 15 June 2019 in a friendly against Uganda, as a starter.[9]
