The GLOBAL SPIRIT TOUR[1] was a worldwide concert tour by English electronic music band Depeche Mode in support of the group's 14th studio album, _Spirit_. During the summer 2017, the band played to more than 3 million fans in total.

The tour found the band continuing their charity partnership with Swiss watchmaker Hublot, raising money and awareness for Charity: Water toward its mission of providing safe drinking water to everyone in the world.[2] To promote their worldwide tour, Depeche Mode gave a promotional concert at the Funkhaus Berlin on 17 March in front of 1000 fans and streamed live on the internet.[3]

With 130 live shows, the Global Spirit Tour is Depeche Mode's longest tour. Previously, the longest one was the Touring the Angel, with 124 live shows.
