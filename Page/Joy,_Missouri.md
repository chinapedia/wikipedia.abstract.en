JOY is an unincorporated community in Dent County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Joy was established in 1890, and remained in operation until 1954.[2] The townspeople were overjoyed when they secured the post office, hence the name Joy.[3] Joy_road_marker.jpg Joy_Church_3.jpg
