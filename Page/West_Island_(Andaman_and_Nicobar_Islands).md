WEST ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] The island lies north from Port Blair.


Geography

The island falls in between Coco Islands and North Andaman Island. It lies in the western approach to the Cleugh Passage.[2] The island is small, having an area of .


Administration

Politically, West Island is part of Diglipur Tehsil.[3]
