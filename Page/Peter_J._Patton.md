PETER J. PATTON (born February 9, 1974) is a retired American basketball player and is currently working with the Dallas Mavericks. Patton was previously employed as the shooting coach for the Minnesota Timberwolves. Patton has worked closely with players such as Zach Lavine and Jimmy Butler. Patton, himself was a three-year varsity starter in basketball, baseball, and football at Loyola Academy. After the Chicago Catholic League, Patton received a scholarship to DePaul University.
