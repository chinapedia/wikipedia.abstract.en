FRUZSINA BRÁVIK (born 1986) was a Hungarian female water polo player. She was a member of the Hungary women's national water polo team, playing as a driver.

She was a part of the team at the 2005 World Aquatics Championships,[1] and 2008 Summer Olympics.[2] On club level she played for Dove Dunaújváros in Hungary.
