CHART LEACON TRACTION AND ROLLING STOCK MAINTENANCE DEPOT was at one time a railway depot located in Ashford, Kent, England. The depot is situated to the west of Ashford International station on the south side of the line to Pluckley railway station.[1]

The depot code is AF.


History

In 1987, the depot had an allocation of Class 09 shunters, and was used for stabling DEMUs and EMUs. Chart Leacon is now closed with its successor "Ramsgate EMU Depot" acquiring all class 375 stock [2]


Allocation

In 2014, the depot's allocation consisted of Southeastern Class 375 3/6 & 700 series EMUs.
