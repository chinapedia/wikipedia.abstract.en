WADE is an unincorporated community in Bryan County, Oklahoma, United States.[1] The community is located on Oklahoma State Highway 70E south of Bokchito. Wade had a post office from October 24, 1890, to November 30, 1971. The community was named for Choctaw Alfred Wade.[2]
