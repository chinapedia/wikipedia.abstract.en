GIFT SHOWEMIMO (born 24 May 1974) is a Nigerian footballer who played as a forward for the Nigeria women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level, she played for Kakanfo Babes in Nigeria.[1]
