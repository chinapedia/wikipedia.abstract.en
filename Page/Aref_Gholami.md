AREF GHOLAMI (born 19 April 1997) is an Iranian footballer who played as a center back for Esteghlal in the Persian Gulf Pro League.[1][2][3]


Professional career

He made his first Persian Gulf Pro League debut on 1 December 2016 against Gostaresh Foolad.[4]


Personal life

His older brother Ali Gholami is also a footballer.
