NOWA TV is a Polish television channel, launched on November 9, 2016.[1]


Programming

Nowa TV is a general channel, therefore the programming schedule comprises various programs - movies, series, documentaries, entertainment, news and current affairs.

News/current affairs

-   _24 godziny_ (24 hours) - news programme broadcast daily at 18:30, hosted by Beata Tadla, Joanna Dunikowska, Marek Czyż and Jarosław Kulczycki
-   _Więc jak?_ (So how?) - talk-show, hosted by Sławomir Jastrzębowski, editor-in-chief of _Super Express_
