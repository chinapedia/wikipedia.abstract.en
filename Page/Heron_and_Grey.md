HERON AND GREY was a restaurant in Blackrock, County Dublin, Ireland. It is a fine dining restaurant that is awarded one Michelin star for 2017 and 2018.

The head chef is Damien Grey while Andrew Heron runs the restaurant. They own the restaurant together.[1]
