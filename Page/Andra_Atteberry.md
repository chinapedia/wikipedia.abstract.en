ANDRA ATTEBERRY (born April 8, 1943) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Atteberry was born in Fort Sill, Oklahoma and attended Southern Illinois University. A Democrat, she served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 2001 to 2003 (27th district).[1]
