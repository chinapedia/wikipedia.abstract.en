MORGAN POATY (born 15 July 1997) is a French footballer who currently plays as a defender for Ligue 1 EA Guingamp. He is of Congolese descent through his father.[1]


Career

In September 2018, Poaty joined Ligue 2 side Troyes AC on a season-long loan from Montpellier.[2]
