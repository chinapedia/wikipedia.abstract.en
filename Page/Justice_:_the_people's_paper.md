Front_page_of_Justice_the_people's_paper,_5_December_1891.pdf _JUSTICE : THE PEOPLE'S PAPER_ was an English language newspaper published in Narrabri, New South Wales, Australia.


Newspaper history

_Justice : the people's paper_ was published in 1891 for a few months only.[1] It was printed and published by Patrick Lyons for the proprietor, James Moylan, and was circulated through the Namoi, Gwydir and Gunnedah electorates[2] of New South Wales. The paper made the claim to be "thoroughly independent ... purely original in tone, and a unique and remarkable innovation in country journalism.
