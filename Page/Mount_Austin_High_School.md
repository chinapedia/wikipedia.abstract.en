MOUNT AUSTIN HIGH SCHOOL is a government-funded co-educational comprehensive secondary day school, located in Wagga Wagga, in the Riverina region of New South Wales, Australia.

Established in 1964,[1] the school enrolled approximately 429 students in 2018, from Year 7 to Year 12; of which 45 percent identified as Indigenous Australians and 8 percent from a language background other than English.[2] The school is operated by the NSW Department of Education; and the acting principal is Michelle Waugh.[3]
