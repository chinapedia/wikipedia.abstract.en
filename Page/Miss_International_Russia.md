MISS INTERNATIONAL RUSSIA (Russian: Мисс Интернешнл Россия) is a national Beauty pageant in Russia.


History

In 2016 Miss International Russia began to select its winner to the annual Miss International beauty pageant. Russia has been competed since 1994 and received 9 placements (3 runners-up and 6 semi-finalists) between 1994 and 2015. The first annual Miss International Russia recruits by Anton Paramonov as the current president and national director of the pageant.[1] Before creating Miss International Russia, the delegates audited from casting or finalist at Miss Russia beauty pageant or Miss Moscow/Krasa Rossii winners or sometime the reigning Miss Russia sent to Miss International beauty pageant.[2][3][4]

On July 25th, 2017 Miss International Russia renamed as ZVEZDA ROSSII. The national pageant aims to be a Russian Icon for Miss International, Miss Supranational and Miss Intercontinental.[5]
