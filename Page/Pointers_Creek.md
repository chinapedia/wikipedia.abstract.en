POINTERS CREEK is a stream in Osage County, Missouri.[1]

The stream headwaters are at and the confluence with the Gasconade River is at .[2] The source area for the stream lie just south of U.S. Route 50 and southwest of Potts. The stream flows about four miles to the southeast to enter the Gasconade.[3]

Pointers Creek has the name of the local Pointer family.[4]
