VISHAL KUSHWAH (born 30 November 1990) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 27 October 2016.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 29 January 2017.[3] He made his List A debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2017–18 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 5 February 2018.[4]
