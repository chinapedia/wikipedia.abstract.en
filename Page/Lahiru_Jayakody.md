LAHIRU JAYAKODY (born 29 August 1994) is a Sri Lankan cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Bloomfield Cricket and Athletic Club in the 2016–17 Premier League Tournament on 3 December 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Anuradhaura District in the 2016–17 Districts One Day Tournament on 15 March 2017.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Bloomfield Cricket and Athletic Club in the 2017–18 SLC Twenty20 Tournament on 24 February 2018.[4]
