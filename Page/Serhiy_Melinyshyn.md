SERHIY MELINYSHYN (; born 26 May 1997) is a professional Ukrainian football defender.


Career

Melinyshyn is a product of the Sportive youth school of FC Karpaty Lviv.

He made his debut for FC Volyn Lutsk played as a main-squad player in the game against FC Shakhtar Donetsk on 24 September 2016 in the Ukrainian Premier League.[1]
