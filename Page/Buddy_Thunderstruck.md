_BUDDY THUNDERSTRUCK_ is an American stop motion children's comedy television series created by Ryan Wiesbrock as a Netflix Original series, produced by American Greetings and Stoopid Buddy Stoodios[1] and written by Tom Krajewski.[2] The series stars Brian Atkinson, Ted Raimi, Harry Chaskin and Debi Derryberry. The first season was released on Netflix on March 10, 2017.[3] When promoting the first series Netflix billed it as the 'outrageous, high-octane adventures of Buddy Thunderstruck, a truck-racing dog who brings guts and good times to the town of Greasepit'.[4]
