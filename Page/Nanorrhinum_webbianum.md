_NANORRHINUM WEBBIANUM_ is a species of flowering plant in the family Plantaginaceae, endemic to Cape Verde.[1] The local name of the plant is _agrião-de-rocha_ ("rock-cress").[2]


Distribution and habitat

_Nanorrhinum webbianum_ is found in the island of Santo Antão.[3][4]
