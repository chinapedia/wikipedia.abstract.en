The CANTON OF ARCIS-SUR-AUBE is one of the 17 cantons of the Aube department, in northern France. INSEE code is 1002.[1]

Canton of Arcis-sur-Aube was created in 1801.[2] At the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015, the canton was expanded from 22 to 47 communes. Arcis-sur-Aube was extended by the communes of the canton Ramerupt (24 communes) and Piney (1 commune).[3][4]
