SECOND MOON () is a South Korean ethnic fusion band that has performed in several folk music festivals around the world.[1][2] It achieved mainstream recognition by creating music for popular television shows like _Princess Hours_ (2006), _Love in the Moonlight_ (2016)[3] and _The Legend of the Blue Sea_ (2016).[4]


Beginnings

Second Moon was founded by commercial and film music producers Kim Hyun-bo and Park Jin-woo in 2004.[5]
