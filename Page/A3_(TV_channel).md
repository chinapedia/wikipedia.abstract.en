ALGERIAN THIRD CHANNEL () also known as AL DJAZAIRA AL THALITHA or simply A3 is an Algerian public national television channel. It is part of the state-owned EPTV group, along with Télévision Algérienne, Canal Algérie, Algeria 4 and Coran TV 5, It is an Arab language channel.


History

A3 was founded on 5 July 2001. On 28 October 2015, the channel has started broadcasting its programs in HD.[1]
