KUBILAY KANATSIZKUŞ (born 28 March 1997 in Bursa, Turkey) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a Forward for Turkish club Bursaspor in the Süper Lig.


Professional career

A long-time youth product of Bursaspor, Kubilay made his first professional appearance in a 2-0 Süper Lig loss to Gençlerbirliği S.K. on 13 February 2016, at the age of 17.[1]

On 31 August 2018 he has joined Ankaragücü with a one-year loan deal.[2] After a half year on loan, his loan contract has been terminated and he returned to Bursaspor.


International career

He has represented the Turkish Football Federation at the U19 and U21 levels.[3]
