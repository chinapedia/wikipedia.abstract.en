CRISTIAN NAGORNÎI is a Moldovan professional footballer who plays for Zimbru Chișinău, as a midfielder.[1]He is also a member of the Moldova under-21 team.


Football career

NAGORNÎI made his professional debut for Zimbru in the Divizia Națională on 1 October 2016 against Saxan, coming on as an 89th-minute substitute.[2]
