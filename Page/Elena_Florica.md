ELENA CRISTINA FLORICA (born 11 May 1992) is a Romanian handballer for SCM Râmnicu Vâlcea and the Romanian national team.[1][2]


Achievements

-   LIGA NAȚIONALĂ:
    -   _Winner_: 2019
-   SUPERCUPA ROMÂNIEI:
    -   _Winner_: 2018
-   WORLD UNIVERSITY CHAMPIONSHIP:
    -   _Silver Medalist_: 2016


Individual awards

-   Gala Premiilor Handbalului Românesc Liga Națională Left Wing of the Season: 2019
