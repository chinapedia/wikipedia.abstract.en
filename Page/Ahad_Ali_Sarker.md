AHAD ALI SARKER is a Bangladesh Awami League politician and the former State Minister of Youth and Sports.[1]


Early life

Ahad was born on 3 January 1952. He has a B.A. degree.[2]


Career

Ahad was elected to parliament in 2008 from Natore-2 as a Bangladesh Awami League candidate.[3] He is the Vice-President of Natore Awami League unit.[4] In February 2016, his home in Natore was attacked by activists of Bangladesh Jubo League.[5][6] On 9 July 2019, his rally was attacked by Jubo League activists.[7]
