NICOLE GAMBLE (born June 21, 1977) is a retired American triple jumper.[1]

She competed at the 1994 World Junior Championships, the 1996 World Junior Championships and the 2000 Summer Olympics without reaching the final. Her personal best jump was 14.05 meters, achieved indoors in March 1999 in Indianapolis.[2][3]

Gamble was born in Sumter, South Carolina.[4]
