NARAYAN DEKA is a Bharatiya Janata Party politician from Assam. He has been elected in Assam Legislative Assembly election in 2016 from Barkhetry constituency.

He is the Director of International School Guwahati and is a big politician.

House panel concern over living standard in TEs[1][2] He defeated Diganta Barman(Indian National Congress),son of four times MLA(Dr Bhumidhar Barman) with a margin of nearly 9000.
