_MIN & MAX_ is a 2016 Chinese romantic comedy film directed by Ma Kan and starring Godfrey Gao and Wang Shuilin.[1] It was released in China on November 25, 2016.[2]


Plot

The comedy and love story between a man and a women with height different.
