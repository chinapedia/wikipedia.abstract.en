YANIK FRICK (born 27 May 1998) is a Liechtensteiner footballer who currently plays as a forward for FC Rapperswil-Jona.


International career

He is a member of the Liechtenstein national football team, making his debut in a 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification match against Albania on 6 October 2016. Frick has also made seven appearances for the Liechtenstein U21 since 2015.[1]


Personal life

He is the eldest son of former footballer Mario Frick, who is the record goalscorer for the Liechtenstein national football team.[2] Yanik's younger brother, Noah, is also a Liechtenstein international footballer.[3]
