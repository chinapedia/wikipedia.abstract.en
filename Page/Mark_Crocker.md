MARK CROCKER (born 26 May 1956) is a Hong Kong former swimmer. He competed at the 1972 Summer Olympics and the 1976 Summer Olympics.[1]

Educated at Island School, he trained at the Harry Wright swim club.[2] He then enrolled at the University of Alabama.[3]
