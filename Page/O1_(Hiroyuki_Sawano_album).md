O1 is the first studio album by Hiroyuki Sawano's vocal project SAWANOHIROYUKI[NZK]. It was released on September 9, 2015 on SME Records. o1 was preceded by the singles "A/Z", "aLIEz", "&Z", "X.U." and "scaPEGoat".[1][2]

The album charted #7 daily rank for first week. It charted so far 11 weeks in the Oricon charts.[3]


Background

"A/Z" was used in the anime television series "Aldnoah.Zero" as the first ending theme, while "aLIEz" was used as the second ending theme. "&Z" was used in Aldnoah Zero's second season as the opening theme.[4]

"X.U." was used as the opening theme for the anime television series "Seraph of the End", while "scaPEGoat" was used as the ending theme.[5]
