Masina}} MASINA is a village in Jhalda I CD Block in Purulia Sadar West subdivision of Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Masina had a total population of 2,724 of which 1,395 (51%) were males and 1,329 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 353. The total number of literates in Masina was 1,447 (61.03% of the population over 6 years).[1]


CD Block HQ

The headquarters of Jhalda I CD Block are located at Masina.[2]
