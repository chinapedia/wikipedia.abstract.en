ARRIGA is a locality in the Shire of Mareeba, Queensland, Australia.[1] In the 2011 census, Arriga had a population of 702 people.[2]


Economy

The Tableland Sugar Mill is located in Arriga (). Built in 1997-1998, it was the first new sugar mill in Queensland for 75 years and the most technologically advanced making extensive use of automation and being environmentally-friendly. It started operation on 28 June 1998.[3]

Chettle Road in Arriga is the location of Lotus Glen Correctional Centre.
