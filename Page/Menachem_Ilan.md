MENACHEM ILAN (מנחם אילן; January 18, 1960) is an Israeli former sport shooter.[1]

He is Jewish, and was born in Israel.[2]

He competed for Israel at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, at the age of 32, in Shooting--Men's Small-Bore Rifle, Prone, 50 metres, and came in tied for 39th.[3][4] He also competed in Shooting--Men's Small-Bore Rifle, Three Positions, 50 metres, and came in 41st.[5][6]
