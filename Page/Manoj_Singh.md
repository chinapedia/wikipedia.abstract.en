MANOJ SINGH (born 15 October 1990) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 27 October 2016.[2] In October 2017, he scored his maiden first-class century for Chhattisgarh against Goa in the 2017–18 Ranji Trophy.[3]

He made his Twenty20 debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 29 January 2017.[4] He made his List A debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 25 February 2017.[5]
