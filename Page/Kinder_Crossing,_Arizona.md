KINDER CROSSING, also known as KINDER SPRING, is a populated place situated in Coconino County, Arizona.[1] Its name is derived from Runyon C. Kinder, who herded sheep in the area during the 1880s.[2] It has an estimated elevation of above sea level.[3]
