Biography

On 3 January 1599, Leonardo Mocenigo was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VIII as Bishop of Ceneda.[2] In 1599, he was consecrated bishop by Pope Clement VIII.[3] He served as Bishop of Ceneda until his death on 20 May 1623.[4] While bishop, he was the principal co-consecrator of Giovanni Delfino (camerlengo), Bishop of Vicenza (1603).[5]
