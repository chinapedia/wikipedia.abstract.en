JAMES WOODBURN-HALL (born 2 February 1995) is an English rugby league footballer who plays as a or for Halifax in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

Woodburn-Hall was born in Isleworth, London, England.


Career

Woodburn-Hall has previously played for the London Broncos in the Super League,[2] spent time on loan at the London Skolars and played for the Skolars and Hemel Stags in Championship One.[3]
