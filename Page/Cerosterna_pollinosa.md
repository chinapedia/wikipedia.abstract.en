_CEROSTERNA POLLINOSA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Buquet in 1859. It is known from Sumatra, Java, Borneo and Laos.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Cerosterna pollinosa pollinosa_ Buquet 1859
-   _Cerosterna pollinosa sulphurea_ Heller 1907
