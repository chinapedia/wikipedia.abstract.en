JOSÉ ANTONIO SERRANO RAMOS (born 6 August 1984), known as JOSÉ ANTONIO, is a Spanish footballer who plays for CDA Navalcarnero as a left back.


Club career

José Antonio was born in Madrid, and represented Getafe CF as a youth. After making his senior debut with the reserves in 2003 in the regional leagues, he made his first team – and La Liga – debut on 29 August 2004, starting and scoring his team's only in a 1–3 away loss against Real Zaragoza.[1]

José Antonio only left _Geta_ in 2008, and subsequently joined CD Toledo in Tercera División. He subsequently resumed his career in Segunda División B but also in the fourth division in the following seasons, representing AD Colmenar Viejo, UD Marbella, CDA Navalcarnero (two stints),[2] CD Puerta Bonita and CF Rayo Majadahonda.[3]
