ROCKY FUENTES is a Filipino professional boxer.

He has twice fought for flyweight world boxing title. He lost to Amnat Ruenroeng for the International Boxing Federation world title.[1][2] He also lost to Román González for the World Boxing Council world title.[3][4]

Fuentes also has a loss to Richie Mepranum by split decision.[5]
