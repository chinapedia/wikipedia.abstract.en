YAAQOUB AL-SAADI (, born June 13, 1996) is an Emirati swimmer. He competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics in the men's 100 metre backstroke; his time of 59.58 seconds in the heats did not qualify him for the semifinals.[1][2]
