EBADOT HOSSAIN (born 7 January 1994) is a Bangladeshi cricketer. He made his international debut for the Bangladesh cricket team in February 2019.[1]


Domestic career

He made his first-class debut for Sylhet Division in the 2016–17 National Cricket League on 25 September 2016.[2]

He made his List A debut for Mohammedan Sporting Club in the 2016–17 Dhaka Premier Division Cricket League on 8 May 2017.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Rangpur Riders on 6 November 2017 in the 2017–18 Bangladesh Premier League.[4]

He was the leading wicket-taker for Central Zone in the 2017–18 Bangladesh Cricket League, with thirteen dismissals in six matches.[5]

In October 2018, he was named in the squad for the Sylhet Sixers team, following the draft for the 2018–19 Bangladesh Premier League.[6] The following month, bowling for North Zone in the 2018–19 Bangladesh Cricket League, he took his maiden ten-wicket match haul in first-class cricket.[7]


International career

In November 2016, he was named in a 22-man preparatory squad to train in Australia, ahead of Bangladesh's tour to New Zealand.[8]

In February 2019, he was added to Bangladesh's Test squad for their series against New Zealand.[9] He made his Test debut for Bangladesh against New Zealand on 28 February 2019.[10]
