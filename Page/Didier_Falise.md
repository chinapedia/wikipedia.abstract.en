DIDIED FALISE (born 3 March 1961) is a retired Belgian triple jumper.

He was born in Oreye.[1] He finished tenth at the 1985 World Indoor Games,[2] eighth at the 1986 European Championships,[3] seventh at the 1987 European Indoor Championships,[4] eighth at the 1987 World Indoor Championships,[5] eleventh at the 1988 Olympic Games[6] and twelfth at the 1989 European Indoor Championships,[7] He also competed at the 1987 World Championships[8] and the 1990 European Championships without reaching the final.[9]

He became Belgian champion every year from 1980 through 1990, except for 1983.[10] His personal best jump was 16.86 metres, achieved in August 1986 in Brussels.[11]
