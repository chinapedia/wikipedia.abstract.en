DOUGLAS K. SHULL (born January 14, 1943) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Shull was born in Sac City, Iowa and attended the University of Iowa. A Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1979 to 1983 (92nd district) and the Iowa Senate from 2003 to 2007 (37th district).[1]
