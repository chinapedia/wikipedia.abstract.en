ÅKNES is a village in Åseral municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. The village is located in the northern part of the Logna river valley, about northeast of the municipal centre of Kyrkjebygda. The village sits on the north shore of the lake Lognavatnet. The Åknes Chapel was built here in 1873 to serve the northern part of the municipality. The small village of Bortelid lies about to the north.
