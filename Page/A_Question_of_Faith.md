_A QUESTION OF FAITH_ is a 2017 American Christian drama film.[1][2] This film was released on September 29, 2017.[3]


Plot

When tragedy strikes three families, their destiny forces them on a converging path to discover God's love, grace and mercy as the challenges of their fate could also resurrect their beliefs.

Release

_A Question of Faith_ was released in the United States on September 29, 2017, and made $1 million from 661 theaters in its opening weekend (an average of $1,551 per venue).[4]

Critical response

On review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, the film has an approval rating of 40% based on 5 reviews, and an average rating of 5.8/10.[5]


Soundtrack

The film features music by Nelson Jackson with additional score by Jason Solowsky. A soundtrack album was released that contains songs by The Nelons, Cecil Thompson, Amber Nelon Thompson, Deloris White, Y'Anna Crawley, and John Paul McGee.
