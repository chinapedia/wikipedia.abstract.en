_CONCHOECIA_ is a genus of ostracods in the subfamily Conchoecinae.

The genus contains bioluminescent species.[1] Interestingly, _Conchoecia_ have a coelenterazine luciferin based bioluminescent system, unlike the cypridinid luciferin based system found in the Cypridinidae.[2]
