STEPHEN DARYL KING (born November 1963) is a British economist. He is senior economic adviser at HSBC Holdings, where he was chief economist from 1998 to July 2015.[1] King also works as a journalist and consultant, and is a specialist adviser to the House of Commons Treasury Committee.[2]

He is author of _Losing Control: The Emerging Threats to Western Prosperity (2010)_, _When the Money Runs Out: The End of Western Affluence (2013)_ and _Grave New World: The End of Globalization, the Return of History (2017)_.[3]

Stephen D. King identifies as a "Jew through choice", converting to Judaism in 1991. He has described experiencing antisemitism since his conversion and believes that antisemitism has become worse over time.[4]
