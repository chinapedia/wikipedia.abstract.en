MIA BAGLEY (born ) is an American actress, singer, and YouTube personality.


Career

Bagley has appeared in three films: _Scoot and Cassie's Christmas Adventure_ (2013), _I'm Not Ready for Christmas_ (2015), and _Cat_ (2015).

Bagley, along with her family, features in a cover video of _Frozen_'s "Do You Want to Build a Snowman?" as a young Princess Anna as well as other covers. The videos are popular on YouTube and have garnered significant media coverage.
