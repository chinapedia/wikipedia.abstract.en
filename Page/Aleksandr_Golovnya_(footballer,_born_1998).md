ALEKSANDR YEGOROVICH GOLOVNYA (; born 27 October 1998) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Rodina Moscow.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Solyaris Moscow on 2 October 2016 in a game against FC Torpedo Vladimir.[1]
