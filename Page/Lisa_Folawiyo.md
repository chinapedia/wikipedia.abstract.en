thumb LISA FOLAWIYO is a Nigerian fashion designer who is best known for incorporating traditional West African textiles with modern tailoring techniques with an emphasis on beading and sequin trim.[1]


Biography

Lisa Folawiyo has a background in law, which she studied at the University of Lagos.[2][3][4][5][6]

Lisa Folawiyo started her label "Jewel by Lisa" in 2005 from her home.[7]


Fashion activities

thumb Lisa Folawiyo has showrooms in Nigeria as well as New York. Celebrities such as Issa Rae have worn her pieces. Rae wore a cummerbund dress to the _BET Presents the American Black Film Festival Honors_.[8] Her collections are also showcased in the US, the UK, Nigeria and South Africa.[9][10] .


Prizes

-   2012: Africa Fashion Award
-   2014: One of eight emerging talents by WWD Women's Wear Daily[11]
-   2015: Featured in the BOF500[12]


Personal life

Folawiyo had been married for 14 years. She is a mother of two children. Her father-in-law was the industrialist Wahab Iyanda Folawiyo.[13] [14]
