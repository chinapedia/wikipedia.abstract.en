POSTOAK (see also POST OAK, POST OAK SPRINGS) is an unincorporated community in Roane County, Tennessee, United States.[1]


Notable people

-   Joshua Berkey, politician, newspaper editor, and Christian minister, was born in Post Oak Springs, in Roane County.[2]
