TIMOTHY JOHN RAPHAEL (26 September 192920 November 2016)[1] was an Anglican priest.[2]

Raphael was educated at Christ's College, Christchurch and the University of Leeds. after which he was a Scientific Officer at RAE Bedford. He was a curate at St Stephen, Westminster[3] from 1955 to 1960. He was Vicar of Welling from 1960 to 1965; Dean of Dunedin from 1965 to 1973;[4] Vicar of St John's Wood, London from 1973 to 1983;[5] and Archdeacon of Middlesex from 1983[6] to 1996.[7]
