IDRISS MHIRSI (born 21 February 1994) is a Tunisian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Red Star in the French second tier. He is a youth product of ES Tunis.[1]


International career

Mhirsi debuted for Tunisia in 2013 against Morocco.[2]
