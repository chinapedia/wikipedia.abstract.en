DANIEL MCKAY BROADHEAD was a Scottish amateur football wing half who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1][2]


Personal life

As of 1911, Broadhead was a student teacher.[3] He served as a private in the Highland Light Infantry during the First World War.[4]
