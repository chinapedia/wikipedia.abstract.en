JEFRI KURNIAWAN (born in Malang, 18 March 1991) is an Indonesian footballer of Chinese Indonesians descent who currently plays for Badak Lampung in the Liga 1.

He was called up to Indonesian national team for a friendly match against Vietnam on 9 October 2016.[1]


Career

Jefri Kurniawan was named as the best player in the 2015 Merdeka Cup when playing for Persinga Ngawi. Jefri also played for Persegres Gresik United in the 2015 Jendral Sudirman Cup.[2]

Pusamania Borneo F.C.

Jefri made his debut with Pusamania Borneo in 2016 Indonesia Soccer Championship A against Bali United. He scored his first goal in the third matchday, playing against Persiba Balikpapan.
