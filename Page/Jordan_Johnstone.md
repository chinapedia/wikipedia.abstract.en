JORDAN JOHNSTONE (born 24 May 1997) is an English rugby league footballer who plays as a for the Widnes Vikings in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

Johnstone was born in Whitehaven, Cumbria, England.


Career

He was in the Cumbria Regional Academy system[2] and played his junior rugby league for the Kells club.[3]

He made his début for Whitehaven in the Kingstone Press Championship in 2015.[4][5]

At the end of 2015 he moved to the Widnes Vikings,[6] making his début in 2016 against the Catalans Dragons.[7]

In 2018 Johnstone joined the London Broncos on loan, and made his Broncos debut in the victory over the Dewsbury Rams in Round 2 of the Betfred Championship.[8]
