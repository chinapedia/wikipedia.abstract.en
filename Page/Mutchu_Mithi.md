MUTCHU MITHI (born April 28, 1978 at Roing, Arunachal Pradesh is an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Mithi was elected from Roing seat in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election,.[2][3][4] In terms of educational qualification, he is a graduate.
