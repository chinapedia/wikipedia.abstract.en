ANANDA MILLANGODA (Sinhalese: ආනන්ද මිල්ලන්ගොඩ ) is a famous elephant owner and a politician in Sri Lanka. He was born on 25 September 1952 at Millangoda Village in Kegalle District. He and his father owned the world famous tusker, Millangoda Raja[1] together with many more elephants. Currently he is the chief organizer of Kegalle electorate of Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP).[2] He is also serving as a member of provincial council of Sabaragamuwa.[3]
