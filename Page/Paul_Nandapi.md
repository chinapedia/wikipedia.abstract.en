PAUL NANDAPI (born 21 December 1961) is a retired Australian discus thrower.

He finished sixth at the 1985 World Cup,[1] won the bronze medal at the 1985 Pacific Conference Games,[2] the silver medal at the 1986 Commonwealth Games,[3] finished fifth at the 1989 World Cup[4] and won the bronze medal at the 1990 Commonwealth Games.[5]
