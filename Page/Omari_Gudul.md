OMARI GUDUL (born May 18, 1994) is a Congolese professional basketball player who plays for Levski Sofia. Standing at 6 ft 9 in (2.06 m), Gudul usually plays as power forward or center.


Professional career

In August 2016, Gudul signed his first professional contract with BC Beroe of the Bulgarian NBL.[1] In his first NBL season, Gudul averaged 11.8 points and 6.2 rebounds per game.[2]
