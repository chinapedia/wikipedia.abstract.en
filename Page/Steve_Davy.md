STEVE DAVY is a British bass guitarist and vocalist, known for being a member of the blues-rock band Steamhammer. He played on the first three Steamhammer albums.[1] Subsequently he left the band[2] and was replaced by Louis Cennamo.[3]

On the _Steamhammer_ (also known as _Reflection_) and _Mk II_ albums of 1969, Davy played bass guitar and sang backing vocals.[4] On the 1970 _Mountains_ album he also played organ.

In 2005 and 2008 he was credited with photography for the _Language_ and _Dooji Wooji_ albums by Lorraine Feather.[5]
