RÖVSVETT is a hardcore punk rock band from Sweden. It was formed in 1983 in Tranås, Jönköping County. The name is approximately translated as "Ass sweat".[1][2][3][4]

Their records were released mostly by labels Birdnest Records, Bucho Discos, Just4Fun Records, and SixWeeks Records.
