SNEAD'S GREEN, sometimes written SNEADS GREEN, is an area of Droitwich Spa, Worcestershire, England.[1][2]

Francis Moule, of Snead's Green House, sold the manorial rights in 1809.[3] The house had been owned by the Moule (or Moyle) family since 1621.[4]
