_HUNYADISCUS SAURINI_ is a species of air-breathing land snails, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusc in the family Plectopylidae.

The specific name _saurini_ is in honour of French geologist and malacologist Edmond Saurin (1904â€“1977), who collected type material.[1] Saurin's type material is as of 2016 the only known material of this species.[2]


Distribution

The distribution of _Hunyadiscus saurini_ includes Laos.[3]

The type locality is "Laos, Pa Hia (Ancienne province Tran Ninh)".[4]
