JEAN DELL (born 17 March 1961) is a French actor and writer.


Career

After starting out in radio, he turned to the stage, film and television, where he will create and perform several sketches of the show Les Grosses Têtes. He also participated in the show _La Classe_, where he met his wife, Christiane Bopp.[1]
