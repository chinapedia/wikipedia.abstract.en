Dr. MOHAMED HASSOUNA FHEMA (; d. 2008) served as Chairman of the Arab Scout Committee.

In 1983, he was awarded the 166th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
