_DIALYCERAS_ is a genus of trees in the family Sphaerosepalaceae. The species are all endemic to Madagascar.


Species

_The Plant List_ and _Tropicos_ recognise 3 accepted species:[1][2]

-   _Dialyceras coriaceum_
-   _Dialyceras discolor_
-   _Dialyceras parvifolium_
