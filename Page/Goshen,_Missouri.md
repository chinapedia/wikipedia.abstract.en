GOSHEN is an unincorporated community in Mercer County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Goshen was originally called McKinneysville, and under the latter name was platted in 1860.[2] A post office called Goshen was established in 1854, and remained in operation until 1904.[3] The community derives its name from the Land of Goshen, a place mentioned in the Hebrew Bible.[4]
