THOMAS R. PATTERSON (born May 6, 1943) was an American politician in the state of Florida.

Patterson was born in Michigan and moved to Florida in 1965.[1] He served in the Florida House of Representatives for the 2nd district from 1976 to 1982, as a Democrat.[2]
