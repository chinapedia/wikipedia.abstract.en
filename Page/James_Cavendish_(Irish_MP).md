JAMES CAVENDISH (circa 1749 – January 1808) was an Anglo-Irish politician.

Cavendish sat in the Irish House of Commons as the Member of Parliament for Lifford between 1773 and 1776, before sitting for Banagher from 1776 to 1783.[1]
