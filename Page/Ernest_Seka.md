ERNEST SEKA (born 22 June 1987) is a Guinean professional footballer who plays as a defender for AS Nancy.[1]


International career

Seka was born in France to an Ivorian father and Guinean mother.[2] On 23 April 2018, he agreed to represent the Guinea national football team.[3]

Seka made his debut for Guinea in a 1–0 2019 Africa Cup of Nations qualification win over Central African Republic on 9 September 2018.[4]
