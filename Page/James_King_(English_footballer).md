JAMES KING (born 27 December 1996) is an English professional footballer who plays for Bradford City, as a central defender.


Career

King began his career at Bradford City, signing a new one-year contract with the club in June 2016.[1] He made his senior debut for the club on 9 November 2016, in a EFL Trophy game against Morecambe, playing out-of-position as a central midfielder.[2][3] In May 2017 it was announced that he would be released at the end of the season, when his contract expired.[4]
