GIFTY EUGENIA KUSI (born 11 February 1958) is a Ghanaian politician. She was the member of parliament for the Tarkwa-Nsuaem (Ghana parliament constituency) from 2001 to 2017. She is also the principal research assistant in the department of Community Health at the University of Ghana Medical School-Korle-Bu.[1][2][3]


Early life

She hails from Tarkwa in the Western Region of Ghana.[4]


Education

Kusi holds an M.Phil. degree in Sociology from the University of Ghana.[5]


Personal life

She is married with four children.[6]


Career

Kusi started career in politics from the year 2001, when she contested for the Member of Parliament on the ticket of the New Patriotic Party (NPP) for the Tarkwa-Nsueam Constituency.[7][8]

Committees

Kusi is a member of:

-   New Patriotic Party(NPP) Disciplinary Committee[9]
-   Parliament of Ghana Business Committee.[10][11]
-   Parliament of Ghana Committee on Gender and Children.[12]
-   Parliament of Ghana committee on Health.[13]
