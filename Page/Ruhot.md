RUHOT is a village in the western part of Kosovo[1][2] which falls under the jurisdiction of municipality of Peja.[3] It is located in a larger region which is known as Rrafshi i Dugagjinit, famous for its fertile land and beautiful nature.[4] White Drin flows along the whole north side of the village and is used as a source of water for crops.[5]
