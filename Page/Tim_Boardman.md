TIMOTHY BOARDMAN (born May 23, 1996) is an American actor, singer, and dancer. He is best known for portraying Miles Walton in _Miles_,[1] John Ryan in _Madam Secretary_, and Dylan in _The Wilde Wedding_.


Early life

Boardman was born and raised in Fairfield, Connecticut, where he began performing at the age of 4. It was his mother and her twin sister singing in church that first inspired him to pursue his love of the arts.


Career

Boardman worked alongside Glenn Close and John Malkovich in Damian Harris' _The Wilde Wedding_ (2017). He starred in _Miles_ as Miles with Molly Shannon and Paul Reiser, which premiered in 2016.[2][3][4][5][6][7] He also appears in Tina Fey's _Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt_ on Netflix as Dylan[8] and _Madam Secretary_ as John Ryan on CBS.[9]
