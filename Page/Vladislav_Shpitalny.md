VLADISLAV VADIMOVICH SHPITALNY (; born 5 September 1996) is a Russian football player. He plays in Portugal for Paredes.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Sokol Saratov on 15 October 2016 in a game against FC Yenisey Krasnoyarsk.[1]


Personal life

His father Vadim Shpitalny played in the Russian First Division for FC Sokol Saratov and FC Amkar Perm. His grandfather, also called Vadim Shpitalny, played for Sokol Saratov in the Soviet First League and then was the manager of Sokol.
