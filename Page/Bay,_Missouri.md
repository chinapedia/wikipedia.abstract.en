BAY is an unincorporated community in Gasconade County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Bay was established in 1860, and remained in operation until 1963.[2] It is not known to the State Historical Society of Missouri why the name "Bay" was applied to this community.[3]
