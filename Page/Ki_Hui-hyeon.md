KI HUI-HYEON (born June 16, 1995), better known by the mononym HUIHYUN and former stage name CATHY, is a South Korean singer and rapper. She is best known as a member of the South Korean girl group DIA.


Career

Pre-debut

She is a former Woollim Entertainment trainee and trained with members of the girl group Lovelyz.

2015â€“present: Debut and _Produce 101_

In February 2015, MBK Entertainment announced its plans to debut a new girl group under survival program through which the public could become familiar with the group members. In June, MBK announced that they had canceled their plans for a survival program, and decided to select the members internally. MBK Entertainment revealed the final line-up and the group name.[1]

Ki debuted under stage name Cathy as the main rapper of DIA. The group made their official debut stage on the music program Mnet's _M Countdown_ on September 17, 2015. Shortly after debut with the group in December 2015, MBK Entertainment announced she had temporarily withdrawn from the group, along with her bandmate Jung Chae-yeon, to represent MBK Entertainment on reality girl group survival show _Produce 101_ for the chance to debut in a Mnet girl group. She auditioned and signed contracts with the show before DIA's official debut.[2] Ki finished in 19th place.[3] On May 11, 2016, MBK Entertainment confirmed that she and Jung Chaeyeon had re-joined DIA.[4] She has changed her stage name from Cathy to her real name (Huihyeon) since rejoining the group.[5][6] On August 17, it was announced that Huihyeon would be collaborating with I.O.I members Choi Yoo-jung and Jeon So-mi for the digital single, _"_Flower, Wind and You_"_, which was produced by Boi B.[7][8] She is the current leader of the group, starting from the promotions of the mini album _Spell_.

In 2016, she also appeared on Mnet's rap survival program _Unpretty Rapstar 3_ as a contestant.[9]
