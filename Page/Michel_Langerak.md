MICHEL LANGERAK (born 8 July 1968, Dordrecht) is a Dutch former professional soccer player and a soccer coach for amateur and semi-professional teams.

As a professional soccer player, Langerak played for FC Dordrecht (different iterations), VVV Venlo (on loan), NEC Nijmegen, AZ Alkmaar, and Sparta Rotterdam.

Next he played for the amateur clubs Delta Vlaardingen and FC Lienden in the Hoofdklasse.

He coached De Zwerver, LRC Leerdam, Kozakken Boys, and ASWH.[1] In 2016, he coaches VV Sliedrecht.[2]
