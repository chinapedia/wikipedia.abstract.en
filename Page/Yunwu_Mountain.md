YUNWU MOUNTAIN (), also known as THE CLOUD AND MIST MOUNTAIN, is a mountain located in Shaoyang, Hunan, China, with a height of above sea level. It is a part of Xuefeng Mountains.[1]

Yunwu Mountain is noted for Buddhist temples, such as Yunwu Temple (), Chaoyang Temple () and Baoding Temple ().[2][3]
