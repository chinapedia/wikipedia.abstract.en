MANSOUR BENOTHMANE (born August 7, 1997 in Tiaret) is an Algerian footballer who is currently playing as a midfielder for ASO Chlef in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1 on loan from MC Alger.

Benothmane is an Algeria under-20 international.


Club career

On November 26, Benothmane made his senior debut for Club Africain in a league match against CS Hammam-Lif, scoring a goal just a few minutes after coming onto the pitch.[1]
