HENRY NUZUM (born March 4, 1977) is an American rower. He competed at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, where he placed 6th in the men's double sculls, along with Aquil Abdullah. Nuzum was born in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.[1]
