SEYED MOHAMMAD NAZEMASHARIEH (, born 22 September 1969) is an Iranian retired futsal player and current coach. He is head coach of Iran national futsal team.

He nominated for world best futsal coach title in 2016.[1]


Honours

National team

-   Third-place, FIFA Futsal World Cup, 2016
-   Champions, AFC Futsal Championship, 2016 ; 2018
-   Runners-up, Grand Prix de Futsal, 2015
-   Champions, Futsal at the 2017 Asian Indoor and Martial Arts Games
