MANFREDO ALIPALA is a Filipino boxer who competed at the 1964 Summer Olympics.

He won a gold medal at the 1962 Asian Games.[1]

Alipala died in his sleep at his family residence in Barangay San Roque, Tarlac City on October 8, 2006 at age 68. He was buried at the Garden of Peace Memorial Park in Sapang Maragul, also within the city.[2]
