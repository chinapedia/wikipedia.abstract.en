MOHAMED AFILAL (; ) served as Chairman of the Arab Scout Committee from 2004 to 2007, as well as General Commissioner of the Fédération Nationale du Scoutisme Marocain in 2005.

In 2005, he was awarded the 304th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1][2]
