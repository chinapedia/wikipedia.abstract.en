_GO JU JU GO_ is a live album released in 1987 by the Washington, D.C.-based go-go band Experience Unlimited.[1]


Track listing

1.  "Go Ju Ju Go" – 4:45
2.  "Shake It Like a White Girl" – 2:30
3.  "Alright Oh No" – 2:30

2.  "Go Go Trail" – 4:30
3.  "Pump It Up" – 2:00
4.  "Hey Hey Hey" – 2:00
