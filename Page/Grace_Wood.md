GRACE WOOD (born 19 August 1932) is a British former swimmer.


Swimming career

She competed in the women's 400 metre freestyle at the 1952 Summer Olympics.[1]

She represented England and won a bronze medal in the 440 Yard Freestyle Relay at the 1950 British Empire Games in Auckland, New Zealand.[2][3]
