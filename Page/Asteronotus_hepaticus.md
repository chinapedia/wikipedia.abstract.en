_ASTERONOTUS HEPATICUS_ is a species of sea slug or dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Discodorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was originally described from the Pacific Ocean. It is known from Vanuatu, Indonesia, the Solomon Islands and Papua New Guinea.[2]


Description

_Asteronotus hepaticus_ is a large dorid nudibranch with a distinctive deep purple colouration.[3]
