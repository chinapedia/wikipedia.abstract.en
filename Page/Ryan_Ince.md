RYAN INCE (born 16 September 1996) is an English rugby league footballer who plays on the wing for the Widnes Vikings in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

Ryan Ince was born in Widnes England.


Playing career

He graduated from the Widnes Vikings Academy system[2] and played his junior rugby league for the Widnes Moorfield club.[3] He has represented England at youth team level.[4]

He made his début for Whitehaven, whilst on loan at the club in the Kingstone Press Championship in 2016.[5][6] In 2016, he made his Widnes Vikings début against Hull FC.[7]
