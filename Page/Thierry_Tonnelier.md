THIERRY TONNELIER (born 28 December 1959) is a retired French middle-distance runner who competed primarily in the 800 metres.

He won the bronze medals at the 1983 European Indoor Championships[1] and the 1986 European Indoor Championships[2] He became French indoor champion in 1984 and 1985.[3]
