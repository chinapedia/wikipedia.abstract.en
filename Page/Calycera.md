_CALYCERA_ is a genus of flowering plants in family Calyceraceae endemic to South America. It has been considered to contain 14 species, or more recently nine.[1]


Species

Species include:[2]

-   _Calycera calcitrapa_
-   _Calycera crassifolia_
-   _Calycera eryngioides_
-   _Calycera herbacea_
-   _Calycera horrida_
-   _Calycera leucanthema_
-   _Calycera pulvinata_
-   _Calycera sessiliflora_
-   _Calycera sympaganthera_
