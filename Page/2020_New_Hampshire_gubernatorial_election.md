The 2020 NEW HAMPSHIRE GUBERNATORIAL ELECTION will be held on November 3, 2020, to elect the Governor of New Hampshire. Incumbent Republican Governor Chris Sununu is eligible to run for re-election to a third term in office, and has declared his intent to do so.[1]


Republican primary

Candidates

Declared

-   Chris Sununu, incumbent Governor of New Hampshire[2]

Potential

-   Kelly Ayotte, former U.S. Senator[3]
-   Frank Edelblut, commissioner of the New Hampshire Department of Education and candidate for governor in 2016[4]
-   Chuck Morse, minority leader of the New Hampshire Senate[5]


Democratic primary

Candidates

Formed exploratory committee

-   Andru Volinsky, member of the Executive Council of New Hampshire[6]

Potential

-   Joyce Craig, mayor of Manchester[7]
-   Dan Feltes, majority leader of the New Hampshire Senate[8]
-   Molly Kelly, former state senator and nominee for governor in 2018[9]
-   Steve Marchand, former mayor of Portsmouth and candidate for governor in 2016 and 2018[10]

Declined

-   Mindi Messmer, former state representative and candidate for New Hampshire's 1st congressional district in 2018 (running for Executive Council)[11]
