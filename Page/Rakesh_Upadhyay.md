RAKESH UPADHYAY is an Indian film producer,[1] singer and business partner of Himesh Reshammiya[2]


Career

Known for his film _Khiladi 786_ Rakesh Upadhyay was also the Creative Producer and project consultant of Bollywood movies, including _The Xpose_, _Teraa Surroor_, _Best of Luck_, _Holiday_, and _The Shaukeens_.
