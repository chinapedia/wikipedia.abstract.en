THE RICHEST AFRICANS is an annual ranking of the richest African people, compiled and published by the American business magazine _Forbes_ . The list has been published since 2015. Dangote Group founder Aliko Dangote has topped the 2018 list.[1] In 2018, there was a record of 23 African billionaires on the list.[2]
