_GUDEODISCUS FISCHERI_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Plectopylidae.


Distribution

The distribution of _Gudeodiscus fischeri_ includes Vietnam.[1]


Ecology

It is a ground-dwelling species as all other plectopylid snails in Vietnam.[2]

_Gudeodiscus anceyi_ and _Gudeodiscus emigrans quadrilamellatus_ live at geographically close sites to _Gudeodiscus fischeri_.[3]
