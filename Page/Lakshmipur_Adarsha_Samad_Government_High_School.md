LAKSHMIPUR ADARSHA SAMAD GOVERNMENT HIGH SCHOOL () is a boys' high school located in Lakshmipur, Bangladesh. It is one of the oldest schools in the country. The school was established in 1887 Lakshmipur Sadar Upazila, Bangladesh.[1] It was established in 1886[2] by the summit of local advocates and authorised by the University of Kolkata. The school is under the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Comilla.
