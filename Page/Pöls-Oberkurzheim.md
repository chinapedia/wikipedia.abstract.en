PÖLS-OBERKURZHEIM is a municipality since 2015 in the Murtal District of Styria, Austria.

The municipality, Pöls-Oberkurzheim, was created as part of the Styria municipal structural reform,[1] at the end of 2014, by merging the former market town Pöls with the municipality Oberkurzheim.[2]

The municipality consists of the six Katastralgemeinden Allerheiligen, Enzersdorf, Oberkurzheim, Pöls, Thalheim and Unterzeiring.

Tourismus

The municipality formed, together with Pusterwald and Pölstal, the tourism agency "Region Pölstal". The base is in the town Pölstal.[4]
