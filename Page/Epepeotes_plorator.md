_EPEPEOTES PLORATOR_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Newman in 1842. It is known from Sulawesi and the Philippines.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Epepeotes plorator celebensis_ Aurivillius, 1921
-   _Epepeotes plorator plorator_ (Newman, 1842)
-   _Epepeotes plorator sanghiricus_ Breuning, 1938
-   _Epepeotes plorator uniformis_ Breuning, 1943
