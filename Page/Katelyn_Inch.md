KATELYN INCH (born 1995) is a New Zealand international lawn bowler.[1]


Bowls career

World Championships

Inch was born in Rangiora and brought up in Oxford, New Zealand. She made her debut for New Zealand in 2015 and won a bronze medal in the fours at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch with Angela Boyd, Val Smith and Kirsten Edwards.[2]

Commonwealth Games

She was selected as part of the New Zealand team for the 2018 Commonwealth Games on the Gold Coast in Queensland.[3]

Asia Pacific

Inch has won three medals at the Asia Pacific Bowls Championships, the latest being a gold medal at the 2019 Asia Pacific Bowls Championships in the Gold Coast, Queensland.[4][5]
