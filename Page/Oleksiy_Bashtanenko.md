OLEKSIY BASHTANENKO (; born 16 March 1994 in Dnipropetrovsk, Ukraine) is a Ukrainian football goalkeeper.


Career

Bashtanenko is a product of Youth Sportive School #2 Dnipropetrovsk (first trainer was Vadym Matyash) and FC Dnipro Youth Sportive School Systems (first trainers were Oleksiy Chystyakov and Serhiy Maksymych).

From 2011, after graduation from the youth sportive school, he played in the FC Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk reserves. In the main-team squad Bashtanenko made his debut as a start-squad player in the match against FC Zirka Kropyvnytskyi on 17 September 2016 in the Ukrainian Premier League.[1]
