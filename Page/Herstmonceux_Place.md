Herstmonceux_Place_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1586400.jpg HERSTMONCEUX PLACE is an 18th-century country house in Herstmonceux, East Sussex. It was divided into apartments in the 1950s. The house stands within a shared estate with Herstmonceux Castle. Herstmonceux Place is a Grade I listed building.[1]

The gardens and parks of Herstmonceux Place and Castle are Grade II* listed on the Register of Historic Parks and Gardens.[2]
