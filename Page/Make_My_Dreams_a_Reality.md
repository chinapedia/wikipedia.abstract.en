"MAKE MY DREAMS A REALITY" is a song written and performed by GQ. It reached No. 8 on the U.S. R&B chart in 1979.[1] The song was featured on their 1979 album, _Disco Nights_[2] and later became the B-side to their next single, "I Do Love You".

The song was produced by Jimmy Simpson and Beau Ray Fleming.[3]
