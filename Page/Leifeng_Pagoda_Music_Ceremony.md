''''' Leafing Pagoda Music Ceremony ''''' _( 雷峰夕照音乐大典 )_ is a work for music ceremony,[1][2] composed and directed by He Xuntian.[3][4]

1300 performers made a debut of it on 25 October 2002 in Hangzhou.[5]


Summary

_Leifeng Pagoda Music Ceremony_ has ten movements: _Cloud Bells_; _Paramita_; _Song of the Enlightenment_; _Song of Pipa_; _Monks_; _Heart Sutra_; _Earth Drums_; _Dance of the White Snake_; _Spring Song_; _Moons upon a Thousand River_.[6]
