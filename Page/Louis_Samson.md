LOUIS SAMSON (born 3 July 1995) is a German footballer who plays as a midfielder for Erzgebirge Aue.[1]


Career

Samson joined Erzgebirge Aue from Hertha BSC II in June 2015.[2] On 1 June 2017, Eintracht Braunschweig announced the signing of Samson on a two-year contract.[3]


Personal life

He is of Nigerian descent through his father.[4]
