MARTÍN ACUÑA, O. CARM. (died 1593) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Lipari (1585–1593).[1]


Biography

Martín Acuña was ordained a priest in the Carmelite Order.[2] On 11 December 1585, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Sixtus V as Bishop of Lipari.[3] He served as Bishop of Lipari until his death in 1593.[4]
