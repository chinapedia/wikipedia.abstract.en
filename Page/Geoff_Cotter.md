GEOFFREY DAVID COTTER (born 4 October 1966) is a New Zealand rower.

Cotter was born in 1966 in Taihape, New Zealand.[1] [2] He represented New Zealand at the 1988 Summer Olympics in the coxless four in a team with Campbell Clayton-Greene, Bill Coventry, and Neil Gibson, where they came seventh.[3] He is listed as New Zealand Olympian athlete number 542 by the New Zealand Olympic Committee.[4]
