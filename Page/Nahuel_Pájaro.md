NAHUEL PÁJARO (born 9 May 1997) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Aldosivi.[1][2]


Career

Pájaro began in the youth ranks of Aldosivi at the age of eight, eleven years later he was promoted into the club's first-team.[3] His debut came on 3 February 2016 in a 1–1 draw against Newell's Old Boys during the 2016 Argentine Primera División season. He made three further appearances in 2016.[4][5] However, Pájaro appeared in zero matches for Aldosivi during 2016–17 as the team were relegated to Primera B Nacional.[6] He joined Primera B Metropolitana side San Telmo on loan in February 2018.[7]
