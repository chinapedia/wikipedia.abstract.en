'''Linda Eshun ''' (born 5 August 1992) is a Ghanaian international footballer who plays as a defender who has played for the Ghana women's national football team since 2014. She competed at the 2014 African Women's Championship. At the club level, she played for Hasaacas Ladies in Ghana.[1]
