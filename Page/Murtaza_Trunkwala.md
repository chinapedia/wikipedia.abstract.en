MURTAZA TRUNKWALA (born 20 January 1996) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Maharashtra in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 20 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Maharashtra in the 2017–18 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 17 February 2018.[3]
