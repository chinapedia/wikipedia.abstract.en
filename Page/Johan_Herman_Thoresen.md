JOHAN HERMAN THORESEN (14 July 1832  – 28 January 1914) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Herøy to priest Hans Konrad Thoresen and Sara Margrethe Daae. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1854, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1884.[1]

He was decorated Knight, First Class of the Order of St. Olav in 1892, and Commander in 1897.[2]
