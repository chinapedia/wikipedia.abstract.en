LASSOUMTI is a village in the commune of Mayo-Baléo in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, near the .


Population

In 1971, Lassoumti contained 240 inhabitants, mainly Kutin.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 477 people in the village.[2]
