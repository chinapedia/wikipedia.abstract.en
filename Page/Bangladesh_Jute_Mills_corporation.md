BANGLADESH JUTE MILLS CORPORATION or BJMC, is a public corporation that owns and manages all government jute mills in Bangladesh and is located in Dhaka.[1]


History

The corporation was formed in 1972 when the government of Bangladesh nationalised all the Jute Mills in the country.[2] It manages Adamjee Jute Mills.[3] It is responsible for nine state run jute mills in Bangladesh.[4] Team BJMC is a professional football team of the corporation.[5] In 2016 the government announced plans to upgrade the jute mills spending US$340 million with Chinese assistance.[6]
