FEISTRITZTAL is since 2015 a municipality in Hartberg-Fürstenfeld District in Styria, Austria.

The municipality was founded as part of the Styria municipal structural reform,[1] on 31 December 2014, from the dissolved independent municipalities Blaindorf, Kaibing, Sankt Johann bei Herberstein and Siegersdorf bei Herberstein (all in political District Hartberg-Fürstenfeld), plus Hirnsdorf (in political Weiz District.[2]

The border of the Hartberg-Fürstenfeld District and Weiz District was altered, that the new municipality lies completely in Hartberg-Fürstenfeld District.[3]


Municipality arrangement

The municipal territory includes the following seven sections (populations as of 2015[4]):

-   Blaindorf (305)
-   Hirnsdorf (673)
-   Hofing (128)
-   Illensdorf (230)
-   Kaibing (391)
-   Sankt Johann bei Herberstein (387)
-   Siegersdorf bei Herberstein (293)

The municipality consists of 7 Katastralgemeinden (areas 2015[5]):

-   Hirnsdorf (456.78 ha)
-   Hofing (535.21 ha)

-   St. Johann bei Herberstein (282.11 ha)
-   Siegersdorf ()
