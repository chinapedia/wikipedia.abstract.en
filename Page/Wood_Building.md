WOOD BUILDING, also known as the CENTRAL INN, CENTRAL HOTEL, CENTRAL TAVERN, CENTRAL BAR, CORNER INN, CORNER BAR, CORNER PUB, and MAC'S TAVERN, is a historic commercial building located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It built about 1908-1910, and is a rectangular, three-story, red brick, two-part commercial block building. It features an original, decorative sheet-metal cornice, a prominent parapet, and a cast-iron column at the recessed corner entrance.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2003.[2]
