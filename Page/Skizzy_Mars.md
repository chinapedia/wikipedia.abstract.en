MYLES MILLS[1] (born June 8, 1993),[2] better known by his stage name SKIZZY MARS, is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer from Harlem, New York.[3]


Early life

Mills was born and raised in Harlem, New York to his father, a therapist, and his mother, who operated two daycare centers in Manhattan.[4] He attended an all-boys school St. Bernard’s School during his elementary years and later graduated from the Browning School. Mills attended Union College to originally pursue a sports journalism major,[5] but he dropped out ten weeks after starting classes to begin his rapping career.[6][7]
