GOJEN GADI is an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Gadi was elected from Basar seat in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election, standing as a People's Party of Arunachal candidate.[2][3][4] he lost the 2019 MLA election to Mr Gokar basar of NPP
