GRAEME ARCHER (born 1 February 1967) is a Scottish lawn bowler from Edinburgh.

He won the lawn bowls gold medal in the triples competition at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship.[1] In 2014 he married English international bowler Carol Ashby.[2]

He was National champion in 1995.[3]
