BRUSHY CREEK is a stream in Morgan County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Gravois Creek.

The stream headwaters arise at [2] adjacent to Missouri Route 135 and north of the Proctor Towersite State Wildlife Area at an elevation of .[3] The stream flows northwest crossing under Missouri Route J to enter Gravois Creek at and an elevation of .[4] The confluence is adjacent to Missouri Route 5 approximately 2.5 miles northwest of the community of Gravois Mills.[5]

Brushy Creek was so named on account of brush near its course.[6]
