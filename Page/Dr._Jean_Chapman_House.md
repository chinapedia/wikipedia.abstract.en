DR. JEAN CHAPMAN HOUSE is a historic home located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It built in 1963, and is a one-story, "V"-shaped dwelling with Wrightian style influences. It is built on a steeply sloped lot and is constructed almost entirely of local Ste. Genevieve limestone.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2015.[2]
