GÁBOR BORSOS (born 30 June 1991) is a Hungarian tennis player.

Borsos has a career high ATP singles ranking of 723 achieved on 24 October 2016. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 188, achieved on 13 November 2017. Borsos has won 20 ITF doubles titles.

Borsos has represented Hungary at Davis Cup, where he has a win-loss record of 2–3.
