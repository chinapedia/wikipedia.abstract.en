WHEATFIELDS is a census-designated place (CDP) in Gila County, Arizona, United States. The population was 785 at the 2010 United States Census.[1] It is one of two places in Arizona with this name, the other being a populated place in Apache County.


Geography

Wheatfields is located in southern Gila County in the valley of Pinal Creek. Arizona State Route 188 forms the western edge of the CDP, leading northwest to Theodore Roosevelt Lake and south to Globe, the county seat.
