HUGH BUTLER was a Scottish amateur football inside right who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

Butler worked as an iron manufacturer's clerk.[2] He served as a private in the Cameronians (Scottish Rifles) and the King's Own Yorkshire Light Infantry during the First World War.[3]
