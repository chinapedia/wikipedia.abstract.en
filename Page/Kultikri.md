KULTIKRI is a village, in Sankrail CD Block in Jhargram subdivision of Jhargram district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Kultikri had a total population of 1797 of which 907 (50%) were males and 890 (50%) were females. Population below 6 years was 89. The total number of literates in Kultikri was 622 (78.04% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Transport

Kultikri is on the Rogra-Hatigeria Road which meet SH5 at Hatigeria. The Guptamoni-Kultikri Road and the Kultikri-Poradiha Road both meet in NH6 converge on the village. More over, another two PMGSY roads which connect two corner sides.[2]


Education

Sankrail Anil Biswas Smriti Mahavidyalaya was established in 2007 and is affiliated to Vidyasagar University. It offers arts courses with honours in English, Bengali, Sanskrit, Santali, history and political science.[3] Kultikri Teachers' Training Institute of Higher Studies is also a D.El.Ed. Cum B.Ed. training Institute affiliated to West Bengal University of Teachers' Training, Education Planning and Administration There are several High Schools here for School level Education among of them notable are Kultikri Girls' High School,Kultikri S.C. High School(ESTD 1945), one of the oldest and renowned school of this area as well as the district
