GROUP D OF THE 2017 AFRICA CUP OF NATIONS was played from 17 to 25 January 2017 in Gabon. The group consisted of Ghana, Mali, Egypt, and Uganda.[1]

Notes


Standings

In the quarter-finals:

-   The group winners, Egypt, advanced to play the runners-up of Group C, Morocco.
-   The group runners-up, Ghana, advanced to play the winners of Group C, DR Congo.
