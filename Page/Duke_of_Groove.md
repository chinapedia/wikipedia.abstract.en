_DUKE OF GROOVE_ is a 1996 American short film directed by Griffin Dunne. It was nominated for Academy Award for Best Live Action Short Film.[1] [2]


Plot

A mother (Kate Capshaw) takes her teenage son (Tobey Macguire) to a party, to make sure he will not be at home when father leaves the family for good.


Cast

-   Tobey Macguire as Rich Cooper
-   Kate Capshaw as Rebecka
-   Kiefer Sutherland as The Host
-   Uma Thurman as Maya


Production

American rock band Swell also appears in the film and contribute to the music.[3]
