BAKARI BATA (also Bakiri Bata) is a village in the commune of Belel in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon.


Population

In 1967, the settlement contained 681 inhabitants, mostly Fula people[1] In the 2005 census, 879 people were counted there.[2]


Infrastructure

There is a public school in Bakari Bata.[3]
