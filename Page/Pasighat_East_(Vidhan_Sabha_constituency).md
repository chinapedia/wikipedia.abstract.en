PASIGHAT EAST is a village in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh in East Siang.

Pasighat East is located in East Siang, district administrative headquarter is East Siang. It is surrounded by Bilat Tehsil towards South and Koyu Tehsil towards the West. Tinsukia, Dibrugarh, Silapathar are the nearby cities. It is one of the 60 constituencies of Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh.


Member of Legislative Assembly
