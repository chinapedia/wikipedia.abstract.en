The 2016 MAGYAR KUPA, known as () for sponsorship reasons, is the 90th edition of the tournament.


Schedule

The rounds of the 2016–17 competition are scheduled as follows:[1]

  Quarter-finals      7 November 2016, 12:00 CET   2, 4 December 2016
  Final four          5 December 2016, 12:00 CET   17–18 December 2016
