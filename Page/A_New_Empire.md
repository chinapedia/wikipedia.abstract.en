_A NEW EMPIRE_ is the fourth extended play by South Korean singer Ailee. It was released on October 5, 2016 by YMC Entertainment and distributed by LOEN Entertainment.[1]


Background and release

On August 23, 2016, the digital song "If You" was pre-released, along with a music video for it.[2]

On September 23, 2016, it was revealed that Ailee would make comeback in mid-October.[3] On September 28, 2016, the trailer was released for the music video of song "Home" and it was announced that the album was to be released on October 5, 2016.[4]

On October 5, 2016, the music video for "Home" and album were both released.[5]
