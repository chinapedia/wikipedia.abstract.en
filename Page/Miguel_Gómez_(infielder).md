MIGUEL GÓMEZ (born December 17, 1992) is a Dominican professional baseball infielder who is a free agent. He has played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the San Francisco Giants.


Career

San Francisco Giants

Gómez signed with the San Francisco Giants as an international free agent in September 2011. The Giants added him to their 40-man roster after the 2016 season.[1]

Gomez started 2017 with the Richmond Flying Squirrels and was called up on July 7 to the Giants. He made his debut the same day against the Miami Marlins as a pinch hitter, going hitless in his only at bat.[2] He picked up his first major league hit and RBI two days later. He elected free agency on November 2, 2018.


International career

Before the 2019 season, he was selected for Dominican Republic national baseball team at the 2019 Pan American Games Qualifier.[3]
