JOSÉ JESÚS ANTONIO AGUILAR JIMÉNEZ (born 9 October 1960),[1] known as ANTONIO AGUILAR, HIJO (ANTONIO AGUILAR, JR.), is a Mexican singer and actor. He is the eldest son of singers and actors Antonio Aguilar and Flor Silvestre.

He made his acting debut in his parents' film _La yegua colorada_ in 1972. His debut studio album, _Toda mi vida_ (1994),[2] was produced and directed by his brother, Pepe Aguilar, and released by EMI Latin.[3] "Por ti no voy a llorar", a track from the album, became his first hit single and peaked at number 20 on _Billboard_ Hot Latin Tracks in July 1994.[4]
