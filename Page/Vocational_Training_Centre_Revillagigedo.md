Fundacion_Revilla_Gigedo.jpg VOCATIONAL TRAINING CENTRE REVILLAGIGEDO is a work of the Jesuits in Gijon, Asturias, Spain.[1] Early through higher grade vocational training courses are offered following the regulations for vocational training in Spain.[2]
