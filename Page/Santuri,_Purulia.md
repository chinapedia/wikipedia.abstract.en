SANTURI is a village, with a police station, in Santuri CD Block in Raghunathpur subdivision of Purulia district in West Bengal, India.


Geography

Santuri is located at .


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Santuri had a total population of 2,700 of which 1,390 (51%) were males and 1,310 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 322. The total number of literates in Santuri was 1,576 (66.27% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Police station

Santuri police station has jurisdiction over Santuri CD Block. The area covered is 179.69 km² and the population covered is 78,461.[2][3]


Transport

State Highway 8 (West Bengal) running from Santaldih (in Purulia district) to Majhdia (in Nadia district) passes through Santuri.[4]


Healthcare

Santuri Primary Health Centre functions with 10 beds.[5]
