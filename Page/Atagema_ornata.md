_ATAGEMA ORNATA_ is a species of sea slug or dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Discodorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the Red Sea. It is considered to be synonymous with _Atagema intecta_ by some authors.[2]


Ecology

This dorid nudibranch feeds on sponges.
