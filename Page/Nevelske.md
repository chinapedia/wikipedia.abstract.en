The War in Donbass, that started in mid-April 2014, has brought along both civilian and military casualties. Two Ukrainian servicemen were killed and two others were wounded in action at Nevelske on 8 November 2014.[1] Two Ukrainian soldiers were wounded at Nevelske on 16 December 2016.[2]
