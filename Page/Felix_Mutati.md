FELIX C MUTATI (born 29 January 1959) is a Zambian politician representing the Movement for Multi-Party Democracy party.[1] . He did not run for a Member of Parliament seat and received a nominated seat by the president.[2]

Previously Mutati served as Minister of Works and Supply from February 2018 to November 2018, Minister of Energy and Water Development from 2002 to 2004 under Levy Mwanawasa and Minister of Commerce, Trade and Industry from 2004 to 2011 under Rupiah Banda,[3] and Minister of Finance from 2016 until 2018.
