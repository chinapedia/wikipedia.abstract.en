JAYARAM KOMATI (born 26 September 1956) is an Indian businessman politician. He is Telugu Desam Party's NRI wing leader and a former president and Executive Vice-president for Telugu Association of North America (TANA).[1] He is a Special Representative for North America by the Government of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. He served as President and Chairman for many Indian organisations in North America.
