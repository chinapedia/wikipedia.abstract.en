LIKOK-KOUBA is a village in the commune of Martap, in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon.


Population

In 1967, Likok-Kouba contained 110 inhabitants, mainly Niam-Niam.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 312 people in the village.[2]
