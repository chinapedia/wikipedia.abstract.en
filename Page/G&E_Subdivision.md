The G&E SUBDIVISION is a railroad line owned by CSX Transportation in the U.S. State of West Virginia. It was formerly part of the CSX HUNTINGTON EAST DIVISION.[1] It became part of the CSX Florence Division on June 20, 2016. The line runs from Charmco, West Virginia to Peaser, West Virginia for a total of 14.2 miles. At its east end it continues west from the Sewell Valley Subdivision and at its west end the main track ends.[2]
