IFEANYI CONVENANT OFOEGBU (born 5 January 2000) is a footballer.


Career

Ofoegbu joined Chesterfield from Huddersfield Town in 2016.[1] He made his senior debut at the age of 16 in a 4–1 defeat by Accrington Stanley in an EFL Trophy group stage match at the Proact Stadium on 4 October 2016.[2] In doing so he became the club's fifth youngest-ever player.[3] Ofoegbu was offered his first professional contract in May 2018, and was released a year later, having made 5 senior appearances for the club.[4]
