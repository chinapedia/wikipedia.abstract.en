KISHORILAL JUBILEE HIGH SCHOOL & COLLEGE () is a private school in Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh.[1] The school offers education for students ranging from kindergarten to twelfth grade. Zamindar founded the school in 1866.[2] He named the school after himself.


Notable alumni

-   Brojen Das, swimmer[3]
-   Meghnad Saha, astrophysicist[4]
