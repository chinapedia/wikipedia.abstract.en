SUSAN DEL PERCIO (born 19 November 1969) is an American political strategist and media and Republican political analyst.[1]

She holds a master's degree in political communication from Emerson College.[9]


Education and private life

Del Percio has a bachelor's degree in communications and a master's degree in political communication from Emerson College.[10] She lives in New York City.
