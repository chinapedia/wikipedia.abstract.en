PATRICK KAMMERBAUER (born 11 February 1997) is a German professional footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for SC Freiburg.[1]


Career

In January 2018, Kammerbauer joined SC Freiburg after rejecting 1. FC Nürnberg's offer to extend his contract which was due to expire in summer 2018. The transfer fee paid to Nürnberg was reported as in the region of €200,000 to 300,000.[2][3]

On 28 January 2019, Kammerbauer joined Holstein Kiel on a loan deal until summer 2020 with an option to make the deal permanent.[4][5]
