MICHAEL ANTHONY PLUMB (born 19 July 1954) is a retired rower from the United States.

Plumb was born in 1954 in Syracuse, New York, United States.[1] He is a member of Vesper Boat Club in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.[2] He represented the United States at the 1976 Summer Olympics in the coxed four event.[3]
