PUNJAB COUNCIL OF ARTS (PUCAR) is a provincial institute in that promotes art culture and Literature in Punjab Province Pakistan. Its Director is Chaudhary Muhammad Asif [1][2]


The Council of arts Act 1975

The Punjab Council of arts was Founded in 1975. The Council was founded for the purpose of the promotion, the patronage, and development of the arts and culture and for matters connected therewith in Punjab Pakistan.


Headquarters

The headquarters of the Council shall be at Lahore.
