ADILSON REIS DA ANUNCIACAO, (; born 27 September 1992) is a Brazilian footballer who plays for Cascavel as a winger.


Career

Hapoel Haifa

Adilson came to the training camp of Hapoel Haifa in Italy to audition in a group, in a friendly against Bologna scored a goal for Hapoel Haifa, who lost 2–1 at the end,[1] on 31 July 2016 conquered his debut in his first appearance as part of the Toto Cup in front of Bnei Sakhnin who which ended in a 2–2 draw, the game has signed Adilson season with an option for another season.[2] On 20 August 2016 premiere captured his first appearance on the first round of the Premier League against Hapoel Ashkelon after the game ends Hapoel Haifa lost 3–2.[3]
