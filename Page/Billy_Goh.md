BILLY GOH PIAK CHIANG served as the Assistant Chief Commissioner, Executive Director and International Commissioner of the Singapore Scout Association.[1]

In 1997, Goh was awarded the 257th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2]
