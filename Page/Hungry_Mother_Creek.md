HUNGRY MOTHER CREEK is a stream in Howard County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

The stream headwaters arise in the northeast corner of Howard County at a small lake just west of Missouri Route A about three miles south of Higbee. The stream flows south-southwest passing under Missouri Route O just west of Bunker Hill. It continues to the south-southwest passing under Missouri Route 124 and on to its confluence with Moniteau Creek west of Sebree.[2]
