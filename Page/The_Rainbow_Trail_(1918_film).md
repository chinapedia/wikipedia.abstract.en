_THE RAINBOW TRAIL_ is a lost[1] 1918 American silent western film directed by Frank Lloyd.[2]

The picture was an adaptation of Zane Grey's novel of the same name. It was a sequel to the 1918 film _Riders of the Purple Sage_, which also starred William Farnum as Lassiter. _The Rainbow Trail_ was remade, with sound, in 1931.


Cast

-   Ann Forrest as Fay Larkin
-   Mary Mersch as Jane Withersteen
-   William Burress as Waggoner
-   William Nigh as Shad (credited as William Nye)
-   Genevieve Blinn as Ruth
-   George Ross as U.S. Marshall
-   Buck Jones as Cowboy (credited as Buck Gebhart)
