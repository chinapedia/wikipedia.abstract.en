OLE KRISTIAN HAFNOR (15 April 1882 – 5 August 1962) was a Norwegian factory owner, newspaper editor and politician.

He was born in Modum to wheelmaker Christen Nilsen Hafnor and Anna Dorthea Jensdatter. From 1910 to 1922 he was running a lorry factory in Solum. He was chief editor of the newspaper _Telemark Arbeiderblad_ from 1926 to 1928. He was elected representative to the Storting for the period 1928–1930, for the Labour Party.[1] He died in 1962.[2]
