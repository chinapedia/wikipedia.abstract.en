CHAUDHARY MUHAMMAD TUFAIL () is a Pakistani politician who had been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan, from September 2016 to May 2018.


Political career

He has served as Tehsil Nazim of Chichawatni.[1]

He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Pakistan Muslim League (N) (PML-N) from Constituency NA-162 (Sahiwal-III) in by elections held in September 2016.[2][3][4] He received 76,580 votes and defeated Rai Murtaza Iqbal, a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI).[5] The seat was became vacant after Rai Hassan Nawaz who won it in 2013 election, was disqualified to continue in office because of inaccurate assets declaration.[6]

In October 2017, he was appointed as Federal Parliamentary Secretary for privatisation.[7]
