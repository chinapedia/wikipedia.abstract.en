CHARLES CHUKA (born 15 August 1991) is a Nigerian footballer who plays as a forward for Feni soccer club and the Nigeria national team. He is known for his speed on the ball. [1]


Career

Early career

Unoneme joined the local football heartland club , having been scouted by manager amonike while playing on the streets of his hometown. After a year with the club, he signed for Mohammedan,and in his first year season he scored 7 goals in league debut the following season, Chuka joined Feni soccer club .

Mohammedan

For the 2013–2014 season Chuka played for Mohammedan who were based in Dhaka. After his maiden season with Mohammedan, Chuka finished with 16 caps in the B and 9 goal [ Bangladeshs League] .

Feni

After one season at Mohammedan on 2014 it was announced that Chuka has signed with Feni soccer clubof the Bangladesh soccer league for the 2014–2015 season.
