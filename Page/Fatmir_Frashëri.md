FATMIR FRASHËRI (born in 3 April 1941) is an Albanian former football player who played for 17 Nëntori Tirana, Partizani and the Albania national team.


International career

He made his debut for Albania in a June 1963 European Championship qualification match away against Denmark and earned a total of 12 caps, scoring no goals. His final international was a October 1970 European Championship qualification match against Poland.[1]

Managerial career

Following his retirement in 1972 he became the head coach of 17 Nëntori Tirana, Naftëtari Qyteti Stalin, Besa Kavajë and Dinamo Tirana.[3]
