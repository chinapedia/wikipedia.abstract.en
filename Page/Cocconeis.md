_COCCONEIS_ is a genus of diatoms. Members of the genus are elliptically shaped diatoms.

The green alga _Cladophora_ is frequently covered with _Cocconeis_, as are individuals of Antarctic minke whales, often found with orange-brown to yellowish patches of _Cocconeis ceticola_ on their bodies.
