MOUTUSHI BISWAS is a Bangladeshi model and actress.[1] She made her television debut in the program _Adventure Bangladesh_, aired in Ekushey Television Channel.[2]


Career

Biswas hosts the ATN Bangla television program _"Shera Rondhon Shilpi 2017"_.[3]
