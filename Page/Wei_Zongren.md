WEI ZONGREN (; ; born 8 August 1998) is a Chinese footballer who plays for Guangzhou R&F.


Club career

Wei Zongren started his professional football career in August 2016 when he was loaned to Hong Kong Premier League side R&F, which was the satellite team of Guangzhou R&F.[1] He made his senior debut on 18 September 2016 in the 2016–17 Hong Kong Senior Challenge Shield against BC Glory Sky, coming on as a substitute for Lau Tak Yan in the 57th minute.[2] His league debut came on 24 September 2016 in a 2–0 away defeat against BC Glory Sky.[3]
