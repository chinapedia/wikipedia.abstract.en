The KNYSNA VELVET WORM (_Peripatopsis clavigera_) is a species of velvet worm in the Peripatopsidae family.[1]

_Peripatopsis clavigera_ is found in subtropical or tropical moist montane forests of South Africa where it is threatened by habitat loss. Consequently, this species is listed as Vulnerable on the IUCN Red List.[2][3]
