OZERYANIVKA (; ) is a settlement in Horlivka municipality of Donetsk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, at 41.8 km NNE from the centre of Donetsk city.

The settlement was taken under control of pro-Russian forces During the War in Donbass, that started in 2014. Raions_of_Horlivka.png:
