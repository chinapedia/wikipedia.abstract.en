VÁCLAV BECVÁR (17 September 1908 – 1978) was a Czechoslovak male weightlifter, who competed in the heavyweight class and represented Czechoslovakia at international competitions. He won the bronze medal at the 1947 World Weightlifting Championships in the +82.5 kg category.[1]
