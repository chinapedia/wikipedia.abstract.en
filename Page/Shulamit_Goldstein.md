SHULAMIT GOLDSTEIN (שולמית גולדשטיין; born May 4, 1968) is an Israeli former Olympic rhythmic gymnast.[1]


Rhythmic Gymnastics career

Her club was Hapoel Azur.[2] She was the Israeli national Rhythmic Gymnastics Champion in 1985-87.[3] At the 1985 World Rhythmic Gymnastics Championships, she came in 49th.[4][5]

She competed for Israel at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea, at the age of 20 in Rhythmic Gymnastics.[6] In Women's Individual All-Around she came in tied for 35th.[7] She came in 37th in Hoop, tied for 26th in Rope, 37th in Clubs, and tied for 34th in Ribbon.[8] When she competed in the Olympics, she was 5-5.5 (167 cm) tall and weighed 112 lbs (51 kg).[9]

After her days competing, she became a commentator on the sport.[10]
