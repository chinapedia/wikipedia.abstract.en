'''Wi Jong-sim ''' (born 13 October 1997) is a North Korean footballer who plays as a forward for the North Korea women's national football team and North Korea women's national under-20 football team.[1] She was part of the team at the 2014 Asian Games and 2015 EAFF Women's East Asian Cup.[2] At the club level, she played for Kalmaegi in North Korea.
