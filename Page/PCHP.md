1-(1-PHENYLCYCLOHEXYL)-4-HYDROXYPIPERIDINE (PCHP) is a metabolite of phencyclidine (PCP).[1] PCHP can be detected in the hair, urine, stool, sweat, and saliva of PCP users.[2]
