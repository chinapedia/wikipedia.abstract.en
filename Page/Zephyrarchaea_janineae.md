_ZEPHYRARCHAEA JANINEAE_ is a species of spider of the family Archaeidae.[1] The Latin species name was chosen to honor Janine Wojcieszek who helped in discovering the first live specimens of the species in 2006.[2] _Zephyrarchaea janineae_ is endemic to the South West Region in Western Australia.[3]
