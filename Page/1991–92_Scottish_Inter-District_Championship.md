The 1991–92 rugby union Scottish Inter-District Championship was a curtailed championship.[1]

Unusually 6 teams competed in this year's tournament. Glasgow District, Edinburgh District, South, North and Midlands and the Anglo Scots were joined by a SRU Presidents XV side.[2]

Each team played only 2 matches. No winner was declared.
