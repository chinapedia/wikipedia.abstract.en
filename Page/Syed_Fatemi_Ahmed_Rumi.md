SYED FATEMI AHMED RUMI is a Bangladesh Army officer and former Director General of Special Security Force.[1][2]


Career

He served as the general officer commanding of Rangpur Cantonment.[3] In 2009 he was made the chairman of Bangladesh Institute of International and Strategic Studies a state operated think tank.[4]
