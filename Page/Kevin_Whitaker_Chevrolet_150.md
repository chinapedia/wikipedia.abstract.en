The KEVIN WHITAKER CHEVROLET 150 [1] is a NASCAR K&N Pro Series East race held annually from 2006 to 2017 at Greenville-Pickens Speedway. First held in 2006, it was a race.


History

The speedway had two races held at the track from 2011-2014 and 2016.
