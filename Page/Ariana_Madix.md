ARIANA MADIX (born June 24, 1985), is an American television personality, model, and actor. She currently stars on the Bravo reality television series _Vanderpump Rules_.


Early Life

Ariana was raised in Melbourne, Florida by her father Jim and mother Tanya. Her brother Jeremy was born in 1992. She attended Eau Gallie High School (Class of 2003) and Flagler College (Class of 2007). She studied Theatre Arts at Flagler, learning important lessons in stage combat and historical film costuming. While in college, she worked at Walt Disney World as costumed characters[1] such Chip, Dale, Ariel[2], and Cinderella.


Career

After graduating college, Madix moved to New York City. She was frequently cast in videos for CollegeHumor[3].

In 2010, Madix moved to Los Angeles. Destiny led her to Lisa Vanderpump's Beverly Hills restaurant, Villa Blanca. It was there she met her future Vanderpump Rules castmates (and boyfriend, Tom Sandoval).
