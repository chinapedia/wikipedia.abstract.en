THERRY AQUINO SORIANO (born 26 July 1996) is a Dominican Republic male badminton player.[1][2] In 2016, he was selected as the Dominican Republic national badminton team.[3] In August 2016, he became the runner-up of the Carebaco International tournament in the men's doubles event partnered with Reimi Starling Cabrera Rosario.[4] He and Cabrera, also the semi-finalist at the 2016 Santo Domingo Open tournament.[5] Together with the Dominican Republic badminton team, he won the mixed team gold medal at the 2016 Caribbean Badminton Championships.[6]
