PEDRO JESÚS AQUINO SÁNCHEZ (born 13 April 1995) is a professional Peruvian football midfielder currently playing for León on loan from Monterrey.


Club career

Sporting Cristal

He made his senior league debut for Sporting Cristal on 2 June 2013 in a Peruvian Primera División 0–2 away loss at Cienciano.[1]

Lobos BUAP

On 22 July 2017 Aquino made his Liga MX debut against Santos Laguna playing 76 minutes in the 2-2 draw.[2]


International career

He made his debut for the senior national team on 1 September 2016 in a 0–2 loss to Bolivia in the 2018 World Cup qualifying round.[3]

In May 2018 he was named in Peru’s provisional 24 man squad for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia.[4]
