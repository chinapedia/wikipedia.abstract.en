OLIVER JANSO (born 8 October 1993) is a Slovak footballer who plays for Ústí nad Labem as a left back.


Club career

Spartak Trnava

Janso made his professional league debut for Spartak Trnava against Senica on 24 September 2016.[1]


Honours

SPARTAK TRNAVA

-   Slovak Super Liga: 2017–18
