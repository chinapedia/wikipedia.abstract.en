The EURASIA BULGARIA INTERNATIONAL is an international badminton tournament held in Bulgaria since 2012. This tournament also known as Bulgarian Hebar Open or Bulgarian Eurasia Open.[1] The 2014 tournament used the best of five games 11 points scoring system.[2] The 2016 tournament has been an BWF International Series rated event with total prize money US$ 6.000.[3] Another badminton tournament in Bulgaria sponsored by Babolat is Bulgarian International.
