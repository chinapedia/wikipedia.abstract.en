YURI VSEVOLODOVICH DOYKOV () (born 1955) is a Russian historian.


Biography

Yuri Doykov was born on July 20, 1955 in Arkhangelsk, at that time in the USSR. In 1988 he took a Ph.D. in Science in Russian History at Leningrad University. Since 1991, he has worked as an independent scholar in Arkhangelsk, specializing in Russia, Ukraine and Eurasia. He publishes a newsletter, _Yuri Doykov's Daily_, which started in 2011.
