BROADWAY THEATER, also known as the NEW BROADWAY THEATRE, FOX BROADWAY, KERASOTES BROADWAY THEATRE, is a historic vaudeville house and movie theater located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It built in 1921, and is a two-story, red brick building with a white, glazed brick fa√ßade. The building consists of the theatre/storefront zone on the first level and a second zone with office space on the second level.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2015.[2] It is located in the Broadway Commercial Historic District.
