SERGEI YURYEVICH PODOKSYONOV (; born 29 July 1997) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut for FC Ural Yekaterinburg on 21 September 2016 in a Russian Cup game against FC Chelyabinsk.[1]

He made his Russian Premier League debut for FC Ural Yekaterinburg on 25 September 2016 against FC Orenburg.[2]
