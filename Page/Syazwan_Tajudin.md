MUHAMMAD SYAZWAN BIN TAJUDIN (born 7 January 1994 in Kedah) is a Malaysian footballer who mainly operates as defender but can also play as a midfielder for Kedah.

Owing to injury sustained in a match against Johor Darul Ta'zim Syazwan unable to play the remainder of the 2016 Malaysia Super League from April 30.[1]
