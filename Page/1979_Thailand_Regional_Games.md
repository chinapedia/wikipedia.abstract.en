THE 13TH THAILAND NATIONAL GAMES (Thai:กีฬาเขตแห่งประเทศไทย ครั้งที่ 13) also known (1979 National Games,1979 Inter-Provincial Games) held in Lampang, Thailand during 23 to 29 December 1979, Representing were 14 sports and 10 regions. This games was the qualification Thai athletes of 1978 Asian Games.[1]Thailand National Games' history


Emblem

The emblem of 1979 Thailand National Games was the brown circle, on the inside was the emblem of Sports Authority of Thailand or SAT and surrounded by the text

The 13th Thailand National Games represented 10 regions from 72 provinces. The country that made their Thailand National Games debut was Phayao, formerly part of Chiang Rai, .
