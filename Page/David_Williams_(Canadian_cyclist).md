DAVID SYLVESTRE-WILLIAMS (born 1 October 1994) is a Canadian-Trinidadian racing cyclist. Williams turned professional for the UCI Continental, Al Marakeb Cycling Team on 23 October 2016.[1] He is the great-great-grandson of human rights activist Henry Sylvester-Williams.
