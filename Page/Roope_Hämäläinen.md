ROOPE HÄMÄLÄINEN (born August 18, 1992) is a Finnish ice hockey player. He is currently playing with Jokipojat of the Finnish Mestis.[1]

Hämäläinen made his Liiga debut playing with SaiPa during the 2009–10 SM-liiga season. [2]
