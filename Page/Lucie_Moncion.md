LUCIE MONCION (born October 25, 1958) is a Canadian banker, who was appointed to the Senate of Canada on October 30, 2016.[1] She sits in the Senate as an independent, representing the province of Ontario.[2]

Prior to her appointment, Moncion was president and chief executive officer of the Alliance des caisses populaires de l’Ontario, a network of 12 _caisses populaires_ serving Northern Ontario, and served on the boards of Nipissing University, Collège Boréal and Direction Ontario.[3]
