NAZAR PENKOVETS (; born 2 September 1987 in Kiev, Ukrainian SSR) is a professional Ukrainian football goalkeeper who plays for Wisła Puławy in the Polish I liga.

Penkovets is a product of the FC Obolon-Zmina Youth Sportive School System. He played for the Ukrainian and Polish amateur or lower level clubs.[1]
