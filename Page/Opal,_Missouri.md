OPAL is an unincorporated community in Lawrence County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

Opal is located on Missouri Route 39-Missouri Route 265 about midway between Mount Vernon to the northwest and Aurora to the southeast.[2]

The community most likely has the first name of a local woman.[3]
