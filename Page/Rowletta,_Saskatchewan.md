ROWLETTA is a locality[1] in the Rural Municipality of Marquis No. 191, Saskatchewan, Canada. It is located about 5 km west of Highway 643 on Range Road 35, approximately 10 km northwest of the Village of Caronport on the former Grand Trunk Pacific/(Canadian National Railways) Central Butte Subdivision. Rowletta Civic Centre[2] is located to the south of Rowletta at the intersection of Township Road 190 and Range Road 292.
