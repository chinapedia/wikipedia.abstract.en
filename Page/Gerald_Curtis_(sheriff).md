GERALD COLVILLE SEYMOUR CURTIS, OBE was High Sheriff of Essex[1] from 1973 to 1974.[2]

The son of Sir George Curtis, [3] he was an administrator in the Indian Civil Service.[4] In 1935 he married Decima Pryor[5] at Bishops Stortford.[6]
