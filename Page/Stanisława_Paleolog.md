STANISŁAWA FILIPINA PALEOLOG (4 May 1892 in Rumno, Galicia - 3 December 1968 in Great Britain) was a Polish official, military and political activist. She participated in warfare within the Polish army, served as commissionaire within the Polish State Police, officer within the Blue Police, Captain within the Union of Armed Struggle and cabinet minister in the first government of Antoni Pająk.
