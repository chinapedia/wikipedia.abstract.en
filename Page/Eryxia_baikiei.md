_ERYXIA BAIKIEI_ is a species of leaf beetle of Mali[1] and Senegal,[2]. It was first described by Joseph Sugar Baly in 1865, based on a specimen of the species sent to him by the late Dr. Baikie, who had collected it from "the banks of the Niger".[3]
