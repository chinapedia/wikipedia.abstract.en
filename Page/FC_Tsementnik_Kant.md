FC TSEMENTNIK KANT is a Kyrgyzstani football club based in Kant, Kyrgyzstan.


History

-   1992: Founded as _FC Tsementnik Kant_.
-   2001: Renamed to _FC KTsShK Kant_.
-   2002: Renamed to _FC Tsementnik Kant_.

FC Tsementnik Kant started in the 1995 season.


Achievements

-   KYRGYZSTAN LEAGUE:
-   8th place: 1995

KYRGYZSTAN CUP:

-   1/8 finals: 1992, 2002
