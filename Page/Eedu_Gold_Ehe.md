_EEDU GOLD EHE_ is a Telugu comedy film directed by Veeru Potla starring Sunil and Sushma Raj Richa Panai in lead roles. This movie is produced by Ramabrahmam Sunkara on AK Entertainments Banner while Sagar Mahati scored the Music. The movie is based on Jewel Thief.


Plot

Bangarraju (Sunil) is an innocent & good-hearted person he leaves for Hyderabad in search of job,Meeting an elderly woman who helps him get a job,he lands in trouble from a lookalike Sunil Varma (Sunil) who robs a Diamond-studded idol from a mafia don's house,are they both twins or is sunil varma actually bangarraju ? this is the basic story.
