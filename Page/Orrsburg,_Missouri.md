ORRSBURG is an unincorporated community in Nodaway County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Orrsburg was laid out in 1881, and named after an early settler.[2] A post office called Orrsburg was established in 1881, and remained in operation until 1902.[3]
