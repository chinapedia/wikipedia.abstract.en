FABIAN SCHLEUSENER (born 24 October 1991) is a German professional footballer who plays as a forward for 1. FC Nürnberg.[1]


Career

In July 2019, Schleusener joined 1. FC Nürnberg on a three-year contract. The transfer fee paid to from SC Freiburg was reported as about €450,000.[2]
