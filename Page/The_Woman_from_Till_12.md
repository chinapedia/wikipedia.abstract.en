_THE WOMAN FROM TILL 12_ (German: _DAS FRÄULEIN VON KASSE 12_) is a 1928 German silent film directed by Erich Schönfelder and starring Werner Fuetterer and Dina Gralla.[1] Much of the film's action takes place in a department store.

The film's sets were designed by Kurt Richter. It was released as part of the Parufamet agreement between UFA and the major Hollywood companies.
