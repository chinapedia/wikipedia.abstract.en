This is a LIST OF PEOPLE BORN AT ESPERANZA BASE, Antarctica. As of 2010, the following people were named by the Argentine Army as having been born at Esperanza Base.[1]

-   Emilio Marcos Palma (1978), often called the first person born in Antarctica
-   Marisa de las Nieves Delgado (1978)
-   Rubén Eduardo de Carli (1979)
-   Francisco Javier Sosa (1979)
-   Silvia Analía Arnouil (1980)
-   José Manuel Valladares Solís (1980)
-   Lucas Daniel Posse (1980)
-   María Sol Cosenza (1983)
