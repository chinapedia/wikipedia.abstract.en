BUTTON ISLANDS are a group of islands of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the South Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1]


History

Several lighthouses were established on the islands.
There is a lighthouse at the tallest hill on North button island, established 1983. [2]
There is a lighthouse at the tallest hill on Middle button island, established 1983. [3]
There is a lighthouse at the tallest hill on south button island, established 1983. [4]

Administration

Politically, Button Islands are part of Port Blair Taluk.[6] Rani Jhansi Marine National Park is located in the vicinity of the islands.
