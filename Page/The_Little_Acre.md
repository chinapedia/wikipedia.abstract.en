_THE LITTLE ACRE_ is an adventure game developed by Pewter Games Studios with Charles Cecil and published by Curve Digital.[1][2][3][4] It was released on PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Steam on December 13, 2016.[5][6][7][8] It was originally planned to be released November 22, 2016.[9]


Plot

Set in 1950’s Ireland,[10][11] The Little Acre tells the story of a small family. The player controls Aidan a job-hunting engineer and his daughter Lily. One morning Aidan goes missing and Lily decides to go and find him. During her search, she discovers an entrance-way to a mysterious world in the family’s garden shed.[12][13][14][15]
