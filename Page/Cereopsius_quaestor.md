_CEREOPSIUS QUAESTOR_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Newman in 1842, originally under the genus _Monohammus_. It is known from the Philippines and Moluccas.[1]


Varietas

-   _Cereopsius quaestor var. confluens_ Breuning, 1944
-   _Cereopsius quaestor var. luctuosus_ Pascoe, 1866
-   _Cereopsius quaestor var. nigrobasalis_ Kriesche, 1928
-   _Cereopsius quaestor var. tricinctus_ Pascoe, 1866
