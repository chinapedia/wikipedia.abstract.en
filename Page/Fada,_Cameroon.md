Fada}}

FADA (also Fata) is a village in the commune of Meiganga in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, near the border with the Central African Republic.


Population

In 1967, Fada contained 1267 inhabitants, mostly Wodaabe and Fula people[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 1048} people in the village.[2]
