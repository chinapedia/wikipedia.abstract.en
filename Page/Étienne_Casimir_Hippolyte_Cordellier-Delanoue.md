ÉTIENNE CASIMIR HIPPOLYTE CORDELLIER-DELANOUE (19 September 1806, Grenoble – 14 November 1854, Paris) was a 19th-century French dramatist, novelist and poet.[1]


Works

Theatre

-   1831: _Kernox le fou_, drama in four acts and in verse, Théâtre de l'Odéon, 17 May
-   1832; _Le Barbier de Louis XI, 1439-1483_ Read online
-   1834: _Marguerite de Montmorency_ in _Le Livre de beauté : souvenirs historiques_, preface by Charles Nodier
-   1835: _Cromwell et Charles Ier_, drama in 5 acts, preceded by _Un dernier jour de popularité_, prologue in 1 act, Théâtre de la Porte-Saint-Martin, 21 May
-   1839: _Isabelle de Montréal_, drama in 2 acts, mingled with singing, with Paul Foucher, Paris, Théâtre de la Gaîté, 10 June
