The POPATLAL DHANJINBHAI MALAVIYA COLLEGE OF COMMERCE or PDMCC is a college affiliated with Saurashtra University. PDMCC is in Rajkot, Gujarat, India.[1]


Campus

PDMCC_Campus_front.jpg The main campus is located on Gondal Road in south Rajkot. It has facilities such as a gym, sports ground, NCC, and NSS.

-   Management
