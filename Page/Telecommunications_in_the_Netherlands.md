Communications in the Netherlands.


Mail

The postal service in the Netherlands is performed by PostNL in most cases—which has, as of 2008, a monopoly on letters lighter than 50 g.[1] The monopoly is planned to expire in 2009.[2] PostNL's competitors include Selekt Mail and Sandd.[3][4] Post offices that are owned by Postbank and TNT Post have been earmarked for closure between 2008 and 2013.[5][6]

Postal codes in the Netherlands are formed of four digits then two letters (in capitals), separated by a space—1234 AB, for example.
