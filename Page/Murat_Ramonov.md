MURAT RAMONOV (born July 21, 1990) is a Kyrgyzstani Greco-Roman wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 130 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated in the round of 32 by Sergey Semenov.[1][2]
