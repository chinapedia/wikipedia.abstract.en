'''Siti Noor Halimi ''' (born 22 February 1984) is a Malaysian women's international futsal player who plays as a defender. She is a member of the Malaysia women's national futsal team. She won the bronze medal at the 2013 Southeast Asian Games where she scored one goal[1] and was part of the team at the 2015 AFC Women's Futsal Championship.[2][3] On club level she played for FELDA United FC in Kuala Lumpur.
