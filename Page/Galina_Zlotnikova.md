GALINA BORISOVNA ZLOTNIKOVA (Галина Борисовна Злотникова, born 1984) was a Russian female water polo player.

She was a member of the Russia women's national water polo team, playing as a goalkeeper. She was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level she played for Kinef Kirishi in Russia.
