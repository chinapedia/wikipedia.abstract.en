_KANHA_ (Marathi: "कान्हा") is a 2016 Marathi language action drama film. film is directed by Avdhoot Gupte.[1] It stars Gashmeer Mahajani and Vaibbhav Tatwawdi in lead roles.[2] Movie had its theatrical release in India on 26 August 2016.[3]


Synopsis

Kanha is based on the festival of Dahi Handi, which is very popular in Maharashtra. The film is about this festival, the politics involved in it and the way it has become an integral part in the life of the Marathi Manoos.[4]

Release

_Kanha_ was released on 26 August 2016 with English subtitles in Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa, Madhya Pradesh, Delhi, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.[5]
