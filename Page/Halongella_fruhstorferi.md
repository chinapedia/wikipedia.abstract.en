_HALONGELLA FRUHSTORFERI_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Plectopylidae.


Distribution

The distribution of _Halongella fruhstorferi_ includes only Kebao Island/Vân Đồn Island in Hạ Long Bay area in Quảng Ninh Province, Vietnam.[1]


Ecology

It is a ground-dwelling species as all other plectopylid snails in Vietnam.[2]
