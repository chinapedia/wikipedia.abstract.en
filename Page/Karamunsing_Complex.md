KARAMUNSING COMPLEX is a shopping centre located in the city of Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia. It is one of the earliest shopping mall in the city, together with Centre Point and Wisma Merdeka.[1] The complex is mainly known for selling IT products and services.[2]
