KRYAN JOHNSON (born 23 March 1994) is a rugby league footballer who plays for Oldham in Betfred League 1. He plays as a winger.[1]

Johnson started playing rugby with Lock Lane when he was seven. He joined Featherstone on a junior scholarship when he was 13 but also played for Castleford Panthers until he was 16 when he signed exclusively with Featherstone.[2] He also spent time on loan at the Hunslet Hawks in Kingstone Press League 1.[3] In October 2017 he joined Oldham on a one-year deal.[4]
