_CALLOPANCHAX OCCIDENTALIS_ is a species of fish in the Aplocheilidae family. It is endemic to Sierra Leone and western Liberia.


Ecology

Callopanchax_occidentalis_Teme_Yella_SL89_DRCH,_Female.png The species is an annual killifish whose eggs survive in the mud during the dry season.[1] It inhabits pools, swampy sections of streams and temporary swamps in rainforest and forested savannah, feeding on small invertebrates.[2]
