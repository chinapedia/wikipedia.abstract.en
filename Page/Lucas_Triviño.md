LUCAS EXEQUIEL TRIVIÑO RODRIGUEZ (born April 24, 1992 in La Plata, Argentina) is an Argentine footballer currently playing for Ñublense of the Primera División B in Chile.


Teams

-   Ñublense 2010-2011

-   Trasandino 2012-2013

-   Ñublense 2013-2014

-   San Antonio Unido 2014-2015

-   Ñublense 2015

-   San Antonio Unido 2015-2016

-   Ñublense 2016–present
