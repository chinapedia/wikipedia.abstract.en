SPRING CREEK is a stream in northwestern Newton County, Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Shoal Creek.

The headwaters are located west of Spring City at . The stream flows northeast passing under and running along Missouri Route 86. The stream confluence is at Redings Mill at .[2][3][4][5]

Spring Creek was so named for the fact it is fed by a spring in its headwaters.[6]
