CHRISTER JOHNSGÅRD (born 17 February 1987) is a Norwegian footballer who plays as a striker for Tromsdalen.

Johnsgård was born in Silsand.[1] After one season in Tromsø IL he joined Tromsdalen UIL ahead of the 2017 season.[2] Later that same season he moved on to fourth-tier club FK Senja.

He has also had a career in television, presenting the children's show _Kråkeklubben_ on NRK Super.[3]
