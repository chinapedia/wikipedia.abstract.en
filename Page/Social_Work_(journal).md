_SOCIAL WORK_ is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal in the field of social work. It was established in 1920 as _THE COMPASS_ and was renamed _SOCIAL WORK JOURNAL_ in 1948. It obtained its current name in 1956.[1][2] It is published by Oxford University Press on behalf of the National Association of Social Workers, of which it is the official journal. The editor-in-chief is Tricia Bent-Goodley (Howard University). According to the _Journal Citation Reports_, the journal has a 2015 impact factor of 1.145.[3]
