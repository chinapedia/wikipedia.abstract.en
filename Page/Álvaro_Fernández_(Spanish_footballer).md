ÁLVARO FERNÁNDEZ LLORENTE (born 13 April 1998) is a Spanish footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for SD Huesca.


Club career

Born in Arnedo, La Rioja, Fernández represented CA Osasuna as a youth. He made his senior debut with the reserves on 12 April 2015, starting in a 1–1 away draw against CD Izarra for the Tercera División championship.

On 25 September 2016 Fernández made his La Liga debut, coming on as a second half substitute for injured Mario Fernández in a 1–3 loss at Villarreal CF.[1][2] It was his maiden appearance for the first team, which suffered relegation as dead last.

On 11 July 2017, Fernández signed a three-year deal with Ligue 1 side AS Monaco FC.[3] After only appearing with the reserve team, he returned to Spain on 7 August 2018, after agreeing to a one-year loan deal with Extremadura UD.[4]

On 29 June 2019, Fernández agreed to a three-year contract with SD Huesca, freshly relegated to the second division.[5]
