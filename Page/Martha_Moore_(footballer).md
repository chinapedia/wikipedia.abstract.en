MARTHA OFELIA MOORE CAMACHO (born 14 April 1981) is a Mexican retired international footballer who played as a defender. She was a member of the Mexico women's national football team.

Born in the United States, Moore qualified to represent Mexico internationally through her mother.[1] She was part of the team at the 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup and the 2002 CONCACAF Women's Gold Cup.[2][3]
