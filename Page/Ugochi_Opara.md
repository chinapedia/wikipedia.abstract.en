'''Ugochi Opara ''' (born 27 May 1976) is a Nigerian footballer who played as a goalkeeper for the Nigeria women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1995 FIFA Women's World Cup and 2003 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1]
