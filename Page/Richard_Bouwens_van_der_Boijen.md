RICHARD HERMANN ANTOINE BOUWENS VAN DER BOIJEN (11 October 1863 – 31 August 1939) was a French architect. In 1899, he was one of the winners of the Concours de façades de la ville de Paris for the realization of an Hôtel particulier, 8, rue de Lota in the 16th arrondissement of Paris.

He is buried at Père-Lachaise Cemetery (36th division).
