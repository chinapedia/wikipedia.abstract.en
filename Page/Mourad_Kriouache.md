MOURAD KRIOUACHE (born 10 March 1991) is a French rugby league footballer who last played for Toulouse Olympique in the Betfred Championship. He plays as a scrum-half or .[1]

Kriouache is a French international.[2] He has also represented France at junior level.[3]

Kriouache has previously played for the London Skolars.[4][5] He has also played for Carcassonne XIII and the Toulouse Olympique Broncos in the French competition.[6]

His brother Hosni Kriouache is also a professional rugby league footballer.[7]
