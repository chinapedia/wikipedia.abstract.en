THOMAS NØRGAARD (born 7 January 1987) is a retired Danish professional footballer.[1] He is currently the goalkeeper coach of Silkeborg IF.


Coaching career

After retiring at the end of the 2018/19 season, Nørgaard became goalkeeper coach of Silkeborg IF.[2]
