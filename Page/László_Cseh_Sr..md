LÁSZLÓ CSEH SR. (born 14 March 1952) is a Hungarian former swimmer. He competed at the 1968 Summer Olympics and the 1972 Summer Olympics.[1] His son, László Cseh, also represented Hungary at the Olympics in swimming.
