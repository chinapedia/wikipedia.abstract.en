During the decade 2010–2019, the Nigeria national football team has played in several international tournaments and many friendly matches. In the FIFA World Cup, Nigeria placed bottom in Group B in 2010; reached the Round of 16 in 2014; and placed third in Group D in 2018. The team entered the Africa Cup of Nations in 2010 (third place) and 2013 (winners), and has qualified for the 2019 tournament. Nigeria gained third place in the 2014 African Nations Championship.
