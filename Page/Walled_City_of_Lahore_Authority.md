The WALLED CITY OF LAHORE AUTHORITY () is a semi-government organisation in Lahore, Pakistan, established and funded by the Government of Punjab for conservation, planning and development, regulation and management of the Walled City of Lahore.[1][2]

The organisation working autonomously and was established in 2012 after Provincial Assembly of the Punjab amended the Walled City of Lahore Bill 2011 to create the authority. It look after the heritage sites in the area and specifying penalties for damaging buildings or setting up encroachments as well run the functions of the Old City of Lahore.[3] The authority also help promote cultural activities and tourism in Lahore.[4]
