MONNUJAN SUFIAN is a Bangladesh Awami League politician who is the current Jatiya Sangsad member from Khulna-3 constituency and the current State Minister of Labour and Employment.[1][2][3][4]


Background

Sufian completed her bachelor's from Khulna Girls' College in 1969. She was married to Abu Sufian. She is the daughter of Raja Shah Surjo Rajon and the eldest among her siblings.[5]


Career

Sufian served as the president of Khulna Girls' College unit of Bangladesh Chhatra League in 1968.[6]
