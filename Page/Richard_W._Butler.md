RICHARD W. BUTLER is an aviator and officer in the United States Navy.[1][2]

He was the commandant of Joint Task Force Guantanamo from July 2013 to July 2014.[3]

Butler went on record as supporting President Barack Obama's plan to close the detention camp.[4]
