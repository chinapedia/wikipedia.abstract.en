COTTONWOOD POINT is a census-designated place in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Cottonwood Point was established in 1853, and remained in operation until 1912.[2] The community was named for a grove of cottonwood trees near the original town site.[3] It is also the home of the Historic Taylor Cemetery and the Taylor Cemetery Restoration Project started in 2017.[4]
