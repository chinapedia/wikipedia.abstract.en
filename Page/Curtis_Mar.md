CURTIS MAR (born 1967) is a Fijian international lawn bowler and team manager.[1]

In 2008 he won the silver medal in the triples at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch along with Keshwa Goundar and Samuela Tuikiligana.[2]

In 2016 he was the team manager during the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship.
