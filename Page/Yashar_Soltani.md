YASHAR SOLTANI () is an Iranian journalist who is managing-director and editor-in-chief of _Memari News_ website.


Arrest

In September 2016, following publishing a set of declassified reports involving General Inspection Office on the controversial transfer of properties held by the Tehran Municipality to a number of people, he was arrested.[1][2] He was released in November.[3]


Politics

Soltani stood as a candidate for the City Council of Tehran in the Iranian local elections, 2017.[4] He was placed 43rd with 256,424 votes, unable to win a seat.[5]
