SERDAR DELIKTAŞ (born 4 August 1986 in Elazığ) is a Turkish football forward who currently plays for Karabükspor.[1]


Career

On 28 June 2016, Deliktaş joined Kardemir Karabükspor on a two-year contract.[2]
