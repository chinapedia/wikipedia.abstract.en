"THROWING STONES" is a song by the Grateful Dead. It appears on their 1987 album _In the Dark_.[1] It was also released as a single, with a B-side of "When Push Comes to Shove".[2]

The first live performance of "Throwing Stones" was on September 17, 1982 at the Cumberland County Civic Center in Portland, Maine.[3]
