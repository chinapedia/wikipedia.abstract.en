ANDRIY LEMISHEVSKY (born October 1, 1987) is a Ukrainian footballer.


Playing career

Lemishevsky began his career in 2006 with Iskra-Skirts in the Ukrainian Football Amateur League. In 2007, he signed with FC Podillya Khmelnytskyi of the Ukrainian Second League, where he featured in a total of 28 matches.[1] In 2008, he transferred to FC Dynamo Khmelnytskyi, and played in 23 matches.[2] Lemishevsky had stints with FC Yednist' Plysky, FC Dynamo Khmelnytskyi, and FC Ahrobiznes Volochysk.[3] In 2016, he went overseas to Canada to sign with FC Ukraine United of the Canadian Soccer League.[4] The following season he played with FC Vorkuta.[5]
