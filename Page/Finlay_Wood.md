FINLAY WOOD is a Welsh football midfielder.


Career

Wood is a product of the Newport County academy. He made his senior debut for Newport in a 1–0 defeat to Yeovil Town in League Two on 15 October 2016 as a second-half substitute.[1] Wood made his first appearance in the Newport starting line-up on 21 December 2016 in the FA Cup 2nd round replay versus Plymouth Argyle.[2]

On 9 May 2017, Wood was released by Newport at the end of the 2016–17 season.[3]
