DESMOND AKAWOR is a diplomat and politician who was Ambassador of Nigeria to South Korea from 24 April 2008 to 6 August 2015.[1] He was appointed Administrator of the Greater Port Harcourt City Development Authority, a position he has held since 12 June 2015. He was the Director-General of the Wike Gubernatorial Campaign Organization.[2][3]
