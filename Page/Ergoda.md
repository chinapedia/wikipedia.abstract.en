ERGODA is a village and gram panchayat in Binpur II CD Block in Jhargram subdivision of Jhargram district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Ergoda had a total population of 1,408 of which 728 (52%) were males and 680 (48%) were females. Population below 6 years was 140. The total number of literates in Ergoda was 1,102 (71.16% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Economy

United Bank of India has a branch at Ergoda.[2]


Education

Ergoda Nityananda Vidyayatan is a co-educational school.[3]
