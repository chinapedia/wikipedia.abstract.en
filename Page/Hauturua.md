_HAUTURUA_ is an extinct genus of sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Distribution

These extinct species were found in Miocene strata of New Zealand, age range: 12.7 to 11.608 Ma.


Species

Species within the genus _Hauturua_ include:

-   † _Hauturua bijuga_ (Marwick, 1931)
-   † _Hauturua exiguescens_ (Marwick, 1931)
-   † _Hauturua laevella_ (Marwick, 1931)
-   † _Hauturua vellai_ (Beu, 1970)
