LOUISA WARWICK (born 25 September 1992) [1] is an English fashion model & actress.[2]


Early life

She was born in Bristol, UK. She is studying Sports Management at New York University. In 2011 & 2012, She represented England in Cross-country.[3]


Career

Since 2012, she has modeled for magazines such as _GQ_, Spotlight magazine, Jute magazine, Company magazine, OK magazine magazine and brands such as _Cadillac_, Dior Beauty and Calvin Klein.[4] She has also walked in over 20 shows over the years during New York Fashion week.[5][6]

In 2013, she had a supporting role in the movie _Rush_.[7]
