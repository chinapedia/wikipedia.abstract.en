GIOVANNA FLETCHER (née FALCONE; born 29 January 1985) is a British author, presenter, actress, blogger, and vlogger.


Early life

Fletcher was born in Essex, England. Daughter of an Italian father, Mario and Argentine mother, Kim Falcone, she has two siblings: Giorgina and Mario. Her brother Mario Falcone is an English television personality, best known for appearing in the ITV2 semi-reality programme _The Only Way Is Essex_.

She attended the performing arts school, Sylvia Young Theatre School in London, followed by the Rose Bruford College of Theatre and Performance,[1] graduating in 2006.


Career

Television and film work

Fletcher has been on screen several times, including an uncredited role in _The Boat That Rocked_, as a guest on _Loose Women_, and as a contestant on _All Star Mr & Mrs_. And most recently as the presenter of The Baby Club on cbeebies. [2] She has had appearances on ITV's morning TV show _Lorraine_ since 2015, including presenting a segment for the show called _Take 5_.[3]

Writing

Fletcher's writing career started with an unpublished satirical project entitled _Dating McFly is for Dummies_.[4]

Prior to becoming an author, Fletcher wrote for several magazines, including work experience at _Heat_, where she wrote book reviews and copy for their website, and an internship at _Bliss_ magazine. Fletcher also worked at _Recognise Magazine_[5]

Today she has a regular blog feature on _Hello!_ magazine's website.[6]

Through her blogging and book reviewing work, Fletcher networked with authors and literary folk at book events. It was in one of these events that Fletcher spoke to novelist Dorothy Koomson, and it was this pivotal conversation that inspired Fletcher to write a book of her own.[7]

Fletcher's debut novel, _Billy and Me_, was published in May 2013, followed by _You're the One That I Want_ in May 2014. _Dream a Little Dream_ was published in June 2015, and a fourth novel, _Always with Love_ followed in June 2016.[8]

In 2017 she published her first non-fiction book, _Happy Mum, Happy Baby: My Adventures in Motherhood_.

Music

Fletcher has sung in musical performances on several YouTube videos, posted to her husband, Tom's, YouTube channel. She has performed duet covers in a series called "Me & Mrs F", covering songs including "How Do You Like Your Eggs In the Morning?",[9] "It Ain't Me Babe",[10] "L-O-V-E",[11] "You Really Got a Hold on Me",[12] "Moon River",[13] "Love Is on the Radio",[14] and "Tonight You Belong to Me".[15] Most of these covers have over half a million views each, with "Love Is on the Radio" having accumulated 1.8 million views over 3 years.

In December 2016 she had her professional concert debut, singing on stage in London's West End in Alan Menken's _A Christmas Carol_ at the Lyceum Theatre.[16]

In December 2017 she performed in the musical production of husband Tom Fletcher's novel _The Christmasaurus_ alongside Tom, Carrie Hope Fletcher, Harry Judd, and Matt Willis.[17]

YouTube

In October 2011 Fletcher launched her own YouTube channel, _Giovannasworld_ where she films videos for an audience of over 180,000 subscribers and counting, amassing over 17 million views to date.

Of her vlogs and social media presence, she says her aim is to "create a fun and positive little place on the Internet for people to come and feel uplifted. I document my life as an author, presenter, blogger and columnist, but most importantly my roles as wife to Tom and mum to Buzz and Buddy."[18]


Personal life

On 18 April 2011, Fletcher became engaged to her schoolmate and longtime boyfriend Tom Fletcher. He proposed to her at the Sylvia Young Theatre School where they first met at age 13.[19] They were married on 12 May 2012 with his McFly bandmates serving as his groomsmen.[20] The McFly song All About You was written by Tom about Giovanna as a Valentine's present in 2004.[21] Tom reworked the song, as well as Obviously and The Heart Never Lies, for his wedding speech. The video of the speech went viral and to date has more than 20 million views on YouTube.[22]

It was announced on Tom's YouTube channel on 29 October 2013 that the couple were expecting their first child, with a video titled, _We Have Some News..._[23] At exactly 7 pm on 13 March 2014, their son Buzz Michelangelo Fletcher was born.[24] Tom and Giovanna created a YouTube video, called _Bump to Buzz_ to announce the arrival of their first son Buzz,[25] which has over 13 million views.

The Fletchers announced on 3 September 2015, again on YouTube, that Giovanna was expecting their second child, by means of a video entitled 'Player 2'.[26] On 16 February 2016, their second son Buddy Bob Fletcher was born. The video announcing the birth of their second son was titled _Bump to Buddy_, where they took pictures documenting their second pregnancy,[27] together along with their son Buzz present.

On 3 March 2018, the Fletchers announced via their YouTube channel that they were expecting their third child. The video is titled _Snow Way...we have something to tell you._ and shows Tom and Giovanna, with their two sons Buzz and Buddy, building a snowman which shows the scan of their third child.[28] On the 24th August 2018, they welcomed their third son called Max Mario Fletcher.
