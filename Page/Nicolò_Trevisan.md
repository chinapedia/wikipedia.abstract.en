NICOLÒ TREVISA (died 1498) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Ceneda (1474–1498).[1]


Biography

On 15 Jun 1474, Nicolò Trevisa was appointed during the papacy of Pope Sixtus IV as Bishop of Ceneda.[2] He served as Bishop of Ceneda until his death on 10 Jan 1498.[3]
