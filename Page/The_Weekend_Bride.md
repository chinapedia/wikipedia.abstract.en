_THE WEEKEND BRIDE_ (German: _DIE WOCHENENDBRAUT_) is a 1928 German silent comedy film directed by Georg Jacoby and starring Elga Brink and Ossi Oswalda.[1]

The film's art direction was by Franz Schroedter.
