HANA MACIUCHOVÁ (born 25 November 1945) is a Czech film, television and stage actress. She studied at the Faculty of Theatre in Prague.[1] She was awarded the Czech Medal of Merit in 2010.[2]


Selected filmography

-   _Chalupáři_ (television, 1975)
-   _Prázdniny pro psa_ (1980)
-   _Krkonošské pohádky_ (television, 1984)
-   _In the Coat of Lioness' Arms_ (1994)
-   _Loners_ (2000)
-   _Ulice_ (television, 2005-2015)
-   _Dobrá čtvrť_ (television, 2008)
