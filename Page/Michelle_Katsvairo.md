MICHELLE KATSVAIRO is a Zimbabwean footballer who currently plays for Free State Stars.

Having been a product of Zimbabwe's renowned Black Ace's Academy, he has already scored on two occasions in all competitions for the ''Amakhosi.[1]


Club career

Back in Chicken Inn FC, he managed a total of 14 goals in 2015-16.[2] Joining from Chicken Inn F.C. in the summer, Katsvairo enhanced Kaizer Chiefs FC's striking options.[3] He made his league debut for the club on 14 September 2016 in a 1-1 home draw with Platinum Stars F.C.. He was subbed on for Lucky Baloyi in the 67th minute.[4] He scored his first goal against Free State Stars to secure a 2-0 win. His goal came in the 63rd minute.[5][6]
