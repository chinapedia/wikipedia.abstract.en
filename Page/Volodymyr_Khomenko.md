VOLODYMYR PETROVYCH KHOMENKO () (born 26 July 1954, in Cheremoshne, Pohrebyshche Raion, Ukraine), is a Ukrainian politician.

In 2007 he served as a Presidential representative of Ukraine in Crimea.
