OPEN IPTV is a Bosnian IPTV provider, subsidiary of the Telekom Srpske.[1]

OPEN IPTV service provides various thematic channels, HD and Timeshift channels.


Open IPTV BiH channel line-up

The program packages offered by Open IPTV currently (_as of January 2018_) offers more than 220[2] TV channels (160 in basic offer).
