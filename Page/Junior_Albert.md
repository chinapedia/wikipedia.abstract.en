JUNIOR ALBERT (born 25 July 1994) is a Solomon Islands footballer who plays as a defender for Kossa. He made his debut for the national team on October 8, 2016, in a 1–0 loss against New Caledonia.


International

Albert was named in the Solomon Islands national squad for the first time for two friendly matches against New Caledonia. He made his debut on October 8, 2016, in the second game, a 2–0 loss.[1]
