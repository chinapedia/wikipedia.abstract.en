SHAHIDUL ISLAM (born 5 January 1995) is a Bangladeshi cricketer who plays for Dhaka Metropolis.[1] He made his Twenty20 (T20) debut on 21 November 2016 playing for Chittagong Vikings in the 2016–17 Bangladesh Premier League.[2] In November 2018, bowling for Central Zone in the 2018–19 Bangladesh Cricket League, he took his maiden five-wicket haul in first-class cricket.[3] He was the leading wicket-taker for Central Zone in the tournament, with eighteen dismissals in four matches.[4]
