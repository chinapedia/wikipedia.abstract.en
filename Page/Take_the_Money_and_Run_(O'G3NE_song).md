"TAKE THE MONEY AND RUN" is a single by Dutch three-piece girl group O'G3NE. The song was released in the Netherlands as a digital download on 29 April 2016 through BMG as the lead single from their third studio album _We Got This_ (2016). The song peaked at number 130 on the Dutch Singles Chart.
