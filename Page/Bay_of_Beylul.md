BAY OF BEYLUL or BĒYLUL BAHIR SELAT'Ē,[1] also known as BADI BAY, is a bay on the Red Sea, on the coast of Eritrea. The bay is located about 450 km southeast of Asmara.


Geography

The Bay of Beylul is open towards the north and the Hanish Islands lie 50 km to the northeast. The eastern headland is _Ras Darma_. The settlement of Beylul lies on the western shore of the bay.[2]
