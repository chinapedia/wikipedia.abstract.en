_SEDILIA APHANITOMA_ is an extinct species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description


Distribution

This extinct marine species was found in Quaternary strata of Florida, USA; age range: 2.588 to 0.781 Ma.
