KIM-PASCAL BOYSEN (born 19 June 1988) is a German footballer who plays as a forward for SpVgg Fortuna Edingen.[1]


Career

Boysen made his professional debut for Kickers Offenbach in the 3. Liga on 19 December 2008, matchday 20 of the 2008–09 season, coming on as a substitute for Matthias Morys in the 84th minute of the match against VfB Stuttgart II.[2]


Personal life

He is the son of Hans-Jürgen Boysen, who was head coach while Kim-Pascal was at Kickers Offenbach.
