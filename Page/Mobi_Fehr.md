MOBI FEHR (born December 13, 1994 in New York City) is an American soccer player who currently plays for Las Vegas Lights FC in the USL Championship.


Playing career

Mobi Fehr played for Major League Soccer club; Portland Timbers in 2013 season.[1] In 2014, he moved to J3 League club; SC Sagamihara.[2]

In November 2016, Fehr joined Vietnamese V.League 1 club Hoàng Anh Gia Lai on a 1-year deal.[3]


Personal life

Mobi Fehr is born in New York City to a Swiss father and Japanese mother. He moved with his family to Tokyo in 2000.[4]

He was in Tokyo Verdy Youth, a very strong famous soccer team in Japan, and also known for leading many players to professional soccer teams. He was in Tokyo Very Youth which the famous Japanese actor, Ryoma Takeuchi(竹内涼真) also was in as well.
