'''Edith Eluma ''' (born 27 September 1958) is a Nigerian footballer who played as a defender for the Nigeria women's national football team. She was part of the team at the inaugural 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level, she played for Princess Jegede in Nigeria.[1]
