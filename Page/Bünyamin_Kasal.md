BÜNYAMIN KASAL (born 13 January 1997 in Bursa, Turkey) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a defender for Turkish club Karacabey Birlikspor in the TFF Third League.

Kasal made his professional debut in a 2-5 away win against Mersin İdmanyurdu.[1]
