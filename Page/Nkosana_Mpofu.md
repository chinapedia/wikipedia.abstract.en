NKOSANA MPOFU (born 10 July 1990) is a Zimbabwean first-class cricketer who plays for Matabeleland Tuskers.[1][2][3] Outside of Zimbabwe, he has also played cricket for Strabane Cricket Club in Ireland.[4]
