IDA MARIA _MARJA_ LAHTI (3 December 1901, Kymi – 19 April 1967) was a Finnish politician who served as Deputy Minister of Agriculture in the Virolainen Cabinet.

Lahti worked for a small farmers' federation in her local area, before being elected to the Parliament of Finland for the Agrarian League at the 1954 general election.[1] She remained a member until her death.[2] In parliament she served on the Economic Committee, the Grand Committee and the Banking Committee.[3] From 1964 to 1966 she served as second minister of Agriculture in the cabinet of Johannes Virolainen.[4][5]
