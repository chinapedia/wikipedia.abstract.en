DIMBESWAR DAS is a Bharatiya Janata Party politician from Assam. He has been elected in Assam Legislative Assembly election in 2016 from Raha constituency.[1][2][3]

On 19 January 2017, Das' car was removed by an engineer in Nagaon district for blocking the way to an office.[4] The engineer was made to apologize for the incident by touching Das' feet.
