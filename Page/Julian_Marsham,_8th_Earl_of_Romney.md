JULIAN CHARLES MARSHAM, 8TH EARL OF ROMNEY (born 1948, of Gayton Hall[1]) is an English peer.


Biography

Lord Romney is the son of Col Peter William Marsham, a grandson of Charles Marsham, 4th Earl of Romney, and his wife Hersey Coke, granddaughter of Thomas Coke, 2nd Earl of Leicester.[2]

He was educated at Eton and became a land agent and farmer.[3]

In 2007, Lord Romney served as High Sheriff of Norfolk.[4]


Marriage & children

In 1975, Romney married Catriona Ann Stewart, the daughter of Robert Christie Stewart, by whom he has two sons and one daughter:[5][6]

-   Major David Charles Marsham, Viscount Marsham[7][8] (born 18 April 1977); married Katherine F Phillips, has issue[9]
-   Hon Michael Julian Marsham (born 3 March 1979) married Hon. Lucy Harriet Beaumont, daughter of Wentworth Peter Ismay Beaumont, 4th Viscount Allendale[10]
-   Lady Laura Clare Marsham (born 18 March 1984), married James Meade, son of Olympian Richard Meade[11] Lady Laura is a godmother of Prince Louis of Cambridge.[12]

He succeeded his first cousin once removed, Michael Marsham, 7th Earl of Romney, in the earldom and the titles Baron Romney and Viscount Marsham in 2004.
