_DORIS BERTHELOTI_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Dorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the Canary Islands. It has been reported from the Mediterranean Sea and Senegal.[2]
