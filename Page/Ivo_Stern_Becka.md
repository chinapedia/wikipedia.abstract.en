IVO STERN BECKA served as the Chairman of the Asociación de Scouts de México, A.C., the Chairman of the Interamerican Scout Committee, and a member of the World Scout Committee.

In 2000, he was awarded the 282nd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting, at the 25th World Scout Conference.[1][2]
