Tan Sri KAMARUL ARIFFIN BIN MOHAMED YASSIN (born 12 August 1934)[1][2] served as the Chairman of the World Scout Committee.[3]

In 1983, he was awarded the 160th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting, at the 25th World Scout Conference.[4] He was also a recipient of the Silver World Award.
