'''Kelly Heuchan ''' (born 30 January 1980) was an Australian female water polo player. Married (Steve Parks USA rower) with three children. She was a member of the Australia women's national water polo team, playing as a driver.

She was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level she played for Fremantle Marlins in Australia.
