_SCYTHRIS BORNICENSIS_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Jäckh in 1977. It is found in France, Germany, Spain and Switzerland.[1][2]

The wingspan is about 15 mm. The fore- and hindwings have a clear chocolate brown, bronzy ground colour. There is no patterns on the wings.[3]
