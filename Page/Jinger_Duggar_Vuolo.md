JINGER NICOLE VUOLO (née DUGGAR; born December 21, 1993)[1] is an American author and television personality. She is known for her television appearances on TLC reality shows _19 Kids and Counting_ and _Counting On_. A member of the Duggar family, Vuolo is the fourth daughter born to Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar. Vuolo co-authored _Growing Up Duggar: It's All About Relationships_ with her sisters Jana, Jill and Jessa in 2015.


Career

Vuolo first began her television career appearing on the documentary _14 Children and Pregnant Again_ in 2004, which featured the Duggar family and was broadcast on the Discovery Health Channel. She appeared on a subsequent documentary, _Raising 16 Children_ in 2006.[2] In 2008, she made regular appearances on _19 Kids and Counting_ which featured the family and broadcast on TLC.[3] After the cancellation of the show, the spin-off series _Counting On_ was created in 2015 with Vuolo as one of the main cast members along with her sisters Jana, Jill and Jessa. She also published a book with her sisters called _Growing Up Duggar_ later that same year.[4]


Personal life

Vuolo met former professional athlete Jeremy Vuolo while on a mission trip to Central America in 2015.[5] The two announced that they were in a courtship in June 2016.[6] The couple was engaged one month later, and married on November 5, 2016.[7] The Vuolos reside in Laredo, Texas, where Jeremy works as the pastor of a local church. She is also an accomplished pianist. In March 2019, Jinger and Jeremy announced they are moving to Los Angeles, California for Jeremy to pursue graduate studies at the Master’s Seminary.

The Vuolos announced in January 2018 that they were expecting their first child in July 2018.[8] On April 9, 2018 they revealed they were having a girl, making Vuolo the first biological Duggar daughter to have a girl (sisters Jill, Jessa, and Joy-Anna all had sons at the time).[9] On July 19, 2018 at 4:37 am, Jinger gave birth to Felicity Nicole Vuolo.[10] Felicity is the twelfth grandchild, but only the third granddaughter, on the Duggar side, and the first grandchild and granddaughter on the Vuolo side.
