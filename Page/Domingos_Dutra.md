DOMINGOS FRANCISCO DUTRA FILHO (born March 20, 1956) is a Brazilian politician. He was state deputy (1991–1995, 2003–2007), vice-mayor (1997–2000) and federal deputy (1995–2003, 2007–2015). Dutra is a mayor of Paço do Lumiar since 2017.[1]
