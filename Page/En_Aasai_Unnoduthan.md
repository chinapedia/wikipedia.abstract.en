_EN AASAI UNNODUTHAN_ () is a 1983 Indian Tamil-language romance film edited and directed by K. Narayanan.[1] The film stars Prem Menon and Poornima Bhagyaraj.[2]


Cast

-   Prem Menon
-   Poornima Bhagyaraj[3]
-   Jaishankar[4]


Soundtrack

The soundtrack was composed by Sankar Ganesh.[5][6] The song "Devi Koondhalo" is based on "Happy Together" by The Turtles.[7]
