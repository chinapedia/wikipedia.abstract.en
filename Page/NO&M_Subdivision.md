The NO&M SUBDIVISION is a railroad line owned by CSX Transportation in the U.S. states of Alabama, Mississippi & Louisiana. The line runs from Mobile, AL to New Orleans, LA for a total of 138.5 miles. At its east end it continues west from the M&M Subdivision and at its west end it continues west as the CSX Transportation.[1][2]
