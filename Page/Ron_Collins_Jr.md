RON COLLINS (born September 30, 1971) is a former professional American football player.

Ron was born in Tokyo, Japan, and played as an offensive lineman with the NFL Europe's Frankfurt Galaxy in their 1996 and 1997 seasons.[1] He also played in the NFL off-season and on NFL practice squads with the San Francisco 49ers, Jacksonville Jaguars, Indianapolis Colts, Miami Dolphins, Chicago Bears.[2][3][4][5] In college, Collins played for the Fresno State Bulldogs.[6]
