SABBIR AHMED,SBP, OSP, SGP, ndc, psc, is a retired Lieutenant General of Bangladesh Army who served as Chief of General Staff in Bangladesh Army.[1][2] He also served as General-Officer-Commanding-(GOC) of Army Training and Doctrine Command (ARTDOC).[3] Before that he was GOC of the 24th Infantry Division in Chittagong.[4] He also served as Director of Military Training (DMT) of Army Headquarters Military Training Directorate of Bangladesh Army.
