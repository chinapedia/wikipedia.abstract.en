'''Pernilla Larsson ''' (born 19 February 1969) is a Swedish footballer who played as a midfielder for the Sweden women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level, she played for Gideonsbergs IF in Sweden.[1] Larsson served as a referee at the 2015 FIFA Women's World Cup in Canada[2] and the 2014 FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup.[3]
