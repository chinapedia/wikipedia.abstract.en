DEBORAH GRAMICCIONI is a lawyer based in New Jersey who has worked in the administration of Governor Chris Christie and as the Deputy Executive Director of the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey. She sits on the Ocean County Family Court.


Background

Gramiccioni attended Livingston High School and the University of Pennsylvania.[1] She has been on the board of directors of the North Jersey Transportation Planning Authority.[2] She has worked as a special assistant to the New Jersey Attorney General.


US attorney

Gramiccioni worked in the office of United States Attorney for the District of New Jersey under Christie[3] She was later appointed Director of the New Jersey Division of Criminal Justice.[4]


Christie administration

After he was elected governor, Christie brought Gramiccioni into his administration as Director of the Authorities Unit.[5]


PANYNJ

Gramiccioni was appointed by New Jersey Governor Chris Christie as Deputy Executive Director of the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey in December 2013 after the resignation of Bill Baroni.[6][7][8][9][10]


Judgeship nomination

Christie nominated Gramiccioni to a Superior Court vacancy in Mercer County in May 2016, but no confirmation hearing was held. In October 2016 Christie nominated her to a vacancy in Ocean County.[11]
