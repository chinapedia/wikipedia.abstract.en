KASPER ENGHARDT (born 27 May 1992) is a Danish professional footballer who currently plays for Lyngby BK.[1]


Career

After three season, Enghardt left Randers FC.[2] On 4 July 2019, he joined newly promoted Danish Superliga club Lyngby Boldklub.[3]
