WESTBURY STREET HOLDINGS (WSH) is an English contract catering company.

WSH was founded in 2000 by Alastair Storey, who is the company's chairman and chief executive (CEO).[1]

WSH owns BaxterStorey, Portico, Caterlink, Holroyd Howe, Benugo and Searcys, and its headquarters, WSH International Investments Limited, is in Reading, Berkshire.[2]

In 2014, WSH acquired Searcys for an undisclosed sum, although it was marketed at £25-30 million.[3]
