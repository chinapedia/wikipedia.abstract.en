The GEORGE ROMEY HOUSE, also known as the BRUCE GIRTON HOUSE, is a historic building located in Mason City, Iowa, United States. George A. Romey was a local realtor who worked in partnership with William L. Patton. He had Fred Lippert design this Prairie School house, which was built by J.M. Felt & Company in 1920.[1] Bruce Girton, the later owner, operated the family feed business. The two-story brick house features wide eaves, broad hip roof, and groups of casement windows.[2] It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.[3]
