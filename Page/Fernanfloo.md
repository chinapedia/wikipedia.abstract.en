LUIS FERNANDO FLORES ALVARADO[1] (known on YouTube as FERNANFLOO) is a Salvadoran gaming YouTuber. With a total of over 6.8 billion views, more than 570 videos and 32 million subscribers,[2] his channel is the 22nd most-subscribed channel on YouTube and the most subscribed channel from his native country of El Salvador.[3]


History

Flores was born on July 7, 1993 in San Salvador, El Salvador.[4]

In December 2015, Flores launched an app called _Fernanfloo_, which received 2.3 million downloads within its first week of release[5].

In April 2018, Fernanfloo began to make gameplays on Twitch.[6]

In January 2019, he announced his collaboration with Resident Evil 2 game publisher Capcom to have himself on the remake. Weeks later, Capcom released the official trailer of the 1-Short demo with him as the zombie. Later on, he uploaded a video on his YouTube channel about his collaboration with Capcom and his reaction about the trailer.
