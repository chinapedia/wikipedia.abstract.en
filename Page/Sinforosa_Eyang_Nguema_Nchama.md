SINFOROSA EYANG NGUEMA NCHAMA (born 26 April 1994),[1] better known as MIREY,[2] is an Equatoguinean football manager and former player who coaches Deportivo Evinayong in the Equatoguinean women's league.[3]

She was a midfielder during her playing career, which included to be a member of the Equatorial Guinea women's national team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she played for E Waiso Ipola in her country.[4]
