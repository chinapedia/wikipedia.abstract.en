GARETH POTTS (born 25 July 1990) is an English rugby league footballer who plays for the Hunslet in the Betfred League 1. He plays as a .


Background

Potts was born in England.


Career

Potts was in the junior systems at the Wakefield Trinity Wildcats and spent time on loan at Doncaster.[1] He has previously played for the Batley Bulldogs.[2]

He has previously played Halifax in the Kingstone Press Championship.[3]
