XIANGMI LAKE() is man-made recreational lake located in Futian District, Shenzhen, Guangdong, China.[1][2][3] It covers a total surface area of and has a storage capacity of some of water. The lake provides landscape water and is used for flood and silt control, and recreational activities, such as boating, swimming, and fishing.[4]


History

Xiangmi Lake was established and opened to the public in 1966, which has fun, food and drink, and tourism.[5] At that time it bore the name XIANGMAOCHANG RESERVOIR (), which was extended to a small reservoir in 1978.[6]


Transportation

-   Take bus No. 44, 46, 65, 107, 108, 235 or 237 to Xiangmi Lake North Bus Stop ()
-   Take subway Line 1 (Luobao Line) to get off at Xiangmi Station.
