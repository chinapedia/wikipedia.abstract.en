JASMINE MATTHEWS (born 24 March 1993) is an English footballer who plays as a defender for Bristol City. She has previously played for Liverpool and represented England at under-17, under-19 and under-23 level.


Club career

Matthews signed with Bristol City ahead of the 2011 FA WSL season. She made her debut for the senior squad during a match against Birmingham City L.F.C. on 14 April 2011.[1] She made seven appearances during her first season with Bristol. The team finished in fifth place with a record.[2] During the 2012 FA WSL season, Matthews made seven appearances helping the team finish in fourth place with a record.[3]

In 2014, Matthews played for Bristol in the 2014–15 UEFA Women's Champions League.[4] Bristol was the only English team to make the quarterfinals where they were eliminated by eventual winners Frankfurt.[5] In 2015, she competed in the 2015–16 UEFA Women's Champions League for the second time.

In July 2018, Matthews signed for Liverpool.[6]

After one season with Liverpool, it was announced on 12 July 2019 that Matthews was returning to Bristol City ahead of the 2019–20 season.[7]


International

Matthews has represented England on the under-17,[8][9] under-19 and under-23 national teams.[10][11][12]
