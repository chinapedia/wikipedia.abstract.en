_BETTER TOGETHER_ is an album by Gaither Vocal Band. It earned the group a Grammy Award nomination for Best Roots Gospel Album.[1]


Awards and accolades

On August 9, 2017, it was announced that _Better Together_ would be nominated for a GMA Dove Award in the Southern Gospel Album of the Year category at the 48th Annual GMA Dove Awards.[2]
