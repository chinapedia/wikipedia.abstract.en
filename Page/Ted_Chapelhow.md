EDWARD "TED" CHAPELHOW (born 21 September 1995) is an English rugby league footballer who plays as a prop for the Widnes Vikings in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

Ted Chapelhow was born in Widnes, Cheshire, England. His twin brother Jay Chapelhow also plays for Widnes Vikings in the Super League.[2]

Chapelhow is a graduate of the Widnes Vikings Academy system[3] and played his junior rugby league for the West Bank Bears club.[4][5] He has represented England at youth team level.[6][7]
