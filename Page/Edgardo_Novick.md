HÉCTOR EDGARDO NOVICK VARELA (born 22 November 1956 in Montevideo) is a Uruguayan businessman and politician.


Biography

Starting out as a street market worker with his father, Novick developed an important business career. Novick co-owns Nuevocentro Shopping.[1]

In 2015 Novick was a candidate in the Municipal Elections.[2]

On 7 November 2016 Novick founded a political party, Partido de la Gente.[3]

Novick is father of the footballers Marcel and Hernán Novick.
