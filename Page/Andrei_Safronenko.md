ANDREI IGOREVICH SAFRONENKO (; born 8 January 1998) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Irtysh Omsk on 9 October 2016 in a game against FC Dynamo Barnaul.[1]
