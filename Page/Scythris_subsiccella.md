_SCYTHRIS SUBSICCELLA_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Bengt Å. Bengtsson in 1997. It is found in France.[1]


Etymology

The species name refers to the close external similarity to _Scythris siccella_, plus Latin sub- (meaning near).[2]
