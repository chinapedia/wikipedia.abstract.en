NYAPIN is a subdivision of Kumey Kumey district in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Nyapin is located 56 km towards South from District headquarters Koloriang. Natives of Nyapin pronounced Nyapin as Nyoping. It is divided broadly into Upper Nyapin and Lower Nyapin. Consisting of various clans, like Gyamar,Tadar,Kipa,Tarak,Tame,Tai etc. Nyapin is the most beautiful place among the civilizations prevailing in kurung kumey District. Surrounded by high mountains from all side, it is an elongated valley drained by kiding river and panyu river dissecting the beautiful valley into upper and lower nyapin.Nyapin consist of a higher secondary school with commerce and arts stream and government college for bachelor's degree.people of Nyapin having agrarian economy mostly practices wet paddy cultivation and zhum cultivation as well. Presently people have also started cultivating spice like large cardamom for higher returns.people here also produces chilly,king chilly,brinjal,cucumber,ragi,maize, green leafy vegetables etc. Nyapin is Nyishi dominated area. People here practices Christian and Doni Poloism. Nyokum being the harvesting festival of Nyishi is profoundly celebrated along with the Christmas of Christian neighbourhoods. It is one of the 60 constituencies of Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh. Name of current MLA (August-2016) of this constituency is BAMANG FELIX.


Climates

Nyapin has a subtropical highland climate (Cwb in the Köppen climate classification). Summers are warm with very high rainfall; winters are mild with moderate rainfall. It is very hilly with high geographical terrain. Most of the time it is covered by clouds. The area is windy in nature.
