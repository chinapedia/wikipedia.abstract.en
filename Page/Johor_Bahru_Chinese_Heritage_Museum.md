The JOHOR BAHRU CHINESE HERITAGE MUSEUM () is a museum in Johor Bahru, Johor, Malaysia. The museum is about the history of Chinese community in Johor Bahru.[1][2][3]


Architecture

The museum is housed in a four-story shop house building.[4]


Exhibitions

Collections in the museum include documents, music instruments, old money, photos, porcelain etc.


Opening time

The museum opens everyday except Mondays from 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. for MYR3.[5]
