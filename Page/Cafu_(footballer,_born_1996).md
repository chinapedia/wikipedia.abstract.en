OCTACILIO BRITO ALVES, also known as CAFU (born 22 January 1996), is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for São Bento on loan from Portuguese club Estoril.


Career

Born in Niterói, Cafu joined the youth side of Flamengo in 2010. On 5 September 2016 it was announced that he had signed on loan with Mumbai City of the Indian Super League.[1] He made his professional debut for the club on 3 October 2016 against Pune City. He came on as a 73rd minute substitute for Matías Defederico as Mumbai City won 1–0.[2]
