DEMOCRAT CREEK is a stream in Knox County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary to Rock Creek. The confluence is approximately one mile south-southeast of Edina.[2]

Democrat Creek was named for the fact a large share of the early settlers were Democrats.[3]
