TEDA INVESTMENT HOLDING CO., LTD. known as TEDA HOLDING is a Chinese state-owned enterprise based in Tianjin. The company was supervised by the .

TEDA was the acronym of Tianjin Economic-Technological Development Area. The Chinese name () was in turn the transliteration of TEDA.


History

TEDA HOLDING (), as the name suggested, was originally related to Tianjin Economic-Technological Development Area (; acronym TEDA) as an investment vehicle of Tianjin Municipal People's Government.[1] It was founded in 1984,[2] after the marketization of China. It was renamed to the current name (from ) in 2001, after a merger with sister company TEDA Group () and Tianjin TEDA Construction Group ().[3]

It was later expended to multiple sector, which now included football club, securities trading services, trust investment company and steel manufacturing.[4]
