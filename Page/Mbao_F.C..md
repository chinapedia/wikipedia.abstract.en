MBAO FC is a football club from Mwanza, Tanzania. 'Mbao' is the Swahili word for 'wood'.


Achievements

2015/2016

They won promotion to the Tanzanian Premier League after the 2015/2016 season.[1]

2016/2017

In the 2016/2017 season they are one of two teams from Mwanza playing in the Premier League. The other being Toto African.[2]
