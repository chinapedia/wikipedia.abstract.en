KLOSTERMANN BLOCK, also known as the ALLIANCE BUILDING, is a historic commercial building located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It built in 1905, and is a two-story, rectangular brick, building measuring 100 feet by 75 feet. It features a cornice and frieze of pressed metal.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1994.[2]
