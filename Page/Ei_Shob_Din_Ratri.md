_EI SHOB DIN RATRI_ (English: _All These Nights and Days_) is a 1985 Bengali Family-Drama written by Humayun Ahmed and aired on BTV. The drama tells the story of a middle-class joint family that lives in the capital city Dhaka. The drama shows the happiness, love, marriage, deaths and troubles a middle-class family in Bangladesh faces in everyday life. The story ends with the death of a young girl called "_Tuni_" due to a life-threatening disease called Leukemia.[1][2] Many viewers of the serial requested Humayun Ahmed to save the character "_Tuni_". But Ahmed stood on his decision.[3]
