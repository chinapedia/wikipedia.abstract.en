is a Japanese football player.[1] He plays for FC Tokyo.[2]


Club career

Hatano joined FC Tokyo in 2016. On May 29, he debuted in J3 League (v AC Nagano Parceiro).[3][4]


National team career

In May 2017, Hatano was elected Japan U-20 national team for 2017 U-20 World Cup. But did not play in the match, as he was the team's reserve goalkeeper.
