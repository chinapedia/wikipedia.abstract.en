RONALD A. SILVER (born June 25, 1943) is an American former politician in the state of Florida.

He served in the Florida House of Representatives for the 100th district from 1982 to 1992, as a Democrat.[1][2] He also served in the Florida State Senate from 1992 to 2002.[3][4]
