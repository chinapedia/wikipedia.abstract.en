_DORIS WELLINGTONENSIS_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Dorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from Wellington, New Zealand. It has been reported from Tasmania and Victoria, Australia.[2]
