DRITA D'AVANZO (née SELMANI; born February 6, 1976) is an Albanian-American reality star on the VH1 series _Mob Wives_. She is also known for her hot-tempered personality.[1]


Personal life

D’Avanzo grew up in Staten Island after her family migrated from Albania. Her father was a strict soccer coach and forced her to train hard. She ultimately earned an invitation to play for the Women’s National Soccer Team, but never did.

She and her husband, Lee D'Avanzo, have two children: Aleeya and Gizelle. Her husband’s father was an estranged cousin of former New York City mayor Rudolph Giuliani.


Career

D’Avanzo appeared on _Mob Wives_ from 2011–16.

She also appeared on _Mob Wives_ spinoff _Big Ang_.

On _The Tomorrow Show with Keven Undergaro_ D’Avanzo stated that she is working on a spinoff of her own.[2]

D’Avanzo continues to make regular appearances as herself on television, with notables including _Celebrity Ghost Stories_ and _Watch What Happens: Live_.[3]

She has worked for several upscale cosmetic companies as a freelance makeup artist and has launched her own cosmetics line called "Lady Boss by Drita".[4]

In August 2017, she was announced as a cast member on the _Big Brother (US)_ style VH1 reality show _Scared Famous_.[5]
