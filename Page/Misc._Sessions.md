_MISC. SESSIONS_ is an EP by Rob Dougan in preparation for a new album, recorded at Abbey Road Studios and featuring a 10-piece chamber string section alongside a larger 50-piece orchestra, and five new songs. It was released on October 23, 2016 on Gumroad.[1]
