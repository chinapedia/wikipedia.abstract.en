SO STRESSED is a noisy American rock and roll band from Sacramento, CA that uses synthesizer, drums, and guitar to make music. Currently, the band's line-up includes Morgan Fox and Kenneth Draper, while guest guitarists join them during live performances.[1] Their music is frequently highlighted for the loud, abrasive, anxious, and catchy nature of their songs.[2][3][4][5][6][7]


History

So Stressed started as a group of friends that played shows around Northern California as of 2009.[8] Draper stated in an interview with BL!SSS Magazine that the group's origins were in producing instrumental and improvisational music.[9] For several years, membership in the band included Andrew Garcia as guitarist.[10]

Their music is recorded with Patrick Hills at Earth Tone Records, also in Sacramento, CA.[11]

The band's first EP, _Attracted to Open Mouths,_ was released by Music Ruins Lives.[12] While on Meredith Graves' record label, Honor Press, they released their first LP_The Unlawful Trade of Greco-Roman Art_ .[13][14][15] Current music is released on vinyl through LA-based label, Ghost Ramp.[16] They have toured with bands, such as Wreck and Reference, Kino Kimino, and Wavves.
