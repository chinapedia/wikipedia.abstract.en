_DESOLATION ANGELS_ is a 1995 independent film written and directed by Tim McCann.[1]

The film premiered at the 1995 Telluride Film Festival.[2][3] It then won the International Critics Prize (FIPRESCI Prize) at the Toronto International Film Festival,[4] and was accepted to the Rotterdam Film Festival.[5]


Plot

A man discovers that a close friend has raped his girlfriend and vows revenge.
