CHAK 285 JB (Jhang Branch) and CHAK 284 JB are villages of Toba Tek Singh District in the Punjab province of Pakistan. Their postal code is 36101. [1]

They are located at 31°03′52″N 72°37′52″E with an altitude of 162 metres (534 feet). Neighbouring settlements include Randian and Bilasur. It is located at Gojra - Toba Tek Singh Road.
