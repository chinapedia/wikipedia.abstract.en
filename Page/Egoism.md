EGOISM is an ethical theory that treats self-interest as the foundation of morality.[1]


Origin

Latin word "ego" meaning "I"[2].


Forms

-   Ethical egoism, the doctrine that holds that individuals _ought_ to do what is in their self-interest
-   Psychological egoism, the doctrine that holds that individuals are always motivated by self-interest
-   Rational egoism, the belief that it is rational to act in one's self-interest
