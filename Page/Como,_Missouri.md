COMO is an unincorporated community in New Madrid County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Como was laid out in 1879 when the railroad was extended to that point.[2] A post office called Como was established in 1879, and remained in operation until 1938.[3] The community was named after Como, in Italy.[4]
