The BOYS’ 110 METRES HURDLES competition at the 2014 Summer Youth Olympics was held on 20–23 August 2014 in Nanjing Olympic Sports Center.

  ---------------- ------- -------
  20 August 2014   19:45   HEATS
  23 August 2014   19:55   FINAL
