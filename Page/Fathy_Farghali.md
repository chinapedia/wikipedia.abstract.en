FATHY FARGHALI () served as Deputy Regional Director for Community Development of the World Scout Bureau Arab Regional Scout Office.

In 2011, he was awarded the 329th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
