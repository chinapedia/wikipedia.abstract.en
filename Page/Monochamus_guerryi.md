_MONOCHAMUS GUERRYI_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Maurice Pic in 1903. It is known from Myanmar, China, and Thailand. It feeds on _Pinus kesiya_, _Castanea mollissima_, _Malus pumila_, and _Quercus glauca_.[1]
