STATE HIGHWAY 3 (WEST BENGAL) is a state highway in West Bengal, India.


Route

SH 3 originates from Krishnanagar and passes through Hanskhali, Helencha, Bangaon, Gaighata, Maslandapur, Kholapota, Baduria, Haroa, Baguiati, Dhapa, Bhangar, Minakhan, Bamanpukuria, Ghatakpukur, Sonakhali, Basanti and terminates at Gosaba.[1]

The total length of SH 3 is 260 km.[2]

Districts traversed by SH 3 are:[3]
Nadia district (0 - 32 km)
North 24 Parganas district (32 - 164 km)
South 24 Parganas district (164 - 260 km)
