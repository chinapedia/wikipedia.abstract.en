_ASYMMETRURA_ is a genus of moths in the family Scythrididae.[1]


Species

-   _Asymmetrura albilineata_ (Walsingham, 1888)
-   _Asymmetrura brevistrigella_ (Chambers, 1875)
-   _Asymmetrura graminivorella_ (Braun, 1920)
-   _Asymmetrura impositella_ (Zeller, 1855)
-   _Asymmetrura reducta_ (Braun, 1923)
-   _Asymmetrura scintillifera_ (Braun, 1927)
