STEFAN JEVTOSKI (; born 2 September 1997) is a Macedonian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Arsenal Kyiv in the Ukrainian Premier League.[1]


Career

In January 2016, Jevtoski signed a three-year contract with Bulgarian club Lokomotiv Plovdiv.[2] He made his competitive debut on 29 November against Montana in which he came on in the 81st minute for Dani Kiki. His first goal came on 12 May 2017 against Levski Sofia when he joined from the bench in the 54th minute and scored the final goal for the 2–1 win.[3] On 15 August 2017, Jevtoski's contract was terminated by mutual consent.[4] He currently plays for NK Varazdin in the Croatian second league and made his debut against NK Lucko in 1:0 win. In July 2018, Jevtoski moved to Arsenal Kyiv on a free transfer. He made his league debut for the club on 29 July 2018, playing all 90 minutes in a 3-0 away defeat to Shakhtar Donetsk.[5]


International career

Youth levels

Jevtoski made his debut for Macedonia U21 on 28 March 2017 in a friendly match against Bulgaria U21.[6]
