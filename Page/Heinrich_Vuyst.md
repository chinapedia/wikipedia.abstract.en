HEINRICH VUYST, O.F.M. or HEINRICH WUST (died 1468) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Auxiliary Bishop of Paderborn (1462â€“1468).[1][2][3][4][5][6]


Biography

Heinrich Vuyst was ordained a priest in the Order of Friars Minor.[7][8] On 31 Dec 1462, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Pius II as Auxiliary Bishop of Paderborn and Titular Bishop of _Tiflis_.[9][10] On 25 Jan 1464 he was consecrated bishop.[11][12] He served as Auxiliary Bishop of Paderborn until his death in 1468.[13][14]
