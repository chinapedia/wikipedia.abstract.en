Other appearances

Pre-_Ex on the Beach_

As well as appearing on _Ex on the Beach_, many cast members had appeared on other well known shows. Series 1's Vicky Pattison and her ex Ricci Guarnaccio were already known for being cast members on _Geordie Shore_. Daniel Conn was also known to be an Australian model and rugby league player, as well as briefly appearing in the sixth series of _Geordie Shore_.[8] _Geordie Shore's_ Gary Beadle and Charlotte Crosby also joined Series 2 of the show, with Gary being an original cast member and Charlotte joining as his ex.[9] Series 3 included former _The Only Way Is Essex_ star Kirk Norcross, his ex Cami-Li who is an American model who also appeared in Celebrity Big Brother 15. Former Ibiza Weekender cast member Jordan Davies was also included in this series.[10] Series 4 of the show featured Scotty T, another _Geordie Shore_ cast member,[11] as well as _Judge Geordie_ star Lewis Good.[12] Whilst Series 5 mainly focused on the return of old cast for unfinished business, it also featured Charlotte Dawson, the daughter of the late Les Dawson.[13]

Series 6 of the show included _Love Island_ star Joshua Ritchie, as well as another _Geordie Shore_ star Aaron Chalmers and _The Only Way Is Essex_ star Nicole Bass making appearances as exes.[14] Jenny Thompson also appeared as an ex having previously being known as one of the prostitutes involved with Wayne Rooney in 2010.[15] The seventh series of the show will include _Love Island_ winner Max Morley, _Beauty School Cop Outs_ cast member Savannah Kemplay, and _Geordie Shore_ star Chloe Ferry as Marty McKenna's ex. Sam Reece will also appear in this series having been in the headlines before following his controversial relationship with Stephanie Davis.[16]

Post-_Ex on the Beach_

Star of the first series Chloe Goodman became the first cast member to appear in _Celebrity Big Brother_ in 2015, taking part in the fifteenth series but became the first housemate to be evicted.[17] In January 2016, Series 3's Megan McKenna became the second _Ex on the Beach_ star to enter the house, this time taking part in the seventeenth series of the show. She later became the fourth housemate to be evicted.[18] In March 2016, Marty McKenna joined the cast of _Geordie Shore_,[19] and Megan McKenna went to star in _The Only Way Is Essex_.[20] In July 2016, Bear became the third cast member to enter the _Celebrity Big Brother_ house, participating in the eighteenth series, where he was later announced as the winner.[21] Series 3 ex Sarah Goodhart and Series 6 main cast Zahida Allen later joined the fourteenth series of _Geordie Shore_.[22] The eighteenth series of _Big Brother_ included Series 2 and 5 cast member Kayleigh Morris, Series 4's Kieran Lee, and Series 6 star Chanelle McCleary. Charlotte Dawson also went onto star in the third series of _Celebs Go Dating_. In August 2017, Jemma Lucy and Jordan Davies entered the _Celebrity Big Brother_ house for the twentieth series. Jordan was the third to be evicted whilst Jemma reached the final, ultimately finishing sixth. In January 2018, Jess Impiazzi, star of Series 2 and 5, took part the twenty-first series.
