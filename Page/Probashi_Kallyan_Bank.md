PROBASHI KALLYAN BANK is a state-owned bank in Bangladesh, a specialized financial institution for non-resident Bangladeshis.[1][2]


History

The Bank was started in 2010 by the Government of Bangladesh to provide financial services to oversea Bangladeshis.[3] It had a start up capital of 1 billion taka. 95 percent of the capital came from Wage Earners' Welfare Fund and five percent from the government of Bangladesh.[4]
