PRESLEY BRANCH is a stream in northern Fulton County, Arkansas and southeastern Howell County, Missouri.[1]

The stream headwaters in Howell County are at and the confluence with Myatt Creek in Fulton County is at .[2] The stream flows south roughly parallel to Missouri Route PP then crosses under Missouri Route 147 and enters Arkansas where it turns southwest and enters Myatt Creek about one-half mile south of the border.[3][4][5][6]

Presley Branch has the name of Pinkney Presley, a pioneer citizen.[7]
