ED BARBER (born 26 April 1990) is an English rugby league footballer who plays as a or for Halifax in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

Barber was born in Halifax, West Yorkshire, England.


Career

Barber has previously played for the Western Suburbs Rosellas, Dewsbury Rams[2] and the Swinton Lions.[3]
