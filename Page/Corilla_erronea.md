_CORILLA ERRONEA_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Corillidae.

Variety of this species include _Corilla erronea_ var. _eronella_ Gude, 1896.[1]


Distribution

Distribution of _Corilla erronea_ includes Sri Lanka.
