The PALONG TIN MUSEUM is a museum in Ipoh, Kinta District, Perak, Malaysia.[1]


History

The establishment of the museum is part of tourism-related project undertook by Kinta Riverfront Hotel and Suites in collaboration with Ipoh City Council and Perak Water and Drainage Department, which also includes the beautification of Kinta River side landscape.[2]


Exhibitions

The museum exhibits information on Malaysia mining history, Kinta Valley tin mining activities, mining methods etc.
