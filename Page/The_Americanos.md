THE AMERICANOS are an American electronic dance music DJ trio based in Los Angeles[1] consisting of DJ Felli Fel, Lex Larson and Louie Rubio.[2]


Background

Felli Fel, a veteran DJ and radio host, formed the group alongside Lex Larson and Louie Rubio.[3] They have collaborated with several notable hip-hop artists such as Tyga, Lil Jon, Ty Dolla $ign, French Montana, Lil Yachty and Juicy J.


History

2015

On 14 August 2015, they released their debut single "BlackOut" featuring Lil Jon, Juicy J & Tyga.[4] The single was featured on the We Are Your Friends movie soundtrack and in the movie Office Christmas Party. The single debuted on Billboard's Dance/Electronic songs chart at 18th.[5]

2016

On 11 July 2016, they released the single "In My Foreign" featuring Ty Dolla Sign, Nicky Jam, French Montana and Lil Yachty which was featured in the movie XXX: Return of Zander Cage.[6]
