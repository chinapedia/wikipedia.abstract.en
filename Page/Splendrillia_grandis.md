_SPLENDRILLIA GRANDIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell attains 18 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs in the Caribbean Sea off the Campeche Bank, Yucatán, Mexico.
