DANIEL "DANNY" AMOS (born 22 December 1999) is an English-born Northern Irish professional footballer who plays as a left-back for Doncaster Rovers .

He has played youth football for Northern Ireland.


Playing career

Club

Amos came through the Doncaster Rovers youth team, and made his senior debut on 30 August 2016, coming on as a 71st-minute substitute for Matty Blair in a 2–0 win over Mansfield Town in an EFL Trophy group stage game at Field Mill.[1]

International

On 29 September 2017 Amos was added to the Northern Ireland under-19 squad for upcoming matches against Poland, Germany and Belarus.[2] He was placed into the starting eleven for the opening match against Poland, scoring his side's only goal as they were defeated 2–1.[3]

He played in a Northern Ireland U21 side in a 1–0 friendly win against Mexico U22's in Spain on 25 March 2019.[4]
