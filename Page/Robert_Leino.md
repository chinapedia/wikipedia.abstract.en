ROBERT LEINO (born April 14, 1993) is a Finnish ice hockey player. He is currently playing with Ilves of the Finnish Liiga.

Leino made his Liiga debut playing with HPK during the 2012–13 Liiga season [1] and has also played for HIFK.[2]
