'''Lorraine Jossob ''' (born 4 May 1993 in Keetmanshoop) is a Namibian women's international footballer who plays as a defender. She is a member of the Namibia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship. On club level she played for Spfr. Neukirch in Germany.[1][2]
