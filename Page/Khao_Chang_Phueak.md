KHAO CHANG PHUEAK is a mountain in Thailand's Thong Pha Phum National Park.[1][2] It is in Pilok Subdistrict, Thong Pha Phum District, Kanchanaburi Province. Khao Chang Phueak is in elevation[3] and it is the third highest mountain in Kanchanaburi.[4]

An trail ascend to the mountain's summit. Visitors who intend to hike the trail must first register with Thong Pha Phum National Park officials, and the number of visitors to Khao Chang Phueak is limited to 60 persons per day.[5]
