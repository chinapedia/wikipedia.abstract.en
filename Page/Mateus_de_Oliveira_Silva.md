MATEUS DE OLIVEIRA SILVA (born 27 September 1994), commonly known as MATEUS, is a Brazilian football midfielder who currently plays for Fortuna Liga club FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce - Vráble.


Club career

FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce - Vráble

Mateus de Oliveira Silva made his professional Fortuna Liga debut for FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce - Vráble[1] against FK Senica on 19 November 2016. He also netted his first goal for Zlaté Moravce in the match against FK Senica.
