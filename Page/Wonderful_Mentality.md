_WONDERFUL MENTALITY_ (French: _BELLE MENTALITÉ_) is a 1953 French comedy film directed by André Berthomieu and starring Jean Richard, Michèle Philippe and Jean Martinelli.[1]

The film's sets were designed by the art director Raymond Nègre.
