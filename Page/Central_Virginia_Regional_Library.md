CENTRAL VIRGINIA REGIONAL LIBRARY serves Buckingham and Prince Edward counties in Virginia, United States. The library system is within Region 2 of Virginia Library Association (VLA).[1]


Service area

According to the FY 2014 Institute of Museum and Library Services Data Catalog, the Library System has a service area population of 40,662 with one central library and one branch library.[2]


Branches

-   Buckingham County Public Library (Dillwyn)
-   Farmville-Prince Edward Community Library (Farmville)
