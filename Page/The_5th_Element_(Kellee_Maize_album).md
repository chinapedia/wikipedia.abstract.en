_THE 5TH ELEMENT_ is the sixth studio album by American rapper Kellee Maize. The album was distributed by Maize's marketing company Nakturnal and revolves around the theme of love.[1][2]


Production

The album was produced by Pittsburgh-based producers Nice Nate and Headphone Activist. It was mixed and mastered by producer J. Glaze.[3]
