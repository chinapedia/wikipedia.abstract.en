ILO-Logo.svg The ILO-MOTORENWERKE in Pinneberg existed from 1911 to 1990 and were one of the biggest manufacturers of two-stroke engines in Germany.

_ILO_ comes from the Esperanto word for "tool". The name was stylized as _JLO_ in the company logo.


Engines

The appended "V" stands for Ventilation, i.e. an additional cooling blower.[1]
