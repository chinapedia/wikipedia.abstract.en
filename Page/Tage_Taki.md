TAGE TAKI is an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Taki was elected from the Ziro-Hapoli constituency in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election, standing as an BJP candidate.[2][3][4] He is an engineer by qualification, having served as a Superintending Engineer in the Arunachal Pradesh Public Works Department. He is from Lempia village in Ziro valley of Arunachal Pradesh.[5] He succeeded Padi Richo, who was the previous MLA from Ziro-Hapoli region by defeating him by a 1219 votes.
