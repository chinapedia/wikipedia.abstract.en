Shaw_Park,_Clayton.jpg SHAW PARK is a 47 acre park located in Clayton, Missouri. It is bordered by Forsyth Boulevard and Forest Park Parkway on the north and south, and Interstate 170 and Forsyth Boulevard on the west and east. It is the largest and oldest park in the area.


History

Shaw Park was founded in 1935 by the Mayor Charles A. Shaw.[1]


Amenities

Shaw_Park,_Clayton_3.jpg, _Molecular Bloom with Single Flower_, steel and bronze, 2013, Shaw Park, Clayton]] Shaw Park has a trail, playgrounds, a pool, tennis courts, baseball fields, volleyball courts, a sensory garden, and multiple pavilions.
