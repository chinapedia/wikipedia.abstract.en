PRAIRIE VIEW USD 362 is a unified school district headquartered in La Cygne, Kansas, United States.[1]


Schools

It includes La Cygne Elementary, Parker Elementary, Prairie View Middle School and Prairie View High School.


Sports

Prairie View High School sports include baseball, boys' and girls' basketball, cross country, cheer and football, softball, track, volleyball and wrestling.
