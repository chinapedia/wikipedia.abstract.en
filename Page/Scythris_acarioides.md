_SCYTHRIS ACARIOIDES_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Bengt Å. Bengtsson in 1997. It is found in Italy.[1]


Etymology

The species name refers to the sclerotized formation of stermum 7, reminiscent of a tick (belonging to the order Acari).[2]
