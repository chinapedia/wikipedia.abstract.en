RYAN O'MALLEY (born July 24, 1993) is an American football tight end who is currently a free agent. He played college football at the University of Pennsylvania, and signed with the Oakland Raiders as an undrafted free agent in 2016.


Early years

O'Malley was a three-year letterman in football at Summit High School in Summit, New Jersey, where he played wide receiver and defensive end.[1][2][3] He was a member of the 2009 team that went 12-0 and won the Group 2 State Championship.[4][5][6] He was a team captain as a senior in 2010.[7][8] He was named First Team All-County and First Team All-Conference in 2010.[9][10] He also earned various All-State honors in 2010.[11][12][13] In 2010, he recorded 11 receptions, 195 receiving yards, seven receiving touchdowns, 56 tackles and 14 sacks.[14] He was also a two-year letterman in track and basketball.[15]

He graduated from Summit High School in 2011.[16]


College career

O'Malley played tight end for the Penn Quakers of the University of Pennsylvania from 2011 to 2015. He did not appear in any games in 2011.[17]

He played in ten games in 2012, recording six receptions for 93 yards and two touchdowns. He played in ten games, in 2013, catching 10 passes for 93 yards and two touchdowns.[18]

In 2014, he was named to the Preseason College Football Performance Awards Tight End Watch List.[19][20] He appeared in ten games, recording seven receptions for 103 yards and two touchdowns. He garnered Second Team All-Ivy accolades.[21]

He played in ten games in 2015, catching 20 passes for 202 yards and three touchdowns.[22] He was named Second Team All-Ivy and was also a team captain.[23][24][25]

O'Malley played in 40 career games, recording 43 receptions for 495 yards and nine touchdowns.[26][27]


Professional career

O'Malley was rated the 15th best tight end in the 2016 NFL Draft by NFLDraftScout.com.[28]

Oakland Raiders

O'Malley signed with the Oakland Raiders on May 10, 2016, after going undrafted in the 2016 NFL Draft.[29][30] He was released on September 3 and signed to the team's practice squad the next day.[31][32] He was promoted to the active roster on October 5.[33] He made his NFL debut on October 9, 2016, against the San Diego Chargers.[34] He was released by the Raiders on November 26, 2016, and was re-signed to the practice squad on November 29.[35][36] He signed a reserve/future contract with the Raiders on January 9, 2017.[37] On September 2, 2017, he was waived by the Raiders.[38]

Buffalo Bills

On October 10, 2017, O'Malley was signed to the Buffalo Bills' practice squad.[39] He was released on November 1, 2017.

New York Giants

On December 12, 2017, O'Malley was signed to the New York Giants' practice squad.[40] He was promoted to the active roster on December 30, 2017.[41]

On April 6, 2018, the Giants re-signed O'Malley.[42] He was waived on September 1, 2018.[43]

Atlanta Legends

O'Malley was signed by the Atlanta Legends of the Alliance of American Football, but was waived before the start of the 2019 regular season.
