Old_Vestry_Office,_Enfield_Town_02.jpg Old_Vestry_Office_Enfield_02.jpg The OLD VESTRY OFFICE is a grade II listed building at 22 The Town, Enfield, London. It was built around 1800[1] or 1830[2] as the town beadle's office. It was used as the town police station from 1840 to 1872[3] and contained two prison cells.[4]
