RICHARD E. WATERS[1] (born 18 May 1945) is an English former footballer who played as a goalkeeper in the Football League for Darlington, and in non-league football for Blyth Spartans and Gateshead.[2][3] As a 19-year-old amateur standing in for Ray Snowball, Waters played twice in the Fourth Division in March 1965, in a 2–1 defeat at home to Torquay United and a 3–0 loss away to Chesterfield.[4]
