IBRAHIM SANGARÉ (born 2 December 1997) is an Ivorian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for French club Toulouse.[1]


Club career

Sangaré debuted for Toulouse in a 0–0 tie with Angers SCO on 22 October 2016.[2]


International career

Sangaré represented Ivory Coast U20 at the 2015 Toulon Tournament, and the Ivory Coast U23s in 2 friendlies.[3]
