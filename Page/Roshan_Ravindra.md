ROSHAN RAVINDRA (Sinhala: රොෂාන් රවින්ද්‍ර) is a Sri Lankan actor and producer. He received the Sumathi Award for Best Actor in 2011,[1] and was a nominee for Best Tele-drama Actor in the 2013 Raigam Tele’es.[2]


Career

Ravindra's first major role in a romantic film was _Aadaraneeya Wassaanaya_ (2004) as Kasun Directed by Senesh Dissanaike Bandara.[3] He later played Saradiyal in the teledrama _Uthuwan Kande Sura Saradiyal_Directed by Sudath Rohana. His first project as a television producer was the teledrama _Rasthiyadukaraya_(රස්තියාදුකාරයා) Directed by Lakmini Amaradewa,[4] followed by _Kadadora_ (කඩදොර) Directed by Rajiv Ananda.[5]


Personal life

Ravindra is married to Sanjeewani, and they have a son Nethruu and daughter Kethaki.[6] He is a disciple of Sri Lankan actor Mahendra Perera.[7]
