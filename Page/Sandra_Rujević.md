SANDRA RUJEVIĆ (born 29 June 1990 as _Sandra Halilović_) is a Serbian badminton player.[1][2] She was the women's doubles national championships from 2010-2013 and in the mixed doubles in 2010.[3] In 2013, she won the international tournament in the women's doubles event partnered with Elme de Villiers of South Africa at the Botswana and South Africa.[4][5] At the Balkan Championships, she won the bronze medal in the mixed team event.[6]
