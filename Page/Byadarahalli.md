Mandya._Byadara_Halli_RS.jpg BYADARA HALLI is a village in Mandya district of Karnataka state, India.


Location

Byadara Halli is located between Mysore and Bangalore cities.[1]


Access

Byadarahalli village has a railway station where only slow trains stop. Buses are available from Mandya town.


Post office

There is a post office in the village and the pincode is 571602.[2]
