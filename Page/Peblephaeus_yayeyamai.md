_PEBLEPHAEUS YAYEYAMAI_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Stephan von Breuning in 1955, originally under the genus _Blepephaeus_.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Peblephaeus yayeyamai caesius_ Takakuwa, 1984
-   _Peblephaeus yayeyamai yayeyamai_ (Breuning, 1955)
