CALLUM CASEY (born 6 June 1990) is an Irish rugby league footballer who plays for the Hunslet Hawks in the Kingstone Press Championship One competition. He plays as a , stand off or loose forward.[1]

Casey is an Irish international.[2]

Casey has previously played for the Hunslet Hawks, Halifax and the Batley Bulldogs.[3][4] He came through the academy system at the Leeds Rhinos and spent time on loan at Oldham in 2011.[5] He played on loan at Oxford in 2016.[6]

He is the son of the former Great Britain Leo Casey.[7]
