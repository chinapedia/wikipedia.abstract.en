MOARK is an extinct town in Dunklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. The GNIS classifies it as a populated place.[1]

A post office called Moark was established in 1900, and remained in operation until 1905.[2] The community derives its name from the Missouri-Arkansas Lumber Company.[3]
