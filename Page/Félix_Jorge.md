Career

Minnesota Twins

Jorge signed with the Minnesota Twins as an international free agent in February 2011.[1] He made his professional debut that season with the Dominican Summer League Twins. He pitched 2012 with the Gulf Coast Twins, 2013 with the Elizabethton Twins, 2014 with Elizabethton and Cedar Rapids Kernals and 2015 with Cedar Rapids.[2][3] Jorge pitched 2016 with the Fort Myers Miracle and Chattanooga Lookouts.[4] The Twins added him to their 40-man roster after the season.[5] He was released by the organization on July 4. He resigned a minor league deal the next day but became a free agent after the 2018 season.[6]

Cincinnati Reds

On January 10, 2019, the Cincinnati Reds signed Jorge to a minor league deal with an invite to Spring Training.
