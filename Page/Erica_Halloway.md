ERICA HALLOWAY (born 4 November 1984) is an Australian association football player, who currently plays for Western Sydney Wanderers in the Australian W-League.


Early life

Born in Dubbo, a city located in the Orana region of New South Wales, Australia, Halloway played for the Bathurst City Panthers. In 2002, she earned Women’s Super League player of the year honors.[1]


Playing career

Halloway signed with Western Sydney Wanderers in 2015.[2][3] In her 11 appearances with the team, she scored two goals.[4] The team finished the regular season in seventh place with a record.[5] Halloway was awarded W-League Player of the Year honors following her rookie season.[6][7]
