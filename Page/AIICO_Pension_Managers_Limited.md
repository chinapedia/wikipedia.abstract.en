AIICO PENSION MANAGERS LIMITED is a pension funds management company in Nigeria. It is a 65 percent owned subsidiary of _AIICO Insurance_, a Nigeria Stock Exchange listed insurance services provider.[1][2]

, the company's total assets were NGN:1,337,546,000, with shareholders' funds of NGN:1,275,178,000.[3] As at September 2016, the company had over 200,000 registered clients and has in excess of NGN:60 billion under management.[4]


History

AIICO Pension Managers Limited was founded in February 2005. In April 2006 the company was awarded a licence to operate as a _pension funds administrator_ (PFA).[5] In August 2016, to mark 10 years in business, the company appointed Nollywood actress Joke Silva as its brand ambassador, to promote the company services and products.[6][7][8]
