Naval career

Promoted to captain on 31 December 1905,[1] Greatorex was given command of the protected cruiser HMS _Eclipse_ in January 1906 and the armoured cruiser HMS _Natal_ in June 1911. He was appointed Director of Naval Equipment at the Admiralty from 14 January, 1915 to 8 October, 1917. Promoted to the rank of Rear-Admiral he was then appointed Flag Officer, Shetlands in October 1917. During the First World War.[2]
