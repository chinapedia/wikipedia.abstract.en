SAM GEE (born 28 February 1987) is a former rugby league footballer who last played for Oldham. His position was hooker or loose forward.[1]


Early

Gee was born in Wigan, Greater Manchester, England.


Career

Gee also played for the London Skolars[2] and Whitehaven.[3]During a seven year professional career.

He is the grandson of former Wigan prop Ken Gee.[4]
