CAMDEN NATIONAL BANK is a bank headquartered in Camden, Maine, with 61 branches, all of which are in Maine. It is a subsidiary of Camden National Corporation, a bank holding company. It is the largest bank headquartered in Maine.[1]

The bank was founded in 1875.[2]
