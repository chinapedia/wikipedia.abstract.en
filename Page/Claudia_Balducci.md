CLAUDIA BALDUCCI is an American politician from the state of Washington. She serves on the King County Council, representing District 6. She was previously the mayor of Bellevue, Washington.

Balducci was elected to the Bellevue City Council in 2004, and as mayor in 2014. She defeated six-term incumbent Jane Hague in the 2015 election to the King County Council.[1][2]

In 2009, Balducci and her husband adopted a boy, Victor, from Kazakhstan.[3]
