JUNAID DAWOOD (born 2 October 1996) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his Twenty20 debut for Eastern Province against KwaZulu-Natal in the 2016 Africa T20 Cup on 25 September 2016.[2] He made his first-class debut for Eastern Province in the 2016–17 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 20 October 2016.[3] He made his List A debut for Eastern Province in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 15 January 2017.[4]

In July 2018, he was named in the Cricket South Africa Emerging Squad.[5]
