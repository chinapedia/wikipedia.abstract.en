HOLD ME TIGHT () is a 2010 Danish drama film directed by Kaspar Munk. It was the director's first film.[1]

Critical reception

Variety said the film "is a classic example of what happens when bad scripts tackle good subjects."[2] The film has a few powerful scenes smothered by stereotypes along with "one of the most spectacularly misconceived endings in memory."[3]
