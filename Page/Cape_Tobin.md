CAPE TOBIN (, "that which one burns oneself on"[1]) is a cape located in the northeast of Sermersooq, Greenland. It is located near the settlement of Ittoqqortoormiit, where short trips and long hauls provide access to the cape.[2] Cape Tobin is located opposite Cape Brewster (Kangikajik).
