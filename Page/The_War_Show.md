_THE WAR SHOW_ is a 2016 Syrian-Danish-German documentary film co-written and directed by Andreas Dalsgaard and Obaidah Zytoon. It was awarded best film in the Venice Days section at the 73rd edition of the Venice Film Festival.[1]

The film recounts the Arab Spring days in Syria and the subsequent civil war though the eyes of a Syrian former radio DJ.[2][3]
