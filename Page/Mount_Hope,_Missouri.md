MOUNT HOPE (also spelled (MOUNTHOPE) is an unincorporated community in Franklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Mounthope was established in 1903, and remained in operation until 1909.[2] The community was named after a nearby mine of the same name.[3]
