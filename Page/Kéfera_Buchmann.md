KÉFERA BUCHMANN DE MATTOS JOHNSON PEREIRA (born January 25, 1993) is a Brazilian actress, vlogger and writer. She is best known for her YouTube channel _5INCO MINUTOS_, which was the first female lead channel of Brazil to reach one million subscribers.[1] In 2016 _Forbes_ named her one of the most promising young women in Brazil.[2]

She currently has more than 11 million subscribers on her YouTube channel _5inco Minutos_, making her one of the most famous internet personalities in Brazil.[3]


Biography

Kéfera was born and raised in Curitiba, where she completed her primary and secondary education. She was raised in a Catholic family.[4] Buchmann is the daughter of Zeiva (Zeivanez) Buchmann, who works in advertising.[5] After kindergarten, she completed her education in a Catholic school,[6] the same which her mother's studied.[7] In high school she was torn between studying dentistry, psychology or chemical engineering, but ended up not majoring in any of these areas.[8]

She studied theater for five years.[9] Kéfera got her actress record in 2013, earning the highest possible score.[10] Her name, "Kéfera", is of Egyptian origin and means "first morning sunshine".[11] During her high school years, Kéfera was very devoted to St. Anthony (a Catholic Saint).[12] Kéfera is bilingual, being fluent in English in addition to her native language Portuguese. She has also worked as an English teacher.[13]

Kéfera suffered bullying when she was younger.
