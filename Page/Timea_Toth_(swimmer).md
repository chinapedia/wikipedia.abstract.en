TIMEA TOTH (טימאה טוט; born February 2, 1968) is an Israeli former Olympic swimmer.[1]

She is a former member of the Romanian national team, and immigrated to Israel from Romania in 1990.[2][3]

Toth competed for Israel at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain, at the age of 24.[4] In the Women's 200 metre Butterfly she came in 20th with a time of 2:16.84, and in the Women's 100 metre Butterfly she came in tied for 29th with a time of 1:03.18.[5]
