SIMONAS PAULIUS (born 12 May 1991) is a Lithuanian footballer who plays as a Midfielder for Widzew Łódź.


Club career

On 1 July 2012 Paulius moved to FK Ventspils.

In 2018 at first half of season played for FK Kauno Žalgiris. In second half for Widzew Łódź. (Currently)
