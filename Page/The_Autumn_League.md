THE AUTUMN LEAGUE is a one-man project that originated in 2012 in Sydney, Australia. The project is signed to Rottweiler Records and SkyBurnsBlack Records.


Background

The Autumn League started as a project in 2012 with founder PJ Somers aka Matt Hudson.[1] The Autumn League has a debut album, _Concept of Irony_[2] and a single, "Lament".[3]
