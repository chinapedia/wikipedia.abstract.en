NATALIE ROSER is an Australian fashion model.[1][2][3]


Life and career

Born in Newcastle, New South Wales in March 18, 1990, Roser started modelling from the age of 13.[4] She currently lives in Melbourne.[5] She was Fox8's face of the year in 2014. She was finalist in the Miss Universe Australia,[6][7][8] as well as a contestant on the first season of The Face Australia, where she was the first eliminated, finishing in twelfth place. After this, she announced the launch her clothing company.[9] She became the Maxim cover girl for October 2016.[10]
