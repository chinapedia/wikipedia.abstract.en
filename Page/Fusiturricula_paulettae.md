_FUSITURRICULA PAULETTAE_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]

According to Gastropods.com it is a synonym of _Fusiturricula jaquensis_ (Sowerby, G.B. II, 1850),[2] but it belongs to _Fusiturricula_ according to B. Landau and C. Marques da Silva 2010


Description

The length of an adult shell attains 55 mm ; its diameter 19.9 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs in the Gulf of Venezuela.
