MELANIE LIBURD is a British actress known for her roles in _Dark Matter_ and _The Grinder_.[1]

In 2016, Liburd appeared as a red priestess in "No One", an episode of the sixth season of the HBO series _Game of Thrones_.[2] She was cast in the Netflix psychological thriller television series _Gypsy_ in late 2016.[3]

Since 2018, Liburd has portrayed Zoe in _This Is Us_.
