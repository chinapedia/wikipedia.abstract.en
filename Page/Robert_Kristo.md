ROBERT KRISTO (born 14 May 1993) is a footballer who plays as a forward for North Carolina FC in the USL Championship.


Early life

Kristo was born to Croatian parents in Bosnia and Herzegovina. He is a practicing Catholic. He grew up in St. Louis, Missouri and attended Saint Louis University.[1]


Professional

Kristo joined USL Championship side North Carolina FC on 23 January 2019.[2]
