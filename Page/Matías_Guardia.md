MATÍAS JOSÉ GUARDIA TORRES (born March 5, 1991 in Mendoza, Argentina) is an Argentine association footballer who currently plays for Iberia in the Primera B de Chile.


Teams

-   Independiente Rivadavia 2010-2012

-   Gutiérrez SC 2012-2013

-   Leonardo Murialdo 2013-2014

-   Huracán Las Heras 2014-2015

-   Unión La Calera 2015

-   Huracán Las Heras 2016

-   Iberia 2016–present
