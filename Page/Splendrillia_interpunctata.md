_SPLENDRILLIA INTERPUNCTATA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 7 mm and 16.5 mm.


Distribution

This species occurs in the Caribbean Sea.
