JULANI ARCHIBALD is a footballer who plays as goalkeeper for W Connection F.C. and the Saint Kitts and Nevis national football team.


Career

In the second edition(2015 edition) of the MLS Caribbean Combine, Archibald was mentioned in the footballers invited as one of two goalkeepers in the 19-player list and was the one of two players from Saint Kitts and Nevis in the 2015 version.[1] [2]

The goalkeeper made the winning save to prevent domestic rivals Central F.C. in a dramatic penalty-shoot-out; accordingly, W Connection F.C. made the final of the 2015 Trinidad and Tobago Classic otherwise known as the TOYOTA Classic.[3]


International

Was the No. 8 goalkeeper for the 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification stages opposing the Turks and Caicos Islands national football team and a few other games onwards regarding 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification.[4]

Played as goalkeeper in two international friendlies including match against the Andorra national football team as St. Kitts and Nevis registered an unprecedented win against a European adversary.[5]
