CHILKIGARH is a village in Jamboni CD Block in Jhargram subdivision of Jhargram district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Geography

Chilkigarh_Rajbari.jpg Chilkigarh is generally known as a village in Jamboni CD Block in Jhargram district.It is located around 14km away from Jhargram.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Chilkigarh had a total population of 1,553 of which 794 (51%) were males and 759 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 178. The total number of literates in Chilkigarh was 980 (63.10% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Transport

SH 9 from Durgapur (in Paschim Bardhaman district) to Nayagram (in Jhargram district) passes through Chilkigarh.[2]
