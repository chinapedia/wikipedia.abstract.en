AHMED AL-SHAMRANI (; born 24 April 1994) is a Saudi Arabian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Al-Hazem .[1]


Club Career

On 30 July 2017, Al-Shamrani signed for Al-Wehda.[2] He helped the club achieve promotion to the Saudi Professional League in his first season.
