GEORGINA GARCIA RIEDEL is an American filmmaker and scriptwriter.


Early life

Riedel was born and raised in Arizona, a first generation American with Mexican parents.[1]

She attended the American Film Institute where she graduate with an MFA in directing.


Career

Riedel made her debut feature film in 2005 with the film _How the Garcia Girls Spent Their Summer_ starring America Ferrera. The film was financed entirely by her parents and took over a year to edit as Riedel could not raise additional funds to finish the film.[2] The film premiered In Competition at the 2005 Sundance Film Festival but was not released until 2008.

In 2015 Riedel released her second feature film, _Ana Maria in Novela Land_ starring Edy Ganem.

She directed the Chapter Thirty-Eight episode of _Jane the Virgin._
