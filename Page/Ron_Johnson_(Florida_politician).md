RONALD CLYDE JOHNSON (born September 29, 1949) is a former American politician in the state of Florida.

Johnson was born in Alabama and came to Florida in 1952. He is a business and public relations consultant.[1] He served in the Florida House of Representatives for the 8th district from 1978 to 1990, as a Democrat.[2]
