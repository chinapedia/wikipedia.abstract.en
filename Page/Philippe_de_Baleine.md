PHILIPPE DE BALEINE (27 September 1921 – 7 June 2018), also known as PHILIPPE DE JONAS,[1] was a French author.[2] He is the recipient of two literary prizes from the Académie française: the Prix J.-J. Weiss for _Hôtel des Piranhas_ in 1984, and the Prix de Littérature générale for _Voyage espiègle et romanesque sur le petit train du Congo_ in 1992.[3] He died in June 2018 at the age of 96.[4]
