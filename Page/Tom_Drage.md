THOMAS B. "TOM" DRAGE JR (born December 19, 1948) was an American politician in the state of Florida.

Drage was born in Fort Myers and is an attorney.[1] He served in the Florida House of Representatives for the 47th district from 1980 to 1990, as a Republican.[2]
