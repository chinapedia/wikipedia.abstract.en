AMIRABAD JANAKALYAN HIGH SCHOOL is a secondary school in Amirabad, Lohagara Upazila, Chittagong District, Bangladesh.[1] It was established in 1970.


Campus

It has four academic buildings & one hostel. Amirabad_Janakalyan_High_School.jpg


Academics

It has three groups,these are :

-   Science
-   Business Studies
-   Humanities
