JOHN SHAW was a professional rugby league footballer who played in the 1910s. He played at club level for Warrington (Heritage № 204), as a , i.e. number 3 or 4.[1]


Playing career

Club career

John Shaw made his début for Warrington in the heavy defeat by Hull F.C. at The Boulevard, Hull on 17 January 1914.[2]
