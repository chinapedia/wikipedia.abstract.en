_CREMNOSTERNA PLAGIATA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by White in 1858. It is known from Myanmar, Malaysia, India, and Thailand.[1]


Varietas

-   _Cremnosterna plagiata var. maculicornis_ (Thomson, 1865)
-   _Cremnosterna plagiata var. reducta_ Breuning, 1943
