KARAN SHINDE (born 19 September 1997) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his Twenty20 debut for Andhra in the 2015–16 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy on 4 January 2016.[2] He made his first-class debut for Andhra in the 2018–19 Ranji Trophy on 7 January 2019.[3]
