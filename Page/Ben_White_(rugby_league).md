BEN WHITE (born 27 October 1994) is an English rugby league footballer who plays as a scrum-half or stand-off for Halifax in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

White was born in Hounslow, London, England


Playing career

White is an England Youth international.[2] He was in the Leeds Rhinos Academy system and played his junior rugby league with the Saddleworth Rangers club.[3][4]
