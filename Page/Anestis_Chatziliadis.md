ANESTIS CHATZILIADIS (; born 17 February 1991 in Thessaloniki) is a Greek footballer who plays as a defender for Irodotos.


Career

On 7 September 2016, Chatziliadis signed with Lokomotiv Sofia[1] but was released in June 2017.[2]

In August 2017, Chatziliadis signed with Agrotikos Asteras.[3]
