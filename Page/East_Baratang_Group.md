EAST BARATANG GROUP are an island group of the Andaman Islands, located east of Baratang Island.


Geography

major islands in the group: Long, Strait, North Passage, Colebrooke, Guitar [1]


Administration

Politically, the East Baratang Islands are part of Rangat Taluk.[2]
