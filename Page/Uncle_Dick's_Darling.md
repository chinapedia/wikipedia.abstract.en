_UNCLE DICK'S DARLING_ is a 1920 British silent comedy film directed by Fred Paul and starring George Bellamy, Athalie Davis and Humberston Wright.[1]


Cast

-   George Bellamy as Uncle Dick
-   Athalie Davis as Mary
-   Humberston Wright as Chevenix
-   Ronald Power as Mr. Lorimer
-   Sydney Folker
-   Frank Dane
-   Gordon Craig
