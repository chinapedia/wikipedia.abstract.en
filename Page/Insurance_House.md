INSURANCE HOUSE PSC (IH) () is a Public Stock Company incorporated in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates known for offering Insurance products and services.[1][2][3]


History

Insurance House was established in 2011, as part of the Finance House P.J.S.C group of companies with its headquarters located in Abu Dhabi and its branches in Dubai and Sharjah.[4] The company commenced its operations with a paid up capital totaling AED 120 Million[5] and launched the first successful IPO the UAE markets have seen in about two years after the global economic downturn and Dubai's debt crisis.[6][7][8]

Insurance House is currently listed on Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange(ADX) with its shares being publicly traded under the ticker symbol, IH.[9]


Products and services

Insurance House offers Vehicle insurance, Health Insurance, Property insurance and other insurance instruments tailored to suit the Energy, Marine, Engineering and Aviation sectors.[10][11][12][13]
