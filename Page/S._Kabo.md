S. KABO ( - in Watutau), was the fourth regent of Poso Regency, Central Sulawesi, Indonesia; who ruled from 1957 to 1959. He is the third regent who has no military background.[1]

He graduated from Poso's Holland Inlandsche School and OSVIA in Makassar, South Sulawesi.[2] In September 1945, S. Kabo appointed as Head of Government of Poso Regency. He died on June 26, 1982 in Wanga.[3]
