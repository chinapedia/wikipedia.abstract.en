BEN ADDISON (born 8 January 1985 in Scotland) is a former Scotland Sevens international rugby union player and now coach who played fullback and on the wing for Glasgow Warriors.

At university Addison was studying to be a physiotherapist.[1]


Amateur career

Addison came through the age grades at amateur provincial level playing with Caledonia at U16, U18 and U19[2]

He played for Stirling County.[3][4] He played in the British & Irish Cup for County.[5]

He then moved to Glasgow Hutchesons Aloysians in 2015.[6][7]


Professional career

Addison played for Glasgow Warriors in 2005-06 season.

He started the match against Ayr.[8]

He also started on the bench in the Warriors match against Edinburgh Rugby that same season.[9]

He broke into Glasgow's academy squad for season 2006-07.[10] He played in the Warriors match against the Scotland U20 side.[11]


International career

Addison came through the age grades for Scotland. He played for Scotland U19[12][13] and at Scotland U21[14][15][16]

He was then capped by Scotland Sevens.[17][18]


Coaching career

In 2014 he became an assistant coach at Stirling County.[19]

In 2015 he joined Glasgow Hutchesons Aloysians as a player-coach.[20]

In 2016 he was made a backs coach at Glasgow Hutchesons Aloysians.[21]
