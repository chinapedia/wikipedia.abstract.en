_ZEPHYRARCHAEA BARRETTAE_ is a species of spider of the family Archaeidae.[1] The Latin species name was chosen to honor Sarah Barrett, who first discovered assassin spiders in the Stirling Range National Park.[2]


Distribution and habitat

_Zephyrarchaea barrettae_ is endemic to the South West Region in Western Australia.[3] It has only been found on Talyuberlup Peak.[4]
