_AGELASTA KONOI_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Masao Hayashi in 1956.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Agelasta konoi amamiana_ (Hayashi, 1962)
-   _Agelasta konoi konoi_ (Hayashi, 1962)
-   _Agelasta konoi kumejimana_ (Kusama & Takakuwa, 1984)
-   _Agelasta konoi okinawana_ (Hayashi, 1960)
-   _Agelasta konoi okinoerabuensis_ (Ohbayashi, 1959)
