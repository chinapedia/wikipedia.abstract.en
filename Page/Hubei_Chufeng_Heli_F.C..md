HUBEI CHUFENG HELI FOOTBALL CLUB () is an amateur Chinese football club that currently participates in the China League Two. The team is based in Wuhan, Hubei.


History

Wuhan Chufeng Heli F.C. was founded on 8 July 2016 combined by amateur club Wuhan New Era and unpunished players from Wuhan Hongxing after the Wuhan Hongxing–Jiangsu Suning brawl.[1] They played in the 2016 China Amateur Football League and won the winners of 2016 Wuhan City Super League and the first place of northwest region final–group B successively.[2] Wuhan Chufeng Heli finished third place in the national finals and won the qualification for promoting to 2017 China League Two.[3] However, they gave up to turn professional and remained at the amateur league.
