_' Walkossam_' is a village in the commune of Tignère, in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon.


Population

In 1967, Walkossam comptait 70 inhabitants, mainly Kutin.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 885 people in the village.[2]
