"LUCIEN" BAINBRIDGE CRIST (Feb 13, 1883-Feb 7, 1969) was an American composer, conductor, and teacher.[1]

Crist created 186 works in 260 publications in three languages and 1,025 library holdings.[2]

Crist's work, such as "_Egyptian Impressions",_ suite (first performed in America at the Boston Pops, June 22, 1915)[3] and his popular "Chinese Mother Goose Rhymes"[4] were well received and widely known in America.
