ROBERT CHRICHTON MCBEAN was a Scottish amateur football outside left who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park and Hamilton Academical.[1][2][3]


Personal life

McBean served as a corporal in the Highland Light Infantry during the First World War.[4]
