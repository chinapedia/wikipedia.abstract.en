_CERITHIOPSIDA_ is a genus of sea snails in the family Newtoniellidae.[1]

Some species are also represented in the fossil record.


Species

-   _Cerithiopsida diegensis_ (Bartsch, 1911) (type)
-   _Cerithiopsida echinata_ (Golikov & Gulbin, 1978)
-   _Cerithiopsida elegans_ (Golikov & Gulbin, 1978)
-   _Cerithiopsida lata_ (Golikov & Gulbin, 1978)
-   _Cerithiopsida rowelli_ (Bartsch, 1911)
