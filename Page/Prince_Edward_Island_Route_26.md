ROUTE 26, also known as GEORGETOWN ROAD and POWNAL ROAD, is a , two-lane, uncontrolled-access, secondary highway in central Prince Edward Island. Its western terminus is at Route 1 in Stratford, and its eastern terminus is at Route 1 in Waterford. The route is entirely in Queens County.


Route description

The route begins at its western terminus and heads southeast. It curves east near Tea Hill and continues east until its eastern terminus.
