MOHAMED REDA BOUMECHRA (born June 3, 1997 in Oran) is an Algerian footballer who plays for Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 2 club USM El Harrach. He plays primarily as a defensive midfielder.


Club career

In the summer of 2016, Boumechra signed a four-year contract with USM Alger.[1]
