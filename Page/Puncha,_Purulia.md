PUNCHA is a village, with a police station, in Puncha CD Block in Purulia Sadar East subdivision of Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Puncha had a total population of 5,404 of which 2,744(51%) were males and 2,660 (51%) were females. Population below 6 years was 573. The total number of literates in Puncha was 3,788 (78.41% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Police station

Puncha police station has jurisdiction over parts of Puncha and Manbazar I CD Blocks. The area covered is 330.11 km² and the population covered is 123,768.[2][3]


CD Block HQ

The headquarters of Puncha CD Block are located at Puncha.[4][5]


Transport

Hura-Puncha Road and Puncha-Payrachali Road meet at Puncha.[6]


Healthcare

Puncha Block Primary Health Centre functions with 30 beds.[7]
