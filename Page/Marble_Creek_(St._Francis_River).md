MARBLE CREEK is a stream in Iron and Madison Counties in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the St. Francis River.

The stream headwaters arise in Iron County southwest of Arcadia at and it flows southeast and then east to its confluence with the St. Francis in Madison County just east of French Mills at .[2][3]

Marble Creek was so named because the limestone in the stream bed had the appearance of marble.[4]
