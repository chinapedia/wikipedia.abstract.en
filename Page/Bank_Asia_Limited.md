BANK ASIA LIMITED is a private sector commercial bank in Bangladesh.[1] Mr. Arfan Ali is the President and Managing Director of the bank.[2]


History

The Bank was established in 1999 and incorporated in 1999. it expanded through the purchase of the branches of Bank of Nova Scotia and Muslim Commercial Bank Limited.[3]

_Bank Asia Awarded:_

Bank Asia has been awarded Third Prize in Corporate Governance Disclosures and achieved Certificate of Merit Under Integrated Reporting Category in the 17th ICAB National Award for BPAR-2016.
