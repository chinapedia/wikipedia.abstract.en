VILMA 'RONALYN' GREENLEES (born 1971) is an Philippines international lawn bowler.[1]

She reached the final eight in the pairs at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship but came to prominence when winning a bronze medal at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch in the fours with Hazel Jagonoy, Rosita Bradborn and Sonia Bruce.[2]
