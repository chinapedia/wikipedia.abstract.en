_CHOROMYTILUS CHORUS_, common name CHORUS MUSSEL or CHORO MUSSEL, is a species of mussel, a marine bivalve mollusc in the family Mytilidae.[1]


Description

Shells of _Choromytilus chorus_ can reach a length of 7 inches and a height of 3.5 inches.[2]


Distribution

This species is present in Peru[3] and in Chile.[4][5][6]
