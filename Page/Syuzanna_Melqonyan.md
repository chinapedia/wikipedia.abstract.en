SYUZANNA MELQONYAN (, born 1993), is an Armenian singer. She took part in _Hay Superstar_, _The Voice of Armenia_,[1] _X-Factor_[2] and _Depi Evratesil_. During _Depi Evratesil_ she was in the group of Inga Arshakyan and advanced to the top 3.[3] On July 30, she and the _Freedom_ band were the special guests of Arena Live hosted by Garik Papoyan.[4] On May 5, she published her first music video "I found love" via her YouTube account,[5] which was written by Ellen Nahapetyan. In 2017, Melkonyan represented Armenia along with Erna Mir at the New Wave international contest in Sochi and gained the second place. She also took part in the competition called _1 in 360_, a national final to select San Marino's entry for the Eurovision Song Contest 2018.[6][7] The singer is a Grand Prix winner of various contests and festivals since 2011.[8]
