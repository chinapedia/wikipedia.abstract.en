'''Mark Stewart ''' (born 25 August 1995) is a Scottish male road and track cyclist, representing Great Britain and Scotland at international competitions. He won the bronze medal at the 2016 UEC European Track Championships in the team pursuit.[1] He was announced as part of the team's squad for the 2017 season.[2] In November 2018 it was announced that he would ride for Ribble Pro Cycling in the 2019 season.[3]
