CHRISTOPH KOSCHEL (born 9 April 1976) is a German dressage rider.[1] He competed at the 2010 World Equestrian Games and at the 2011 European Dressage Championship. At both of the occasions he won a medal in team competition (bronze in 2010 and silver in 2011). Meanwhile, his current best individual championship result is 6th place in freestyle competition from the 2010 World Games.
