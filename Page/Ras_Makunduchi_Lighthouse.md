The RAS MAKUNDUCHI LIGHTHOUSE is located at the south eastern tip of Ugunja island in Zanzibar, Tanzania.[1] The lighthouse is located near the village of Makunduchi The lighthouse is a 27m squared concrete tower painted red and white. The tower has 165 steps to the top and has two non resident operators.[2] Since the lighthouse is in Zanzibar, it is managed by the Zanzibar Ports Corporation instead of the Tanzania Ports Authority.[3]
