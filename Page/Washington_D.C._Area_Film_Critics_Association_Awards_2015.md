Multiple nominations and awards

These films had multiple nominations:

-   7 nominations: _Carol_ and _Mad Max: Fury Road_
-   6 nominations: _Brooklyn_ and _The Revenant_
-   5 nominations: _Steve Jobs_
-   4 nominations: _The Martian_ and _Sicario_
-   3 nominations: _Ex Machina_, _The Hateful Eight_, _Room_, and _Spotlight_
-   2 nominations: _Bridge of Spies_, _The Danish Girl_, and _Inside Out_

The following films received multiple awards:

-   3 wins: _Mad Max: Fury Road_
-   2 wins: _Inside Out_, _The Revenant_, _Room_, and _Spotlight_
