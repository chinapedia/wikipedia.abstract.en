KEREN REGAL (קרן רגאל; born January 23, 1977) is an Israeli former Olympic swimmer.[1]

She competed for Israel at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain, at the age of 15.[2] In the Women's 400 metres Individual Medley she came in 29th with a time of 5:07.97, in the Women's 200 metres Individual Medley she came in 37th with a time of 2:27.85, and in the Women's 50 metres Freestyle she came in 41st with a time of 27.93.[3] As of 2012, she was the youngest Olympic participant ever for Israel.[4]
