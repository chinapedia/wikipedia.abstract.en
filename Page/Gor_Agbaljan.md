GOR AGBALJAN (born 25 April 1997) is an Armenian professional footballer who plays for Dutch club Go Ahead Eagles, as a midfielder.


Career

Born in Talış, Agbaljan has played club football for Heracles Almelo and Go Ahead Eagles.[1]

He has represented Armenia at under-19 and under-21 youth international levels.[2]
