'''Domingo de Salinas, O.P. ''' (1547–1600) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Coro (1597–1600).[1][2]


Biography

Domingo de Salinas was born in Spain in 1547 and ordained a priest in the Order of Preachers.[3] On 10 November 1597, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VIII as Bishop of Coro.[4][5] In 1598, he was consecrated bishop.[6] He served as Bishop of Coro until his death on 10 June 1600.[7][8]
