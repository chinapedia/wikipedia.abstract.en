BARIŞ-FAHRI ATIK (born 9 January 1995) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Dynamo Dresden.[1]


International career

Atik was born in Germany to parents of Turkish descent. He is a youth international for Turkey at the U18 and 19 levels.[2]
