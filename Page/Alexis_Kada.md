ALEXIS YOUGOUDA KADA (born 29 November 1994) is a Cameroonian international footballer who last played for Swedish club Örgryte, as a midfielder.


Career

Born in Mokolo, Kada has played club football for Coton Sport, Nantes B and Örgryte.[1][2]

He made his international debut for Cameroon in 2013.[3]
