_CELEBRITY BROMANCE_ () is a South Korean variety show broadcast on MBig TV , available to mobile users. "Celebrity Bromance" portrays star friendships, and is filmed the "paparazzi - style" from a distance to allow the cast members to feel less overwhelmed by cameras. The episodes are available on Naver TVCast and YouTube on every Tuesday on 11 PM KST.

The program premiered on February 4, 2016, with the singer and actor V and actor Kim Min-jae .[1][2]
