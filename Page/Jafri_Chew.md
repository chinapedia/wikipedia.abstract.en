MOHD JAFRI FIRDAUS CHEW (born 11 June 1997) is a Malaysian footballer who plays as a striker for Penang FA.

He started his footballing career with Harimau Muda B in 2013.[1] On 25 November 2015,it was confirmed that the Harimau Muda has disbanded by FAM which means all the player from Harimau Muda A,Harimau Muda B and Harimau Muda C will be returned to their own state.[2] He also has been call by Frank Bernhardt, the Malaysian Under-19 National Coach for preparation of the 2016 U19 AFF Championship. The tournament was held in Hanoi, Vietnam from 11 to 24 September.[3]
