_KURDALAGONUS_ is a genus of cetotheriid mysticete in the subfamily Cetotheriinae from the Miocene of the Russian Caucasus.[1]

_"Kurdalagonus" adygeicus_ has been reassigned to _Mithridatocetus_, while the referral of _"Cetotherium" maicopicum_ to _Kurdalagonus_ has been questioned.[2]
