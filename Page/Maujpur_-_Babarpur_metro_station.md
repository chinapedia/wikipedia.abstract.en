Maujpur_-_Babarpur_Metro_Station.png The MAUJPUR - BABARPUR Metro Station is located on the Pink Line of the Delhi Metro.

As part of Phase III of Delhi Metro, Maujpur - Babarpur is metro station of the Pink Line.[1]


Station layout

Interchanging station for pink line and new line from maujpur to mukundpur. 2 level elevated station 4 platforms.
