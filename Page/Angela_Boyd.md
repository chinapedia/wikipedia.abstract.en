ANGELA BOYD (born 1986) is a New Zealand international lawn bowler.[1]


Bowls career

Angela's sister Mandy Boyd is also an international bowler.[2]

Boyd has won four national titles. In 2016, she won a bronze medal in the fours at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch with Katelyn Inch, Val Smith and Kirsten Edwards and a silver medal with Jo Edwards in the pairs.[3]

In addition to her international success she has won the 2014/15 & 2015/16 pairs title and four 'fours' titles at the New Zealand National Bowls Championships when bowling for the Burnside Bowls Club.[4]
