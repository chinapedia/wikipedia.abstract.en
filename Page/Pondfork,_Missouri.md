PONDFORK is an unincorporated community in northwest Ozark County, Missouri.[1] Pondfork lies on the Hay Hollow tributary to Pond Fork Creek which lies to the west. The community is about three miles WSW of Thornfield. Access is via Missouri Route D from Missouri Route 95 to the southeast. Pondfork lies within the Mark Twain National Forest.[2][3]


History

A post office called Pondfork was established in 1907, and remained in operation until 1955.[4] The community takes its name from nearby Pond Fork creek.[5]
