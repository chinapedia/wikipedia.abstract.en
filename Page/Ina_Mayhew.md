INA MAYHEW is a New York-based production designer known for her work with directors Spike Lee[1] and Tyler Perry.[2] She studied fine arts at SUNY Purchase and traveled in Africa before completing a technical theater degree at the Parsons School of Design.[3] She was among the 683 individuals invited to join the Motion Picture Academy of Arts and Sciences in 2016.[4]
