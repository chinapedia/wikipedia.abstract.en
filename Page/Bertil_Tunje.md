BERTIL TUNJE served as the Vice-Chairman of the World Scout Committee.

In 1996, Tunje was awarded the 255th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1] He was also the recipient of the Silver Wolf, Sweden's highest Scout award.
