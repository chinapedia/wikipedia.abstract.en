DORIAN WEBER (born May 15, 1982 in Manhasset, New York) is an American rower, Paralympic silver medallist and world bronze medalist.[1] Weber has competed in the 2013 world rowing championships in the Lwt Men's 8, 2016 Rio Paralympic Games and the 2012 London Paralympic Games. Dorian Weber is the only athlete to have medaled in rowing as both an able bodied and Para athlete at the senior level.
