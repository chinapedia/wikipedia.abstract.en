ANGUS MCCUISH was a Scottish amateur football inside left who appeared in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

McCuish served as a lieutenant in the 2nd Queen Victoria's Own Rajput Light Infantry during the First World War.[2]
