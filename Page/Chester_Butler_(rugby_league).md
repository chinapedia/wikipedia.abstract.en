CHESTER BUTLER (born ) is a Wales international rugby league footballer who plays as a for Halifax in the Betfred Championship.[1][2][3]

Butler was named in the Wales squad for the 2017 Rugby League World Cup.[4]

Butler's father Peter Butler was a professional footballer,[5] and his maternal grandfather, Colin Dixon, was a Welsh international rugby league player. On 20 May 2019 Chester signed for the Huddersfield Giants.[6]
