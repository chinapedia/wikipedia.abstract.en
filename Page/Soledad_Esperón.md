SOLEDAD ESPERÓN (; born 8 February 1985) is a retired professional Argentine female tennis player.

Esperón has won 10 singles and 25 doubles titles on the ITF circuit in her career. Her highest WTA singles ranking is 166, which she reached on 23 March 2009. Her career high in doubles is 139, which she reached on 2 October 2006.

Esperón made her WTA main draw debut at the 2009 Copa Sony Ericsson Colsanitas in the doubles event partnering Tamaryn Hendler.

Partnering Monique Adamczak, Esperón won her first $75,000 ITF tournament in April 2006 at the Hardee's Pro Classic, defeating Edina Gallovits and Varvara Lepchenko in the final.
