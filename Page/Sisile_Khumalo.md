SISILE KHUMALO was Ndlovukati (Queen mother) of Swaziland during the reign of Dlamini IV.[1] Her son, Ludvonga, died without heirs and was never made king.[2]

Khumalo was influential in the choice of Mbandzeni (later Dlamini IV) as successor to King Mswati II.[3]

Khumalo was executed after quarrelling with Dlamini IV.[4]
