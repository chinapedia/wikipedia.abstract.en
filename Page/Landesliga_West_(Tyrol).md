The LANDESLIGA WEST is, together with the Landesliga Ost,[1] the second-highest division of Tyrol and the fifth-highest division in Austrian football. The champions advance into the Tiroler Liga. In the 2015/16 season, FC Zirl was able to secure the championship.[2]
