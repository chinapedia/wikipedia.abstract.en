SEZEN DJOUMA is a child actress, with her most noticeable credit being Grandpa in My Pocket, a children's television series that currently airs on Cbeebies. Sezen has numerous other television credits, including an appearance on ITV's programme, "Keep It In The Family". Sezen Djouma also has some live theatre credits to her name, including West End Live 2016. Furthermore, Sezen performed at Move It Dance Exhibition's 'Main Stage' with her theatre company. She currently attends Spirit Young Performers Company , they currently boast over 31 million views on their YouTube Channel.
