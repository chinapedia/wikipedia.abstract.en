_EUCALYPTUS NORMANTONENSIS_ is a eucalypt that is native to Western Australia.[1]

The mallee typically grows to a height of and has rough, sub-tessellated bark that is grey on the trunk and grey brown on higher stems and branches. It is found growing in sandy, gravelly or loam-sandy soils among woodlands on flat or undulating areas or near drainage lines in the central Goldfields-Esperance region of Western Australia.
