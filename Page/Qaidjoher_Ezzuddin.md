Shahzada Dr. QAIDJOHER EZZUDDIN is the eldest son of Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin, the grandson of Syedna Taher Saifuddin and the eldest brother of the incumbent Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin.[1] He is one of four the rectors of Al Jamea tus Saifiyah.[2][3]


Personal life

Dr. Qaidjoher Ezzuddin was born on 27 November 1942 (19 Dhu al-Qi'dah 1361 Hijri). He is the eldest son of Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin, thus Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin selected his teknonym Kunya (Arabic) as Abu-al-Qaidjoher (Arabic: ابو القائد جوهر).


Career

Qaidjoher Ezzuddin has served under the administration of Dawat-e-Hadiyah[4] under the Da'i al-Mutlaq for over 50 years and has administered numerous community development and public relation projects.[5] He also heads the legal department of Dawat-e-Hadiyah[6] missions in governing the various trusts and foundations of the Dawoodi Bohra community.[7]

In 1987 (1407 Hijri) he was appointed one of four the rectors of Al Jamea tus Saifiyah.[8][9][10] He also headed the Dawoodi Bohra youth organization Shabab ul-Eidiz Zahabi.[11][12]

He was also a member of the Haj Committee of India from June 1999 to March 2003.[13] He is the Chairman of the Saifee Hospital Trust[14] and the Saifee Burhani Upliftment Trust (SBUT).[15][16]

Dr. Ezzuddin headed the project for the revival and restoration of the Great Mosque of Kufa in 2010, during the tenure of Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin.[17]

He was appointed by Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin as one of the witnesses of the Nass (appointment) on Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin in 2005.[18] In 2011,[19] he was instructed to make a public announcement of this appointment in a Majlis[20] held in al Masjid ul Husseini[21] in Northolt; London on 5 June 2011. The video of this Majlis was recorded and was broadcast on the same day in Dawoodi Bohra community centers worldwide.[22]

On 27 Jumada al-Thani 1440H (4 March 2019), he was appointed in the position of Mukasir by Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin.


Recognition

-   - The Mithaq of Qaidjoher Ezzuddin was taken by his grand father Syedna Taher Saifuddin on the 27th of Dhu al-Qi'dah 1379H (22 May 1960 AD), during the 75th birthday celebrations of Syedna Taher Saifuddin.[23]

-   - The Open International University for Complementary Medicines,[24] Sri Lanka conferred upon him the degree of Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D) (Honoris Causa - Spirituality) in January 2011.[25]

-   - On 23 October 1995 he was presented the Key to the City of Irving, Texas by Dr. Morris Parrish.[26]

-   - He was awarded an Honorary Degree of Doctor of Business Administration from the University of East London on 20 November 2013.[27][28]
