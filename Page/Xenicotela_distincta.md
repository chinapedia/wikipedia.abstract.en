_XENICOTELA DISTINCTA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Charles Joseph Gahan in 1888. It is known from Vietnam, India and Laos. It contains the varietas _Xenicotela distincta var. tonkinensis_.[1][2]
