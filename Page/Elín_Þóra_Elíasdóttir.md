ELÍN ÞÓRA ELÍASDÓTTIR (born 7 June 1992) is an Icelandic badminton player.[1]


Career

Elín made it to the semi finals of Iceland International tournament in 2009 and was the runner-up in 2012, with her partner Helgi Jóhannesson, losing in the finals against Chou Tien-chen and Chiang Mei-hui.[2][3] In 2013, she won Icelandic National Badminton Championships in the women's doubles event with her partner Rakel Jóhannesdótir.[4][5]
