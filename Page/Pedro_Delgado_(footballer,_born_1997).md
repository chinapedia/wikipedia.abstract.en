PEDRO MIGUEL GOMES DELGADO (born 7 April 1997) is a professional footballer who plays for Shandong Luneng, as a midfielder.

Born and raised in Portugal, Delgado gained Chinese citizenship in 2019.[1] However, having represented Portugal at the 2017 FIFA U-20 World Cup, he cannot represent Chinese national teams at any level according to FIFA’s rules.[2]


Football career

On 28 August 2016, Delgado joined Sporting from Inter Milan as part of João Mário's transfer deal.[3] On 11 September 2016, Delgado made his professional debut with Sporting B in a 2016–17 LigaPro match against Varzim.[4]

Delgado joined Chinese Super League side Shandong Luneng Taishan in August 2018.[5] However, he failed to register for the official matches in the 2018 season due to the foreign player limit.[6]
