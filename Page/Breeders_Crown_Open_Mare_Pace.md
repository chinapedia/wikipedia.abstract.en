The BREEDERS CROWN OPEN MARE PACE is a harness racing event for four-year-old and older Standardbred mare pacers. It is one part of the Breeders Crown annual series of twelve races for both Standardbred trotters and trotters. First run in 1986, it is contested over a distance of one mile. Race organizers have awarded the event to various racetracks across North America.[1] The 2017 race will be held at Hoosier Park in Anderson, Indiana, United States.[2]


Historical race events

In 2010, Pocono Downs became the first venue to host all 12 events on a single night.[3]
