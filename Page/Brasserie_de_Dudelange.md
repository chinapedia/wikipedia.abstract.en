BRASSERIE DE DUDELANGE, was a Luxembourgish brewery which was founded by Jean-Pierre Steyer in 1937 and brewed in the city of Dudelange. In 1964 it was taken over by Brasserie Funck-Bricher. Brewing in Dudelange as well as producing beer under the _Brasserie de Dudelange_ brand name stopped.
