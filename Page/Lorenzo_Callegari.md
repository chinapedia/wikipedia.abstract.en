LORENZO CALLEGARI (born 27 February 1998) is a French footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Serie C side Ternana, on loan from Genoa.[1]


Club career

Paris Saint-Germain

Callegari made his professional debut on 30 November 2016 in the Ligue 1 against Angers. He replaced Lucas after 87 minutes in a 2–0 home win.[2]

Genoa

On 10 July 2018, Serie A club Genoa confirmed the signing of Callegari on free transfer. He signed a 4-year contract with the Italian side. [3]

He was loaned out to Serie C club Ternana on 24 August 2018.[4]


Personal life

He is Italian through his father who is from Bettola.[5]
