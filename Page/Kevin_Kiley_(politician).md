KEVIN KILEY (born January 30, 1985) is an American politician serving in the California State Assembly. He is a Republican representing the 6th district, encompassing an outer ring of northern and eastern Sacramento suburbs. Prior to being elected to the state assembly, he was a deputy attorney general. He is currently running for the Senate Seat in California's 1st District. This special election occurs on March 26, 2019.
