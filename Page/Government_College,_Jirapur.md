GOVERNMENT COLLEGE, ZIRAPUR is a degree college in Jirapur, Madhya Pradesh affiliated to Barkatullah University, Bhopal[1][2]

    Affiliated to: Barkatullah University, Bhopal
    Duration: 3 Years

    Affiliated to: Barkatullah University, Bhopal
    Duration: 3 Years

-   B.Sc. Science

    Affiliated to: Barkatullah University, Bhopal
    Duration: 3 Years

-   M.A. Economics

    Affiliated to: Barkatullah University, Bhopal
    Duration: 2 Years

-   M.A. Political Science

    Affiliated to: Barkatullah University, Bhopal
    Duration: 2 Years
