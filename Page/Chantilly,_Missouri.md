CHANTILLY is an unincorporated community in Lincoln County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. The GNIS classifies it as a historically populated place.[1]


History

Chantilly was laid out in 1852, and named after Chantilly, in France.[2] A post office called Chantilly was established in 1840, and remained in operation until 1918.[3]
