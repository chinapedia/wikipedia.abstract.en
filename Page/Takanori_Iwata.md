Biography

In 2010, Iwata made his debut with Sandaime Jsoul Brothers' single "Best Friend's Girl".

In 2014, He passed the final audition to join EXILE as a performer along with other 4 new members making him currently member of both Exile and Sandaime Jsoul Brothers.

In October the same year, he appeared in Fuji TV's drama "Dear Sister".

In 2015, he appeared in two NTV dramas, "WILD HEROES" in April and "HiGH & LOW ~THE STORY OF S.W.O.R.D.~" in October.

In June 2016, he played his 1st lead role in the movie "Evergreen Love", for which he won Best New Artist Award at the "41st Hochi Film Awards" and Newcomer of the Year Award as well as Popularity Award at the "40th Japan Academy Film Prize".
