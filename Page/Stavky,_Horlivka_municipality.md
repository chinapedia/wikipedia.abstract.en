STAVKY (; ) is a settlement in Horlivka municipality of Donetsk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, at 40.3 km NNE from the centre of Donetsk city.

The settlement was taken under control of pro-Russian forces during the War in Donbass, that started in 2014.[1] Raions_of_Horlivka.png:
