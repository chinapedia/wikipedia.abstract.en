FAINA POTAPOVA (born 12 September 1996) is a Kazakhstani professional racing cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Women's Team .[1]


Major results

2014
    2nd Scratch Race, Track Clubs ACC Cup

2016
    2nd Team Pursuit, Track Clubs ACC Cup (with Nadezhda Geneleva, Tatyana Geneleva and Yekaterina Yuraitis)
