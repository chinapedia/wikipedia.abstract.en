STEVEN CHAMBERS (born 8 November 1990) is an Australian professional baseball player for the Canberra Cavalry of the Australian Baseball League.

He was a member of the Australia national baseball team in the 2017 World Baseball Classic Qualification[1] and 2016 Haarlem Baseball Week.[2]

On 12 February 2019 he invited for spring training of Yokohama DeNA Bay Stars of Nippon Professional Baseball (NPB) with Steven Kent.[3]
