THE QUEEN'S HEAD is a pub at 144 Stockwell Road, Stockwell, London SW9. In 2019 it followed the growing trend towards veganism by converting to an entirely plant-based menu and drinks selection.

It is a Grade II listed building, "of Regency appearance with alterations".[1]
