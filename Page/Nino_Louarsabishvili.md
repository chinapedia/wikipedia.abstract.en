NINO LOUARSABISHVILI (born 3 February 1977) is a retired professional Georgian female tennis player.

Louarsabishvili has won six singles and seven doubles titles on the ITF tour in her career. On 24 April 1995, she reached her best singles ranking of world number 135. On 21 July 1997, she peaked at world number 111 in the doubles rankings.

Playing for Georgia at the Fed Cup, Louarsabishvili has a win–loss record of 17–23.[1]

Louarsabishvili retired from tennis 2000.
