NIKO GUIDO (born 1966, Istanbul) is a Turkish photographer.[1]

After finishing Galatasaray High School, he graduated from Bosphorus University.[2][3] He opened exhibitions in Venice, Paris, İzmir and İstanbul; his photographs have published on magazines and newspapers.
