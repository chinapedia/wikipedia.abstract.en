FARMERSVILLE is an unincorporated community in Livingston County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Farmersville was laid out in 1870, and named for its location in an agricultural region.[2] A post office called Farmersville was established in 1867, and remained in operation until 1901.[3]
