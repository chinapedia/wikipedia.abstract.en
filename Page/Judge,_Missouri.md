JUDGE is an unincorporated community in Osage County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Judge was established in 1914, and remained in operation until 1922.[2] The community was named for the fact A. J. Branson, a local judge, was the original owner of the town site.[3]
