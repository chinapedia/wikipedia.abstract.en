LUMLEY BRANCH is a stream in Dade County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Sac River.[2]

The stream headwaters arise just north of U.S. Route 160 east of Everton at and it flows north approximately five miles to join the Sac River northwest of the community of Comet at .[3][4]

Lumley Branch most likely was named after William Lumley, an early settler.[5]
