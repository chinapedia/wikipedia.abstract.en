DUNKSBURG is an unincorporated community in Johnson and Pettis counties, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Dunksburg was originally called Dunkley's Store, and under the latter name was founded in 1858, and named after B. F. Dunkley, a local merchant.[2] A post office called Dunksburg was established in 1890, and remained in operation until 1904.[3]
