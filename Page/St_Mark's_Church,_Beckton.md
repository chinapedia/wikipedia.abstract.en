ST MARK'S ECUMENICAL CHURCH AND COMMUNITY CENTRE is an ecumenical church and community centre on Tollgate Road in the Beckton area of Newham, London.[1] It opened in 1987 and its congregations include a Church of England congregation (which also forms the area's Anglican parish church)[2] and a Baptist one.[3]
