"GIRL WATCHER" is a song written by Ronald B. Killette and Wayne Pittman and performed by The O'Kaysions. It reached #5 on the _Billboard_ Hot 100 and #6 on the _Billboard_ Hot Rhythm & Blues Singles chart in 1968.[1] The song was featured on their 1968 album, _Girl Watcher_.[2]

The single was first released in March 1968 on North State Records, but it did not become a hit until re-released in June 1968 on ABC Records.[3]

The song was produced by North State Music.[4]

"Girl Watcher" received gold record status for a million sales from the R.I.A.A. in December 1968.[5]

The single ranked #45 on the _Billboard_ Year-End Hot 100 singles of 1968.[6]


Other versions

-   Tam White released a version of the song as a single in 1968 in the UK.[7]
-   Spontaneous Combustion released a version of the song on their 2004 album _Strike Anywhere_.[8]
