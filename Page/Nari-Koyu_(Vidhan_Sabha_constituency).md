NARI and KOYU are villages in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh[1][2] East Siang is the name of the district that contains village Nari-Koyu.

Nari-Koyu is one of the 60 constituencies of Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh. Name of current MLA (December-2016) of this constituency is Kento Rina.


Member of Legislative Assembly

-   2019: Kento Rina, Bharatiya Janata Party
