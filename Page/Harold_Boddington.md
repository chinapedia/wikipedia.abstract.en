HAROLD BODDINGTON (active 1903–05) was an English footballer who played in the Football League as an outside left for Middlesbrough in 1903–04. He also played non-league football for Darlington.[1]

Boddington made his only League appearance for Middlesbrough on 28 March 1904 in a 3–0 defeat away to Sheffield United in the First Division.[2] He played and scored for Darlington of the Northern League in the 1904–05 FA Cup.[3]
