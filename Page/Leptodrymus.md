_LEPTODRYMUS_ is a genus of snake in the family Colubridae that contains the sole species _LEPTODRYMUS PULCHERRIMUS_.[1] It is known as the STRIPED LOWLAND SNAKE[2][3] or GREEN-HEADED RACER.[4]

It is found throughout Central America, in Costa Rica, Guatemala, Nicaragua, El Salvador and Honduras.[5][6]
