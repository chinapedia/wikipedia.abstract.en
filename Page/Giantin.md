GIANTIN or GOLGIN SUBFAMILY B MEMBER 1 is a protein that in humans is encoded by the _GOLGB1_ gene.[1][2][3] Giantin is a disulfide-linked homodimer.


Interactions

GOLGB1 has been shown to interact with ACBD3 and with PLK3.[4]
