RICHARD FLOOD (born 28 July 1982) is an Irish actor best known for the character Tommy McConnel in the series _Crossing Lines_ and as James McKay in the series _Red Rock_.


Biography

In 2012, Flood began dating Italian actress Gabriella Pession, and soon they were engaged. Shortly after, they announced that they were expecting their first child, Giulio Flood, born in 2014.[1][2][3] On September 3, 2016, Flood married his long-time love Pession in an intimate wedding in Portofino, Italy.


Career

In 2012, Flood appeared in an episode of the series _Titanic: Blood and Steel_, as Samuel Johanson.

In 2013, Flood joined the main cast of the new series Crossing Lines, playing detective Tommy McConnel,[4] a specialist in weapons and tactics from Northern Ireland.

In 2015, Flood joined the main cast of the new series Red Rock, where he played the police superintendent James McKay,[5][6]
