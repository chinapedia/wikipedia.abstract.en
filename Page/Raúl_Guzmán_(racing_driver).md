RAÚL GUZMÁN MARCHINA is a Mexican racing driver.


Career

Karting

Born in Guadalajara, Guzmán began karting in 2011, at age eleven, and claimed the SuperKarts USA title in 2014.[1]

Italian F4 Championship

The following year he graduated to single-seaters in Italian F4, taking seventeenth in his debut season and third the following year after a battle for the title between himself, Mick Schumacher and eventual champion Marcos Siebert.

For 2017, Guzman switched to the Formula Renault 2.0 championships with R-ace GP.[2] He had three point-scoring finishes including highest finishing position on the fifth place at Silverstone.[3] While in the standings he ended seventeenth, behind his teammates Will Palmer, Robert Shwartzman and Max Defourny.
