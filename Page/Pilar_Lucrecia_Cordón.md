PILAR LUCRECIA CORDÃ“N MURO (born 4 March 1973) is a Spanish Olympic show jumping rider.[1][2] She competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, where she placed 11th in the team and 46th in the individual competition.

Cordon participated at two World Equestrian Games (in 2010 and 2014) and at three European Show Jumping Championships (in 2011, 2013 and 2015). She finished 6th in the team competition at the 2015 European Championships held in Aachen, Germany.
