The DAS ALMAS RIVER (), or JEQUIÉ RIVER () is a river in the state of Bahia, Brazil. It empties into the Atlantic Ocean.

The Wenceslau Guimarães Ecological Station was created in 1997 to protect the headwaters of the Das Almas River.[1]
