Rear Admiral GIORGIO LAZIO is an Italian Navy officer. He is designated as the next Commander Maritime Command North ().[1]

He graduated from the Italian Naval Academy in 1983.[2]

He has completed a degree in Maritime and Naval Sciences as well as a master's degree in International Strategic-Military Studies.[3]
