DANIELA MÚÑOZ GALLEGOS (born 30 January 1984) is a Mexican female tennis player.

Múñoz Gallegos has a career high WTA singles ranking of 328, achieved on 8 October 2007. Múñoz Gallegos also has a career high WTA doubles ranking of 280 achieved on 14 September 2009. Múñoz Gallegos has won 4 ITF singles and 21 doubles titles.

Playing for Mexico at the Fed Cup, Múñoz Gallegos has a win–loss record of 14–14.[1]

Múñoz Gallegos made her WTA main draw debut at the 2010 Abierto Mexicano Telcel in the doubles event partnering Ximena Hermoso.
