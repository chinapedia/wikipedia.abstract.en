_PARASCYTHRIS MUELLERI_ is a moth of the family Scythrididae, and the only species in the genus _PARASCYTHRIS_. It was described by Mann in 1871. It is found in Germany, Austria, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, Poland, Latvia, Belarus, Ukraine and Russia.[1][2]
