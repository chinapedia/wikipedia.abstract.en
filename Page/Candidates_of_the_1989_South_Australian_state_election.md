This article provides information on candidates who stood for the 1989 South Australian state election, held on 25 November 1989.


Retiring Members

Labor

-   Roy Abbott MHA (Spence)
-   Gavin Keneally MHA (Stuart)
-   Terry McRae MHA (Playford)
-   Ron Payne MHA (Mitchell)
-   Keith Plunkett MHA (Peake)
-   Jack Slater MHA (Gilles)
