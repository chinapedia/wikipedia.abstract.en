XILI RESERVOIR () is a reservoir located in Xili Subdistrict, Nanshan District, in southwestern Shenzhen in the southwestern China.[1]


History

Construction began in December 1959 and completed in March 1960. Its drainage basin is about , and it can hold up to of water at full capacity. It belongs to the first grade water source protection area () and is part of Shenzhen's water supply network.[2] The reservoir provides drinking water and water for irrigation. The reservoir discharges into Dasha River which empties into the South China Sea in Shenzhen Bay.


Public Access

It is not open to the public.[3]
