_LIFE'S CIRCUS_ (German: _MANEGE_) is a 1928 German silent drama film directed by Max Reichmann and starring Raimondo Van Riel, Ernst Van Duren and Kurt Gerron.[1]

The film's sets were designed by Leopold Blonder. It was made by the German branch of First National Pictures.
