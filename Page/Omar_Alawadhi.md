OMAR ALAWADHI (born 16 January 1982) is an Emirati tennis player.

Alawadhi has a career high ATP singles ranking of 805 achieved on 14 July 2003. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 1005 achieved on 26 May 2003.

Alawadhi made his ATP main draw debut at the 2002 Dubai Tennis Championships in the doubles draw. Alawadhi represents the United Arab Emirates in the Davis Cup.
