METALLA was a Roman mining center located in the Iglesiente region of Sardinia entrusted to a _procurator metallorum_ where were destined Christians and slaves condemned to forced labor. Mentioned in ancient sources, it hosted a thermal building with mosaic floors and a public clock. Here passed the last stretch of the road form Tibulas Sulcis up to Sulci. In the sources would be recognized in _Sardiparias_, the _Sartiparias_ of the Ravenna Cosmography and the _Sardopatoris Forum_ of Ptolemy.
