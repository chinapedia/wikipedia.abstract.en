SERGEI SERGEYEVICH KUZNETSOV (; born 8 March 1999) is a Russian football player who plays for FC Nosta Novotroitsk.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Nosta Novotroitsk on 2 October 2016 in a game against FC Lada Togliatti.[1]
