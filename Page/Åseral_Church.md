ÅSERAL CHURCH () is a parish church in Åseral municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Kyrkjebygda. The church is part of the Åseral parish in the Mandal deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden, cruciform church was built in 1822 by the architects Olav Aasland and Olav Bakken. The church seats about 300 people.[1][2]
