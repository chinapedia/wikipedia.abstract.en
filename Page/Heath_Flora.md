HEATH FLORA (born July 19, 1983) is an American politician serving in the California State Assembly.


Career

Flora served for over 15 years as a volunteer fire fighter.[1] From 2005 to 2007, Flora was a fire fighter with the California Department Forestry and Fire Protection.[2] Flora is a businessman and owner of Golden Valley Equipment.[3]

In 2016, Flora's political career began when he won the election against Ken Vogel. Flora is a Republican representing the California's 12th State Assembly district, encompassing part of the San Joaquin Valley.[4][5][6][7]
