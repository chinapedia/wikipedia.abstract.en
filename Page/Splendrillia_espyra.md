_SPLENDRILLIA ESPYRA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 5 mm and 8 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off Eastern Brazil.
