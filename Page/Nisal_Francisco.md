NISAL FRANCISCO (born 1 July 1997) is a Sri Lankan cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Bloomfield Cricket and Athletic Club in the 2016–17 Premier League Tournament on 3 December 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Anuradhaura District in the 2016–17 Districts One Day Tournament on 15 March 2017.[3]

In April 2018, he was named in Colombo's squad for the 2018 Super Provincial One Day Tournament.[4]
