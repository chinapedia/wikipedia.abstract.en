GALDI (also Galde) is a village in the commune of Nyambaka, in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, in the south-eastern part of the Adamawa Plateau.[1]


Population

In 1967, Galdi contained 182 inhabitants, mainly Fula people[2]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 1529 people in the village.[3]

There is a Sunday market in the village.[4]


Rock art

A site with rock art has been discovered in the vicinity of the village, with more than 150 motifs, including stylised metal weapons, arrangements of cups, and other unidentified signs.[5]
