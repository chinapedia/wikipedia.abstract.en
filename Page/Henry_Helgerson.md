HENRY M. HELGERSON, JR. (born January 12, 1952) is an American politician. He has served as a Democratic member for the 83rd district in the Kansas House of Representatives since 2016.[1] He previously served in the House from 1983 to 2000 and in the Kansas Senate from 2003 to 2004 (28th district).
