_RHINOBOTHRYUM_ is a genus of snakes in the family Colubridae.


Geographic range

The genus _Rhinobothryum_ is endemic to Central America and South America.[1]


Species

Two species are recognized as being valid.[2][3]

-   _Rhinobothryum bovallii_ Andersson, 1916
-   _Rhinobothryum lentiginosum_ (Scopoli, 1785)
