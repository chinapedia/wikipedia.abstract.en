'''Tihomil Vranje≈° ''' (born 10 November 1977) is a Croatian male water polo player. He was a member of the Croatia men's national water polo team, playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played most notably for VK Jug Dubrovnik in Croatia and Olympiacos in Greece.[2]
