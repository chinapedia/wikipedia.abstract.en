_' Efecan Karaca_' (born 16 November 1989) is a Turkish footballer who plays for Alanyaspor. He is a product of the Galatasaray youth academy.[1]

Efecan has represented the Turkish Football Federation at the U18 and U19 levels.[2] On 25 March 2019 he made a debut in the national senior team in a 4–0 home win against Moldova.[3]
