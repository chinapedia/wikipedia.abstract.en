The MIRI CROCODILE FARM CUM MINI ZOO () is a crocodile farm and zoo in Miri, Sarawak, Malaysia. It is registered by the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora.[1]


History

The farm and zoo was opened on 16 August 1998.[2] It became the second crocodile farm opened in Sarawak after the Jong Crocodile Farm and Zoo in Kuching.[3]


Architecture

The farm and zoo is located in a 9 hectare of area near the mouth of Baram River. Man-made ponds were created for the breeding ground of the crocodiles.[4]


Exhibition

The crocodile farm houses more than 1,000 species of crocodiles.[5] It also features an information center about various aspects of crocodiles, such as fatal clashes with humans, life cycle etc.[6]


Operation

The monthly expenses for the farm and zoo is around MYR40,000-50,000, in which mostly goes to buying foods for the animals and its 15 staffs salary. They regularly seek assistance from the Tourism Board.[7]


Opening time

The farm and zoo is opened everyday from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. for MYR20.[8]
