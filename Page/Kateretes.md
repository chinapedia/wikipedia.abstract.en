_KATERETES_ is a genus of short-winged flower beetles in the family Kateretidae. There are about six described species in _Kateretes_.[1][2][3][4]


Species

These six species belong to the genus _Kateretes_:

-   _Kateretes dalmatinus_ (Sturm, 1844)^(g)
-   _Kateretes flavicans_ (Fairmaire, 1860)^(g)
-   _Kateretes mixtus_ Kirejtshuk, 1989^(g)
-   _Kateretes pusillus_ (Thunberg, 1794)^(g\ b)
-   _Kateretes rufilabris_ (Latreille, 1807)^(g)
-   _Kateretes scissus_ (Parsons, 1943)^(i\ c\ g)

Data sources: i = ITIS,[5] c = Catalogue of Life,[6] g = GBIF,[7] b = Bugguide.net[8]
