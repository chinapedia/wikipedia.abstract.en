HARRY HOOKEY is a singer-songwriter from Victoria, Australia.[1] His debut album _Misdiagnosed_ was nominated for a 2014 ARIA Award for Best Blues & Roots Album.[2][3]


Discography

-   _Old Noise_ EP (2012)
-   _Sometimes_ EP (2013)
-   "Man On Fire" (2014)
-   _Misdiagnosed_ (2014) - Warner Music[4]
