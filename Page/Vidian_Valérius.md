VIVIAN VALÉRIUS (born June 24, 1988) is a Guadeloupe football player who plays as forward for FC Fleury 91 and the Guadeloupe national football team.


Early life

At an early age, Vidian started had a partiality for basketball but began to play football before or around the age of 13.


Career

Soon after the National Cup of regions in Clairefontaine, Paris FC recruited him but he only spent one season there.[1]

After becoming the third top scorer in the Championnat de France Amateur Group D in 2014 with 11 goals, Valérius signed for FC Fleury 91.[2]

Late in 2016, he signed for AS Vitré.[3]
