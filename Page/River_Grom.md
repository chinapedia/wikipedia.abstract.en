River_Grom_-_geograph.org.uk_-_656773.jpg.]] The RIVER GROM is a short tributary of the River Medway near Tunbridge Wells.[1][2] Flowing westwards through High Rocks and Groombridge, it forms the Kent-Sussex border for part of its length.[3] A diversion feeds the moat at Groombridge Place.[4]
