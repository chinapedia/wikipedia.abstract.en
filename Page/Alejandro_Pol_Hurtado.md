ALEJANDRO ANDRÉS POL HURTADO (born 29 March 1990) is a Venezuelan footballer playing as Midfielder for FK Nevėžis.


Career

Born in Caracas,[1] Alejandro Pol Hurtado had a spell in Spain with RCD Espanyol youth team in 2003–04.[2]

He started his senior career in 2007 playing with Monagas Sport Club playing in the 2007–08 Venezuelan Primera División.[3] In the following season, he moved to Universidad Central de Venezuela FC, usually known as UCV FC or simply Universidad Central.[4]

In 2009, he took his chance again in Europe, this time as a senior, and played with Italian side Ternana Calcio in the 2009–10 Lega Pro Prima Divisione.[5] The following season, he crossed the Adriatic and settled in Serbia where he played with FK Srem in the Serbian First League.[6]

In 2011, he returned to Venezuela, and has been playing back with his former club, Universidad Central de Venezuela FC.[7] Aside a half-season he spent with Deportivo Petare in the 2012–13 Venezuelan Primera División,[8] he has been loyal to UCV FC from 2011 till 2016, the season he is playing at time of this writing.[9]
