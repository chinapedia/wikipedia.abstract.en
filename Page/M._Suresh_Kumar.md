MANI SURESH KUMAR (born 19 April 1973) is a first-class cricketer who played for Kerala in the Ranji Trophy. He was born in Alleppey, Kerala, India.[1]

Mani is a left-hand batsman and slow left-arm orthodox bowler. He took a hat-trick in the 1995-96 Ranji Trophy playing for Kerala against Rajasthan.[2]
