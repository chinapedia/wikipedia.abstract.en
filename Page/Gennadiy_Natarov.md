GENNADIY NATAROV (born 23 January 1992) is Ukrainian male badminton player.[1][2]


Career

He started playing badminton in 1991 at badminton club in KPI, and made his international debut in 2006 at Poland.[3] In 2013, he competed at the Summer Universiade in Kazan, Russia.[4] In 2014, he won Hatzor Israel International tournament in men's and mixed doubles event, then became the runner-up of the Polish International tournament in mixed doubles event.[5][6]
