LENKA TERMEROVÁ (born 12 July 1947) is a Czech film, stage and television actress. She won the Czech Lion award for Best Supporting Actress in 2008 for her role in the film _Děti noci_. She is the mother of actress Martha Issová.[1]


Acting career

Termerová studied at the Faculty of Theatre in Prague.[2] She went on to perform in the Divadlo Petra Bezruče in Ostrava, before moving to Studio Ypsilon in Prague in 1981.[3] Termerová won the Czech Lion award for Best Supporting Actress in 2008 for her role in the film _Děti noci_.[4] She had a starring role in the Prima television series _Velmi křehké vztahy_,[5] which was originally broadcast between 2007 and 2009. Termerová was diagnosed with breast cancer in 2009.[6]
