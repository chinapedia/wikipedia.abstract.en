HERBERT STUART PAKINGTON, 4TH BARON HAMPTON (1883–1962), served as Chief Commissioner of The Scout Association.[1]

He was educated at Wellington College, Berkshire, and the Royal Military College, Sandhurst.[2] He was a 1931 recipient of the Silver Buffalo Award.
