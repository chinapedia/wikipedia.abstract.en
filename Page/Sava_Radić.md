SAVA RADIĆ (; born 4 March 1998) is a Serbian footballer, who plays as a defender for Trajal.[1]


Club career

Čukarički

Born in Niš, Sava played for youth selections of Nacional Niš.[2] He moved to Čukarički as a cadet in 2013. After two years spent with youth team, Radić signed his first three-year professional contract with club at the beginning of December 2015.[3] He also continued playing with youth team and was a member of generation which won Serbian youth league and promotion in 2016–17 UEFA Youth League.[4] Radić made his senior debut in the 6th fixture match of the 2016–17 Serbian SuperLiga campaign against Vojvodina, played on 21 August 2016, when he performed as a right-back.[5] While with youth setup, Radić collected 6 games as a senior in both domestic competitions until the end of 2016–17 season.[6] After overgrowing youth team, Radić has permanently joined first squad in summer 2017, when he converted his squad number, being assigned number 2 jersey.[7] On 18 April 2018, Radić scored own goal in the first leg of the semifinal cup match against Partizan, after which he was replaced by Stefan Veličković in second half.[8]


International career

Radić was a member of Serbia U16,[9][10] U17[11][12] and U18[13][14] national football teams between 2013 and 2016. Radić was also called into Serbia U19 level for memorial tournament "Stevan Vilotić - Ćele" in August 2016.[15] He played as a member of the selection until 2017.[16]
