Niigata-kotsu-699.jpg is a rechargeable contactless smart card ticketing system for public transport in Niigata, Japan, introduced by Niigata Kotsu.


Usable area

, the card is usable on most of the bus lines operated by Niigata Kotsu. The card cannot be used on JR lines.


Points of purchase

-   Niigata Station Bandai Exit
-   Bandai City Bus Center
