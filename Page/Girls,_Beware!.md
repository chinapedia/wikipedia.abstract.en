_GIRLS, BEWARE!_ (German: _MÄDCHEN, HÜTET EUCH!_) is a 1928 German silent drama film directed by Valy Arnheim and starring Gritta Ley, Egon von Jordan and Hanni Weisse.[1]

The film's sets were designed by the art director August Rinaldi.
