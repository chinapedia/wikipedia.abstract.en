OLOM PANYANG is an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Panyang was elected from the Mariyang-Geku constituency in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election, standing as an BJP candidate.[2][3][4]
