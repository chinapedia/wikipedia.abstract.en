PIETRO BASTONI (1570–1622) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Umbriatico (1611–1622).[1]


Biography

Pietro Bastoni was born in 1570.[2] On 24 January 1611, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul V as Bishop of Umbriatico.[3][4] On 6 February 1611, he was consecrated bishop by Giovanni Garzia Mellini, Cardinal-Priest of Santi Quattro Coronati, with Jullio del Carretto, Bishop of Casale Monferrato, and Antonio d'Aquino, Bishop of Sarno, serving as co-consecrators.[5] He served as Bishop of Umbriatico until his death in 1622.[6][7] While bishop, he was the principal co-consecrator of Andrea Pierbenedetti, Bishop of Venosa (1611).[8]
