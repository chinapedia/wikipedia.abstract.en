MOHD FAIZAT BIN MOHAMAD GHAZLI (born 28 November 1994) is a Malaysian footballer who plays as a forward for Malaysia Super League club PKNS FC.[1]

Faizat is the younger brother of fellow footballer Failee Ghazli.


Club career

Harimau Muda

Faizat started his football career with Harimau Muda B in 2013 until 2015.[2] On 25 November 2015, it was confirmed that the Harimau Muda programme has disbanded by Football Association of Malaysia which means all the player from Harimau Muda A, Harimau Muda B and Harimau Muda C will be returned to their own state.[3]

PKNS FC

On December 2017, Faizat signed a contract with Malaysia Super League club PKNS FC after two seasons at Penang.[4]


International career

Faizat has featured in 2015 Southeast Asian Games represented Malaysia U23. He has made 4 appearances during the tournament.
