F. O. OGUNLANA served as the Treasurer of the Africa Scout Region of the World Scout Bureau.

In 1979, he was awarded the 138th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1] He was also a recipient of the Silver World Award.[2]
