MP-2001, also known as 2,3,4-TRIMETHOXYESTRA-1,3,5(10)-TRIEN-17Î’-OL or 2,4-DIMETHOXYESTRADIOL 3-METHYL ETHER, is a steroid and derivative of estradiol that was described in 1966 and is devoid of estrogenic activity but produces potent analgesic effects in animals.[1][2] It was never marketed.[3]
