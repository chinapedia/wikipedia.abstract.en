JORGE EDUARDO BIANCO (born April 28, 1956 in Villa Constitución (Santa Fe), Argentina) is a former Argentine footballer who played for clubs in Argentina, Chile, Colombia, El Salvador and Peru.


Teams

-   River Plate 1976

-   All Boys 1976

-   River Plate 1977

-   Universidad Católica 1978

-   San Marcos de Arica 1979-1980

-   Talleres de Córdoba 1981-1982

-   Rosario Central 1982

-   San Marcos de Arica 1983

-   Cúcuta Deportivo 1984

-   Central Norte 1985

-   Ferro Carril Oeste 1986-1987

-   Alianza 1988-1992

-   San José 1993
