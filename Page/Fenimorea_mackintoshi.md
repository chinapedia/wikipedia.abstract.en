_FENIMOREA MACKINTOSHI_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of this marine shell varies between 12 mm and 15.5 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the Bahamas.
