The TAM KUNG TEMPLE () is a temple in Coloane, Macau, China. It is dedicated to Tam Kung.[1]


History

The temple was constructed in 1862 during the Portuguese Macau.[2]


Architecture

There is a long whale bone with the shape of dragon boat inside the temple main altar.[3] There is also a bronze spouting bowl in the hall.[4]
