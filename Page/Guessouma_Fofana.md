GUESSOUMA FOFANA (born 17 December 1992) is a French footballer who plays as a midfielder for En Avant de Guingamp.[1] He is the brother of the former Lyon player Gueïda Fofana.


Club career

In August 2018, Fofana joined En Avant de Guingamp on a three-year contract. The transfer fee paid to Amiens was estimated at €1 million.[2]


International career

He is of Malian descent, and made himself eligible for the Mali national football team.[3]
