JOYCE SCOTT LINDORES (2 May 1944 â€“ 18 June 2017) was a Scottish international indoor and lawn bowler.

Lindores won the Women's singles at the 1995 World Indoor Bowls Championship defeating Margaret Johnston in the final.[1] Three years later she won the pairs gold medal with Margaret Letham at the 1998 Commonwealth Games.[2] The pair then won the pairs title at the 2000 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Moama, Australia.

Lindores remained in Melbourne after the 2006 Commonwealth Games held there.[3] She died on 18 June 2017 in Melbourne.[4][5]
