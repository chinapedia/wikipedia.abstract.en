NICOLAS PIETRULA (born 5 August 1995) is a Czech male track cyclist, representing Czech Republic at international competitions. He competed at the 2015 UEC European Track Championships in the team pursuit event and at the 2016 UEC European Track Championships also in the team pursuit event.[1]
