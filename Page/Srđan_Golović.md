SRĐAN GOLOVIĆ (; born 22 March 1967) is a Montenegro football manager and former player.


Career

Born in Podgorica, known as Titograd back then and capital of SR Montenegro, SFR Yugoslavia, he started his career at OFK Titograd where, after playing in the youth teams, he debuted as senior and played there till 1989 when he joined FK Budućnost Titograd[1] and played with them between 1989and 1991 in the Yugoslav First League.[2][3] He then played with FK Rudar Pljevlja, FK Obilić, FK Čukarički Stankom and FK Radnički Niš[4][5] before moving to Hungary where he played with MTK Budapest FC and Győri ETO FC in the Nemzeti Bajnokság I between 2000 and 2002.[6][7]

After retiring, he stayed at Čukarički, coaching youth levels in the club. He holds UEFA A coaching licence, and between 2005 and 2009 he has worled with the first team of Čukarički.[8] By Autumn 2016 he was the coach of the youth section of FK Sinđelić Beograd.[9]
