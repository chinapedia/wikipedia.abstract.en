The MATT-BAHLS HOUSE is a historic building located in Guttenberg, Iowa, United States. The two-story vernacular stone structure was built sometime before 1858. It features a full-length, two story, frame porch on its south side. The single-story frame addition on the northeast side blends into the older structure.[1] The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.[2]
