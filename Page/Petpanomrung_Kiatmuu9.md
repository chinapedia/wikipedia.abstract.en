PETPANOMRUNG KIATMUU9 is a Thai Muay Thai fighter, originally from Isaan, in the northeast of Thailand, Petpanomrung is from the same Muay Thai gym of the multiple time Lumpinee Stadium champion Singdam Kiatmuu9.[1]

As of 1 November 2018, he is ranked the #3 featherweight in the world by Combat Press.[2]

Petpanomrung was contracted in August 2016 to fight for Glory promotion.[3]


Titles and accomplishments

-   GLORY
    -   2018 Year in Review - Knockout of the Year[4]
    -   2018 Glory Featherweight Champion (one time; Current)
    -   2018 interim Glory Featherweight Champion
    -   2017 Glory Featherweight (-65 kg/138.9 lb) Contender Tournament Winner
-   2016 WMC Muaythai Lightweight World Champion[5]
-   2015 Toyota Vigo Marathon Tournament Champion (64 Kg)
-   2013–2014 Thailand (PAT) Super Featherweight Champion[6]
-   2011 Thailand (PAT) Bantamweight Champion
