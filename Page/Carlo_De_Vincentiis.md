CAROLO ANTONIO DI VINCENZO[1] also known as CARLO DE VINCENTIIS or ACQUAVIVA (1 May 1622[2] – 1677) was an Italian violinist.

He was born in Acquaviva delle Fonti to Donato and Livia Cassotta.[3] He was one of the most important Neapolitan violinists of the 17th century, teacher of Pietro Marchitelli since 1657.
