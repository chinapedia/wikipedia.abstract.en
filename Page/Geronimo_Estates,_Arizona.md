GERONIMO ESTATES is both a census-designated place (CDP) and a populated place in Gila County, Arizona, United States. The population of the CDP was 60 at the 2010 census.[1]


Geography

Geronimo Estates CDP is located in northern Gila County in the valley of Webber Creek, north of Payson via Paint Pony Drive. It is within Tonto National Forest, south of the Mogollon Rim.
