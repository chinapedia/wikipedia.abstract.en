ERKAM REŞMEN (born 8 January 1996 in Antalya, Turkey) is a Turkish football player who plays as defender for Samsunspor. He plays mostly as a right-back, and occasionally as a centerback.

Reşmen represented the Turkey national under-19 football team in a 4-3 friendly loss to Sweden U19 in 2007.[1]
