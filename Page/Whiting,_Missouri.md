WHITING is an unincorporated community in Mississippi County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Whiting was established in 1889, and remained in operation until 1909.[2] The community has the name of Justin R. Whiting, a businessperson in the local lumber industry.[3]
