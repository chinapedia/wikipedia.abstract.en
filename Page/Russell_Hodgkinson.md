RUSSELL HODGKINSON (born August 14, 1959) is an American actor, known for his role in the Syfy television network series _Z Nation_ as Steven "Doc" Beck.


Personal life

Hodgkinson was born on August 14, 1959 at Homestead Air Force Base in Florida and served in the military before becoming an actor.[1]
