The 2017 PING AN CHINESE FOOTBALL ASSOCIATION SUPER LEAGUE ()[1] was the 14th season since the establishment of the Chinese Super League.[2][3] The league title sponsor is Ping An Insurance.[4][5] Guangzhou Evergrande Taobao won their seventh consecutive title of the league.

This season witnessed a huge change in the regulation to the players. Only three foreign players can play at one game and at least two domestic players who are under the age of 23 (born on or after 1 January 1994) must be in the 18-man list, including at least one must be in the starting list.[6]
