ARIEL RAUL BULASIO (born 4 June 1994) is an Argentine footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Jorge Newbery de Maipu.[1][2]


Career

Bulasio began with Aldosivi and was promoted into the first-team in 2012.[3] He made his debut in a Primera B Nacional match against Ferro Carril Oeste on 21 October. A week later he started his second match for Aldosivi, a 0–2 win against Defensa y Justicia. He was also on the bench 27 times in 2012. Between 2013 and 2016, Bulasio did not feature for Aldosivi but was an unused substitute on 18 occasions in league and cup.[4][5] On 21 January 2016, Bulasio joined Torneo Federal B side Atlético Progreso on loan for six months. However, he returned to Aldosivi a short time later after deciding against playing for Atlético Progreso.[6][7]

Liga Maipuense team Jorge Newbery de Maipu signed Bulasio in 2016.[8]
