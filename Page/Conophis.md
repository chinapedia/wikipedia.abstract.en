_CONOPHIS_ is a genus of snakes in the subfamily Dipsadinae of the family Colubridae. The genus is endemic to Mexico and Central America.


Species and geographic ranges

The genus _Conophis_ contains the following three species which are recognized as being valid.[1]

-   _Conophis lineatus_ – Belize, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico, Nicaragua
-   _Conophis morai_ – Mexico
-   _Conophis vittatus_ – Guatemala, Mexico

_Nota bene_: Binomial authorities in parentheses indicates that the species was originally described in a genus other than _Conophis_.
