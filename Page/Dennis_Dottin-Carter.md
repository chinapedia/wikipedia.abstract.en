DENNIS DOTTIN-CARTER (born February 4, 1981) is an American football coach. He is currently the defensive line coach for the University of Connecticut. He served as the interim head coach at Delaware in 2016, having been named to the position on October 16, 2016.[1] He previously has been an assistant coach at both Delaware and the University of Maine. He played football for Maine under coach Jack Cosgrove.

†Delaware had a record of 2–4 (0–3 CAA) prior to Dave Brock being relieved of head coaching duties.
