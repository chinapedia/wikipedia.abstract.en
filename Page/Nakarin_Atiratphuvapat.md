NAKARIN ATIRATPHUVAPAT (;[1] born 17 January 1996) is a Thai motorcycle racer.


Career

He was an Asia Talent Cup contestant in 2014 and finished the season 8th overall. He remained in the Asia Talent Cup for 2015 and finished the season 4th overall.

He was signed up to race in the Moto3 World Championship for Honda Team Asia for 2017.[2]
