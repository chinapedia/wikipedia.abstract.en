_OFF THE WALL_ is a 1983 American crime comedy film directed by Rick Friedberg, written by Dick Chudnow, and starring Paul Sorvino, Patrick Cassidy, and Rosanna Arquette.


Plot

A young woman frames two hitchikers for her crimes. Feeling guilty, she tries to break them out.


Reception

Roger Ebert of the Chicago Sun-Times gaves the film 1.5 stars out of 4 and dubbed it, _one of the most lame-brained movies of recent years._[1]
