TAVERN is an unincorporated community in Maries County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Tavern was established in 1879, and remained in operation until 1933.[2] A variant name was "Humphreys".[3] Besides the post office, the community had Humphrey's country store.[4]
