REPLICATION PROTEIN A 32 KDA SUBUNIT is a protein that in humans is encoded by the _RPA2_ gene.[1][2]


Interactions

RPA2 has been shown to interact with:

-   Cyclin O,[3]
-   DNA-PKcs,[4]
-   Ku70,[5]
-   MEN1,[6]
-   RPA3,[7][8]
-   Replication protein A1,[9][10]
-   STAT3,[11]
-   TP53BP1[12] and
-   Uracil-DNA glycosylase.[13]
