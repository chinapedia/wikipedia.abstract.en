KHETAG MURATOVICH KHOSONOV (; born 18 June 1998) is a Russian football player who plays as a defensive midfielder for Tambov on loan from CSKA Moscow.


Club career

He made his debut for the main squad of PFC CSKA Moscow in the Russian Cup game against FC Yenisey Krasnoyarsk on 21 September 2016.[1]

He made his Russian Premier League debut for CSKA on 8 September 2017 in a game against FC Amkar Perm.[2]

On 27 July 2018, he scored the only goal of the game in extra time to win the 2018 Russian Super Cup for CSKA.[3]

On 25 January 2019 he joined Tambov on loan until the end of the 2018–19 season.[4] On 5 July 2019, the loan was extended for the 2019–20 season.[5]
