Biography

Francesco Trivulzio was born ordained a priest in 1605.[2] On 29 March 1621, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul V as Bishop of Nocera de' Pagani.[3] In April 1621, he was consecrated bishop by Giovanni Garzia Mellini, Cardinal-Priest of Santi Quattro Coronati.[4] He served as Bishop of Nocera de' Pagani until his death on 22 August 1631.[5]
