KIRIL GROZDANOV (; born 27 September 1997) is a Bulgarian footballer who plays as a forward for Pirin Blagoevgrad.


Club career

Pirin Blagoevgrad

On 26 July 2016, Grozdanov signed his first professional contract with Pirin Blagoevgrad.[1] On 31 August 2017, he was sent on a season-long loan at Second League club Sozopol.[2]
