QUEEN JEONGSUN (Hangul:정순왕후 송씨, Hanja:定順王后 宋氏) (1440 – 7 July 1521) also known as QUEEN DOWAGER UIDEOK (의덕왕대비) was Queen consort of King Danjong of Joseon, the 6th monarch of the Joseon Dynasty.


Family

-   Father: Song Hyeon-Su (1417 – 21 October 1457) (송현수)
    -   Grandfather: Song Bok-Won (1390 – 1454) (송복원)
    -   Grandmother: Lady Kim of the Suncheon Kim clan (순천 김씨)
-   Mother: Lady Min of the Yeoheung Min clan (1418 – 1498) (여흥 민씨)
    -   Grandfather: Min So-Saeng (민소생)
    -   Grandmother: Lady Gu of the Neungseong Gu (능성 구씨)
-   Husband: King Danjong of Joseon (9 August 1441 – 7 November 1457) (조선 단종)


In popular culture

Portrayed by Kim Min-jung (actress) in the 1998-2000 KBS TV series _King and Queen._

Portrayed by Jo Jung-eun in the 2011-2012 JTBC TV series _Queen Insoo._
