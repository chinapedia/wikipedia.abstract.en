ANDREW TODD is a New Zealand international lawn bowler.[1]


Bowls career

In 2008 he won the gold medal in the fours at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch along with Gary Lawson, Russell Meyer and Richard Girvan.

Todd represented New Zealand at the 2010 Commonwealth Games.[2]

He won the 2010 singles title at the New Zealand National Bowls Championships when bowling for the Burnside Bowls Club.[3]
