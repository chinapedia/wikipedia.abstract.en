_MELANOPOLIA FRENATA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Henry Walter Bates in 1884. It is known from Gabon and the Democratic Republic of the Congo.[1]


Varietas

-   _Melanopolia frenata var. latefasciata_ Breuning, 1944
-   _Melanopolia frenata var. ligata_ (Jordan, 1894)
-   _Melanopolia frenata var. nubilosa_ Breuning, 1944
