VLADYSLAV DMYTRENKO (; born 24 May 2000) is a professional Ukrainian football striker who currently plays for the Ukrainian Premier League club Volyn Lutsk.


Career

Dmytrenko is a product of the FC Volyn Sportive youth school system. He made his debut for FC Volyn Lutsk played as a substituted player in the game against FC Shakhtar Donetsk on 24 September 2016 in the Ukrainian Premier League.[1]

He is also a member of the different Ukrainian youth representations.
