THEODOR SOMMERSCHIELD (14 November 1924 – 29 November 1986) was a Norwegian competitive sailor. He was born in Oslo. He competed at the 1968 Summer Olympics in Mexico City, in the dragon class, and at the 1972 Summer Olympics in Munich.[1]
