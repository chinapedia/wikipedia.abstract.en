is a Japanese football player for Tochigi Uva FC.[1]


Career

Toyoshima played with Kashiwa Reysol Academy from 2002 till 2009.[2] During that period he got to be part of the Japanese national U14 and U15 teams.[3] In August 2010, aged 20, he joins C.S. Visé playing in the 2011–12 Belgian Second Division.[4] Next, he plays with FC Zaria Bălți in the 2012–13 Moldovan National Division,[5] then he joined FC Jūrmala playing in the 2013 Latvian Higher League.[6] During winter-break of the 2013–14 season he joined Montenegrin side FK Lovćen,[7] however made no appearances in the 2013–14 Montenegrin First League.[8][9] Following season Toyoshima was loaned to FK Berane playing with them in the 2014–15 Montenegrin First League.[10][11]
