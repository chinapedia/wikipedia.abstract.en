ULFKETIL was a bishop of Aarhus, Denmark, from 1102 to 1134.[1] Ulfketil was the third bishop of the Ancient See of Aarhus and the second after almost a century of vacancy in the diocese. Ulfketil is especially remembered for participating in the Battle of Fotevik on the side of king Niels of Denmark. Ulfketil died during the battle along with 4 other Danish bishops.[2]
