_ONE SUMMER'S DAY_ is a 1917 British silent drama film directed by Frank Goodenough Bayly and starring Fay Compton, Owen Nares and Sam Livesey.[1]


Cast

-   Fay Compton as Maisie
-   Owen Nares as Captain Dick Rudyard
-   Sam Livesey as Philip Marsden
-   Eva Westlake as Chiana
-   A.G. Poulton
-   Caleb Porter
-   Roy Royston
-   Gwendoline Jesson
