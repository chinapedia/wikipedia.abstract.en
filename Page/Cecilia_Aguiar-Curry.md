CECILIA AGUIAR-CURRY (born September 25, 1954) is an American politician serving in the California State Assembly. She is a Democrat representing the 4th Assembly District, encompassing Wine Country and parts of the Sacramento Valley.


Biography

Cecilia grew up in western Yolo County and has long served her community. She went to San Jose State University and earned her degree in business administration.[1] After going to school and working in the Bay Area for several years, she moved back to her hometown of Winters.[2]Prior to her election to the Assembly, she was the first female Mayor of Winters.[3]
