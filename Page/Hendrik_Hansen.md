HENDRIK HANSEN (born 4 November 1994) is a German footballer who plays as a centre-back for Würzburger Kickers.[1]


Career

In July 2017, Hansen left his hometown club VfL Wolfsburg after 15 years, and joined 3. Liga side Würzburger Kickers on a two-year deal.[2]
