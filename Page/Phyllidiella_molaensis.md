_PHYLLIDIELLA MOLAENSIS_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusk in the family Phyllidiidae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the Caribbean Sea coast of Panama. It has been reported from Costa Rica.[2]


Description

This nudibranch is white with a pattern of black concentric rings. The rhinophores are black with white tips.[3]


Diet

This species feeds on sponges.
