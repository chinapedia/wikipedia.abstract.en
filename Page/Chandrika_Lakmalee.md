CHANDRIKA LAKMALEE (born 27 October 1978) is a Sri Lankan former cricketer. She played ten Women's One Day International matches and one Women's Test match for Sri Lanka women's national cricket team.[1] She was part of Sri Lanka's squad for the 2000 Women's Cricket World Cup.
