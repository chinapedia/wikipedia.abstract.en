The NORWEGIAN GYMNASTICS FEDERATION (, NGTF) is the national gymnastics association in Norway.

The Norwegian Gymnastics Federation was founded in 1890 as _De Norske Turn- og Gymnastikkforeninger_, and gymnastics was the first sport in Norway to be organized as a federation. The federation is a member of the Norwegian Confederation of Sports (NIF), the European Union of Gymnastics (UEG) and the International Federation of Gymnastics (FIG). Its headquarters are in Oslo.[1][2]
