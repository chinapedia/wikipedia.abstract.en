The LITTLE VALENTINE RIVER is a river in Cochrane District in northeastern Ontario, Canada.[1] It is in the James Bay drainage basin, and is a right tributary of the Valentine River.


Course

The Valentine River begins on open ground in geographic Way Township[2] and flows west into geographic Irish Township.[3] It turns northwest, enters geographic Stoddart Township,[4] and reaches its mouth at the Valentine River. The Valentine River flows via the Pivabiska River, the Missinaibi River and the Moose River to James Bay.
