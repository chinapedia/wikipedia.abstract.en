Datuk Syed HASHIM BIN ABDULLAH served as the National Chief Commissioner of the Persekutuan Pengakap Malaysia. In 1974, he was awarded the 87th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
