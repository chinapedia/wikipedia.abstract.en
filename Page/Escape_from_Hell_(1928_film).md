_ESCAPE FROM HELL_ (German: _FLUCHT AUS DER HÖLLE_) is a 1928 German silent drama film directed by Georg Asagaroff and starring Jean Murat, Ágnes Eszterházy and Louis Ralph.[1]

The film's art direction was by Alexander Ferenczy.
