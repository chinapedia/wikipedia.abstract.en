ZENATHA GOEIEMAN COLEMAN (born 25 September 1993) is a Namibian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Spanish club Valencia CF and Namibia women's national team.


International career

Coleman was part of the Namibian team at the 2014 African Women's Championship.[1]
