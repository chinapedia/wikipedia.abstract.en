LYRIC THEATER, also known as the THESPIAN HALL, a historic theatre located at Boonville, Cooper County, Missouri. It was built in 1855-1857, and is a two-story, rectangular Greek Revival style brick building. The front facade features a portico with four unfluted Doric order columns constructed of wedged shaped brick. The building was enlarged in 1901. When constructed, the basement was originally used as reading room; main floor used as combined theater and lecture hall or auditorium. The second floor housed the city hall, Masonic lodge, and Odd Fellows hall.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1969.[2] It is located in Historic District D.
