M. MOKSHED ALI was an Indian politician, belonging to the Revolutionary Communist Party of India.[1]

Ali won the Santipur seat in the West Bengal Legislative Assembly in the 1969 election.[2] Ali and the other RCPI legislator Anandi Das were expelled from RCPI by party general secretary Sudhin Kumar in July 1969 for "anti-Party and anti-UF activities".[3][4][5] In the 1971 West Bengal Legislative Assembly election Ali ran as an independent in Santipur, against the official RCPI candidate Bimalananda Mukherjee.[6] He finished in third place with 3,744 votes (8.84%).[7]
