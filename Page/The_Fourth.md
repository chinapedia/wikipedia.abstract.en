_THE FOURTH_ is a Russian 2016 short film written, produced and directed by Kirill Safonov starring Polina Kutepova and Valentin Gaft.[1][2]


Plot

Based on actual events. In late 19th century, a young woman deals with a problem of choice: illusions or reality, past or present, kill or give life? A question arises, whether if choice exists at all.
