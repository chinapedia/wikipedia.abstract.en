_PROMICROMONOSPORA ENDOPHYTICA_ is a Gram-positive and aerobic bacterium from the genus of _Promicromonospora_ which has been isolated from the surface of the root of the tree _Eucalyptus microcarpa_ from the Bedford Park in Adelaide in Australia.[1][2][3][4][5]
