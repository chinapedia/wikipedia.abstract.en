'''Rachael Yamala ''' (born 12 February 1975) is a Nigerian footballer who played as a midfielder for the Nigeria women's national football team. She was part of the team at the inaugural 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level she played for Kakanfo Babes in Nigeria.[1]
