KALLAHALLI is a village in Mysore district of Karnataka state, India. Hunsur._Kallahalli.jpg


Location

Kallahalli is located between Hunsur and Gonikoppal in the Mysore district. It is located west of Mysore town and from Bangalore.


Tourist attractions

Kallahalli Srinivasa temple is a tourist attraction in Kallahalli.[1]


Post office

There is a post office in Kallahalli and the pincode is 571105


Administration

Kallahalli is administered as part of Hunsur Taluk in Mysore district.
