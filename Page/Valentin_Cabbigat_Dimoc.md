VALENTIN CABBIGAT DIMOC is a Filipino Bishop and the current Vicar-Apostolic of the Apostolic Vicariate of Bontoc-Lagawe since 2015.[1]


Early life

He was born on February 14, 1969, in Lagawe, Ifugao. Appointed by Pope Francis in 2015 as Vicar-Apostolic of Bontoc- Lagawe titular bishop of Bapara.[2]
