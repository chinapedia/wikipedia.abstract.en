CHARLES CHURCH (7 July 1929 – 11 April 2010) was a Scottish amateur football forward who made over 200 appearances in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

Church attended St Aloysius' College, Glasgow.[2] He was married with two sons and a daughter and worked for his family's bookmaking company and then as a civil servant.[3]


Honours

QUEEN'S PARK

-   Scottish League Second Division: 1955–56[4]
