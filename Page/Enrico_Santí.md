ENRICO SANTÍ is an American professor and author or Hispanic and Latin American culture, currently the William T. Bryan Professor of Spanish and Hispanic Studies, and also formerly the Director of Graduate Studies, at University of Kentucky,[1][2] and also a published author.[3] In 1996, he was also the Emilio Bacardí Moreau Visiting Professor at University of Miami. He is a Guggenheim Fellow.[4]
