'''Petra Damm ''' (born 20 March 1961) is a German women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Germany women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1991 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she plays for VfR Eintracht Wolfsburg in Germany.[1]
