'''Samantha Ceroni ''' (born 26 February 1973 in Ponte San Pietro) is an Italian former footballer who played as a midfielder for the Italy women's national football team. She was part of the team at the UEFA Women's Euro 2001. On club level she played for Ruco Line Lazio in Italy.[1]
