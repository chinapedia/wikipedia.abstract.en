CHRISTOS PAPAGEORGIOU ( Athens, Greece October 13, 1944) served as the Secretary General of the International Link of Orthodox Christian Scouts, and now serves as honorary President.

In 2016, Papageorgiou was awarded the 353rd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
