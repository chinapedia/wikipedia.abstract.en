[1] is a Japanese kabuki actor. His trade name is _Yorozuya_. His mon is the _Kirichō_.


Biography

Nakamura is the eldest son of Shinjiro Nakamura (currently as Nakamura Kinnosuke II). He debuted at the kabuki-za _Sugawara Denju Tenarai Kagami_ as Kotaro in February 2002. In December 2007 Nakamura won the National Theatre of Japan Special Award for his role as Sachi in _Horibe Yahee_.
