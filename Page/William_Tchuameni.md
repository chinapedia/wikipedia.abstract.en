WILLIAM TCHUAMENI KOUEMO (born 25 December 1996) is a Cameroonian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Danish Superliga club SønderjyskE.[1][2]


Career

SønderjyskE

Tchuameni joined Danish club SønderjyskE in July 2017.[3] He left the club after two years.[4]
