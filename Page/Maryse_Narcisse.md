MARYSE NARCISSE (born 1958) is a Haitian politician.[1] She was a candidate in the 2016–17 presidential election, representing the Fanmi Lavalas party.[2]


Biography

Maryse Narcisse was born in 1958 in Pétion-Ville.[3] She is the daughter of Denise Péan Narcisse, a teacher, and Marc Narcissus, a judge.[4] She holds a Masters in Public Health from Tulane University in the United States.[5] She was married twice, the first time when she was 23, and she has had one son.[6]
