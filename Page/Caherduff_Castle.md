CAHERDUFF CASTLE ([1]) is a tower house and National Monument located in Achill Island, Ireland.[2]


Location

Caherduff Castle is located north of Lough Corrib, northwest of Cross, County Mayo.


History

The townland takes its name from the Irish for "black fort."[3]

Caherduff Castle was acquired by the state in 1918.[4]
