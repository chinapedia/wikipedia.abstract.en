RAFAEL RIPOZ, O.P. (1553–1620) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Perpignan-Elne (1618–1620).[1][2][3]


Biography

Rafael Ripoz was born in Barcelona, Spain, in 1553 and ordained a priest in the Order of Preachers.[4] He was appointed Bishop of Perpignan-Elne on 12 November 1618, during the papacy of Pope Paul V.[5] On 25 November 1618, he was consecrated bishop by Giovanni Garzia Mellini, Cardinal-Priest of Santi Quattro Coronati, with Diego Alvarez (archbishop), Archbishop of Trani, and Alessandro Guidiccioni (iuniore), Bishop of Lucca, serving as co-consecrators.[6] He served as Bishop of Perpignan-Elne until his death on 17 December 1620.[7]
