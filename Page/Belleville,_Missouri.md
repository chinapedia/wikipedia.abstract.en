BELLEVILLE is an unincorporated community in Jasper County, Missouri, United States.[1]


History

Belleville was named after a mining official.[2] Variant names were "Bellville" and "Zincite".[3] A post office called Zincite was established in 1885, and remained in operation until 1918.[4]
