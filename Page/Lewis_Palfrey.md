LEWIS PALFREY (born 25 February 1990) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a stand-off or fullback for the Rochdale Hornets in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Palfrey began his career at the Salford City Reds.[2] He has also played for Whitehaven,[3] Batley Bulldogs[4] and Oldham.[5]
