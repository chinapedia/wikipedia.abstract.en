LISLETOWN is an extinct town in Osage County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

Variant names were "Lisle" and "Mariosa Delta".[2] A post office called Lisle was established in 1842, and remained in operation until 1860.[3] The community was named after Benjamin Lisle, a first settler.[4] As of 2016, Mari Osa Delta Campground occupies the site.[5][6]
