JOSÉ ELEONILDO SOARES, better known as PINTO ITAMARATY (born May 10, 1960 in São Luís) is a Brazilian politician. He was alderman (2001–2007) and federal deputy (2007–2015). Itamaraty is senator from Maranhão.[1]
