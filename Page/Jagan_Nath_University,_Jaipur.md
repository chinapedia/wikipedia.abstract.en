JAGAN NATH UNIVERSITY, JAIPUR, Village Rampura, Tehsil Chaksu, Jaipur (Rajasthan) is a private university founded in 2008 in Rajasthan.[1] Professor Dr. Madan Mohan Goel is Vice-Chancellor of the University.[2] . UGC has approved the University after submission of compliance.[3]
