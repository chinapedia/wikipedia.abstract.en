is a railway station on the Ōu Main Line in Tendō, Yamagata, Japan, operated by East Japan Railway Company (JR East).


Lines

Tendō-Minami Station is served by the Ōu Main Line, and is located 98.3 rail kilometers from the terminus of the line at Fukushima Station.


Station layout

Tendō-Minami Station has one side platform for bi-directional traffic. The station is unstaffed.

History

Tendō-Minami Station opened on 14 March 2015.[1]
