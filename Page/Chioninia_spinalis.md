_CHIONINIA SPINALIS_ is a species of skink in the family Scincidae. It is endemic to the Cape Verde Islands, and has been found on the islands of Sal, Boa Vista, Maio, Santiago, Fogo and several smaller islets.[1][2]


Subspecies

There are five subspecies:[3]

-   _Chioninia spinalis boavistensis_ Miralles, Vasconcelos, Perera, Harris & Carranza, 2010[4]
-   _Chioninia spinalis maioensis_ (Mertens, 1955)
-   _Chioninia spinalis salensis_ (Angel, 1935)
-   _Chioninia spinalis santiagoensis_ Miralles, Vasconcelos, Perera, Harris & Carranza, 2010[5]
-   _Chioninia spinalis spinalis_ (Boulenger, 1906)
