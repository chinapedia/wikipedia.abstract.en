MARIE HOURIHAN (born 10 March 1987) is an Irish footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Braga. She previously played for Chelsea, Birmingham City and Manchester City W.F.C..[1][2][3]


Playing career

Birmingham City, 2011–2013

Hourihan's debut for Birmingham City was a 4–0 shutout win against Bristol City on 14 April 2011.[4] She made 11 appearances for the club during the 2011 FA WSL season helping the team finish in second place with a record.[5]

During the 2012 FA WSL season, she made seven appearances to help the team finish in second place with a record.[6]

Chelsea, 2013–2015

In July 2013, Hourihan signed with Chelsea L.F.C. for the 2013 FA WSL season.[7]

Manchester City, 2016–2018

In December 2015, Hourihan signed with Manchester City W.F.C. for the 2016 FA WSL season.[8]

Brighton, 2018–present

Hourihan joined Brighton & Hove Albion W.F.C. in January 2018, on a loan deal until the end of the season.[9] The move was made permanent in the summer of 2018.[10]


Personal life

Hourihan is a second cousin of the footballer Conor Hourihane.[11]
