_CACIA SEMILUCTUOSA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Blanchard in 1853.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Cacia semiluctuosa reducta_ Breuning, 1939
-   _Cacia semiluctuosa semiluctuosa_ Blanchard, 1853
