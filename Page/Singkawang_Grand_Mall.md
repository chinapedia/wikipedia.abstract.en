SINGKAWANG GRAND MALL (abbreviated as SGM) is a shopping mall in Singkawang City, West Kalimantan Province, Republic of Indonesia. This mall was officially opened in 2015 and since that time become the largest shopping mall in West Kalimantan. The building has 12 floors, with the lowest 5 floors allocated for shopping mall and the rest are for hotel.
