THANGARASU NATARAJAN (born 27 May 1991) is an Indian cricketer.[1]


Career

Tamil Nadu cricket team

He made his first-class debut for Tamil Nadu in the 2014–15 Ranji Trophy on 5 January 2015.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Tamil Nadu in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 29 January 2017.[3] He made his List A debut for Tamil Nadu in the 2018–19 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 20 September 2018.[4]

Indian Premier League

In February 2017, he was bought by the Kings XI Punjab team for the 2017 Indian Premier League for .[5]

In January 2018, he was bought by the Sunrisers Hyderabad in the 2018 IPL auction.[6]
