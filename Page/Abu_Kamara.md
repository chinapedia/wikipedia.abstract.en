ABU RAZARD KAMARA (born 1 April 1997) is a Liberian professional footballer who plays for Rudar Velenje.


Club career

In March 2014, Abu Kamara began his senior career with Liberian first division club Ganta Black Stars.

In 2016, Kamara signed for the Lao Premier League team CSC Champa, where he became the top goalscorer for the 2016 season.[1][2]
