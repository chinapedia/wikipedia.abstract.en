ANTHONY THNG BOCK BOH served as the Chairman of the Asia-Pacific Scout Region from 2001 to 2004, as well as International Commissioner of the Singapore Scout Association.

In 2005, he was awarded the 307th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
