The PINGTUNG HAKKA CULTURAL MUSEUM () is a museum about Hakka people in Zhutian Township, Pingtung County, Taiwan.


History

The museum was opened in 2001.[1]


Architecture

The museum building was built with Hakka architectural layout over two floors in a cage layout.[2][3] The exhibition areas are divided into the Hakka cultivation, Hakka farm life, Hakka life, Hakka culture and heritage.[4]


Transportation

The museum is accessible within walking distance north west of Xishi Station of Taiwan Railways.


Opening time

The museum is opened everyday except Mondays from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.[5]
