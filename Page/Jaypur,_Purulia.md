JAYPUR (also spelled JAIPUR or JOYPUR) is a census town in Joypur CD Block in Purulia Sadar West subdivision of Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India, Jaypur had a total population of 10,259 of which 5,344 (52%) were males and 4,915 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 1,373. The total number of literates in Jaypur was 5,893 (66.32% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Police station

Joypur police station has jurisdiction over Joypur CD Block. The area covered is 230.50 km² and the population covered is 133,240. It has 52 km of inter-state borders with Jaridih, Balidih and Pindrajora police stations in Bokaro district of Jharkhand.[2][3]


Transport

SH 4A running from Tulin to Chas Morh (both in Purulia district) passes through this block.[4]

There is a station at Garh Jaipur on the Purulia-Kotshila branch line of South Eastern Railway.[5]


Healthcare

Joypur Block Primary Health Centre functions with 15 beds.[6]
