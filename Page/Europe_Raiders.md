_EUROPE RAIDERS_ () is a 2018 action thriller film from. It the third film in the Jingle Ma-directed Hong Kong cinema franchise, following _Tokyo Raiders_ (2000) and _Seoul Raiders_ (2006).[1] The film was released on August 17, 2018.[2]


Plot

Mr. Lin and Ms. Wang, two bounty hunters, have to find Hand of God. Sophie wanted Rocky released from prison to obtain the software upgrades to the Hand of God which was created by him.

Lin Zai Feng and Wang Chao Ying are known respectively as the undisputed number 1 and 2 of the bounty hunter world for many years. When a surveillance program 'God's Handâ€˜ created by world's top hacker Mercury gets stolen by Sophie, the CIA sends both Lin and Wang to investigate.

Production

Filming began on August 31, 2016 in Italy.[5]


Reception

As of the opening weekend, the Europe Raiders film has received a score of 9/10 review from Avi Offer, a movie critique, who said it was a crowd pleasing thrill ride.[6]

The film debuted promisingly in second place on its opening Friday in China, but dropped off to the fifth and the seventh place on the weekend due to terrible word of mouth.[7]

It currently holds a 3.7 out of 10 on the Chinese review site Douban.[8]
