STEPHAN DE FREITAS BARCHA (born 27 October 1989) is a Brazilian Olympic show jumping rider.[1] He competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, where he finished 5th in the team and 60th in the individual competiiton
