The 1937 PACIFIC TIGERS FOOTBALL TEAM represented the College of the Pacific (COP)[1] during the 1937 college football season.

COP competed in the Far Western Conference (FWC).[2] The team was led by head coach Amos Alonzo Stagg, and played home games at Baxter Stadium in Stockton, California. This was Stagg's 48th year as a head coach and his fifth at College of the Pacific. The Tigers finished with three wins, five losses and two ties (3–5–2, 3–1 FWC). Overall, the Tigers were outscored by their opponents 58–122 for the season.[3]
