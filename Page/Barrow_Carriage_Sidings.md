BARROW CARRIAGE SIDINGS are located in Barrow-in-Furness, Cumbria, England, near Barrow-in-Furness station on the Cumbrian Coast Line.


Present

Stabling is provided for Northern's Mark 2 carriages, (hauled by their Class 37 locomotives)[1]Class 150/156 Sprinters[2] and Class 185 Desiros (on lease to Northern from TransPennine Express).[3]
