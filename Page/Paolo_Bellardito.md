Biography

On 17 October 1580, Paolo Bellardito was appointed during the papacy of Pope Gregory XIII as Bishop of Lipari.[2][3] On 28 Oct 1580, he was consecrated bishop by Giovanni Antonio Facchinetti de Nuce, Titular Patriarch of Jerusalem with Bartolomeo Ferratini (iuniore), Bishop Emeritus of Amelia, and Giovanni Battista Soriani, Bishop of Bisceglie, serving as co-consecrators.[4]

He served as Bishop of Lipari until his resignation in 1585.[5][6]
