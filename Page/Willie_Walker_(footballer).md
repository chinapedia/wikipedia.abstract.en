WILLIAM F. WALKER was a Scottish amateur football centre forward who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

Walker's brothers Jim and Frank were also Queen's Park footballers.[2] Prior to the First World War, Walker worked for the Todd & Walker law firm in Paisley.[3] During the war he served as a lieutenant in the Argyll and Sutherland Highlanders and was wounded in May 1917.[4]
