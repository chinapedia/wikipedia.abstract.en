Early life and background

Ahmed was born on 17 November 1953 in Khustia, Khulna Division.[9] His birth name is Mizanur Rahman. During his childhood, he was very interested in the theatre. Following year, he involved local drama theatre group.


Film career

In 1978, Ahmed made his film debut with the film Trishna.[10] After a couple of years later, he established himself as a villain in the Dhallywood film industry. He is also producing film from his own banner _Friends Movies_.[11]


Death

At the age of 63, Ahmed died on 27 March 2017 due to cardiac arrest.[12]
