_THE LITTLE SLAVE_ (German: _DIE KLEINE SKLAVIN_) is a 1928 German silent comedy film directed by Jacob Fleck and Luise Fleck and starring Grete Mosheim, Fritz Richard and Trude Hesterberg.[1]

The film's art direction was by Erich Zander.
