BÁRBARA GATICA (born 16 August 1996) is a Chilean tennis player.

She has a career-high WTA singles ranking of 440, achieved on 23 October 2017. She also has a career-high WTA doubles ranking of 490, achieved on 30 April 2018. Gatica has won one singles title and ten doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit.

Playing for Chile in Fed Cup, Gatica has a win/loss record of 8–7.
