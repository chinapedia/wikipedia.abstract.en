ÉMILIE JOUVET, born 29 July 1976, is a French filmmaker, photographer and contemporary artist.


Biography

In 2006 she directed her first feature, a post-porn and feminist pro-gender queer lesbian and transgender film, _One Night Stand_.[1]

In 2009 she assembled an ephemeral troupe of performers, consisting of Wendy Delorme, Judy Minx, DJ Metzgerei, Mad Kate, Sadie Lune and Madison Young, for a European tour during the shooting of her film _Too Much Pussy! Feminist Sluts, A Queer X Show_. This feminist sex-positive, documentary road movie came out in French cinemas in 2010.[2]

Her first monograph of photographs, 'Émilie Jouvet The Book', was published by Womart editions in 2014.
