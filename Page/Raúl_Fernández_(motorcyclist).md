RAÚL FERNÁNDEZ GONZÁLEZ (born 23 October 2000) is a Spanish motorcycle racer. He competes in the Moto3 World Championship aboard a KTM RC 250 GP.


Career

Fernández competed in the Red Bull MotoGP Rookies Cup in 2015 and 2016.[1] In the latter year he also made his Grand Prix debut at Valencia in the Moto3 class as the replacement for the injured María Herrera,[2] scoring championship points with an 11th place.
