Early life

He was born on 14 January 1924 in Kathira, Barisal, East Bengal, British Raj. He graduated from Bishnupur Mission School and Brojomohun College.[2]


Career

He was the president of the Bangladesh Baptist Sangha from 1968 to 1997 and Bangladesh Church Council. He was a state minister and afterwards he was a government adviser on NGO Affairs. He collaborated with poet Jibananda Das.[3]


Death

He died on 15 July 1997 in Kolkata, West Bengal, India from Cancer.[4][5]
