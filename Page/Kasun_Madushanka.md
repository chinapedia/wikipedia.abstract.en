KASUN MADUSHANKA (born 16 July 1991) is a Sri Lankan first-class cricketer.[1] He was selected for Sri Lanka's Test squad for their tour of Zimbabwe in October 2016, but he did not play.[2]

In August 2018, he was named in Galle's squad the 2018 SLC T20 League.[3]
