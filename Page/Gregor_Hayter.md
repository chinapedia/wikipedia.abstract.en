GREGOR HAYTER (born 13 August 1976 in Scotland) is a Scottish former rugby union player who played for Glasgow Warriors at the Lock position. He also previously played for Caledonia Reds and Edinburgh Rugby.[1]

He also spent five years in Italy playing for Crociati Parma Rugby FC and Rugby Viadana.[2]

He also played for Newbury,[3][4] Rotherham Titans and played and coached Sheffield Tigers.[5]

He played for England Counties XV and Scotland at age grade.[6]

He now works as an Electrical Technician.[7]
