THOMAS GARDNER (17 March 1923 – 7 November 2016) was an English footballer who played as a winger.[1]

Gardner made one appearance for Everton in the 1947–48 season before being forced to retire through injury.[2]

Gardner died on 7 November 2016 at the age of 93. He was Everton's oldest surviving player at the time of his death.[3]
