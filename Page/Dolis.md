is a josei manga by Maki Kusumoto.[1][2] Published in Japan by Shodensha and licensed in English by Tokyopop.[3][4]


Characters

-   Mitsu – Deeply disturbed female protagonist with an interest in art.[5][6][7]
-   Kishi – Love–struck male protagonist working as a bookstore clerk.[8][9][10]


Reception

Dominic Nguyen of _Newtype USA_ and Carlo Santos of _Anime News Network_ have noted the artwork in this romantic tragedy.[11][12]
