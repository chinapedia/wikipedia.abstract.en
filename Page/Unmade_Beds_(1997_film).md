_UNMADE BEDS_ is a 1997 art house film[1] by the British director Nicholas Barker set in the United States. It shows the lives of four New York singles, playing themselves.[2][3]

It won the Bronze Horse prize for best 1997 feature film in the Stockholm International Film Festival.[4]
