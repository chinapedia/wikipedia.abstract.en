KRISTIAN HANSEN is a Norwegian handball player.

He made his debut on the Norwegian national team in 1995, and played 57 matches for the national team between 1995 and 2001.[1] He competed at the 1997 World Men's Handball Championship, and again at the 2001 World Men's Handball Championship.
