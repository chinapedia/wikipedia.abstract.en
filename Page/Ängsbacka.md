ÄNGSBACKA is a course and festival center focused on personal development, spirituality and self-expression. It is located in Molkom, Sweden, and was started in 1996.[1]

The center hosts an average of seven festivals per year,[2] mainly during the summer. The No Mind Festival is the largest of them with around a thousand participants.

It is registered as a not-for-profit organization[3] owned by members and is run in a large part by volunteers.

The course center and its No Mind Festival were the feature of the 2008 documentary _Three Miles North of Molkom_.
