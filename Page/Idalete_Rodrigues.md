IDALETE RODRIGUES DOS SANTOS, better known as IDALETE RODRIGUES (born November 9, 1984 in Pinheiro) is a Brazilian politician. She was vice-president of UEP (2002–2009) and councilor of CEJOVEM (2009–2011). Rodrigues is a president of Socialist Youth of the PDT in Maranhão since December 2011.[1]
