PAULA PELÁEZ G. served as the President of the Asociación de Guías y Scouts de Chile. In 2000, Peláez was awarded the 285th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1][2]
