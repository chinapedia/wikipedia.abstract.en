JOHN HULL MOLLENKOPF (born March 16, 1946) is an American political scientist and sociologist, currently the Distinguished Professor of Political Science and Sociology and also Director of Center of Urban Research at City University of New York,[1][2] and also a published author in both research and books.[3][4] He has also been a visiting scholar at Russell Sage Foundation.[5] He attended Harvard University for his MA and PhD.[6]
