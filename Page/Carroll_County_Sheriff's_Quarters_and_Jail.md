CARROLL COUNTY SHERIFF'S QUARTERS AND JAIL is a historic combined sheriffs residence and jail located at Carrollton, Carroll County, Missouri. It was built in 1878, and consists of the two-story brick residence with an attached jail constructed in 1958 to replace the original jail, which had collapsed. The residence is a Classical Revival style brick building topped by a hipped roof.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1979.[2]
