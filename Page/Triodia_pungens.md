_TRIODIA PUNGENS_, commonly known as SOFT SPINIFEX, is a species of grass native to northwestern Australia.[1]

Originally described by botanist Robert Brown in his 1810 work _Prodromus Florae Novae Hollandiae_,[2] _Triodia pungens_ still bears its original binomial name.
