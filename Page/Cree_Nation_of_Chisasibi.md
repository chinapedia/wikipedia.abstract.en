Cris_Chisasibi.jpg The CREE NATION OF CHISASIBI is a Cree First Nation in Quebec, Canada. It is headquartered at the Cree village of Chisasibi in the Eeyou Istchee territory in Northern Quebec. The nation also has a _terre réservée crie_ of the same name covering 776 km² around the village. The Cree Nation of Chisasibi is in negotiation with the government of Canada to obtain its self-governance.[1] In 2016, it has a registered population of 4,585 members.[2]
