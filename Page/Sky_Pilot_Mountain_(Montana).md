SKY PILOT MOUNTAIN () is in the Beartooth Mountains in the U.S. state of Montana.[1] The peak is in the Absaroka-Beartooth Wilderness on the borders of Custer and Gallatin National Forests. The Hopper Glacier lies on the east flank of the peak.
