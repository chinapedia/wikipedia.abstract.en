_Monochamus mutator_}} _MONOCHAMUS CLAMATOR_, the SPOTTED PINE SAWYER,[1] is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by John Lawrence LeConte in 1852.[2]

-   _Monochamus clamator linsleyi_ Dillon & Dillon, 1941
-   _Monochamus clamator nevadensis_ Dillon & Dillon, 1941
-   _Monochamus clamator rubigineus_ (Bates, 1880)
