ION HORTOPAN ( in Câmpina - 1980) was a Romanian male former weightlifter, who competed in the 52 kg category and represented Romania at international competitions. He won the bronze medal in the snatch at the 1973 World Weightlifting Championships lifting 100.0 kg. He participated at the 1972 Summer Olympics in the 52 kg event.[1]
