JOBE is an unincorporated community in Oregon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A variant name was "Job".[2] A post office called Jobe was established in 1848, and closed in 1888. The post reopened as Job in 1892, and closed in 1906.[3] The community has the name of Jacob Job, an early settler.[4]
