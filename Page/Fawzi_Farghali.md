FAWZI FARGHALI () (-August 31, 2009 in Cairo) served as Arab Regional Scout Executive for the World Scout Bureau.

In 1986, he was awarded the 186th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
