MISCHA DOHLER is a Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering, Fellow of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts (RSA) and a Distinguished Member of Harvard Square Leaders Excellence. He is Professor in Wireless Communications at King's College London, where he directs the Centre for Telecommunications Research. [1][2][3][4]


Life and career

He was CTO Worldsensing .[5]
