DAVID FRASER THOMAS (born January 1963) is a British businessman; the chief executive (CEO) of the housebuilder Barratt Developments.


Early life

David Fraser Thomas was born in January 1963.[1] He has a degree from the University of Strathclyde, and is a chartered accountant.[2]


Career

Thomas was deputy CEO and finance director of Game Group form 2007 to 2009.[3]

Thomas was finance director of Barratt since July 2009, when he took over from Mark Clare, who had been CEO for nine years, in July 2015.[4][5]

Thomas has been CEO of Barratt since 2015.[6]
