DESMOND GERALD FITZGERALD (1834-1905) was an English electrician and spiritualist.

Fitzgerald founded a weekly magazine known as _Electrician_ in 1861. It later became a monthly magazine.[1] Fitzgerald was also involved in improving the manufacture of white lead.[2]

Fitzgerald was a vice-president of the British National Association of Spiritualists and an editor for a spiritualist journal _Spiritual Notes_. He was a convinced believer in mesmerism and spiritualism. He also defended the discredited "Odic force" of Carl Reichenbach.[3]

He was an early council member of the Society for Psychical Research.[4]
