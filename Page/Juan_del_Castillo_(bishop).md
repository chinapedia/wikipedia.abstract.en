Biography

Juan del Castillo was born in Quintanar de la Orden, Spain.[2] On 28 April 1564, Juan del Castillo was appointed during the papacy of Pope Pius IV as Bishop of Santiago de Cuba.[3][4] In June 1567, he was consecrated bishop.[5] He served as Bishop of Santiago de Cuba until his resignation on 3 October 1578.[6][7] He died in June 1593.[8]
