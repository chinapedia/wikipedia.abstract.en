TEONA BAKRADZE (, born January 24, 1996) is a Georgian football forward. She is a member of the Georgian national team. Currently, she is playing in the Turkish Women's First Football League for Hakkarigücü Spor with jersey number 77.[1]


Playing career

Club

TeonaBakradze_(12).jpg in the 2018–19 Turkish Women's First League. ]]

She played for FC Hereti Lagodekhi in Georgia,[2][3] before she moved to Turkey in November 2916, and joined Kdz. Ereğlispor to play in the Turkish Women's First Football League.[4][5]

In the 2018–19season, she transferred to the newly-promoted First League club Hakkarigücü Spor.[6][7]

International

Bakradze appeared for Georgia women's national football team in four matches at the UEFA Women's Euro 2017 qualifying Group 6 in 2016 and in three matches at the 2019 FIFA Women's World Cup qualification – UEFA preliminary round Group 1 in 2017.[8][9]
