PAT WALKER (born 24 March 1986) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a stand off or loose forward for the Sheffield Eagles in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Walker has previously played for the Sheffield Eagles, Dewsbury Rams and the Batley Bulldogs.[2]
