_NARI_ is a monthly women's magazine published nationally in Nepal by Kantipur Publications, a media company based in Kathmandu.


History and profile

The magazine was started as _Sarbottam_ in 2002.[1] Two years later it was renamed _Sarbottam Nari_.[2] The magazine is part of Kantipur Publications.[3] It is published in Nepali on a monthly basis.[4][5] It is also published to the Nepali diaspora in Hongkong, Malaysia, Japan and Australia.[6] Subash Dhakal is editor in chief of Nari Magazine.
