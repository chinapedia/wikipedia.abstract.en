is the second studio album by Japanese singer and songwriter Rina Aiuchi. It was released on 15 May 2002 by Giza Studio[1] . The dance music-influenced J-pop album has spawned six singles, including four Japan top 5 singles: "Navy Blue", "Forever You: Eien ni Kimi to", "I Can't Stop My Love for You", and "Can You Feel the Power of Words?". The album topped on the _Oricon_ albums chart in its first week and charted for 13 weeks on the chart, making it Aiuchi's best-selling album[2].

In support of the album, Aiuchi embarked on the concert tour entitled _Rina Aiuchi Live Tour 2002 "Power of Words"_ from May 2002[3].
