MIGUEL A. MARINO is an American engineer, currently the Distinguished Professor Emeritus of Hydrologic Sciences, Civil and Environmental Engineering and Biological & Agricultural Engineering at University of California, Davis,[1] and also a published author.[2] He was honored as a Distinguished Member of the American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE) in 1999, and a Life Member in 2005, as well as being the ASCE's Editor of its journal Journal of Water Resources Planning and Management in 1984-88.[3]

-   Ph.D., Engineering (1972) University of California, Los Angeles
