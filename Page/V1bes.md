_V1BES_ is the second mixtape by american singer and actor Leon Thomas III released on January 1, 2014 by Rostrum Records[1][2] at midnight via Datpiff.[3] It features remixes of the song "Bonita Applebum" by A Tribe Called Quest, and Wu-Tang Forever by rapper Drake. The song "Chill" contains samples from the 1989 Spike Lee film Do The Right Thing.


Track listing

Thomas composed all of the songs himself, except where noted.
