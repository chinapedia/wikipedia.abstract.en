LASSE SIGURDSEN (born 1 June 1997) is a Norwegian football striker who plays for 2. Divisjon side Fløy on loan from Tippeligaen side IK Start.

He started his career in Fløy, and played for the senior team before joining IK Start. He then made his Tippeligaen debut in March 2016 as a last-minute substitute against Lillestrøm.[1] In 2018 he returned to Fløy on loan.[2]
