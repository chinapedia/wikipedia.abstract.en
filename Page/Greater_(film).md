_GREATER_ is a 2016 American biographical sports film directed by David Hunt and starring Christopher Severio as American football player Brandon Burlsworth.[1][2] The film was released on August 26, 2016.[3]


Cast

-   Christopher Severio as Brandon Burlsworth
-   Neal McDonough as Marty Burlsworth
-   Leslie Easterbrook as Barbara
-   Michael Parks as Leo
-   Nick Searcy as The Farmer
-   Wayne Duvall as Pastor Rick
-   Quinton Aaron as Coach Aaron


Reception

On Rotten Tomatoes the film has an approval rating of 67% based on 15 reviews, with an average rating of 6.7/10.[4] On Metacritic, the film has a score of 35 out of 100 based on 4 critics, indicating "generally unfavorable reviews".[5]
