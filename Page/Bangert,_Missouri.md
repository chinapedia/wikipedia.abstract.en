BANGERT is an unincorporated community in Dent County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

An early variant name was Avery.[2] A post office called Avery was in operation from 1880 until 1886, the name was changed to Bangert in 1902, and the post office closed in 1951.[3] Avery was the name of C. A. Avery, an early postmaster, and Bangert was the name of a railroad official.[4]
