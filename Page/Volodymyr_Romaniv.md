VOLODYMYR ROMANIV (born June 16, 1985) is a Ukrainian footballer who plays with FC Ukraine United in the Canadian Soccer League.


Playing career

Romaniv began his career in 2005 with FSC Bukovyna Chernivtsi in the Ukrainian Second League, where he appeared in 10 matches.[1] In 2011, he signed with FC Rukh Vynnyky in the Lviv Oblast League where won the Lviv Oblast title, Lviv Oblast Super Cup, and Lviv Oblast Cup. In 2016, he went overseas to Canada to play with FC Ukraine United of the Canadian Soccer League.[2] In his second season he assisted FC Ukraine in achieving a perfect season, and winning the Second Division Championship.[3] While in his third year he assisted in securing the First Division title.[4]
