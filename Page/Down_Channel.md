_DOWN CHANNEL_ is a 1929 British silent adventure film directed by Michael Barringer and starring Henry Victor, Alf Goddard and Roy Travers. It was made at Cricklewood Studios.[1]


Cast

-   Henry Victor as Smiler
-   Alf Goddard as Nixon
-   Christopher Anthony as The Boy
-   Roy Travers as Smuggler
