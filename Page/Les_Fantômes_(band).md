LES FANTÔMES were a French rock guitar group formed in 1961. They were an instrumental group modeled on the English group The Shadows.[1]

They made albums in Canada for ARC Records and in English Canada were known as "The Phantoms". They played instrumental covers of hits from groups like "The Ventures".

They released an album called "Wheels and Other Guitar Hits", in 1964.
