Waylon_Brown.jpg WAYLON BROWN (born July 13, 1979) is an American politician who was elected as a Republican member of the Iowa State Senate's 26nd district in 2016. He defeated Democratic incumbent Mary Jo Wilhelm.[1]

A resident of St. Ansgar, Iowa, he is a farmer and a small businessman who owns a construction company. He also serves as the Vice President of the Mitchell County, Iowa Farm Bureau Board.[2]
