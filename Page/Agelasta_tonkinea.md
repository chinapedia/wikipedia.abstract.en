_AGELASTA TONKINEA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Pic in 1925.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Agelasta tonkinea omeishana_ Gressitt, 1951
-   _Agelasta tonkinea palminsulana_ (Gressitt, 1940)
-   _Agelasta tonkinea tonkinea_ Pic, 1925
