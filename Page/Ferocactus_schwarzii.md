_FEROCACTUS SCHWARZII_ is a species of Ferocactus from Mexico.[1]


Distribution

_Ferocactus schwarzii_ is commonly found in Sinaloa, Mexico and occurs between elevations between 30 and 200 meters. The IUCN Red List classifies this species as "Data Deficient" (DD).[2]
