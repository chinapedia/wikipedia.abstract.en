RAHAMATULLA MOLLA (born 3 March 1987) is an Indian former athlete.

Rahamatulla, the son of a carpenter, is one of six siblings from a Muslim family in West Bengal.[1]

He won a bronze medal as a member of India's 4 x 100 metres relay team at the 2010 Commonwealth Games, which set a national record in the final.[2]
