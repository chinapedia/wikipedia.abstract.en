PETER G. JORDAN is the President of Tarrant County College, a community college in Tarrant County, Texas.[1] Formerly, he served as the Vice Chancellor of CUNY, as well as Vice President of Vaughn College of Aeronautics and Technology and Vice President of LaGuardia Community College.[2] He was also a Dean at New York Institute of Technology,[3] and a Dean at Adelphi University.[4] He served as an Overseer and trustee for Colby College.[5][6] He holds a bachelor's degree from Colby College, an M.S. from Polytechnic Institute of New York University, and a Ph.D from the University of Pennsylvania.[7][8][9]
