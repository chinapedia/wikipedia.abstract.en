SANDEEP PATTNAIK (born 17 May 1998) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Odisha in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 6 October 2016.[2] He scored his maiden century in his second match for Odisha on 13 October 2016.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Odisha in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 29 January 2017.[4] He made his List A debut for Odisha in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 25 February 2017.[5]
