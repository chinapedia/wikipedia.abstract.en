NATIONAL HIGHWAY 315A (NH 315A) is a National Highway in North East India that connects Khonsa in Arunachal Pradesh and Tinsukia in Assam.[1][2]


Route

Tinsukia - Naharkatia - Hukanjuri - Khonsa.[3][4][5]


Junctions

-   Junction with National Highway 15 near Tinsukia.[6][7]
-   Junction with National Highway 215 near Khonsa.[8][9]
