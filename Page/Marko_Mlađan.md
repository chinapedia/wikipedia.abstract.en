MARKO MLAĐAN (Anglicized: MARKO MLADJAN, ; born March 26, 1993), is a Swiss-Serbian professional basketball player for Lions de Genève. He is a 2.05 m (6 ft 9 in) tall forward.


Professional career

On September 21, 2016, he signed a one-year deal with Slovenian club Union Olimpija.[1]


Personal life

His older brother Dušan is also a professional basketball player.
