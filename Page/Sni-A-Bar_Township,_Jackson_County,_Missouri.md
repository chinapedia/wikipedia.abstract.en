SNI-A-BAR TOWNSHIP is an inactive township in Jackson County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

Sni-A-Bar Township was established in 1834, taking its name from Sni-A-Bar Creek.[2]

The township included Blue Springs, Oak Grove, and the village of Grain Valley. An atlas showing the townships of Jackson County in about 1930 is on page 2 at
