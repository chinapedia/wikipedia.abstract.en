AGNIESZKA MIERZEJEWSKA, née CIOŁEK (born 22 October 1985 in Zaklików) is a Polish long-distance runner.

At the 2016 European Athletics Championships she finished 9th in the women's half marathon.[1]

She won the 2016 Polish national championship in the marathon, but came two minutes and four sections shy of selection to compete in the 2016 Olympics.[2] She finished second overall in the race, but was the top finishing Pole.[3]

She finished 8th at the 2016 Chicago Marathon.[4]
