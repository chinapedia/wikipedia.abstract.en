RONIVALDO BERNARDO SALES is a footballer from Brazil who currently plays for Austria Lustenau.[1][2] Previously, he has played for Kapfenberger SV, Arapongas, and lastly Taubate.


Club career

He scored a hat-trick in a resounding 8-3 triumph against LASK Linz.[3] He scored another hat-trick against Floridsdorfer AC on September 12, 2017. https://newsoccerlive.com/soccer/austria/erste-liga/austria-lustenau-vs-floridsdorfer-ac/1-2532038/


Transfers

Ronivaldo transferred to FK Austria in the summer of 2015.[4]
