_DIESEL BROTHERS_ is an American reality television series. The series premiered on January 4, 2016, on Discovery Channel.[1][2]

The Guys Are Not Really Brothers. Although they look somewhat alike, they are only brothers in the “bro” sense of the word. Best buds “Heavy D” Sparks and “Diesel Dave” Kiley started a fix it shop for all types of vehicles and brought in two other guys as shop helpers[3].
