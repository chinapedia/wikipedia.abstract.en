RED EMPEROR may refer to:

-   Red Emperor or Red Deity (赤帝 _Chìdì_), one of the cosmological _Wǔfāng Shàngdì_ (五方上帝), gods of Chinese religion. The Red Emperor is the god of southern Chinese, associated to the directional south, fire, summer and the planet Mars.
-   Mao Zedong was also known as the "Red Emperor" due to his Communist and often violent rule,[1][2] juxtaposed to the benevolent founding figure of the Yellow Emperor

-   Emperor red snapper, a fish
-   "Emperor" type of grape from Australia
