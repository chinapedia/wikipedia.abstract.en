'''Karin Johanne Bredland ''' (born 7 January 1978) is a retired Norwegian women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Norway women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2003 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she played for Røa IL in Norway.[1]
