_LADO LUNAR_ was Rui Veloso's seventh album released on EMI Portugal in late 1995, the next in four years after _Auto da Pimenta_.[1] The album is named after the first track which means the lunar side in Portuguese and was recorded in Sintra, Portugal between July and August 1995.
