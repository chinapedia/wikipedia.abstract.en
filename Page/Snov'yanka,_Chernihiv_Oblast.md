SNOV'YANKA () is a town in the Chernihiv Oblast of Ukraine, with a current population of 626.


History

Snov'yanka was founded near a Bronze Age Neolithic settlement , two Scythian settlements from the period of ancient Rus and a barrow burial ground. The town was officially created near the end of the 1940s.
