HAYIM DE LAH ROZAH[1] (also spelled_' Chaim Delharoza[2] OR de la Rosa_') was a Spanish rabbi and kabbalist.[3]

After the Spanish expulsion, he wandered until he reached Jerusalem where he joined the _chavurah_ of Rabbi Shalom Sharabi, becoming a scholar of the Beit El Synagogue and a close disciple and friend of Rabbi Sharabi.[4]

Rabbi de lah Rozah authored the _sefer Torat Hakham_.[5][6]
