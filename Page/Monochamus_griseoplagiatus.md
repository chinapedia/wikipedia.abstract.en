_MONOCHAMUS GRISEOPLAGIATUS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by James Thomson in 1858. It is known from Sierra Leone and Gabon.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Monochamus griseoplagiatus griseoplagiatus_ Thomson, 1858
-   _Monochamus griseoplagiatus leonensis_ (Dillon & Dillon, 1961)
