Urrila-Ehrstedt-Kaleva-1964.jpg IRMA URRILA (born 29 January 1943) is a Finnish operatic soprano,[1] best known internationally for her role as Pamina in Ingmar Bergman's 1975 film production of Mozart's opera, _The Magic Flute_.

Urrila was born in Helsinki. In 1967, she sang the role of Marzelline in a film version of Beethoven's _Fidelio_ from the Savonlinna Opera Festival, and in 1978 she sang Tatjana in a film version of Tchaikovsky's _Eugene Onegin_.
