BUSHBURG (formerly spelled BUSHBERG) is an unincorporated community in Jefferson County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Bushberg was established in 1868, and remained in operation until 1904.[2] The community derives its name from Isador Bush, the operator of a local plant nursery.[3]
