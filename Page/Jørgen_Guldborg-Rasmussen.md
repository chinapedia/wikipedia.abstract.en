JØRGEN GULDBORG-RASMUSSEN served as the President of the Fællesrådet for Danmarks Drengespejdere Danish Scout Council, as well as a member of the National Board of the Danish Scout and Guide Association.[1]

In 1993, Rasmussen was awarded the 228th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2][3]
