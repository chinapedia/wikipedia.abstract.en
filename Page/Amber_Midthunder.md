AMBER MIDTHUNDER is an American actress. She is known for her main role in the FX series _Legion_, and appearances in _Longmire_ and _Banshee_.[1][2]


Biography

Amber's father, David Midthunder, is an actor and her mother is a casting director.[3] Amber is Native American and an enrolled member of the Fort Peck Sioux Tribe.[4][5]
