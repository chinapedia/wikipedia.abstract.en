DEVAKI is an Indian Tamil language film directed by R. S. Mani and released in 1951. The film features N. N. Kannappa and V. N. Janaki in the lead roles.[1]O

Soundtrack

Music was composed by G. Ramanathan and lyrics were penned by A. Maruthakasi, Ka. Mu. Sheriff and Kannadasan. Singers are T. M. Soundararajan, S. Balachander & Master Subbaiah. Playback singers are Thiruchi Loganathan, P. Leela, Jikki, N. L. Ganasaraswathi and A. G. Rathnamala.[4]
