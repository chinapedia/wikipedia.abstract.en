GUJARAT FLUOROCHEMICALS LIMITED (GFL) is India's largest producer (by volume) of chloromethanes, refrigerants and polytetrafluoro-ethylene (PTFE).[1] The firm, which was incorporated in 1987, is a subsidiary of Inox Leasing and Finance Limited. GFL has two manufacturing facilities in Gujarat, a refrigerant plant at Ranjitnagar, and a PTFE facility at Dahej.[2][3] It has two subsidiaries - Gujarat Fluorochemicals Americas LLC and Gujarat Fluorochemicals GmbH.

Inox Group

Inox Group has interests in various businesses including industrial gases, chemicals, refrigerants, cryogenic engineering, engineering plastics, renewable energy and entertainment. The group employs over 9,000 people and has a distribution network that is spread across more than 50 countries. Besides Gujarat Fluorochemicals Limited, the group includes Inox Air Products Limited, Inox Wind Limited, Inox India Limited, Inox Renewables Limited, and Inox Leisure Limited.[6]
