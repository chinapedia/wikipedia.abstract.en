_SUNDAY BEAUTY QUEEN_ is a 2016 Philippine documentary film directed by Baby Ruth Villarama and follows a group of expatriate domestic workers in Hong Kong as they prepare to take part in an annual beauty pageant. It made its world premiere in the Wide Angle documentary competition at the 21st Busan International Film Festival in 2016.[1][2]

The film is an official entry to the 2016 Metro Manila Film Festival.[3][4]
