_PAL PAL DIL KE PAAS_ () is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language romantic drama film directed by Sunny Deol and produced by Sunny Sounds P Ltd and Zee Studios. Principal photography began on 21 May 2017.[1] Karan Deol and Sahher Bambba were cast for the lead roles. Sunny Deol had auditioned over 400 women for Bambba's role.[2][3] The film is scheduled for release on 20 September 2019.[4]

Filming

The Film is mostly shot in Manali, Himachal Pradesh and some of the parts are shot in Delhi with a racing car sequence being shot at Buddh International Circuit in NCR.[5]


Marketing and release

Sunny Deol has launched two teaser posters of _Pal Pal Dil Ke Paas_ on valentines day of 2019. Another set of posters were released on 18 June 2019, giving the new release date as 20 September 2019.[6]


Soundtrack

The title song will be recreated with the original lyrics but with a new vocal. Music director duo Sachet-Parampara is composing the music for the film. The music rights were acquired by Zee Music Company.
