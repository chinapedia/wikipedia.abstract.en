WILCOXSON AND COMPANY BANK, also known as the FARMER'S BANK OF CARROLTON and FARMER'S BANK OF BOGARD, is a historic bank building located at Carrollton, Carroll County, Missouri. It was built in 1904, and consists of two two-story buildings, a corner building and a building that wraps around it on two sides. The buildings are visually tied together by a denticulated projecting cornice and stone coping on the tall roof parapet.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1983.[2]
