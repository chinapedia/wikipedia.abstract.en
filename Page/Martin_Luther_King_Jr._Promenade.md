Martin_Luther_King_Promenade.jpg MARTIN LUTHER KING JR. PROMENADE is a linear park and memorial promenade in San Diego, California, in the United States.[1][2] Children's Park lies within the promenade.[3] The promenade was dedicated in 1992.[4]
