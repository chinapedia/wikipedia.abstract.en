AHSANULLAH MASTER (; 9 November 1950 – 7 May 2004) is a Bangladesh Awami League politician and the former Member of Parliament from Gazipur-2.[1] He was a well known trade union leader in Bangladesh who was assassinated in August 2004.[2]


Career

He is a former member of Mukti Bahini and fought in the Bangladesh Liberation War. He was a former Gazipur Upazila council chairman. He was elected to parliament form Gazipur in 1996 and 2001.[3]


Death

He was shot dead in a conference along with Omar Faruq Ratan in Tongi, Gazipur, Bangladesh on 7 May 2004.[4][5]


Personal life

His son Zahid Ahsan Russel is a current member of parliament.[6]
