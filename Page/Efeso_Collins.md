Fa'anānā EFESO COLLINS is a New Zealand politician who is an Auckland Councillor.


Political career

At the 2013 Auckland elections, Collins was elected to the Ōtara-Papatoetoe Local Board and became the Chairperson.[1][2] He contested the Labour party candidate selection for the Manukau East seat before the 2014 general election, but ultimately Jenny Salesa was selected.[3][4]

At the 2016 Auckland elections, Collins was elected to the Auckland Council, replacing Arthur Anae, who didn't seek re-election.[5] He was sworn in as a councillor for the Manukau ward on 1 November.[6]
