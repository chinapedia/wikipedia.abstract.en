NICOLE MONIQUE NG (born 26 August) is an Australian diver.

Ng was selected to represent Australia at the 2004 World Cup, Athens.[1] She was a 10-meter platform diver.[2] At the 2004 Australian Olympic trials, Ng placed third to Loudy Wiggins and Chantelle Newbery.[3]

Ng earned her first international medal at the 2005 China international, Chenzhou. She represented Australia for eight years and was selected to compete at multiple FINA Diving Grand Prix competitions.[4] Ng held a scholarship at the Australian institute of Sports[5] and completed a Bachelor Science at Griffith University whilst training.[6]
