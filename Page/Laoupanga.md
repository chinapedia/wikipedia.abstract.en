LAOUPANGA is a village in the commune of Nyambaka in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, located on the road from Ngaoundéré to Wouro Sangue[1]


Population

In 1967, Laoupanga comptait 194 inhabitants, mostly Fula people[2] At the time of the 2005 census, there were 424 people in the village.[3]
