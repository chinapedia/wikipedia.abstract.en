TOM HEARDMAN (born 12 September 1995) is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker.


Career

Heardman began his career with Newcastle United and joined League Two side Hartlepool United on loan in August 2016.[1] He made his professional debut on 27 September 2016 in a 1–1 draw with Luton Town.[2] He joined Bury on a season-long loan in July 2017,[3] but returned before the end of August.[4]

At the end of 2017/18 season, Heardman was released by Newcastle United.[5]
