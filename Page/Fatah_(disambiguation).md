FATAH is a secular Palestinian political party, and the largest faction of the confederated multi-party Palestine Liberation Organization.

FATAH may also refer to:


Political organizations

-   Fatah al-Intifada, a Palestinian militant faction
-   Fatah Halab, a joint operations room of Syrian rebel factions operating in Aleppo, Syria
-   Fatah Hawks, two Palestinian militant groups
-   Fatah Special Operations Group, a radical Palestinian faction
