_SPLENDRILLIA BAHAMASENSIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 7 mm and 21 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the Bahamas.
