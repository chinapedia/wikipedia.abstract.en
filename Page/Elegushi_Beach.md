Ikate_Elegushi.jpg ELEGUSHI BEACH is a private beach located at Lekki, Lagos state, southwest Nigeria.[1] The beach is owned by the Elegushi royal family in Lekki, Lagos state.[2][3] Elegushi private beach is seen as one of the best beaches in Lagos and Nigeria at large. The beach entertains close to 40,000 guests every week with Sundays being the best day on the beach. Over half of all guests that are entertained on the beach weekly visit on Sundays.

As it stands, there is no platform for online payment to enter the beach as people who want to enter the beach have to pay at the point of entry.
