SIGNALS MIDWEST are an American punk rock band from Cleveland, Ohio.[1]


History

Signals Midwest formed in 2008. In 2009, the band self-released their first full-length album titled _Burn the Blueprints_.[2] In 2011, the band released their second full-length album titled _Latitudes and Longitudes_ after signing with the record label Tiny Engines.[3] Also in 2011, the band released a split with Shady Ave.[4] In 2013, the band released their third studio album and second with Tiny Engines titled _Light on the Lake_.[5][6] In 2016, Signals Midwest released their fourth full-length album and third with Tiny Engines titled _At This Age_.[7][8][9][10][11][12] On June 19, 2019 Signals Midwest premiered the track, “Your New Old Apartment,” off their forthcoming record _Pin_, which is out August 2 via Lauren Records.[13]
