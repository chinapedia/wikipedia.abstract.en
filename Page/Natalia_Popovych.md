NATALIA KOSTIANTYNIVNA POPOVYCH () (born 16 March 1968, in Hurzuf, Yalta municipality, Ukraine), is a Ukrainian politician.

In 2014 she was appointed as the Presidential representative of Ukraine in Crimea that was shortly before that transferred to Kherson due to the annexation of Crimea by the Russian Federation[1].
