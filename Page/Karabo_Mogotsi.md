KARABO MOGOTSI (born 24 April 1995) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Gauteng in the 2014–15 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 11 December 2014.[2]

In September 2018, he was named in Free State's squad for the 2018 Africa T20 Cup.[3] He was the leading run-scorer for Free State in the 2018–19 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge, with 177 runs in eight matches.[4]
