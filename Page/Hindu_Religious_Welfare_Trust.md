HINDU RELIGIOUS WELFARE TRUST is a national government trust that looks after the welfare of the Hindu community of Bangladesh and is located in Dhaka, Bangladesh.[1][2][3]


History

The Hindu Religious Welfare Trust was established in 1983. It is under the Ministry of Religious Affairs.[4] In 2016-2017 budget session 2 billion taka was allocated to the trust by the finance ministry.[5]
