RAJAJI is an Indian film actor who has appeared in Tamil language films. He made a breakthrough appearing in _Moodar Koodam_ (2013).


Career

Rajaji was working for an IT company Tata Consultancy Service since 2015,due to his passion and interest towards to film industry made his breakthrough as an actor playing the lead role in Naveen's critically acclaimed dark comedy _Moodar Koodam_ (2013), which won positive reviews and did well at the box office.[1][2][3] Rajaji's second film _Sathuran_ won positive reviews, but the low key nature of the release meant that the film did not gain notice at the box office.[4]

He is currently featuring in _Enkitta Mothathe_, where he portrays a Kamal Haasan fan club member in the 1980s, and stars in an ensemble cast including Natarajan Subramaniam, Parvathy Nair and Sanchita Shetty. He is also working on Naveen's second film, _Kolanji_, which has been in production since early 2015.[5][6]
