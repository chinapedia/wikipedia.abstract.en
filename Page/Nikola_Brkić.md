NIKOLA BRKIĆ (born 1 September 1998) is a professional water polo player of Montenegro. Brkić started his career at PVK Jadran, making his professional debut for PVK Jadran in 2014-15 season.


Junior team success

With junior team of PVK Jadran , he won tournaments such as Montenegrin first league and Montenengrin cup, Adriatic tournament Olympics hope,[1] Adriatic tournament Final 10.[2] With junior national team of Montenegro, he won gold medal on tournament "4 nations" in Bečej,[3] gold medal on tournament in Szentes , silver medal on tournament "Darko Čukić" in Belgrade,[4] and was part of the Montenegrin team at the 2015 European Games in Baku.[5]
