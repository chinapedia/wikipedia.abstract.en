JOEL FARRELL is a Jamaican rugby league footballer who plays as a forward for the Sheffield Eagles in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Farrell is a Jamaican international.[2] He has previously spent time on loan at the Gateshead Thunder in the Kingstone Press League 1 competition.[3]

He has also previously played for the Dewsbury Rams and the Batley Bulldogs.
