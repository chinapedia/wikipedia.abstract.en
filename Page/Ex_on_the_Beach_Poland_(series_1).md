The FIRST SERIES of _Ex on the Beach Poland_, a Polish television programme, began airing on 7 November 2016 on MTV. The show was announced in 17 October 2016.[1] Cast member for this series include _Warsaw Shore_ star Wojtek Gola. The series was filmed in Croatia.


Cast

The official list of cast members was released on 24 October 2016 and includes four single boys: Adam Zając, Dawid Ambro, Michał Spała and _Warsaw Shore_ cast member Wojtek Gola; as well as four single girls; Joanna Kościak, Jola Mróz, Marta Różańska and Sandra Sarapata.[2]
