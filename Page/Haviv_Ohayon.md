HAVIV OHAYON (born 17 August 1998) is an Israeli footballer who plays for Hapoel Hadera.[1][2]


Career

Seen as a precocious talent, Ohayon joined the senior team at the age of 17 and became their third goalkeeper behind Juan Pablo Colinas and Barak Levi. On 10 August 2015 Made his debut in a 3-0 loss to Maccabi Petah Tikva. Following a trial with Watford F.C.,[3] Maccabi Tel Aviv loaned him on loan the English club for an estimated amount of 1.75 million pounds, where he joined their under-23 team.[4] Meanwhile, he also extended his Maccabi Tel Aviv contract for another five years.[5]


Trophies

-   Israel State Cup (1): 2014-15
-   Toto Cup (1): 2014–15
