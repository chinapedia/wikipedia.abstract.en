YVONNE ROZILLE, _née_ MARIE-YVONNE GILBERTE ROUZILLE (5 January 1900, Commentry – 1 December 1985, Grasse) was a French film actress.

In 1937, she married Georges Gaillard, honorary prefect and codirector of the Théâtre du Vaudeville, founder of the _Revue de Hollande_.[1]
