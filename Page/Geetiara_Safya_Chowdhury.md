GEETIARA SAFYA CHOWDHURY is a Bangladeshi Business Woman and former Advisor of Caretaker Government led by Fakhruddin Ahmed. She is the Chairperson of ADCOMM.[1]


Career

In 1978 she became the first female member of the Dhaka Club.[2] She was an advisor of Caretaker Government led by Fakhruddin Ahmed. She was in charge of the Ministry of Industry, Ministry of Textiles and jute, Ministry of Social Welfare, Ministry of Women and Children Affairs.[3][4]


Personal life

She is married to Nazim Kamran Choudhury, a former member of Parliament.[5]
