BUNKER HILL is an unincorporated community in Howard County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

The community was named in commemoration of the Battle of Bunker Hill.[2] The post office at Bunker Hill was called Myers, after Henry Myers, a pioneer citizen.[3] The Myers post office was established in 1859, and remained in operation until 1905.[4]
