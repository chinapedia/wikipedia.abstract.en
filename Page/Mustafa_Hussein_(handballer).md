'''Mustafa Hussein ''' (, born 8 January 1984) is an Egyptian male handball player. He was a member of the Egypt national handball team, playing as a right wing. He was a part of the Egyptian squad at the 2008 Summer Olympics.[1][2] On club level, he played for Al Ahly in Egypt.
