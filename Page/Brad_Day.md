BRAD DAY (born 23 September 1994) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a or for Featherstone Rovers in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Day has previously played for the Castleford Tigers in the Super League and the Batley Bulldogs in the Betfred Championship.[2][3]
