MCNATT is an unincorporated community in north central McDonald County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

The community is located adjacent to Indian Creek along Missouri Route C.[2]


History

A post office called McNatt was established in 1901, and remained in operation until 1907.[3] J. J. McNatt was the proprietor of a mill near the original town site.[4]
