YAO DIEUDONNE (born 14 May 1997) is an Ivorian footballer who plays for Vendsyssel FF on loan from Odense BK.[1][2]


Club career

OB

Yao went on a trial at OB in the autumn 2015. That went successfully, and he was offered a contract.[3]

Yao got his debut on 15 May 2016 against Hobro IK in the Danish Superliga, that OB lost 0-1.[4] He came on the pitch in the 82nd minute, where he replaced Mikkel Desler. He was moved up to the first team squad in the summer 2016, after playing a half year at the U19 squad. In November 2016, his contract got extended until 2020.[5]
