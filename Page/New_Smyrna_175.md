The NEW SMYRNA 175 is a NASCAR K&N Pro Series East race held annually at New Smyrna Speedway in New Smyrna Beach, Florida. The race is 84 miles in distance. Derek Kraus is the defending champion.


History

The 2015 race was extended due to a green-white-checker finish, and saw the most cautions issued.
