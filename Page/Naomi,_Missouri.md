NAOMI is an unincorporated community in Marion County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Naomi was platted in 1869, and named after Naomi, a figure mentioned in the Hebrew Bible.[2] A post office called Naomi was established in 1873, and remained in operation until 1905.[3]
