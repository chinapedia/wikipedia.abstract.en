ÁGÚSTA ÞORSTEINSDÓTTIR (17 April 1942 – 21 August 2008) was an Icelandic swimmer. She competed in the women's 100 metre freestyle at the 1960 Summer Olympics, where she was eliminated in the heats.[1]
