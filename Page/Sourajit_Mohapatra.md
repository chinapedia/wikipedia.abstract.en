SOURAJIT MOHAPATRA (born 7 September 1968) is a first-class cricketer who played for Orissa in the Ranji Trophy. He was born in Cuttack, Orissa, India.[1]

Mani is a left-hand batsman and right-arm medium bowler. He took a hat-trick in the 1987-88 Ranji Trophy playing for Orissa against Tripura.[2]
