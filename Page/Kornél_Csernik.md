KORNÉL CSERNIK (born 2 July 1998 in Budapest) is a Hungarian football player who currently plays for Soroksár SC on loan from Ferencváros TC.


Career

Ferencváros

On 13 August 2016, Csernik played his first match for Ferencváros in a 1-2 loss against Vasas in the Hungarian League.[1]
