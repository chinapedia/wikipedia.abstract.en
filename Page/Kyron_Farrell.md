KYRON CASSIUS DANIEL FARRELL (born 10 May 1996) is a professional footballer who plays for Cray Wanderers, as a midfielder.


Club career

Farrell began his career with Millwall, signing a professional contract in May 2016.[1]

He moved on loan to Braintree Town in January 2017.[2]

On 15 June 2017, it was announced that Farrell would join National League South side Concord Rangers following his release from Milwall.[3] He moved to Cray Wanderers in March 2018.[4]


International career

Born in England, Farrell was an Irish youth international at under-19 level.[5]
