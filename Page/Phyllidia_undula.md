_PHYLLIDIA UNDULA_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusk in the family Phyllidiidae.[1]


Description

This species has previously been confused with _Phyllidia ocellata_.[2]


Distribution

This species was described from the Red Sea. It appears to be confined to the western Indian Ocean including the Red Sea, the African coast and the Island of Réunion.[3]


Diet

This species feeds on a sponge.
