SHIVAM CHAUDHARY (born 4 August 1997) is an Indian first-class cricketer who plays for Uttar Pradesh.[1] He made his first-class debut for Uttar Pradesh in the 2014–15 Ranji Trophy on 6 February 2015.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Uttar Pradesh in the 2016–17 Inter State Twenty-20 Tournament on 5 February 2017.[3] He made his List A debut for Uttar Pradesh in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 1 March 2017.[4]
