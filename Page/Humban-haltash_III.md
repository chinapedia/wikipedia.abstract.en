HUMBAN-HALTASH III or UMANALDASH was the last ruler of Elam. He became king 640 BC.[1] During his kingdom, the Assyrian people attacked Elam and occupied Madektu. This facilitated their way to the Karkheh river. In 631 BC, Shush fell and Ashurbanipal killed Humban Haltash.[2][3]
