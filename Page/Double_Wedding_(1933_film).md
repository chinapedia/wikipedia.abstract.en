_DOUBLE WEDDING_ is a 1933 British comedy film directed by Frank Richardson and starring Joan Marion, Jack Hobbs and Viola Keats. It was made at Teddington Studios as a quota quickie.[1]


Cast

-   Joan Marion as Daisy
-   Jack Hobbs as Dick
-   Viola Keats as Mildred
-   Anthony Hankey as Roger
-   Mike Johnson as George
-   Ernest Sefton as PC
