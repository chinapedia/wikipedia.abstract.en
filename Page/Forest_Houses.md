The FOREST HOUSES or MORRISANIA HOUSES were completed in Morrisania, Bronx near the end of 1956. NYCHA residents joined with artist Thomas Hirschhorn to build a new monument.[1]

Some notable residents are: Fat Joe, Lord Finesse, Diamond D,Showbiz, LandFam, Petawane[2]
