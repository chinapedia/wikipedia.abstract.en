WINCHESTER is an unincorporated community in Clark County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Winchester was platted in 1837, and most likely was named after Winchester, Virginia.[2] A post office called Winchester was established in 1840, and remained in operation until 1905.[3]
