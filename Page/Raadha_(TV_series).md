_RAADHA_ is a popular Bengali television Soap Opera that premiered on October 31, 2016 and airs on Zee Bangla. It is produced by Surinder Films and stars Aemila Sadhukhan and Ravi Shaw in lead roles and Monalisa Paul as the main antagonist. The show telecasts at Monday to Friday at 10:30 pm. The show aired at 6:00pm. Before and then it got a new timeslot at 10:30 pm. Because of Joyee Replacing its old timeslot. The Show was one of the Best serial running in this period its was very Popular among the Audience. The Show went off air on 8 December 2017. It was replaced by Rangiye Diye Jao. The show aired its last episode on 8 December 2017.
