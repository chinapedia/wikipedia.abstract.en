HART is an extinct town in Macon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. The GNIS classifies it as a populated place.[1]

A variant name was "Evelyn".[2] A post office called Evelyn was established in 1888, and remained in operation until 1916.[3] Besides the post office, Hart had a railroad depot.[4]
