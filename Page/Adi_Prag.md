ADI PRAG (עדי פרג; born December 15, 1957) is an Israeli former Olympic swimmer.[1]


Swimming career

He competed for Israel at the 1974 Asian Games in Tehran, Iran, in swimming in the 100 m and 200 m butterfly (coming in 4th in both), in the 200 m freestyle (coming in 5th), in the 100 m freestyle (coming in 6th), and in the 200 m individual medley.

Prag represented Israel at the 1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, in swimming, at the age of 18.[2] He was the youngest Israeli competitor at the 1976 Games.[3] He competed in the Men's 100 metre butterfly, and came in 6th in Heat 1.[4][5] He also competed in the Men's 200 metre butterfly, and came in 5th in Heat 2.[6][7]
