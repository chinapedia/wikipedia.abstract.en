DOUG GARWOOD (born March 31, 1963) is an American professional golfer.

Garwood played college golf at Fresno State University where he was a two-time All-American.[1]

Garwood played on the Nationwide Tour from 2002 to 2005. His best finish was a playoff loss to Andy Miller in the 2002 State Farm Open.[2]

Garwood began playing on the PGA Tour Champions in 2013 and won the 2016 SAS Championship.[3]
