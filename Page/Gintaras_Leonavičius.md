GINTARAS LEONAVIČIUS (born October 29, 1983) is a Lithuanian former professional basketball player, playing as a shooting guard. Born in Panevėžys, Lithuania, he is 6 ft 4.5 in (1.94 m) in height and he weighs 185 lb (84 kg).[1][2]
