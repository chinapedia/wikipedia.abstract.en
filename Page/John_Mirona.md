JOHN MICHEL MIRONA (جون ميشيل ميرونا; born 1962) is a former Olympic boxer from Sudan.[1]


Boxing career

He competed for Sudan at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea in boxing.[2] In Men's Featherweight he came in tied for 17th.[3]

He received a bye in the first round, and was defeated by Ya'acov Shmuel of Israel in the second round.[4] Shmuel knocked out Mirona in their 125.5-lb bout at 1:15 of the first round.[5]
