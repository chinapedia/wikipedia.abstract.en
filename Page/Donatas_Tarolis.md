DONATAS TAROLIS (born March 30, 1994) is a Lithuanian professional basketball player, playing as a power forward. Born in Gargždai, Lithuania, he is 6 ft 7.5 in (2.02 m) in height and he weighs 200 lb (91 kg).[1][2]
