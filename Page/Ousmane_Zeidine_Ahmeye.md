OUSMANE ZEIDINE AHMEYE (born 9 June 1994), is a Nigerien footballer who plays as a forward, most recently for FC Dordoi Bishkek in Kyrgyzstan.


Career

Club

In June 2013, Ahmeye signed for French Ligue 2 side Angers.[1] On 17 August 2016, Ahmeye signed for Kyrgyzstan League side Dordoi Bishkek.[2]

International

Ahmeye made his debut for the Niger national team against Burkina Faso on 23 March 2013.[3]
