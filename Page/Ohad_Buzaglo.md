OHAD BUZAGLO (born January 9, 1982) is a former Israeli footballer who now works as a manager.[1]


Personal life

Ohad's father is Jacob Buzaglo a former player who played in the 70's and 80's in Hapoel Tel Aviv, Beitar Jerusalem and Hapoel Jerusalem and his younger brothers are Asi Buzaglo, Maor Buzaglo and Almog Buzaglo.[2]
