CHAMSEDDINE RAHMANI (born September 15, 1990) is an Algerian footballer who plays for Damac in the Saudi Professional League and the Algeria national team.


Club career

In January 2016, Rahmani extended his contract with MO Béjaïa for another 2 years.[1]


International career

On September 29, 2016, Rahmani received his first ever call-up to the Algeria national football team for a 2018 FIFA World Cup qualifier against Cameroon.[2]
