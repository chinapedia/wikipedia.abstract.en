PARTHA PRATIM ROY is an Indian politician from the state of West Bengal.

In 2016, he was elected to Lok Sabha from Cooch Behar parliamentary constituency of West Bengal on TMC ticket in a bye-election when the seat fell vacant due to the death of the sitting MP Renuka Sinha.[1][2]
