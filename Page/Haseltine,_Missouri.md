HASELTINE is an unincorporated community in Greene County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] The community location was west of Springfield on U.S. Route 266, adjacent to Interstate 44.[2]


History

Variant names were "Dorchester" and "Hazeltine Station".[3] A post office called Dorchester was established in 1881, and remained in operation until 1904.[4] The community has the name of the local Hazeltine family.[5]
