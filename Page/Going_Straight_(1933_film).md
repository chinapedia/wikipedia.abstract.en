_GOING STRAIGHT_ is a 1933 British comedy film directed by John Rawlins and starring Moira Lynd, Helen Ferrers and Joan Marion. It was made at Teddington Studios as a quota quickie.[1]

-   Helen Ferrers as Lady Peckham
