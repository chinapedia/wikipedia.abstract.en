_GARJANAI_ (English: Roar) is an upcoming Indian Tamil thriller film directed by Sundar Balu. Inspired by Navdeep Singh's Hindi film _NH10_ (2015), the film features Trisha in the lead role Trisha, with Vamsi Krishna and Amit Bhargav in supporting roles

Production

In October 2018, reports suggested that either Samantha or Trisha would replace Anushka Sharma in the Tamil remake of the Hindi thriller film, _NH10_ (2015).[1] Trisha later confirmed that she would be working in the film, which would feature actors Amit Bhargav and Vamsi Krishna in supporting roles.[2][3]

The film's shoot began in Karaikudi .[4][5]
