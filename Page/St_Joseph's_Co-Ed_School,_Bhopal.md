ST JOSEPH'S CO-ED SCHOOL is a school in Arera Colony of Bhopal, India.[1]

Campus

The school's campus is located in Bhopal's E-6 Arera Colony. It has a main playground with three other small playgrounds.There is a separate kindergarten building with an auditorium.


Academics

The school has classes in English and Hindi mediums of instruction. It has classes from Standard nursery to XII.The students belonging to the scheduled castes and scheduled tribes are offered fee concessions.

The school is considered to be the top most school in Madhya Pradesh in terms of overall development of its students.
