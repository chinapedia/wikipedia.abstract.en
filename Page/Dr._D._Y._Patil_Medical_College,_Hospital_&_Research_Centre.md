DR. D. Y. PATIL MEDICAL COLLEGE is a medical college located in Pune, Maharashtra. It is named after D. Y. Patil or Dnyandeo Yashwantrao Patil. It has 250 undergraduate seats for MBBS course.[1]


Ranking

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) has ranked Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical College, Hospital and Research Centre, Pune at 20TH position all over India and the 1st position (only the Medical College) in Maharashtra by MHRD in 2019.

Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical College, Hospital and Research Centre, Pune received an award of “The most promising Medical College in India” at the 9th Asian Education Summit and Awards 2019 held on 6th April 2019 at New Delhi.

As per the survey conducted by ‘India Today’, we are the 2ND amongst the private Universities in India.

the college was ranked 115TH among medical colleges in India in 2017 by _India Today_.[2]
