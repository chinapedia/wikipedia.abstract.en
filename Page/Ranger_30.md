The RANGER 30 is an American sailboat, that was designed by C. Raymond Hunt Associates and first built in 1977. The design is out of production.[1][2]

The boat was built by Ranger Yachts in the United States.[3]


Design

The Ranger 30 is a small recreational keelboat, built predominantly of fiberglass, with wood trim. It has a masthead sloop rig, an internally-mounted spade-type rudder and a fixed fin keel. It displaces and carries of ballast. The boat has a draft of with the standard keel.[4][5]

The boat is fitted with a Universal Atomic 4 gasoline engine.[6]
