UENDI VECAJ (born 18 February 1997), is an Albanian professional footballer who plays as a right winger for Teuta Durrës on loan from Croatian club Dinamo Zagreb II and the Albania national under-21 football team.[1]


Club career

Early career

Dinamo Zagreb

On 31 August 2017 Vecaj completed a transfer to Croatian First Football League giants GNK Dinamo Zagreb.[2]


International career

He received his first international level call up to the Albania national under-21 team by coach Redi Jupi for the 2017 UEFA European Under-21 Championship qualification closing match against Israel U21 on 10 October 2016.[3] He debuted for the under-21 national side against Israel U21 playing as a starter until half time where he was replaced by Ardit Hoxhaj in a 4–0 loss.[4]
