STEPHEN BERNARD BLUMENFELD is an American-New Zealand labor-relations academic.


Academic career

After degrees from Carroll University, University of Wisconsin-Madison and University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign, Blumenfeld got his PhD, also from Illinois, in 1997. He started at Victoria University of Wellington in 1999, rising to become Senior Lecturer and Director of the Centre for Labour Employment and Work.[1] He has also worked at Drake University and Simon Fraser University[2]

Blumenfeld's work regularly gets reported in the New Zealand national media.[3][4]

In 2013 he was awarded a Meritorious Service Award by the Tertiary Education Union.[5]
