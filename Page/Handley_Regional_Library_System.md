HANDLEY REGIONAL LIBRARY SYSTEM serves the city of Winchester and counties of Frederick and Clarke in Virginia.[1] The library system is within Region 6 of Virginia Library Association (VLA).[2]


Service area

According to the FY 2014 Institute of Museum and Library Services Data Catalog, the Library System has a service area population of 119,534 with one central library and two branch libraries.[3]


Branches

-   Bowman Library (Stephens City) established 2001.
-   Clarke County Library (Berryville) established in 1985.
-   Handley Library (Winchester) established 1913.
