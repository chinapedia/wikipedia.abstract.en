GASPARE RICCIULLO DEL FOSSO, O.M. (1496–1592) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Archbishop of Reggio Calabria (1560–1592),[1] Bishop of Calvi Risorta (1551–1560),[2] and Bishop of Scala (1548–1551).[3]


Biography

Gaspare Ricciullo del Fosso was born in Rogliano, Italy on 6 January 1496 and ordained a priest in the Order of the Minims in November 1521.[4][5] On 17 May 1548, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul III as Bishop of Scala.[6][7] On 22 April 1551, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Julius III as Bishop of Calvi Risorta.[8][9][10] On 17 July 1560, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Pius IV as Archbishop of Reggio Calabria.[11][12][13] He served as Archbishop of Reggio Calabria until his death on 28 December 1592.[14][15][16]
