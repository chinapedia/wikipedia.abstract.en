AZIZ AL-ABUB (a.k.a. Ibrahim al-Nadhir or al-Nahdhir) was a Lebanese psychiatrist and medical torture expert affiliated with Hezbollah.[1]

Aziz al-Abub is accused of having used brainwashing, drugs, and physical torture on CIA operatives in Beirut,[2] in particular, the 444-day (1984-1985) torture and mental derangement of American William Francis Buckley.[3]
