RANA MANSOUR (; born November 16, 1981 in Alexandria, Virginia) is an Iranian-American musician and singer.


Early life

She attended the Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts where she received her Bachelor's in Music with Honors. She released her first Persian album in September 2015.


TV Presence

Rana has also been seen on the hit Bravo show, The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, performing her original song, "No Place Like Home".[1]
