HORNDON MEADOW is a 0.8 hectare nature reserve north of Stanford-le-Hope in Essex. It is managed by the Essex Wildlife Trust.[1]

This site is an unimproved hay meadow, which has around eighty flower species, such as green-winged orchids, yellow rattles, musk mallows and black knapweeds. Other plants include adder's tongue ferns.[2]

There is access from South Hill, at Tyelands Farm.[3]
