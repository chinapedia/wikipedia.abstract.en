SHERRY YARD (born January 11, 1964)[3][4][5] is an American chef, restaurateur and cookbook author.


Career

Yard has served as a chef in several Wolfgang Puck restaurants, including Spago,[6][7][8] Rainbow Room and Tribeca Grill.[9] She also serves as Chief Operating Officer, Restaurant Division at iPic Entertainment, a national entertainment group known for their upscale movie theaters, in-cinema dining and restaurants.[10][11]

Yard has stated that her signature dessert is Kaiserschmarrn.[12]

Television

Yard has served as a judge on several cooking shows, including the Food Network series _Halloween Baking Championship, Cake Wars Christmas_, _Christmas Cookie Challenge_, Cooking Channel television series _Sugar Showdown_ and more recently on ABC's The Great American Baking Show: Holiday Edition.[13][14][15] She has been a guest judge on _Top Chef: Just Desserts, Food Network Star, Duff Till Dawn, Iron Chef America_ and _Cutthroat Kitchen._[16] She has also competed in the Food Network show, _Cutthroat Kitchen Superstar Sabotage_.[17]


Personal life

Yard stands at 5 feet 2 inches tall.[18] On May 3, 2008, she married dentist Edward M. Ines in Malibu, California in a destination wedding hosted by Wolfgang Puck, whom Yard served under as the corporate executive pastry chef at the time.[19][20] They live in Manhattan Beach, California.[21]
