TANAJI SAWANT () is a businessman , Shiv Sena politician and deputy leader from Solapur district, Maharashtra. He is current Member of Legislative Council as a member of Shiv Sena representing Yavatmal Local Authorities constituency. [1] He got elected to legislative council with a record margin of 270 votes, securing 348 votes.

He came into prominence for his Water conservation project, part of Shiv Sena’s ‘Shiv Jal Kranti’ scheme in Osmanabad district in May 2016.[2]


Positions held

-   2016: Deputy Leader, Shiv Sena
-   2016: Elected to Maharashtra Legislative Council [3]
-   2017: Appointed as Shiv Sena Sampark Pramukh Osmanabad and Solapur District[4]
-   2019: Minister of Water Conservation in Maharashtra State Government [5]
