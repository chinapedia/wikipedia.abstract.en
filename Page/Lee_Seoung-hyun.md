LEE SEOUNG-HYUN (), born April 16, 1992, is a South Korean professional basketball player for the Goyang Orions of the Korean Basketball League.

He represented South Korea's national basketball team at the 2016 FIBA Asia Challenge, where he recorded most minutes for his team.[1]
