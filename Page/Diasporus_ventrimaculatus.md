_DIASPORUS VENTRIMACULATUS_ is a species of dink frogs, in the genus _Diasporus_. It was originally described as _Diasporus ventrimaculatus sp. nov._ in Zootaxa in 2009.[1]

It is classed as a vulnerable species on the International Union for Conservation of Nature's Red List.[2]
