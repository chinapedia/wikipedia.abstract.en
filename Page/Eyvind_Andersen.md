EYVIND ANDERSEN (7 July 1874 – ) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Halden to Victor Christian Andersen and Theodora Caroline Jacobine Frølich. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1899, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1921.[1]
