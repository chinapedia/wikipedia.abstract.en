GBENRO AJIBADE (born GBENRO EMMANUEL AJIBADE; December 8) is a Nigerian actor,[1] producer, model, and presenter.


Biography

He attended Makurdi International School and later Mount Saint Gabriels Secondary School. He then completed his education by graduating from Benue State University with a degree in Biology.[2]

He is a leading character in the popular soap opera_Tinsel_ and has acted in _Gbomo Gbomo Express_ and _The Wages_.

Ajibade won Most Outstanding Actor/Model of the Year at the 2011 Nigerian Model Achievers Awards.


Personal life

He is married to actress Osas Ighodaro, together, they have one daughter, Azariah Tiwatope Osarugue Ajibade.[3]
