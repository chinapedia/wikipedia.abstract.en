JACK HOLMES (born 15 January 1994) is a rugby league footballer who plays for Oldham in Betfred League 1. He plays as a centre, wing, fullback or scrum-half.

Holmes has previously played for the Salford City Reds,[1][2] Rochdale Hornets,[3] Halifax[4] and the Leigh Centurions.[5]
