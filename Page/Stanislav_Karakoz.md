STANISLAV SAVVICH KARAKOZ (; born 20 October 1999) is a Russian football player. He plays for FSC Dolgoprudny.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Chertanovo Moscow on 23 October 2016 in a game against FC Kaluga.[1]
