HARQUA is a populated place situated in Maricopa County, Arizona.[1] It has an estimated elevation of above sea level.[2]

Built along the Southern Pacific Railroad, the townsite of Harqua is approximately 18 miles SSE of Tonopah, Arizona. Today it stands as a ghost town, (see: List of ghost towns in Arizona) with little more than a handful of crumbling foundations remaining.
