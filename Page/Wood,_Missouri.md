WOOD (also known as WOODS) is an unincorporated community in DeKalb County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Woods was established in 1898, and remained in operation until 1902.[2] The community has the name of J. Wood, the original owner of the site.[3]
