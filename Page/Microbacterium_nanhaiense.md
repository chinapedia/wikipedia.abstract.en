_MICROBACTERIUM NANHAIENSE_ is a Gram-positive, heterotrophic, anaerobic, non-spore-forming, rod-shaped and non-motile bacterium from the genus of Microbacterium which has been isolated from sea sediments from the South China Sea.[1][2][3][4]
