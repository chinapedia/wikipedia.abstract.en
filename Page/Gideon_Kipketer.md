Am2014-gideon-kipketer-branko-collin-01.jpg GIDEON KIPKETER is a Kenyan long-distance runner, who specialises in road running events. Kipketer is currenty part of the NN Running Team, an international team of elite long-distance runners managed by Global Sports Communication in Nijmegen, Netherlands.


Cross-country running

He finished 8th in the junior race at the 2010 IAAF World Cross Country Championships and won the 2012 Tuskys Wareng Cross Country.[1]


Marathon running

Kipketer won the 2016 Mumbai Marathon, setting a new course record.[2] He was only in the race as a pace-setter, but decided to continue the race for the win.[3]

He finished 3rd at the 2016 Chicago Marathon.[4]

His personal best is 2:08:14, set at the 2012 Amsterdam Marathon.[5]
