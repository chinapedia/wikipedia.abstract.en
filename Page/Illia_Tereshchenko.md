ILLIA TERESHCHENKO (; born 27 May 1998), is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays for Slovan Šaľa on loan from Slovan Bratislava.[1]


Club career

Tereshchenko made his professional Fortuna Liga debut for MFK Zemplín Michalovce against FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce on 5 November 2016.[2]
