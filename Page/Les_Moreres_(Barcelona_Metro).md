LES MORERES is a Barcelona Metro station, located in the El Prat de Llobregat municipality, in the Metropolitan area of Barcelona. The station is served by line L9.[1][2]

The station is located underneath the square between _Av. de la Verge de Montserrat_ and _Carrer de les Moreres_. There are two entrances at _Av. de la Verge de Montserrat_, which serve an underground ticket hall. The two long side platforms are at a lower level.[3][4]

The station was opened in 2016, when line L9 was extended from Zona Universitaria station to Aeroport T1 station.
