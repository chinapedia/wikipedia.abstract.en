ROUND VALLEY is a census-designated place (CDP) in Gila County, Arizona, United States. The population was 487 at the 2010 census.[1]


Geography

The CDP is located in northern Gila County, just south of the town of Payson. Arizona State Route 87, the Beeline Highway, forms the western edge of the CDP and runs north to the center of Payson.
