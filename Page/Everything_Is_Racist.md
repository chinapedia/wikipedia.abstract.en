_EVERYTHING IS RACIST_ is the sixth album by American comedy artist Rucka Rucka Ali. It was released December 25, 2016 on iTunes.[1]


Release

The release of the new album was first announced on "The Best Debate In The Universe - Episode #2" on June 14, 2016. It was then announced 30 November 2016 on Ali's Instagram page. The title of the album is a parody of the song "Everything is Awesome" from the 2014 _The Lego Movie_ by Warner Bros. Pictures.
