KARLO ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] The island lies north from Port Blair.


Geography

The island belongs to the Stewart Sound Group and lies between Oyster and Orchid Islands.


Administration

Politically, Karlo Island, along neighboring Stewart Sound Group Islands, belongs to Diglipur Taluk.[2]


Demography

Karlo Island was previously inhabited. a small jetty and a sole house are all the remaining ruins of the village.
