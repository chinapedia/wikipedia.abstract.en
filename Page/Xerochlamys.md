_XEROCHLAMYS_ is a genus of trees and shrubs in the family Sarcolaenaceae. The species are all endemic to Madagascar.


Species

Eight species are recognised:[1][2]

-   _Xerochlamys bojeriana_
-   _Xerochlamys coriacea_
-   _Xerochlamys diospyroidea_
-   _Xerochlamys elliptica_
-   _Xerochlamys itremoensis_
-   _Xerochlamys tampoketsensis_
-   _Xerochlamys undulata_
-   _Xerochlamys villosa_
