PARAMASIVUM PILLAY "BARLEN" VYAPOORY (; born 1945/46) is a Mauritian politician who has served as vice president of Mauritius since 2016.[1]


Early life

Barlen Vyapoory previously served as High Commissioner of the Republic of Mauritius to South Africa. Vyapoory has served as President of that organization on several occasions.[2] He is a member of the Militant Socialist Movement.[3]

Upon the effectivity of Ameenah Gurib's resignation, he is serving as Acting President of Mauritius.
