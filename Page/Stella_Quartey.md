'''Stella Quartey ''' (born 28 December 1973) is a Ghanaian footballer who played as a midfielder for the Ghana women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she played for Ghatel Ladies in Ghana.[1]
