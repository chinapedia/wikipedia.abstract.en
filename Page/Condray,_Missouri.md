CONDRAY is an unincorporated community in Dent County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Condray was established in 1886, and remained in operation until 1910.[2] The community has the name of Thomas H. Condray, an officer at a nearby blast furnace.[3]
