OLIVIER SHYAKA (born August 14, 1995) is a Rwandan professional basketball player. He currently plays for the Espoir BBC Kigali club of the NBL Rwanda.

He represented Rwanda's national basketball team on many occasions. At the 2013 AfroBasket, he was one of Rwanda's main players.[1]
