STOCKLEIGH ENGLISH is a village and civil parish in Devon, England. It has a 15th-century church, Saint Mary the Virgin,[1] which was restored 1878-83.[2]

Stockleigh Court in the parish is a grade II listed building which was the home of the Bellew family who lived at Stockleigh Court from the 16th to 20th century.[3]
