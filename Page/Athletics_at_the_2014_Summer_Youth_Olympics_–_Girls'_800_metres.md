The GIRLS’ 800 M competition at the 2014 Summer Youth Olympics was held on 20–23 August 2014 in Nanjing Olympic Sports Center.


Schedule

  ---------------- ------- -------
  20 August 2014   21:15   HEATS
  23 August 2014   21:45   FINAL
