_' Mead Ranch_' is a census-designated place (CDP) in Gila County, Arizona, United States. The population was 38 at the 2010 census.[1]


Geography

The CDP is located in northern Gila County at the southern foot of the Mogollon Rim. It is by dirt roads north of Arizona State Route 260 at Kohls Ranch, and northeast of Payson. According to the United States Census Bureau, the Mead Ranch CDP has a total area of , all of it land.[2]
