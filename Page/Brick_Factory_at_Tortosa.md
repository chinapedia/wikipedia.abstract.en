_BRICK FACTORY AT TORTOSA_ (__) is a 1909 painting by Pablo Picasso.

It belongs to Picasso's African Period and is considered a Proto-Cubist work.


Reception

Jonathan Jones of _The Guardian_ called the work "formidable"[1] and viewed the painting as "an experiment in how brutally you can reduce, simplify, solidify and abstract forms and still produce a picture that is not simply recognisable, but profoundly full of life."[2]
