Santa_Fe_Playhouse,_Santa_Fe_NM.jpg In 1919, American novelist and essayist Mary Austin founded The Santa Fe Little Theatre[1] (operating today as THE SANTA FE PLAYHOUSE). The Santa Fe Little Theatre was incorporated in 1922, and it is “the oldest continuously running theatre west of the Mississippi.”[2] The theater found a permanent home in 1962 located at 142 East De Vargas Street in Santa Fe, New Mexico in the heart of the Barrio De Analco Historic District.
