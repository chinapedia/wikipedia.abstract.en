DMITRY ANTILEVSKY (; ; born 12 June 1997) is a Belarusian professional footballer. As of 2018, he plays for Dinamo Minsk.[1]


Honours

BATE BORISOV

-   Belarusian Premier League champion: 2016
-   Belarusian Super Cup winner: 2017
