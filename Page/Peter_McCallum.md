PETER MCCALLUM (born Ayr, Scotland in 1992) is a Scottish rugby union player who plays for Ayr RFC as a Number Eight.[1]


Rugby Union career

Amateur career

McCallum has played for Ayr RFC since 2011[2][3] and is now the captain of the club. He has played for Ayr in the British and Irish Cup[4] and the BT Premiership[5][6][7] and against Scotland U20.[8] He won BT Premiership Player of the season for 2015-16.[9][10]

Professional career

McCallum played for Glasgow Warriors against Canada 'A' on the 30 August 2016.[11][12][13][14]

International career

He has been called up for Scotland Club XV several times.[15][16][17][18][19]


Outside rugby

McCallum is a Director of Tumax Storage.[20][21]
