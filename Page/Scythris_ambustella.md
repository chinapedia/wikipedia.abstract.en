_SCYTHRIS AMBUSTELLA_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Bengt Å. Bengtsson in 1997. It is found in Greece.[1][2]


Etymology

The species name refers to the appearance of the moth and is derived from Latin _ambustum_ (meaning to burn).[3]
