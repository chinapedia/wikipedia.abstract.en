The son of John Taylor and Elizabeth Payne, he was born in Madawaska and was educated there and in Renfrew. In 1917, Taylor married Catherine Hamilton. He was chief clerk for the Temiskaming and Northern Ontario Railway. Taylor was mayor of Cobalt for four years.[3]
