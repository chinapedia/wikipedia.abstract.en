LOUIS SCHLAEFLI (4 December 1938, Neuf-Brisach[1]) is a Franco-Swiss scholar, collector, and librarian. Since 1964 he has been the librarian of the for which he composed several major catalogs. He is also the author of more than five hundred articles and contributions[2] related to the heritage and history of Alsace, mostly religious ones.
