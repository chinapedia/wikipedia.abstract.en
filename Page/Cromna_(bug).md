_CROMNA_ is a genus of fulgoroid planthoppers in the family Flatidae. It was described by Francis Walker in 1857.


Species

-   _Cromna acutipennis_ (Walker, 1857)
-   _Cromna albidosparsa_ (Distant, 1910)
-   _Cromna albopunctata_ (Kirby, 1891)
-   _Cromna andamanensis_ (Distant, 1906)
-   _Cromna aspera_ (Melichar, 1902)
-   _Cromna sinensis_ (Walker, 1851)

Species brought to synonymy:

-   _Cromna angulifera_, synonym of _Phyllyphanta angulifera_
-   _Cromna asperum_, synonym of _Colgar asperum_
-   _Cromna centralis_, synonym of _Neomelicharia sparsa_
-   _Cromna chlorospila_, synonym of _Taparella doryca_
-   _Cromna elegans_, synonym of _Siphanta acuta_
-   _Cromna farinosa_, synony of _Colgar farinosa_
-   _Cromna frontalis_, synonym of _Colgaroides acuminata_
-   _Cromna nasalis_, synonym of _Euphanta munda_
-   _Cromna notata_, synonym of _Colgar notata_
-   _Cromna obtusa_, synonym of _Colgar albescens_
-   _Cromna peracuta_, synonym of _Parasalurnis roseicincta_
-   _Cromna quadripunctata_, synonym of _Euphanta quadripunctata_
-   _Cromna surrecta_, synonym for _Colgar surrectum_
