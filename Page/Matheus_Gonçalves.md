MATHEUS TRINDADE GONÇALVES (born 6 March 1996) is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Tombense.


Career

Born in Rio de Janeiro, Gonçalves started his career in the youth academy of Flamengo. In August 2016, he signed with Indian Super League side Goa on loan until January.[1] Gonçalves made his professional debut for Goa on 4 October 2016 in their opening match against NorthEast United.[2]

In January 2019, Gonçalves joined Tombense.[3]
