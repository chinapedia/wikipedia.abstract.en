_MONSIEUR SCRUPULE, GANGSTER_ (French: _MONSIEUR SCRUPULE GANGSTER_) is a 1953 French crime film directed by Jacques Daroy and starring Tilda Thamar, Yves Vincent and Howard Vernon.[1]


Cast

-   Tilda Thamar as Rolande
-   Yves Vincent as M. Scrupule
-   Howard Vernon as L'ami de Rolande
-   Henri Arius
-   René Blancard
-   Serge Davin
-   Dora Doll
-   Fransined
-   Annie Hémery
