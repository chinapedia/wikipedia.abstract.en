GERMÁN DARÍO PORTANOVA (born October 19, 1973 in Argentina) is a former Argentine footballer who played for clubs of Argentina, Chile, Paraguay and Spain.


Teams

-   Chacarita Juniors 1994-1996

-   Tristán Suárez 1996-1997

-   Cerro Porteño 1997-1998

-   Rangers 1999-2001

-   Racing Ferrol 2001

-   Tristán Suárez 2002

-   Deportes Melipilla 2002-2003
