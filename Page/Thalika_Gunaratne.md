THALIKA GUNARATNE (born 9 March 1975) is a Sri Lankan former cricketer. She played thirteen Women's One Day International matches for Sri Lanka women's national cricket team.[1] She was part of Sri Lanka's squad for the 2005 Women's Cricket World Cup.
