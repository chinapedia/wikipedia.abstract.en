CARMEN BRUSCA (born 7 November 1985) is an Argentine futsal player[1] and a former footballer who played as a defender.

She was a member of the Argentina women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2007 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she played for Boca Juniors in Argentina.[2]
