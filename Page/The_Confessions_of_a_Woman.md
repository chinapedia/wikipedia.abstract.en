_THE CONFESSIONS OF A WOMAN_ (Italian: _LE CONFESSIONI DI UNA DONNA_) is a 1928 Italian silent drama film directed by Amleto Palermi.[1] The film relates a woman falling into high-class prostitution, and her eventual redemption. It is set in Palermo.
