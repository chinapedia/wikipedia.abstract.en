MOSES REYNOLDS (born October 13, 1997) is an American Football safety for the Texas A&M Aggies, and younger brother of Texas A&M Aggies star wide receiver Joshua Reynolds (American football).1


Personal life

Moses Reynolds is the younger brother of Josh Reynolds Los Angeles Rams star wide receiver 2 and son of Michele Reynolds.3


High school career

Reynolds attended John Jay High School (San Antonio) . He played numerous positions offensively and defensively. Offensively Reynolds accumulated a total of 7,051 yards of total offense and 77 touchdowns. Even though he played mainly offense defensively his senior year he had a total of 23 tackles and 3 interceptions 45. Reynolds was rated by Rivals.com as a three star recruit and was ranked among top overall prospects in his class.6 He then continued his career and committed to play football for the Texas A&M Aggies.7


College career

Reynolds is currently a freshmen for the Texas A&M Aggies football team.8
