DMYTRO IHOROVYCH SHCHERBAK (; born 8 December 1996) is a Ukrainian football player.


Club career

He made his Russian Premier League debut for FC Anzhi Makhachkala on 5 December 2016 in a game against FC Arsenal Tula.[1]
