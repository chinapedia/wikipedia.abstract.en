WILCOX is an unincorporated community in Nodaway County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Wilcox was laid out in 1879, and named after B. S. Wilcox, a first settler.[2] A post office called Wilcox was established in 1880, and remained in operation until 1954.[3]
