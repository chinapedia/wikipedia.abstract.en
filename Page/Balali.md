BALALI is a village in the Charkhi Dadri district of Haryana State, India.[1][2]


Notable personalities

-   Mahavir Singh Phogat, Dronacharya Awardee, father and mentor of the famous Phogat sisters — his four daughters Geeta, Babita, Ritu, and Sangita and his two nieces Vinesh and Priyanka — hail from this village.[3]
