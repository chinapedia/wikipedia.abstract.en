The HAWTHORN GREYHOUND TRACK was a former greyhound racing track in Pontypridd, Rhondda Cynon Taf, Wales.

The stadium was situated on Fairfield Lane off Cardiff Road and on the south side of the Rhyd-y-Felin railway branch (now the A470).[1] Racing began in March 1932[2] and ended in 1970s with the site now being a football ground used by Rhydyfelin AFC and is called the old dog track.[3]
