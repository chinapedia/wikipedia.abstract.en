MUHAMAD ABU HENA (Bengali: মুহাম্মদ আবু হেনা) served as the International Commissioner of the Bangladesh Scouts, as well as the Chairman of the Asia-Pacific Scout Committee.

In 1994, he was awarded the 232nd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.
