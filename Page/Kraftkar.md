KRAFTKAR is a blue cheese from Tingvoll in Nordmøre, Western Norway, made from unskimmed cow's milk and cream, with injected culture of the mold _Penicillium roqueforti_.[1]

The name _Kraftkar_ ("strongman") relates to the legendary farmhand Tore Nordbø and his supernatural strength and size.[2]


History

Produced by the cheesemaker Tingvollost, Kraftkar was first introduced in 2004.[3]


Awards

Kraftkar was awarded gold medal for blue cheese at the World Cheese Awards in 2011, gold medal at the International Cheese Awards in 2013, and silver medal at the 2015 World Cheese Awards.[4][5] At the 2016 World Cheese Awards in San Sebastián, Kraftkar was selected "World Champion" (all categories), among more than 3,000 participating cheeses from 35 countries.[6][7][8][9]
