GEORGI POMASHKI () (born 10 January 1960) is a retired Bulgarian triple jumper.

He finished sixth at the 1986 European Indoor Championships,[1] fourth at the 1986 European Championships,[2] eighth at the 1987 European Indoor Championships,[3] and competed at the 1987 World Championships without reaching the final.[4]

He became Bulgarian champion in 1983, before Khristo Markov took five titles in a row,[5] and also became Bulgarian indoor champion in 1985 and 1988.[6] His personal best jump was 17.03 metres, achieved in August 1988 in Budapest; and also 7.95 metres in the long jump, achieved in July 1988 in Pleven.[7]
