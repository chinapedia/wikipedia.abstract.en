MICHAEL G. CORMACK (born April 22, 1970) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Cormack was born in Fort Dodge, Iowa and attended Minnesota State University, Mankato. A Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1995 to 2003 (13th district)[1]
