_DELIATHIS NIVEA_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Henry Walter Bates in 1869. It is known from Nicaragua, Guatemala, Costa Rica, Honduras, and Panama.[1]


Varietas

-   _Deliathis nivea_ var. _detersa_ Bates, 1885
-   _Deliathis nivea_ var. _femelle_ Bates, 1885
-   _Deliathis nivea_ var. _inermis_ Aurivillius, 1922
