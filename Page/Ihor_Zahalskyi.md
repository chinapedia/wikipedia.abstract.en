IHOR ZAHALSKYI (; born 19 May 1991) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder who plays for SC Dnipro-1.


Career

Zahalskyi is a product of the different sportive schools in his native Kirovohrad Oblast. He spent time with different Ukrainian teams, and in 2012 he signed a contract with FC Zirka from his native Kropyvnytskyi.

In 2015 Zahalskyi was a member of the Ukrainian student football team at the Summer Universiade in Gwangju, South Korea.[1]

On 28 May 2018 he signed contract with SC Dnipro-1.[2]
