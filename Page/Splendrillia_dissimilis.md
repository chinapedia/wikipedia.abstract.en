_SPLENDRILLIA DISSIMILIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 8.9 mm and 13.2 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the Virgin Islands.
