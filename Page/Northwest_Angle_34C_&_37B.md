NORTHWEST ANGLE 34C & 37B is a First Nations reserve[1] in Kenora District, Ontario. It borders Manitoba, and is adjacent to the Northwest Angle in Minnesota. It is one of the reserves of the Animakee Wa Zhing 37 First Nation.
