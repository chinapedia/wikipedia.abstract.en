Born in Rieti, Solmi's studies mainly focused on French literature and on Italian contemporary literature. He released several collections of poetries, with a style influenced by hermeticism.[1]

During his career Solmi was recipient of several literary awards, including the Bagutta Prize and two Viareggio Prizes.[2]
