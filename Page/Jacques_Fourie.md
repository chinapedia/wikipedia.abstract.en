JACQUES FOURIE (born 20 December 1996) is a Namibian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Namibia in the 2016–17 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 17 November 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Namibia in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 15 January 2017.[3]
