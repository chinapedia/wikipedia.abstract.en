ELIZABETH CHURCH (born 11 March 1930) is a British former swimmer.


Swimming career

She competed in the women's 200 metre breaststroke at the 1948 Summer Olympics.[1]

She represented England and won a silver medal in the 330 yard Medley Relay and a bronze medal in the 220 yard Breaststroke at the 1950 British Empire Games in Auckland, New Zealand.[2][3]
