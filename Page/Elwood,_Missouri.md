ELWOOD or ELWOOD SIDING is an unincorporated community in Greene County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] The community lies along the railroad southwest of the Springfield airport. The headwaters of Clear Creek are just to the northeast.[2]


History

Variant names were "Campbells Station" and "Ellwood".[3] A post office called Elwood was established in 1897, and remained in operation until 1940.[4] The community has the name of the local Ellwood family.[5]
