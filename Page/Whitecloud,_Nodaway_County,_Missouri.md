WHITECLOUD (also spelled WHITE CLOUD) is an unincorporated community in Nodaway County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called White Cloud was established in 1856, and remained in operation until 1901.[2] The community was named after nearby White Cloud Creek.[3]
