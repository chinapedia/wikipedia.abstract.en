A. ERIK ENDE served as the President of the Svenska Scoutrådet, the Swedish national Scouting federation, from 1976 to 1983 for his association Svenska Scoutförbundet.[1]

In 1977, Ende was awarded the 118th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2] He was also the 73rd recipient of the Silver Wolf, Sweden's highest Scout award, in 1969.
