DOGODO BAU (born 5 December 1994) is a cricketer from Papua New Guinea. He is a right-handed batsman and a wicket-keeper.

He made his first-class debut for Papua New Guinea against Namibia on 16 October 2016.[1] He made his List A debut against Namibia on 21 October 2016.[2]


International career

Bau made his One Day International (ODI) debut on 4 November 2016 against Hong Kong.[3] He made his Twenty20 International (T20I) debut on 14 April 2017 against the United Arab Emirates.[4]
