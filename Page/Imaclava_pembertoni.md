_IMACLAVA PEMBERTONI_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 25 mm and 42 mm.


Distribution

This species occurs in the Sea of Cortez, Western Mexico
