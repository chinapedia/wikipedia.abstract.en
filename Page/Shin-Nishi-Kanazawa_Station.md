is a railway station on the Hokuriku Railroad Ishikawa Line in the city of Kanazawa, Ishikawa Prefecture Japan, operated by the private railway operator Hokuriku Railroad (Hokutetsu).[1]


Lines

Shin-Nishi-Kanazawa Station is served by the Hokuriku Railroad Ishikawa Line between and , and is 2.1 km from the starting point of the line at . The station is located in front of Nishi-Kanazawa Station on the Hokuriku Main Line of JR West.


Station layout

The station consists of one island platform serving two tracks, connected to the station building by a level crossing.
