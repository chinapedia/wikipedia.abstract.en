BRANDON ALEX GRAHAM HANLAN (born 31 May 1997) is an English professional footballer who plays as a forward for Gillingham.


Career

Hanlan joined the academy at Charlton Athletic in November 2012, and signed a two-year contract extension in April 2016.[1] He made his first team debut as an 84th-minute substitute for Kevin Foley in a 2–0 defeat to Bury at Gigg Lane in a League One match on 6 August 2016.[2]

On 31 August 2017, Hanlan joined Colchester United on loan until the middle of January 2018.[3] He made his club debut as a substitute on 12 September and scored in the 1–1 draw with Chesterfield.[4]

On 1 February 2018, Hanlan rejoined Bromley on loan until the end of the 2017/18 season.[5]

He was released by Charlton at the end of the 2017–18 season.[6]

On 2 June 2018, Hanlan signed for Gillingham on a two-year deal, and scored the club's first goal of the season on his full Gillingham debut in a 2-0 away win against Accrington Stanley.[7] He was named as the club's Young Player of the Season at the end of his first season with the club.[8]
