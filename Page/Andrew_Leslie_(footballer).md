ANDREW ROBERTSON LESLIE was a Scottish amateur football wing half who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

As of 1911, Leslie was an apprentice engineer.[2] Leslie served as a lance corporal in the Highland Light Infantry during the First World War and was wounded in August 1916.[3] He was later commissioned as a second lieutenant in the London Regiment.[4]
