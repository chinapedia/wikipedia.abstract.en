'''Sahar Zaman ''' (born 13 June 1996) is a Pakistani women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Pakistan women's national football team.


Career

Zaman is from the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan.[1] In an interview, Zaman said she was inspired by American goalkeeper Hope Solo.[2] Zaman made her Pakistan debut aged 13 in a 2009 match against India women in Bangladesh.[3] She took part in a Pakistan training camp prior to the 2012 SAFF Women's Championship, and was also in the squad for the 2014 Pak Bahrain Women Series, which preceded the 2014 SAFF Women's Championship.[4][5] At club level, Zaman has played for Young Rising Star FFC in Pakistan.[6]
