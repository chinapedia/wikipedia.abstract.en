ÖMER AŞIK (born August 07, 1991) is a Turkish Paralympian archer competing in the Men's compound bow W1 event.[1]


Early life

Ömer Aşık was born on August 07, 1991.[2] He lives in Bolu, Turkey.[3]


Sporting career

Aşık began his archery career in 2013, and debuted internationally in 2015. He has been coached by Ahmet Soner Mersinli at Bolu Polissporgücü Club since 2012.[4]

He obtained a quota for the 2016 Summer Paralympics Games in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
