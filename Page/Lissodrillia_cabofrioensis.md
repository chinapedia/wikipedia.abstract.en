_LISSODRILLIA CABOFRIOENSIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 6 mm and 7.5 mm.


Distribution

This species occurs in the Atlantic Ocean off Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
