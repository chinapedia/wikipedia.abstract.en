MARJAN OP DEN VELDE (born 9 January 1971) was a Dutch water polo player. She was a member of the Netherlands women's national water polo team.

She competed with the team at the 2000 Summer Olympics.[1] She was also part of the national team at the 1991 World Aquatics Championship,[2] 1998 World Aquatics Championships, 1999 Women's European Water Polo Championship and 1999 FINA Women's Water Polo World Cup.
