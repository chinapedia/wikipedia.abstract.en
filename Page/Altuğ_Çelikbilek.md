ALTUĞ ÇELIKBILEK (born 7 September 1996) is a Turkish tennis player.

Çelikbilek has a career high ATP singles ranking of 407 achieved on 26 February 2018. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 258, achieved on 21 May 2018. Çelikbilek has won 2 ITF singles titles and 10 ITF doubles titles.

Çelikbilek has represented Turkey at Davis Cup, where he has a win-loss record of 2–6.
