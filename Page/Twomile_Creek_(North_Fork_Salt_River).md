TWOMILE CREEK (also called TWO MILE BRANCH) is a stream in Knox and Macon Counties in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the North Fork Salt River.

Twomile Creek is about two miles from Locust Hill, which may account for the name.[2]
