GROUP A OF THE 2017 AFRICA CUP OF NATIONS was played from 14 to 22 January 2017 in Gabon. The group consisted of Gabon, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, and Guinea-Bissau.[1]

Notes


Standings

In the quarter-finals:

-   The group winners, Burkina Faso, advanced to play the runners-up of Group B, Tunisia.
-   The group runners-up, Cameroon, advanced to play the winners of Group B, Senegal.
