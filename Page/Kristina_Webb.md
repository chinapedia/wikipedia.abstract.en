KRISTINA MARY WEBB is a New Zealand artist, Instagrammer and published author. She is best known for her Instagram art under the username @Colour_me_creative, for which she has received almost two million followers.[1] Her first book with HarperCollins New York, _Color Me Creative_,[2][3] was released to stores in the U.S., U.K., Australia and New Zealand in September 2015. In September 2016, she released a second book with HarperCollins, an adult coloring book _Color Me Inspired_.[4][5]
