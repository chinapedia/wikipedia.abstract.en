_AGELERADIX_ is a genus of East Asian funnel weavers first described by Xu & Li in 2007.[1]


Species

it contains six species:[2]

-   _Ageleradix cymbiforma_ (Wang, 1991) — China
-   _Ageleradix otiforma_ (Wang, 1991) — China
-   _Ageleradix schwendingeri_ Zhang, Li & Xu, 2008 — China
-   _Ageleradix sichuanensis_ Xu & Li, 2007 — China
-   _Ageleradix sternseptum_ Zhang, Li & Xu, 2008 — China
-   _Ageleradix zhishengi_ Zhang, Li & Xu, 2008 — China
