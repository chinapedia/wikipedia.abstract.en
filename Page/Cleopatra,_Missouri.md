CLEOPATRA is an unincorporated community in Mercer County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Cleopatra was originally called Somerset, and under the latter name was platted in 1856.[2] A post office called Cleopatra was established in 1861, and remained in operation until 1905.[3]
