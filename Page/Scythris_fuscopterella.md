_SCYTHRIS FUSCOPTERELLA_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Bengt Å. Bengtsson in 1997. It is found in Belarus, Moldova, Ukraine, eastern Siberia, France, Finland and Sweden.[1][2]

The wingspan is 13–14 mm.[3]

The larvae possibly feed on Ericaceae species.
