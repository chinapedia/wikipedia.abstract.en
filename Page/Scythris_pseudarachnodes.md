_SCYTHRIS PSEUDARACHNODES_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Bengt Å. Bengtsson in 1997. It is found on the Canary Islands (Tenerife).[1]


Etymology

The species name refers to the similar species _Scythris arachnodes_ plus Latin _pseudo_ (meaning false).[2]
