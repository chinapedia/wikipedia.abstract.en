ÉMILE BAAS (3 March 1906 – 4 June 1984[1]) was a 20th-century French essayist.


Works (selection)

-   1945: _Réflexions sur le régionalisme_, les Éditions Scouts de France, La Hutte, Lyon
-   1953: _Introduction critique au marxisme : perspectives marxistes, perspectives chrétiennes_, Alsatia, Colmar, (new edition, fully revised, of the booklet: _L'Humanisme marxiste_, by the same author, 1947)
-   1973: _Situation de l'Alsace_, Alsatia, Colmar (1945 text with an afterword by the author)
