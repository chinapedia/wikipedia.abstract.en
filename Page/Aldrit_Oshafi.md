ALDRIT OSHAFI (born 26 March 2000) is an Albanian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Albanian club Besa Kavajë.


Career

Aldrit Oshafi made his professional debut on September 28, 2016, in a 3-0 defeat against FK Kukësi. On October 26, 2016, he was called up to the Albania U-17 squad in a 1-0 defeat against Italy. He scored his first goal in for Tomori Berat on April 29, 2017 against Shkumbini Peqin.[1]
