_RHODODENDRON SERPYLLIFOLIUM_ (ウンゼンツツジ) is a rhododendron species native to Honshu, Kyushu, and Shikoku, Japan. It is a shrub that grows to 120 cm. (4 ft.) in height. Flowers are white to pink.


Synonyms

-   _Azalea serpyllifolia_ A.Gray
