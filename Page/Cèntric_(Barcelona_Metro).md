CÈNTRIC is a Barcelona Metro station, located in the El Prat de Llobregat municipality, in the Metropolitan area of Barcelona. The station is served by line L9.[1][2]

The station is located underneath the local _Plaça de Catalunya_ . There are two entrances on the same square, one in the center and the other close to the _Carrer de Lleida_, which serve a below ground ticket hall. The two long side platforms are at a lower level.[3][4]

The station was opened in 2016, when line L9 was extended from Zona Universitaria station to Aeroport T1 station.
