The CHINA AEROSPACE MUSEUM () is a museum in Fengtai District, Beijing. It highlights the technologies of the Chinese Space Program.[1] The museum has an area of more than 10,000 square meters. [2]The museum was founded in October 1992.[3] The museum has not been rated.[4]

China Space Museum is 3 floors with a of dedicated exhibition halls open to the general public.[5]

On display at the two hall are:

-   carrier rockets
-   manned spacecraft
-   satellites
-   moon exploration apparatus
-   section dedicate to China's space industry
