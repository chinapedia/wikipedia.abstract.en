DANIEL TEMPLETON was a Scottish amateur football inside forward who appeared in the Scottish League for Queen's Park, Ayr United and Partick Thistle.[1]


Personal life

Templeton served as an Air Mechanic 2nd Class in the Royal Air Force during the First World War.[2]
