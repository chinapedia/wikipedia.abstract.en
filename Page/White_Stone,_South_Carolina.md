WHITE STONE is an unincorporated community in Spartanburg County, South Carolina, United States.[1] The community is located along South Carolina Highway 295, east-southeast of Spartanburg. White Stone has a post office with ZIP code 29386, which opened on February 2, 1827.[2][3]
