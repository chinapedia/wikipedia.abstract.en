_THE FOREIGN LEGIONNAIRE_ (German: _FREMDENLEGIONÄR_) is a 1928 German silent adventure film directed by James Bauer and starring Dorothea Wieck, Ferdinand Martini and Therese Giehse.[1]

It was made at the Emelka Studios in Munich. The film's art direction was by Ludwig Reiber.
