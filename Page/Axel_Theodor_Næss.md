AXEL THEODOR NÆSS (19 December 1874 – 4 December 1945) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Mandal to printer and editor Anton August Næss and Angelica Thekla Schleusz. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1896, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1927. He was a member of the supervisory council for Botsfengselet from 1920.[1]
