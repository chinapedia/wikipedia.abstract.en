JACK HENRY PHILIP TAYLOR (born 23 June 1998) is a professional footballer who plays for Barnet.


Career

Club

Taylor was in the youth team at Chelsea for seven years,[1] before joining Barnet in 2012. He signed a two-year professional contract in April 2016.[2] He made his debut for the first team on 4 October 2016 in an EFL Trophy match against Norwich City.[3]

Taylor joined Hampton & Richmond Borough on loan for a month on 28 October 2016.[4] He was recalled from loan in December, and made his league debut against Yeovil Town on 10 December.[5]

International

Taylor was called up to the Republic of Ireland U21 squad for the 2019 Toulon Tournament in June 2019.[6] Taylor scored a brace in a training match against the senior team on 30 May 2019.[7] He made his debut as a substitute against Bahrain U23 on 9 June.[8]


Personal life

Jack also holds Irish citizenship. His father is from County Galway in Ireland. Taylor's older brother Harry (born 1997) was also in the youth team at Chelsea and is now a professional at Barnet.[9]
