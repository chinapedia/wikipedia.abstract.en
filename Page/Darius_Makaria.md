DARIUS CĂTĂLIN MAKARIA (born 2 February 1993)[1] is a Romanian handballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Cavigal Nice Handball in the France ProLigue.


Achievements

-   JUNIOR NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP :
    -   Gold Medalist: 2012
    -   Silver Medalist: 2009, 2011
-   LIGA NAȚIONALĂ:
    -   _Gold Medalist_: 2016
    -   _Bronze Medalist_: 2015
