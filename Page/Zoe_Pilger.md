ZOE PILGER (; born 1984) is an English author and art critic. Her first novel, _Eat My Heart Out_ won a Betty Trask Award and a Somerset Maugham Award.[1] She is the daughter of journalists John Pilger and Yvonne Roberts.[2] She is currently living in Berlin.[3]


Career

Pilger has been an art critic for _The Independent_, a British newspaper, since 2012. Her first novel, _Eat My Heart Out_ was published by Serpent's Tail. The novel has been described as a post-feminist satire about modern romance.[4]

She is currently researching her PhD on romantic love and sadomasochism in the work of female artists at Goldsmiths, University of London.[5]
