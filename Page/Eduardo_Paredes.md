EDUARDO JOSÉ PAREDES DURÁN (born March 6, 1995) is a Venezuelan professional baseball pitcher for the Kansas City T-Bones of the American Association of Independent Professional Baseball. He previously played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Los Angeles Angels.


Career

Los Angeles Angels

Paredes signed with the Los Angeles Angels as an international free agent in March 2012. The Angels added him to their 40-man roster after the 2016 season.[1]

Paredes started 2017 with the Mobile BayBears. He was called up to the Salt Lake Bees on May 3, and later to the Angels on June 23.[2] Paredes made his MLB debut the same day, allowing 2 runs on 2 hits in 2 innings of work against the Boston Red Sox. He elected free agency on November 2, 2018.[3]

Detroit Tigers

On December 13, 2018, Paredes signed a minor league deal with the Detroit Tigers.[4] He was released on April 24, 2019.

Kansas City T-Bones

On June 1, 2019, Paredes signed with the Kansas City T-Bones of the independent American Association.
