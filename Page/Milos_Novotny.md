MILOS NOVOTNY (born 1942) is an American chemist, currently the Distinguished Professor Emeritus and Director of the Novotny Glycoscience Laboratory and the Institute for Pheromone Research at Indiana University,[1] and also a published author.[2][3] Along with holding honorary doctorates from Uppsala University, Masaryk University and Charles University, he has been a major figure in analytical separation methods.[4]
