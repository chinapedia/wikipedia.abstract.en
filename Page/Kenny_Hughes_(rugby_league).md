KENNY HUGHES (born 13th September 1990) is a rugby league footballer who plays for the North Wales Crusaders in Betfred League 1. He plays as a hooker.[1]

Hughes came through the St Helens academy[2] and joined Oldham to play in the reserves in 2011.[3]

Hughes previously played for Oldham in the Kingstone Press Championship and Betfred League 1.
