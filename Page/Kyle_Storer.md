KYLE STORER (born 30 April 1987) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Solihull Moors.[1]


Club career

In July 2015, Storer signed for National League club Cheltenham Town.[2] In the last match of the season, he was involved in an incident in which he reportedly bit another player and for which he was suspended for eight matches.[3] In October 2016, he made his first League Two appearance for the club in a match against Crawley Town.[4][5]

In January 2018, Storer left Cheltenham Town and signed for National League club Solihull Moors.[6]
