RANDI GOTENI (born 5 July 1995) is a Congolese footballer who plays as a midfielder for Dunkerque, on loan from Troyes.[1]


Club career

in summer 2017, Goteni joined Championnat National side Béziers on loan from Troyes for the 2017–18 season.[2]


International career

Goteni was born in the Republic of the Congo, but raised in France. He represented the France U16 in a friendly in 2011.[3] Goteni debuted for the Congo U20s for a friendly in 2015.[4]
