_ONE NIGHT WITH YOU_ (Italian: _UNA NOTTE CON TE_) is a 1932 Italian comedy film directed by Ferruccio Biancini and E.W. Emo and starring Elsa Merlini, Nino Besozzi and Ugo Ceseri.[1] It was made as a MLV, with a German version _Little Girl, Great Fortune_ also released.


Cast

-   Elsa Merlini as La falsa ladra
-   Nino Besozzi as Il giovine scapolo
-   Ugo Ceseri as Il commissario
-   Martha Ziegler as La cameriera straniera
-   Giana Cellini as Fidanzata del scapolo
-   Angelo Ferrari
-   Evelina Paoli
