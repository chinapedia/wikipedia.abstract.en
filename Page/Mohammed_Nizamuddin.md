MOHAMMED NIZAMUDDIN (d. 2016) was an Indian trade unionist and politician, belonging to the Communist Party of India (Marxist).[1] Mohammed Nizamuddin won the Entally constituency seat of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly in the 1971, 1977, 1982 and 1991 elections.[2] He served as general secretary of the All India Beedi Workers Federation and was a member of the All India Working Committee of the Centre of Indian Trade Unions.[3] He died in Kolkata on June 21, 2016, at the age of 83.[4]
