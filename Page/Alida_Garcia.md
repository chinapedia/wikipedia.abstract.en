ALIDA GARCIA is an American social activist whose major work is centered on immigration issues.[1] She was arrested during her first quarter of college, after a protest against janitor related layoffs at the Stanford Hospital.[2][3][4][5]


Career

Garcia currently serves as the Director of Coalitions & Policy for FWD.us, a lobbying group Co-Founded by Mark Zuckerberg. Some main goals of the initiative are to provide immigration reform, enhance the education system and invest in the sciences.[6] Additionally, she is the Co-Founder & Executive Director of Inclusv.[7][8]
