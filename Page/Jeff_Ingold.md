JEFF INGOLD is an American screenwriter and producer. He has worked on TV shows such as Scrubs, Undateable, Ground Floor, Rush Hour, The Office, Parks and Recreation, 30 Rock, Community and Will & Grace.

He currently works with Bill Lawrence as the Head Producer and President of Lawrence's production company, Doozer. He is the former head of comedy at NBC.[1]


Early life and education

Jeff Ingold grew up in Detroit, Michigan. Ingold graduated from Princeton University in 1990 with a degree in history. He received a MBA from the USC Marshall School of Business.[2]


Personal life

Ingold resides in Los Angeles, California, with his wife and two children, Lucy and Theodore.
