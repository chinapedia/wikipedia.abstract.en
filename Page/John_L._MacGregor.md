JOHN L. MACGREGOR served as the Executive Director of the Relationships Committee of Scouts Canada.

In 1983, MacGregor was awarded the 164th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1] He was also a recipient of the Silver World Award.[2]
