WILLIAM HENRY MAUGHAN (July 1894 – 2 October 1916) was an English professional football right half who played in the Football League for Fulham.[1]


Personal life

Maughan served as a corporal in the Durham Light Infantry during the First World War.[2] He was seriously wounded during the opening phase of the Battle of Le Transloy and died at a Casualty Clearing Station in Dernancourt, France on 2 October 1916.[3] Maughan was buried in Dernancourt Communal Cemetery Extension.[4]
