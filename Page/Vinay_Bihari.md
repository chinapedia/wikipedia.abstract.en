VINAY BIHARI is a politician and film director from Bihar.[1] He has won the Bihar Legislative Assembly election in 2010 and 2015 from Lauriya Assembly constituency Lauriya.[2][3]

On 1 March 2017, Bihari ended a months long protest in which he called for the government to build a 44 km road in his constituency by stripping to his underwear.[4]
