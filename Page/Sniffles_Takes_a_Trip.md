_SNIFFLES TAKES A TRIP_ is a 1940 _Merrie Melodies_ cartoon directed by Chuck Jones.[1]


Plot

Sniffles goes into the countryside hoping to live there and escape city life.[2] He finds that the countryside is very scary and gets lost. Sniffles manages to escape as he much prefers the city over the countryside.
