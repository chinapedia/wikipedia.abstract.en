_REPENTANCE_ is a 1922 British silent drama film directed by Edward Gordon and starring Peggy Hathaway, Roy Raymond and Geoffrey Benstead.[1]


Cast

-   Peggy Hathaway as Queenie Creedon
-   Roy Raymond as Frank Hepburn
-   Geoffrey Benstead as Toby
-   Ray Lankester as Dr. Smith
-   Fabbie Benstead
-   Ward McAllister
