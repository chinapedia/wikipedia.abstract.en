DANIEL JAY HEGEMAN (born March 4, 1963) is a Republican member of the Missouri Senate, representing the 12th district, which covers parts of northwestern Missouri. He was first elected unopposed in 2014.[1]

Hegeman previously served as president of the Andrew County Farm Bureau, member of the Nodaway County Economic Development Board, and member of the Maryville and Savannah Chambers of Commerce.[2] He also served in the Missouri House of Representatives from 1991 until 2003.[3]


Personal life

Hegeman and his wife, Francine, have 4 children; Hannah, Joseph, Heidi, and Joshua. They reside in Cosby, Missouri.[4]

Hegeman graduated from Savannah High School in 1981, and from the University of Missouri in 1985.[5]
