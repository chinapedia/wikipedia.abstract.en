EOIN O'BROIN (), better known by his stage name NOISESTORM, is an Irish DJ and music producer. He is best known for his song "Crab Rave", which peaked at 14 on _Billboard_{{'}}s Dance/Electronic Songs chart.


Career

On April 1, 2018, O'Broin released his song "Crab Rave" on Monstercat as an April Fools' Day joke. The song gained popularity as an internet meme, with the song debuting at number 36 on Billboard's "Hot Dance/Electronic Songs" category.[1] Following Crab Rave, O'Broin released the song "Breakout" with English hip hop group Foreign Beggars.[2] Critics praised the release, with _Dancing Astronaut_ praising the mixture of rap with the synths and drops.[3]
