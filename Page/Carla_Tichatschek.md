KARLA MARIA "CARLA" TICHATSCHEK[1] (born 6 January 1941)[2] is an Italian former figure skater who competed in ladies' singles. She represented Italy at the 1960 Winter Olympics in Squaw Valley, California.[3] She also appeared at four World Championships and three European Championships.
