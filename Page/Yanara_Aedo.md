YANARA KATHERINE NICOLE AEDO MUÑOZ (born 5 August 1993) is a Chilean footballer who plays as a forward for the Chile women's national team.


Club career

Aedo left Colo-Colo for American National Women's Soccer League team Washington Spirit in January 2015.[1] She helped the club's reserve team win the 2015 USL W-League season championship, scoring twice in the 2–1 final win over Colorado Pride.[2]

In September 2016 she transferred to Spain's Primera División club Valencia CF Femenino.[3] On 27 June 2017, the Washington Spirit announced that they had re-signed Aedo.[4] She was placed on waivers by the Spirit on 21 June 2018.[5]

On 13 July 2018 it was announced that Aedo was returning to Valencia CF Femenino, the club she had played with from 2016–2017.[6] She left the Spanish club at the end of the season.[7]


International career

In early September 2010, seventeen-year-old Aedo represented Chile at the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup. Later that month, she was named in Chile's 20-player senior squad for the 2010 South American Women's Football Championship in Ecuador.[8] She scored the opening goal in Chile's 3–1 win over Peru. Aedo scored three goals at the 2018 Copa América Femenina,[9] where Chile qualified to a FIFA Women's World Cup for the first time in its history.
