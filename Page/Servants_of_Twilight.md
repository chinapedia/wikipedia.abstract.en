_SERVANTS OF TWILIGHT_ is a 1991 American horror film directed by Jeffrey Obrow and starring Bruce Greenwood, Belinda Bauer, Grace Zabriskie and Jarrett Lennon. It is based on the novel _The Servants of Twilight_ by Dean Koontz.[1]


Plot

P.I. Charlie Harrison is hired by Christine Scavello to protect her and her son Joey from "Mother" Grace Spivey, the leader of a religious group that believes young Joey is the Antichrist and will stop at nothing to kill him. On the run, with Spivey and her fanatics around every corner, Charlie must make the decision of who is right and wrong.


Cast

-   Bruce Greenwood as Charlie Harrison
-   Belinda Bauer as Christine Scavello
-   Grace Zabriskie as Grace Spivey
-   Richard Bradford as Henry Rankin
-   Jarrett Lennon as Joey Scavello
-   Carel Struycken as Kyle Barlow
-   Jack Kehoe as Dr. Denton Boothe
-   Kelli Maroney as Sherry Ordway


Release

Initially intended as a theatrical release, _Servants of Twilight_ instead made its debut Showtime on October 4, 1991. It was later released on VHS in the U.S. in December 1991 by Vidmark Entertainment.
