DANIEL ORLANDO DÍAZ MUÑOZ (born 7 August 1948 in Santiago, Chile) is a former Chilean footballer who played in 4 clubs of Chile and the Chile national football team in the Copa América 1975.


Teams

-   Universidad Católica 1968-1971

-   Huachipato 1972-1976

-   Colo Colo 1977-1982

-   Rangers 1982


Titles

-   Huachipato 1974 (Chilean Primera División Championship)

-   Colo Colo 1979 and 1981 (Chilean Primera División Championship), 1981 and 1982 (Copa Chile)
