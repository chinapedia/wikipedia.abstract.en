_FENIMOREA ABSCONDITA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of this marine shell varies between 20 mm and 36 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off Florida, USA.
