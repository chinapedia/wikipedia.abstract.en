CILLA NAUMANN (born 16 July 1960) is a Swedish author and journalist. Her first book, _Vattenhjärta_, came out in 1995. It won the Katapulpriset for "Best debut of the Year".[1] Her next book was _Dom_, published in 2000.[2]
