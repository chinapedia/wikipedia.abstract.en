RICHLAND CHRISTIAN CHURCH is a historic Disciples of Christ church located at Kingdom City, Callaway County, Missouri. It was built in 1873, and is a one-story, rectangular, three bay, frame building with Italianate style design elements. It has a front gable roof and rests on eight stone piers. Also on the property is a contributing outhouse.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2001.[2]
