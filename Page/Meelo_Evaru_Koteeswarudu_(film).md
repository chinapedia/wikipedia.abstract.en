_MEELO EVARU KOTESWARUDU_ (English: Who is the millionaire among you all?)[1] is a Telugu Film directed by E. Satti Babu and produced by K. K. Radhamohan starring Naveen Chandra,[2] Shruti Sodhi, Prudhviraj and Saloni Aswani among others. The film is set to release in the end of 2016.


Cast

-   Prudhviraj as Variation Star
-   Saloni Aswani as Samantha
-   Jaya Prakash Reddy
-   Posani Krishna Murali
-   Murali Sharma as Priya's father
-   Raghu Babu
-   Prabhas Sreenu
-   Chalapathi Rao
-   Dhanraj
-   Pilla Prasad
-   Saana
-   Vidyullekha Raman as Priya's friend


Production

The first schedule of the movie started in mid 2016, and few pictures and teaser[4] of the movie floated on the internet.


Soundtrack

Audio of the movie is set to release on 19 October.[5]
