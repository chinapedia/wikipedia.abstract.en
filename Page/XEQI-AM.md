XEQI-AM is a radio station on 1510 AM in Monterrey, Nuevo León. It is owned by the state government of Nuevo León and known as Radio Alternativa.


History

XEQI_LaRadioAlternative1510AM_logo.jpg XEQI opened in 1979, a year after XHQI-FM Radio Gobierno signed on. It became Opus after the format was moved from 102.1 FM on July 31, 2017, replacing "La Radio Alternativa".[1]

XEQI is nominally a daytimer but transmits from 6am to midnight.
