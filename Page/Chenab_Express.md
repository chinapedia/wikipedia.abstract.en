_CHENAB EXPRESS_ () is a passenger train operated daily by Pakistan Railways between Sargodha and Lala Musa.[1] The trip takes approximately 3 hours and 30 minutes to cover a published distance of , traveling along a stretch of the Shorkot–Lalamusa Branch Line. The train named after the Chenab River.
