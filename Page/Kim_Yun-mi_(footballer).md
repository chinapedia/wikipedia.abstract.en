'''Kim Yun-Mi ''' (born 1 July 1993) is a North Korean footballer who plays as a midfielder for the North Korea women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 Algarve Cup,[1] 2014 Asian Games[2] and 2015 EAFF Women's East Asian Cup.[3] At the club level, she played for April 25 Sports Club in North Korea.[4]
