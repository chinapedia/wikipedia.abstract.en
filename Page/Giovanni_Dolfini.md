GIOVANNI DOLFINI (1885-1968) was an Italian stage and film actor.[1] He also directed the 1920 silent film _Dopo_.


Selected filmography

-   _Agrippina_ (1911)
-   _The Last Adventure_ (1932)
-   _Sleeping Beauty_ (1942)
-   _A Yank in Rome_ (1946)
-   _Hotel Luna, Room 34_ (1946)
-   _Honeymoon Deferred_ (1950)
-   _Who Is Without Sin_ (1952)
-   _The Dream of Zorro_ (1952)
-   _Torna!_ (1954)
