KARUNYA RAM is an Indian film actress who works in Kannada-language cinema. She began her film career as a lead in the 2015 film _Vajrakaya_.[1][2][3]


Early life

She was born to B.C.Ramachandra Gowda & Prema on 10 August in Bangalore & her native is Melukote.


Filmography

She was brought up in SRVHS school in Bangalore & pursued her PUC in SJRC women's college & degree in Surana college south end circle. Karunya Ram was an all rounder during her school days She has paired opposite to Dr.Shiva Rajkumar in the film. She was also the part of big reality show Biggboss season 4 Kannada. In 2017 she was paired opposite to Vijay Raghavendra in Eradu Kanasu.
