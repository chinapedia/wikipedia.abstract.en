WILLIAMS ČERNEKA (born 19 September 1959 in Rijeka) is a former Croatian handball player. Since 2002 he's been the tehniko of RK Zamet[1]

He is the father of Dario Černeka.


Honours

Zamet

-   Yugoslav Second League (1): 1977-78
-   Yugoslav Third League (1): 1976-77
