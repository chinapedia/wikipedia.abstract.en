Professor ARMANDO GÁLVEZ C. served as the National Executive Commissioner of the Asociación de Scouts de Guatemala.

In 1974, he was awarded the 85th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
