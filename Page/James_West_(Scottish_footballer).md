JAMES LOCKHART WEST was a Scottish amateur football right back who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1] He later became a member of the club's committee.[2]


Personal life

West fought with the Highland Light Infantry during the First World War and rose from private to second lieutenant during the course of the war.[3]


Honours

QUEEN'S PARK STROLLERS

-   Scottish Amateur League (1): 1911–12[4]
-   Scottish Amateur Cup (2): 1911–12, 1919–20[5]
