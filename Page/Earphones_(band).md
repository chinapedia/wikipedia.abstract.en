is a Japanese idol group formed in 2015. The members of the group are Rie Takahashi, Yuki Nagaku, and Marika Kouno under Evil Line Records label.[1] Originally, a fictitious idol group consisting of three main characters from the anime _Seiyu's Life!_, Earphones soon became a real idol group, its three members the voice actresses responsible for their respective characters in the anime.
