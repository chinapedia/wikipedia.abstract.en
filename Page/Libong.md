_' Libong_' (also Libong I) is a village in the commune of Tignère in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon.


Population

In 1971, Libong contained 95 inhabitants, mainly Fula people.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 318 people in the village.[2]
