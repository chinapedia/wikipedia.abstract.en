_ROTUNDARIA_ is a genus of freshwater mussels, aquatic bivalve mollusks in the family Unionidae, the river mussels.


Species

Species within this genus include:[1]

-   _Rotundaria asperata_ (I. Lea, 1861)
-   _Rotundaria aurea_ (I. Lea, 1859)
-   _Rotundaria couchiana_ (I. Lea, 1860)
-   _Rotundaria houstonensis_ (I. Lea, 1859)
-   _Rotundaria infucata_ (Conrad, 1834)
-   _Rotundaria kieneriana_ (I. Lea, 1852)
-   _Rotundaria kleiniana_ (I. Lea, 1852)
-   _Rotundaria nodulata_ (Rafinesque, 1820)
-   _Rotundaria petrina_ (Gould, 1855)
-   _Rotundaria pustulosa_ (I. Lea, 1831)
-   _Rotundaria refulgens_ (I. Lea, 1868)
-   _Rotundaria succissa_ (I. Lea, 1852)
-   _Rotundaria tuberculata_ (Rafinesque, 1820)
