_ORRMAESIA ANCILLA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description


Distribution

This species occurs in the demersal zone of the Indian Ocean off the Agulhas Bank and the Eastern Cape, South Africa
