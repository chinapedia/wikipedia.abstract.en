CHOW TEWA MEIN is an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Chow Tewa Mein was elected unopposed from Chowkham seat in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election, standing as a People's Party of Arunachal candidate.[2][3][4] In terms of educational qualification, he is a graduate (B.A.).

He was one of 6 MLAs along with Chief Minister Pema Khandu to be suspended by the PPA for anti-party activities.[5]
