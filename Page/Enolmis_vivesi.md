_ENOLMIS VIVESI_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Bengt Å. Bengtsson and Pietro Passerin d'Entrèves in 1988. It is found in Spain.[1]


Etymology

The species is named in honour of Dr. A. Vives, Madrid, who provided much of the type material.[2]
