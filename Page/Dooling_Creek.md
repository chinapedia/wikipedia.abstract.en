DOOLING CREEK is a stream in Osage County of central Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Missouri River.

The stream headwaters are at at an elevation of about and it flows north and northeast passing west and north of Chamois before reaching its confluence with the Missouri at at an elevation of .[2][3]

Variant names were "Doolings Creek" and "Doolins Creek".[4] The creek derives its name from the local Doolin family.[5]
