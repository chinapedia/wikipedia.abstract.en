_CLOCK WITHOUT HANDS_ is American author Carson McCullers' final novel. It was published on September 18, 1961 by Houghton Mifflin.


Reception

The book received primarily positive reviews. _Kirkus Reviews_ stated that the novel "embellishes an already fine literary reputation though it lacks the sting of [McCullers'] previous work"[1] while _The Atlantic_ called the book "the masterly new novel by Carson McCullers."[2]
