BAWAKA is a village in the commune of Ngaoui in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, near the border with the Central African Republic.


Population

In 1967, Bawaka contained 420 inhabitants, mostly Wodaabe and Gbaya people[1]

In the 2005 census, 1804 people were counted there.[2]
