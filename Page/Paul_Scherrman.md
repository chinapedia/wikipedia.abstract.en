PAUL J. SCHERRMAN (born August 27, 1948) is an American politician and businessman in the state of Iowa.

Schermann was born in Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin and attended Campion High School in Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin. He then went to St. Mary's University of Minnesota in Winona, Minnesota. He was vice-president of J. P. Scherrman, Inc. in Dubuque, Iowa. A Democrat, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1997 to 2003 (33rd district).[1]
