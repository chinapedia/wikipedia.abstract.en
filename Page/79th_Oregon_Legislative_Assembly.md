Oregon_Senate_map.svg The 79TH OREGON LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY is the current meeting of the Oregon Legislative Assembly, since January 9, 2017.

In the November 2016 elections, the Democratic Party of Oregon lost one seat in the Senate, leaving a 17–13 majority and maintaining its 35–25 control of the House.[1]


Senate

Based on the results of the 2016 elections, the Oregon State Senate is composed of 17 Democrats and 13 Republicans. Republicans gained one seat in District 3.[2]
