YEVHEN KOTYUN (; born 31 July 1996 in Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder who plays for Volyn Lutsk in the Ukrainian First League.


Career

Kotyun is a product of the FC Volyn Youth Sportive School System. Then he signed a professional contract with FC Volyn Lutsk in the Ukrainian Premier League, spent before some time as player in FC Laska Boratyn amateur team.

He made his debut in the Ukrainian Premier League for FC Volyn on 26 November 2016, playing in the match against FC Dynamo Kyiv.[1]
