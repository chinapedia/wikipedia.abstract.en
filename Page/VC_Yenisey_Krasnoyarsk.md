YENISEY (__) also spelled YENISEI and ENISEY, is a Russian women's volleyball club based in Krasnoyarsk. The club was founded in 1992 and currently plays in the super league, the top Russian league.


Previous names

-   SIBIRYACHKA (1992–1995)
-   NIKA (1995–1997)
-   BOGUR (1997–2000)
-   YENISEYUSHKA (ENISEYUSHKA) (2000–2003)
-   METROSTROY (2003–2004)
-   STROYTEL (2004–2010)
-   YONOST (2010–2012)
-   YENISEY (YENISEI / ENISEY) (2012–present)


History

The club was established in 1992 as . It has changed names many times (Nika, Bogur, Yeniseyushka, Metrostroy, Yonost) and has been competing mainly in the lower leagues of the Russian Championship and the Cup of Siberia and Far East. It played at the Super League for the first time in the 2004–05 season and finished last.[1][2]

In January 2012 the club came under the professional structure of Volleyball Club Yenisey and was renamed .[3][4]
