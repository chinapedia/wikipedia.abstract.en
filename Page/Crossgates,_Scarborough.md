CROSSGATES is a village in Scarborough, North Yorkshire, England, between Seamer to the west and Eastfield to the east.[1] At Crossgates there is a railway station called Seamer station.

The Village was built after the Second World War. There were new houses built in the village in 2000. Nearby points of interest include Star Carr, a Mesolithic archaeological site.

Towns nearby include Scarborough and Filey. it's part of Seamer.
