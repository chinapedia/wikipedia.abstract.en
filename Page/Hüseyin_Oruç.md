HÜSEYIN ORUÇ is a Turkish male badminton player.[1] He won the silver medal at the 2013 Mediterranean Games in the men's doubles event partnered with Emre Aslan.[2] In 2012, he and Aslan also won the men's doubles title at the Iraq International tournament.[3]
