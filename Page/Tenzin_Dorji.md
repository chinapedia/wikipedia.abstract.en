TENZIN DORJI is a Bhutanese international footballer. He made his first appearance in an unofficial friendly match against India.[1] He made his official debut in their 2019 AFC Asian Cup qualifying match second leg against Bangladesh, being named in the starting lineup and playing the whole game.[2]
