The CATALINA 320 is an American sailboat, that was designed by Gerry Douglas and first built in 1993. The design is out of production.[1][2][3]

The boat was built by Catalina Yachts in the United States.[4]


Design

Catalina_320_sailboat_Wanderer_0813.jpg Catalina_320_sailboat_4177.jpg The Catalina 320 is a small recreational keelboat, built predominantly of fiberglass. It has a masthead sloop rig, an internally-mounted spade-type rudder and a fixed fin bulb keel. It displaces and carries of ballast.[5][6]
