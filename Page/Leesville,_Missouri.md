LEESVILLE is an unincorporated community in Henry County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Leesville was originally called "Tebo", and under the latter name was platted in 1854.[2] A post office called Leesville was established in 1857, and remained in operation until 1918.[3] The present name is after Andrew Jackson Lee, an early settler.[4]
