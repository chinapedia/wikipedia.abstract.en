SPACE INVADAS is an Australian hip-hop duo made up of Katalyst (Ashley Anderson) and Steve Spacek (Steve White).[1] Their album, _Soul:Fi_ (22 March 2010), was nominated at the ARIA Music Awards of 2010 for Best Urban Album.[2] It peaked at No. 20 on the ARIA Hitseekers Albums chart.[3]
