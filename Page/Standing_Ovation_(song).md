"STANDING OVATION" is a song written and performed by GQ. It reached #12 on the U.S. R&B chart and #35 on the U.S. dance chart in 1980.[1] The song was featured on their 1980 album, _Two_.[2]

The song was produced by Jimmy Simpson.[3]
