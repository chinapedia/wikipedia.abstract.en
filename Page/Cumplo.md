CUMPLO is a crowdlending company, operating in Mexico and Chile, which connects loan applicant companies with a network of investors through its online platform. It is a closed stock company, consisting of a directory of five members, whose president and founder is Nicolas Shea Carey. Since its beginning, it has funded more than USD 330 million in loans. It is certified as a B corporation.
