NIZHNYAYA MAKTAMA (; , _Tübän Maqtama_) is an urban locality (an urban-type settlement) in Almetyevsky District of the Republic of Tatarstan, Russia. As of the 2010 Census, its population was 9,924.[1]


History

It was granted urban-type settlement status in 1966.


Administrative and municipal status

Within the framework of administrative divisions, the urban-type settlement of Nizhnyaya Maktama is subordinated to Almetyevsky District.[2] As a municipal division, Nizhnyaya Maktama, together with one rural locality (the _selo_ of Tikhonovka), is incorporated within Almetyevsky Municipal District as NIZHNYAYA MAKTAMA URBAN SETTLEMENT.[3]
