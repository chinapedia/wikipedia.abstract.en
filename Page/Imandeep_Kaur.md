IMANDEEP KAUR (born 8 October 1985) is a British social and civil activist. She is the director of Impact Hub: Birmingham.[1]


Early life and education

Kaur was born in Birmingham, England to parents of Indian origin. Her family originates from Punjab, India.

Kaur was educated at The Solihull School. Kaur then entered University of Cardiff in 2004 graduating with a BSc in Physiology in 2007, achieving an Upper Second Class classification. In 2011, Kaur graduated from University of Birmingham with a MSc in International Development.[2]


Awards

On April 2016, Kaur won the 2016 Asian Business Women of the Year award.[3]

On 1 April 2016, Kaur was listed as one of the most influential people within the world of business lobbying within the West Midlands.[4]
