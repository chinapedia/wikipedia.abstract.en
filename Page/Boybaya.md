BOYBAYA (also Boy Baya) is a village in the commune of in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon.


Population

In 1967, Boybaya contained 216 inhabitants, mostly Gbaya people and Wodaabe[1]

In the 2005 census, 986 people were counted there.[2]
