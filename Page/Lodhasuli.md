LODHASULI is a village in Jhargram CD Block in Jhargram subdivision of Jhargram district in the state of West Bengal, India. The headquarters of this CD Block are located here.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Lodhasuli had a total population of 1,102 of which 569 (52%) were males and 569 (52%) were females. Population below 6 years was 127. The total number of literates in Lodhasuli was 814 (73.87% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Transport

NH 49 running from Bilaspur to Kharagpur meets SH 5 running from Rupnarayanpur (in Bardhaman district) to Junput (in Purba Medinipur district) at Lodhasuli.[2][3]
