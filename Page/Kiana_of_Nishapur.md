KIANA SĀDĀT-HOSSEINI renowned as LITTLE KIANA or '''Kiana of Nishapur ''' also THE SEVEN-YEAR-OLD KIANA (2009–2016) was an Iranian seven-year-old Preschool student from Nishapur. She was kidnapped, raped and murdered by her aunt's husband _Mehdi N_ on September 15, 2016. He was hanged in public 15 days later on September 30, 2016 in Nishapur with a special court order signed by Chief Justice Sadegh Larijani.[1][2][3][4][5]
