_OLIGOSPIRA_ is a genus of air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Acavidae. These snails are endemic to Sri Lanka.

Three species are recognized.[1] They have less conical shells with flat tops.


Species

-   _Oligospira polei_ (Collet, 1899)
-   _Oligospira skinneri_ (Reeve, 1854)
-   _Oligospira waltoni_ (Reeve, 1842)
