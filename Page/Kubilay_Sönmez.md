KUBILAY SÖNMEZ (born 17 June 1994 in Kamen, Germany) is a Turkish professional footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Hatayspor.[1]

Sönmez was born in Germany to parents of Turkish descent. He represents the Turkish Football Federation, and has represented the Turkish U20 and U21 national teams.[2]
