SALESBOX CRM is a Sales CRM tool from Stockholm, Sweden.[1] It is designed as an integrated end-to-end application (OMNI-channel) for sales administration.[2][3][4]


History

Salesbox CRM was founded in 2014[5] and the system can be accessed worldwide, and is available in 16 languages, including English, Spanish, Portuguese, German and French.[6][7]

Salesbox has won the "Best Sales CRM Product or Service Award" at the Sales Innovation Expo in 2016.[8]
