MANASA RADHAKRISHNAN (born 29 September 1998[1]) is an Indian film actress who appears predominantly in Malayalam cinema Industry.[2][3][4]


Personal life

She was born in Ernakulam, Kerala, India to Radhakrishnan V. K. and Sreekala and was raised in Dubai. She studied till class 10 at The Indian High School, Dubai, and her higher secondary education was at The Choice School in Thrippunithura. She has learnt Indian classical dance, cinematic dance, and guitar. Manasa is currently pursuing Computer Science and Engineering at Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS).
