EMMA SWIFT is an Australian country singer.

-   -   Her self-titled EP, recorded in Nashville with producer Anne McCue, was nominated for a 2014 ARIA Award for Best Country Album.[1]

She relocated to Nashville in 2013.[2]

Swift is also a radio broadcaster, hosting Americana music show _In the Pines_ on FBi Radio[3] and _Revelator_ on Double J at Australian Broadcasting Corporation in Sydney, Australia.[4]

She has toured the US, Australia, UK, and Europe with Robyn Hitchcock, and they have released two singles together, "Follow Your Money" in 2015 and "Love Is a Drag" in 2016, produced by Norman Blake of Teenage Fanclub .[5] Swift also performs backing vocals along with Gillian Welch and Pat Sansone of Wilco on Hitchcock's 2017 release.

Swift opened for Ryan Adams in Australia in 2016.[6]
