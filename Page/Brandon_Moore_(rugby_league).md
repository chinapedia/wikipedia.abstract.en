BRANDON MOORE (born 27 July 1996) is an English rugby league footballer who plays as a for Halifax in the Betfred Championship.[1]


Background

Moore was born in Cumbria, England.


Career

Moore has previously played for the Castleford Tigers in the Super League.[2]
