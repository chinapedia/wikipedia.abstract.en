KOHL CITY (also known as KOHL) is an unincorporated community in Franklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Kohl was established in 1892, and remained in operation until 1903.[2] The community derives its name from the local Kohlbusch family.[3]
