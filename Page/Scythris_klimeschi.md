_SCYTHRIS KLIMESCHI_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Passerin d'Entrèves in 1983. It is found on the Canary Islands.

The wingspan is 9-11.5 mm.[1]

The larvae have been recorded feeding on _Salsola oppositifolia_, _Salsola longifolia_ and possibly _Atriplex parvifolius_.[2]
