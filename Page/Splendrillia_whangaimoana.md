_SPLENDRILLIA WHANGAIMOANA_ is an extinct species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell attains 14.3 mm, its diameter 6.1 mm.


Distribution

This extinct marine species was endemic to New Zealand
