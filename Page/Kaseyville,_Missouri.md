KASEYVILLE is an unincorporated community in Macon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Kaseyville was established in 1867, and remained in operation until 1908.[2] Singleton L. Kasey, an early postmaster, gave the community his last name.[3]
