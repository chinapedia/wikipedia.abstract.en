ANDRÉ LUIZ DE CARVALHO RIBEIRO (born August 27, 1989) is a Brazilian physician and politician. He was State Deputy (2011–2015). Fufuca is Federal Deputy and President of Progressistas in Maranhão.[1]


Political career

Fufuca voted in favor of the impeachment of then-president Dilma Rousseff.[2] Fufuca voted against the 2017 Brazilian labor reform,[3] and would vote against a corruption investigation into Rousseff's successor Michel Temer.[4]
