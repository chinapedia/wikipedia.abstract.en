PIERRE EUGÈNE ALEXANDRE MAROT (15 December 1900, Neufchâteau (Vosges) – 28 November 1992, Paris) was a 20th-century French medievalist historian, director of the École Nationale des Chartes. He was a member of the Institut de France, the Académie de Stanislas and the Académie des Inscriptions et Belles-Lettres
