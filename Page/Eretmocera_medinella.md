_ERETMOCERA MEDINELLA_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Otto Staudinger in 1859.[1] It is found in Spain, Sardinia, Russia[2] and Turkey (Erzurum and Igdir Provinces).[3]

The wingspan is 10–12 mm.[4]

The larvae feed on _Suaeda_ species.
