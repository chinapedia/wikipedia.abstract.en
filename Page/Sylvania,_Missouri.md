SYLVANIA is an unincorporated community in northwest Dade County, Missouri, United States.[1] Sylvania is located at the intersection of Missouri routes D and E, south of Jerico Springs. The Stony Point Prairie Conservation Area lies just to the north along route D.[2]


History

A post office called Sylvania was established in 1869, and remained in operation until 1907.[3] The community's name is derived from Pennsylvania, the home base state of a coal company operating in the area.[4]
