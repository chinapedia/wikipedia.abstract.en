JOHN R. PHILLPOT served as the International Commissioner of the Scout Association of the Bahamas.[1]

In 1986, Phillpot was awarded the 187th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2] He was also a recipient of the Silver World Award.[3]
