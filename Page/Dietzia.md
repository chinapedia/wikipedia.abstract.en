_DIETZIA_ is a Gram-positive bacterial genus from the family of Dietziaceae which occur in many different habitats including humans and animals.[1][2][3][4] The species Dietzia maris is a human pathogen.[5][6] The genus _Dietzia_ is named after the American microbiologist Alma Dietz.[7]
