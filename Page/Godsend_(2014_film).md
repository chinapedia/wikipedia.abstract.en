_GODSEND_ () is a 2014[1] South Korean drama film directed by Si-hyun Moon. It was written by the renowned art house moviemaker Kim Ki-duk.[2]


Plot summary

A teenage girl is pregnant and prepared to give up her baby in exchange for an expensive car.[3][4]
