_POINTS WEST_ is a 1929 silent film western directed by Arthur Rosson and starring Hoot Gibson. It was produced and distributed by Universal Pictures.[1][2]


Cast

-   Hoot Gibson - Cole Lawson, Jr.
-   Alberta Vaughn - Dorothy
-   Frank Campeau - McQuade
-   Jack Raymond - His Nibs
-   Martha Franklin - The Mother
-   Milt Brown - Parsons
-   Jim Corey - Steve


Preservation status

-   A print survives but no archive is stipulated at the Library of Congress/FIAF database.[3]
