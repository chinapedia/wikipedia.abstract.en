OLIVIER BÉRIOT (born 30 November 1962) is a French costume designer.[1]

He received César Award nominations for Best Costumes in 2011 for _The Extraordinary Adventures of Adèle Blanc-Sec_[2] and in 2014 for _Me, Myself and Mum_.[3]
