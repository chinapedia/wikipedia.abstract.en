NEIL MULHOLLAND (born 13 February 1980) is a Northern Irish international lawn bowler.


Bowls career

He won a silver medal in the men's triples at the 2014 Commonwealth Games.[1]

In 2016 he was part of the fours team with Martin McHugh, Simon Martin and Ian McClure that won a bronze medal at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch.[2]

He won the 2014 Irish National Bowls Championships singles.[3]
