The FLORA GARDEN (; ) is a flower garden in São Lázaro, Macau, China. The garden is located at the foothill of Guia Hill.[1] It is Macau's largest public park.[2]


History

The garden used to be the ground for Flora Palace, a mansion during the Portuguese Macau era.[3]


Attractions

The garden consists of an aviary, small zoo and patio. It also has flowerbeds, small waterfalls and belvedere.


Opening time

The garden opens everyday from 6:00 a.m. to 8:30 p.m.
