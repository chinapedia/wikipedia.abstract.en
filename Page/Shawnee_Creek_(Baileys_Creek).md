SHAWNEE CREEK is a stream in Osage and Gasconade counties of central Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Baileys Creek.

The stream headwaters are at and the confluence with Baileys Creek is at .[2] The stream source is just east of Missouri Route 89 and it flows north to northeast passing under Missouri Route FF and entering the Missouri River valley midway between Chamois on the west and Morrison on the east. The stream turns east and flows along the south edge of the Missouri Valley. It crosses under Missouri Route 100 northeast of Morrison and enters Baileys Creek just after entering Gasconade County.[3]

Shawnee Creek was named after Shawnee Indians in the area.[4]
