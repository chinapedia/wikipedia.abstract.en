IAIN ALEXANDER DOUGLAS BLAIR COCHRANE, 15TH EARL OF DUNDONALD (born 17 February 1961), styled LORD COCHRANE until 1986, is a Scottish peer.

Dundonald is the only son of Iain Cochrane, 14th Earl of Dundonald and his first wife Aphra Farquhar (d. 1972).[1]

He was educated at Wellington College and RAC Cirencester. He succeeded his father in the earldom in 1986, and became chairman of Duneth Securities in the same year.[2]

On 4 July 1987, he married M. Beatrice L. Russo, of Gibraltar, by whom he had two sons and a daughter before their marriage was dissolved in 2011:[3]

-   Archie Iain Thomas Cochrane, Lord Cochrane (b. 1991)
-   Lady Marina Aphra Mariola Cochrane (b. 1992)
-   Hon. James Douglas Richard Cochrane (b. 1995)

He has been a director of Anglo Digital, Ltd., since 2000. Dundonald lives at Lochnell Castle and is the honorary Chilean consul to Scotland.[4]
