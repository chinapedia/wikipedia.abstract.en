GREGORY MICHAEL MENSCHING is an American artist. He began doing graffiti at age 13 transitioned into murals in his later twenties.[1] He is known for street art,[2][3] mural painting[4][5][6][7] and Loudmouf Icon[8] in the United States, Australia, Canada and Switzerland.[9] In 2008, he founded ABV Gallery and Agency in Atlanta, GA which exhibits artists from around the world and works with brands on commercial design projects.[10][11]
