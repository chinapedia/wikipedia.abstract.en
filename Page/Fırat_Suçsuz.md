FIRAT SUÇSUZ (born 27 June 1996) is a Turkish-German footballer who plays as a left back or left winger[1] for Fatih Karagümrük in the Turkish TFF Second League.[2]


Career

In January 2019, Suçsuz agreed the termination of his contract with 3. Liga side Carl Zeiss Jena.[3] On 14 January, he then joined Fatih Karagümrük in the Turkish TFF Second League.[4]
