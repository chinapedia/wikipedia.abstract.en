_BAEOLIDIA RANSONI_ is a species of sea slug, an aeolid nudibranch. It is a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Aeolidiidae.[1]


Distribution

The holotype of this species was collected in Koukura Is, Tuamotu Archipelago, in the central Pacific Ocean. It has been reported from Japan, Indonesia and Australia.[2]


Description

The body of _Baeolidia ransoni_ is translucent white. The cerata are overlaid with brown spots, which are groups of zooxanthellae.[3]


Ecology

_Baeolidia ransoni_ feeds on colonial sea anemones of the genus _Palythoa_.
