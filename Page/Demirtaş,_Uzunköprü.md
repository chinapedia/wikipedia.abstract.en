DEMIRTAŞ is a village in the Edirne Province of Turkey. It is incorporated as a neighborhood () of Uzunköprü, which is located about south of Demirtaş. The Istanbul-Pythio railway runs through the village and owns the Uzunköprü railway station. The _Turkish Grain Board_ (TMO) maintains a small office in Demirtaş for freight railway shipments.
