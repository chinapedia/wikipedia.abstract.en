MOHAMMAD NAQASH is a Pakistani cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Islamabad in the 2016–17 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy on 22 October 2016.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Islamabad in the 2017–18 National T20 Cup on 12 November 2017.[3]
