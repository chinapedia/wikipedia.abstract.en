JAMES BRYCE BENNETT (28 May 1891 – 24 October 1955) was a Scottish amateur footballer who played as an outside forward in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

In November 1914, three months after Britain's entry into the First World War, Bennett enlisted in the Highland Light Infantry.[2] While holding the rank of sergeant, he received a gunshot wound to the back on 15 July 1916, but survived.[3] He later worked as a textile manufacturer and died of stomach cancer in 1955, age 64.[4]
