HOURGLASS is the debut studio album by American DJs Mako.[1][2][3] It was released on 9 December 2016 via Ultra Records.[4] A remix album would be released on April 28, 2017, via Ultra Records.[5]


Background

Several songs from the album were recorded few years ago including "I Wish You Back", "Let Go Of The Wheel" and "Our Story" which was re-created to be added to the album.[6] Seaver said 2015 was the year it became apparent that they were making an album.[7]

Title

The album title was named because Seaver felt that an hourglass represents time in a creative way.[8] The time he spent in working on the album was also a reason of the name.[9]


Singles

"Smoke Filled Room" was released as the first single on 14 August 2015.[10] Its accompanying music video was released

"Way Back Home" was released as the second single on 18 December 2015.[11]

"Into the Sunset" was released as the third single on 22 April 2016.[12]

"Wish You Back" featuring American singer Kwesi was released as the fifth single on 9 December 2016, together with the album.

Promotional singles

The Him's remix of "Wish You Back" was released as the first promotional single on 3 November 2016.

"Let Go of the Wheel" was released as the second promotional single on 19 November 2016.[13] Alex Seaver of Mako commented on Facebook that it took them a whole year to finish the track, as well as it also being one of his favorite songs they have ever made.[14][15]
