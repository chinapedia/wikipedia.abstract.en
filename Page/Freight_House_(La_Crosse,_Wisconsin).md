THE FREIGHT HOUSE is a restaurant in La Crosse, Wisconsin located in the Historic Downtown District close to the river front and Riverside Park. The building was a freight house that was converted in 1978 into a restaurant.[1] It is on the National Register of Historic Places.[2] FreightHouseLAX.JPG Freighthouse_sign.JPG
