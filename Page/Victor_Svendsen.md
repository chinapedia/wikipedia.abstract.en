VICTOR SVENDSEN (born 2 August 1995) is a Danish badminton player.[1] Sevendsen has shown his talent as a badminton player since he was young. Trained at the Vendsyssel's talent department, he claimed the national U–13 elite team title and won the bronze medals in the singles and doubles event.[2] In the national event, he previously played for Ikast, and now was part of the Vendsyssel Elite Badminton.[3][4]
