DENNIS W. PARMENTER (born December 27, 1950) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Parmenter was born in Elkhart, Iowa and attended Iowa State University or Drake University. A Democrat, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1999 to 2001 (62nd district).[1]
