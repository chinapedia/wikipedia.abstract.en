WELDON RIVER is stream in Clark and Decatur counties in southern Iowa and Grundy and Mercer counties in northern Missouri of the United States. It is a tributary of the Thompson River.[1] The confluence is two miles northwest of Trenton.[2]

Weldon River most likely was named after James Weldon, a pioneer settler.[3]
