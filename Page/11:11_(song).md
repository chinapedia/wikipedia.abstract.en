"11:11" is a song recorded by South Korean singer Taeyeon. It was released as a digital single on November 1, 2016 by SM Entertainment. The song's lyrics were penned by Kim Eana while its music was composed by Christian Vinten and Chelcee Grimes. The song was later featured in the Deluxe edition of Taeyeon's first studio album _My Voice_ which was released on April 5, 2017.


Background and release

On October 28, 2016, Taeyeon was announced to be releasing a digital single titled "11:11". The song is described as a pop ballad that features the acoustic guitar's calm melody which suits the late autumn atmosphere.[1][2] Its lyrics are about a girl's reminiscences about making wishes with her former lover when the clock struck 11:11.[3]

"11:11" and its music video were released on November 1. Taeyeon later filmed a live acoustic performance of the song, which was released on November 8.[4]


Reception

"11:11" debuted at number two on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart for the chart issue dated October 30 â€“ November 5, 2016,[5] and sold 238,197 digital units during its first week of release.[6] It was the second best-performing single of November 2016 on the Gaon Digital Chart, ranking behind Twice's "TT".[7] "11:11" charted at number 89 on the Gaon Year-end Digital Chart of 2016,[8] and sold 668,462 digital units in South Korea in 2016.[9] It additionally peaked at number 5 on the _Billboard_ World Digital Songs chart.[10]

_Billboard_ ranked the song at number 7 on their top 20 K-pop songs of 2016.[11] By the end of 2017, "11:11" sold over 1.4 million digital copies in South Korea.[12]
