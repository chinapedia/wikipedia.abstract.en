PRISCILA FARIA DE OLIVEIRA commonly known as PRISCILA (born 10 March 1982) is a Brazilian women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Brazil women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup and 2003 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1]
