PLEASANT RIDGE is an unincorporated community in Harrison County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Pleasant Ridge was established in 1854, and remained in operation until 1876.[2] The name Pleasant Ridge is commendatory.[3]
