SCOTT TURNER (born 15 April 1988) is a professional rugby league footballer who most recently played for Featherstone Rovers in the Kingstone Press Championship. He plays as a or fullback.[1]

Turner has previously played at the Dewsbury Rams and Sheffield Eagles in the Championship.[2]
