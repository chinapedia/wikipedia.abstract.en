Biography

On 5 March 1586, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Sixtus V as Bishop of Ceneda.[2] On 12 March 1586, he was consecrated bishop by Decio Azzolini (seniore), Bishop of Cervia, with Pietro Lunello, Bishop of Gaeta, and Giovanni Battista Santorio, Bishop of Tricarico, serving as co-consecrators.[3] He served as Bishop of Ceneda until his resignation in 1598[4] or 1599.
