ZIRO and HAPOLI are villages in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh[1] Lower Subansiri is the name of the district that contains village Ziro-Hapoli.

Ziro-Hapoli is one of the 60 constituencies of Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh. Name of current MLA (October-2016) of this constituency is Tage Taki, who is an engineer by qualification.
