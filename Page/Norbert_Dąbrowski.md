NORBERT DĄBROWSKI (born 26 August 1988) is a Polish professional boxer in Light heavyweight division.[1]


Boxing career

Dąbrowski professional boxing debut 17 September 2010 in Olsztyn where he defeated by TKO first round Andrejs Suliko.[2]

In 12 December 2016 Dąbrowski he will compete in Canada with Eleider Alvarez for WBC Light heavyweight nr.1 pretender.[3]
