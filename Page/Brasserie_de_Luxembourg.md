BRASSERIE DE LUXEMBOURG is the second largest brewery in Luxembourg,[1] based in Diekirch. They also export to Belgium. They brew beer under the brand name _Diekirch_ and _Mousel_.

The Brasserie de Luxembourg was born in 2000 from the fusion of two breweries: The Brasserie Diekirch, founded in 1871, and the Brasserie Mousel founded in 1825.
