CHICAGO JEWISH NEWS is a newspaper mainly serving the Jewish population in Chicago. The newspaper won two Simon Rockower Awards in 2015, where it is categorized as a newspaper with a circulation of less than 15.000 copies.[1] The newspaper states a readership of over 40,000.[2]
