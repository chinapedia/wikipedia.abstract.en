CLINTON ROETS is a South African international lawn bowler.[1]

Roets won the bronze medal in the fours with Billy Radloff and Brian Dixon and Wayne Perry at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch.[2]

He won a silver medal in the Men's Fours at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Adelaide.[3]
