PETER DELANEY (born 9 September 1941) is a New Zealand rower.

Delaney was born in 1941 in Wellington, New Zealand.[1] [2] He represented New Zealand at the 1964 Summer Olympics. He is listed as New Zealand Olympian athlete number 170 by the New Zealand Olympic Committee.[3]
