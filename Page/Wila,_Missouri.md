WILA is an extinct town in New Madrid County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

A post office called Wila was established in 1894, and remained in operation until 1898.[2] A variant name was "Wiley".[3] The community derives its name from Wiley Webb, the son of a local merchant.[4]
