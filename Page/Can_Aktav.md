CAN DEMIR AKTAV (born 31 August 1994 in Seyhan) is a Turkish football player. He currently plays as a defender for Konyaspor in the Süper Lig.


Professional career

Aktav was a youth footballer for Konya Anadolu Selçukspor, and was a constant starter for them before his transfer to Konyaspor. Can made his professional debut for Konyaspor in a 2-1 Süper Lig win over Çaykur Rizespor on 21 January 2017.[1]
