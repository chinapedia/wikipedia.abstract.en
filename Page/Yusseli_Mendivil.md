YUSSELI MENDIVIL SOTO (born 6 September 1991) is a Mexican racing cyclist, who last rode for American amateur team ISCorp Pro Cycling. Mendivil has also competed for UCI Women's Team in 2017.[1] She rode in the women's road race at the 2016 UCI Road World Championships, finishing in 21st place.[2][3]
