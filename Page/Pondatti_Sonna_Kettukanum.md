_PONDATTI SONNA KETTUKANUM_ () is a 1991 comedy-drama film written and directed by V. Sekhar. The film was produced by K. Parthiban, Vetriyur K. Sethu and A. Rajendran under Jayalakshmi Art Creations.[1] The film stars Bhanupriya, Chandrasekhar in lead roles with Goundamani, Senthil, Charle and Chinni Jayanth portraying supporting roles.[2][3]

The film revolves around three wives who decided to teach lesson to their chauvinist husbands. The film's soundtrack was composed by Chandrabose. The film's cinematography and editing was handled by G. Rajendran and A. P. Manivannan. The film was remade in Telugu as _Pellam Chepithe Vinali_ (1992), in Kannada as _Hendthi Helidare Kelabeku_ (1993) and in Malayalam as "Kottappurathe Koottukudumbam" (1997).
