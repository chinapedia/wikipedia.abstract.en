YOANDRY IRIARTE GALVEZ (born May 5, 1986) is a Cuban fencer. He competed in the men's sabre event at the 2016 Summer Olympics,[1] and was eliminated in the first round by world no. 2 Kim Jung-hwan after a 15-7 loss.[2]

He qualified by placing second (out of 14) in the American continental qualifying tournament, despite being ranked 137th worldwide.[3] He was Cuba's only fencer at the Games, and their first Olympic fencer since 2008.[4]
