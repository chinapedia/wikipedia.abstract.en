SNELL PATEL (born 15 October 1993) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Saurashtra in the 2014–15 Ranji Trophy on 6 February 2015.[2] He made his List A debut for Saurashtra in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 28 February 2017.[3]
