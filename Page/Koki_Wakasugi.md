is a Japanese football player for Tochigi Uva FC.[1]


Playing career

Koki Wakasugi was born in Yamanashi Prefecture. He joined to his local club, Ventforet Kofu, in 2014. On June 3, 2015, he debuted in the J.League Cup (v Sanfrecce Hiroshima).[2][3] In June 2015, he moved to Saurcos Fukui.
