PAUL SHAW is an American designer, calligrapher and historian of design who lives in New York City.[1][2] He has written a book on the history of the design of the New York City Subway system, _Helvetica and the New York Subway System: The True (Maybe) Story_, on the work of William Addison Dwiggins, and for _Print_ magazine.[3][4][5][6][7]
