TYLER is an unincorporated community in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Tyler was established in 1891, and remained in operation until 1954.[2] The community has the name of H. A. Tyler, a businessperon in the lumber industry.[3]
