KARAN RAO (born 01 August 1989) is an Indian, Karnataka-state-class cricketer who played for Karnataka.[1]

He has represented Karnataka State Cricket Association Colts XI, Karnataka State Cricket Association Presidents XI, Karnataka Under-17s, Karnataka Under-22s and Mangalore United.

He is also the grand nephew of legendary Indian Cricketer E. A. S. Prasanna.

He currently attends the University of California, Berkeley School of Information.
