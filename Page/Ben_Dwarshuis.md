BEN DWARSHUIS (born 23 June 1994) is an Australian cricketer.[1] He made his List A debut for New South Wales in the 2016–17 Matador BBQs One-Day Cup on 7 October 2016.[2]

In January 2018, he was named in Australia's Twenty20 International (T20I) squad for the 2017–18 Trans-Tasman Tri-Series, scheduled to start in February 2018.[3] Later the same month, he was bought by the Kings XI Punjab in the 2018 IPL auction.[4]
