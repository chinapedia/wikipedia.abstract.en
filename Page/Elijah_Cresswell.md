ELIJAH J. CRESSWELL (18 July 1889 – 4 November 1931) was a Scottish amateur football forward who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

Cresswell worked as a claims inspector and as of 1915 was married with two children.[2] In 1915, during the second year of the First World War, he enlisted as a guardsman in the Coldstream Guards.[3]
