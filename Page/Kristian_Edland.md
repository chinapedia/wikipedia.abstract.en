KRISTIAN EDLAND (born 12 April 1874) was a Norwegian farmer and politician.

He was born in Mosterøy to farmers David Edland and Serina Døskeland. He was elected representative to the Storting for the periods 1934–1936 and 1937–1945, for the Liberal Party. He served as mayor of Mosterøy from 1925 to 1931.[1]
