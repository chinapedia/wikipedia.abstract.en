ROMOLO DE VALENTIBUS (died 1579) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Conversano (1561–1579).[1][2][3]


Biography

In 1561, Romolo de Valentibus was appointed during the papacy of Pope Pius IV as Bishop of Conversano.[4][5] He served as Bishop of Conversano until his death in 1579.[6][7]
