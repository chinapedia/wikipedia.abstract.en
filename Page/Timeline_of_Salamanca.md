The following is a timeline of the history of the city of Salamanca, Spain.


Prior to 20th century

-   222 BCE - Forces of Carthaginian Hannibal take Salamanca from the Vettones.
-   89 CE - Roman bridge of Salamanca rebuilt (approximate date).[1]
-   500-589 CE - Roman Catholic Diocese of Salamanca established.[2]
-   712 - Muslims in power.[3]
-   1102 - Christian Alfonso VI of León and Castile in power.[4]

-   1253 - University of Salamanca active.[7]
-   1401 - University's established.[8]
-   1415 - University's built.[9]
-   1481 - Printing press in use.[10]
