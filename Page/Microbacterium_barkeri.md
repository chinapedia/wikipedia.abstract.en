_MICROBACTERIUM BARKERI_ is a bacterium from the genus of Microbacterium which has been isolated from domestic sewage and from smear from a cheeses.[1][2][3] _Microbacterium barkeri_ has the ability to degrade polyvinyl alcohol.[4]
