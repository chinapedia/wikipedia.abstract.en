DAVID RINDSKOPF is an American psychologist, currently a Distinguished Professor at City University of New York,[1][2] and a published author.[3] He is a Fellow of the American Statistical Association (ASA), being the President of its New York section, and American Educational Research Association (AERA) and also former editor of the ASA-AERA journal _Journal of Educational and Behavioral Statistics_.[4]

Rindskopf majored in mathematics as an undergraduate at Antioch College, and did his doctorate in psychology at Iowa State University. He joined the CUNY faculty in 1979.[5]
