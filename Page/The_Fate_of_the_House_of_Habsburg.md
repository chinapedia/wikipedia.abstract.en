_THE FATE OF THE HOUSE OF HABSBURG_ (German: _DAS SCHICKSAL DERER VON HABSBURG_) is a 1928 German silent drama film directed by Rolf Raffé and starring Fritz Spira, Alphons Fryland and Leni Riefenstahl.[1] It is based on the Mayerling incident of 1889.

The film's sets were designed by Artur Berger.
