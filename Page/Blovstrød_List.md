The BLOVSTRØD LIST (Danish: _Blovstrød Listen_) is a local political party set in Allerød Municipality.


History

The party was founded in 2001 by Frands Havaleschka, Klaus Kristensen and Matt Axelberg.[1]

The party received 1,092 in 2001, resulting in a single seat in Allerød's municipality council.[2] They received 1,079 votes in 2005, winning them two seats.[3] In 2009 the party received 1,005 votes, equal to 7.6% of all votes in Allerød Municipality. This resulted in a single seat in the municipality council.[4] They received slightly fewer votes in 2013, with 708 votes. This was equal to 4.9% of the votes, allowing them to retain their seat in the municipality council.[5]
