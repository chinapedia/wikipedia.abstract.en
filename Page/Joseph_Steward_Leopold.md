JOSEPH KINSLEY STEWARD LEOPOLD (born 8 June 1989) is a Mauritian footballer who plays for AS Port-Louis 2000 and the national team as a goalkeeper.[1] He was arrested for alleged drug dealing in September 2016,[2] and was subjected to a travel ban.[3]
