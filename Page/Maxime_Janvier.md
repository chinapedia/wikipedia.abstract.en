MAXIME JANVIER (; born 18 October 1996) is a French tennis player.

Janvier has a career high ATP singles ranking of 190 achieved on 27 May 2019. He also has a career high doubles ranking of 305 achieved on 1 July 2019. Janvier has won 1 ATP Challenger singles title at the 2016 Morocco Tennis Tour – Casablanca II.

He was awarded a wildcard to the 2018 French Open.[1]
