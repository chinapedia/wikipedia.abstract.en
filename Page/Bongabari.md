BONGABARI is a village in Purulia II CD Block in Purulia Sadar East subdivision of Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Bongabari had a total population of 4,215 of which 2,082 (49%) were males and 2,133 (51%) were females. Population below 6 years was 500. The total number of literates in Bongabari was 2,559 (68.88% of the population over 6 years).[1]


CD Block HQ

The headquarters of Purulia II CD Block are located at Bongabari.[2]


Transport

State Highway 5 (West Bengal) running from Rupnarayanpur (in Bardhaman district) to Junput (in Purba Medinipur district) passes through Bongabari.[3]
