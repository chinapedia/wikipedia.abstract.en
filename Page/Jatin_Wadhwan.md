JATIN WADHWAN (born 15 September 1994) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his Twenty20 debut for Jammu & Kashmir in the 2015–16 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy on 10 January 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Jammu & Kashmir in the 2017–18 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 8 February 2018.[3]
