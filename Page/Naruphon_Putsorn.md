NARUPON WILD formerly NARUPON PUTSORN (, born July 21, 1988), simply known as NAZZA (), is a Thai professional footballer who plays as a Midfielder for Thai League 1 club Nakhon Ratchasima.


Club career

Born on the small island of Koh Samet in Thailand but also holding a British passport, Nazza was raised all over the world. Since Nazza was a teenager he has travel the world alone in the pursuit to learn, grow and develop as a football player.

He was primarily educated in the Dutch youth football system at AFC Amsterdam and in the English youth system at TNS Football Club. After being offered a full scholarship to the Florida Institute of Technology, he again explored another part of the world playing for the Florida Tech Panthers. After graduating with a bachelors degree in Business Administration, he returned to the Netherlands on trial at Volendam F.C in the Jupiler League before signing a short-term contract with AGOVV Apeldoorn.

His first professional contract was with Bangkok United in the Thai 1st divisions. He help them get promotion to the Thai Premier League in his first year. 3 years later he joined BBCU and also helped them get promoted to the Thai Premier League while also being captain. After 2 successful season Buriram United signed Nazza to a 3 year contract in 2016. He became Thai Premier League Champion in 2017 and was then purchased by his current club Suphanburi FC in 2018.
