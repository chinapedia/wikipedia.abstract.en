KALAIPULI G. SEKARAN is an Indian film distributor, producer, director and actor who has worked in Tamil language films.[1]


Career

G. Sekaran began his career as a film financier and distributor, before becoming a producer and a partner at Kalaipuli Films with S. Thanu and Soori. They debuted as producer with the film _Yaar?_ (1985) by Kannan, with Sekaran portraying a negative role in the film.[2] He subsequently moved on and worked on as a director in films including _Oorai Therunjukitten_ (1988), _Kaaval Poonaigal_ (1989) and _Ulavaali_ (1994), before launching himself as a lead actor in _Jameen Kottai_ (1995). He subsequently also played the lead role in _Kudumba Sangili_ (1999). After that Sekaran went back to distributing films.

In the late 2000s, he became the chairman of the Distributorsâ€™ Council and represented the interest of distributors.[3][4] In 2008, he directed a thriller called _Kattuviriyan_ starring Malavika for which he also composed the music. In 2011, he worked on the production of a film titled _Kallaparundhu_, but the film did not have a theatrical release.[5]
