The GRINDAVÍK WOMEN'S FOOTBALL team, commonly known as GRINDAVÍK or UMFG, is the women's football department of the Ungmennafélag Grindavíkur multi-sport club. It is based in Grindavík, Iceland, and currently plays in the Úrvalsdeild kvenna, the top-tier women's football league in Iceland.[1]
