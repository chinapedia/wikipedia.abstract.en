KWASI OKYERE WRIEDT (born 10 July 1994) is a Ghanaian footballer who plays as a forward for Bayern Munich.[1][2]


Career

Club

Wriedt made his debut for Bayern Munich on 25 October 2017, coming on as a substitute in the 101st minute for Thiago in an away match against RB Leipzig in the second round of the 2017–18 edition of the DFB-Pokal. The match finished 1–1 after extra time, with Bayern advancing after winning 5–4 in a penalty shoot-out.[3]
