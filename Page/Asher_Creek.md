ASHER CREEK is a stream in Greene and Polk counties of southwest Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Little Sac River.

The stream headwaters are just north of Willard at and the confluence with Little Sac is at at an elevation of 899 feet.[2] The stream flows north to northwest passing under Route AC and past Cave Spring and on passing west of Sacville and under Route BB to enter Polk County and its confluence with the Little Sac River southwest of Morrisville.[3] It is located within Lake Wappapello State Park with camping sites available for reservation. [4]

Asher Creek has the name of Samuel Asher, a pioneer citizen.[5]
