METABOLIC TECHNOLOGIES, INC is an American life sciences company that sells dietary supplements and analytical services. Metabolic Technologies is headquartered in Ames, Iowa.[1]

The company has sponsored a number of clinical trials for the nutritional supplement HMB.[2][3][4]
