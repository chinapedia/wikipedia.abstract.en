RODERICK (ROD) MATHESON was a Judge of the Supreme Court of South Australia from 9 August 1979 to 4 August 1998. One of his famous cases was that of James Miller for multiple murders known as the Truro murders.

Matheson has been a significant donor and benefactor to St Mark's College, a residential college affiliated with the Anglican Church of Australia for University students in Adelaide.[1]

Matheson was awarded a Member of the Order of Australia in the 2000 Australia Day Honours for service to the law and the community.[2]
