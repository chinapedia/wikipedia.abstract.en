ASMUND EIRIK SOELSETH (22 August 1886  – ) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Vadsø to Svend Soelseth and Anna Erika Bakke. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1910, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1946 to 1956. He was decorated Commander of the Order of St. Olav in 1953.[1]
