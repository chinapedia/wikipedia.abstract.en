DMITRI YEVGENYEVICH MATVEYEV (; born 9 March 1996) is a Russian football player.

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Volga Ulyanovsk on 2 October 2016 in a game against FC Chelyabinsk.[1]
