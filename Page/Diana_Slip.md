DIANA SLIP was an early 20th-century French manufacturer of lingerie and fetishwear, founded by Léon Vidal, the publisher of Les Éditions Gauloises.[1][2] They employed renowned photographers of the time, including Roger Schall and Brassaï, to photograph their products.[3] Their main competitor was Yva Richard.[4][5]
