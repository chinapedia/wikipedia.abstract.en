The GOTHAM INDEPENDENT FILM AWARD FOR BEST FEATURE is one of the annual Gotham Independent Film Awards and was first awarded in 2004.


Winners and nominees

-   "†" indicates which of the listed films (if any) won the Academy Award for Best Picture
-   "‡" indicates which of the listed films (if any) were nominated for the Academy Award for Best Picture
-   "§" indicates which of the winning films (if any) were not nominated for the Academy Award for Best Picture
