_SPURILLA CROISICENSIS_ is a species of sea slug, an aeolid nudibranch. It is a shell-less marine gastropod mollusc in the family Aeolidiidae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described as common at Le Croisic, France. It was redescribed under the name _Spurilla onubensis_.[2][3]


Description

_Spurilla croisicensis_ is variable in colour, with this variation being correlated with age.
