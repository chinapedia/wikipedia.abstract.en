FLORIAN CHRISTIAN NEUHAUS (;[1][2] born 16 March 1997) is a German footballer who plays as a midfielder for Borussia Mönchengladbach.[3]

Whilst loan at Fortuna Dusseldorf for the 2017/18 season he played a key role in helping them gain promotion to the Bundesliga.[4]


International career

Neuhaus is a youth international for Germany.[5]
