MAHARASHTRA NATIONAL LAW UNIVERSITY NAGPUR (MNLU NAGPUR) is a National Law University located at Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. It is the 19th National Law University established in India by The Maharashtra National Law University Act 2014, passed by Maharashtra Legislature under the Maharashtra Act No VI of 2014.. The proposed permanent campus of MNLU Nagpur is of 60 acres fully firnished campus.


Academics

Admissions to MNLU Nagpur UG and PG courses are done on the basis of Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) score only. MNLU Nagpur is a member of the CLAT Core Committee.[1]


Affiliations

MNLU Nagpur is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) as a state university.[2]
