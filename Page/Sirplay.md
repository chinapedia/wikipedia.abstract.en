SIRPLAY is a sportsbook and gaming software company based in Malta, with offices in Peru, Colombia,[1]Argentina[2] and Nigeria. It supplies bookmakers, lotteries and betting organisations in regulated markets.


History

The company was founded in 2005 in Italy, under the name Teknobet. Established in Malta since 2011 with main development office.[3]

Sirplay's software is tested and certified by GLI Gaming Laboratories International.[4]


Products

Sirplay develops front-end and back-office gaming products, including management and reporting tools. Its Sirplay platform is designed to allow users to bet and play in multiple languages and currencies across several products and platforms, including web, mobile devices and retail terminals.[5][6][7]
