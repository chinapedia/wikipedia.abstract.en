_TELEOPSIS SYKESII_ is the type species of the genus _Teleopsis_ of stalk-eyed flies in the family Diopsidae. This species was described from India [1]


Distribution

This species is distributed in India (Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu) and Myanmar [2]
