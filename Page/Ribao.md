RIBAO is a village in the commune of Mayo-Darlé in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, near the .


Population

In 1967, Ribao contained 1184 inhabitants, mostly Mambila.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 2073 people in the village.[2]
