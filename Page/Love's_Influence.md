_LOVE'S INFLUENCE_ is a 1922 British silent drama film directed by William S. Charlton and Edward Gordon and starring George K. Arthur, Flora le Breton and Simeon Stuart.[1]


Cast

-   George K. Arthur as Johnny O'Hara
-   Flora le Breton as June
-   Simeon Stuart
-   Bertie White
-   George Turner
-   Doris Lloyd
-   William Lugg
-   Marie Gerald
