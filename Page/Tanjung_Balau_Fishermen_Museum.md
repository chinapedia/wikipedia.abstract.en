The TANJUNG BALAU FISHERMEN MUSEUM () is a museum in Tanjung Balau, Kota Tinggi District, Johor, Malaysia about fishermen.[1]


History

The construction of the museum began on 20 August 1990 and completed on 8 July 1992 with a total cost of MYR1.48 million.


Exhibitions

The museum exhibits various artifacts of fishermen equipment.


Opening time

The museum opens everyday from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.[2]
