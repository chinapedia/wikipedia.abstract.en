_THE LURE_ is a 1933 British crime film directed by Arthur Maude and starring Anne Grey, Cyril Raymond and Alec Fraser. It was made at Wembley Studios as a quota quickie.[1]


Cast

-   Anne Grey as Julia Waring
-   Cyril Raymond as Paul Dane
-   Alec Fraser as John Baxter
-   William Hartnell as Billy
-   Philip Clarke as Peter Waring
-   P.G. Clark as Merritt
-   Doris Long as Dorothy
-   Jean Ormond
