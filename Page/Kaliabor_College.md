KALIABOR COLLEGE is the only higher education organization in Kaliabor in the state of Assam, India. It is situated in Kuwaritol, a small town of Kaliabor sub-division in Nagaon district. The campus covers 37 acres and provides indoor and outdoor activities. It has football and cricket fields, basketball, tennis, table tennis and cultural activities. Courses are offered as undergraduate (BA, BSc, BCom) professional UG course (BCA, BBA, BVoC, Tea Garden Management). Kaliabor College is affiliated with Guwahati University and recognized by NAAC. It has 72 academic staff and 30 administrative assistants and hundreds of students graduate every year. Recently NAAC gave "A Grade" for its academic achievements.
