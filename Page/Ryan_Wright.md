RYAN WRIGHT is a professional rugby league footballer who plays for Hunslet in Betfred League 1.

He has played for the Dewsbury Rams (two spells), Doncaster (three spells, including one on loan), Gloucestershire All Golds (loan), and the Keighley Cougars in Kingstone Press League 1, as a .[1]

Wright started his career at Dewsbury Rams in 2013 an spent two season at the club before joining Doncaster partway through the 2015 season.[2] At the end of the 2015 season he rejoined Dewsbury for 2016 but returned to Doncaster on loan during the 2016 season.[3][4] A permanent move to Doncaster was made for 2017 but at the end of the 2017 season he signed for Keighley Cougars for 2018.[5] In July 2018 due to financial problems at Keighley, Wright left the club and joined League 1 rivals, Hunslet.[6]
