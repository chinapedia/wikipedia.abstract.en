_MOTHER AND DAUGHTER SEATED_ (also known as _MOTHER AND DAUGHTER, SEATED_ and _MADRE E HIJA SENTADAS_) is a 1971 outdoor bronze sculpture by Francisco Zúñiga, installed at Balboa Park in San Diego, in the U.S. state of California.[1][2] It is part of the collection of the San Diego Museum of Art.[3]
