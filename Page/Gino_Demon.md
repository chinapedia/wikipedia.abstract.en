GINO DEMON (born 9 May 1997) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays for Rijnsburgse Boys, as a defender.[1][2]


Club career

Demon joined NAC from his Haarlem hometown amateur club EDO in 2015.[3] In June 2017 he moved to Jong FC Utrecht.[4]
