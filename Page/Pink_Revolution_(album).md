_PINK REVOLUTION_ is the third full-length studio album by South Korean girl group Apink. It was released on September 26, 2016 following a hiatus of one year and two months, the group's longest since debut. The title track, "Only One", was used to promote the album along with B-tracks "Boom Pow Love" and "Ding Dong".[1]


Release

The full album was released on September 26, 2016 at midnight.[2] Shortly following the album's release, the title track topped seven Korean music charts.


Singles

The first single from the album, "The Wave" (네가 손짓해주면), was written by group leader Park Cho-rong and was released on April 19, 2016, as a 5th Anniversary song for Apink's fans. _Pink Revolution's_ title track, "Only One", was released on September 26, 2016, and was written and arranged by Black Eyed Pilseung.[3]
