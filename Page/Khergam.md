KHERGAM is a small Town and a Gram Panchayat in Navsari district in the state of Gujarat, India. Khergam is taluka headquarter of newly created Khergam taluka in Navsari district. Khergam is a major junction to reach Navsari, Valsad, Surat, Chikhli, Dharampur, Jujva, Vansada, Limzar, Rumala, Rankuva, Fadvel. It is near to the Arabian Sea.
