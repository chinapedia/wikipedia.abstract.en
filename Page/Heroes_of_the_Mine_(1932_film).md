_HEROES OF THE MINE_ is a 1932 British drama film directed by Widgey R. Newman and starring Moore Marriott, Wally Patch and Terence de Marney. It was made as a quota quickie at Bushey Studios.[1]


Cast

-   Moore Marriott as Gaffer
-   Wally Patch as Bob
-   Terence de Marney as Youngster
-   John Milton as Taffy
-   Eric Adeney as Timberman
-   Agnes Brantford as Mrs. Latham
-   Ian Wilson as Ponyboy
