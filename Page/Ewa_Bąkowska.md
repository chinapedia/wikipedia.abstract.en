Ewa_Bąkowska.JPG EWA BĄKOWSKA (2 August 1962 – 10 April 2010) was a Polish librarian, activist and representative of the Katyn Families.

She died in the 2010 Polish Air Force Tu-154 crash near Smolensk on 10 April 2010. She is buried at the Salwator Cemetery. She was posthumously awarded the Order of Polonia Restituta.
