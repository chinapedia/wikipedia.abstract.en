HARSHITA GAUR (born 12 October 1992 [1]) is an Indian actress who rose to fame with her youth-based show _Sadda Haq_,[2][3] in which she played the lead role of Sanyukta Agrawal.[4][5][6][7]


Early life and career

Harshita started off her career as a model. After her modeling stint, she made her television debut with _Sadda Haq_[8][9] and gained immense popularity amongst youths. She has performed in many commercials, including Dabur Vatika hair oil, Garnier light cream, and Sunsilk. Her latest commercial ad is for Renault Kwid.[10][11][12][13]


Personal life

Harshita was born in New Delhi to a family of doctors. She completed her studies in engineering before stepping into acting. In spite of being an engineer by degree, she was always inclined to become an actor. Harshita is also a trained Kathak dancer and has done stage shows across India. She was an active participant in drama and creative societies during her school and college days. While she was still pursuing her engineering studies, she got an offer from _Sadda Haq_, and she managed to act while finishing her studies.[14][15]
