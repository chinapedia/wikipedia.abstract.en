RUSSELL W. TEIG (born April 11, 1957) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Teig was born in Webster City, Iowa and attended the Iowa State University. A Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1995 to 2003 (17th district)[1]
