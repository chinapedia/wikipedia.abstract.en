TARIQ UL HAKIM is a justice of Dhaka High Court in Bangladesh.[1][2]


Early life and education

Justice Tariqul Ul Hakim son of a diplomat and third generation High Court Justice in Bangladesh, after his father Justice Maksum-ul-Hakim former Ambassador and Justice of Bangladesh High Court and his maternal grandfather Justice Amin Ahmed former Chief Justice of East Pakistan.

Mr Hakim completed his legal education, postgraduate law degree from the UK and was called to Bar of England and Wales from Grays Inn.
