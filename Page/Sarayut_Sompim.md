SARAYUT SOMPIM (Thai ศรายุทธ สมพิมพ์) or simply known as TA-WHAN (), is a Thai professional footballer who plays as a centre back for Thai League 1 club PTT Rayong.


International career

He won the 2015 AFF U-19 Youth Championship with Thailand U19.
