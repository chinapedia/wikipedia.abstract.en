WHITTENBURG CREEK is a stream in Crawford County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

The stream headwaters are at and its confluence with the Meramec River is at .[2] The stream source area liesbetween Steelville and Cherryville. The stream flows generally north passing under Route 19 south of Steelville and under Route 8 just east of Steelville. The stream flows past the historic community of Sankey and on north to join the Meramec at Birds Nest.[3]

Whittenburg Creek has the name of a local family.[4]
