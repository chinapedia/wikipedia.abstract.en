MICHAEL GRACEY is an Australian director and visual effects artist.


Personal

Gracey grew up in Melbourne – in Carlton then Kew – then started working in visual effects and music videos before making his reputation in advertising, gaining a name for with his Christmas commercials in the UK and US.[1]


Career

Gracey made his feature directorial debut in 2017 with _The Greatest Showman_, starring Hugh Jackman as P. T. Barnum.[2][3]

Gracey is also slated to direct an adaptation of manga series _Naruto_,[4] and an adaptation of the novel _Daughter of Smoke and Bone_.[5] Gracey worked for 2 years at animation and visual effects studio Animal Logic from 1994-1996 as an animator and visual effects compositor.
