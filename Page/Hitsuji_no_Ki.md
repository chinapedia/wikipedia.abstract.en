is a Japanese manga series written by Tatsuhiko Yamagami and illustrated by Mikio Igarashi.[1] Published by Kodansha, it was serialized in the _seinen_ manga magazine _Evening_ from 2011 to April 8, 2014,[2] and the chapters were compiled into five volumes.[3] A live action film adaptation directed by Daihachi Yoshida is scheduled for release in Japan in 2018.[4]

Reception

The manga was one of the Jury Selections in the Manga Division of the 16th Japan Media Arts Festival Awards in 2012.[10] It was nominated for the 17th Annual Tezuka Osamu Cultural Prize in 2013.[11] It was also nominated for the 18th Annual Tezuka Osamu Cultural Prize in 2014,[12] and also for the Reader Award at the same awards.[13] It won an Excellence Award in the Manga Division of the 18th Japan Media Arts Festival Awards in 2014.[14]


Live-action adaptation

A live-action movie adaptation of the manga titled _The Scythian Lamb_ will be released in 2018. The film is directed by Daihachi Yoshida and will have its premiere at the 2017 Busan Film Festival.[15]
