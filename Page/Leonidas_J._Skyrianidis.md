LEONIDAS J. SKYRIANIDIS () served as the Headquarters Commissioner of the Scouts of Greece.[1][2][3]

In 1974, Skyrianidis was awarded the 90th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.
