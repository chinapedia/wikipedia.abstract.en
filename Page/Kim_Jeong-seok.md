KIM JEONG-SEOK (born January 3, 1994) is a South Korean football player.[1]


Playing career

Kim Jeong-seok played for Sanfrecce Hiroshima, Roasso Kumamoto, Renofa Yamaguchi FC and FC Kariya from 2013 to 2015.
