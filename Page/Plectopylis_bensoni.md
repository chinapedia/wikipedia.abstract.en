_PLECTOPYLIS BENSONI_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Plectopylidae.

_Plectopylis bensoni_ is the type species of the genus _Plectopylis_.

The specific name _bensoni_ is in honour of malacologist William Henry Benson.
