The PERUVIAN OLYMPIC COMMITTEE or COP ( - COP) is the National Olympic Committee representing Peruvian athletes in the International Olympic Committee (IOC), the Pan American Games and the Central American and Caribbean Games. By the IOC was created 1924 and recognized in 1936.

The Peruvian Olympic Committee is headquartered in Lima, Peru.
