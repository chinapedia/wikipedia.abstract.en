OLE WERNER (born 4 May 1988) is a German football manager, currently managing Holstein Kiel II.

On 17 August 2016, Werner became head coach of Holstein Kiel as interim manager. He managed two matches, first in the Schleswig-Holstein Cup quarter-final against ETSV Weiche which finished as a 2–0 win. He also managed a 3. Liga match against FSV Zwickau, which finished as a 3–0 win.[1] On 27 August, he was replaced by Markus Anfang.
