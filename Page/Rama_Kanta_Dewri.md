RAMA KANTA DEWRI is a Bharatiya Janata Party politician from Assam. He has been elected in Assam Legislative Assembly election in 2016 from Marigaon constituency.[1][2][3][4]

On 18 February 2017, a sex tape featuring Dewri went viral in the Marigaon constituency.[5] Dewri denied involvement in the video and said he would resign if forensic tests showed his involvement.
