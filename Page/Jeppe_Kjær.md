JEPPE KJÆR (born 6 November 1985) is a Danish footballer who currently plays for FC Helsingør.[1]


Career

After leaving Lyngby BK on 25 June 2019[2], he joined FC Helsingør on 9 July 2019 on a 2-year contract.[3]
