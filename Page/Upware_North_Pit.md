UPWARE NORTH PIT is a 1.1 hectare biological Site of Special Scientific Interest north-west of Wicken in Cambridgeshire.[1][2]

This site has several flooded pits with areas of willow and hawthorn. It is one of only two British sites which has water germander, a plant listed in the _British Red Data Books_ of threatened species. Other unusual aquatic plants are great water dock and greater pond sedge.[3]

There is access to the site from the Fen Rivers Way north of Upware.
