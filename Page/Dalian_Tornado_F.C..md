DALIAN TORNADO FOOTBALL CLUB () is an amateur Chinese football club that currently participates in the China Amateur Football League. The team is based in Dalian, Liaoning.


History

Dalian Tornado was established in 2004.[1] Dalian Tornado first entered the final stage of China Amateur Football League in 2011 and won champions. They became a fixture of the Amateur Football League final as well as the Chinese FA Cup. After a stellar 2015 season, The club had to sell their Chinese FA registration and entire first-team to Dalian Boyoung F.C..[2] The following 2017 league campaign saw the club start at the bottom of the Chinese pyramid in the fourth tier with a team assembled from their former reserve squad.[3]
