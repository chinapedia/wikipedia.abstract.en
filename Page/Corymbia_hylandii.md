_CORYMBIA HYLANDII_, commonly known as HYLAND'S BLOODWOOD,[1] is a bloodwood native to Queensland[2]

The tree typically grows to a height of and has persistent grey-brown or red-brown bark on the trunk and larger branches. Adult leaves are disjunct with a narrow lanceolate to lanceolate shape, glossy green in color, long and wide.[3]
