TOMÁŠIKOVSKÝ PRESYPY is a natural monument in Tomášikovo in the Trnava region of Slovakia. The natural monument covers an area of 0.9875 ha on the left bank of the Little Danube. It has a protection level of 4 under the Slovak nature protection system. It was established in 1973 and later amended in 1988.[1]


Description

The area was protected to preserve a sand dune with significant communities of psamophytic and xerophilous species of plants and animals.[2] The sand dune was created under the influence of water and wind activity. The surroundings of the area consist of alluvial sediments of the Danube, namely sand and gravel.
