YOSEF SAMUEL (born July 3, 1997) is an American soccer player who most recently played for Atlanta United 2 in the USL.[1]


Career

Samuel signed to play with United Soccer League side Bethlehem Steel FC in September 2016.[2] He made his professional debut on September 25, 2016, as a 61st-minute substitute during a 0-2 loss against Orlando City B.[3]

Samuel joined new USL team Atlanta United 2 following the 2017 season.

Samuel scored his first professional goal on March 24, 2018 in Atlanta United 2's first ever game, a 3-1 victory over New York Red Bulls II.
