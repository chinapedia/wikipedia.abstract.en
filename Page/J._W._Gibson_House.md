The J. W. GIBSON HOUSE is a historic house located between 6th and Pine Streets in Grandin, Carter County, Missouri.


Description and history

It was built between 1888 and 1909 by the Missouri Lumber and Mining Company. It is a one-story, vernacular frame dwelling with an extra gabled wing in back and lean-to porch in front. The exterior walls are treated with shingle siding.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on October 14, 1980.[2]
