__NOTOC__ BEREZOVE (; ) is a village in Marinka Raion (district) in Donetsk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, at 36 km SW from the centre of Donetsk city.

The War in Donbass, that started in mid-April 2014, has brought along both civilian and military casualties. In a clash between Ukrainian and pro-Russian troops, that took place near the village on 10 November 2014, three Ukrainian servicemen were killed and three others wounded.[1] A 30-year-old female civilian was killed by explosion of a booby trap near the village on 8 September 2015.[2] Two Ukrainian servicemen were killed near the village on 11 March 2017.[3]
