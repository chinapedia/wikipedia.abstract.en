ADRIAN VLĂDUȚ ROTARU (born 1 July 1994)[1] is a Romanian handballer who plays as a pivot for CSM București and the Romania national team.

-   CUPA ROMÂNIEI:

-   World University Handball Championship Top Scorer: 2018
