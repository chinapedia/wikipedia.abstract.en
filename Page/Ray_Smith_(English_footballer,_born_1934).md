HAROLD RAYMOND "RAY" SMITH (born 13 September 1934) is an English former footballer who played as an inside forward. He scored 43 goals from 148 appearances in the Football League playing for Hull City, Peterborough United, Northampton Town and Luton Town.[1][2] Smith also scored 75 goals from 115 appearances in the Midland League for Peterborough before their admission to the Football League in 1960.[3]
