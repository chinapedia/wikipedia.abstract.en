The 2016–17 AZERBAIJAN CUP is the 25th season of the annual cup competition in Azerbaijan. The final is set to be played on 5 May 2017.[1]


First round

The First Round games were drawn on 6 October 2016.[2]


Second round

The two winners of the First Round will progress to the Second Round, which was also drawn on 6 October 2016.[3]


Quarterfinals

The eight winners from the Second Round are drawn into four two-legged ties.[4]
