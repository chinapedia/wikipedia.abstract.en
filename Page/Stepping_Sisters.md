_STEPPING SISTERS_ is a 1932 American pre-Code comedy film directed by Seymour Felix and starring Louise Dresser, Minna Gombell and Jobyna Howland.[1]


Premise

A trio of former burlesque dancers now living in high society try to prevent their past occupation from being discovered.
