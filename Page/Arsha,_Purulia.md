ARSHA is a village, with a police station, in Arsha CD Block in Purulia Sadar West subdivision of Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Arsha had a total population of 5,958 of which 3,057 (51%) were males and 2,901 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 1,047. The total number of literates in Arsha was 2,725 (55.49% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Police station

Arsha police station has jurisdiction over Arsha CD Block and parts of Purulia I CD Block. The area covered is 187.5 km² and the population covered is 154,542.[2][3]


Healthcare

Arsha Public Health Centre functions with 10 beds.[4]
