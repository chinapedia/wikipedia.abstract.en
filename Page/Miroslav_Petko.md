MIROSLAV PETKO (born 24 March 1995) is a Slovak football defender who currently plays for FC Lokomotíva Košice.


Club career

1. FC Tatran Prešov

Petko made his debut for 1. FC Tatran Prešov[1] against FK Bodva Moldava nad Bodvou on 3 October 2014.
