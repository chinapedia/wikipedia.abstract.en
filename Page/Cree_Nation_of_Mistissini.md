Mistissini018.JPG in 2012]] The CREE NATION OF MISTISSINI (Cree: ᒥᔅᑎᓯᓃ) is a Cree First Nation of Canada. It is headquartered at the Cree village of Mistissini and also has a _terre réservée crie_ of the same name, both in Northern Quebec. The nation is in negotiation with the government of Canada to obtain its self-governance.[1] In 2016, it has a registered population of 3,964 members.[2]
