Early life and education

Masters was born in Omaha, Nebraska, and raised in Asheville, North Carolina.[4] She graduated from Appalachian State University in Broadcasting with a minor in Modern Dance.[5] She later attended Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism.


Career

After college, Masters worked as a model and news reporter[6] and landed an uncredited role as a news reporter in the feature film _The Siege_.[7] She appeared in TV commercials and later, the feature films 'The Stepford Wives, It's Complicated.[8]''

Masters continued modeling and acted in several television series, including an episode each in _Law & Order: Criminal Intent_ and _Law & Order: Special Victims Unit_,[9] _Ugly Betty_, _Iron Fist_, _Gossip Girl_, _Nashville_, _Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt_.[10]
