V&A _DIGITAL FUTURES_ is a series of events organized by the Victoria and Albert Museum (V&A) in the area of digital art.

_Digital Futures_ events are organized by Irini Papadimitriou of the V&A,[1] who started the events in 2012,[2] some at the V&A museum itself[3] and some elsewhere around London especially[4] but also elsewhere in the United Kingdom.[5] Some _Digital Futures_ events have been held in conjunction with the annual EVA London conference.[6][7][8] There are some associated publications.[9][10]

The V&A museum has a significant collection of computer art.[11]
