_IL VUOTO_ (Italian for "The void") is a studio album by Italian singer-songwriter Franco Battiato, issued in 2007. Its songs have as common theme the spiritual void.[1]

Battiato performed the songs "Il vuoto" and "I giorni della monotonia" out of competition at the 57th edition of the Sanremo Music Festival.[2]
