GARDABHILLA was a king of Ujjain in the first century CE.


Life

He is said to have kidnapped the sister of Kalakacharya II (a Jain monk). According to Merutunga's _Vicarasreni_, he rose to power in 74 BCE and was defeated by Sakas in 61 BCE.
