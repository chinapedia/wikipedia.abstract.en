'''Hazel Nali ''' (born 4 April 1998) is a Zambian footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for the Zambia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship.[1][2][3] On club level she played for Chibolya Queens in Zambia.[4]
