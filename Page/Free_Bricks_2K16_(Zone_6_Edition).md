_FREE BRICKS 2: ZONE 6 EDITION_ is a collaborative EP by American rappers Gucci Mane and Future. It was released on November 14, 2016. The EP features production by Southside, London on da Track, Metro Boomin and Zaytoven.[1] It is a follow up to their 2011 mixtape _Free Bricks_.
