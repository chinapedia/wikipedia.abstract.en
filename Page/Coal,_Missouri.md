COAL is an unincorporated community in Henry County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Coal was originally called Coale's Store, and under the latter name was laid out in 1859, and named after Stephen Coale, an early settler.[2] Other variant names were "Coale", "Coalesburg", "Colesburg", and "Galbreath".[3]
