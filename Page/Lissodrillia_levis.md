_LISSODRILLIA LEVIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 6 mm and 8 mm.


Distribution

This species occurs in the Gulf of Mexico off Florida.
