ABBY LEE (born 1971/72) is a Republican member of the Idaho Senate representing District 9 since 2014.


Idaho Senate

Committee assignments

-   FINANCE
-   HEATH AND WELFARE
-   JUDICIARY AND RULES

Lee previously served on the Agriculture Affairs Committee from 2014-2016 and the Commerce and Human Resources Committee from 2014-2015.


Elections

[TABLE]

: DISTRICT 9 SENATE - Adams, Payette, and Washington Counties and a portion of Canyon County.


Personal life

Lee and her husband, Brian, have 2 daughters. They reside in Fruitland, Idaho.[1][2]
