OLEKSANDR HOLOVKO (; born 11 March 1995) is a Ukrainian football defender who plays for Hirnyk-Sport.


Career

Holovko is a product of the different youth sportive schools in his native Kiev. He signed a three-year contract with FC Dynamo Kyiv in the Ukrainian Premier League in February 2013,[1] but did not debut for the first-squad team. In October 2016, Holovko signed a contract with the Ukrainian First League FC Desna Chernihiv.[2]


Personal life

Holovko Jr. is the eldest son of the retired Ukrainian international footballer and current manager Oleksandr Holovko. He also has a brother Andriy and sister Arina.
