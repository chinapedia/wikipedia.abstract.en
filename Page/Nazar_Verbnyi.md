NAZAR VERBNYI (; born 26 July 1997) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder who currently plays for club Karpaty Lviv in the Ukrainian Premier League.


Career

Verbnyi is a product of the FC Karpaty Lviv School System. His first trainer was Vasyl Leskiv.[1] He made his debut for FC Karpaty as a main-squad player in a match against FC Chornomorets Odesa on 17 September 2016 in the Ukrainian Premier League.[2]

He also played for Ukrainian under-16 national football team.

His elder brother Volodymyr Verbnyi also is a football player.
