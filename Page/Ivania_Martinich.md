IVANIA MARTINICH SORIANO (born 25 July 1995) is a Chilean tennis player.

Martinich has a career high WTA singles ranking of 692 achieved on 4 November 2013. She also has a career high WTA doubles ranking of 826, achieved on 31 March 2014.

Playing for Chile in Fed Cup, Martinich has a win-loss record of 1–1.
