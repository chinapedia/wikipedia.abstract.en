HENRY BENTON SAYLER (born January 16, 1921) was an American politician in the state of Florida.

Sayler was born in Georgia and came to Florida in 1941. He attended the United States Military Academy where he attained a Bachelor of Science degree in 1943. He also served in World War II in the US Army Air Forces.[1] Sayler served in the Florida State Senate from 1967 to 1971 and 1973 to 1978, as a member of the Republican Party (20th district and 21st district).[2][3]
