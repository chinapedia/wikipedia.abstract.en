HEATHER STEWART (born 28 September 1976)[1] is a British journalist, the joint Political Editor of _The Guardian_, and the former Economics (Business) Editor of _The Observer_.


Early life

She studied Philosophy, Politics and Economics (PPE) at Magdalen College, Oxford from 1995 to 1998. From 1998 to 2000 she completed a BPhil in Philosophy. She joined HM Treasury in 2000 as a researcher.

Journalism

In 2001, she joined _The Guardian_ as a junior reporter, later becoming Business Editor at _The Observer_, then its Economics Editor. In January 2016 she became joint Political Editor of _The Guardian_.[2]
