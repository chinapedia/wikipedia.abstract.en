MILENA NEKVAPILOVÁ (born 09 May 1977) is a retired Czech tennis player.

She has career-high WTA rankings of 214 in singles, achieved on 17 November 1997, and 146 in doubles, set on 22 February 1999. Nekvapilová won four singles titles and 21 doubles titles on the ITF circuit.

She made her WTA main-draw debut in the doubles event of the 1999 Belgian Open partnering Hana Šromová.

Nekvapilová retirement from tennis 2009.
