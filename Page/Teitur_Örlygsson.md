TEITUR ÖRLYGSSON (born 9 January 1967) is an Icelandic former professional basketball player and a coach. During his career he won the Icelandic national championships ten times with Njarðvík.[1] He spent the 1996–1997 season with G.S. Olympia Larissa B.C. of the Greek Basketball League.[2]


Icelandic national team

Between 1986 and 2000, Teitur played 118 games for the Icelandic national basketball team.[3]


Personal life

Teitur is the brother of former Icelandic national team player Sturla Örlygsson and former Althing member Gunnar Örn Örlygsson.[4][5][6] He is also uncle of Örlygur Aron Sturluson and Margrét Kara Sturludóttir who both played for the Icelandic national teams.[7][8]
