ELLIOTT is an unincorporated community in Lee County, South Carolina, United States.[1] The community is located at the junction of U.S. Route 401 and South Carolina Highway 527, south-southeast of Bishopville. Elliott has a post office with ZIP code 29046, which opened on March 20, 1888.[2][3]
