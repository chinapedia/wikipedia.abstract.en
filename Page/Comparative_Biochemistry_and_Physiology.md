COMPARATIVE BIOCHEMISTRY AND PHYSIOLOGY is a series of three journals published by Elsevier with coverage of three aspects of biochemistry and physiology.

These are:

-   _Comparative Biochemistry and Physiology A_, covering Molecular & Integrative Physiology
-   _Comparative Biochemistry and Physiology B_, covering Biochemistry and Molecular Biology
-   _Comparative Biochemistry and Physiology C_, covering Toxicology and Pharmacology
