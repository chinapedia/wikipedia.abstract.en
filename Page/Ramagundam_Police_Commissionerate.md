RAMAGUNDAM POLICE COMMISSIONERATE is a city police force with primary responsibilities in law enforcement and investigation within

Peddapalli, Mancherial, Ramagundam and Godavarikhani areas.[1][2].

Ramagundam Police Commissionerate covers entire Peddapalli and Mancherial districts.

Present Commissioner of police is Mr P.Satyanarayana
