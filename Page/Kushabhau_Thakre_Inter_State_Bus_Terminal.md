The KUSHABHAU THAKRE INTER-STATE BUS TERMINUS also known as _Bhopal ISBT_ or _ISBT_, located in Bhopal is the newest Inter State Bus Terminals inaugurated on 1 November 2010 in central India. It operates bus services between Bhopal and many cities in India.[1]
