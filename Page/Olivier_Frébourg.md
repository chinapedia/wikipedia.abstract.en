OLIVIER FRÉBOURG (14 September 1965, Dieppe) is a French journalist, writer and publisher.


Career

As a journalist, he wrote for _Libération_, _Le Figaro Littéraire_, _Géo_ (as great reporter), _le Figaro Magazine_, __, __ and several foreign newspapers.

A literary advisor at Éditions du Rocher from 1988 to 1990, then literary director at La Table Ronde from 1992 to 2003, he created the Éditions des Équateurs in 2003.

Always fascinated by the sea, himself an _emeritus_ navigator, he was received among Les Écrivains de marine in October 2004, a literary group founded by Jean-François Deniau.
