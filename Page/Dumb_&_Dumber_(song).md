"DUMB & DUMBER" is the first Japanese song by South Korean boy group iKon. The single was released by YGEX on September 9, 2016. The single has three B-side songs, "#WYD" and "Sinosijak (Remix)" with a new song "Love Me".[1]


Commercial performance

The song debuted at number one on Japan's official chart Oricon Singles Chart with 48,749 physical copies sold in the first week,[2] while on Billboard Japan, the song debuted also at number one on _Japan Hot 100_ and _Hot Single Sales_ with total 114,653 on the first week.[3]

In _Oricon_ singles year end chart, the song charted in No.93 selling 60,887.[4]
