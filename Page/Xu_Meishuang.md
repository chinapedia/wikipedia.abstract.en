'''Xu Meishuang ''' (born 28 May 1986) is a Chinese women's international footballer who plays as a goalkeeper. She is a member of the China women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2007 FIFA Women's World Cup. On club level she plays for Changchun Yatai in China.[1]
