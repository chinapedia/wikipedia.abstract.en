DAVID DALE is a former Democratic US politician from Lancaster, New York. Dale represented the 5th District in the Erie County Legislature. He also served as a Sloan village attorney.[1]

Dale served as a legislator from 2002-2003.[2]

In 2012, Dale pled guilty to first-degree felony scheme to defraud and one count of practice of law by an attorney who has been disbarred.[3] His appeal was denied in February 2013.[4]
