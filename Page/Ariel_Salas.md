ARIEL SALAS (born October 9, 1976 in Chile) is a former Chilean footballer who played for clubs of Chile. He played for Chile in the FIFA U-17 World Championship 1993 in Japan and the FIFA World Youth Championship 1995 in Qatar.


Teams

-   Colo-Colo 1993-1996

-   Magallanes 1997-2001

-   Deportes Antofagasta 2002-2003

-   Deportes La Serena 2004

-   Deportes Ovalle 2005


Titles

-   Colo-Colo 1993 (Chilean Primera División Championship), 1996 (Chilean Primera División Championship and Copa Chile)
