Multiple nominations and awards

These films had multiple nominations:

-   9 nominations: _La La Land_ and _Moonlight_
-   8 nominations: _Arrival_ and _Manchester by the Sea_
-   6 nominations: _Hell or High Water_ and _Jackie_
-   4 nominations: _Fences_ and _A Monster Calls_
-   3 nominations: _20th Century Women_, _Loving_, _Nocturnal Animals_, and _Zootopia_
-   2 nominations: _Finding Dory_, _Lion_, _Moana_, and _The Witch_

The following films received multiple awards:

-   7 wins: _La La Land_
-   2 wins: _Jackie_ and _Manchester by the Sea_
