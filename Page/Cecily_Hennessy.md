CECILY JANE HENNESSY, FSA, is programme director, Art and Collecting: Antiquity to Renaissance at Christie's Education in London.[1] She is an authority on paintings of children in Byzantium. She completed her PhD thesis at the Courtauld Institute of Art in 2001. In November 2016 she was elected a fellow of the Society of Antiquaries of London.[2]
