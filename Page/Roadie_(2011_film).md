_ROADIE_ is a 2011 American comedy film directed by Michael Cuesta and written by Gerald Cuesta and Michael Cuesta. The film stars Ron Eldard, Jill Hennessy, Bobby Cannavale, Lois Smith, David Margulies and Catherine Wolf. The film was released on January 6, 2012, by Magnolia Pictures.


Cast

-   Ron Eldard as Jimmy
-   Jill Hennessy as Nikki
-   Bobby Cannavale as Randy Stevens
-   Lois Smith as Mom
-   David Margulies as Don Muller
-   Catherine Wolf as Marilyn Muller
-   Suzette Gunn as Lizette


Release

The film premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival on April 23, 2011.[1] The film was released on January 6, 2012, by Magnolia Pictures.[2]
