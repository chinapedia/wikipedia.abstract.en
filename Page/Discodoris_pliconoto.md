_DISCODORIS PLICONOTO_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, shell-less marine opisthobranch gastropod molluscs in the family Discodorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the island of Sal, Cape Verde. The specimens were found in the intertidal zone or up to 1 m depth.[2]


Description
