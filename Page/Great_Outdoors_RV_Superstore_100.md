The GREAT OUTDOORS RV SUPERSTORE 100[1] is a NASCAR K&N Pro Series East race held annually at Watkins Glen International, on the road course.


History

When the KNPSE returned to The Glen in 2008, Matt Kobyluck won his second race of the season. After a five-year absence, it returned again in 2014.
