_DORIS STICTA_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Dorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from Plymouth, Devon, England. It has been reported from Ireland south to the Mediterranean Sea.[2][3]
