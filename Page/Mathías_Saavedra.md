MATHÍAS SAAVEDRA PERDOMO (born April 30, 1989) is a Uruguayan footballer who plays as a forward for Rampla Juniors.

Saavedra began his career in 2010 with Nacional. Since then, he has played for many different teams in Uruguay, Spain and Argentina.[1]
