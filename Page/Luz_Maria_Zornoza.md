LUZ MARIA ZORNOZA ROCA REY (born 15 October 1994) is a Peruvian female badminton player.[1][2] She plays in the mixed's doubles with Diego Mini. They participated in the 2016 Brazil Open Grand Prix.


Career

In 2016, she won silver medal at the Pan Am Badminton Championships in women's doubles event, and bronze medals in mixed doubles and team events.[3][4][5]
