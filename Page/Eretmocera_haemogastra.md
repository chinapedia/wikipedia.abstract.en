_ERETMOCERA HAEMOGASTRA_ is a moth of the Scythrididae family. It was described by Edward Meyrick in 1936. It is found in the Democratic Republic of the Congo (Equateur).[1]

The wingspan is about 11Â mm. The forewings are deep blue-purple and the hindwings are dark bronzy-grey, sometimes with a faint reddish tinge.[2]

The larvae feed within the flowers of _Coleus_ species.[3]
