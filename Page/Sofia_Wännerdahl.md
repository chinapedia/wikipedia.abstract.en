SOFIA WÄNNERDAHL (born 3 December 1995) is a Swedish football defender who plays for IF Limhamn Bunkeflo in the Elitettan.


Club career

Sofia Wännerdahl played at Borgeby FK in her youth years.[1] In 2014, she joined IF Limhamn Bunkeflo.
