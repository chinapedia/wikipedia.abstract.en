WILLIAM B. ROSSOW is an American atmospheric scientist, who was for many years the head of the International Satellite Cloud Climatology Project. He is currently a Distinguished Professor at City University of New York,[1] and also a published author.[2][3] He is a Fellow of the American Geophysical Union and American Mathematical Society.[4]
