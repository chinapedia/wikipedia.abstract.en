ARMY GOLF CLUB[1][2] is a golf club in Dhaka Cantonment, Bangladesh operated by Bangladesh Army.[3][4] The present president of the club Major General SM Motiur Rahman, afwc,psc [5][6]


History

Golf_Garden.jpg

Army Golf Club was founded on 28 May 2001. It was opened by the first President of the Club and then Chief of Army Staff Lt General M Harun-Ar-Rashid.[7] Bangladesh Golf Academy, first golf academy of Bangladesh was opened on 9 June 2010.[8] The club is affiliated with Kurmitola Golf Club. It has a 9-hole course and over 40 acre in size.[9] Golf garden restaurant is located in the club.[10]
