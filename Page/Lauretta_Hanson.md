LAURETTA HANSON (born 29 October 1994) is an Australian professional racing cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Women's Team .[1]

Prior to joining , Hanson competed for in 2016 and in 2017 and 2018.[2][3] In January 2018, Hanson was selected in the Korda-Mentha Australian cycling team to race the Women's Tour Down Under, the Women's Herald Sun Tour and the Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race.[4][5]
