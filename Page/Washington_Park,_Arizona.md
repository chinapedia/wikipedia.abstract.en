WASHINGTON PARK is a census-designated place (CDP) in Gila County, Arizona, United States. The population was 70 at the 2010 United States Census.[1]


Geography

Tonto Village is located in northern Gila County in the valley of the upper reaches of the East Verde River, just below (south of) the Mogollon Rim. It is north of Payson via Houston Mesa Road and Belluzzi Boulevard. According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of , all of it land.[2]
