The HONG KONG FILM AWARD for _Best Film From Mainland And Taiwan_ is an annual Hong Kong industry award presented for a film considered the best of the year. In order to be eligible for the award films must be in a Chinese languages and have at least one film company legally registered in Mainland China or Taiwan.[1]


History

-   This award replaced Hong Kong Film Award for Best Asian Film.
-   The first award was presented during the 2012 31st Hong Kong Film Awards ceremony for the film You Are the Apple of My Eye directed by Giddens Ko.
