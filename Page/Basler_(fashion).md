BASLER is a German fashion brand founded in Berlin, Germany in 1936.

Fritz Basler GmbH & Co. KG was founded by Fritz Basler and his wife Elisabeth, with manufacturing in Kreuzberg, and management in management relocated to Berlin-Charlottenburg. In 1959, manufacturing moved to Aschaffenburg, Bavaria. In 1994, Luke Basler took over the business, and later expanded production into Erfurt, Thuringia. Online sales accounted for 81% of total sales in 2012. [1]
