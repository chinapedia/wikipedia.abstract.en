PAGE CITY is an unincorporated community in Lafayette County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Page City was platted in 1871, and named after G. R. and Joseph H. Page, the original owners of the town site.[2] A post office called Page City was established in 1872, and remained in operation until 1901.[3]
