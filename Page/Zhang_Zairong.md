ZHANG ZAIRONG (張 載榮, born ) is a Chinese male former weightlifter, who competed in the flyweight class and represented China at international competitions. He won the bronze medal at the 1990 and 1991 World Weightlifting Championships in the 52 kg category. He participated at the 1992 Summer Olympics in the 52 kg event.[1] He set one flyweight snatch world record at the 1991 World Championships (120.5 kg).
