DORKA GRYLLUS (born 26 December 1972) is a Hungarian actress. She is the daughter of Dániel Gryllus, a Hungarian musician, performer and composer, founding member of folk music group Kaláka. She attended drama school in Budapest. Her breakthrough role came in the 2009 film _Soul Kitchen_.[1] She has appeared in more than sixty films since 1995.
