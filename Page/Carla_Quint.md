CORNELIA ANTONIA "CARLA" QUINT (born 22 September 1972) was a Dutch female water polo player. She was a member of the Netherlands women's national water polo team.

She competed with the team at the 2000 Summer Olympics.[1] She was also part of the national team at the 1998 World Aquatics Championships and 2003 World Aquatics Championships.
