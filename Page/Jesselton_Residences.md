Jesselton_Residences_2.jpg The JESSELTON RESIDENCES is a twin towers building comprising shopping malls and condominium.[1]


History

Originally developed by Palikota Sdn Bhd, a subsidiary of Jesselton Waterfront Holdings, the building construction project awarded to a French firm Vinci Grand Projects Sdn Bhd in 2014 to complete it.[2][3]

In early 2017, the building received the certificate of occupancy and will start to open for retail leasing following the building completion.[4]
