PARC LOGÍSTIC is a Barcelona Metro station, in the Zona Franca district of Barcelona. The station is served by line L9.[1][2]

The station is located underneath the intersection of _Avinguda Parc Logístic_ and the _carrer de Número Vint i Cinc_. There are two entrances at the same intersection, which serve a below ground ticket hall. The two long side platforms are at a lower level.[3][4]

The station was opened in 2016, at the time of the extension of line L9 from Zona Universitaria station to Aeroport T1 station.
