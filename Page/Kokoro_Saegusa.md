[1] is a Japanese fashion model. She is represented with Neutral Management.

Saegusa's brother is businessman Yasuaki Miyashita.[2]


Biography

Saegusa was born in Anamizu, Hōsu District, Ishikawa. During middle school, she won sixteen rounds in the table tennis national tournament.[3] While going to Ishikawa Prefectual Wajima High School, Saegusa's table tennis recommendation admission was declined.[4] She later graduated from Aoyama Gakuin Women's Junior College. Saegusa's hobby is golf in which she won the best score of 83 in the regular tee and 78 in the ladies tee.
