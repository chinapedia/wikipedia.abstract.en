HANS-MARTIN LOTZ (born 1 November 1947) is a retired Australian hammer thrower.

He finished fourth at the 1982 Commonwealth Games, won the silver medal at the 1985 Pacific Conference Games[1] finished sixth at the 1985 World Cup[2] and seventh at the 1986 Commonwealth Games. He also competed at the 1983 World Championships without reaching the final.[3]
