THIERRY LODER (born 15 December 1975 in Geneva) is a Swiss-born French former cyclist.[1]


Major results

1995
    1st Tour du Lac Léman

1998
    3rd Overall Tour de l'Avenir

2000
    2nd Overall Tour de l'Ain

2004
    2nd Overall Tour des Pyrénées
