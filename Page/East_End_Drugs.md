EAST END DRUGS is a historic commercial building located at Jefferson City, Cole County, Missouri. It was built between 1892 and 1898, and is a two-story, Romanesque Revival style brick two-part commercial block. It features a corner entrance, as well as a highly detailed cornice and attic level windows. It has two individual storefronts on the first floor, and two apartments upstairs behind a single facade.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2003.[2]
