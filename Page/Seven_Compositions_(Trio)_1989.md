_SEVEN COMPOSITIONS (TRIO) 1989_ is a live album by American composer and saxophonist Anthony Braxton recorded in France in 1989 and released on the hatART label.[1][2][3]


Reception

AllMusic awarded the album 4 stars and the review by Scott Yanow stated "As usual Braxton's improvising is quite advanced and original but is colorful and fiery enough to always hold on to open-eared listener's attention. This is one of literally dozens of stimulating Anthony Braxton sessions currently available".[4] On _All About Jazz_ Troy Collins noted "_Seven Compositions (Trio) 1989_ is a telling document of Braxton's versatility as an innovative composer and congenial collaborator".[5]
