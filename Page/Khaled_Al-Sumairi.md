KHALED AL-SUMAIRI () (born 1 January 1997) is a Saudi Arabian footballer who plays as a Midfielder for Al-Ittihad in the Saudi Professional League.[1]


International career

Khaled Al-Sumairi played his first international game with the Saudi Arabia Under-20 on 28 December 2017 against Senegal (0–2), in the 2017 FIFA U-20 World Cup.


Honours

Al-Ittihad

-   Saudi Crown Prince Cup: 2016–17
-   King Cup: 2018
