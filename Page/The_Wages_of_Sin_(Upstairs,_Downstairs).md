'''The Wages of Sin ''' was the twelfth episode of the second series of the British television series, _Upstairs, Downstairs_. The episode is set in 1910.


Cast

Guest cast

-   Nicola Pagett (Elizabeth Kirbridge)
-   Donald Burton (Julius Karekin)
-   Mairhi Russell (Mademoiselle Jeanette)


Plot

Sarah and Thomas Watkins, who had previously been employed as the valet of Lawrence Kirbridge, fall in love, resulting in Sarah becoming pregnant again. They marry.[1][2][3]
