_I'LL TRY LIVING LIKE THIS_ is the twelfth album by Vaporwave project Death's Dynamic Shroud.wmv, part of the Ghost Diamond collective[1], released on May 15, 2015 to Bandcamp.[2] _I'll Try Living Like This_ features largely Korean track titles in contrast to the Japanese aesthetic/song titles used for most vaporwave albums. It's been in print on multiple physical releases. [3]


Reception

_I'll Try Living Like This_ was listed as the fifteenth best album of 2015 by Fact magazine.[4]
