_CATHEDRAL: THE STORY OF ITS CONSTRUCTION_ is an illustrated book by David Macaulay. Published in 1973 by Houghton Mifflin, it was the author's first book.

_Cathedral_ tells the story of the construction of a great medieval cathedral using pen-and-ink drawings.[1][2]

_Cathedral_ won the 1975 Deutscher Jugendliteraturpreis for children's non-fiction.
