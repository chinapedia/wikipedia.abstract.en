MBELLA-NGOU (also Mbelangou, Mbella-Ngon) is a village in the commune of Djohong in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, near the border with the Central African Republic


Population

In 1967, Mbella-Ngou contained 715 inhabitants, mainly Fula people.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 1664 people in the village.[2]
