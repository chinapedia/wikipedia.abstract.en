CLAIRE DUKE married name CLAIRE TURLEY is an Australian international Lawn Bowls player.[1]

In 2010, she won the silver medal in the triples at the 2010 Commonwealth Games in the Women's triples event.[2] She married fellow Australian bowls international Wayne Turley in 2013.[3]
