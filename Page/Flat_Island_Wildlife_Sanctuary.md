FLAT ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] the island is lying north from Port Blair.


Geography

The island is situated west of Great Andaman near the shores of Yadita village.


Administration

Politically, Flat Island is part of Rangat Tehsil.[2]


Fauna

The island is the location of a wildlife sanctuary, although there is no station on the island. [3]
