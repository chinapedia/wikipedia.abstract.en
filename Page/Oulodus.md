_OULODUS_ is a genus of conodonts in the family Prioniodinidae.

_O. elegans_ is found in the Late Silurian to Early Devonian Keyser Formation, a mapped limestone bedrock unit in Pennsylvania, Maryland, Virginia, and West Virginia.
