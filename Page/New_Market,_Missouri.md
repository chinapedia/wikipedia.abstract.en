NEW MARKET is an unincorporated community in northern Platte County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] The community is located on Missouri Route 371 about 1.5 miles south of the Platte-Buchanan county line. Dearborn is two miles to the northeast across U.S. Route 71 and Bee Creek flows past the west side of the community.[2]


History

New Market was originally named Jacksonville, and under the latter name was platted in 1830.[3] A post office called New Market was established in 1839, and remained in operation until 1959.[4]
