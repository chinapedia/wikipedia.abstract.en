BRIG. GENERAL (RETD) MUHAMMAD ENAMUL HUQ is a Bangladesh Awami League politician and the former State Minister of Power, Energy and Mineral Resources.[1]


Early life

Huq was born on 10 January 1947.[2]


Career

Huq served as a commissioned officer in the Bangladesh Army. He retired as a Brigadier General. He was elected to Parliament from Chapai Nawabganj-1 as a candidate of Bangladesh Awami League in 2008.[3]
