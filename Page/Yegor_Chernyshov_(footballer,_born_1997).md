YEGOR ALEKSANDROVICH CHERNYSHOV (; born 18 April 1997) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut for the main squad of FC Tom Tomsk in the Russian Cup game against FC Sibir Novosibirsk on 21 September 2016.[1]
