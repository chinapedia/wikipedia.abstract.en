JOHN JAMES INGLIS HIGHET was a Scottish amateur football outside right who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1][2]


Personal life

Highet's father was Scottish international footballer Thomas Highet.[3] He served as a second lieutenant in the Machine Gun Corps during the First World War.[4]
