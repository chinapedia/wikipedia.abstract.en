OLIVER HOUSTON is an American emo band from Grand Rapids, Michigan.


History

Oliver Houston formed in 2011 as a side project for vocalist Kyle Luck and drummer Garret Cabello's band The Exploration.[1] The band released their first EP in early 2015, and released it on vinyl through Broken World Media and Too Far Gone Records later the same year.[2][3] The band's debut full-length album titled _Whatever Works_ was self released on January 23, 2017.[4][5]
