_COGNITIVE EMANCIPATION_ is the third EP album by Behold... The Arctopus, released in 2016. This is the first album to feature current drummer Jason Bauers after the departure of previous drummer Weasel Walter, and the first EP since the original release of Nano-Nucleonic Cyborg Summoning in 2005.
