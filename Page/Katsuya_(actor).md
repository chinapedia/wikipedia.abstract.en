Biography

Katsuya was born as the youngest brother.[2] He had learned football when he was in elementary school.[3] When he moved to Tokyo at the age of 22 Katsuya had a part-time job as a construction assistant.[4]

He said that his great-grandfather came from the Netherlands who had come to Japan to sell jewelry during the Meiji period.[5]
