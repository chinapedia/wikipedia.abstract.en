_MONE PORE AJO SEI DIN_ (Bengali: মোনে পোরে অজো শেই দীন; English: I remember that day) is a Bengali language romantic drama film directed by Ajay Singh and Sudipto Ghatak. The family entertainer is a love story with a twist. The title of the movie name is based on its musical drama content. The soundtrack of the film was composed by Indradeep Dasgupta, with one song by Dev Sen. The film stars Joy Kumar Mukherjee, Sayantika Banerjee in the lead roles.[1]
