MERT ÖRNEK (born 12 February 1997 in Aydın, Turkey) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a right winger for Turkish club Giresunspor.

He has represented the Turkish Football Federation at the U19 level, and debuted in a 4-3 loss to the Belgium U19s.[1]
