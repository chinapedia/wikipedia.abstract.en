_YOUR FATHER'S ROOM_ () is a 2004 novel by the French writer Michel Déon. It is set in Paris and Monaco in the 1920s and follows a young boy, Édouard, called Teddy. The book is a fictionalised autobiography based on Déon's childhood.[1]


Publication

The book was published in France on 5 February 2004 through éditions Gallimard.[2] An English translation is set to be published through Gallic Books on 1 June 2017 in the United Kingdom and 13 June 2017 in the United States.[3]


Reception

Yasmina Reza of _Le Figaro_ called the book "one of the most beautiful stories about grief that you can read".[4]
