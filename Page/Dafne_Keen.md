DAFNE KEEN FERNÁNDEZ (born 4 January 2005) is a British and Spanish actress who played the role of Ana "Ani" Cruz Oliver in the television series _The Refugees_, and the mutant Laura Kinney / X-23 in the 2017 film _Logan_.


Life and career

Keen was born in Madrid.[1] She is the daughter of British actor Will Keen, and Galician actress,[2][3] theatre director and writer María Fernández Ache.[4] Her paternal great-grandfather was peer Edward Curzon, 6th Earl Howe.

Keen made her acting debut in 2014 alongside her father Will Keen with the television series _The Refugees_, where she played Ana "Ani" Cruz Oliver.[5]

Keen starred in the 2017 film _Logan_ as Laura, the mutant child of Wolverine.[6]
