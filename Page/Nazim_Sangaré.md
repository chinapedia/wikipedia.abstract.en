NAZIM SANGARÉ (born 30 May 1994) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a right back for Antalyaspor.[1]


International career

Sangaré was born in Germany to a Guinean father and a Turkish mother.[2] He was called up to the Turkish national team on 24 May 2019.[3] He made his debut on 30 May 2019, in a friendly against Greece, as a starter.[4]
