AMRAN EL KRENAWY (, ; born 21 February 1996) is an Arab-Israeli footballer, who plays for Hapoel Be'er Sheva as a Winger.


Career

Hapoel Bnei Lod

In Season 2016–17 loaned El Krenawy to Hapoel Bnei Lod, In 5 August 2016 value of its debut as part of the Toto Cup game against Hapoel Petah Tikva, after the game won 1–3 Bnei Lod. In 15 August, scored his first team, as part of the Toto Cup in victory 1–0 on Beitar Tel Aviv Ramla. In August 19, the value El Krenawy in Bnei Lod first appearance as part of the first class of the Liga Leumit against Hapoel Ramat Gan, Result 3–0 in favor of Ramat Gan.

Hapoel Acre & Bnei Sakhnin

In Season 2017–18 loaned El Krenawy to Hapoel Acre.[1] In 2 August 2017, El Krenawy made his debut in Hapoel Acre in a 1–2 win over Hapoel Haifa in the Toto Cup. In 8 August 2017, El Krenawy scored his first goal in Hapoel Acre in a 2–1 win over Bnei Sakhnin in the Toto Cup. In 20 August 2017, El Krenawy made his debut in Hapoel Acre in a 2–1 loss to Bnei Sakhnin in the Premier League. In 26 August 2017, El Krenawy scored his first goal in Hapoel Acre in a 2–2 draw against Maccabi Netanya in the Premier League.

In the window of the winter transfers season 2017–18, El Krenawy moved on loan until the end of the season for Bnei Sakhnin. In 2 February 2018, El Krenawy made his debut in Bnei Sakhnin in a 0–1 loss to Hapoel Haifa at the Sammy Ofer Stadium.

Ashdod

In Season 2018–19 loaned El Krenawy to Ashdod.[2] In 12 January 2019 El Krenawy made his debut in a 1–0 loss to Maccabi Netanya in the Premier League. By the end of the season, El Krenawy had made eight appearances.
