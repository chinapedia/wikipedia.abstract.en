_LA MODA_ is a 2012 studio album by Italian singer Garbo. For this, the singer's fourteenth album, most of the songs were co-written with Luca Urbani, ex :it:Soerba.[1] Reviewers noted the influence, beyond Garbo's debt to David Bowie, of Virgin Prunes, Depeche Mode and Nine Inch Nails.[2][3]
