FRANK S. MESSERSMITH (born October 5, 1942) was an American politician in the state of Florida.

Messersmith was born in Springfield, Illinois.[1] An attorney, he served in the Florida House of Representatives for the 85th district from 1980 to 1990, as a Republican.[2]
