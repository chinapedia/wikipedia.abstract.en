_WORLD OF WEIRD_ is a British documentary series narrated by Sarah Millican. Vicky Pattison, Joel Dommett, Ayo Akinwolere, Ben Shires, Brent Zillwood and others meet people who undertake what are considered to be unusual activities, and after learning about the activity, then try it for themselves. It is broadcast on Channel 4. The pilot was shown in September 2015[1] and a six episode series began in September 2016.
