GERRIT NAUBER (born 13 April 1992) is a German footballer who plays as a defender for SV Sandhausen.[1]


Career

He moved to MSV Duisburg for the 2017–18 season.[2] He left Duisburg after the 2018–19 season.[3] On 10 July 2019, he signed for SV Sandhausen.[4]
