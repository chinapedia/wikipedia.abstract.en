Zrní-Křídla2011l.jpg ZRNÍ is a Czech band from Kladno.

Their first album, _Voní_, was released after eight years of their existence, in 2009. Second album _Hrdina počítačový hry jde do světa_ was released in 2011 by famous songwriter Radůza on her label _Radůza Records_. In 2012 the band released album _Soundtrack ke konci světa,_[1] that was named for 2012 Vinyla and Apollo award for best album of the year and in four categories in 2012 Anděl Award. The succeed in the category _Discovery of the year_. In September 2014, the band released their latest album _Následuj kojota._ The album was named for best album of the year in Apollo prize and also received three nominations at the 2014 Anděl Awards.

The band prepared for the 2015 Colours of Ostrava special concert in collaboration with Janáček Philharmonic Orchestra and with the Slovak violinist Stano Palúch.[2]
