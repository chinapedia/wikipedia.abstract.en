MECHWARRIOR 5: MERCENARIES is a BattleTech single-player combat simulation game under development by Piranha Games and due for release in September 10, 2019.[1] It is the first single player MechWarior game since 2002. It is slated to have raytracing powered by Nvidia RTX as well as DLSS. [2]


Synopsis

MechWarrior 5: Mercenaries is set in 3015 during the final decade of the Third Succession War, casting the player as a rookie mercenary MechWarrior able to accept contracts from the various factions available in this time period. The campaign is said to go all the way up to 3049, just before the Clan Invasion.
