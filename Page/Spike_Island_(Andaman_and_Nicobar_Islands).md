SPIKE ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] The island lies north from Port Blair.


Geography

The island belongs to the West Baratang Group and lies north of Bluff Island.


Administration

Politically, Spike Island, along neighboring Baratang Islands, is part of Rangat Taluk.[2]


Transportation

you can travel by Dinghy through Port Anson from Mayabuner, on demand.


Demographics

The island was previously inhabited. [3]
