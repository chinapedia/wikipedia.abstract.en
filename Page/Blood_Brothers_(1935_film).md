_BLOOD BROTHERS_ (German: _BLUTSBRÜDER_) is a 1935 German drama film directed by Johann Alexander Hübler-Kahla and starring Brigitte Horney, Carl Esmond and Attila Hörbiger. It is set in Bosnia.[1]
