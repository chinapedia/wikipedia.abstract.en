PUJA BHARDWAJ is an Indian television journalist and producer currently working with NDTV India. She anchors the night bulletins of the channel.


Early life

Bhardwaj was born in Patna, Bihar and completed her initial education from Patna. Later on she moved to Mumbai and started working with NDTV


Career

Bhardwaj started her journalism career at a young age of 14. She joined a local cable network followed by a regional news network. She gained prominence after she joined NDTV.[1] She produces and anchors entertainment bulletins on NDTV. Bhardwaj can be seen on night bulletins of the channel. She is known for her plain style of journalism.
