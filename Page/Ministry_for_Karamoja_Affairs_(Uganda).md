The MINISTRY FOR KARAMOJA AFFAIRS is a cabinet level government ministry of Uganda. The ministry is responsible for the coordination of all government programs in the five districts of the Karamoja sub-region.[1]

John Byabagambi is the minister for Karamoja affairs.[2][3][4]


Location

The headquarters of the ministry are located in the Twin Towers, Sir Apollo Kaggwa Road, in the Central Division of Kampala, Uganda's capital and largest city. The coordinates of the ministry headquarters are 0°18'58.0"N, 32°35'11.0"E (Latitude:0.316111; Longitude:32.586389).[5]


Overview

The ministry is part of the office of the prime minister of Uganda.[6]


Administrative structure

The cabinet minister is assisted by State Minister for Karamoja Affairs Moses Kizige.[7][8] Christine Guwatudde Kintu is the ministry's chief accounting officer.[9]
