SIDDHARTHA NUNI is a filmmaker and cinematographer based out of Tallinn, Estonia..


Early life

Siddhartha graduated in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Madras. He studied cinematography at Mindscreen Film Institute, Chennai run by the Indian cinematographer Rajiv Menon. Siddhartha finished the cinematography course at Mindscreen Film Institute in 2007.[1]


Career

Siddhartha started his career working on travel documentaries for Mahindra Holidays 1. He worked in the on-set VFX crew of Life of Pi (film) in 2010. He moved into film cinematography working on independent feature films in Bangalore, India in 2011. Siddhartha is working on a Tamil film directed by the national award winning filmmaker Balaji Sakthivel titled Ra Ra Rajasekhar.[2]
