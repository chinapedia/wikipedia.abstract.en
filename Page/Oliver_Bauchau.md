OLIVER BAUCHAU is an American aerospace engineer, currently the Igor Sikorsky Distinguished Professor in Rotorcraft at University of Maryland, College Park,[1] and also a published author.[2][3] he is a member of the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics, American Society of Mechanical Engineers and American Helicopter Society.[4]
