is a 1952 Japanese drama film directed by Senkichi Taniguchi. It stars Shirley Yamaguchi and Toshirō Mifune. It was produced by Toho Studios. The film, based on a novel by Jirô Osaragi,[1] is about a Japanese woman, the lover of an American, who falls in love with her servant.


Cast

-   Shirley Yamaguchi as Chiyo (Hana)
-   Toshirô Mifune as Chiyokichi
-   Bob Booth as Takashi Shimura
-   Fuyuki Murakami
-   Noriko Sengoku
