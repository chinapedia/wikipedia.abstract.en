MATTHIEU CHEMIN (born 20 April 1984) is a French footballer who plays as a defender.[1]


Career

In June , Chemin joined Orléans, newly promoted to Ligue 2, after captaining Luçon in the third-tier Championnat National. He signed a one-year contract with an optional second.[2]

After six months with no club,in December 2018 he signed with Les Herbiers VF.[3]
