DOBRICA TEGELTIJA (born 6 October 2000) is a Bosnian-Herzegovinian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for First Vienna.


Club career

Vojvodina

On 21 October 2016 Tegeltija made his debut for Vojvodina, in 3:1 home win against Voždovac.[1]

First Vienna

On July 2019, Tegeltija was presented as new player of Austrian side First Vienna.[2]


International career

Tegeltija is currently a member of Bosnia and Herzegovina U18 national team.
