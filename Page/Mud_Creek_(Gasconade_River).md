MUD CREEK is a stream in Gasconade and Osage counties of central Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Gasconade River.

The stream headwaters are located in Osage County at and the confluence with the Gasconade in Gasconade County is at .[2] The stream source lies north of Hope and it flows to the southeast to enter the flood plain and then turns north for about one mile before entering the main channel of the Gasconade.[3]

Mud Creek was named for the muddy character of its water.[4]
