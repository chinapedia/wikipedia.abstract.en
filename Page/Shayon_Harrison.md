SHAYON ADAM HARRISON (born 13 July 1997) is an English professional footballer who plays as a forward for Melbourne City on loan from Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur.


Club career

Harrison made his professional debut for Tottenham on 25 October 2016, coming on in the 83rd minute for Georges-Kévin Nkoudou in a 2–1 defeat to Liverpool in the EFL Cup.[1]

On 20 January 2017, Harrison joined League Two side Yeovil Town on loan until the end of the season.[2] He made his debut for Yeovil in a 2–2 draw against Blackpool, on 21 January 2017.[3] For the second half of the 2017-18 season Harrison went on loan to Southend United.[4]

In the January 2019 transfer window Harrison out on loan to Australian club Melbourne City of the A-League.[5]
