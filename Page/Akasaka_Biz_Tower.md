The is a skyscraper located in Akasaka, Tokyo, Japan.

The super high-rise is a result of the "Akasaka 5-chome TBS Development Project" into the complex together with the TBS Broadcasting Center, the Akasaka Blitz music venue, and the Akasaka ACT Theater.[1]
