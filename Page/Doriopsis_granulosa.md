_DORIOPSIS GRANULOSA_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Dorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the Sandwich Islands. It has been reported from many places in the Indo-West Pacific.[2] Different colour forms may turn out to be a species complex.[3][4][5]
