The 2016 WOMEN'S OCEANIA HANDBALL CHAMPIONSHIP was held on 5-6 October at Blacktown Leisure Centre, Stanhope Gardens, Sydney, Australia.

Australia and New Zealand played in a two-legged game against each other, the aggregate winner would be the Oceania Champion.[1][2] Australia won the two game series. [3] The next step in the qualification process for the 2017 World Women's Handball Championship will be the Asian Championships in Korea in March 2017.
