PAUL DALY (born 28 March 1972) is a Northern Irish international lawn bowler.


Bowls career

He won a silver medal in the men's triples at the 2014 Commonwealth Games.[1] He also won a bronze medal in the triples and fours at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship.[2]

He won the 2011 Irish National Bowls Championships singles.[3]
