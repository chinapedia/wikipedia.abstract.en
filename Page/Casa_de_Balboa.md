SanDiegoCasaDeBalboa.jpg CASA DE BALBOA is a building in San Diego's Balboa Park, in the U.S. state of California.[1] The building was originally known as the COMMERCE AND INDUSTRIES BUILDING, and later called the CANADIAN BUILDING, the PALACE OF BETTER HOUSING, and the ELECTRIC BUILDING.[2]
