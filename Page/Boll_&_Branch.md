BOLL & BRANCH is a privately held, U.S. based, e-commerce company that sells luxury bedding online.[1] Headquartered in Summit, New Jersey, the company manufactures and sells organic cotton bed linens, blankets and bath towels and are the first maker of such goods to be certified by Fair Trade USA.[2][3]

Boll & Branch launched in January 2014 by a former third grade teacher, Missy Tannen[4] and her husband, a former Wrigley and Kraft Foods exec, Scott Tannen, who self-funded the venture.[5][6][7][8] Most of the company's products are manufactured in Kolkata, India with the cotton being grown by CHETNA Organic in Orissa, India.[9]

The company's 2016 revenue was estimated to be $40 million. Its marketing campaigns promote that "3 US Presidents sleep on Boll & Branch sheets," which includes Presidents George H.W. Bush, Bill Clinton and George W. Bush.[10]

In August 2017, they announced the opening of its first brick & mortar retail location in The Mall at Short Hills in Short Hills, New Jersey[11]


Products

Boll & Branch has gained acclaim for the ethical and sustainable manufacturing of luxury bed sheets.[12][13] In August 2015, the Company launched a line of organic cotton bath towels and introduced a line of crib sheets and blankets for babies in October 2015.[14]

In October 2016, the Company launched a line of flannel bedding made in Guimaraes, Portugal.[15]

In June 2018, Boll & Branch introduced a line of ethically sourced Down pillows.[16]

In October 2018, the Company expanded into the mattress industry. They promote their "luxury mattress" as not containing memory foam, rather using wool, coils and organic cotton.[17]


Investments

Boll & Branch received $7 million Series A funding from Silas capital in April 2016 and raised a $4 million Series B investment in October 2017.[18]
