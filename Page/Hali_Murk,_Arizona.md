HALI MURK is a populated place situated in Pima County, Arizona.[1] The name became official in 1941 through a decision of the Board on Geographic Names. It has also been known by HAAL-MUIHEDAK, HARDIMUI, HARLE MUHETA, HARLEMUHETA, MESQUAL, and MESQUIT. It has an estimated elevation of above sea level.[2]
