PAVLO MYKOLAYOVYCH KIKOT (; born 27 September 1967, Kiev, Ukrainian SSR) is a former Soviet and Ukrainian footballer and football coach.


Career

Since 2016 till January 2017 he worked a coach of FC Sumy.[1]
