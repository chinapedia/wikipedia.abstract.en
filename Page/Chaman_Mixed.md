_CHAMAN MIXED_ () is a passenger train operated daily by Pakistan Railways between Quetta and Chaman.[1] The trip takes approximately 4 hours and 45 minutes to cover a published distance of , traveling along a stretch of the Rohri–Chaman Railway Line.
