_TRITONIOPSIS FRYDIS_ is a species of dendronotid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Tritoniidae.


Description

The maximum recorded body length is 20 mm.[1]


Habitat

Minimum recorded depth is 2 m.[2] Maximum recorded depth is 3 m.[3]
