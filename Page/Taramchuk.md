TARAMCHUK (; ) is a village in Marinka Raion (district) in Donetsk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, at 33.9 km SW from the centre of Donetsk city.

The War in Donbass, that started in mid-April 2014, has brought along both civilian and military casualties.[1]


Demographics

-   Russian 31.47%
