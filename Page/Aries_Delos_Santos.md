ARIES DELOS SANTOS (born 18 December 1989) is a Filipino male badminton player.[1][2]


Career

In 2015, he became the runner-up of Iran Fajr International tournament in men's doubles event.[3] In the Same year he plays for Egret Club in mixed doubles event at the Chicago Open and reached the third place.[4] Now he works as a coach in Egret Badminton Club in Chicago.[5]
