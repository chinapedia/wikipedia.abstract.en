The COPA MÉXICO 1933–34 was the 18th staging of this Mexican football cup competition that existed from 1907.

The competition started on July 1, 1934 and concluded on July 15th 1934 in which Asturias lifted the trophy for fourth time after a 3-0 victory over Necaxa.
