AMBROS SOLLID (born 21 May 1880) was a Norwegian agronomist and politician.

He was born in Heddal to teacher Ambros Torgrimsen Sollid and Gunhild Smedsrud. He was elected representative to the Storting for the period 1937–1945, for the Liberal Party.[1]

Sollid was elected member of the municipal council of Skien from 1928 to 1937, and served as mayor 1935–1937.[2]
