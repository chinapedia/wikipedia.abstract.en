ENGRACIA FERNANDES (born 23 November 1987) is an East Timorese women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Timor-Leste women's national football team.


International career

She was part of the team at the 2016 AFF Women's Championship.[1] She make her official debut in match against Myanmar on 27 July 2016.[2]
