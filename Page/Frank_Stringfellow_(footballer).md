FRANK STRINGFELLOW (March 1888 – 1948) was an English professional football inside forward who played in the Football League for Bournemouth & Boscombe Athletic, Portsmouth and The Wednesday.[1][2][3][4][5]


Personal life

Stringfellow served as a private in the Machine Gun Corps of the British Army during the First World War.[6] He was wounded in September 1918, just under two months before the Armistice.[7]
