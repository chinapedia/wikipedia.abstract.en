LINDELL is an unincorporated community in Maries County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Lindell was established in 1884, and remained in operation until 1919.[2] The community took its name from the Lindell Dry Goods Company, a company with which an early settler had business contacts.[3]
