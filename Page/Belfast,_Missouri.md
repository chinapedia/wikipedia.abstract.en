BELFAST is an unincorporated community in Newton County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Belfast was founded ca. 1880, and named after Belfast, in Ireland, the ancestral home of a share of the first settlers.[2] Until 1887, it was called Iron Switch.[3] A post office called Belfast was established in 1889, and remained in operation until 1901.[4]
