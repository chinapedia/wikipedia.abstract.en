_CEROSTERNA PULCHELLATRIX_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Westwood in 1837. It is known from Thailand and the Philippines.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Cerosterna pulchellatrix pulchellatrix_ (Westwood, 1837)
-   _Cerosterna pulchellatrix argenteomaculata_ Aurivillius 1887
