PERCY ARTHUR GORDON CHAMPION (March 1887 – 1957) was an English professional football outside left who played in the Football League for Fulham.[1]


Personal life

Champion served in the East Lancashire Regiment prior to the First World War and after the outbreak of the war in August 1914, he re-enlisted as a sergeant in the Royal Army Service Corps.[2]
