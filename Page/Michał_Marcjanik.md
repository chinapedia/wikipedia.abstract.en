MICHAŁ MARCJANIK (born 15 December 1994) is a Polish footballer who plays as a defender for Wisła Płock on loan from Empoli.


Club career

Empoli

Loan to Carpi

After joining Empoli in the summer of 2018, he remained on the bench for every Serie A game in the first half of the season. On 31 January 2019, he joined Serie B club Carpi on loan until the end of the season.[1]

Loan to Wisła Płock

On 1 July 2019, Marcjanik was loaned out to Wisła Płock for the 2019/20 season.[2]
