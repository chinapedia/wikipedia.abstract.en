BANGLADESH JUDICIAL SERVICE COMMISSION is a government commission that is responsible for the recruitment and examination of judges in Bangladesh and is located in Dhaka, Bangladesh.[1][2] Hasan Foez Siddique is the present chairman of the commission.[3]


History

The commission was established in 2007. A judge of the Appellate Division is selected to the chairman of the commission.[4] It is managed by a chairman and 10 member governing body.[5] There has been allegations of cheating in the examinations conducted by the commission.[6]
