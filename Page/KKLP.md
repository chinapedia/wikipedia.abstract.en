KKLP (91.1 FM, K-LOVE 91.1) is a class A radio station licensed to the Educational Media Foundation. It is licensed to Perris, California.


History

KKLP began broadcasting on March 28, 2011 as KJZD. Almost four years later, on February 6, 2015, it changed its callsign to KNVE.[1] The station changed its call sign to KKLP on December 4, 2017.
