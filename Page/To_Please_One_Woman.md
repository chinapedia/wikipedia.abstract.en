_TO PLEASE ONE WOMAN_ is a 1920 American silent drama film produced and directed by Lois Weber and starring Claire Windsor. It was distributed by Famous Players-Lasky and Paramount Pictures.[1][2]


Plot

The film is a take on the "vamp" genre by pioneer Lois Weber, just as that genre was waning.
