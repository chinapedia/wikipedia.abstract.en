Playing career

Toshikazu Soya joined to Albirex Niigata Singapore in 2012. In September, he moved to Grulla Morioka and played to 2015. In 2016, he moved to Saurcos Fukui, then he changed to MIO Biwako Shiga for 2017 season.[2]
