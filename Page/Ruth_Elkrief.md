RUTH ELKRIEF (born 1 December 1960) is a French television journalist.


Early life

Ruth Elkrief was born on December 1, 1960, in Morocco.[1] Her great-uncle, Chalom Messas, was a rabbi.[2] She emigrated to France with her family as a teenager.[3]

Elkief graduated from Sciences Po.[4] She earned a postgraduate degree in journalism from the Centre de Formation des Journalistes.[5]


Career

Elkrief was a correspondent for TF1 in Washington, D.C. in 1990.[6] A year later, in 1991, she was appointed as the head of its political programmes.[7] She joined La Chaîne Info in 1994 and BFM TV in 2005.[8][9]

Elkrief became a Knight of the Legion of Honour in 2008.[10]


Personal life

Elkrief is married to Claude Czechowski.[11] They have two daughters.[12]
