MASC () is South Korean boy band formed by J Planet Entertainment in 2016.[1] They debuted as an four-member group with Woosoo, 26, ACE and Heejae on August 19th, 2016 with the mini album _Strange_.[2] In late July 2018, members ACE and Chibin left the group due to assault allegations against the former.[3][4] Soon after, member 26 left the group to focus on a career in film.[5]
