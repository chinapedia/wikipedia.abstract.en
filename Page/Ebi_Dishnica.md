EBI DISHNICA (born 24 February 1997 in Tirana, Albania) is a professional Albanian basketball player. She currently play for KB Tirana in the First League in Albania. She is the youngest player to ever play in the national basketball Albania women's national basketball team number 12. From 30 July to 9 August 2015, she played for U18 European Championship and scored 12.1 ppg and 8.6 rpg. She is 182 cm (6.0 ft) tall.[1][2][3][4][5]
