ALPAY KOÇAKLI (born 19 September 1998) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a right midfielder for Sivas Belediye Spor.


Career

Having already featured in three Turkish Cup matches for Karabükspor, Koçaklı made his senior league debut on 29 May 2015, playing the last four minutes in a 4-3 loss to Kayseri Erciyesspor.[1]


International career

Koçaklı has represented Turkey at under-15, under-16, under-17, under-18 and under-19 level.[2]
