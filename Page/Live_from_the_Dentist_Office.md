_LIVE FROM THE DENTIST OFFICE_ is the second mixtape from Arizona hip hop trio Injury Reserve. It was released on July 22, 2015. The mixtape features guest appearances from Glass Popcorn, Curtis Williams, Chuck Inglish, and Demi Hughes. The mixtape was recorded in the dentist office of the grandfather of producer Parker Corey.[1]
