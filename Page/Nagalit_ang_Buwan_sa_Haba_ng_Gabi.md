_NAGALIT ANG BUWAN SA HABA NG GABI_ () is a 1983 Filipino film, written and directed by Danny Zialcita.


Cast

-   Laurice Guillen as Delza Almeda
-   Gloria Diaz as Stella de Joya
-   Dindo Fernando as Miguel Almeda
-   Eddie Garcia as Dimitri de Joya
-   Janice de Belen as Jenny Almeda
-   Tommy Alvaro as Tony Almeda
-   Suzanne Gonzales
-   Odette Khan
-   Tommy Abuel as Raul


Remake

This movie was remade in 2013 under the title When the Love Is Gone and directed by Andoy Ranay. It stars Cristine Reyes playing Gloria Diaz's character, Gabby Concepcion as Dindo Fernando's character, Alice Dixson playing Laurice Guillen's role, Andi Eigenmann in Janice de Belen's previous role, and Jake Cuenca playing the role Eddie Garcia portrayed.
