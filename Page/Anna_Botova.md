ANNA BOTOVA (born March 25, 1987) is a Russian model, singer and a one-time Miss Globe International 2010 (1st runner-up)[1] and Miss Asia Pacific World 2011 (1st runner-up).[2][3]


Early life and education

Born in Astrakhan, Russia, Botova grew up under the tutelage of her grandmother who enrolled her in a music school. She graduated from the Peoples' Friendship University of Russia with a degree in law.[4]


Career

Botova began a career in music from her early childhood. She became a soloist for a music group known as "Genuch DI7".[5] Botova started touring across Russia performing as a model and singer at various occasions. She obtained a modeling contract in Paris and eventually became the face of a Parisian boutique.[6] She later returned to Russia and worked for the biggest Russian modeling agency. She participated and won prizes in various beauty pageant contests held across Russia.[7]

-   Miss Beauty & Miss Global Beauty Queen International 2009 (1st runner-up)
-   Miss Runet 2010 (1st place)[13]
-   Miss Bikini International 2010 (1st Lady Winter)[14][15]
-   Miss Globe International 2010 (1st runner-up)[16]

-   Miss Asia Pacific World 2011 (1st runner-up)[18]

Personal life

Botova lives in Moscow.[20]
