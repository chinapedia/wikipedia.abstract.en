FRANCISCO MACÍAS VALADÉZ served as the International Commissioner of the Asociación de Scouts de México, Asociación Civil.

In 1971, he was awarded the 69th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
