MONICA MADALINA FLOREA is a female long-distance runner from Romania.

In 2015, she had two 4th place finishes at the 2015 European Athletics U23 Championships - one in the 5,000 meters and one in the 10,000 meters.[1][2]

Florea finished 67th at the 2013 IAAF World Cross Country Championships.[3]

At the 2016 European Athletics Championships she finished 7th in the women's half marathon.[4]

She has also won the national title in the 10,000 meters.[5]
