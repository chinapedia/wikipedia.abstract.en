POLINA MERENKOVA (born 11 November 1995) is a professional Uzbekistani female tennis player.

Merenkova has a career high WTA singles ranking of 774, achieved on 9 June 2014. She also has a career high WTA doubles ranking of 431 achieved on 17 July 2017. Merenkova has won four ITF doubles titles.

She made her WTA Tour main-draw debut at the 2016 Tashkent Open in the doubles event partnering Dayana Yastremska.
