LOPASKINE (; ) is a village in Novoaidar Raion (district) in Luhansk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, on the left bank of the Donets river.


Demographics

Native language as of the Ukrainian Census of 2001:[1]

-   Ukrainian 9.42%
-   Russian 90.58%
