YOON SEUNG-WON (; born 11 February 1995) is a South Korean football midfielder who plays for FC Seoul. Until 2014, He was calle as Yoon Hyun-oh.


Club career

Yoon joined FC Seoul in 2014[1] and made his league debut against Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors on 3 November 2016.
