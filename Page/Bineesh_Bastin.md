BINEESH BASTIN is an actor who has appeared in Malayalam and Tamil films. He rose to fame through the film _Theri_.[1][2]


Personal life

Bineesh Bastin hails from Ernakulam, Kerala. He completed his schooling at Cochin Refineries School, Ambalamugal, Kochi. Bineesh did his college education from St.Alberts College, Ernakulam.


Acting career

Bineesh has an experience of working in the Malayalam film industry for the past 10 years. During his acting career, Bineesh appeared in a number of films. His popular films are _Angel John_, _Pokkiri Raja_, _Annan Thampi_, _Sound Thoma_, _Thappana_, _Dam 999_, _Passenger_, _Koratty Pattanam Railway Gate_, _Daivathinte Swantham Cleetus_, _Double Barrel_, _Theri_ and _Kattumakkan_.
