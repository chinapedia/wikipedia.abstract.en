TEXAS STATE HIGHWAY SPUR 331 (SPUR 331) is a spur located in southeast Lubbock in the northwestern part of the U.S. state of Texas which is 2.903 miles long.[1] It was established in 1957.[2] The route starts at U.S. Route 84 (US 84), travels northwest, has an interchange with Loop 289 and ends at Farm to Market Road 835. The entire route is known as SOUTHEAST DRIVE.


History

SPUR 331 was designated on October 30, 1957 on its current route.
