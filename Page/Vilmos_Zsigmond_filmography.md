Cinematographer Vilmos Zsigmond, also formerly credited as William Zsigmond, was nominated for four Academy Awards for Best Cinematography (_Close Encounters of the Third Kind_, _The Deer Hunter_, _The River_, and _The Black Dahlia_), winning once for his work on _Close Encounters of the Third Kind_.[1] He also won two American Society of Cinematographers Awards, one Primetime Emmy Award, and one British Academy Film Award.

This article includes all of Zsigmond's credits as a cinematographer both in film and television.
