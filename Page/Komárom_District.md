KOMÁROM () is a district in north-western part of Komárom-Esztergom County. _Komárom_ is also the name of the town where the district seat is found. The district is located in the Central Transdanubia Statistical Region.


Geography

Komárom District borders with the Slovakian region of Nitra to the north, Tata District to the east, Oroszlány District and Kisbér District to the south, Győr District _(Győr-Moson-Sopron County)_ to the west. The number of the inhabited places in Komárom District is 9.

Ethnicity

Besides the Hungarian majority, the main minorities are the Roma (approx. 350), German (250) and Slovak (150).
