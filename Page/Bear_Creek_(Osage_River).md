BEAR CREEK is a stream in Miller County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Osage River.

The stream headwaters arise on the north side of a ridge about two miles north of Brumley (at [2]) at an elevation of about .[3] The stream flows northwest to north passing the old community of Blackmer to its confluence with the Osage adjacent to Wolf Creek (at ) at an elevation of .[4][5]

Bear Creek was so named due to frequent sightings of bears near its course.[6]
