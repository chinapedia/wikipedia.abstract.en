RINA CHOUDHURY () is a formerTollywood actress who is now working as a film director and writer.

Her first film was _Geetsangeet_. She directed _Kalpataroo_. She also appeared in the Bengali serial _Erao Sotru_ in Zee Bangla.[1]


Personal life

She is the daughter of famous writer and director Anjan Choudhury and Jaysree Choudhury. Her daughter's name is Ankita Mukherjee.
