ERADU KANASU (English: Two Dreams) is a Kannada film directed by Madan, it stars Vijay Raghavendra and Karunya Ram.[1][2]

Erasu Kanasu Dubbed In Hindi As Ab Insaaf Hoga By Pen Movies.


Cast

-   Vijay Raghavendra
-   Karunya Ram
-   Krishi Tapanda
-   Petrol Prasanna
-   Kuri Pratap


Production

The film began production in September 2015 with Vijay Raghavendra announcing that he would act in a project directed by Madan with the title named after 1974 film of same name.[3] Coincidentally S. K. Bhagawan, director of that film clapped the first shot during the launch at KAnteerava Studios.[4]
