SULTAN AL-BRAKE (Arabic:سلطان البريكي) (born 7 April 1996) is a Qatari footballer. He currently plays for Al-Duhail .[1]


Career

Al-Brake was loaned out to ASPIRE-owned Spanish club Cultural Leonesa in November 2015.[2]
