SON SE-HWAN (born July 29, 1995) is a South Korean football player.[1]


Playing career

Son Se-hwan joined to Tokushima Vortis in 2014. On May 24, he debuted in J.League Cup (v Nagoya Grampus).[2][3] In July 2016, he left the club.[4]
