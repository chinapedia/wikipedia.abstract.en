RALLI BEN-YEHUDA (ראלי בן יהודה; born September 26, 1934) is an Israeli former Olympic gymnast.[1][2]

She was born in Tel Aviv, Israel, and is Jewish.[3][4]


Gymnastics career

She competed for Israel at the 1960 Summer Olympics in Rome, Italy, in gymnastics at the age of 25.[5] In the Women's Individual All-Around she came in 81st, in the Women's Floor Exercise she came in 72nd, in the Women's Horse Vault she came in tied for 96th, in the Women's Uneven Bars she came in tied for 62nd, and in the Women's Balance Beam she came in 86th.[6] When she competed in the Olympics she was 5-1.5 (157 cm) tall and weighed 115 lbs (52 kg).[7]
