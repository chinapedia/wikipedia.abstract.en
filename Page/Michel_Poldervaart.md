MICHEL POLDERVAART (born 28 March 1988) is a Dutch former professional footballer who played as a midfielder.


Career

Poldervaart moved from Feyenoord to SC Heerenveen in September 2008.[1] He spent the 2009–10 season on loan with FC Emmen, and signed a permanent two-year contract with the club in June 2010.[2] He left Emmen at the end of the 2011–12 season, after his time with the club had been marred by injury.[3] He made a total of 56 league appearances for Emmen, as well as two Cup appearances.[4]


Later life

In 2014, he opened a clothing store in Rotterdam with Royston Drenthe.[5]
