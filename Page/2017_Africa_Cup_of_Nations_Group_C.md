GROUP C OF THE 2017 AFRICA CUP OF NATIONS was played from 16 to 24 January 2017 in Gabon. The group consisted of Ivory Coast, Morocco, DR Congo, and Togo.[1]

Notes


Standings

In the quarter-finals:

-   The group winners, DR Congo, advanced to play the runners-up of Group D, Ghana.
-   The group runners-up, Morocco, advanced to play the winners of Group D, Egypt.
