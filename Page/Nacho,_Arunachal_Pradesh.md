NACHO is a village in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1] Upper Subansiri is the name of the district that contains village Nacho.

Nacho is located 65 km north of the district headquarters, Daporijo. It is one of the 60 constituencies of the Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh. The current MLA (September-2016) of this constituency is TANGA BYALING.
