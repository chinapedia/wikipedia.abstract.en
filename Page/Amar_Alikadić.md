AMAR ALIKADIĆ (born 15 June 1998) is a Bosnian-Herzegovinian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina club FK Velež Mostar.[1]


Club career

Velež Mostar

Alikadić is a product of the FK Velež Mostar youth academy. He made his professional debut for Velež on 27 February 2016, against NK Vitez in a Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina match.

On 25 May 2019, Alikadić won the First League of FBiH with Velež after the club beat NK Bosna Visoko 0–2 away and got promoted to the Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina.[2]
