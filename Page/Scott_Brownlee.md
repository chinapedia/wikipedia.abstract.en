SCOTT ALEXANDER BROWNLEE (born 19 March 1969) is a New Zealand rower.

Brownlee was born in 1969 in Christchurch, New Zealand.[1] [2] His father is the rower Mark Brownlee[3] and his cousin is the politician Gerry Brownlee.[4]

He represented New Zealand at the 1992, 1996, and 2000 Summer Olympics in the coxless four.[5] He is listed as New Zealand Olympian athlete number 600 by the New Zealand Olympic Committee.[6] Brownlee works as an executive in the freight management business.[7]
