_LEPTADRILLIA GUIANENSIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 15 mm and 23 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the French Guiana
