BLACK POND TOWNSHIP is an inactive township in Oregon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

Black Pond Township was established in the 1880s, taking its name from a pond of the same name within its borders.[2] The precise location of the former lake is unknown to the GNIS.[3]
