AZHAGIYA THAMIZH MAGAL () is a 2016-2016 Singapore-Tamil soap opera, starring Vikneswary Se, Bharathi, Narain, Leena, Shamini Gunasagar, Mahalakshmi and among others. It aired on MediaCorp Vasantham every Monday to Thursday at 22:00 starting 26 September 2016 and 9 December 2016.[1][2]


Plot

Azhagiya Thamizh Maga is the story of five very different live and unique women. It explores their motivations, their strengths and weaknesses and ultimately how they handle the different problems that arise in their lives. ATM explores the themes of family, work and relationships. These women have individual experiences that not only pose challenges but also serve as learning points to them. These experiences eventually shape the women into who they become by the end.
