BRAD L. HANSEN (born October 30, 1968) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Hansen was born in Onawa, Iowa. He attended University of Iowa and is a businessman. A Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1997 to 2003 (83rd district and briefly the 100th district in 2003).[1]
