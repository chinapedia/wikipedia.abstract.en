ANDREAS WILDHAGEN (born 31 July 1988 in Oslo, Norway) is a Norwegian jazz drummer.[1]


Biography

Wildhagen was raised in Oslo and attended the Norwegian Academy of Music in 2012, and earned his Masters degree in 2014.[2] He has become a much sought after jazz drummer and participates on a series of records with bands like Paal Nilssen-Love Large Unit, Nakama, Lana Trio, Momentum, Jonas Cambien Trio, and Mopti. In 2016 he released his debut solo album _No Right No Left_ on Nakama Records.
