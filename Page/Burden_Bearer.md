_BURDEN BEARER_ is a bluegrass gospel album by Doyle Lawson and Quicksilver, released on July 8, 2016.[1] The album was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Bluegrass Album in December 2016.[2]


Critical reception

John Lawless of _Bluegrass Today_ says the album may be "the definitive album in [the bluegrass gospel] genre" and believes the performances sets it apart more than the songs themselves.[3]
