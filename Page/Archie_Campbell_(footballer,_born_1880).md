ARCHIBALD C. CAMPBELL (1880 – 14 September 1918) was a Scottish professional football outside left who played in the Scottish Football League for Ayr United, Ayr, Abercorn and Clyde.[1]


Personal life

Campbell served as a driver in the Royal Field Artillery during the First World War and died of wounds in northern France on 14 September 1918.[2] He was buried in La Kreule Military Cemetery, Hazebrouck.[3]
