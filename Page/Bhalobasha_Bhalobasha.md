_BHALOBASHA BHALOBASHA_ is a Bengali television soap opera that premiered on May 9, 2016, and airs on Colors Bangla. It is produced by Blue Water Pictures and stars Rupsha Mukhopadhyay and Suvajit Kar in lead roles.
