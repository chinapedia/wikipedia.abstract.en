JULIE SOLOMON is an American businesswoman and publicist.[1][2] She is the founder and President of EmpowerYou, Inc, and is married to actor Johnathon Schaech.[3][4][5]


Early life and education

Solomon was born in McMinnville, Tennessee, and moved to Nashville when she was 7. She completed a Bachelor of Arts in Public Relations Management at the University of Tennessee-Knoxville.[6] She then moved to New York City and worked for Press Here Publicity.[7]


Career

In 2010, Solomon moved back to Nashville and started working as a publicist for Thomas Nelson Publishers, under the leadership of Michael Hyatt.[8] Her work included publicity efforts on the book, _Heaven is for Real_, which has sold over 10 million copies worldwide.[9] After leaving Thomas Nelson Publishers in October 2011, she joined Abingdon Press as a publicity manager.[10] Solomon assisted on various projects and campaigns relating to music, film, television, interior design, and books,[11] including band Def Leppard and singer Lenny Kravitz.[12]

In 2013, Solomon and Beth Hood-Fromm co-founded OMG! Publicity.[13] The company focuses on managing publicity campaigns for authors, bloggers, entrepreneurs, NGO and creative new product launches. In 2016, Solomon created an online program, Pitch It Perfect, that teaches how to move from one-off paid-to-post content to long-term brand collaborations.[14] In 2017, she launched The Influencer Podcast, a podcast that explores the trends of influencer marketing and the careers of social influencers.[15] In 2018, Solomon was named by Influence Co. as one of The Top 100 Most Influential People in Influencer Marketing.[16] In 2018, she created The Influencer Academy, a brand-building online marketing program.[17]


Personal life

Solomon resides in Los Angeles, California, with her husband, actor Johnathon Schaech, whom she married in July 2013 in Ocean City, Maryland.[18] Their son, Camden Quinn, was born in September 2013.[19][20][21][22]
