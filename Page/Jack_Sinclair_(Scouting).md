JACK SINCLAIR served as the President and International Commissioner of Scouts Canada. In 2004, Sinclair was awarded the 302nd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1][2]
