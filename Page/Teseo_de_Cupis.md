TESEO DE CUPIS or TESEO DE CUPPIS (died 1528) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Macerata (1507–1528)[1] and Bishop of Recanati (1507–1516).[2]


Biography

On 20 October 1507, Teseo de Cupis was appointed during the papacy of Pope Julius II as Bishop of Macerata and Bishop of Recanati.[3][4][5][6] On 16 January 1516, he resigned as Bishop of Recanati.[7][8][9] He served as Bishop of Macerata until his death in 1528.[10][11][12]
