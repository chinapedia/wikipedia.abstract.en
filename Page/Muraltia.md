_MURALTIA_ is a genus of plants in the family Polygalaceae.[1][2]

The species _Muraltia scoparia_ and _Muraltia spinosa_ are commonly known as "Tortoise berry" plants ("skilpadbessie"). This is because of the bright edible berries that they produce, which are relished by the tortoise species of the fynbos.[3]
