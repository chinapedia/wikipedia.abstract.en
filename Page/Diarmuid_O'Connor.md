DIARMUID O'CONNOR (born 10 January 1995)[1] is an Irish sportsperson. He has played Gaelic football for both his club Ballintubber and the Mayo senior inter-county teams.[2]

He was awarded All Stars Young Footballer of the Year in 2015 and 2016.[3][4][5]
