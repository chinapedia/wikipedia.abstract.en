ARNT FERDINAND MOSS (born 20 February 1880) was a Norwegian accountant and politician.

Moss was born in Sør-Odal to farmer Anton Jacob Fredriksen Moss and Kari Pedersdatter Lilleseth. He was appointed municipal accountant in Drammen from 1921. He was elected representative to the Storting for the periods 1934–1936 and 1937–1945, for the Labour Party.[1]
