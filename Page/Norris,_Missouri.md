NORRIS is an unincorporated community in Henry County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

The community took its name from a nearby creek of the same name.[2] An early variant name was "Norris Forks".[3] A post office called Norris Fork was established in 1852, the name was changed to Norris in 1882, and the post office closed in 1902.[4]
