LEONEL NAZARENO is an Ecuadorian footballer player who plays as a goalkeeper for his club L.D.U. Quito.[1] [2]


Early life

Leonel at age 16 almost ruminated on a career in boxing and basketball, but chose to play football instead.[3] As second-tallest player in his team, he said his tall stature was an advantage, especially in sports.[4]


Career

Due to starting goalkeeper Daniel Viteri not being able to play, Leonel Nazareno made his senior team debut in a game where the opposition was S.D. Aucas.[5]


Honours
