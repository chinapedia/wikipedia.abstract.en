YOANN SALMIER (born 21 November 1992) is a French international footballer who plays for Troyes as a centre back.[1]


Club career

After having spent the 2018/19 season at Troyes AC on loan from Strasbourg, Troyes bought him free and signed him on 3-year deal.[2]


International career

Salmier made his debut for the French Guiana national football team in a 2–1 2017 Caribbean Cup qualification loss to the Dominican Republic on 7 June 2016.[3]
