PLATTIN CREEK is a stream in northeastern St. Francois and Jefferson counties in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Mississippi River.

The headwaters arise in St. Francois County at and the confluence with the Mississippi is at at an elevation of 390 feet.[2]

Plattin Creek most likely derives its name from nearby La Platte Mine.[3]
