NAI HARVEST was a British emo band from Sheffield, United Kingdom.


History

Nai Harvest began in 2011.[1] The band released their debut full-length album in 2013 titled _Whatever_ on Dog Knights Productions and Pinky Swear Records.[2] The band does not view _Whatever_ as an album, rather a collection of early songs.[3] In 2015, Nai Harvest released their second full-length album titled _Hairball_ on Topshelf Records.[4][5][6][7]

The band has also released numerous EPs and splits, such as _Feeling Better_,[8] a split with Bonjour, Invalids, and Reno Dakota,[9] a split with Playlounge,[10] an EP titled _Hold Open My Head_[11] and A/B side singles titled _Just Like You/Jelly_.[12]

In July 2016, Nai Harvest announced they were breaking up.[13]


Band members

-   Ben Thompson (vocals, guitar)
-   Lew Currie (drums)
