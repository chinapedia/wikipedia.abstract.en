_SAARA AKAASH_[1] is a television show broadcast on Star Plus in 2003-2004, starring Sai Deodhar,[2] Shakti Anand,[3] Sonal Sehgal,[4] Kiran Kumar,[5] Parmeet Sethi,[6] Manav Gohil,[7] Manish Goel,[8] Anuj Saxena[9] and others. The fictional show depicted personal and professional lives of Indian Air Force officers and was produced by Miditech.[10][11]


Cast

-   Kiran Kumar[12] as Air Commodore AOC Suraj Singh
-   Sai Deodhar[13] as Flight Lieutenant Monica Singh, Air Force cargo pilot, Suraj's daughter and Vikram's wife
-   Parmeet Sethi[14] as Squadron Leader Srinivas Rao
-   Shakti Anand[15] as Flight Lieutenant Vikram Kocchar
-   Kanwaljit Singh[16] as Flight Lieutenant Ajay Kocchar, Vikram's Father, Monica's Father-in-law
-   Anuj Saxena[17] as Flight Lieutenant Karan Singh Rathore
-   Manish Goel[18] as Flight Lieutenant Saurav Singh, Suraj's son and Monica's brother
-   Manav Gohil[19] as Flight Lieutenant Jatin Gohil
-   Sonal Sehgal[20] as cactus/pakistani agent
