MAHMOUD HAMDY ATTIA (; born 1 June 1995), also known as EL-WINSH, is an Egyptian footballer who plays as a centre back for Zamalek.[1]

In May 2018 he was named in Egypt’s preliminary squad for the 2018 World Cup in Russia.[2]
