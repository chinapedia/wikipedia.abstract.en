_AMPHICOELINA_ is a genus of air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Camaenidae.[1]


Distribution

Distribution of the genus _Amphicoelina_ includes Hunan and Hubei provinces in southern China.[2]


Species

Species within the genus _Amphicoelina_ include:

1.  _Amphicoelina biconcava_ (Heude, 1882) - type species of the genus _Amphicoelina_[3]
2.  _Amphicoelina diplomphala_ (Möllendorff, 1885)[4]
3.  _Amphicoelina omphalospira_ (Möllendorff, 1897)[5]
4.  _Amphicoelina subobvoluta_ (Ancey, 1882)[6]
