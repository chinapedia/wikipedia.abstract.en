HASSAN AL-ALI () served as General Secretary and International Commissioner of the Kuwait Boy Scouts Association.

In 1981, he was awarded the 145th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
