NATIONAL ACADEMY FOR PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT (NAPD) is a Government of Bangladesh Ministry of Planning not-for-profit training and research institution that specializes in post-graduate education in related to public management and development. The Academy started in 3 February in 1985.[1] It was made a statutory organization in 2014.[2][3]
