_NOIR_ is the second studio album by the South Korean boy group B.A.P. It was released on November 7, 2016, under the label of TS Entertainment. "Skydive" was used as the lead single.


Track listing


Promotion

Promotions for the album started on November 11, 2016, at Mnet's _M Countdown_ and continued onto KBS2's _Music Bank_, MBC's _Show! Music Core_, SBS's _Inkigayo_, SBS MTV's _The Show_, and MBC Music's _Show Champion_.


Other news

Bang Yong-guk, the leader of B.A.P, took a hiatus from musical activities and did not participate in the promotions of this album due to health reasons.[1]
