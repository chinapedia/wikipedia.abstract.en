TOLONA is an unincorporated community in Lewis County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Tolona was founded in 1872, and named by postal officials.[2] A post office called Tolona was established in 1872, and remained in operation until 1938.[3] An old variant name was "Blue Grass".[4]
