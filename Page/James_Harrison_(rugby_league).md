JAMES HARRISON is a rugby league footballer who plays as a second row or prop for Featherstone Rovers in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Harrison has previously played for the Batley Bulldogs and spent time on loan at Oxford.[2]

He is the son of former England international and Super League coach Karl Harrison.
