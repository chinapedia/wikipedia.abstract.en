SAJID KHAN (born 3 September 1993) is a Pakistani cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Peshawar in the 2016–17 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy on 22 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut on 20 January 2017 for Peshawar in the 2016–17 Regional One Day Cup.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Peshawar in the 2018–19 National T20 Cup on 11 December 2018.[4]
