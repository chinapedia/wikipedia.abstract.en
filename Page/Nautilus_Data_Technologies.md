NAUTILUS DATA TECHNOLOGIES, based in Pleasanton, California, is an American company developing floating data centers. It was founded by U.S. Navy veteran Arnold Magcale.[1] , the CEO is James L. Connaughton.[2] The floating data center concept, also called a data barge, is being developed at the former Mare Island Naval Shipyard in association with elements of the U.S. Navy including SPAWAR.[3] The company plans to operate a barge in the Stockton Deep Water Channel, circulating water onboard to cool equipment and returning it to the source hotter.[4]
