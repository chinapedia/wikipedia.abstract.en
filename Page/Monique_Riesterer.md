MONIQUE RIESTERER-LUDWIGS (born 6 December 1971 in Rheinfelden) was a German female weightlifter, competing in the +75 kg category and representing Germany at international competitions.

She participated at the 2000 Summer Olympics in the +75 kg event.[1] She competed at world championships, most recently at the 2002 World Weightlifting Championships.[2]
