_MEN OF THE NIGHT_ is a 1926 silent crime film directed by Albert S. Rogell and starring Herbert Rawlinson. It was produced and distributed by independent company Sterling Pictures.[1]


Cast

-   Herbert Rawlinson - J. Rupert Dodds
-   Gareth Hughes - Dick Foster
-   Wanda Hawley - Trixie Moran
-   Lucy Beaumont - Mrs. Abbott
-   Jay Hunt - Thomas Bogen
-   Mathilde Brundage - Lady Broderick


Preservation status

-   A copy is preserved in the Library of Congress collection.[2][3]
