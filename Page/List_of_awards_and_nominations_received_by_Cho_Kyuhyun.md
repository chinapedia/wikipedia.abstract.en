This is a list of awards and nominations received by South Korean singer CHO KYU-HYUN (born February 3, 1988)[1] is a South Korean singer and actor. He is a member of the South Korean boy band Super Junior, its sub-groups, Super Junior-M and Super Junior-K.R.Y., and a former member of the South Korean ballad group S.M. The Ballad. He is one of the first four Korean artists to appear on Chinese postage stamps.[2]
