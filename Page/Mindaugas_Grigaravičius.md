MINDAUGAS GRIGARAVIČIUS (born 15 July 1992) is a Lithuania National team footballer who plays as a winger for FK Trakai.

In February 2019 became a member of FK Riteriai.[1]


International career

Mindaugas Grigariavičius first call-up to the senior Lithuania squad came in August 2016 for the World Cup qualifiers against Slovenia. He made his national team debut coming as substitute on 70th minute against Slovenia in the World Cup qualifier on 4 September 2016.[2]
