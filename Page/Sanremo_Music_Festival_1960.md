The SANREMO MUSIC FESTIVAL 1960 was the tenth annual Sanremo Music Festival, held at the Sanremo Casino in Sanremo, province of Imperia between 28 and 30 January 1960.[1]

The show was presented by Paolo Ferrari and Enza Sampò. Ezio Radaelli served as artistic director.[2]

According to the rules of this edition every song was performed in a double performance by a couple of singers or groups.[3] The winners of the Festival were Tony Dallara and Renato Rascel with the song "Romantica".[4]
