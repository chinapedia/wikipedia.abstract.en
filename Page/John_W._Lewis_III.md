JOHN W. LEWIS III (born August 5, 1949) is a former American politician in the state of Florida.

Lewis was born in Georgia and came to Florida with his family in 1950.[1] He served in the Florida House of Representatives for the 18th district from 1974 to 1986, as a Democrat.[2]
