_ACROCHAETIUM_ is a genus of marine red alga.

Names brought to synonymy

_Acrochaetium elegans_ (K.M.Drew) Papenfuss 1945[1] is a synonym of _Colaconema elegans_ (K.M.Drew) I.-K.Hwang & H.-S.Kim.
