_AMERICA'S ORIGINAL SIN: RACISM, WHITE PRIVILEGE, AND THE BRIDGE TO A NEW AMERICA_ is a 2015 book by Jim Wallis.[1][2][3]


Overview

A look into the United States' issues with race and a call to action in overcoming the racism ingrained in American society.
