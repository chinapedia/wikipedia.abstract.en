LICHT-OASE (Light Oasis) was a cult led by Arno Wollensak and Julie Ravell with around 40 members. Reports of sexual abuse of minors within the cult lead to a police investigation.[1] In 2007 Wollensak failed to appear in court and fled to Uruguay with Ravell in 2008 using forged Surinamese documents.[2] Wollensak was arrested by police in Uruguay in 2015.[3][4] He was found murdered in Uruguay August 28, 2016.[5][6]
