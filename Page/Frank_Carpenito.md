FRANK CARPENITO is the Chief Executive Officer[1] and President of Dancing Deer Baking Company.[2] He has been with the company since 2010, where he had previously been COO.[3] Carpenito's experience has spanned consumer goods companies and entrepreneurship ventures.[4] He is also a turnaround specialist.[5] In April 2013, Carpenito told _The Wall Street Journal_ that he had changed his company's incorporation status to that of a benefit corporation, in order to protect the company from possible shareholder lawsuits against the company's social contributions.[6]
