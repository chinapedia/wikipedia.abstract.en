COTTON HILL is a historic mansion on a former plantation in Limestone County, Alabama, U.S. The house was built in the middle of a 1,000-acre plantation by William Parham for Luke Matthews in the 1830s.[1] It was designed in the Federal architectural style.[2] The house was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2014.[3][4]
