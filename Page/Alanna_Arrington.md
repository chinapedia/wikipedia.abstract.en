ALANNA ARRINGTON (born November 18, 1998) is an American model from Cedar Rapids, Iowa.[1] She has been on the cover of Maxim and Elle U.K..[2][3] She is a model for Victoria’s Secret.[4]


Early Life

Born to Anthony and Amy Arrington, Arrington has one sister named Amyah.[5] Arrington is biracial. She attended Washington High School in Cedar Rapids and was in their varsity basketball team.[6]


Career

Arrington was scouted by the talent agents Jeff and Mary Clarke of Mother Model Management, in St Louis, Missouri.[7] She subsequently signed to Next Model Management. She first worked in Los Angeles before moving to New York City to do high fashion modeling.[8]

Arrington debuted during the 2016 F/W ready-to-wear collections, opening the Altuzarra show.[9][10] She walked over 20 shows that season, including Chanel, Tommy Hilfiger, Louis Vuitton, Etro, Céline and Diane Von Furstenberg.[11] Since then she has walked the runway for Missoni, Max Mara, Mulberry, Tom Ford, Alberta Ferretti, Stella McCartney, Balmain, Roland Mouret, and Yeezy.[12][13][14]

Arrington has appeared in editorials for magazines such as Vogue,[15] British Vogue, Vogue España, Vogue Russia, Teen Vogue,[16] Harper’s Bazaar,[17] Elle,[18] Interview,[19] Allure,[20] i-D,[21] and Wonderland Magazine.[22] Arrington has appeared in advertisements for Polo Ralph Lauren,[23], Gap Inc., Urban Outfitters, DKNY, Mango, Stella McCartney,[24] H&M,[25] Levi’s, Express, and Aldo.[26]

She walked the 2016-2018 Victoria's Secret Fashion Shows.[27][28]

She is ranked as a "Money" girl on models.com[29]

In 2017, she was chosen as one of the "11 Models You Need to Know" by Vogue Arabia.[30]
