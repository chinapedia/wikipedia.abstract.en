MALAVANITIPPA is a village in the West Godavari district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.[1] It is located in Kalla mandal of Narasapuram revenue division.[2]


Government and politics

Malavanitippa gram panchayat is the local self-government of the village.[3] Malavanitippa in Kala mandal is represented by Undi assembly constituency of Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly.[4]
