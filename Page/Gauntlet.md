Fictional characters

    -   _Gauntlet Dark Legacy_, a 2000 arcade game
    -   _Gauntlet: Seven Sorrows_, a 2005 video game
    -   _Gauntlet_ (2014 video game), developed by Arrowhead Game studios
    -   _Gauntlet_ (Nintendo DS), an unreleased remake of the first game
-   _Gauntlet_ (Donald R. Lebeau video game), a 1984 shoot 'em up game for the Atari 8-bit family
-   _Gauntlet_ (Micro Power video game), a 1984 _Defender_ clone
-   _The Gauntlet_ (module), an adventure module for _Dungeons & Dragons_
