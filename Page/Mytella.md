_MYTELLA_ is a genus of saltwater clams, marine bivalve molluscs in the family Mytilidae, the mussels.[1]


Species

-   _Mytella charruana_ (d'Orbigny, 1842)
-   _Mytella guyanensis_ J.B.P.A. Lamarck, 1819[2]
-   _Mytella maracaibensis_ Beauperthuy, 1967
-   _Mytella speciosa_ (Reeve, 1857)
-   _Mytella tumbezensis_ (Pilsbry & Olsson, 1935)
