[1] is a Japanese former gravure idol and actress. She was represented with Suns Entertainment.

Alisa's skill is dancing to classical ballet.


Biography

After graduating from Aoyama Gakuin University Faculty of Letters English and American Literature Department, she once worked as an office lady for IT Kigyō in March 2011.[2] After working for six months,[3] she was later scouted by Suns Entertainment president Yoshiharu Noda and made her debut as a gravure idol[4] in 2012.[5] She announced her photobook _Alisa_[6] and the DVD _Milky Glamour_[7] later in the year.

In the 26 March 2015 she said in her blog that she began to thought deeply about between her career and herself and felt that she wanted to learn English better, later she announced that she will be on hiatus but in fact she retired from the entertainment industry.
