This is a list of the Annelida recorded from Ireland.

In Ireland the number of species is -

Class Polychaeta 404 species Class Clitellata Subclass Oligochaeta 179 species Subclass Hirudinea 32 species [1]

Taxonomy Here the leeches and oligochaetes are placed together in the Class Clitellata. The marine Polychaeta are ranked as a Class.These groups (and the Annelida) may be monophyletic, paraphyletic or polyphyletic. (Rouse and Pleijel, 2001).
