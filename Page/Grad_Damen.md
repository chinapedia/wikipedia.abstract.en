GRAD DAMEN (born 14 August 1997) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays for NAC Breda, as a midfielder.[1]


Club career

Damen joined NAC's youth set-up in 2006 from Sint Anna Boys and made his professional debut in October 2015 against Sparta.[2]


Personal life

His grandfather Grad "Gerrie" Damen also played professionally for NAC and his cousin Grad is a well-known Dutch singer.[3]
