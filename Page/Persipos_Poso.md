PERSATUAN SEPAK BOLA POSO, commonly known as PERSIPOS POSO, or PERSIPOS, is an Indonesian football club based in Poso Regency, Central Sulawesi. Club played in Liga Indonesia Second Division.[1]

In September 2014, Persipos junior team won football branch at the 7th Games of Central Sulawesi Province, after beating Persipal Palu junior team in the final with a score of 4-1.[2]
