LIST OF LEICESTER TIGERS PLAYERS SELECTED FOR INTERNATIONAL RUGBY is a list of people who have represented their national rugby union team whilst playing club rugby for Leicester Tigers.

Note that the "position" column lists the position at which the player played in his first international whilst a member of Leicester Tigers, not necessarily the position for which he is best known, where brackets () are used this indicates an appearance as a replacement. Ben Youngs for instance made his debut as a replacement wing but is best known as a scrum half. Also note the "date first cap obtained" column lists the date of their first cap obtained whilst at Leicester Tigers not their international debut. JP Pietersen for instance won many international caps for South Africa before joining Leicester.
