_SOUNDS FROM THE HEART OF GOTHENBURG_ is the second live album and video by Swedish melodic death metal band In Flames.[1] Filmed on 8 November 2014 in front of a sold-out venue of 10,000 fans, right inside the Scandinavium in the band's hometown Gothenburg, Sweden.[2][3] The Concert was directed by Patric Ullaeus. It is the last release to feature long time drummer Daniel Svensson who left the band in 2015.


Background

Commenting on the release guitarist Björn Gelotte said "This DVD is for everyone who following our career; we have songs from the early years, the middle years and the new era".[4]


Reception

Adam Rees of Classic Rock magazine gave the album a positive review. Calling it "another accomplished landmark for this enduring band", before rating it 3 and a half stars.[5]
