_PHYLLIDIA SCOTTJOHNSONI_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusk in the family Phyllidiidae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the Hawaiian Islands.[2]


Description

This nudibranch has a partly translucent, white dorsum with large round black spots. There are small, scattered, tubercles all over the back.[3][4]


Diet

This species feeds on a sponge.
