MOHAMMED ASSIRI (, born 18 October 1992) is a Saudi Arabian football player who currently plays for Al-Thoqbah as a goalkeeper .[1]


Club Career

Al-Qadisiyah

In 2013, Assiri joined Al-Qadisiyah. He played his debut against Al-Ahli in 15 October 2016, which they lost 2-0 [2]. In 21 October 2016, He played his second match in the league against Al-Batin F.C., which the drew 1-1. His last match of the 2016-17 was against Al-Faisaly, which Al-Qadisiyah lost 3-2. In 11 August 2017, Assiri won his first match ever against Al-Raed 2-1 [3].
