JAKES CORNER is a census-designated place (CDP) in Gila County, Arizona, United States. The population was 76 at the 2010 census.[1]


Geography

The CDP is located in northwestern Gila County, in the valley of Hardt Creek, a tributary of Tonto Creek. Arizona State Route 188 passes through the community, leading northwest to State Route 87 and south to Theodore Roosevelt Lake. Payson is north via Routes 188 and 87. According to the United States Census Bureau, the Jakes Corner CDP has a total area of , all of it land.[2]
