SRI SRAVANTHI MOVIES[1][2][3][4] is an Indian film production company established by Sravanthi Ravi Kishore. , located in Hyderabad. Sri Sravanthi Movies is one of India's largest film production companies with over 30 years[5] of contribution to Telugu cinema. The first film of this production house was Ladies Tailor with Rajendra Prasad in 1986. Ram Pothineni, Rashi Khanna starrer Shivam (2015 Telugu film)[6][7] is a landmark film for Sri Sravanthi Movies banner, which celebrated its 30th anniversary[8] in the year 2015.
