HUMBERTO J. CORTINA (born August 7, 1941) was an American politician in the state of Florida.

Cortina was born in Cuba and came to the United States in 1960. He is a veteran of the Bay of Pigs Invasion.[1] He served in the Florida House of Representatives from 1982 to 1984 (113th district).[2]
