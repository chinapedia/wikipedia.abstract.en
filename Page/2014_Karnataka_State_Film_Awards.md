The KARNATAKA STATE FILM AWARDS 2014, presented by Government of Karnataka, to felicitate the best of Kannada Cinema released in the year 2014.[1]

Jury

A committee headed by K. Shivarudraiah was appointed to evaluate the awards. He said that the committee watched 73 films watched in 45 days before shortlisting 12 for a "re-watch ... in the second round".[2]
