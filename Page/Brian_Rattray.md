BRIAN RATTRAY (born 1950) is a former Scottish international lawn and indoor bowls player.[1]

Rattray won a silver medal in the triples and a bronze medal in the fours at the 1984 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Aberdeen.[2]

He was National champion in 1982.[3] [4]
