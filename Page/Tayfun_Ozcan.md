TAYFUN OZCAN is a Dutch/Turkish kickboxer. Tayfun Ozcan is born and raised in Tilburg, Netherlands.

Tayfun has faced notable names on the sport like Andy Souwer, Fabio Pinca, Harut Grigorian,[1] Mohammed Jaraya, William Diender,[2] and a victory over the former Glory lightweight champion, Davit Kiria.[3]

As of 1 November 2018, he is ranked the #8 lightweight in the world by Combat Press.[4]


Titles and accomplishments

-   2019 Arena Fight Kickboxing Champion (-72.000 kg)
-   2016 Enfusion 16-Man 70 kg World MAX Tournament Champion[5]
-   2015 Enfusion 72.5 kg World Champion[6]
-   2015 Enfusion 2015 70 kg Tournament Champion[7]
