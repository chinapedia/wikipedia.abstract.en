TOUHFAT MOUHTARE is a Comorian writer.

Born in Comoros, Mouhtare has lived in various countries in Africa, and studied in France,[1][2] where she received a diploma in foreign languages from the Sorbonne.[3] She is the second published Comorian woman prose writer, after Coralie Frei,[4] and has written poetry as well.[5] _Ames suspendues_, a collection of novellas, was published in 2011.[6] She has also published articles on various subjects.[7]
