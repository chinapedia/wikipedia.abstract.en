VAITELE SOI (born 5 August 1987, Samoa) is a retired Samoan professional boxer.

Soi has fought a few notable boxers in his career including Mohamed Azzaoui, Shane Chapman,[1] Brad Pitt[2] and Anthony McCracken.

The biggest fight in Soi career was against Anthony McCracken for the WBA Pan African, WBO Asia Pacific and WBC – OPBF Cruiserweight title.[3] Despite losing the fight by unanimous decision, Soi received a WBO Asia Pacific ranking peaking at 5th.[4]


Professional boxing titles

-   World Boxing Foundation
    -   WBF Asia Pacific cruiserweight title (186¾ _Ibs_)
-   Samoa
    -   Samoa National cruiserweight title (194 _Ibs_)
-   Tournament
    -   2011 New Zealand cruiserweight Tournament winner (196¾ _Ibs_)
    -   Super 8 II runner up (196¾ _Ibs_)
