BEAR CREEK is a stream in Henry County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Deepwater Creek.

The stream headwaters arise in the southwest corner of Henry County about 2.5 miles north of Appleton City (at [2]) at an elevation of about adjacent to Missouri Route KK. Bear Creek most likely was named for the bears in the area.[5]
