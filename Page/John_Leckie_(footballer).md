JOHN SINCLAIR LECKIE was a Scottish amateur football centre forward who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

Leckie worked as a commercial traveller.[2] In December 1916, over two years since the beginning of the First World War, Leckie enlisted in the Argyll and Sutherland Highlanders and rose to the rank of sergeant.[3]
