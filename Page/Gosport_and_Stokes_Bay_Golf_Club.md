GOSPORT AND STOKES BAY GOLF CLUB is a golf club in Gosport, Hampshire, England. The club was established in 1885 as the _' United Services Club_'.[1] The 9-hole, 5957-yard course lies just to the west of Portsmouth Harbour, mile east of Stokes Bay, near Gilkicker Point, overlooking the Solent.[2][3] It is described as a water course, with "natural hazards".[4]
