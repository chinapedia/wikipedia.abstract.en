TAMEEM AL-MUHAZA (Arabic:تميم المهيزع) (born 21 July 1996) is a Qatari footballer. He currently plays for Al-Gharafa.[1]


Career

In 2014, he was playing for Atlético Madrid's youth teams.[2] He was loaned out to ASPIRE-owned Spanish club Cultural Leonesa in November 2015.[3]
