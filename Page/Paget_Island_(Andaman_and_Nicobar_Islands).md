PAGET ISLAND is an island of the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the North and Middle Andaman administrative district, part of the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.[1] the island is lying north from Port Blair.


Geography

The island is in the Temple Sound area of North Andaman shores. It is 76 m high to the tops of the trees. Point Island lies to its southwest.[2]


Administration

Politically, Paget Island, along neighboring Temple Sound Islands, is part of Diglipur Tehsil.[3]
