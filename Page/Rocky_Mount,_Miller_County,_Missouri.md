ROCKY MOUNT is an unincorporated community in Miller County, Missouri United States.[1]


History

Rocky Mount was founded in the 1840s, and named for drainage divide near the original town site.[2] A post office called Rocky Mount was established in 1839, and remained in operation until 1919.[3]
