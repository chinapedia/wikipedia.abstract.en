MISSOURI CITY SAVINGS BANK BUILDING AND MEETING HALL, also known as the NOWLIN STORE BUILDING, is a historic commercial building located at Missouri City, Clay County, Missouri. The two connected three bay sections were built about 1858. It is a two-story, brick building with store spaces in the first story and private space in the second story. It is Missouri City's oldest and largest remaining commercial structure.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2010.[2]
