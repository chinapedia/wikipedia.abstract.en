JIN JIANXIAO (, 1910 – 1936) was the penname of one Manchurian poet.[1][2] __TOC__


Biography

Jin Jianxiao was born on December in 1910 at Fengtian city. His name is JIN CHENGZAI (金承載), courtesy name PEIZHI (培之), pseudonym MENGCHEN (夢塵), penname JIANXIAO (劍嘯), JIANSHUO (健碩), BALAI (巴來).[3]
