BLUE MILLS is an unincorporated community in Jackson County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

The community was named for a watermill on the Blue River.[2] A post office called Blue Mill was established in 1835, and remained in operation until 1895.[3] A variant name was Twyman.[4]
