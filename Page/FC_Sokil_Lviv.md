FC SOKIL LVIV was a football club based in Lviv, Lvivska Oblast, Ukraine.


History

The club was founded in 1960s and disappeared sometime after 1981. Following the fall of the Soviet Union, the club was revived as SOKIL-LORTA[1] (LORTA is the Lviv defence company).
