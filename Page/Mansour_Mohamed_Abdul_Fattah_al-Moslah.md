Career

Mansour Mohamed Abdul Fattah al-Muslah is a powerful Qatari citizen who plays a prominent role in Qatar's business world. He received his bachelor's degree in social science from Qatar University.[1] He is currently preparing for his MBA.[2]

In his early career al-Moslah worked in various posts for Qatar's Ministries of Interior and Defense before being employed later in his career as the Non-Executive Director of Solidarity Group Holding BSC.[3][4]

Currently, al-Muslah is the chairman at Al Andalus Private Schools, the chairman of the Board of Trustees at the Mayfair Islamic Center - London, he sits on the board of directors at both Qatar Islamic Bank (QIB) and Al Jazeera Finance Company.[5][6][7] He has played the role of non-independent non-executive director of QIB since 1996, and is continued this role as recent as his 2014 appointment by election at the bank's general assembly meeting. In the past, he was a board member at Tadhamon International Islamic Bank.[8]
