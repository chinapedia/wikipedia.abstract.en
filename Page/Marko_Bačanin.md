MARKO BAČANIN (, born 9 July 1998 in Smederevo) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Javor Ivanjica.[1]


Club career

On 4 November 2016 Bačanin made his debut for Vojvodina, in 2:0 home win against Radnik Surdulica.[2]
