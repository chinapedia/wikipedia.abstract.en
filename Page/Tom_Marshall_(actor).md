'''Tom Marshall ''' is a British actor playing in the National Theatre, West End theatre, Royal Court Theatre, and Menier Chocolate Factory.[1][2] In July 2016 he was nominated for an Offie Award for _It is easy to be dead_.[3]

In 1970, he played Bryan in _There's a Girl in My Soup_. In 1974, Marshall appeared as William in an episode of _Upstairs, Downstairs_ entitled "I Dies from Love". In 1978, he played Mike in _Killer's Moon_. In 1988, he played Browns umpire in _Eight Men Out_.[4]
