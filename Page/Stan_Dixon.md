EDWARD STANLEY DIXON (26 May 1894 – 13 August 1979) was an English professional football wing half and inside right who played in the Football League for Hull City, Newcastle United and Blackburn Rovers.[1][2]


Personal life

In April 1915, with the First World War underway, Dixon enlisted in the Tyne Electrical Engineers.[3] He had risen to the rank of acting corporal by July 1917 and spent the war on anti-aircraft duty in Britain.[4] After his retirement from football, Dixon managed a cinema in Hull.[5]
