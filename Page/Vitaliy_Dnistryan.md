VITALIY DNISTRYAN (born March 31, 1990) is a Ukrainian footballer.


Playing career

Dnistryan began his career in 2008 with FC Enerhetyk Burshtyn in the Ukrainian First League.[1] The following season he signed with FC Skala Stryi in the Ukrainian Second League.[2] He later had stints with MFC Mykolaiv, FC Krymteplytsia Molodizhne, FC Stal Kamianske, and NK Veres Rivne.[3][4]

In 2016, he went overseas to play with FC Ukraine United in the Canadian Soccer League.[5][6] In his debut season he appeared in 19 matches and recorded 3 goals.[7] The following season he played with FC Vorkuta.[8][9]
