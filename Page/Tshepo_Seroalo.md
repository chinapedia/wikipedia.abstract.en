TSHEPO SEROALO (born 22 June 1997) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for North West in the 2016–17 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 17 November 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for North West in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 20 November 2016.[3]
