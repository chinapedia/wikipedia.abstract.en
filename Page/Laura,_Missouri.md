LAURA is an unincorporated community in Lewis County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Laura was laid out in 1893.[2] A post office called Laura was established in 1892, and remained in operation until 1902.[3] The identity of Laura's namesake has been lost to history.[4]
