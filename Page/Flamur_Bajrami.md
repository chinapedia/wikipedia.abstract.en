FLAMUR BAJRAMI (born 10 February 1997) is a Kosovo Albanian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Kosovan club Llapi.


Club career

Early career

In April 2014, Bajrami played in a Pristina Under-17 Regional League Tournament and was the best scorer in Group B with two goals.[1] In June 2015, he was at trial with the reputable Hajduk Split B, but didn't make the cut.[2]

Vëllaznimi

On 28 December 2015, Bajrami joined First Football League of Kosovo side Vëllaznimi, on a season-long contract.[3]

Tirana

On 26 August 2016, Bajrami joined Albanian Superliga side Tirana.[4] On 23 October 2016, he made his debut in a 2–1 away defeat against Luftëtari Gjirokastër after coming on as a substitute at 65th minute in place of Gjergji Muzaka.[5]

Return to Vëllaznimi

On 18 January 2018, Bajrami joined Football Superleague of Kosovo side Vëllaznimi.[6]

Llapi

On 5 January 2019, Bajrami joined Football Superleague of Kosovo side Llapi, on a two and a half year contract.[7]
