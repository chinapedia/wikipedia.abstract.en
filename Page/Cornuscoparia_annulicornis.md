_CORNUSCOPARIA ANNULICORNIS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Heller in 1897, originally under the genus _Jonthophana_. It is known from Papua New Guinea.[1]


Varietas

-   _Cornuscoparia annulicornis var. hirticornis_ (Heller, 1910)
-   _Cornuscoparia annulicornis var. wollastoni_ Gahan, 1915
