_CLEORA LEUCOPHAEA_ is a species of moth in the family Geometridae. It is found in East Asia (Russia, Taiwan, Japan, South Korea).


Subspecies

-   _Cleora leucophaea leucophaea_
-   _Cleora leucophaea taiwanensis_ Sato, 2002
