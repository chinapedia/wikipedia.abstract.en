CARPISA is an Italian manufacturer and retailer of luggage, handbags, wallets and accessories, with over 600 outlets worldwide.

Carpisa was founded in 2001, is owned by Kuvera SpA, and has a "franchise network of more than 600 stores in Italy and abroad".[1] Carpisa has been called a "fast fashion brand".[2]

The headquarters building of over 10,000 square meters, the CasaCarpisa, is located in the Nola Interporto, in Nola, Italy.[3]
