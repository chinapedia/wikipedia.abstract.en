Kaminuma is represented by her agency, Kaminuma Jimusho. She dropped out from Empire Girls High School Owada School (now Osaka International Waseda High School).

Kaminuma is one of the leading broadcast personalities in the Kansai region. She has often appeared in Kansai local programmes, and has garnered high ratings from around the late 1990s.

She is married with two children. Kaminuma's husband is Shinpei Kaminuma, a Kansai Telecasting Corporation (KTV) television director, producer, executive director, and production director, as well as Media Pulpo (KTV's subsidiary) representative director and chairman.
