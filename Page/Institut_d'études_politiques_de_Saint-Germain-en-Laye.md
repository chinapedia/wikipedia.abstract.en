SCIENCES PO SAINT-GERMAIN-EN-LAYE, or INSTITUT D'ÉTUDES POLITIQUES DE SAINT-GERMAIN-EN-LAYE, is a French political science grande école situated on Saint-Germain-en-Laye, close to Paris and is attached to the Versailles Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines University and the Cergy-Pontoise University. It was established in 2013. Sciences Po Saint-Germain-en-Laye is one of the ten Instituts d'études politiques of France, and a so-called "Grande Ecole".[1]

The Institute has approximately 300 students, admitted after a rigorous selection.[2] Academic studies last five years, at the end of which a student graduates with the Diploma of Sciences Po Saint-Germain-en-Laye (which is equivalent to a master's degree).
