CHERRY VALLEY is an unincorporated community in Crawford County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Cherry Valley was established in 1853, and remained in operation until 1857.[2] The community was named for the valley of the Cherry Valley Creek.[3]
