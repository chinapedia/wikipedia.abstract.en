DAVID FRASER CAMERON was a Scottish professional football wing half who played in the Football League for Chelsea.[1][2][3]


Personal life

Cameron served in the Highland Light Infantry and the Queen's Own Cameron Highlanders during the First World War and rose to the rank of sergeant.[4][5] His elder brother Macdonald was killed at Passchendaele in 1917.[6]
