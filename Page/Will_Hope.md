WILLIAM HOPE (born 2 June 1993) is an Irish rugby league footballer who plays as a second row or for the Swinton Lions in the Championship.[1]

Hope is an Irish international.[2] In 2014, he was called up to the squad and has been ever present in the team. In 2016, he was called up to the Ireland squad for the 2017 Rugby League World Cup European Pool B qualifiers.[3]

Hope has previously played for the Salford City Reds. He has also spent time on loan at the Sheffield Eagles and Oldham.[4] He joined Oldham in 2015 and was released at the end of the 2016 season.[5][6] He then signed for Sheffield in January 2017.[7]

Hope was selected to travel with the Ireland squad for the 2017 World Cup, he only made one appearance but scored twice against Wales.
