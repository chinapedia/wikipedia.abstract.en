BENIK HOVHANNISYAN (Armenian: Բենիկ Հովհաննիսյան) is an Armenian footballer who now plays for FC Alashkert.[1]


Career

One of his goals was scored in the last round in the 2015-16 Armenian Premier League for FC Ararat Yerevan in a pulsating 1-2 loss to Shirak.[2]

He is listed as the #19 player on the roster.[3]

The Armenian made his international debut in a 5-0 loss to Romania.[4]
