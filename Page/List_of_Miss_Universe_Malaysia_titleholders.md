Notes

-   Miss Universe Malaysia 2011, Deborah Henry was one of the top contender to win the crown in Miss World 2007 but at last she ended up as one of the Top 15 semifinalists.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 2004, Andrea Fonseka was the national director of Miss Universe Malaysia from 2005 until 2009 and 2012.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 2003, Elaine Daly is the current national director of Miss Universe Malaysia since 2016 replacing Carey Ng.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 1999, Jeanette Ooi competed in Miss Model of the World 1997 and placed as one of the Top 15 semifinalist.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 1997, Trincy Low was the winner of Miss Malaysia World 1995 but was unplaced in Miss World 1995.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 1990, Anna Lin is the national director of Miss Malaysia World.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 1978, Yasmin Yusoff was one of national director of Miss Universe Malaysia from 1983 until 2003.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 1970, Josephine Wong was the only Miss Malaysia/Universe that entered the Top 15 semifinalist.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 1968, Maznah Ali was appointed by the Miss Malaysia pageant as there is no pageant in 1968.
-   Miss Universe Malaysia 1963, Azizah Yahya did not compete in Miss Universe 1963 due to the formation of Malaysia in 1963 incorporate with Sarawak, North Borneo (Sabah) and Singapore.
-   From 1953 until 1962, Miss Malaya representatives for Miss Universe were crowned from a small beauty contest named Miss Federation of Malaya, unfortunately all of them were not able to compete in Miss Universe due to lack of sponsorship from the Malaya government to send them to compete in the 50s Miss Universe pageant. Only one Miss Malaya succeed to compete in Miss Universe which is in 1962.
