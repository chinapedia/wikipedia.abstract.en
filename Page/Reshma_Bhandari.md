RESHMA BHANDARI (born 3 January 1994) is a National Volleyball player of Nepal. She plays from Armed Police Force Nepal's APF Club of 2013 (2070 in Nepali calendar).[1] [2]


Early life

She was born on 3 January 1994 and is from Kathmandu, Nepal. She studied at Intensive International College
