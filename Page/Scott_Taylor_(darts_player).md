SCOTT TAYLOR (born August 22, 1991) is a darts player from Bolton, England. He plays in Professional Darts Corporation events.[1]

Taylor reached the quarter-finals of the 2015 PDC World Youth Championship and lost 6−5 to Dean Reynolds.[2] He won the eighth Challenge Tour event of 2016 by beating Barry Lynn 5−4.[3] His first match win in a European Tour tournament came at the German Darts Championship when he defeated Andy Boulton 6−3. Taylor lost 6−1 to Alan Norris in the second round.[4]

Taylor won a two-year PDC Tour Card in January 2017 by defeating Antonio Alcinas 5−1 on the third day of Q-School.[5]
