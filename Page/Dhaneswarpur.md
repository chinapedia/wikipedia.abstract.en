DHANESWARPUR is a village, in Dantan II CD Block in Kharagpur subdivision of Paschim Medinipur district in the state of West Bengal, India.


CD Block HQ

The headquarters of Dantan II CD Block are located at Dhaneswarpur.[1]


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Dhaneswarpur had a total population of 2,000 of which 1,020 (51%) were males and 98 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 199. The total number of literates in Dhaneswarpur was 1,341 (67.05% of the population over 6 years).[2]


Transport

SH 5 connecting Rupnarayanpur (in Bardhaman district) and Junput (in Purba Medinipur district) passes through Dhaneswarpur.[3]
