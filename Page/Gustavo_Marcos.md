'''Gustavo Marcos Herrero ''' (born 23 December 1972) is a Spanish male water polo player in the late '90s and early '00s. He was a member of the Spain men's national water polo team, playing as a centre back. He was a part of the team at the 2000 Summer Olympics and 2004 Summer Olympics.[1] On club level he played for CN Sabadell in Spain.
