Career

Osman was elected to the parliament of Jatiya Sangsad four times in 1986, 1988, 2008, and 2014.[3]


Personal life

Osman's father, AKM Samsuzzoha, was a member of parliament and his youngest brother Shamim Osman is member of parliament from Bangladesh Awami League.[4] Osman died 30 April 2014.[5] After his death, his younger brother, Salim Osman was elected from the same constituency.[6]

Osman was married to Parvin Osman.[7] Together they had a son Azmir Osman and two daughters.[8]
