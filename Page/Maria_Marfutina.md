MARIA SERGEYEVNA MARFUTINA (, born 5 June 1997) is a Russian tennis player.

She has a career-high WTA singles ranking of 354, achieved on 9 May 2016. Marfutina also has a career-high WTA doubles ranking of 253, achieved on 6 June 2016. She has won seven singles and nine doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit.

Marfutina won her biggest title to date at the 2016 Neva Cup, an $100,000 tournament, in doubles with Anna Morgina, defeating Raluca Olaru and Alena Tarasova in the final.
