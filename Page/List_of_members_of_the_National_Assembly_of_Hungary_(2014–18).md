The LIST OF MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY OF HUNGARY (2014–18) is the list of members of the National Assembly – the unicameral legislative body of Hungary – according to the outcome of the Hungarian parliamentary election of 2014. The members of the new National Assembly were installed on May 6, 2014 and their mandates lasted until May 7, 2018. There has been a sizable number of mutations since due to the particular nature of the Hungary constitutional system. New members are supplied from their party lists so the resignation of individual members' seats does not change the balance of power in the National Assembly.
