SENE is an American luxury designer brand headquartered in New York City and Los Angeles. It deals in the Men's clothing and accessories.[1]


History

"The company was founded by Ray Li in 2014 and started with suiting. It has since expanded into outerwear, chinos,[2] joggers, and shirting.[3] Formerly known as _Suitable_,[4] the brand changed its name to SENE in October 2016.[5]

The brand was first headquartered in New York. It later expanded to a second headquarters in Los Angeles in October 2016.


Collaborations

SENE worked with Patrick Janelle, CFDA Instagrammer of the Year, to design a capsule collection for F/W 2016. The collection included three suits and a coat.[6]
