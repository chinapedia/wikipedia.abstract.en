MEBO is a Tehsil in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1] East Siang is the name of the district that contains Tehsil Mebo.

Mebo is located towards East from District headquarters Pasighat. It is from the state capital of Itanagar towards West. It is one of the 60 constituencies of Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh. The name of current MLA (October-2016) of this constituency is LOMBO TAYENG.
