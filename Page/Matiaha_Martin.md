MATIAHA MARTIN (born 3 June 1992) is a New Zealand rugby union player who currently plays as a lock for in New Zealand's domestic Mitre 10 Cup.[1][2]


Senior career

After coming through the ranks of Counties Manukau's under-18, B and development teams, Martin finally cracked the senior squad in 2016 Mitre 10 Cup.
