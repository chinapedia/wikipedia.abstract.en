SANGAREYA BAY ()[1] is a bay in the coast of Guinea on the Atlantic Ocean.


Geography

The Sangareya Bay opens to the west and is located just to the north of Conakry. The Los Islands _(Îles de Los)_ are located about 2 km off the headland limiting the bay on its southern side.
