_EPEPEOTES TAENIOTINUS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Heller in 1924. It is known from the Philippines and Moluccas.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Epepeotes taeniotinus gebehensis_ Breuning, 1974
-   _Epepeotes taeniotinus taeniotinus_ (Heller, 1924)
