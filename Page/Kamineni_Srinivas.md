KAMINENI SRINIVAS is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party from Andhra Pradesh. He has won the 2014 Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly election from Kaikalur.[1][2]

He won with 88,092 votes in Assembly Election in Krishna district by a margin of 21,571 compared to his political rival Ram Prasad Uppala of YSR Congress Party.[3] He was the Health minister of Andhra Pradesh from 2014 - March 2018. In his tenure he carried out many reforms in Health Ministry
