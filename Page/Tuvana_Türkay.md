TUVANA TÜRKAY (born 3 October 1990) is a Turkish actress.[1][2]


Early life

Türkay was born on 3 October 1990 in Üsküdar, Istanbul, Turkey.[3] She studied at Beykent University in the Department of Radio, Television and Cinema.

Her older sister, Katre Türkay, is also a model and an actress.[4]
