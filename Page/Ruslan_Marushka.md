RUSLAN MARUSHKA (; born 23 August 1998 in Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football defender who currently plays for the Ukrainian First League club Rukh Vynnyky.


Career

Marushka is a product of the Sportive youth school of FC Lviv.

He made his debut for FC Volyn Lutsk played as a second-time substituted player in the game against FC Dnipro on 22 October 2016 in the Ukrainian Premier League.[1]
