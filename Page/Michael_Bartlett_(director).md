MICHAEL F. BARTLETT is an American filmmaker, producer, screenwriter, and editor.


Life and career

Bartlett directed 'Breaking Bad' series star R.J. Mitte in his first feature film, the supernatural thriller[1] 'House of Last Things.' Bartlett sometimes stages his screenplays within his own residences, and 'House of Last Things' was written to fit the location exactly.[2][3]
