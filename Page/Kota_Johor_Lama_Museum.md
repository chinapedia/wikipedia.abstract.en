The KOTA JOHOR LAMA MUSEUM () is a museum in Johor Lama, Kota Tinggi District, Johor, Malaysia. The museum is about the history of Johor Lama.[1]


History

The museum started to be constructed in August 2004 and completed in February 2007. It was opened to the public in October 2007.[2]


Architecture

The museum resembles a traditional house.


Opening time

The museum opens everyday from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. free of charge.
