JOSEPH THOMAS ENRIGHT was an Irish professional football forward who played in the Football League for Leeds City.[1][2] He also played in the Southern League for Newport County and Coventry City and won an international cap for Ireland.[3][4]


Personal life

Enwright was born at home on Glasses Lane (now Griffith Street) in Athlone to Thomas Enright, a labourer, and Elizabeth Enright (_née_ Garty).[5] Enwright served in the Royal Army Ordnance Corps during the First World War.[6] As of March 2014, his great nephew Chris Enright was on the staff at Athlone Town.[7]
