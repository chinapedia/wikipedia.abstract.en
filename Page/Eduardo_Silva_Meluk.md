EDUARDO SILVA MELUK (Cúcuta, Colombia, January 15, 1970), best known as El Presi,[1] is a business administrator and the 39th president of the football club, Deportivo Independiente Medellin.


Career

He was Millonarios' legal agent.

He introduced membership cards, that allow members easy access to Atanasio Girardot Stadium.[2]

He helped Medellín acquire excellent players: Juan Fernando Caicedo, Hernan Hechalar and Didier Moreno in 2015; Juan David Perez, Faider Burbano, Daniel Torres and Luis Carlos Arias and Matías Cahais.

He hired Leonel Álvarez as coach, who managed the team to qualify for Copa Sudamericana 2016 and Copa Libertadores 2017.
