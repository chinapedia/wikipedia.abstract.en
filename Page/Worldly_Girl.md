_WORLDLY GIRL_ () is a 2016 Italian drama film co-written and directed by Marco Danieli, at his feature film debut.[1][2]

The film was screened at the 73rd edition of the Venice Film Festival in the Venice Days section, in which it was awarded the Lizzani Award.[3]
