Las_Illas_-_vue_d'ensemble.jpg LAS ILLAS is a former commune in Pyrénées-Orientales (France), now part of Maureillas-las-Illas.


Geography

Las Illas is located south of Maureillas and north of Maçanet de Cabrenys (Spain).


History

Las Illas becomes a commune in 1790.

Even though the commune repeatedly asked to have electricity since 1923, Las Illas will only be linked to the electric network on 26 July 1955.[1]

On 20 June 1972, Las Illas is linked with Maureillas and Riunoguès to create the new commune of Maureillas-las-Illas.[2]
