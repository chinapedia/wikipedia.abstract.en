APIA MURI is a Papua New Guinean professional basketball player. He is one of the country's most prominent basketball figures.[1]

He led Papua New Guinea's national basketball team all the way to the bronze medal match of the 2015 Pacific Games, where he recorded most minutes, points, rebounds and blocks for his team.[2]
