_THE CAROUSEL OF DEATH_ (German: _DAS KARUSSELL DES TODES_) is a 1928 German silent film directed by Heinz Paul and starring Claire Rommer, Anton Pointner and Jean Murat.[1]

The film's art direction was by Otto Erdmann and Hans Sohnle.
