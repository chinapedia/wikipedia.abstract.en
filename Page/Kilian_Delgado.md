KILIAN DELGADO QUIJONES (born 1 April 1990 in Valdivia, Chile) is a Chilean footballer that currently plays for Coquimbo Unido in the Chilean Primera Division B.


Career

Starts his football career in Deportes Valdivia the year 2009, through the various divisions of the chilean soccer with the club. In the year 2016 achieved with the _Torreon_ the title of the Segunda División. Completed its contract with Deportes Valdivia, signed with Coquimbo Unido, club with which will play for the year 2016.
