_CALL ME THIEF_ () is a 2016 South African crime film directed by Daryne Joshua. It was selected as the South African entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 89th Academy Awards but it was not nominated.[1]


Cast

-   Sandi Schultz as Kettie
-   Dann-Jacques Mouton as Abraham
