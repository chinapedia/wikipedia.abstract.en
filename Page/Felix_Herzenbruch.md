FELIX HERZENBRUCH (born 8 August 1992) is a German footballer who plays as a left back for SC Paderborn.[1]

He made his professional debut for SC Paderborn in the first round of the DFB-Pokal against 2. Bundesliga club SV Sandhausen on 22 August 2016, losing 1–2.[2]
