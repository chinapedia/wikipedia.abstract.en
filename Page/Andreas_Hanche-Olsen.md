ANDREAS SCHJØLBERG HANCHE-OLSEN (born 17 January 1997) is a Norwegian professional footballer who plays for Eliteserien club Stabæk as a defender.


Career

Club

On 26 November 2015, Hanche-Olsen signed his first professional contract with Stabæk.[1] He was named captain ahead of the 2018 season after former captain Morten Morisbak Skjønsberg retired.
