_AGELASTA YONAGUNI_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Masao Hayashi in 1962. It is known from Japan.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Agelasta yonaguni kashiwaii_ (Kusama & Takakuwa, 1984)
-   _Agelasta yonaguni similaris_ (Kusama & Takakuwa, 1984)
-   _Agelasta yonaguni subkonoi_ (Breuning, 1964)
-   _Agelasta yonaguni yonaguni_ (Hayashi, 1962)
