PEDRO ORDÓÑEZ Y FLÓREZ (1560–1614) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Archbishop of Santafé en Nueva Granada (1610–1614).[1]


Biography

Pedro Ordóñez y Flórez was born in Brozas, Spain in 1560.[2] On 19 Apr 1610, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul V as Archbishop of Santafé en Nueva Granada.[3] On 21 Dec 1611, he was consecrated bishop by Bartolomé Lobo Guerrero, Archbishop of Lima.[4] He served as Archbishop of Santafé en Nueva Granada until his death on 11 Jun 16149.[5]
