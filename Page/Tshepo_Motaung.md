TSHEPO MOTAUNG (born 9 August 1999) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his List A debut for Easterns in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 9 October 2016[2] and his first-class debut for Easterns in the 2016–17 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 6 October 2016.[3]
