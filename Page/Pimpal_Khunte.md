PIMPAL KHUNTE is a village in India, situated in Mawal taluka of Pune district in the state of Maharashtra. It encompasses an area of .[1]


Administration

The village is administrated by a sarpanch, an elected representative who leads a gram panchayat. At the time of the 2011 Census of India, the gram panchayat governed two villages and was based at Bebad Ohol.[2]


Demographics

At the 2011 census, the village comprised 130 households. The population of 715 was split between 366 males and 349 females.[3]


Air travel connectivity

The closest airport to the village is Pune Airport.
