RAÚL KAMFFER (16 Abril 1929 – 19 May 1987) was a Mexican filmmaker. For his film _{{'}}Ora Sí ¡Tenemos Que Ganar!_ (1981), Kamffer earned the Ariel Award for Best Director and Best Picture.[1]
