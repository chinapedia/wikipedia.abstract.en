AZZEDINE MIHOUBI (born January 1, 1959) is an Algerian journalist, poet, novelist and politician. He serves as the Algerian Minister of Culture.


Early life

Career

Mihoubi started his career as a journalist in 1986.[3] He was the head of information for Algerian Television from 1996 to 1997.[4] He served as the chief executive of the Algerian Radio from 2006 to 2008, and the National Library of Algeria from 2010 to 2013.[5]

Mihoubi served as a member of the People's National Assembly from 1997 to 2002.[6] He has served as the Algerian Minister of Culture since 2015.[7]

Mihoubi is the author of ten poetry collections and four novels.[8] He is the recipient of several literary prizes for his poetry.[9]
