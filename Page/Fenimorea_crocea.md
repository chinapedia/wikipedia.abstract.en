_FENIMOREA CROCEA_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of this marine shell attains 15.6 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs in the Caribbean Sea off the Campeche Bank, Yucatán, Mexico.
