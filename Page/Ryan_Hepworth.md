RYAN HEPWORTH (born 16 January 1981) is a professional rugby league footballer who most recently played for the Dewsbury Rams in the Kingstone Press Championship. He plays as a prop or second row.[1]

Hepworth previously played for the Sheffield Eagles in the Championship.[2]
