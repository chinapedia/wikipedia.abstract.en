TREVOR ELHI (born 11 April 1993) is an Estonian professional footballer who plays as a left back for Finnish club SJK and the Estonia national team.


International career

Elhi began his international career with the Estonia under-16 team in 2008, and went on to represent the under-17, under-18, under-19, under-21, and under-23 national sides.

Elhi made his senior international debut for Estonia on 22 November 2016, in a 1–0 away win over Antigua and Barbuda in a friendly.[1]
