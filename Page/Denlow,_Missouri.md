DENLOW is an unincorporated community in north central Douglas County in the Ozarks of Missouri.[1] Denlow is located on Missouri Route 76 just west of the Fox Creek bridge.[2]


History

A post office called Denlow was established in 1896, and remained in operation until 1919.[3] An early variant name was "Upshaw".[4]
