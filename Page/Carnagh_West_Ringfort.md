CARNAGH WEST RINGFORT is a ringfort and National Monument located in County Roscommon, Ireland.[1]


Location

Carnagh West Ringfort is located halfway between Lough Funshinagh and Lough Ree.


History and description

Carnagh West Ringfort is a rath or ringfort with foundations of rectangular huts. The double-walling suggests a Stone Age date. The townland name is from the Irish _carnach_, "abounding in heaps/cairns", due to the large number of raths in the area.
