Benzi3hr.jpg ROBERTO BENZI (born 1937) is a French conductor and former child actor.


Early life

Roberto Benzi was born on December 12, 1937, in Marseille, France.[1]


Career

Benzi acted in two films as a child.[2]

Benzi was the conductor of the Orchestre National Bordeaux Aquitaine from 1973 to 1987.[3] He was the conductor of the Arnhem Philharmonic Orchestra in Arnhem, The Netherlands from 1989 to 1990.[4][5] He later conducted orchestras in Russia and Japan.[6]


Personal life

Benzi resides in Paris.[7] He was married to opera singer Jane Rhodes, who died in 2011.[8]
