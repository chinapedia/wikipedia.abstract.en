GARFIELD is an unincorporated community in southeast Oregon County, in the Ozarks of southern Missouri.[1] The community is located adjacent to Missouri Route E about three miles southeast of Couch.[2]


History

A variant name was "Sittonville".[3] A post office called Garfield was established in 1882, and remained in operation until 1906.[4] The community has the name of James A. Garfield, 20th President of the United States.[5]
