EREZ SHEMESH (ארז שמש; born October 23, 1970) is an Israeli Olympic competitive sailor.[1]

Shemesh was born in Israel, and is Jewish.[2]


Sailing career

Shemesh competed for Israel at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Olympic Port, Barcelona, at the age of 22 in Sailing--Men's Two Person Dinghy (470 event) with partner Shai Bachar.[3][4] They finished in 9th place out of 37 competitors.[5][6][7]

In 1993 at the 470 World Championships, Shemesh and Bachar won the bronze medal.[8]

In 1995, Shemesh and Bachar finished in 17th place at the 470 World Championships.[9]
