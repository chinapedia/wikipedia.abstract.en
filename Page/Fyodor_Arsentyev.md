FYODOR SERGEYEVICH ARSENTYEV (; born 17 February 1999) is a Russian football player who plays for FC Volga Ulyanovsk.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk on 13 November 2016 in a game against FC Khimki.[1]
