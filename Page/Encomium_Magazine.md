_ENCOMIUM MAGAZINE_ is a Nigerian magazine published by Encomium ventures limited. Its head office is located in Ikeja, Lagos. It started publishing since 1997.[1]

_Encomium Magazine_ is among the top selling soft sell magazine in Nigeria. Its coverage includes celebrity gossip, health, style and wellbeing.[2]
