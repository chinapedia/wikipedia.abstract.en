DENIS DMITRIEVICH BUSHUEV (; born 15 February 1982) is a Russian football manager.

On 9 May 2016, Bushuev became head coach of 1860 Munich as interim manager. He managed one match, a 1–2 loss against FSV Frankfurt in the 2. Bundesliga.[1]

On May 2019, he became top candidate to lead FC Torpedo Moscow during 2019-2020 season in the Football National League.
