NORRIS CREEK is a stream in Henry and Johnson Counties in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of Big Creek.

The stream headwaters arise in Johnson County between the communities of Chilhowee and Denton at [2] and the stream flows south crossing under Missouri Route 2 and enters Henry County to its confluence with Big Creek south of Blairstown at .[3][4]

Norris Creek has the name of William Norris, the proprietor of a mill along its course.[5]
