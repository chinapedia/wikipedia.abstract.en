The SANREMO MUSIC FESTIVAL 1961 was the eleventh annual Sanremo Music Festival, held at the Sanremo Casino in Sanremo, province of Imperia between 26 January and 6 February 1961.[1]

The show was presented by Lilli Lembo and Giuliana Calandra for the first three nights, while Alberto Lionello replaced Calandra in the final night. Ezio Radaelli served as artistic director.[2]

According to the rules of this edition every song was performed in a double performance by a couple of singers or groups.[3] The winners of the Festival were Luciano Tajoli and Betty Curtis with the song "Al di là".[4]
