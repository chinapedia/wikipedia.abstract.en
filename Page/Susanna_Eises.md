'''Susanna Eises ''' (born 18 January 1991) is a Namibian women's international footballer who plays as a goalkeeper. She is a member of the Namibia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship. On club level she plays for Khomas Nampol ladies FC in Namibia.[1][2]
