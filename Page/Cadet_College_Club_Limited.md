CADET COLLEGE CLUB LIMITED[1] or CCCL[2] is pan-national club for the alumni of all cadet colleges in Bangladesh and is located in Gulshan, Dhaka.[3] Group Captain Muhammad Alamgir, acsc(Retd) is the present president of the club.[4] The club was formed in 2003 with the alumni from 10 cadet colleges and Bangali alumni of military schools situated in the then West Pakistan.[5] The Clubs holds its own annual book fair.[6]
