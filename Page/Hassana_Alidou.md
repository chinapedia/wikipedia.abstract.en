HASSANA ALIDOU (born March 29, 1963) has been Niger's ambassador to the United States since February 2015.[1]

Her twin sister Ousseina Alidou is an Africanist scholar specialising in the study of Muslim women in Africa, and a professor in the Department of African American and African Studies at Rutgers University.[2][3][4]
