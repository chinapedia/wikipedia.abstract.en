DAVID MCINTOSH was a Scottish amateur football forward who played in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1]


Personal life

McIntosh served as a sergeant in the Highland Light Infantry during the First World War.[2] He was evacuated to Britain after being wounded in late 1917.[3]
