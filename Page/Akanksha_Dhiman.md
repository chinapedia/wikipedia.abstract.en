'''Akanksha Dhiman ''' (born 27 December 1991 in Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh) is an Indian model, actress, beauty queen and brand ambassador. Akanksha can speak some English, & Perfect Hindi .She won Miss Glory of Earth in 2013.[1]

Akanksha Dhiman was a queen is India beauty pageant field. She made a mark by winning the crown of Glamanand Super Model India where she has represented India in international pageants.

She is the brand ambassador for South Korean auto company KR Motors at the time of Miss Asia Pacific.
