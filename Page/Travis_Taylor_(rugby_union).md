TRAVIS TAYLOR (born 22 February 1995) is a New Zealand rugby union player who currently plays for in the Mitre 10 Cup. His position of choice is hooker.[1]

Originally from Waipukurau, Taylor has captained the Manawatu U19s and been a part of the Manawatu development squad.[2]

Taylor made his debut for the Manawatu senior squad in 2016. On 1 October 2016, in a 30-21 loss against Hawke's Bay, Taylor scored two tries.
