MYKHAYLO MESKHI (; born 26 February 1997 in Donetsk, Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football midfielder of Georgian descents, who plays for Mezőkövesdi SE in the Hungarian Nemzeti Bajnokság I.


Career

Meskhi is a product of the youth team system of present-day defunct FC Metalurh from his native city Donetsk.[1]

From 2015 he plays for FC Stal Kamianske and made his debut for FC Stal in the game against FC Shakhtar Donetsk on 4 December 2016 in the Ukrainian Premier League.[2]

On 13 July 2018 he signed a contract to Hungarian first division team Mezőkövesdi SE.[3]
