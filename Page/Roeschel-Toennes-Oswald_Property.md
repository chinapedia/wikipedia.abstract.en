ROESCHEL-TOENNES-OSWALD PROPERTY, also known as the OSWALD FARM and BOYCE PROPERTY, a historic home located at Boonville, Cooper County, Missouri. The original section was built in the 1850s-1860s and tool its present form in 1905. It is a one- and two-story, frame and brick I-house form dwelling with Queen Anne design influences. It features a projecting two-story bay and shingled gables with returns.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1983.[2]
