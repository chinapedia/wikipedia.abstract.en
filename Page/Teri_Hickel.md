TERI HICKEL is a Republican former member of the Washington House of Representatives. She was elected in 2015 to finish out the term of Roger Freeman, who died.[1] Carol Gregory had been appointed to serve the first year of Freeman's term, which was to be confirmed by special election in 2015. Hickel won that election, but lost re-election in the regular 2016 election to Kristine Reeves.[2]


Personal life

Hickel and her husband, Tim, have two children; Mackenzie and Morgan.[3]
