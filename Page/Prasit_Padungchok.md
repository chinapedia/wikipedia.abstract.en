PRASIT PADUNGCHOK (, born October 13, 1982) is a Thai professional footballer who plays as a Goalkeeper for Thai League 1 club Police Tero. Thailand national team.


International career

he was named in head coach Milovan Rajevac's squad for Thailand to the Friendly on October 2017, but did not make an appearance.
