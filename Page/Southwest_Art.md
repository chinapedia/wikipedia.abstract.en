_SOUTHWEST ART_ is a magazine published by F+W that specializes in fine art depicting artwork of the American Southwest.


History and profile

_Southwest Art_ was first published in May 1971.[1] It first dealt with arts in Houston and then its coverage expanded.[2] The magazine features Art of the American Southwest usually in form paintings and sculpture.
