PATRICK SCHIKOWSKI (born 20 June 1992) is a Polish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Rot-Weiß Oberhausen. Born and raised in Germany, he also holds German citizenship.[1]

He is the older brother of fellow footballer Florian Schikowski.[2]
