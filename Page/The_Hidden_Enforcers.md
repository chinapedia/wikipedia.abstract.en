_THE HIDDEN ENFORCERS_ is a 2002 Hong Kong action film directed by Nam Yin and starring Sammo Hung.


Plot

After a botched drug raid 15 years ago, retired police officer King (Sammo Hung) is now the head of a vigilante group hunting down criminals that killed his fellow officers.[1]


Cast

-   Sammo Hung as King a retired vigilante cop
-   Nadia Chan as King's vigilante assistant
