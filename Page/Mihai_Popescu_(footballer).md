MIHAI POPESCU (born 1 September 1993) is a Romanian footballer who plays as a defender for Dinamo București.


Career

Popescu joined Scottish Premiership side St Mirren on loan in January 2019, until the end of the season.[1] In June 2019 the possibility that the deal would be extended was thrown into doubt after St Mirren manager Oran Kearney left the club,[2] and he returned to Bucharest in July 2019.[3]


Honours

-   Romanian Supercup: 2017
