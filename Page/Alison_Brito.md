ALISON BRITO AGUES (born 27 October 1984) is a Cape Verdean footballer who plays as a goalkeeper. He currently plays with Windsor Arch Ka I in the Macanese Premier Division.

He first appeared in the Macanese club CD Monte Carlo and was one of the first Cape Verdeans to play soccer with a Macanese club. A year later, he played with Casa do FC Porto for the next three years and in 2013 with Windsor Arch Ka I as of 2014.[1]
