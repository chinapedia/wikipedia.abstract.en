_GUDEODISCUS PHLYARIUS_ is a species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Plectopylidae.

_Gudeodiscus phlyarius_ is the type species of the genus _Gudeodiscus_.[1]


Distribution

The distribution of _Gudeodiscus phlyarius_ includes Vietnam.[2]


Ecology

It is a ground-dwelling species as all other plectopylid snails in Vietnam.[3]

It co-occur with other plectopylids in Vietnam: with _Gudeodiscus anceyi_, _Gudeodiscus messageri raheemi_ and with _Gudeodiscus villedaryi_.[4] _Gudeodiscus francoisi_, _Gudeodiscus giardi_, _Sicradiscus mansuyi_, _Gudeodiscus dautzenbergi_ and _Gudeodiscus suprafilaris_ live at geographically close sites to _Gudeodiscus phlyarius_.[5]
