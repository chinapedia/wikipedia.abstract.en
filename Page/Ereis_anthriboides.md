_EREIS ANTHRIBOIDES_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Francis Polkinghorne Pascoe in 1857. It is known from Sumatra and Malaysia.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Ereis anthriboides annamensis_ Breuning, 1967
-   _Ereis anthriboides anthriboides_ (Pascoe, 1857)
