ANDRÉ FILIPE REBELO MESQUITA (born 10 October 1997) is a Portuguese footballer who plays as a forward.


Club career

On 17 September 2016, Mesquita made his professional debut with Fafe in a 2016–17 LigaPro match against Penafiel.[1]
