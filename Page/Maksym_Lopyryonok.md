MAKSYM LOPYRYONOK (; born 13 April 1995) is a professional Ukrainian football defender.


Career

Lopyryonok is a product of the UFK Dnipropetrovsk and FC Dnipro Youth Sportive School systems. His first trainers were Volodymyr Herashchenko and Kostyantyn Pavlyuchenko.

He made his debut for FC Dnipro in the match against FC Stal Kamianske on 30 October 2016 in the Ukrainian Premier League as a main-squad player.[1]
