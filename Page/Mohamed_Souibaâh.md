MOHAMED SOUIBAÂH (born 25 December 1991 in Baraki) is an Algerian footballer who plays for ES Sétif in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1.[1]


Club career

On 22 July 2016 Souibaâh signed a contract with MC Oran, joining them on a transfer from Paradou AC.[2]
