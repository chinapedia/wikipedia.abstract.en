SENTA-SOFIA DELLIPONTI (born April 16, 1990 in Wolfsburg ) is a German singer, songwriter and actress. Since January 2014 she is under the stage name OONAGH ()[1][2]. Her signature musical style is inspired by the mystical lore of J. R. R. Tolkien's universe and by ethnic sounds throughout the world.

She had her breakthrough in _Star Search_,[3][4] a television talent show. She released a Christmas album with TV Allstars in 2003.

In 2015, she was a guest singer on the Celtic Woman album _Celtic Woman: Destiny_.[5] She sang the theme song to _Big Brother_ in Germany in 2007.


Biography

Senta-Sofia Delliponti is the daughter of an Italian and a Bulgarian and grew up in Gifhorn, Lower Saxony . Her father Maik Delliponti is a pizza maker, her mother Elena Delliponti a music teacher. The names were inspired by the actress Senta Berger and the capital of her mother's homeland, Sofia. She has a younger brother. She participated in Music competition and was on stage in Musical '' Jim Button ''. In 2006 she completed her secondary school diploma. From 2007 to 2010 she completed training in drama and vocals at Drama School Charlottenburg in Berlin. In December 2017, she gave birth to a daughter.[6]

Career

On July 12, 2003, Delliponti made her first appearance on the Talent Show '' Star Search '' on Sat.1. She participated in the category '' Music Act of 10 to 15 years '' and was able to prevail until the finale. Behind her comrade Daniel Siegert she finished second on August 10, 2003.
