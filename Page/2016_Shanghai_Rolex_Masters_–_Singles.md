Novak Djokovic was the defending champion, but lost in the semifinals to Roberto Bautista Agut.

Andy Murray won the title, defeating Bautista Agut in the final, 7−6^((7−1)), 6−1. Bautista Agut was competing in his first Masters 1000 Series final, while Murray won his 13th Masters 1000 title and third in Shanghai.
