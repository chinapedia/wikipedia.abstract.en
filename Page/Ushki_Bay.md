USHKI BAY (Russian: _Zaliv Ushki_) is a small bay in Khabarovsk Krai, Russian Federation.[1]


Geography

Ushki Bay is located on the northern coast of the Sea of Okhotsk. It lies to the east of the mountainous Lisyansky Peninsula. History

American whaleships cruised for bowhead whales in the bay in the 1840s[4] and 1850s. They called it SHEPHERDESS BAY,[5] after a Mystic ship[6] that frequented the area at the time.
