The MADURU OYA is a major stream in the North Central Province of Sri Lanka. It is approximately in length.[1] Its catchment area receives approximately 3,060 million cubic metres of rain per year, and approximately 26 percent of the water reaches the sea. It has a catchment area of 1,541 square kilometres.[2]
