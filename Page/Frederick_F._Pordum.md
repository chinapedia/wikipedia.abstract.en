FREDERICK F. PORDUM is a Democrat US politician from Lackawanna, New York. He represented the 1st District in the Erie County Legislature from 1968-1971.[1]

Pordum was sent to prison after being convicted of bribery charges in relation to a proposed dome stadium in Lancaster, New York.[2]
