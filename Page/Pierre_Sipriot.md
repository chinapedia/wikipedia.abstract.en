PIERRE SIPRIOT (16 January 1921 – 13 December 1998) was a 20th-century French journalist and principal biographer of Henry de Montherlant.

A journalist for France Culture, Pierre Sipriot produced the __ radio program from 1966.

He was twice a recipient of prizes awarded by the Académie française: the Prix Broquette-Gonin in 1973 and the Prix de la critique in 1977.
