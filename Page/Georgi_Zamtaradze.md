GEORGI TAMAZIEVICH ZAMTARADZE (Russian: Георгий Тамазиевич Замтарадзе; born ) is a Russian male futsal player, playing as a goalkeeper. He is part of the Russia national futsal team. He competed at the UEFA Futsal Euro 2016. At club level he is playing for Dinamo Moskva in in 2016.[1]
