SOUTHARD is an unincorporated community in Laclede County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Southard was established in 1906, and remained in operation until 1939.[2] D. T. Southard, an early postmaster, gave the community his last name.[3]


Notable person

Julia Southard Lee, a textile researcher, was born at Southard in 1897.[4]
