TAMARA SHUKAKIDZE ( born June 11, 1980) of Tbilisi, Georgia is Vice-Chairman of the Eurasia Regional Scout Committee of the World Organization of the Scout Movement (WOSM).[1] She is also Deputy Director for Strategy of the Emergency and Humanitarian Assistance Team of CARE USA based in Washington, D.C..[2] She is married.
