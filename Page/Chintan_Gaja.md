CHINTAN GAJA (born 13 November 1994) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Gujarat in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 27 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Gujarat in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 1 March 2017.[3] In November 2017, in his ninth first-class match, he took 8 wickets for 40 runs in the first innings for Gujarat against Rajasthan in the 2017–18 Ranji Trophy.[4]
