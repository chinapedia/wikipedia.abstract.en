DHARKAYN GEENYO is a town in the southern Sool region of Somaliland.


Overview

It is located on the Somalia-Ethiopia border. Dharkayn Geenyo is a historical town, it was an important border post during the Siad Barre government with one of the biggest Somali military bases, until it was destroyed by the SNM. The town was the centre of clan skirmishes in 2016 and 2017. [1][2]
