NDAWÉ is a village in the commune of Djohong in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, near the border with the Central African Republic


Population

In 1967, Ndawé contained 14 inhabitants, mainly Wodaabe.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 366 people in the village.[2]
