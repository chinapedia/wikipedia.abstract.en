ILYES YAICHE (born October 27, 1997) is an Algerian footballer who plays for Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1 club NA Hussein Dey on loan from USM Alger. He plays primarily as an attacking midfielder.


Club career

In June 2015, Yaiche was a member of the ASM Oran Under-18 team that won the Algerian U18 Cup, scoring a goal in the 3-1 win against USM El Harrach in the final.[1]

In the summer of 2016, Boumechra signed a four-year contract with USM Alger.[2]
