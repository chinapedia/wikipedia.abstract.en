The KLIESE HOUSEBARN, also known as the LANGHOFF HOUSEBARN, is a historic Housebarn located in Emmet, Wisconsin. It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2008.[1]


Architecture

This house is a traditional housebarn of the German half-timbered style. One side of the building was used as a barn, while the other side was used for human habitation.
