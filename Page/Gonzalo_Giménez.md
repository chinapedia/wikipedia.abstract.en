GONZALO EMILIANO GIMÉNEZ (born 4 September 1995) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Flandria.[1][2]


Career

Giménez made his debut for Arsenal de Sarandí after coming from the youth ranks in an Argentine Primera División match against Crucero del Norte on 1 June 2015.[3][4] Five more appearances followed in all competitions for Giménez in 2016 and 2016–17 respectively between February and October 2016.[5] In September 2017, Giménez joined Estudiantes of Primera B Metropolitana.[6][7] He made his debut for his new club on 24 September in a 1–1 draw versus Colegiales.[8] Fellow Primera B Metropolitana team Flandria signed Giménez in June 2018.[9] In his first start, he scored his first senior goal in a win over Comunicaciones on 29 August.[10]
