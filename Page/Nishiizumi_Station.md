is a railway station on the Hokuriku Railroad Ishikawa Line in the city of Kanazawa, Ishikawa Prefecture, Japan, operated by the private railway operator Hokuriku Railroad (Hokutetsu).[1]


Lines

Station layout

The station consists of one side platform serving a single bi-directional track. The station is unattended.
