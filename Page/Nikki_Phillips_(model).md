NIKKI PHILLIPS (born 1983[1][2]) is a New Zealand model, blogger, and television host.

Phillips was born in Auckland.[3] She started modelling at age 14 and moved to Australia in 2002. She appeared as a special guest judge in the first series of _New Zealand's Next Top Model_ in 2009.[4] In December 2015 she began hosting _Garage Movie Block_, a Sky Television show in New Zealand.[5] Nikki_Phillips_(8695809630).jpg Phillips married her husband Dane Rumble in Bali in 2014.[6]
