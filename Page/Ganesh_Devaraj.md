GANESH DEVARAJ is an Indian businessman and founder of Soliton Technologies.[1][2][3]

Ganesh holds Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication Engineering from PSG College of Technology and earned Master of Science in Electrical Engineering from University of Michigan.
