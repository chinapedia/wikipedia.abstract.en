LUDOVIC AJORQUE (born 25 February 1994) is a French footballer who plays as a forward for Strasbourg.[1]


International career

Born in Réunion, Ajorque is of Malagasy descent and was approached to join the Madagascar national football team in March 2018.[2] He has said he wants to concentrate on club football.[3]
