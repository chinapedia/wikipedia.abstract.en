NATIONAL HIGHWAY 185 (NH 185) is a highway in India running from Adimali to Kumily through Kattappana and ends in kumily Attapallam Village in Kerala.[1]


Route

Adimali - Cheruthoni, Painavu - Kumily.[2][3]


Junctions

Terminal near Adimali.[4]

Terminal near Kumily.[5]
