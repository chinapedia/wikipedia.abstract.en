GARETH GILL is an Irish rugby league footballer who plays as a for the Longhorns RL in the Irish Elite League.[1][2]

Gill is an Irish international.[3] In 2016 he was called up to the Ireland squad for the 2017 Rugby League World Cup European Pool B qualifiers.[4]
