KERRY PINK was a Tasmanian writer.

He had been a journalist in Burnie with the Advocate in the north west of Tasmania for 40 years [1][2] He had contributed to the Australian Dictionary of Biography [3]

He wrote a number of histories about Western Tasmania and the north coast of Tasmania
