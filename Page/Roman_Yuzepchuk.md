ROMAN YUZEPCHUK (, , born 24 July 1997) is a Belarusian association football player who plays for Dinamo Brest.[1]


Club career

During 2015, he was loaned to Dinamo farm-club Bereza-2010.


International career

He has made 3 appearances and scored 3 goals playing for the Belarus national under-19 football team at the 2016 UEFA European Under-19 Championship qualification.[2]
