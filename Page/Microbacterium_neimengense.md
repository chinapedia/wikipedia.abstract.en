_MICROBACTERIUM NEIMENGENSE_ is a Gram-positive, non-spore-forming, rod-shaped and non-motile bacterium from the genus of _Microbacterium_ which has been isolated from rhizosphere of a maize-plant in China.[1][2][3][4]
