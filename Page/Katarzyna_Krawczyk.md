KATARZYNA KRAWCZYK (born 6 September 1990) is a Polish freestyle wrestler. She competed in the women's freestyle 53 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which she was eliminated in the quarterfinals by Sofia Mattsson.[1][2]
