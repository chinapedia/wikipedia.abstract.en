γ Fornacis}} GAMMA¹ FORNACIS (Γ¹ FOR) is a star in the constellation Fornax. Its apparent magnitude is 6.15. It is a yellow giant that has swollen and brightened to around 80 times as luminous as the Sun.

γ¹ Fornacis has three companions listed in the Washington Double Star Catalog. These are faint 11th - 13th magnitude stars at 11' - 56' distance from γ¹.[1] Gamma² Fornacis is a 5th magnitude star four degrees to the south.
