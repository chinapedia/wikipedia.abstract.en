FC AHROBIZNES VOLOCHYSK is an association football club from Volochysk, Khmelnytskyi Oblast, competing in the 2017–18 Ukrainian Second League.


History

The club was founded in December 2015 in place of recently dissolved FC Zbruch.

Its football heritage the city of Volochysk traces to 1974 when it was represented by FC MASHYNOBUDIVNUK VOLOCHYSK which sometime in 1980s was dissolved. In 1989 the city's team was revived as FC STRILA VOLOCHYSK and won the regional championship (Khmelnytskyi Oblast Football Championship).

In 1990 the club was known as FC ZBRUCH VOLOCHYSK, but after few seasons ceased its operations. Sometime in 2000s the team again was revived.
