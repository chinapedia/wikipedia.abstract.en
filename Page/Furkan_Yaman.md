_' Furkan Yaman_' (born 8 January 1996 in Gaziosmanpaşa, Turkey) is a Turkish football player who plays as forward for Amed SK.[1] He is a product of the Beşiktaş youth academy.

Yaman has represented the Turkish Football Federation at the U15, U16, U17, U18, and U19 levels.
