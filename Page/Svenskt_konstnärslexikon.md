The _SVENSKT KONSTNÄRSLEXIKON_ is a dictionary of Swedish art and artists that was published in five volumes by Allhems Förlag AB from 1952 to 1967.[1] The dictionary includes over 12,000 biographical entries for Swedish artists with detailed bibliographies for each entry.[2] The editors were Gösta Lilja, Bror Olsson, Knut Andersson and S. Artur Svensson, with additional contributions by Johnny Roosval, Ragnar Josephson and Karl Erik Steneberg.
