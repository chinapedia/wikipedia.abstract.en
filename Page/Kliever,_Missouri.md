KLIEVER (also spelled KLEIVER) is an unincorporated community in eastern Moniteau County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] The primary road which passes through Kliever is Missouri Route 87. The community is about five miles northeast of California and five miles south of Jamestown.[2]


History

A post office called Kliever was established in 1891, and remained in operation until 1907.[3] The community was named after one Mr. Kleiver, the original owner of the town site.[4]
