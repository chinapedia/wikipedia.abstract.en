MARIUS SADOVEAC (born 7 May 1985)[1] is a Romanian handballer who plays for SCM Politehnica Timișoara and the Romania national team.


Achievements

-   LIGA NAȚIONALĂ:
    -   _Gold Medalist_: 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015
-   CUPA ROMÂNIEI:
    -   _Winner_: 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015
-   EHF CUP:
    -   _Fourth Place_: 2014
