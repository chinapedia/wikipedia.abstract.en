TE TAI TOKERAU MĀORI are a group of Māori _iwi_ (tribes) based on the Northland Peninsula of New Zealand's North Island. It includes the far northern Muriwhenua _iwi_ (tribes) of Te Aupōuri, Ngāti Kahu, Ngāti Kurī, Te Pātū, Te Rarawa and Ngāi Takoto. It also includes Ngāpuhi and the affiliated iwi of Ngāti Hine. Elsewhere in the region, it includes Whaingaroa, Ngāti Wai and Ngāti Whātua.

The _rohe_ (tribal areas) of these tribes covers Northland and Auckland.[1]
