_NEODRILLIA PRINCEPS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell varies between 32 mm and 47 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off French Guiana and Brazil.
