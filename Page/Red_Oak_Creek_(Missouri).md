RED OAK CREEK is a stream in Gasconade and Franklin counties of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Bourbeuse River.

The stream headwaters are in Gasconade County at and the confluence with the Bourbeuse River is in Franklin County at .[2]

Red Oak Creek was named for the red oak timber lining its course.[3]
