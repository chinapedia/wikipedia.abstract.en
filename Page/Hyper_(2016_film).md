_HYPER_ is a 2016 Indian Telugu-language action drama film[1] directed by Santosh Srinivas and produced under 14 Reels Entertainment banner . The film features Ram Pothineni and Raashi Khanna in the lead roles along with Sathyaraj, Naresh, Rao Ramesh, Thulasi Shivamani and Kasinadhuni Viswanath in the key supporting roles. The songs featured in the film are composed by Ghibran while the background score is composed by Mani Sharma.[2] The film was released worldwide on 30 September 2016. It was also dubbed in Hindi as _Son Of Satyamurthy 2_ in 2017.


Plot

Surya (Ram) is a carefree youth who lives with his father Narayana Murthy (Sathyaraj). He loves his father and is very protective about him. When they visit temple his father meets a displined and valued girl Bhanumathi (Raashi Khanna) whom he wishes which house she will enter it would be very lucky . This is noticed by Surya and he decides to marry only her just for his father's wish . He wants 50,000 for a delivery operation . He gets 40,000 from his friends and meets Bhanumathi (unaware that she is the same girl) and falls for her but doesn't do it because of temple girl. He fools her and takes the remaining 10,000 from her. She tells that her father (Posani Krishna Murali) thinks that money should be with us till last and he even puts specks to her so that anyone couldn't love her if she looks like aunty. Surya makes her life free from her job and career. He takes her to the delivery he helped couple's house . They ask him to name the baby which he names Bhanumathi because she gave the remaining 10,000 which makes Bhanumathi fall for him. She proposes him but is rejected because of temple girl (herself but doesn't knew it). He reprocates it when he discovers she is the same girl. As everything was going correct a political don (Rao Ramesh) troubles Narayana Murthy to get his signature for a file. Murthy objects for this signature and lands in deep trouble. Surya solves the problem. Narayana Murthy resigns happily giving the credit of his happiness to Surya . He later marries Bhanumathi and lives happily
