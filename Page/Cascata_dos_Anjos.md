The CASCATA DOS ANJOS (_Angels Waterfall_) is a waterfall located in the civil parish of Anjos, municipality of Ponta do Sol, on the Portuguese island of Madeira.

The waterfall cascades over the rockface onto the old E.R.101 regional roadway, and spills into the sea below.[1][2]
