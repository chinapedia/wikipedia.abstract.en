CAMILLE DEANGELIS is an American novelist and travel writer.

DeAngelis' novel about teenage cannibals, _Bones & All_, won an Alex Award in 2016. The storyline deals with issues such as feminism and loneliness.[1][2] DeAngelis has also published a self-help/memoir entitled _Life Without Envy: Ego Management for Creative People_ in September 2016.[3]
