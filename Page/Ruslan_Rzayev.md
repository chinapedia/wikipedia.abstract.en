RUSLAN RZAYEV (, ; born 24 January 1998) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Armavir on loan from FC Krasnodar.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Krasnodar-2 on 29 July 2016 in a game against FC Sochi.[1]

On 11 June 2019, he joined FC Armavir on loan.[2]
