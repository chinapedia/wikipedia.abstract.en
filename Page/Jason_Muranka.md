JASON MURANKA is a Serbian[1] rugby league footballer who plays for Keighley Cougars in Betfred League 1. He plays as a second row.[2]

Muranka has previously spent time on loan at the Gloucestershire All Golds[3] and Newcastle Thunder in Kingstone Press League 1.[4]

Muranka is a member Serbian international team.[5]
