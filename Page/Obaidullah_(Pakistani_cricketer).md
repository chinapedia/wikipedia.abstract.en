OBAIDULLAH (born 4 June 1992) is a Pakistani cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Pakistan International Airlines in the 2016–17 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy on 7 October 2016.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Peshawar in the 2017–18 National T20 Cup on 19 November 2017.[3]
