WAGNER DA SILVA SOUZA (born 30 January 1990), or simply WAGUININHO, is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a striker for Suwon Samsung Bluewings.[1]


Career

Waguininho joined the K League 2 side Bucheon FC 1995 in January 2016.[2]
