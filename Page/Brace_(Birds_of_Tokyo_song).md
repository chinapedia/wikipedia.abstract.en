"BRACE" is a song by Australian band, Birds of Tokyo. It was written by the group and released on 21 September 2016 as the lead single from the band's fifth studio album, _Brace_.[1] [2][3]

At the APRA Music Awards of 2018 the song won Rock Work of the Year.[4]
