_FELIMIDA PAULOMARCIOI_ is a species of sea slug, dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Chromodorididae.

The specific name _paulomarcioi_ is in honour of Brazilian malacologist Paulo Marcio Costa.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from Cabo Frio, Brazil.


Ecology

Prey of _Felimida paulomarcioi_ include sponges _Darwinella_ sp. and _Amphimedon viridis_. [2]
