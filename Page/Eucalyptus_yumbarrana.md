_EUCALYPTUS YUMBARRANA_, commonly known as the YUMBARRA MALLEE[1] is a mallee that is native to the south coast of South Australia.

The mallee tree is multi-stemmed typically growing to a height of with rough, grey coloured, fibrous-stringy bark on the trunk and lower stems that becomes smooth, grey to cream above. Adult leaves are a glossy green colour that are long and wide with a lanceolate to broad-lanceolate shape.[2]
