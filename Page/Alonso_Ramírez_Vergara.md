'''Alonso Ramírez Vergara, O.S. ''' (died 19 November 1602) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of La Plata o Charcas (1594–1602).[1]


Biography

Alonso Ramírez Vergara was ordained a priest in the Order of Santiago.[2] On 17 June 1594, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VIII as Bishop of La Plata o Charcas.[3] In 1595, he was consecrated bishop by Toribio Alfonso de Mogrovejo, Archbishop of Lima.[4] He served as Bishop of La Plata o Charcas until his death on 19 November 1602.[5]
