TRISTAN HODGE is a Trinidadian footballer who plays commonly as a defender; at present, he is playing for Memphis 901 on loan from W Connection F.C..

Prior to the 2017 Caribbean Cup qualification, the Trinidad and Tobago coach Stephen Hart selected a 21-man squad, Tristan Hodge being included.[1]


Club career

Hodge made his Toronto FC II debut as a half-time substitute as in a match opposed to Charlotte Independence.[2]
