ANDRÉ BJERREGAARD (born 3 September 1991) is a Danish footballer who plays for Skive IK.[1]


Club career

AC Horsens

Bjerregaard came to AC Horsens from Herning Fremad as a youth player. He signed his first professional contract at the age of 18 in July 2010.[2] Bjerregaard played 6 matches in the Danish Superliga for the club in that season.

In the next season, he extended his contract in May 2011 until 2014.[3] The striker had some injuries during the season, but played 9 league matches for the club.

He got his breakthrough in the 2011/12 season, where he played 28 league matches, scoring 2 goals. Though his low scoring-rate, he extended his contract once again. This time in January 2014 until 2016.[4]

Bjerregaard was often described as a "pestilence for the opponent" because of his effort.[5]

KR Reykjavík

On 11 July 2017, Bjerregaard was officially sold to Icelandic club KR Reykjavík.[6]

Hvidovre IF

On 3 September 2018, Bjerregaard signed for Hvidovre IF.[7]

Skive IK

Skive IK signed Bjerregaard on 12 January 2019.[8]
