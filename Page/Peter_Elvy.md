PETER ELVY (born 3 June 1980) is a retired Australian discus thrower.

He finished eleventh at the 1998 World Junior Championships,[1] and eighth at the 2002 World Cup.[2] He also competed at the 2001 Summer Universiade without reaching the final.

Elvy became Australian champion in 2002 and 2003.[3] His personal best throw was 60.45 metres, achieved in August 2000 in Sydney.[4]
