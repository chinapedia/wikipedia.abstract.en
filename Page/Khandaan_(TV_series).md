_KHANDAAN_[1] is a television series broadcast on Doordarshan in 1985,[2] starring Neena Gupta,[3] Mohan Bhandari,[4] Jayant Kriplani,[5] Dr. Shriram Lagoo, Girish Karnad , Shernaz Patel, Tinu Anand, Uma Banerjee, Sujata Mehta, among others. Screenplay-Direction: Shreedhar Ksheersagar; Dialogue: Harish Bhimani; Music: Vanraj Bhatia. For both Girish Karnad & Neena Gupta, Khandaan was the first TV serial.
