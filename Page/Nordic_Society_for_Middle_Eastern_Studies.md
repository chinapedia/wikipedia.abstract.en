The NORDIC SOCIETY FOR MIDDLE EASTERN STUDIES is a learned society formed in 1989 that is devoted to the promotion of the academic study of the Middle East in the Nordic region.[1][2] The Society arranges an academic conference on Middle Eastern studies, held every three years in a different Nordic country.[3][4] The 2016 conference was hosted by the University of Southern Denmark in Odense.[5] The current chairperson of the Nordic Society is Leif Stenberg, who is director of the Center for Middle Eastern Studies at Lund University.[6]
