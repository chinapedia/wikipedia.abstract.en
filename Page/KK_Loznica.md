'''Košarkaški klub Loznica ''' (, ), commonly referred to as KK LOZNICA, is a men's professional basketball club based in Loznica, Serbia. They are currently competing in the 4th-tier Second Regional League – Western One (Group A).[1] The club was founded in 1963.


Home arenas

The team play domestic home matches in the Lagator Hall.
