_CHARLOTTE SOMEWHAT CRAZY_ (German: _CHARLOTT ETWAS VERRÜCKT_) is a 1928 German silent comedy film directed by Adolf E. Licho and starring Lya De Putti, Livio Pavanelli and Alphons Fryland.[1]


Cast

-   Lya De Putti as Charlott Verloh
-   Livio Pavanelli as Justus Verloh
-   Alphons Fryland as Ludwig Holk
-   Anton Pointner as Cecil
-   André Mattoni as Stanley Fisher
-   Olga Limburg as Cornelia
-   Lotte Lorring as Camilla Blank
-   Hertha von Walther as Hertha
-   Lilian Weiß as Die Freundin
