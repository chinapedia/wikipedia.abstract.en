The 1938 PACIFIC TIGERS FOOTBALL TEAM represented the College of the Pacific (COP)[1] during the 1938 college football season.

COP competed in the Far Western Conference (FWC).[2] The team was led by head coach Amos Alonzo Stagg, and played home games at Baxter Stadium in Stockton, California. This was Stagg's 49th year as a head coach and his sixth at College of the Pacific. The Tigers finished with seven wins and three losses (7–3, 4–0 FWC). Overall, the Tigers outscored their opponents 203–103 for the season.[3]
