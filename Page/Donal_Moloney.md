DONAL MOLONEY (born 1966) is an Irish hurling manager and former player. He has been the joint-manager of the Clare senior team with Gerry O'Connor since 2016.[1]

On 4 June 2017, in his first Championship game in charge alongside Gerry O'Connor, Clare defeated Limerick in the Munster Senior Hurling Championship semi-final at Semple Stadium on a 2-16 to 3-17 scoreline.[2][3]
