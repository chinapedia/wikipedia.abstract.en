FRANK RICHARDSON ARMENGOL (born 21 April 1962) is a Nicaraguan former swimmer. He competed in three events at the 1976 Summer Olympics. He is the brother of Michele Richardson who represented the United States at the 1984 Summer Olympics, winning a silver medal in the women's 800 metres freestyle.[1] He is of American and Cuban descent.[2]
