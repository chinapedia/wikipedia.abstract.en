DRUV KENT is an Indian-origin musician and singer[1] based in Singapore.[2] He is known for his BBC Radio 2 approved[3] songs "Little Bit of God", "I'm Still Riding", "Glitter & Dynamite" and "What It's All About".[4][5] He has recorded his music in the UK's Rockfield Studios in Wales and at Miloco Studios in London.[6] Druv writes about themes to do with the frailty of belief, the bonds of family, the coming of age and finding your passion – all themes deeply personal to him.


Early life

Druv was born in Kolkata, India[7] and did his schooling from La Martiniere Calcutta and The Doon School, Dehradun.[8] He pursued his Master of Finance from Texas A&M University in the United States.[9]


Professional life

Druv started his career in investment banking[10] and over a period of 18 years served organizations like American Express, Credit Suisse and Deutsche Bank.[11] In 2013 Druv left his banking career and got into the music industry.[12]

Druv released his first single "Little Bit of God" in May, 2015[13]

In 2012 he also sang the song Paa Paraa Paa[14] for the Bollywood movie Aalaap.
