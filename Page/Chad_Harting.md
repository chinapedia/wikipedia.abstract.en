CHADWICK E. "CHAD" HARTING (born February 10, 1972) is a retired American pole vaulter.[1]

He won the silver medal at the 1997 Summer Universiade. He competed at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, in the men's pole vault.[2] His personal best jump was 5.80 metres, achieved indoors in January 2000 in Reno, Nevada.[3]

Harting was born in St. Louis, Missouri.[4]
