CHANDRIL SOOD (born 20 February 1991) is an Indian tennis player.

Sood has a career high ATP singles ranking of 908 achieved on 21 December 2015. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 428 achieved on 7 March 2016.

Sood made his ATP main draw debut at the 2015 Aircel Chennai Open in the doubles draw partnering his twin brother Lakshit Sood.
