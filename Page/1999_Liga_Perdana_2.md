The 1999 LIGA PERDANA 2 season is the second season of Liga Perdana 2. A total of ten teams participated in the season.[1]

Selangor and Perlis were relegated from Liga Perdana 1 while TMFC and Kelantan TNB were promoted from Malaysia FAM League to a now increased total number of teams competing in the league from eight to become ten teams.[2]

The season kicked off on 19 March 1999.[3] Johor won the title and was promoted to Liga Perdana 1 alongside Selangor.


Teams

Ten teams competing in the second season of Liga Perdana 2.[4]

-   Johor (1999 Liga Perdana 2 champions)

-   Selangor

-   Johor FC

-   Perlis

-   Kelantan

-   Malacca

-   ATM

-   TMFC

-   NS Chempaka

-   Kelantan TNB
