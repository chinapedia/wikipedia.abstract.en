JACKIE J. REEDER (born June 29, 1958) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Reeder was born in northeastern Iowa. Redder was a real estate agent; she served as a county supervisor and on the school board. A Democrat, she served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 2002 to 2003 (28th district).[1]
