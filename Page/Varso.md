VARSO is a neomodern office building under construction by real estate developer HB Reavis in Warsaw, Poland, designed by Foster and Partners.[1][2][3] The tower will also be the tallest building in Poland and in the European Union upon completion. Varso Tower is estimated to be completed by 2020.[4]
