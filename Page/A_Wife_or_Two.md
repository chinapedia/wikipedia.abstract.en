_A WIFE OR TWO_ is a 1936 British comedy film directed by Maclean Rogers and starring Henry Kendall, Nancy Burne and Betty Astell. It was made as a quota quickie at Beaconsfield Studios.[1]


Cast

-   Henry Kendall as Charles Marlowe
-   Nancy Burne as Margaret Marlowe
-   Betty Astell as Mary Hamilton
-   Fred Duprez as Sam Hickleberry
-   Garry Marsh as George Hamilton
-   Ena Grossmith
-   Wally Patch
-   Leo Sheffield
