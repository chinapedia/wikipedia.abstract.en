The GUAM NATIONAL UNDER-18 AND UNDER-19 BASKETBALL TEAM is a national basketball team of Guam, administered by the Guam Basketball Confederation.[1]

It represents the country in international under-18 and under-19 (under age 18 and under age 19) basketball competitions.
