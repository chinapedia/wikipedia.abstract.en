Light novel

The light novel series is written by Keiichi Sigsawa and illustrated by Kohaku Kuroboshi. The first light novel volume was published on January 10, 2014 by ASCII Media Works under their Dengeki Bunko imprint.[1] As of June 2014, three volumes have been published.[2]
