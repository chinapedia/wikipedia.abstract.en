BUNKER HILL is a former town in Lewis County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

Bunker Hill was laid out in 1855.[2] The community's name commemorated the Battle of Bunker Hill.[3] A post office was established at Bunker Hill in 1855, and remained in operation until 1904.[4]
