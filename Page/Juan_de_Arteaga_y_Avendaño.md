JUAN DE ARTEAGA Y AVENDAÑO (died 1540) was a Roman Catholic prelate who was appointed the second Bishop of Chiapas (1540).[1]


Biography

Juan de Arteaga y Avendaño was born in Estepa, Spain.[2] On 16 Jul 1540, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul III as Bishop of Chiapas.[3][4] He died as before he was consecrated bishop on 8 Sep 1540.[5][6]
