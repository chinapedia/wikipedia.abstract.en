ILYA VLADIMIROVICH NAUMOV (; born 12 June 1994) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Rotor Volgograd on 23 October 2016 in a game against FC Angusht Nazran.[1]
