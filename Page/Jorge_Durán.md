JORGE VICENTE DURÁN GONZÁLEZ (born April 30, 1995) is a Mexican professional football midfielder who is playing for Liga MX team Chiapas


Youth career

Jorge has represented Chiapas F.C. at U-17 and U-20 levels.


Professional career

Professionally, Jorge just was officially promoted to the first team due to him signing his very first professional contract in 2016. So far in total the midfielder has made 3 appearances in the Copa MX competition.[1]

The midfielder's debut was in a group stage game fronting Liga MX outfit Alebrijes de Oaxaca.[2] Before the game, he expressed elatedness at the opportunity as it would be his first chance to demonstrate his skills.[3]
