AROMA is an unincorporated community in Newton County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Aroma was established in 1910, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1911.[2] The area was so named on account of the Aromas strawberry crop produced there.[3]
