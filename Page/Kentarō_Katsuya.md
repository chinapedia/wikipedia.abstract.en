was a Japanese writer, translator, and stageplay script writer.[1][2]

In 1927, Katsuya visited England and spoke directly with Sir Robert Baden-Powell about the origin of the Boy Scout movement and the similarities to Satsuma Domain _terakoya_ educational system.
