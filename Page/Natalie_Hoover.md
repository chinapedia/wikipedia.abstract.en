NATALIE HOOVER is an American voice actress based in Los Angeles and Dallas. She has done work for anime, video games, and other audio productions.


Career

In 2010, Natalie was deemed as the Japanator New York Anime Festival & Comic Con voice acting contest winner.[1][2] In 2012, she was chosen to voice _Lady S_ in the short film _Kick-Heart_ by the creator and director of the film Masaaki Yuasa himself.[3]

In anime, Natalie is known as the voice of Arisa Ahokainen from the _Akiba's Trip the Animation_, Nana Sakurai from _Prince of Stride: Alternative_, Jintsu from _Kancolle_ and Georgette Lemare from _Brave Witches_. In video games, she is known for her performances as Sonia Nevermind from _Danganronpa 2_, Ryubence from _The Witch and the Hundred Knight_, Tiara from _Fairy Fencer F_, Adelheid from _Lord of Magna: Maiden Heaven_ and Ayn Felice from _Tales of Zestiria_ and its anime adaptation.
