_YES, YES, NONET_ is an album by American jazz saxophonist Lee Konitz's Nonet recorded in 1979 and released on the Danish SteepleChase label.[1][2]


Critical reception

Scott Yanow on Allmusic noted: "It's an excellent outing from a somewhat neglected group".[3]
