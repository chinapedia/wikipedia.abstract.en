MIR 'ALI MARDAN SHAH, NUZRAT OL-MOLK (1840 – 1903) was a figure in 19th-century Persia.


Personal life

Mir 'Ali Mardan Shah married Princess Ashraf us-Sultana Qajar. Issue includes Amirteymour Kalali and Hazrat Khanoum Nosrat Saltaneh.
