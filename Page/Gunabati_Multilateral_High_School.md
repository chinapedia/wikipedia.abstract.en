GUNABATI MULTILATERAL HIGH SCHOOL () is located in the heart of Gunabati Bazar. It is affiliated to the Comilla Board.


History

It was established in 1944.


Education program

In Gunabati High School, the educational activities of the SSC section under Comilla Education Board is conducted.


Co-education programs

-   Scouting
-   Sports (athletics, cricket and football)
-   Debating
-   Cultural events
-   Learning tour etc.


Uniform

The uniform of Gunabati High School is white-colored shirt and blue pant. Both long sleeve or short sleeve shirts are acceptable. Shirts must have the right-sleeve School monogram badge.
