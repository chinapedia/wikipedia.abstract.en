KAMBHAMPADU is a village in Guntur district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is located in Macherla mandal of Gurazala revenue division.[1]


Geography

Kambhampadu is situated to the northeast of the mandal headquarters, Macherla,[2] at . It is spread over an area of .[3]


Demographics

Kambhampadu has a population of 8,681 of which 4,434 are male and 4,247 are female, as per the Population Census of 2011.[4] In 2011, the literacy rate of Kambhampadu village was 54.90%, compared to 67.02% in the rest of the state. The male rate in Kambhampadu is 67.82% while the female rate is 41.61%.


Governance

Kambhampadu gram panchayat is the local self-government of the village.[5] It is divided into wards and each ward is represented by a ward member.[6]
