HELTON is an unincorporated community in Marion County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A variant name was "Helton Station".[2] A post office called Helton Station was established in 1878, and remained in operation until 1880.[3] The community was named after Judge Helton, the original owner of the town site.[4]
