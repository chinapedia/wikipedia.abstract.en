FELIX ALEMAO (born 20 July 1995) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Goa in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 13 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Goa in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 28 February 2017.[3]
