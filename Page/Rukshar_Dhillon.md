RUKSHAR DHILLON is an Indian film Actress who started her career in the South Industry [1]. She is now set to make her Bollywood  debut[2] with RSVP dance franchise film titled  _Bhangra Paa Le_ opposite Sunny Kaushal[3] .


Personal life

Rukshar is an Indian-Punjabi, born in Ealing, London. She moved to Goa and started her schooling there at Manovikas English Medium High School. After completing her education in Goa till her 9th grade, she moved to Bangalore for further studies completing her high school from Bishop Cotton Girls School and degree in Fashion designing from Bangalore University. Bangalore is her current place of residence.


Career

In early 2016, she signed _Run Antony_ opposite Vinay Rajkumar, grandson of legendary actor and singer, Dr. Rajkumar . The film was a romantic-thriller written and directed by debutant director, Raghu Shastry. The film did well and she was recognised for her acting skills, playing the role of a suicide bomber. _The Times of India_ reviewed the movie and gave her special mentions. The newspaper stated, "Rukshar excels in her role".[4]

In April 2018, her movie "Krishnarjuna Yudham" opposite Nani (actor) hit the theaters. "The Times of India" stated,"Rukshar Dhillon too looks adorable in her role".[5] "First Post" credited her role and stated "Rukshar does a fine job as a city-bred girl who goes to her village on a vacation" [6]
