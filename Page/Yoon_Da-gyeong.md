YOON DA-GYEONG () is a South Korean actress.[1] She is best known to North American audiences for her performance in the film _In Her Place_, for which she garnered a Canadian Screen Award nomination for Best Actress at the 3rd Canadian Screen Awards in 2015,[2] and a Wildflower Film Award nomination for Best Actress at the 3rd Wildflower Film Awards in 2016.[3]


Personal life

Yoon was born in Seoul on the 10th of July 1971.[4]
