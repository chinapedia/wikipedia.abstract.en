FAÏZA SOULÉ YOUSSOUF (born 1985) is a Comorian journalist and novelist. Her novel _Ghizza, à tombeau ouvert_ was published in 2015.

Youssouf is a native of Moroni.[1] She has served as the president of the Comorian branch of the Union de la presse francophone,[2] and she is the editor-in-chief of _Al-watwan_.[3] She has received awards for her writing as well.[4]
