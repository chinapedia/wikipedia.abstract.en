AMETHYST BRADLEY "SURPRISE" RALANI (born 4 October 1987) is a South African footballer who plays for Cape Town City in the Premier Soccer League.


Career

Playing in the 2016 Allsvenskan, Helsingborgs IF signed him until the conclusion of the current season.[1] He made his debut in July 2016.[2]

Ralani returned to South Africa in January 2018, signing for Premier Soccer League club Cape Town City.[3]
