TOWNLEY is an unincorporated community in Dunklin County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Townley was established in 1905, and remained in operation until 1919.[2] The community was named after one Mr. Townly, a businessperson in the lumber industry.[3]
