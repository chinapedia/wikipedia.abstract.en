_ALONE TOGETHER_ is an album by saxophonist Lee Konitz.


Background and recording

This was Konitz's first recording for Blue Note Records.[1]

The album was recorded at the Jazz Bakery in Los Angeles.[2] The tracks are standards.[3]


Reception

The _AllMusic_ reviewer stated that "all three musicians are restless and inventive, making even the simplest numbers on the disc vibrant, lively and adventurous".[4]
