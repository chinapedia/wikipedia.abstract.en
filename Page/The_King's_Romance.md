_THE KING'S ROMANCE_ is a 1914 British silent adventure film directed by Ernest G. Batley and starring Fred Morgan, Ethel Bracewell and Henry Victor.[1]


Cast

-   Fred Morgan as The Baron
-   Ethel Bracewell as Vera
-   Henry Victor as Prince Andreas
-   George Foley as Dick
-   Ethyle Batley as The Queen
