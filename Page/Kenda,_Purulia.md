KENDA is a village, with a police station, in Manbazar I CD Block in Purulia Sadar East subdivision of Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Kenda had a total population of 3,803 of which 1,922 (51%) were males and 1,881 (49%) were females. Population below 6 years was 477. The total number of literates in Kenda was 2,312 (69.51% of the population over 6 years).[1]


Police station

Kenda police station has jurisdiction over parts of Manbazar I, Puncha and Purulia I CD Blocks. The area covered is 352.81 km² and the population covered is 85,890.[2][3]


Transport

SH 5 running from Rupnarayanpur (in Bardhaman district) to Junput (in Purba Medinipur district) passes through Kenda.[4]
