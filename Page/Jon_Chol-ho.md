JON CHOL-HO (born 7 April 1969) is a North Korean male former weightlifter, who competed in the middleweight class and represented North Korea at international competitions. He won the bronze medal at the 1989 World Weightlifting Championships in the 75Â kg category. He won the bronze medal at the 1996 Summer Olympics in the 76 kg event. He also participated at the 1992 Summer Olympics in the 82.5 kg event and 2000 Summer Olympics in the 77 kg event.[1][2] He won the gold medal at the 1990 Asian Games in the Lightweight class.
