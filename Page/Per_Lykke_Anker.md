PER LYKKE ANKER (10 October 1900  – ) was a Norwegian judge.

He was born in Kristiania to engineer Christian August Anker (1869–1959) and Marie Louise Lykke. He graduated as cand.jur. in 1923, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1962 to 1970. He was a board member of the Norwegian Trekking Association from 1945 to 1951.[1]
