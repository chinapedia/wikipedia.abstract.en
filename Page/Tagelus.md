_TAGELUS_ is a genus of saltwater clams, marine bivalve molluscs belonging to the family Solecurtidae.[1]


Species

-   _Tagelus adansonii_ (Bosc, 1801)
-   _Tagelus affinis_ (C. B. Adams, 1852)
-   _Tagelus californianus_ (Conrad, 1837)
-   _Tagelus divisus_ (Spengler, 1794)
-   _Tagelus dombeii_ (Lamarck, 1818)
-   _Tagelus longisinuatus_ Pilsbry & Lowe, 1932
-   _Tagelus peruanus_ (Dunker, 1862)
-   _Tagelus peruvianus_ Pilsbry & Olsson, 1941
-   _Tagelus plebeius_ (Lightfoot, 1786)
-   _Tagelus politus_ (Carpenter, 1857)
-   _Tagelus subteres_ (Conrad, 1837)
