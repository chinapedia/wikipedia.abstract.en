Mediacorp Channel 8's television series _HERO_ is a family-drama series produced by Mediacorp Studios in 2016, starring Shaun Chen, Jesseca Liu and Chen Hanwei.

The show aired on Mediacorp Channel 8 from 29 November 2016 with 30 episodes.
