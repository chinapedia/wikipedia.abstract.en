MURRAY MCCALLUM (born 16 March 1996) is a Scottish international rugby union player who plays for Edinburgh Rugby in the Pro14.[1]

While at University of Aberdeen he studied for a BSc in Geology and Petroleum Geology.

In January 2018 he was called up to the senior Scotland squad for the 2018 Six Nations Championship.[2][3]
