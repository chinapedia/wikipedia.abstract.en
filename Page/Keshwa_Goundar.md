KESHWA GOUNDAR (born 1957) is a Fijian international lawn bowler.[1]

In 2008 he won the silver medal in the triples at the 2008 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch along with Curtis Mar and Samuela Tuikiligana.[2]
