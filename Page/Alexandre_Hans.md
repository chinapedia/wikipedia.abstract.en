ALEXANDRE HANS (born 18 June 1994) is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a defender for Portuguese club Mafra on loan from Aves.


Career

In July 2016, Hans signed a 2-year contract with Bulgarian side Pirin Blagoevgrad.[1] He managed to make only 6 appearances and his contract was terminated by mutual consent in May 2017.[2]

In June 2017, Hans signed with Portuguese club Aves and was loaned to LigaPro side Sporting da Covilhã.[3][4]
