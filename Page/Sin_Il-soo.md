SIN IL-SOO (; born 4 September 1994) is a South Korean footballer who plays as midfielder for Ansan Greeners FC.


Career

Sin Il-soo was selected by Seoul E-Land in 2015 K League draft.[1]

On 23 August 2017, Sin Il-soo signed with the LigaPro side Varzim.[2]
