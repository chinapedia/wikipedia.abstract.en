CLYDESDALE DUNCAN SKENE MM (24 June 1884 – 29 December 1945) was a Scottish amateur football centre forward who appeared in the Scottish League for Falkirk, Queen's Park and Dundee.[1][2]


Personal life

Skene was the younger brother of fellow footballer Leslie Skene and attended Falkirk High School.[3][4] He served as a corporal in the Royal Field Artillery during the First World War and was awarded the Military Medal for bravery in the field.[5]
