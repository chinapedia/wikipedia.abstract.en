CUREALL is an unincorporated community in Howell County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Cureall was established in 1860, and remained in operation until 1958.[2] The community was so named on account of a nearby mineral spring which was believed to hold medicinal qualities.[3]
