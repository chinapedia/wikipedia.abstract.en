Born in Boutilimit,[2] Ahmedou completed her primary and secondary schooling in Nouakchott, where she then studied the sciences.[3] She has written poetry, articles, and stories,[4] many of which have been published locally in Nouakchott,[5] and has produced translations of Arabic-language poetry as well.[6] In the 1990s she began work on a novel, _La couleur de vent_, which was published in 2014.[7] She has spoken about her work at literary conferences.[8]
