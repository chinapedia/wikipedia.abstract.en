FRANCISCO GONZÁLEZ DE SALCEDO CASTRO (1559–1634) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Santiago de Chile (1622–1634).[1][2][3]


Biography

Francisco González de Salcedo Castro was born in Ciudad Real, Spain in 1559.[4] On 11 Jul 1622, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Gregory XV as Bishop of Santiago de Chile.[5] In 1623, he was consecrated bishop by Jerónimo Tiedra Méndez, Archbishop of La Plata o Charcas.[6] He served as Bishop of Santiago de Chile until his death on 10 Aug 1634.[7]
