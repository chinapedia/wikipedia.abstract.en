The MEN'S TIME TRIAL C5 road cycling event at the 2016 SUMMER PARALYMPICS took place on 14 September at Flamengo Park, Pontal. Fourteen riders from twelve nations competed.[1]

The C5 CATEGORY is for cyclists with least impairment, including single amputation and minimal neurological dysfunction.
