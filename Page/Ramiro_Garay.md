RAMIRO GARAY (born 6 March 1997) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Primera B Nacional side Aldosivi.[1][2]


Career

Garay began his career in the youth ranks of Deportivo de Castelli before joining Argentine Primera División club Aldosivi in 2016.[3][4] He made his competitive Aldosivi debut on 24 February in a defeat against Estudiantes in the 2016 Argentine Primera División, during the 2016 season he was named in Aldosivi's matchday squad on five occasions but only came off the bench twice.[5][6] Three appearances followed within the next two campaigns with the last ending in relegation to Primera B Nacional.[7]
