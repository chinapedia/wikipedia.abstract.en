LEONARDO DOS SANTOS LIMA (born February 24, 1991), also known as LEOZINHO (レオジーニョ), is a Brazilian football player.[1] He plays for Tochigi Uva FC.


Career

Leozinho joined to J3 League club; SC Sagamihara in 2015. In July, he moved to FC Maruyasu Okazaki.
