'''Lukasz Kieloch '''(born 15 March 1976[1]) is a Poland born[2] German male. He was a member of the Germany men's national water polo team[3], playing as a driver. He was a part of the team at the 2004 Summer Olympics.[4] On club level he played for SV Cannstatt[5] in Germany.
