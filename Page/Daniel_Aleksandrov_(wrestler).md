DANIEL TIHOMIROV ALEKSANDROV (born September 13, 1991) is a Bulgarian Greco-Roman wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 75 kg event at the 2016 Summer Olympics, in which he was eliminated in the round of 16 by Péter Bácsi.[1][2]
