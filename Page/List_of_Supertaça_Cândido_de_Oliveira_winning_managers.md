This is a LIST OF SUPERTAÇA CÂNDIDO DE OLIVEIRA WINNING FOOTBALL MANAGERS.

Five men have won the tournament both as a player and as a manager, namely António Oliveira, Manuel Fernandes, Rui Barros, Paulo Bento and Sérgio Conceição.[1]
