AZIZ OSMAN BAKIR () served as the General Secretary of the Arab Scout Bureau.[1]

In 1978, he was awarded the 58th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[2][3]
