[1] is a Japanese model and actor.

Narita is represented with Sony Music Artists.

He started his career as an exclusive model for _Men's Non-no_ in 2013,[2][3] and his acting debut was in the drama _Flashback_ with Rin Takanashi in 2014.[4][5]
