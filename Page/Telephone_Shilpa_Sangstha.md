TELEPHONE SHILPA SANGSTHA or TSS[1] is a government owned telecoms company in Bangladesh.[2] It launched the first Laptop made/assembled in Bangladesh, Doel, in 2011.[3][4] It also manufactures analogue phones.[5] It also assembles smartphone and regular cell phones in Bangladesh.[6]
