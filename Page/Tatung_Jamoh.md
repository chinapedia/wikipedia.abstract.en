TATUNG JAMOH is an Indian politician from the state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1]

Jamoh was elected unopposed from Pasighat West seat in the 2014 Arunachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly election, standing as a People's Party of Arunachal candidate.[2][3][4] In terms of educational qualification, he is a graduate.
