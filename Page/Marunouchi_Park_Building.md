The is an office building in the Chiyoda special ward of Tokyo, Japan. Completed on April 30, 2009, it stands at 170 m (558 ft) tall.[1][2][3] Along with the Mitsubishi Shoji Building, it houses the head office of the Mitsubishi Corporation.[4]
