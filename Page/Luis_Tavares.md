LUIS TAVARES (born 8 September 1991), is a Dutch-Cape Verdean kickboxer.

As of 1 November 2018, he is ranked the #10 heavyweight in the world by Combat Press.[1]


Biography

Tavares is born in the Netherlands and is Dutch. His parents came from Cape Verde, but Tavares grew up in the Netherlands. He played football and eventually ended in Feyenoord, but later switched to kickboxing.[2]


Titles

-   2017 Enfusion Live Heavyweight Champion +95 kg.
-   2017 Enfusion Live Light Heavyweight Champion -95 kg.
-   2015 WFL -95 kg World Tournament Champion
-   2014 IKA -95 kg World Champion
-   2012 IRO -95 kg European Muay Thai Champion
