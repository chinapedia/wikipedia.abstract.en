Castle_Street,_Hertford_from_A_plan_of_Hartford_by_J._Andrews_and_M_Wren_1766.jpg THE WHITE HORSE is a public house on the south side of Castle Street, Hertford. The pub occupies numbers 31 and 33 Castle Street, two of a group of three grade II listed houses that also includes number 35. The timber-framed buildings date from the sixteenth and seventeenth centuries with later additions.[1] The pub is under the management of Fullers Brewery.[2]
