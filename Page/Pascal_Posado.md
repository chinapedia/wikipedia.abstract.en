PASCAL POSADO (May 14, 1925 – November 11, 2016) was a French politician. He served as a Communist member of the National Assembly in 1978, representing Bouches-du-Rhône.


Early life

Pascal Posado was born on May 14, 1925 in Marseille, France.[1] His parents were immigrants from Spain.[2]


Career

Posado started his career as an apprentice for the SNCASE at the age of 15.[3] Meanwhile, he joined the General Confederation of Labour and destroyed Messerschmitt aircraft made by the SNCASE.[4]

Posado joined the French Communist Party in 1944.[5] He served as a Communist member of the National Assembly from January 15, 1978 to April 2, 1978, representing Bouches-du-Rhône.[6] He also served on the city council of Marseille from 1965 to 1983, and as the mayor of the 15th and 16th arrondissements of Marseille from 1983 to 1989.[7][8] He then served on the city council of Marseille again from 1989 to 1995.[9]


Death

Posado died on November 11, 2016 in Marseille.[10][11]
