LAURENT DOMINICÉ of Switzerland served as the Treasurer of the World Scout Committee.

In 1977, Dominicé was awarded the 117th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1]
