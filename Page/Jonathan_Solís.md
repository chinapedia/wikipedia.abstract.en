JONATHAN JOSUÉ SOLÍS RIVAS (born 21 August 1993) is a Guatemalan badminton player.[1][2] In 2010, he won a gold medal at the Central American and Caribbean Games in men's team event. He was the bronze medallists at the 2013 and 2018 Pan Am Championships in the men's doubles event partnered with Rudolfo Ramírez, also in the mixed doubles event in 2018 with Diana Corleto Soto.[3][4]
