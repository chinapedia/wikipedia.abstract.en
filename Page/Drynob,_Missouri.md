DRYNOB or DRYKNOB[1][2] is an unincorporated community in Laclede County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[3]

The community is located above the west bank of the Osage Fork Gasconade River just north of the Missouri Route 32 bridge.[4][5]


History

A post office called Drynob was established in 1881, and remained in operation until 1956.[6] The community was so named on account of its lofty elevation.[7]
