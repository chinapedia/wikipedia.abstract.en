BILLION is a name for a large number. It may refer specifically to:

-   1,000,000,000 (, one thousand million), the short scale definition now standard usage in both British and American English
-   1,000,000,000,000 (, one million million), the long scale definition used formerly in Britain and currently in certain other languages

BILLION may also refer to:

-   _Billions_ (TV series), a Showtime series
-   _Billions_ (film), a 1920 silent comedy
-   Billion (company), a Taiwanese modem manufacturer
-   Jack Billion (born 1939), 2006 Democratic Party candidate for governor of South Dakota
-   _Mr. Billion_, a 1977 film by Jonathan Kaplan
-   "Billions" (song), a song on Russell Dickerson's album _Yours_
