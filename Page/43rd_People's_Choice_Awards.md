The 43RD PEOPLE'S CHOICE AWARDS, honoring the best in popular culture for 2016, were held on January 18, 2017, at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles, California, and were broadcast live on CBS. The ceremony was hosted by Joel McHale.

On November 15, 2016, the nominees were announced. The movie _Captain America: Civil War_ received the most nominations of the year with seven. The TV series _Grey’s Anatomy_ and actor Kevin Hart each received five nominations and singer, Britney Spears and _Zootopia_ received four nominations. The biggest winner of the evening was Spears, who won a total of four awards, and Ellen DeGeneres, who won three and became the most awarded person in the show's history.
