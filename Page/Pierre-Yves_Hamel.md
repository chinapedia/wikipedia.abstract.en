PIERRE-YVES HAMEL (born 3 February 1994) is a French footballer who plays as a forward. He currently plays for the French club FC Lorient.


Club career

Hamel was born in Brest, France. He played his first game in Ligue 1 against FC Nantes, and scored 14 minutes after he entered the game.[1]
