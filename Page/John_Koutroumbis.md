JOHN "JOHNNY" KOUTROUMBIS is an Australian professional football (soccer) player who plays as a defender for Newcastle Jets.


Club career

Newcastle Jets

After initially signing with Newcastle Jets as an injury replacement for Daniel Alessi,[1] on 2 February 2017, Newcastle Jets signed Koutroumbis on a two-year deal.[2]


Personal life

Koutrombis was born in Australia and is of Greek descent. In late 2018, Koutrombis was sidelined because he contracted stage 1 thyroid cancer.[3]
