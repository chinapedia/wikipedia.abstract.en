GIACOMO ANTONIO CARROZZA (died 1560) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Conversano (1534–1560).[1][2]


Biography

In 1534, Giacomo Antonio Carrozza was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VII as Bishop of Conversano.[3][4] He served as Bishop of Conversano until his death in 1560.[5][6]
