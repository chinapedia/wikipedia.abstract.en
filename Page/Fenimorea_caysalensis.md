_FENIMOREA CAYSALENSIS_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of this marine shell varies between 8 mm and 13 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off the Bahamas and the Florida Keys.
