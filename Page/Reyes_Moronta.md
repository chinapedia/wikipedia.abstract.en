REYES ARMANDO MORONTA (born January 6, 1993) is a Dominican professional baseball pitcher for the San Francisco Giants of Major League Baseball (MLB).


Career

Moronta signed with the San Francisco Giants as an international free agent in September 2010. He made his professional debut in 2011 with the Dominican Summer League Giants. He played for the Arizona League Giants in 2012, Salem-Keizer Volcanoes in 2013, Arizona League Giants in 2014, Augusta GreenJackets in 2015 and San Jose Giants in 2016. The Giants added him to their 40-man roster after the 2016 season.[1] Moronta was called up to the Major Leagues on May 10, 2017.[2] During the 2017 season, Moronta pitched in 7 games for the Giants recording 11 strikeouts in 6 innings pitched.
