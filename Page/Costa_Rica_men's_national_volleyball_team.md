The COSTA RICA MEN'S NATIONAL VOLLEYBALL TEAM represents Costa Rica in international volleyball competitions and friendly matches. The team is currently ranked 42 in the world. It is governed by the Federación Costarricense de Voleibol (Costa Rican Federation of Volleyball) and takes part in international volleyball competitions. Costa Rica has multiple championships in the Centroamerican area organized by the AFECAVOL.
