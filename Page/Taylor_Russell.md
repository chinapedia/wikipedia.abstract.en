TAYLOR RUSSELL (born July 18, 1994) is a Canadian actress. She is known for her recurring roles in the television series _Strange Empire_ and _Falling Skies_.[1] As of 2018, she stars as Judy Robinson in _Lost in Space_, the Netflix remake of the original 1965 TV series.[2] She also had a lead role in the thriller ''Escape Room (2019)
