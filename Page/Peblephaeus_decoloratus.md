_PEBLEPHAEUS DECOLORATUS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Bernhard Schwarzer in 1925, originally under the genus _Blepephaeus_.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Peblephaeus decoloratus decoloratus_ (Schwarzer, 1925)
-   _Peblephaeus decoloratus yonagunii_ (Breuning & Ohbayashi, 1966)
