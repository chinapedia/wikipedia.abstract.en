_TEA LEAVES IN THE WIND_ is a 1938 British drama film directed by Ward Wing and starring Nils Asther, Eve Shelley and Gibson Gowland. It was shot on location in Ceylon.[1]


Cast

-   Nils Asther as Tony Drake
-   Eve Shelley as Margot Hastings
-   Gibson Gowland as David Webster
-   Cyril Chadwick
-   Needham Clarke
-   Robert Anthony
-   Marjorie Preece
