SLYDE HANDBOARDS is a bodysurfing and water sports company.[1] It was co-founded in 2010 by Steve Watts and Russell Ellers, and they were later joined by Angela Watts.[2] Slyde secured a round of funding on the seventh season of _Shark Tank_ from Ashton Kutcher and Mark Cuban, who invested $200,000 in exchange for 22% equity of the company.[3]
