KARUNA NUNDY is an Indian lawyer.[1][2][3] She was included by an _Economic Times_ jury in a list of 'Corporate India's Fastest Rising Women Leaders' which cited her as being 'famous in the corporate world for her expertise in commercial law'.[4][5]


Early life and education

Karuna Nundy received a degree in Economics from St. Stephen's College, Delhi University.[6][7] After a short stint as a TV journalist[8][9] she studied law at Cambridge University,[10][11] She later pursued LL.M from Columbia University, New York.[12]


Career

Nundy worked as a lawyer in United Nations.[13] She is a lawyer at the Supreme Court of India and the focus of her work is on constitutional law, commercial litigation and arbitration, media law and legal policy.[14][15]
