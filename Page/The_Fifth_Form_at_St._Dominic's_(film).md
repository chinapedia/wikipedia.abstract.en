_THE FIFTH FORM AT ST. DOMINIC'S_ is a 1921 British silent drama film directed by A. E. Coleby.[1] It is based on the novel _The Fifth Form at St. Dominic's_ by Talbot Baines Reed.

-   Clifford Cobbe as Mr. Rastle

-   Phyllis Shannaw as Nancy Senior
-   Cecil Susands as Bullinger
-   Maurice Thompson as Stephen Greenfield
