VLADIMIR GJONI (born 15 January 1970) is an Albanian football manager and former player.[1][2]


Managerial career

He was put in charge of hometown club Burreli in December 2013[3] and in June 2014, Gjoni's contract as coach was extended after steering the club to safety in his first half season at the club.[4] In December 2018, he was appointed manager of Besëlidhja Lezhë.[5]
