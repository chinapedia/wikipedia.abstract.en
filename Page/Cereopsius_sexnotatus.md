_CEREOPSIUS SEXNOTATUS_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by James Thomson in 1865. It is known from Malaysia and Java.[1]


Varietas

-   _Cereopsius sexnotatus var. octonotatus_ Breuning, 1944
-   _Cereopsius sexnotatus var. quadriplagiatus_ Breuning, 1944
