JEDDO is an unincorporated community in Knox County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]

The first settlement at Jeddo was made in the 1830s.[2] When the railroad bypassed Jeddo in 1872, business activities shifted to other nearby places, and the population dwindled.[3]
