SRINIDHI RAMESH SHETTY (born March 21, 1992) is an Indian model, actress and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned as Miss Supranational India in Miss Diva 2016 pageant and later represented India at Miss Supernational 2016, which she won. She is the second Indian representative to win the title. She is also an actress in the Kannada cinema.


Early life

Srinidhi Ramesh Shetty was born on 21 October 1992[1] in a Tulu speaking Tuluva Bunt family.[2][3] Her father, Ramesh Shetty hails from the town of Mulki and her mother, Kushala is from Thalipady Guthu, in Kinnigoli.[4] She did her schooling in Sri Narayana Guru English Medium School, followed by a pre-university course at St. Aloysius Pre-University College. She received her degree in Electrical engineering at Jain University, Bangalore and graduated with distinction.[5]


Pageantry

In 2012, she competed in the Clean & Clear Fresh Face contest, where she was among the top 5 finalists.[6] Later, she participated in Manappuram Miss South India in 2015 and won the titles of Miss Karnataka and Miss Beautiful Smile[7] and later participated in Manappuram Miss Queen where she was crowned as the 1st runner up.[8][9] While she was employed in Accenture, she worked with many designers as a model.[10]

In 2016, she participated in Miss Diva - 2016 contest, where she was selected as a finalist among thousands of aspirants. She went on to win the Miss Supranational India 2016 title at the pageant.[11][12] She also won 3 subtitles for her smile, body and her photogenic face. She went on to represent India at Miss Supranational 2016 pageant.[13] On 2 December 2016, she was crowned as Miss Supranational 2016 by her predecessor Stephania Stegman of Paraguay in Municipal Sports and Recreation Center, Krynica-Zdr√≥j, Poland. She also won the title of Miss Supranational 'Asia and Oceania 2016' in the pageant.[14]

During her reign as Miss Supranational 2016, Srinidhi traveled to United Arab Emirates,[15] France,[16] Japan,[17] Singapore,[18] Thailand,[19] Slovakia[20] and numerous trips around Poland and India.[21][22]


Career

After winning Miss Supranational, Srinidhi began to receive offers to act in films.[23] She was signed up in a Kannada movie named K.G.F., which is her acting debut.[24] The movie was directed by Prashanth Neel and is the most expensive film of Kannada cinema.[25]
