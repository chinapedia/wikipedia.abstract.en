_DRILLIA SOWERBYI_ is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description

The length of the shell attains 11 mm, its diameter 4.5 mm.


Distribution

This marine species occurs off KwaZulu-Natal. South Africa
