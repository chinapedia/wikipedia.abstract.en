ELIAS PAUL HUTH (born 10 February 1997) is a German footballer who plays as a centre-forward for 1. FC Kaiserslautern.[1]

In June 2017, Huth joined 3. Liga club Rot-Weiß Erfurt on a season-long loan.[2]
