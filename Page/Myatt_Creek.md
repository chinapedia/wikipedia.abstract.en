MYATT CREEK is a stream in northeastern Fulton County, Arkansas and southeastern Howell County, Missouri.[1]

The headwaters of Myatt Creek are in Howell County, Missouri, at and the confluence with the Spring River is in Fulton County, Arkansas, at .[2]

Myatt Creek has the name of a pioneer settler who is said to be buried along its course.[3]
