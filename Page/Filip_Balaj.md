FILIP BALAJ (born 2 August 1997) is a Slovak footballer who plays for Fortuna Liga club MŠK Žilina.


Club career

FC Nitra

He made his professional Fortuna Liga debut for FC Nitra against MŠK Žilina in July 22, 2017.[1]
