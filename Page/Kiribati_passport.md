The KIRIBATI PASSPORT is an international travel document issued for Kiribati citizens.


Visa requirements

By February 2016, Kiribati had visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 79 countries and dependencies, making the Kiribati passport ranking 60th on the Visa Restrictions Index.[1]
