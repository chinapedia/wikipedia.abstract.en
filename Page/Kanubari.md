KANUBARI is a Tehsil in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh.[1] Tirap is the name of the district that contains Kanubari.

Kanubari is located 37 km towards west from District headquarters Khonsa. It is 173 km from State capital Itanagar towards West. It is one of the 60 constituencies of Legislative Assembly of Arunachal Pradesh. Name of last MLA of this constituency is GABRIEL D. WANGSU. He resigned from his post in September 2015 and the position is vacant since then.
