PIERRE VALLON (10 November 1927 – 24 October 2016) was a French businessman and politician. He was an heir to Vallon et Compagnie, a silk company based in La Croix-Rousse, Lyon.[1] He served as a member of the French Senate from 1974 to 1995.[2][3]
