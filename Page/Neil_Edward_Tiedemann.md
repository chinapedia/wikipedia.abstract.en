NEIL EDWARD TIEDEMANN (born March 5, 1948) is an American prelate of the Roman Catholic Church serving as an auxiliary bishop for the Diocese of Brooklyn since 2016.


Biography

On May 16, 1975, Tiedemann was ordained to the priesthood. He served as the Bishop Bishop of Mandeville in Jamaica from 2008-2016. Pope Francis appointed Tiedemann auxiliary bishop for the Diocese of Brooklyn on May 31, 2016.[1] [2]
