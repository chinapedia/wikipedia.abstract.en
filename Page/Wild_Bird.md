_WILD BIRD_ is an album by American pianist Hal Galper released on the Mainstream label in 1972.[1][2][3]


Track listing

All compositions by Hal Galper.

2.  "Trilogy: Wild Bird" – 8:03
3.  "Trilogy: Change Up" – 5:04
4.  "This Moment" – 11:40
5.  "Whatever" – 7:14
