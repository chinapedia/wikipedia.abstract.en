ACORN CORNER is an unincorporated community in Pemiscot County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

The community once contained Acorn Corner School, now defunct.[2] The schoolhouse was named for a grove of acorn-bearing oak trees near the original town site.[3]
