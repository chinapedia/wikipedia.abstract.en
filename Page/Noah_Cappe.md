NOAH CAPPE is a Canadian actor and television host best known for his work on _Good Witch_, _Carnival Eats_ and _The Bachelorette Canada_.


Career

Cappe's most notable acting role is that of Derek Sanders, a role that he began in a series of _The Good Witch_ TV movies before reprising the role on the television series.[1]

Cappe's most notable hosting positions are on the Food Network's _Carnival Eats_,[2][3] and the W Network's _The Bachelorette Canada_,[4] a job he received thanks in part to a tweet he made expressing his excitement for the upcoming show and asking if they needed a host.[5]
