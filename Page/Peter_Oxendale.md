PETER OXENDALE (b. ) is an English forensic musicologist and an expert witness on copyright infringement in music.[1] He was involved as an expert in the notable Blurred Lines lawsuit.[2][3] He was a keyboardist in the glam rock bands Sparks and Jet and musical director for Chris de Burgh.[4] Oxendale also played keyboards on Ian Hunter's _Overnight Angels_ album in 1977. He also played keyboards for 1980s pop group Dead Or Alive, and Frankie Goes To Hollywood. In 1983, he served as touring keyboardist for John Foxx.
