AARON WILSON (born 24 November 1991) is an Australian international lawn bowler.[1]

Wilson won the 2016 World Junior Championships[2] and won the gold medal with bowls partner Brett Wilkie in the pairs at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship and won a silver medal in the fours.[3]

Wilson was selected as part of the Australian team for the 2018 Commonwealth Games on the Gold Coast in Queensland, where he won a gold medal in the singles. He is currently high performance coach of the Cabramatta club in Sydney.[4]
