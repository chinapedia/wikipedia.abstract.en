ARTHUR EUGSTER of Switzerland served as the Regional Treasurer of the European Regional Scout Committee.

In 1975, Eugster was awarded the 98th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting, at the 25th World Scout Conference.[1]
