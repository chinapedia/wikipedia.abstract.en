HARRISTON is an unincorporated community in Cooper County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

Harriston was laid out in 1873 by Dr. N. W. Harris, and named for him.[2] A post office called Harriston was established in 1873, and remained in operation until 1908.[3]
