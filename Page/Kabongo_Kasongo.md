KABONGO KASONGO (born 8 July 1994) is a DR Congolese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Zamalek.


Club career

In 13 June 2017, he moved from Al Ittihad to Zamalek for a fee of $830,000.

On July 2016, Kasongo had a short trial at Saint-Étienne, but they didn't give him a contract and his Visa in France expired, forcing him to join Al Ittihad one month later.


International

He made his debut for Democratic Republic of the Congo national football team on 18 November 2018 in an African Cup of Nations qualifier against Congo and scored a goal on his debut in a 1–1 draw.[1]
