LAL CHINTAMANI SHARAN NATH SHAHDEO (1931 - 10 July 2014), was the last Nagvanshi Maharaja of Chotanagpur.[1] [2][3]

  --
  --


Biography

Lal Chintamani Sharan Nath Shahdeo was born in a royal family of Nagvanshi dynasty in 1931. He studied at Raj Kumar College in Raipur. In 1950, he succeeded his great-grandfather Uday Pratap Nath Shahdeo as the maharaj of Chotanagpur princely state.[4]

He was an Independent MLA from the Ranchi Assembly constituency in 1957 and was the youngest MLA in the Bihar Vidhan Sabha. Later, he was elected to the Bihar Legislative Council as a Congress candidate.[5] [6]

He died in Ranchi on 9 July 2014 following a brief illness.[7]
