Biography

On 30 May 1457, Giacomo Filippo Crivelli was appointed during the papacy of Pope Callixtus III as Bishop of Novara.[2][3]' He served as Bishop of Novara until his death in 1466.[4][5]

While bishop, he was the principal consecrator of Walter Supersaxo, Bishop of Sion (1459).[6]
