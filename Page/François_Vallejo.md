François_Vallejo_(2018).jpg FRANÇOIS VALLEJO (1960, Le Mans) is a French professor of literature and a writer.

Passionate about Claudel, then by Louis-Ferdinand Céline, François Vallejo studied letters. He became a professor of classical literature at Le Havre and began writing novels at the end of the 1990s.

In 2001, his novel _Madame Angeloso_ was part of the second selection of the Prix Goncourt and was also selected for the Femina and the Renaudot.
