_' Alme_' (or Almé) is a village in the commune of Mayo-Baléoin Adamawa Region, Cameroon, near the .


Population

In 1971 Alme contained 300 inhabitants, mostly Kutin.[1]

In the 2005 census, 1813 people were counted in the village of Alme and 4651 in the canton of the same name[2]
