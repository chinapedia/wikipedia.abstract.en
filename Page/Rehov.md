REHOV () is a moshav in northern Israel. Located four kilometres south of Beit She'an, it falls under the jurisdiction of Valley of Springs Regional Council. In it had a population of .


History

The moshav was established in 1951 by immigrants from Kurdistan and Morocco. The name was taken from the ancient city of Tel Rehov, which was located in the area.

It is located on the land of the depopulated Palestinian village of Farwana.[1]
