GISELA RIERA ROURA (born 7 May 1976) is a former Spanish tennis player.

Riera won four singles and eight doubles ITF titles during her career and on 7 June 1999 reached a singles ranking high of world number 136. On 12 June 2000, Riera reached a doubles ranking high of world number 71. Riera retirement from tennis 2010.


Personal life

Born in Barcelona, Riera started playing tennis at age 8. She prefers clay over hard courts. Her father's name is Jose Maria; mother's name is Angel; has four older siblings: Jose Maria, Carina, Leo and Rafael. She likes scuba diving and watching men's tennis.
