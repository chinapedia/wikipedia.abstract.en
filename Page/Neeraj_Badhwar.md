NEERAJ BADHWAR (Hindi:नीरज बधवार) (born in 15 February 1985) is a journalist, satirist and columnist in _The Times of India_.[1][2]


Early life

Neeraj Badhwar was born in Sri Ganganagar.[3] After complete his Graduation, he came to Delhi and work as a Deputy Editor of Sahara Samay.[4] He writes regular column in many newspapers like Dainik Jagran, Navbharat Times etc.[5] He has written dialogues in Bollywood movie BHK Bhalla@Halla.Kom.[6]
