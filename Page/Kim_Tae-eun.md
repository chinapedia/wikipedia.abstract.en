KIM TAE-EUN (; born 21 September 1989) is a South Korean footballer who plays as a full-back for Daejeon Korail.[1]


Career

Kim Tae-eun was selected by Incheon United in 2011 K League draft.

In July 2015, he signed with Seoul E-Land after a four-year spell in Korea National League.[2]
