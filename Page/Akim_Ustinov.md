AKIM YURYEVICH USTINOV (; born 26 May 1999) is a Russian football player who plays for FC Strogino Moscow.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Strogino Moscow on 16 October 2016 in a game against FSK Dolgoprudny.[1]
