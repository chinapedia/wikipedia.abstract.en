DREW is an unincorporated community in southern Laclede County, Missouri, United States.[1] Drew is located on State Route B on a ridge east of Cobb Creek.[2] The townsite is at an elevation of .[3]


History

A post office called Drew was established in 1892, and remained in operation until 1930.[4] The community has the name of S. E. Drew, a pioneer citizen.[5]
