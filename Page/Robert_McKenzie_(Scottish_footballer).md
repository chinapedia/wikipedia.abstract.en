ROBERT COPELAND MCKENZIE was a Scottish amateur football right half who appeared in the Scottish League for Queen's Park.[1][2] He was capped by Ireland at amateur level.[3]


Personal life

McKenzie served as a captain in the Royal Army Medical Corps during the First World War.[4]
