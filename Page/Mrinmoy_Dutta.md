MRINMOY DUTTA is an Indian cricketer who plays for Assam.[1] He made his first-class debut for Assam in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 21 November 2016.[2] He was the leading wicket-taker for Assam in the 2018–19 Vijay Hazare Trophy, with seven dismissals in five matches.[3]
