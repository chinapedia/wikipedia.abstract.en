_AGERATINA VIBURNOIDES_ is a species of plant in the aster family.


Etymology

_Ageratina_ is derived from Greek meaning 'un-aging', in reference to the flowers keeping their color for a long time. This name was used by Dioscorides for a number of different plants.[1]
