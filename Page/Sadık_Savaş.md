SADIK SAVAŞ (born August 16, 1987) is a Turkish Paralympian archer competing in the Men's recurve bow event.[1]


Early life

Sadık Savaş was born on August 16, 1987.[2] He lives in Ankara, Turkey.[3]


Sporting career

Savaş began his archery career in 2010, and debuted internationally in 2013. He has been coached by Oğuzhan Polat since 2012.[4]

He obtained a quota for the 2016 Summer Paralympics Games in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
