_GARGAMELLA BLOKOVERDENSIS_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, shell-less marine opisthobranch gastropod mollusks in the family Discodorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from the Parda reef, Sal island, Cape Verde. The specimens were found in the intertidal zone or up to 1 m depth.[2]


Description

_Gargamella blokoverdensis_ is a small dorid nudibranch, growing to 16 mm in length. It is orange in colour with spots of brown.[3]
