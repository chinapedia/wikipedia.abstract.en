Kloster_Gravenhorst_Annette_Kurschus_01.JPG ANNETTE KURSCHUS (born 14 February 1963 in Rotenburg an der Fulda) is a German Praeses, (or in German _Präses_), of the Evangelical Church of Westphalia. She is also Vice-President of the Council of the EKD.


Life

Kurschus studied theology at the University of Bonn, at the University of Marburg, at the University of Münster and at the _Kirchliche Hochschule Wuppertal/Bethel_. In 2012, she followed Alfred Buß as _präses_ of Evangelical Church of Westphalia.[1] She is since 2015 member of EKD-Rat.[2]
