_MIKOSCH COMES IN_ (German: _MIKOSCH RÜCKT EIN_) is a 1928 German silent comedy film directed by Rolf Randolf and starring Gyula Szőreghy, Lydia Potechina and Claire Rommer.[1]

The film's sets were designed by the art director Ernö Metzner.
