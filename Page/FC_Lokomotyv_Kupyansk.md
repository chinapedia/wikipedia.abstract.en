FC LOKOMOTYV KUPYANSK was an amateur club from Kupyansk competing at the regional competitions of Kharkiv Oblast.

The club was one of the oldest in the country being founded in 1923. With the fall of the Soviet Union, the club was dissolved.

In 1997 Lokomotyv Kupyansk was revived,[1] but on 2 March 2016 it was announced that the club is closed.[2][3]
