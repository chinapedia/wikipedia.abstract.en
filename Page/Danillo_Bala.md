DANILLO SOUZA MUNIZ (born 1 January 1993) is a Brazilian footballer who plays for Remo.


Career

In June 2016, Bala signed with Bulgarian side Montana; the duration of the contract remained undisclosed.[1] He was released on 22 November 2016 after featuring rarely for the team.[2]

In December 2016, Bala joined Juazeirense, signing a 1-year contract.[3]
