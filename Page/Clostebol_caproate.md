CLOSTEBOL CAPROATE (brand name MACROBIN-DEPOT), also known as CLOSTEBOL HEXANOATE or CHLOROTESTOSTERONE CAPROATE (JAN), as well as 4-CHLOROTESTOSTERONE 17Β-CAPROATE or as 4-CHLOROANDROST-4-EN-17Β-OL-3-ONE 17Β-CAPROATE, is a synthetic, injected anabolic-androgenic steroid (AAS) and a derivative of testosterone.[1][2] It is an androgen ester – specifically, the C17β caproate ester of clostebol (4-chlorotestosterone) – and acts as a prodrug of clostebol in the body.[3] Clostebol acetate is administered via intramuscular injection.[4]
