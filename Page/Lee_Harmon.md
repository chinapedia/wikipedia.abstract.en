LEE HARMON (born 20 October 1967)[1][2] is a football executive from Cook Islands.[3]

Since 1997, Lee Harmon has been the president of the Cook Islands Football Association (CIFA). He was last re-elected in April 2016.[4] Furthermore, he has been a member of the FIFA Council since September 2016.[5]
