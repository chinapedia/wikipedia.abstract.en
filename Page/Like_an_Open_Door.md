"LIKE AN OPEN DOOR" is a song written by Sheila Young and performed by The Fuzz. It reached #14 on the U.S. R&B chart and #77 on the _Billboard_ Hot 100 in 1971.[1] The song was featured on their 1971 album, _The Fuzz_.[2]

The song was produced by Joe Tate.[3]
