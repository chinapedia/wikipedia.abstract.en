OYUNBAATAR MIJIDDORJ (; born 22 August 1996) is a Mongolian footballer who plays for the Khangarid FC of the Mongolian Premier League, and the Mongolian national team.[1]


Club career

Mijiddorj has played for Khangarid FC of the Mongolian Premier League since 2010.[2] He was the league's top scorer for the 2016 season with 29 goals in 18 appearances.[3] He also received the award for top forward in the league that year.[4]

In July 2017 he joined Mongolian side Ulaanbaatar City FC along with fellow national team player Murun Altankhuyag.[5]
