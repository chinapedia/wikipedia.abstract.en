PERUMALMALAI is a village in Kodaikanal Taluk, Dindigul District, It is located in the Western side of Dindigul [1][2]


Location

The hillock located about 10 km from Erode Junction and 8 km from Erode Central Bus Terminus on the way to Bhavani. Located amidst the Kalingarayan Canal and River Cauvery. It is about 5 km from Bhavani New Busstand. There is a PUMS school in Perumalmalai.[3][4]
