CAIRNBAINE, also called TIREDIGAN COURT TOMB, is a court cairn and National Monument located in County Monaghan, Ireland.[1][2]


Location

Cairnbaine is located southwest of Three Mile House.[3]


History

Court cairns are the oldest kind of megalithic monument in Ireland, being built from 4000 BC onward.[4]


Description
