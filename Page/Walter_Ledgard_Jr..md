WALTER LEDGARD JR. (born 3 January 1945) is a Peruvian former swimmer. He competed in three events at the 1964 Summer Olympics.[1]

He is the father of Olympic cyclist Tony Ledgard and the son of Olympic swimmer Walter Ledgard Sr.
