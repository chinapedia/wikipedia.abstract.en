The CASA DE PARLEY JOHNSON is a historic house in Downey, California, U.S.. It was built for Alexander Parley Johnson, an heir to citrus fields, real estate and oil investments, in 1927.[1] It was designed in the Spanish Revival architectural style by Roland Coate.[2] It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since March 20, 1986.[3]
