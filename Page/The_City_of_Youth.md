_THE CITY OF YOUTH_ is a 1928 British silent drama film directed by E. H. Calvert and starring Betty Faire, Lilian Oldland and J. Fisher White.[1]


Cast

-   Betty Faire as Barbara
-   Lilian Oldland as Brownie
-   J. Fisher White as Patrick Enderby
-   Desmond Roberts
