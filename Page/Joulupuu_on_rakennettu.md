JOULPUU ON RAKENNETTU (_"Christmas tree has been built"_) is one of the most known Finnish christmas carols.


Lyrics

The song was first published as "Joulu-kuusi" in 1876 in the book _"Uusi Kuwa-Aapinen"_ [1]
