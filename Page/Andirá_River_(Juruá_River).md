The ANDIRÁ RIVER () is a river of Amazonas, Brazil. It is a tributary of the Juruá River.

The river forms the western boundary of the Tefé National Forest, created in 1989.[1] It forms the southwest boundary of the Baixo Juruá Extractive Reserve, created in 2001.[2]
