CLIVE ROSS (born 14 June 1989) is an Irish rugby union player who was educated at Midleton College and plays for Ulster as a flanker.


Ulster

Ross joined Ulster in Summer 2014 after playing for two seasons at Lansdowne where he won the All-Ireland League. He made his Ulster debut in the 13-6 loss away to Zebre, on 27 September 2014.[1] He is the cousin of Irish international Mike Ross.
