JAMEL CHISHOLM (born 11 August 1995) is a Jamaica international rugby league footballer,[1] who plays as a er for Oldham in Kingstone Press League One.[2]

He is a former rugby union player.[3]


Background

Chisholm was born in London, England.


Career

Chisholm has previously played for the Hunslet Hawks on dual-registration from the Leeds Rhinos.[4] He has also played for the Featherstone Rovers,[5] Hemel Stags,[6] York City Knights[7] and the London Skolars.[8]

Chisholm was Super Leagues fastest man.[9][10]

Chisholm has played rugby union for Leeds Carnegie.[11]
