SHANGHAI SUNFUN FOOTBALL CLUB () is a professional Chinese football club that currently participates in the China League Two. The team is based in Shanghai.


History

Shanghai Sunfun F.C. was founded on 30 December 2015 as Shanghai Sun&Fan F.C.().[1] They changed their English name to Shanghai Sunfun F.C. in 2016 while Chinese name remained the same. They played in the 2016 China Amateur Football League and won the winners of 2016 Shanghai Chen Yi Cup and the first place of southeast region final–group A successively.[2] Shanghai Sunfun finished the 4th place in the national finals and won promotion to 2017 China League Two.[3]


Managerial history

-   Cheng Liang (2016–2017)

-   Teshima Atsushi (2018)

-   Zhang Zhigang (2018)
