'''Hellen Mubanga ''' (born 23 May 1995) is a Zambian footballer who plays as a forward [1] for the Zambia women's national football team.[2][3][4][5] She was part of the team at the 2014 African Women's Championship. On club level she played for Bauleni Sports Academy in Zambia.[6]
