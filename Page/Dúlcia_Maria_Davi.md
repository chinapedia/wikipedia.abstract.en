DÚLCIA MARIA DAVI (born 18 January 1982), commonly known as DULCE, is a Brazilian footballer who plays for São José EC as a defender.

She was part of the Equatorial Guinea women's national football team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1]

On October 5, 2017, FIFA declared Dulce and other nine Brazilian footballers ineligible to play for Equatorial Guinea.[2]
