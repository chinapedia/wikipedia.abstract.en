_COLD OF KALANDAR_ () is a 2015 Turkish drama film directed by Mustafa Kara. It was selected as the Turkish entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 89th Academy Awards but it was not nominated.[1][2]


Cast

-   Haydar Sisman
-   Nuray Yesilaraz
-   Hanife Kara
-   Ibrahim Kuvvet
-   Temel Kara


Awards

At the 2016 Haifa International Film Festival, _Cold of Kalandar_ received two awards: the "Golden Anchor" Award (Best Film in the Mediterranean Film Competition) and the Fedeora Award by the Federation of Film Critics of Europe and the Mediterranean for Best Foreign Film.[3] It also won the award for Best Film at the 2016 Asia Pacific Screen Awards.[4]
