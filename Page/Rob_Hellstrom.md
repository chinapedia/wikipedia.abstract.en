ROBERT DOUGLAS HELLSTROM (born 9 November 1977) is a New Zealand rower.

Hellstrom was born in 1977 in Palmerston North, New Zealand.[1] He is a member of Horowhenua Rowing Club.[2] He represented New Zealand at the 2000 Summer Olympics. He is listed as New Zealand Olympian athlete number 794 by the New Zealand Olympic Committee.[3] He lives in Picton.[4]
