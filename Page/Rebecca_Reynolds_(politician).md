REBECCA REYNOLDS-KNIGHT (born May 31, 1949) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Reynolds was born in Evanston, Illinois and attended Valley College in California. A Democrat, she served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1997 to 2002 (94th district).[1]
