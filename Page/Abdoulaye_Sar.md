ABDOULAYE SAR served as the Director of Community Development of the World Scout Bureau.

In 1997, he was awarded the 262nd _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1][2]
