BARIKUL is a village, with a police station, in Ranibandh CD Block in Khatra subdivision of Bankura district in the state of West Bengal, India.


Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Barikul had a total population of 988 of which

-   Males= 494 (50%)
-   Females= 494 (50%)
-   Population below 6 years = 97.

The total number of literates in Barikul was 611 (68.57% of the population over 6 years).[1]
