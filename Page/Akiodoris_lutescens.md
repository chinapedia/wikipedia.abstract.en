_AKIODORIS LUTESCENS_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusc in the family Onchidorididae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from a specimen collected by William Healey Dall in Nazan Bay at Atka Island, Aleutian Islands.[2] It has been redescribed from specimens collected in the Sea of Okhotsk and the Commander Islands, Russia.[3]
