Hartelholz_GO-3.jpg Túnel_natural,_Hartelholz,_Múnich,_Alemania,_2016-04-03,_DD_06.JPG HARTELHOLZ is a forest in the north of Munich, Germany.

It is located north of the Panzerwiese in the boroughs of Feldmoching-Hasenbergl and Milbertshofen-Am Hart.

It is classified as a Naturschutzgebiet protected area.[1]
