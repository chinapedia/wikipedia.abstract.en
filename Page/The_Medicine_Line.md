_THE MEDICINE LINE_ is a Canadian documentary television series that was created and produced by Farpoint Films and premiered in Canada in 2014 on APTN.[1] The show starred Dave Gaudet.

The show won a Golden Sheaf Award for Best Documentary Series at the 2014 Yorkton Film Festival.[2][3][4]
