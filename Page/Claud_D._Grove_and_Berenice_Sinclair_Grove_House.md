CLAUD D. GROVE AND BERENICE SINCLAIR GROVE HOUSE, also known as the HAGENER HOUSE and '''Edward G. Sinclair House ''', is a historic home located at Jefferson City, Cole County, Missouri. It was built about 1912, and is a two-story, Colonial Revival style brick dwelling with a projecting center gable. It has a slate hipped roof with shed dormer. It features a one-story front porch supported by one square and two round Tuscan order columns.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2002.[2]
