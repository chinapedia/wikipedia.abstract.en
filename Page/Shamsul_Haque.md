_' Shamsul Haque_' is a Bangladesh Army General who is the QMG of Bangladesh Army. He was the Director General of National Security Intelligence, the principal intelligence agency of Bangladesh.[1][2] He was the previous chairman of Sena Kalyan Sangstha.[3] He was made the chairman of Sena Kalyan Sangstha in 15 May 2014.[4] He has also served as the Defence Advisor in Bangladesh Permanent Mission of United Nations Headquarters, New York. He also served as Commanding Officer of 6 Rifle Battalion in Bangladesh Rifles (Now Border Guard Bangladesh). Under him construction of a new headquarters of the NSI in Segun Bagicha started in 2015.[5] He replaced Director General Major General M Manzur Ahmed.[6]
