BECCA SCHACK, known by her stage name BECA (also Beca Dreams)[1] is an American singer, songwriter, and producer from New York.[2]


Early life

Beca grew up in Upstate New York. In her youth she played the piano, sang, and studied many genres of music including jazz and classical.[3]

At age 18 she moved to Manhattan to study music composition at Juilliard with composers Samuel Adler, Christopher Rouse, and Elliott Sharp.[4]

She found herself drawn to the club scene and started playing with various indie bands.


Music career

Her two first EPs _Let's Run Wild_ and _Born to Fly_ were released via UK label This Is Music Ltd in 2012.[5] The songs on _Let's Run Wild_ were co-written and co-produced by Findlay Brown.[6]

Her debut album, _Ecliptic_, was independently released in 2015.[7] The album accrued radio spins from over 50 stations nationwide including Los Angeles' KCRW.[8] Her song "Meteor" charted on Beatport's Top 100 Indie Dance Releases[9] and debuted in the Top 90 of the FMQB (Friday Morning Quarterback) Singles Chart.[10]

Beca has performed at festivals and events, including Miami Fashion Week,[11] POP Montreal[12] and North by Northeast (NXNE).[13]

Beca has appeared in many international media publications including: _Stereogum_,[14] _Flaunt_ magazine,[15] PureVolume,[16] _The Deli_ magazine,[17] _Consequence of Sound_[18] and _XLR8R_.[19]

Her music has appeared on _Made in Chelsea_,[20] _Dance Moms_,[21] _Bones_, and _One Life to Live_.
