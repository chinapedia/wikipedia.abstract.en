DILIP DANDEKAR was the chairman and managing director of Kokuyo Camlin Ltd.,[1] taking office from June 1, 2002.[2][3] He joined Camlin Ltd. as ‘management trainee’[4] under his father, D. P. Dandekar and uncle, G. P. Dandekar,[5] who were the founders of the company (initially known as Dandekar and Co.).[6]

He currently serves as the President of Indian Merchants' Chambers.[7] He is presently the chairman of Kokuyo Camlin Ltd., and Camlin Fine Sciences.[8]


Early life

Dilip Dandekar was born the son of Digambar Dandekar,[9] co-founder of Camlin Ltd. and member of the entrepreneurial and aristocratic, Chitpavan brahmin[10] Dandekar family.[11] Dandekar's uncle, G. P. Dandekar was the other co-founder of the company.[12] Dandekar has one older brother, Subhash[13] who served as executive chairman of Camlin until his retirement in 2002.[14]

He was married to Anjali Dandekar (née Gogte; niece of Balkrishna "Raosaheb" Gogte),[15] and they had 3 children together; before her death in 1993.[16]

He later remarried Smita Dandekar (née Soman) in 1998. She has one daughter from her previous marriage.[17]


Career

Dilip Dandekar joined Camlin Ltd. as management trainee[18][19] and became executive director of the company in 1979.[20] He took charge as chairman and managing director from June 1, 2002.[21] This was following the retirement of his brother, Subhash Dandekar, from the post of Executive Chairman after a period of 43 years.[22][23] Dandekar served as a Vice President of Indian Merchants' Chambers[24] and now serves as the Non Executive Chairman of Camlin Fine Sciences Ltd. (formerly, Camlin Fine Chemicals Ltd)[25] and has been its Director since June 2006.[26] Dandekar served as a Joint Managing Director of Kokuyo Camlin Limited. from June 1, 2002 to February 1, 2013.[27]

He has served as the chairman of Camlin Limited since June 1, 2002.[28][29] Under him, in May 2011, Kokuyo S&T Company Ltd,[30] a wholly owned subsidiary of Japan's Kokuyo Company Ltd acquired a controlling stake in Camlin Ltd from the Dandekar family.[31][32] The name of the company was changed to Kokuyo Camlin Ltd.[33][34]

Dilip Dandekar also serves as director to companies Indo Schottle Auto Parts Pvt. Ltd., Nilmac Packaging Industries Ltd.[35] and Camlin International Ltd.[36] He is a member of the executive committee of the Federation of Indian Chamber of Commerce and Industry, is the President of Indian Merchants’ Chamber[37] and on the governing board of Bombay First and the ICFAI Business School.[38][39]
