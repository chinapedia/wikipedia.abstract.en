NOVOOLEKSANDRIVKA (; ) is a village in Popasna Raion (district) in Luhansk Oblast of eastern Ukraine. The village borders in NW with Popasna city.

During the War in Donbass, that started in mid-April 2014, the separation line between the warring parties has been located in the vicinity of the settlement. In January 2018 the Ukrainian military regained control over the village.[1][2]
