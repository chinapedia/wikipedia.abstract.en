MIHAIL MINKOV (; born 6 February 1993) is a Bulgarian footballer who plays as a defender for Lokomotiv Gorna Oryahovitsa.


Career

Minkov is a product of the Vidima-Rakovski Sevlievo academy and it was there that he made his A PFG debut.[1]

In July 2015, Minkov joined Etar Veliko Tarnovo.[2] On 15 August 2017, his contract was terminated by mutual consent.[3] On 16 August 2017, Minkov signed with Litex Lovech.[4]

In June 2018, Minkov joined Dobrudzha Dobrich.[5]
