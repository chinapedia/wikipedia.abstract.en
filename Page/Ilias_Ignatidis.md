ILIAS IGNATIDIS (; born 11 November 1996 in Thessaloniki) is a Greek footballer currently playing for Bulgarian club Levski Karlovo as a striker.


Career

Levski Karlovo

Ignatidis joined the Bulgarian club Levski Karlovo in October. He scored a brace on his debut for the team in a 2–1 home win against Ludogorets Razgrad II.[1]
