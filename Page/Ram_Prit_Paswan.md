DR.RAMPRIT PASWAN is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party from Bihar. He has won the Bihar Legislative Assembly election in 2010 and 2015 from Rajnagar.[1][2][3]

Dr.Ramprit Paswan is presently MLA as well as Sabhapati(Zero Hour) of Bihar Legislative Assembly and also holds a Doctoral Degree from Lalit Narayan Mithila University (LNMU-Darbhanga).
