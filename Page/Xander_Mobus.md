XANDER MOBUS (born July 8, 1992) is an American voice actor, who is known for his work in English-dubbed anime and video games. In 2014, he voiced the announcer and the characters Master Hand and Crazy Hand in _Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Wii U_, and returned for the role in 2018's _Super Smash Bros. Ultimate_. He also voices Christo in _Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance_ and Joker in _Persona 5_.[1] He voice acted various comedic requests through social media during this period, such as singing the Pokémon Theme in the _Super Smash Bros._ announcer's voice.[2]
