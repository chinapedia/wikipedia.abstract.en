'''Kim Chol-ok ''' (born 15 October 1994) is a North Korean footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for the North Korea women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level, she plays for 25 April in North Korea.[1]
