HAPPY DIVING is an American rock band from Oakland, California.


History

Happy Diving began in 2014 with the release of a self-titled EP on Father/Daughter Records.[1] Happy Diving released their first full-length album titled _Big World_ in 2014 on Father/Daughter Records.[2][3][4] In 2015, Happy Diving released an EP titled _So Bunted_ on Topshelf Records.[5] In 2016, Happy Diving released their second full-length album titled _Electric Soul Unity_ via Topshelf Records.[6][7][8][9]
