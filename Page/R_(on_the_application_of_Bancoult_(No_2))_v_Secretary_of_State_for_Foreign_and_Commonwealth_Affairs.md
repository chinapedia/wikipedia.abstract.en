_R (ON THE APPLICATION OF BANCOULT (NO 2)) V SECRETARY OF STATE FOR FOREIGN AND COMMONWEALTH AFFAIRS_ [2016] UKSC 35 was a 2016 judgment of the Supreme Court of the United Kingdom that affirmed the decision of the House of Lords in _R v Secretary of State for Foreign and Commonwealth Affairs, ex p Bancoult (No 2)_ despite new evidence subsequently coming to light.[1]
