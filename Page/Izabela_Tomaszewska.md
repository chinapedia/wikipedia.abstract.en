IZABELA TOMASZEWSKA, née GŁOWACKA (13 September 1955 in Kwidzyn – 10 April 2010) was a Polish government official. She was head of the Protocolar Unit at the Chancellery of the President of the Republic of Poland.

She died in the 2010 Polish Air Force Tu-154 crash near Smolensk on 10 April 2010.[1] She was posthumously awarded the Order of Polonia Restituta.[2]
