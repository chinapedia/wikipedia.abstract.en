MOSTAFA SALEM (; ) served as Chairman of the World Scout Programme Committee, and as a member of the World Scout Committee.

In 2000, he was awarded the 286th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.[1][2]
