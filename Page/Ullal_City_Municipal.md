The ULLAL CITY MUNICIPAL is the municipal corporation responsible for looking after the city administration of the city of Ullal in Mangalore, Karnataka, India.

Ullal is a town situated about 12 km away from Mangalore with a population more than 53,000 as per the 2011 census.[1] The incumbent president of the corporation is K.Hussain and the vice president Chitra.[2] Ullal was upgraded from Town Municipal to City Municipal in the year 2014.[3]

Ullal City Municipal belongs to Mangalore(_erstwhile Ullal_) — Vidhan Sabha constituency of Karnataka Legislative assembly and Dakshina Kannada (Lok Sabha constituency) of the Lok Sabha (lower house of the Indian parliament).
