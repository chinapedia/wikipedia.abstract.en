_CAVALIER IN DEVIL'S CASTLE_ (), also known as _THE CAVALIERS OF DEVIL'S CASTLE_,[1] is a 1959 Italian swashbuckler film written and directed by Mario Costa and starring Massimo Serato, Irène Tunc and Luisella Boni.[2]
