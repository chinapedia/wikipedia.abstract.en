BARRYVILLE is an unincorporated community in Macon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri. The site is approximately southwest of Macon.[1]


History

Barryville had its start as a post office to the surrounding area.[2] A post office called Barryville was established in 1872, and remained in operation until 1899.[3]
