_FAVORINUS BLIANUS_ is a species of sea slug, an aeolid nudibranch in the family Facelinidae.[1]


Description

The maximum recorded body length is 13 mm.[2]


Habitat

Minimum recorded depth is 0 m.[3] Maximum recorded depth is 2 m.[4]
