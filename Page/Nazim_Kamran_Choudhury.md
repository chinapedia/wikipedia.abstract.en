NAZIM KAMRAN CHOUDHURY is a Bangladesh Nationalist Party politician and a former Member of Parliament for constituency Sylhet-9.[1][2]


Career

He is a former member of Parliament from Bangladesh Nationalist Party. He was elected from Sylhet.[3] He was sued in 2007 for attempting to grab an apartment in Gulshan, Dhaka.[4] He was arrested in 2009 by Bangladesh Police.[5] He is the Managing Director at Nazimgarh Resorts in Sylhet.[6]


Personal life

He is married to former adviser of Caretaker Government Geetiara Safya Chowdhury.[7] He is the cousin of former Chief Advisor Fakhruddin Ahmed.[8]
