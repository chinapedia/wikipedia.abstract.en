BRAWLEY is an unincorporated community in Oregon County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

A post office called Brawley was established in 1890, and remained in operation until 1914.[2] The community has the name of James Brawley, an early settler.[3] An early variant name was "Butts".[4]
