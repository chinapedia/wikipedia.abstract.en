TEONA SUKHASHVILI (, born February 6, 1994) is a Georgian football goalkeeper. She is a member of the Georgian national team. Currently, she is playing in the Turkish Women's First Football League for Kdz. Ereğlispor with jersey number 12.[1]


Playing career

Club

Sukhashvili moved to Turkey and joined Kdz. Ereğlispor to play in the Turkish Women's First Football League in November 2016.[2]

International

Sukhashvili was admitted to the Georgia women's national football team and took part at three matches of the 2019 FIFA Women's World Cup qualification – UEFA preliminary round Group 1 at Tbilisi, Georgia in April 2017.[3][4][5][6]
