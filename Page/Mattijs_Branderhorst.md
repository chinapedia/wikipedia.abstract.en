MATTIJS BRANDERHORST (born 31 December 1993) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays for NEC, as a goalkeeper.[1][2]


Club career

Branderhorst came through the youth ranks at Willem II and was loaned to MVV in 2015.[3] He made his debut for Willem II on 15 October 2017 in the home match against FC Twente. On 28 January 2019, he was loaned to N.E.C. for the remainder of the season.[4]
