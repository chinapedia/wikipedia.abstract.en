_PALMOPHYLLUM_ is a genus of alga.[1][2] This genus is known to grow in low light conditions and at depth.[3] The known distribution of this genus is being extended as deeper waters are explored.[4]


Species

-   _Palmophyllum crassum_ (Naccari 1828) Rabenhorst 1868
-   _Palmophyllum foliaceum_ West 1907
-   _Palmophyllum umbracola_ Nelson & Ryan 1986[5]
