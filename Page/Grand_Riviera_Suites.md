The GRAND RIVIERA SUITES is a high rise residential skyscraper located along Roxas Boulevard in Manila, Philippines. At high, the building is the tallest building in Manila city proper.[1]

The building constructed by developer, Moldex Realty was designed by architectural firm, Asya Design Partner. Proposed in 2010 the building was constructed a year after and was completed in 2014.[2] By June 2013, the building was already topped-off.[3]
