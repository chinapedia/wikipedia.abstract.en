VOLODYMYR BAYENKO (; born 9 February 1990 in Komsomolske, Donetsk Oblast, Ukrainian SSR) is a professional Ukrainian football defender for FK Buxoro.


Career

Bayenko is a product of the FC Shakhtar and FC Olimpik Youth Sportive School Systems in Donetsk. He spent time in the different Ukrainian Second League and Ukrainian First League clubs, but in summer 2016 signed a contract with FC Zirka in the Ukrainian Premier League.[1]
