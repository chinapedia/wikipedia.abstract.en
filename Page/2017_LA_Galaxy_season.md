The 2017 LA GALAXY SEASON was the club's twenty-second season of existence, and their twenty-second season in Major League Soccer, the top flight of American soccer.

2017 marked a downturn for the club signalling the end of a dynasty for the Galaxy.[1][2][3] It was the first season since 2008 that the Galaxy failed to qualify for the MLS Cup Playoffs.[4]
