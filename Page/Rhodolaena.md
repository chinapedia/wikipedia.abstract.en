_RHODOLAENA_ is a genus of trees and shrubs in the family Sarcolaenaceae. The species are all endemic to Madagascar.[1][2] The monophyly of the genus is unresolved.[3]


Species

Seven species are recognised:[4][5]

-   _Rhodolaena acutifolia_
-   _Rhodolaena altivola_
-   _Rhodolaena bakeriana_
-   _Rhodolaena coriacea_
-   _Rhodolaena humblotii_
-   _Rhodolaena leroyana_
-   _Rhodolaena macrocarpa_
