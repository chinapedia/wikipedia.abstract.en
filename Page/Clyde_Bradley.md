CLYDE EDWARD BRADLEY (born January 30, 1934) is an American politician in the state of Iowa.

Bradley was born in Luke, Maryland and attended the University of Iowa. A Republican, he served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 1995 to 2003 (37th district), from Clinton County, Iowa.[1]
