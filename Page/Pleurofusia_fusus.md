_PLEUROFUSIA FUSUS_ is an extinct species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.[1]


Description


Distribution

This extinct marine species was found in Oligocene strata of Myanmar, age range 28.4 to 23.03 Ma.
