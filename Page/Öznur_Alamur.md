Ã–ZNUR ALAMUR (born July 7, 1997) is a Turkish female Paralympian athlete competing in the T11 and T12 disability class middle-distance events of 400m and 1500m.[1] She is a member of Gaziantep BB Disabled SK.[2]

She won the third gold medal in the 100m T11, 200m T11 and 400m T11 events at the 2014 IPC Athletics European Championships held in Swansea, Wales, UK.[3]

In 2016, she took the bronze medal in the 400m T12 event, and the gold medal in the 1500m T11 event at the 2016 IPC Athletics European Championships held in Grosseto, Italy.[4]
