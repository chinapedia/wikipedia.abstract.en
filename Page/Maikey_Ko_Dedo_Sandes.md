_MAIKEY KO DEDO SANDEES_ is a Pakistani Soap Opera that first aired on Geo Tv on 17 August 2015. It is on air every Monday to Friday at 7:00 p.m(PST). The soap also reruns on Geo TV sister channel Geo Kahani.
