_DYCKERPOTTS' HEIRS_ (German: _DYCKERPOTTS ERBEN_) is a 1928 German silent comedy film directed by Hans Behrendt.[1]

It was based on a 1917 Robert Grötzach-penned play by the same name. The film's art direction was by Emil Hasler and Oscar Friedrich Werndorff. It was distributed by the German branch of Fox Film.
