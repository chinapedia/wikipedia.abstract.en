MARK CASEY (born 30 January 1982) is an international Australian lawn and indoor bowler.


Bowls career

In 2005 he won the gold medal at the inaugural World Cup Singles in Hong Kong.[1]

The following year in 2006 he won the gold medal in the fours competition at the 2006 Commonwealth Games in Melbourne.[2] He won another the lawn bowls gold medal in the fours competition at the 2012 World Outdoor Bowls Championship.[3]

In 2016 he was part of the triples team with Aron Sherriff and Barrie Lester who won the silver medal at the 2016 World Outdoor Bowls Championship in Christchurch and won another silver medal in the fours.[4]
