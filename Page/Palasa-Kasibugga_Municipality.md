PALASA-KASIBUGGA MUNICIPALITY is the local self government in Palasa of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.[1] It is classified as a _3rd Grade Municipality_.[2] It constitutes total population of 57,507.


Administration

Palasa_kasibugga_municipality_office.jpg Palasa-Kasibugga municipality was formed in the year 2000. The municipality is spread over an area of 37.75 and has 23 election wards.[3] each represented by a ward member and the wards committee is headed by a chairperson.[4] The present municipal commissioner of the city is B.R.S Seshadri.[5]
