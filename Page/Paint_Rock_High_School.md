PAINT ROCK SCHOOL is a public primary, intermediate and highschool located in Paint Rock, Texas (USA) and classified as a 1A school by the UIL. It is part of the Paint Rock Independent School District located in Concho County. In 2015, the school was rated "Met Standard" by the Texas Education Agency.[1]


Athletics

The Paint Rock Indians compete in the following sports:[2]

-   Basketball
-   Cross Country
-   Six Man Football
-   Golf
-   Tennis
-   Track & Field
-   Volleyball
