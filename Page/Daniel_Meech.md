DANIEL WILLIAM MEECH (born 19 November 1973) is a New Zealand equestrian.

Meech competed in individual jumping at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta,[4] and is New Zealand Olympian number 730.[5] He competed in individual and team jumping at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens,[6] In Athens, he was third going into the final round and would have won gold by dropping one bar, but he dropped three bars and came in 12th place.[7] Meech was nominated for the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, but his horse Sorbas was injured and he was replaced by Kirk Webby.[8]
