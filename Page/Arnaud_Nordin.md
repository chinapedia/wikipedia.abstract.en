ARNAUD NORDIN (born 17 June 1998) is a French footballer who plays as a midfielder for Ligue 1 club Saint-Étienne.[1]


Club career

Nordin is a youth exponent from Saint-Étienne. He made his Ligue 1 debut on 25 September 2016 against Lille.[2]


Personal life

Nordin was born in France and is of Martiniquais descent.[3]
