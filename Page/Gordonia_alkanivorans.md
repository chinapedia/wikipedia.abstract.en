_GORDONIA ALKANIVORANS_ is a bacterium from the genus of _Gordonia_ which has been isolated from soil which was contaminated with tar and phenol in Rositz in Germany.[1][2][3][4] _Gordonia alkanivorans_ has the ability to metabolize hexadecane.[5] The strain RIPI90A of _Gordonia alkanivorans_ can desulfurize dibenzothiophene.[6]
