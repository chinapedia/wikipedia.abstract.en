TONDE WANDOU (also Tonde Wadou) is a village in the commune of Galim-Tignère in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, near the .


Population

In 1971, Tonde Wandou contained 290 inhabitants, mainly Wodaabe.[1]

At the time of the 2005 census, there were 619 people in the village.[2]
