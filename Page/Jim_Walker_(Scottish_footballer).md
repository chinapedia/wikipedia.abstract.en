JAMES WALKER was a Scottish professional football inside forward who made over 180 appearances in the Scottish League for Third Lanark. He also played for Dundee United and Queen's Park.


Personal life

Walker's brothers Willie and Frank were also Queen's Park footballers.[1] He served in the Glasgow Highlanders during the First World War.[2]
