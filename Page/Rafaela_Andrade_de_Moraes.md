RAFAELA ANDRADE DE MORAES commonly known as RAFAELA (born 23 May 1981) is a Brazilian women's international footballer who plays as a midfielder. She is a member of the Brazil women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2003 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1]
