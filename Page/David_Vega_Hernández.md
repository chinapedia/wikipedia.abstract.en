DAVID VEGA HERNÁNDEZ (; born 23 June 1994) is a Spanish tennis player.

Vega Hernández has a career high ATP singles ranking of 403 achieved on 21 July 2014. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 111 achieved on 15 October 2018.

Vega Hernández made his ATP main draw debut at the 2015 Estoril Open in the singles draw, entering as a lucky loser.
