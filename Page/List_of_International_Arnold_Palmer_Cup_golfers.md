This is a list of all the International team golfers who have played in the Arnold Palmer Cup through 2018. The Arnold Palmer Cup was known as the Palmer Cup until 2015. Until 2002 the United States played Great Britain & Ireland while from 2003 to 2017 the United States played a European team.
