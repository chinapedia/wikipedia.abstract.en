CAMP FLOG GNAW CARNIVAL, formerly known as the OFWGKTA Carnival or Odd Future carnival, is an annual American music festival and carnival curated by Tyler, the Creator and has been held annually since its inception in the fall of 2012.[1] It features a variety of carnival games and rides (those of which include a massive Ferris Wheel, food vendors, and a myriad of notable artists).

Prior to 2016, the festival had only been held on one day. It is always held on a weekend. There is no explicit date(s) for which Camp Flog Gnaw is held. However, past carnivals have been held at either the end of October or the middle of November (with the exception of the very first festival, which was held on September 30, 2012).
