PRETTY BOB CREEK is a stream in eastern Henry County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary to the South Grand River within Truman Reservoir.

The stream headwaters arise at west of the community of Coal on Missouri Route 7 and the stream flows generally south passing east of the community of Gaines to enter the Truman Reservoir west of the Missouri Route U crossing at .[2][3]

Pretty Bob Creek has the name of Bob "Pretty Bob" Lawler.[4]
