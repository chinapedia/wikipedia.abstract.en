ELOHOR GODSWILL EKPOLO (born 14 May 1995) is a Nigerian professional footballer who plays for Swedish Allsvenskan club BK Häcken. Mainly a right back, he can also play as a central defender.


Playing career

Ekpolo moved with his family to Spain from Nigeria in 2001, and settled in Tarragona. He came through the youth team at FC Barcelona, and played for the reserve team and captained the under-21 team, before he was released in June 2016.[1] He had a trial at Wolverhampton Wanderers the following month.[2] He signed with English League One side Fleetwood Town in November 2016, following a successful two-week trial.[3] He scored his first goal for Fleetwood in an EFL Trophy tie against Leicester City Under-23s on 29 August 2017.[4]

Having been replaced at right-back by Everton loanee Gethin Jones, Ekpolo returned to Spain on 26 January 2018 and signed for Mérida AD of Segunda División B.[5]

On 29 June 2018, Ekpolo signed a half year deal with the Swedish Allsvenskan side BK Häcken [6] and on 12 october 2018 the club extended his contract to the end of 2021.[7]
