DENNIS HADŽIKADUNIĆ (born 9 July 1998) is a Swedish footballer. He plays as a defender in Russia for Rostov.[1]


Career

Club career

Dennis Hadžikadunić signed an apprenticeship contract with Malmö FF on 10 October 2016.[2] On 30 October 2016 he made his first team debut against Gefle IF when he replaced an injured Kári Árnason with 24 minutes left to play.

On 12 July 2018, he signed a 5-year contract with the Russian Premier League club FC Rostov.[3] On 1 November 2018, he scored his first goal for the club, in a Russian Cup 3–1 victory over FC Zenit Saint Petersburg.[4]
