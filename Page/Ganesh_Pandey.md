GANESH PANDEY or GANESA PANDE (Nepali:गणेश पाण्डे वा गणेश पाँडे) was Minister and Accomplice of King Dravya Shah of Gorkha. He helped Dravya Shah to become King of Gorkha and was later appointed Minister (Bhardar) of Gorkha Rajya. His descendant was Kalu Pandey, first Kaji of Gorkha and his sons Dewan Kaji Bamsa Raj Pandey and Mulkaji Damodar Pande.
