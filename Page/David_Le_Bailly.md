DAVID LE BAILLY is a French journalist born in Paris.


Biography

After working in the economic press (L'AGEFI, La Tribune), David Le Bailly joined the editorial staff at Paris Match in 2002. Specializing in investigations, he worked on the Bettencourt, Karachi and affairs.
