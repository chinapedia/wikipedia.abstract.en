JAMES EDISON FIGURADO (born July 25, 1990) is a Sri Lankan footballer who plays as a midfielder for Solid SC and the Sri Lanka national football team.[1][2][3]

Having been named for the Sri Lanka squads for the 2015 SAFF Championship and the Bangabandhu Gold Cup, he scored his first international goal in a 4-2 win to Bangladesh in the Bangabandhu Gold Cup[4][5]

Scored a scintillating goal straight from taking a corner kick to help Solid SC beat Navy SC 2-1 in the 2014–15 Sri Lanka Football Premier League.[6]
