NEMINAKA (also Nemounaka) is a village in the commune of Nyambaka in the Adamawa Region of Cameroon, on the road from Ngaoundéré to Meiganga.[1]


Population

In 1967, Neminaka contained 141 inhabitants, mostly Mboum.[2] At the time of the 2005 census, there were 694 people in the village.[3]
