MOTIN RAHMAN () is a Bangladeshi film director.[1][2] He is also known for the film writer and actor.[3] His debuted film was _Lal Kajol_. In 1993, he won Bangladesh National Film Awards as a Best Director for the film _Andho Biswas_.[4]


Early life

Matin Rahman was born on 18 March 1952 in Naogaon District. He is the eldest son of seven brothers and two sisters. He spent his childhood and graduation from Shntahar village.[5] Later in 1973, he came to Dhaka for the study from _Alamgir Kabir Film Institute_. He is the first student to complete Master's degree from journalism.[6]


Personal life

Matin Rahman married to Nasima Khanam. They have two daughters, Naushin and Naurin and one son Mrittika Rahman. They live in Mohammadpur, Dhaka.[7]
