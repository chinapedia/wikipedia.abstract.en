PHILIP LOWRY (born 15 July 1989) is a Northern Irish semi-professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Crusaders. He has previously played for Portadown, Linfield and Institute. Lowry also works part-time in London.


Club career

Lowry began his career at 18 years of age with local side Institute, attracting interest from Coleraine and having trials with Norwich City and Leicester City at the age of 19.[1] He moved to Linfield in 2009 and went on to win several major honours with the Blues before leaving in 2014 for his hometown club Derry City.

After being released from the Candystripes in 2015 he relocated to London to work for KPMG and trained part-time with English side Wingate and Finchley. In January 2016 he joined Portadown on a short term contract until the end of the season, with Lowry flying over from London every week to play. In the summer of 2016 it was announced that Lowry had joined the champions Crusaders.
