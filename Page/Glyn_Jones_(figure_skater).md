GLYN JONES (born 22 August 1953)[1] is a British former competitive figure skater. He represented the United Kingdom at the 1976 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck, Austria. He placed 19th in the compulsory figures, 17th in the short program, 16th in the free skate, and 16th overall.[2] He appeared at six ISU Championships, achieving his best result, 13th, at the 1977 Europeans in Helsinki, Finland.

As of August 2011, he was working as a manager at the Tampa Bay Skating Academy in Oldsmar, Florida.[3][4]
