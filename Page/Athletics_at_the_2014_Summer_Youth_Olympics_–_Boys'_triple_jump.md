The BOYS’ TRIPLE JUMP competition at the 2014 Summer Youth Olympics was held on 22–25 August 2014 in Nanjing Olympic Sports Center.


Schedule

  Date             Time    Round
  ---------------- ------- ---------------
  22 August 2014   18:30   QUALIFICATION
  25 August 2014   19:35   FINAL
