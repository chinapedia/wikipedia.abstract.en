HOU SHENG-MAO () is Taiwanese physician. He was the Minister of Department of Health from 2005 to 2008.


Education

Hou obtained his doctoral degree in clinical medicine from National Taiwan University (NTU).[1]


Early career

Hou was the vice superintendent of National Taiwan University Hospital, and director and professor of the School of Medicine of NTU.[2]


Department of Health

The nomination of Hou was announced by Premier Frank Hsieh in February 2005.[3][4]
