HODGE is an unincorporated community in Lafayette County, in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1]


History

An early variant name was Edward's Mill, after John Edward, the proprietor of a local gristmill.[2] A post office called Hodge was established in 1888, and remained in operation until 1963.[3]
