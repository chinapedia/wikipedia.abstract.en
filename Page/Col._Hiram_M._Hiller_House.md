COL. HIRAM M. HILLER HOUSE is a historic home located at Kahoka, Clark County, Missouri. It was built in 1874, and is a two-story, vernacular Italianate style frame dwelling. It has a rear ell and wraparound porch with a truncated hipped roof.[1]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1986.[2]
