Tyniec_parish_cemetery,_grave_of_Janina_Natusiewicz-Mirer,_Benedyktyńska_street,_Tyniec,_Krakow,_Poland.JPG JANINA NATUSIEWICZ-MIRER (1 January 1940 – 10 April 2010) was a Polish activist.

She died in the 2010 Polish Air Force Tu-154 crash near Smolensk on 10 April 2010.[1] She was posthumously awarded the Order of Polonia Restituta.[2]
