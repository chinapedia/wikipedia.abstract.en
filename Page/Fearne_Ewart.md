FEARNE EWART (born 20 November 1936) is a British former swimmer.


Swimming career

She competed in two events at the 1956 Summer Olympics.[1]

She represented England and won a bronze medal in the 440 yards freestyle relay at the 1954 British Empire and Commonwealth Games in Vancouver, Canada.[2][3]
