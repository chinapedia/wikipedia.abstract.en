KAMEN DIMITROV () (born 18 January 1962) is a retired Bulgarian discus thrower.

He won the 1981 European Junior Championships[1] and won the Balkan Championships in 1984, 1986 and 1988.[2] He competed at the 1986 European Championships, the 1987 World Championships,[3] the 1990 European Championships[4] and the 1991 World Championships without reaching the final.[5]

He became Bulgarian champion in 1985, 1988, 1989 and 1991. His toughest competitors were Georgi Georgiev and Nikolai Kolev.[6] His personal best throw was 65.40 metres, achieved in May 1986 in Plovdiv.[7]
