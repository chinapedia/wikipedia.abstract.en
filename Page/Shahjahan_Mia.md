SHAHJAHAN MIA is a Bangladesh Awami League politician. He is the current Jatiya Sangsad member from Patuakhali-1 constituency.[1][2] He served as the State Minister of Religious Affairs in the second Sheikh Hasina administration.[3][4][5]
