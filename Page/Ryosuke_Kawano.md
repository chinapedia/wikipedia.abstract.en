Playing career

Ryosuke Kawano played for J1 League club; Shonan Bellmare in 2013. In 2014 season, he move to J3 League club; Fukushima United FC. In January 2018, after three seasons with Verspah Oita, he signed for YSCC Yokohama.[1]
