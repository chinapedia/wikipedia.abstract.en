LIRIDON KALLUDRA (born 5 November 1991) is a Swedish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Eliteserien club Kristiansund.

Kalludra was born in Mitrovica, but grew up in Sweden.[1]


Career

Liridon Kalludra made his debut for Kristiansund in 2010. After four years in Kristiansund Kalludra left the club for Sarpsborg 08, but just after 14 games he returned to Kristiansund.
