MAGOMED BATYROVICH GAZDIYEV (; born 15 December 1996) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Angusht Nazran on 26 September 2016 in a game against FC Legion-Dynamo Makhachkala.[1]
