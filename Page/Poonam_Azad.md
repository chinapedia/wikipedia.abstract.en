POONAM AZAD is the wife of a former Indian cricketer and politician Kirti Azad.

Poonam joined Aam Admi Party (AAP) on Sunday November 13, 2016 after months of speculation.[1] She had contested against then Delhi Chief Minister Sheila Dikshit in the 2003 assembly polls and was serving as a spokesperson of the Bharatiya Janata Partyâ€™s Delhi unit before joining AAP. Now she has joined Indian National Congress.


Personal life

Poonam is married to former cricketer Kirti Azad and has two sons.[2] Her elder son Suryavardhan has played for Delhi Under-17s, Under-19s and Under-22s, while her younger son Somyavardhan has played for Delhi Under-15s and Delhi Under-17s.[3][4]
