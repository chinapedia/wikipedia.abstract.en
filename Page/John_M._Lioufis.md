JOHN M. LIOUFIS (, ) served as the Commissioner of the Greek Scouts in Egypt.[1]

In 1978, Lioufis was awarded the 127th _Bronze Wolf_, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world Scouting.
