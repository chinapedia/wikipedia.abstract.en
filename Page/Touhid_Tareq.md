TOUHID TAREQ (born 17 January 1995) is a Bangladeshi cricketer who plays for Rajshahi Division.[1] He made his List A debut for Shinepukur Cricket Club in the 2017–18 Dhaka Premier Division Cricket League on 9 February 2018.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Gazi Group Cricketers in the 2018–19 Dhaka Premier Division Twenty20 Cricket League on 26 February 2019.[3]
