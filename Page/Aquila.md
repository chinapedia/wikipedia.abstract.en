AQUILA is the Latin and Romance languages word for _eagle_. Specifically, it may refer to:

-   Aquila (constellation), the astronomical constellation, the Eagle
-   _Aquila_ (genus), a genus of birds including some eagles
-   Aquila (name), a given name or surname
-   _Aquila_ (Roman), a Roman military standard


People
