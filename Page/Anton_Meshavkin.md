ANTON SERGEYEVICH MESHAVKIN (; born 30 January 2000) is a Russian football player.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Pskov-747 on 17 October 2016 in a game against FC Domodedovo Moscow.[1]
