DONATO ACQUAVIVA D'ARAGONA (died 1528) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Conversano (1499–1528).[1][2]


Biography

In 1499, Donato Acquaviva d'Aragona was appointed during the papacy of Pope Alexander VI as Bishop of Conversano.[3][4] He served as Bishop of Conversano until his death in 1528.[5][6]
