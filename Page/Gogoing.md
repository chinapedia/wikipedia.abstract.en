GAO DI-PING (born October 17, 1991), better known by his in-game ID GOGOING, is a Chinese retired _League of Legends_ profession player for the team Oh My God (OMG) of the League of Legends Pro League (LPL).[1] He is a notable player of the "Top Lane" position, renowned for his Garen (A character of League of Legends) play.[2] He has had high achievements both in the Chinese league as well as internationally.[3] Gao, in his prime, has been considered by many analysts and other players in the League of Legends scene as one of the most mechanically skillful players and top top lane players, particularly in 2014.[4][5][6]

In August 2016, Gogoing announced the formation of a new team, Go Dream, but he would serve as the manager and not a player.[7]
