SANTIAGO CALATAYUD, O.S.A. or GIACOMO DA CALATAYUD (died 1526) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Santorini (1521â€“1526).[1][2][3]


Biography

Santiago Calatayud was ordained a priest in the Order of Saint Augustine.[4][5] On 20 Feb 1521, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Leo X as Bishop of Santorini.[6][7] He served as Bishop of Santorini until his death in 1526.[8]
