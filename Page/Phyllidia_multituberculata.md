_PHYLLIDIA MULTITUBERCULATA_ is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusk in the family Phyllidiidae.[1]


Distribution

This species was described from Pulu Bambu in the Aru Islands, south-west of Papua New Guinea. It has been reported from the Indian Ocean.[2]


Description

This species has previously been confused with _Phyllidia ocellata_.[3]


Diet

This species feeds on a sponge.
