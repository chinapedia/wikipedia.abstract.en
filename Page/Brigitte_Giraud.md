thumb BRIGITTE GIRAUD (born 1960, Sidi-Bel-Abbès in Algeria) is a French writer, author of novels ands and short stories.

After childhood and youth in the banlieue of Lyon, she studied English and German and worked as a bookseller, translator and journalist. For her first book _La chambre des parents_ (1997), she received the "Prix Littéraire des Étudiants" and for _Nico_ the "Prix Lettres frontière". She now lives near Lyon.
