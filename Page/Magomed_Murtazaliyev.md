MAGOMED GADZHIYEVICH MURTAZALIYEV (; born 7 January 1997) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Legion-Dynamo Makhachkala.


Club career

He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Legion-Dynamo Makhachkala on 9 October 2016 in a game against FC Dynamo Stavropol.[1]
