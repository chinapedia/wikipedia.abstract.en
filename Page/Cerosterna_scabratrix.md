_CEROSTERNA SCABRATRIX_ is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Johan Christian Fabricius in 1781. It is known from Sri Lanka and India.[1]


Subspecies

-   _Cerosterna scabratrix scabratrix_ (Fabricius, 1781)
-   _Cerosterna scabratrix gladiatrix_ Fabricius, 1801
-   _Cerosterna scabratrix griseatrix_ Aurivillius, 1920
-   _Cerosterna scabratrixmurina_ Nonfried, 1894
-   _Cerosterna scabratrix renei_ Pascoe, 1888
-   _Cerosterna scabratrix spinatrix_ Fabricius, 1798
