_MONOCHAMUS IMPLUVIATUS_ (Siberian speckled sawyer) is a species of beetle in the family Cerambycidae. It was described by Motschulsky in 1859. It is recorded from east of the Ural Mountains in Russia through to the Russian Far East, as well as Mongolia, Northeastern China and North Korea.[1]
