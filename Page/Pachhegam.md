PACHHEGAM (DEVANI) is a village and former non-salute Rajput princely state on Saurashtra peninsula in Gujarat, western India.


History

The minor princely state, in Gohilwar _prant_, was ruled by Gohil Rajput Chieftains. In 1901 it comprised the town and two more villages.[1]
