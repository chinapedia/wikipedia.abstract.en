NICK ALFIERI is an American football linebacker currently playing for the Schwäbisch Hall Unicorns in the German Football League.[1] He played college football at Georgetown University.


College career

Alfieri finished his collegiate career with 322 total tackles, making him only the third player in Georgetown football history to reach 300 career tackles. In 2014, he wore the Joe Eacobacci #35 Memorial Jersey - a tribute to a former Georgetown football captain who was killed in the September 11 terrorist attacks.[2][3]

Professional career

On December 16, 2015, it was announced that Alfieri had signed with the Schwäbisch Hall Unicorns of the German Football League. [4]

Personal

Alfieri is from Portland, Oregon. His father, Phil Alfieri, played college football at Oregon State from 1983-1987. His mother ran track at Oregon State from 1984-1988. His younger brother, Joey, currently plays college football for Stanford University.[5]

YouTube Channel

Alfieri has a YouTube channel named NALF. In his vlogs, Alfieri documents his thoughts and experiences about life in Germany as a football player for the Schwäbisch Hall Unicorns. Alfieri gained a large viewership especially in Germany, where his vlogs are appreciated for their interesting as well as entertaining content. [6]
