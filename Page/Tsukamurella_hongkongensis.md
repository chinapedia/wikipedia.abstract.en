_TSUKAMURELLA HONGKONGENSIS_ is a Gram-positive, aerobic, non-spore-forming and non-motile bacterium from the genus of Tsukamurella. It has been isolated from a human clinical sample in the Queen Mary Hospital in Hong Kong.[1][2][3][4]
