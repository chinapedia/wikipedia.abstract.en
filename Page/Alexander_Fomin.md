ALEXANDER VASILYEVICH FOMIN ( born May 25, 1959) is the Russian Deputy Minister of Defence.


Biography

Alexander Fomin was born on May 25, 1959 in Leninogorsk. He attended the Military Institute of the Red Banner of the Ministry of Defence (Now the Military University of the Russian Defence Ministry), graduating in 1984.[1]

Fomin was promoted to Deputy Minister of Defence of the Russian Federation in 2017.[2][3]
