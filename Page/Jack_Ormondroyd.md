JACK ORMONDROYD (born 7 November 1991) is a rugby league footballer who plays as a for Featherstone Rovers in the Betfred Championship.[1]

Ormondroyd is the son of English former professional footballer Ian Ormondroyd.[2] He played in Australia for the Thirlmere Roosters in Group 6 Country Rugby League.[3] Ormondroyd then played for the Featherstone Rovers in the Kingstone Press Championship.[4]
