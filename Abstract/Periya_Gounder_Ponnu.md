_PERIYA GOUNDER PONNU_ (English: Daughter of a big landlord) is a 1992 Tamil drama film, directed by Manivasagam. The film features R. Sarathkumar and Gautami in the lead roles, with Manorama, Goundamani, Senthil, Jaiganesh, Vadivukkarasi, Delhi Ganesh and Vijayaraj in supporting roles. The film, produced by Rajeswari Manivasagam and P. S. Mani, had musical score by Deva and was released on 28 February 1992.[1]
