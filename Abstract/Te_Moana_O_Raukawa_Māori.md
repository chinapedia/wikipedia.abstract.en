TE MOANA O RAUKAWA MĀORI (Cook Strait Māori) and TE ŪPOKO O TE IKA A MĀUI MĀORI (lower North Island Māori) are a Māori iwi from Manawatu, Horowhenua, Kapiti Coast and Wellington. They include the _iwi_ (tribes) of Rangitāne, Muaūpoko, Ngāti Raukawa, Ngāti Toa, Te Atiawa ki Whakarongotai, Te Āti Awa and Taranaki Whānui ki te Upoko o te Ika.[1]
