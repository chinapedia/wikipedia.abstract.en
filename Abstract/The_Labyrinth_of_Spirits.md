_THE LABYRINTH OF SPIRITS_ (original title: _EL LABERINTO DE LOS ESPÍRITUS_) is a fiction novel by Spanish author Carlos Ruiz Zafón. This is the fourth and final book in the Cemetery of Forgotten Books series.[1][2] The novel was initially released on 17 November 2016 in Spain and Latin America by Spanish publisher Planeta. HarperCollins published the English translation by Lucia Graves on September 18, 2018.[3][4][5][6][7][8]
