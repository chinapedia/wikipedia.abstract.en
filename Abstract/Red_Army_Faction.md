Key early figures included Andreas Baader, Gudrun Ensslin, Horst Mahler and Ulrike Meinhof, among others. Ulrike Meinhof was involved in Baader's escape from jail in 1970.[2] The West German government as well as most Western media and literature considered the Red Army Faction to be a terrorist organization.[3][4][5][6]
