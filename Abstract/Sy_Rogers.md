SY ROGERS is an American Christian pastor who was formerly part of the ex-gay movement. In the late 1980s, Rogers was a President of Exodus International,[1][2][3] and became one of the earliest noted personalities associated with the ex-gay movement during the early 1980s. He wrote a life-story testimony entitled "The Man in the Mirror," which was published in pamphlet form by Last Days Ministries.[4][5]
