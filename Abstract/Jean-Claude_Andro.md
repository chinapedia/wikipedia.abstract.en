JEAN-CLAUDE ANDRO (1937, Quimper – 2000) was a French writer. He published his first novel at 22 and then left to teach in Mexico (1960–62). He then pursued a career as a novelist and translator (_Zone sacrée_ and _Chant des aveugles_ by Carlos Fuentes and _Christ des ténèbres_ by Rosario Castellanos).
