The UNITED WORLD COLLEGE THAILAND (UWCT, formerly known as PHUKET INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY) is an independent international school in Thailand, and a member of the United World College (UWC) movement. The school provides a Preschool–Grade 12 education encompasses academics, social and emotional learning and mindfulness, service, outdoor education and activities. The school uses the IB Primary Years Programme for both early childhood (Preschool and Kindergarten), and primary school (grades 1–5), the IB Middle Years Programme for grades 6–10, and the IB Diploma Programme for grades 11–12.
