JAMES "JIM" NIBLETT (born ) is a British former civil servant who served as Head of International Policy at Ofcom. He studied at Trinity College, Cambridge 1971-1975. He began his career in the civil service, and in 1996 moved to Oftel. In 2003, he joined Ofcom when that organisation was created. In 2012, he was made an Officer of the Order of the British Empire for services to telecommunications.[1][2][3]
