JOHN STAUBER is an American progressive writer.[1] Stauber has co-authored five books about government propaganda, private interests and the public relations industry. His work includes one book about how industry manipulates science (_Trust Us, We're Experts_), one about the history and current scope of the public relations industry (_Toxic Sludge is Good for You_), and one about mad cow disease (_Mad Cow USA_), which predicted the surfacing of the disease within the United States.
