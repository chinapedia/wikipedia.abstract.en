MADUKA LIYANAPATHIRANAGE (born 4 January 1992) is a Sri Lankan cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Colombo Cricket Club in the 2011–12 Premier Trophy on 17 February 2012.[2] In January 2016, playing for Chilaw Marians Cricket Club, he took nine wickets in an innings against Tamil Union Cricket and Athletic Club in the 2015–16 Premier League Tournament.[3]
