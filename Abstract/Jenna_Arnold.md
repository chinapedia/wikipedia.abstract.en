JENNA ARNOLD (born Jennifer S Arnold on July 1, 1981) is an American businessperson and serial entrepreneur. She is known as the co-founder of ORGANIZE, for her work at the United Nations and MTV, and most recently as a National Organizer for the Women's March on Washington.[1] Oprah has called Arnold one of the "100 Awakened Leaders who are using their voice and talent to elevate humanity".[2]
