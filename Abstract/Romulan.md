Star_Trek_costume_-_Romulan.jpg The ROMULANS () are a fictional extraterrestrial species in the American science fiction franchise _Star Trek_. They first appeared in the series _Star Trek_ in 1966. As well as featuring in three episodes of that series, they were later used in the television series _The Animated Series_, _The Next Generation_, _Deep Space Nine_, _Voyager_, and _Enterprise_, as well as the feature films _Star Trek: Nemesis_ (2002) and _Star Trek_ (2009). They also appear in various other spin-off media, including books and games.
