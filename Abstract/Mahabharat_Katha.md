_MAHABHARAT KATHA_ is an Indian television series. This show contains portions and stories left out of Mahabharat (1988 TV series). It was produced by B. R. Chopra.[1] and directed by his son Ravi Chopra. The cast and crew were the same as the original series, except some actors like Nitish Bhardwaj who played Krishna in the original series was replaced by Rishabh Shukla.
