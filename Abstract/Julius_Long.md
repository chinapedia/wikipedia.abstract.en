JULIUS LONG (born 28 May 1977) is an American professional boxer who resides in Auckland, New Zealand. He is best known for his physical stature, standing 6 ft 8 in (2.06m) and weighing around 300lbs (136kg). Long is the current Interim WBA Oceania Heavyweight Champion.[1] Outside of the ring, Long is a professional chef in an upscale restaurant.[2]
