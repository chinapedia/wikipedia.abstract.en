JAYA SHARMA is a queer feminist author[1][2][3]https://books.google.com/books?id=5uHIYv6LISMC&pg=PA259 based in New Delhi, India. She identifies as a queer feminist activist, working on issues of gender, education and sexuality. As the founder of Nirantar and part of the sexuality education initiative within Nirantar, she is involved with capacity building, research and advocacy.[4]
