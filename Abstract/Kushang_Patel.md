KUSHANG PATEL (born 13 September 1991) is an Indian first-class cricketer who plays for Saurashtra.[1] He made his first-class debut for Gujarat in the 2012–13 Ranji Trophy on 17 November 2012.[2] He made his List A debut for Saurashtra in the 2016–17 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 25 February 2017.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Saurashtra in the 2017–18 Zonal T20 League on 8 January 2018.[4]
