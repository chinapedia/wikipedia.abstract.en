KANSAS STATE UNIVERSITY (KSU), commonly shortened to KANSAS STATE or K-STATE, is a public research university with its main campus in Manhattan, Kansas, United States. Kansas State was opened as the state's land-grant college in 1863 and was the first public institution of higher learning in the state of Kansas.[1][2] It had a record high enrollment of 24,766 students for the Fall 2014 semester.[3]
