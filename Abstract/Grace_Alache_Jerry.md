GRACE ALACHE JERRY is a Nigerian disability-activist, human rights advocate, a musician and beauty pageant queen who was awarded the Miss Wheelchair Nigeria. She is a spokesperson for over 25 million Nigerians who are living with disability. Grace Jerry, the Executive Director of Xperience Grace Foundation has performed in concerts within and out Nigeria. She is the 2015 Mandela Washington Fellows who were honored by President Barack Obama.[1]
