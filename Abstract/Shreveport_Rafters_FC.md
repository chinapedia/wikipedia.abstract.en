SHREVEPORT RAFTERS FC is a soccer team based in Shreveport, Louisiana. Founded in 2016, the team plays in the National Premier Soccer League (NPSL), a national semi-professional league at the fourth tier of the American Soccer Pyramid, in the Lonestar Conference.[1] The team plays its home games at Independence Stadium.[2] The team's colors are blue, white, and yellow, based on the city's flag.
