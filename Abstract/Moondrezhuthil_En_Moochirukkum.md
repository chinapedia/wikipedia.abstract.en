_MOONDREZHUTHIL EN MOOCHIRUKKUM_ (English: My breath will be there in three letters) is a 1991 Tamil romantic drama film directed by Manobala. The film features Vijayakanth and Rupini in the lead roles, with newcomers Srinivas Varma and Geetha Vijayan, Anandaraj, Thilakan, Mansoor Ali Khan, Jayabharathi and Sathyapriya playing supporting roles. The film, produced by V. N. Selvaraj and K . K. Balasubramaniyan, had musical score by Ilayagangai and was released on 5 November 1991.[1][2][3]
