NADIA CHEAIB is a figure in the field of Health Care Research and Development in the Middle East and Africa. She is the founder of Clingroup Holding International for pharmaceutical research,[1] and the founder of HOPE MCF Mohamed Cheaib Foundation for philanthropic activities.[2] She appeared on Forbes 100 most powerful women in the Middle East in 2014[3] and 2016.[4][5][6][7]
