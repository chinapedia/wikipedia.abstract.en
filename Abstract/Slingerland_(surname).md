SLINGERLAND is a Dutch toponymic surname. It refers to an origin in the heerlijkheid of Slingelandt, now covered by the former community of Nederslingeland and the town of Overslingeland in South Holland. Variants are _(Van) Slingeland_ and _Slingelandt_.
