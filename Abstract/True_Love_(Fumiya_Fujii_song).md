"TRUE LOVE" (styled "TRUE LOVE") is a single by Japanese recording artist Fumiya Fujii. It was released on November 10, 1993.[1] It was number-one on the _Oricon_ Weekly Singles Chart.[2] It was the 29th best-selling single in Japan in 1993, with 806,000 copies sold,[3] the 11th best-selling single in Japan in 1994, with 1.213 million copies sold[4] and it is the 20th best-selling physical single in Japan, having sold a total of 2.023 million copies.[5]
