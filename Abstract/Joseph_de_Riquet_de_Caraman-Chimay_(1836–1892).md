Joseph_de_Riquet_de_Caraman-Chimay_(1836-1892)_B.jpg MARIE-_JOSEPH_-GUY-HENRY-PHILIPPE DE RIQUET DE CARAMAN, 18TH PRINCE DE CHIMAY (9 October 1836, Château de Menars, France – 29 March 1892, Brussels, Belgium), was a Belgian diplomat and politician. He was notable as Ambassador to the Holy See (1846-1847), governor of the province of Hainaut (1870-1878) and Foreign Minister (1884-1892).
