_THE ANTHOLOGY_ is the sixth album and first compilation by Autograph. Released as a two-disc set in 2011, it is a digital remaster of two Autograph albums, _Missing Pieces_ and _Buzz_. The first disc is a reissue of _Missing Pieces_ and contains additional tracks from _More Missing Pieces_. The second disc is the digital remaster of _Buzz_, reissued as _Buzz and Beyond_ with two additional versions of "Turn Up the Radio", an alternate electric version which was featured on Steve Plunkett's _My Attitude_ album and an acoustic version listed as "unplugged".
