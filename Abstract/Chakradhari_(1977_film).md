_CHAKRADHARI_ (English: A PERSON HOLDING A WHEEL - LORD KRISHNA) is a 1977 Telugu biographical film,[1] produced by N. R. Anuradha Devi under the Lakshmi Films Combines banner[2] and directed by V. Madhusudhan Rao.[3] The film stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Vanisri in the lead roles and music composed by G. K. Venkatesh [4] The movie was a remake of the 1974 Kannada movie _Bhakta Kumbara_, which was based on the life of potter-turned-saint Gora Kumbhar. G. K. Venkatesh, himself composed music for the Kannada version. 4 songs were also retained from the original version.[5][6]
