DE DEE HAME AZAADI (Miraculously given us freedom) or SABARMATI KE SANT is an Indian song written by Kavi Pradeep. It is a patriotic song dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi and his non-violence nature.[1][2][3] This a film soundtrack of Bollywood film _Jagriti_ (1954). This song was sung by Asha Bhosle.[4]
