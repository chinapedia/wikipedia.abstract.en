E.SOLUTIONS is a company developing automotive infotainment systems and digital dashboards. It is a joint venture of German Audi and Elektrobit (owned by Continental AG).[1] Audi's 'Virtual Cockpit' instrument cluster is developed by e.solutions. Current products utilize a NVidia Tegra Visual Computing Platform (VCM) graphics processing unit.[2] The company is headquartered in Ingolstadt with another office in Erlangen, both in Germany.[3]
