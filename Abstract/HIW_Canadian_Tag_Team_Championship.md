The HIW CANADIAN TAG TEAM CHAMPIONSHIP is a professional wrestling heavyweight championship in the professional wrestling promotion, High Impact Wrestling Canada. The championship was established on June 8, 2005.[1] On February 22, 2018; Los Rudos unveiled new title belts at Destruction Theory in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. The current champions are the Team Flex Appeal (Michael Allen Richard Clark and Mike McSugar) who are in their second reign. They defeated The Western Lions in a title match at Devil's Night: Halloween Show in a fans bring the weapons match.
