Dubzy_Snazz.jpg JESSE JAMES (born Jesse James on 1 February), better known by his stage name DUBZY, is an English grime MC, actor and entrepreneur. He is the co-founder of music collective and fashion label Snazz. He won Red Bull's 2016 Grime-a-Side championship with the Derby team[1] and finished second in _UK Grime_'s best MC's of 2016.[2] His "Snazz" headwear products, dubbed 'SnazzBacks',[3] were the first snapbacks to feature in GQ Magazine.[4]
