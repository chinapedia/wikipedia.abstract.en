SCHOOLCRAFT COUNTY AIRPORT, is a county-owned public-use airport located 3 miles (5 km) northeast of the central business district of Manistique, Michigan, a city in Schoolcraft County, Michigan, United States.[1] It is included in the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) National Plan of Integrated Airport Systems for 2017–2021, in which it is categorized as a basic general aviation facility.[2]
