OBSERVER MEDIA is an American online media company.[1][2][3] The company was formed through several acquisitions, including acquisition of _The New York Observer_ in 2007.[4][5][6] Observer Media is based in lower Manhattan, New York City, and had been owned by businessman Jared Kushner, until Joseph Meyer was named owner and publisher in January 2017.[7].[8][9][10] It currently publishes the _Commercial Observer_, observer.com, and commercialobserver.com.[11][12] As of November 2016, Observer Media announced it would no longer print the _New York Observer_. The observer.com site is a consolidation of several notable online properties, including _The Gallerist_, _BetaBeat_, _NY Politicker_, and _PolitickerNJ_.[13][14][15]
