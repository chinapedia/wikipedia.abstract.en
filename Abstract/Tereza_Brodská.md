TEREZA BRODSKÁ (born 7 May 1968) is a Czech actress. She is the daughter of Czech acting couple Vlastimil Brodský and Jana Brejchová. Brodská performed her first acting role in 1972. She has performed in long-running Czech soap _Ulice_. She won the Czech Lion award for Best Supporting Actress in 1995, for her role in _Má je pomsta_, and went on to win the award for Best Actress in 1999 for her role in the film _Dvojrole_.
