_TO BUILD A FIRE_ (French: _CONSTRUIRE UN FEU_) is a 2016 French animated short film directed by Fx Goby adapted from the short story _To Build a Fire_ by Jack London. The film has been presented in a number of festivals including the Cannes Film Festival,[1] the Anibar International Animation Festival,[2] the New Orleans Film Festival[3] and the Rhode Island International Film Festival where it won the prize for best animation.[4]
