DEVO (, originally )[1] is an American rock band from Akron, Ohio formed in 1973. Their classic lineup consisted of two sets of brothers, the Mothersbaughs (Mark and Bob) and the Casales (Gerald and Bob), along with Alan Myers. The band had a No. 14 _Billboard_ chart hit in 1980 with the single "Whip It", the song that gave the band mainstream popularity.
