_PAL PAL DIL KE PAAS_ () is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language romantic drama film directed by Sunny Deol and produced by Sunny Sounds P Ltd and Zee Studios. Principal photography began on 21 May 2017.[1] Karan Deol and Sahher Bambba were cast for the lead roles. Sunny Deol had auditioned over 400 women for Bambba's role.[2][3] The film is scheduled for release on 20 September 2019.[4]
