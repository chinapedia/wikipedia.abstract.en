HELLIONS is an Australian punk rock[1] band from Sydney, formed in 2013 after the break up of The Bride, in which drummer Anthony Caruso, bassist turned vocalist Dre Faivre and guitarist/vocalist Matt Gravolin continued under a different moniker. They have released four studio albums; their third, _Opera Oblivia_, was nominated for the 2016 ARIA Award for Best Hard Rock/Heavy Metal Album.[2] _Rue_ their fourth studio album was released on 19 October 2018 via UNFD.[3]
