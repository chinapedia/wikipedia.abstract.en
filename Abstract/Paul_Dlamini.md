PAUL DLAMINI is a Swazi politician. He has been the Deputy Prime Minister of Swaziland since 2013, succeeding Themba Nhlanganiso Masuku in his position. He is the first of the renowned Dlamini clan to be appointed to such a high position in the national government.[1] In October 2015 he led an eight-member delegation to Taiwan to support the 104th National Day celebrations of the Republic of China.[2]
