Houses_of_Parliament_(Cape_Town).jpg in Cape Town.]] CentralPretoria.jpg, the administrative capital of South Africa.]] The Republic of South Africa is a parliamentary representative democratic republic. The President of South Africa serves both as head of state and as head of government. The President is elected by the National Assembly (the lower house of the South African Parliament) and must retain the confidence of the Assembly in order to remain in office. South Africans also elect provincial legislatures which govern each of the country's nine provinces.
