The 2016 Åžemdinli bombing was a car bomb attack on Turkish military base carried out by the PKK's armed wing HPG[1] in Turkey which occurred on 9 October 2016. According to the Turkish authorities, a car bomb killed at least 18 people including 10 soldiers and 8 civilians. Also, another 27 people were wounded according to initial reports. The PKK's armed wing HPG claimed the attack and claimed to have killed more than 32 soldiers.
