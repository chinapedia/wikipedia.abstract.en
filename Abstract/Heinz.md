The H. J. HEINZ COMPANY, better known as HEINZ, is an American food processing company headquartered in Chicago, Illinois, part of the Kraft Heinz Company.[1] The company was founded by Henry John Heinz in 1869. Heinz manufactures thousands of food products in plants on six continents, and markets these products in more than 200 countries and territories. The company claims to have 150 number-one or number-two brands worldwide.[2] Heinz ranked first in ketchup in the US with a market share in excess of 50%;[3][4] the Ore-Ida label held 46% of the frozen potato sector in 2003.[5]
