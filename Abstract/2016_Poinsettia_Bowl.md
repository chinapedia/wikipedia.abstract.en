The 2016 POINSETTIA BOWL was a college football bowl game played on December 21, 2016 at Qualcomm Stadium in San Diego, California. The twelfth and final edition of the Poinsettia Bowl featured the BYU Cougars against the Wyoming Cowboys.[1] It began at 6:00 PM PST and aired ESPN.
