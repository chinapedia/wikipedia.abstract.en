A variety of symbols or iconographic conventions are used to represent EARTH, either in the sense of planet Earth, or the inhabited world. Representations of the globe of Earth, either with an indication of the shape of the continents or with a representation of meridians and parallels remains a common pictographic convention to express the notion of "worldwide, global". The modern astronomical symbol for Earth as a planet uses either a stylized globus cruciger () or a circle with a cross (representing the equator and one meridian; ().[1][2]
