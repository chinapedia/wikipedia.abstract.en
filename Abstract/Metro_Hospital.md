METRO HEART INSTITUTE WITH MULTISPECIALTY HOSPITAL in Faridabad is known for it remarkable medical achievements[1] often in surgical and cardiac areas. It's a National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers approved hospital with a National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories medical laboratory. The hospital has now become a chain of hospitals at multiple locations including Noida, Delhi, Gurgaon, Haridwar, Revari, Meerut, Jaipur and Vadodara.[2]
