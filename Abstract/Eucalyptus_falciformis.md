_EUCALYPTUS FALCIFORMIS_, commonly known as the GRAMPIANS PEPPERMINT[1] or WESTERN PEPPERMINT,[2] is a species of tree or mallee that is endemic in south eastern Australia. It has smooth bark, sometimes with rough, fibrous bark at the base, narrow lance-shaped to egg-shaped or curved adult leaves, flower buds in groups of eleven to twenty one, white flowers and cup-shaped to shortly cylindrical fruit.
