_HOUNDS OF LOVE_ is a 2016 Australian crime-thriller film written and directed by Ben Young. The plot concerns a couple who kidnap and terrorise a young woman in the suburbs of Perth. The film is Young's directorial debut.[1][2]It was selected in the Venice Days competition at the 73rd edition of the Venice Film Festival, in which Ashleigh Cummings was awarded a Fedeora Award for best actress.
