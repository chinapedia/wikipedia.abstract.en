Craig_Johnson.jpg CRAIG PAUL JOHNSON (born September 16, 1963) is an American politician who was elected as a Republican member of the Iowa State Senate's 32nd district in 2016. He defeated Democratic incumbent Brian Schoenjahn.[1] He previously ran for election to the Iowa House of Representatives in 2014 for the 64th district.[2] Johnson, born and raised in Independence, Iowa, is a financial planner and former sales and engineer manager.
