_ESCAPE OR DIE!_ was a Canadian reality television series produced by Farpoint Films that premiered[1] in Canada in 2015 on OLN.[2] Starring escape artist Dean Gunnarson,[3] the series showed the behind-the-scenes preparations and escapes filmed in various locations around the world. The series featured Cary Tardi,[4] Jeff Gunnarson, Ava Darrach-Gagnon, and John MacDonald as part of Dean's team that helped make the escapes happen and keep him safe. The show also featured appearances by magician and skeptic James Randi[5] and Colombian magician Gustavo Lorgia.[6]
