SARAH HAVLIN is the first woman to be appointed to the role of Certification Officer of Northern Ireland, the regulator of trade unions and employer associations.[1] She lives in Bangor, County Down. She is a solicitor by profession, and was appointed by the Northern Ireland Secretary of State as a Parades Commissioner for Northern Ireland in 2013. In 2015 she was appointed as Parliamentary Boundary Commissioner for Northern Ireland in accordance with Schedule 1 of the Parliamentary Constituencies Act 1986.
