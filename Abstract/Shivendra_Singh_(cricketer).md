SHIVENDRA SINGH (born 2 August 1991) is an Indian first-class cricketer who plays for Chhattisgarh.[1] He made his first-class debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2016-17 Ranji Trophy on 29 November 2016.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2017–18 Zonal T20 League on 8 January 2018.[3] He made his List A debut for Chhattisgarh in the 2017–18 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 5 February 2018.[4]
