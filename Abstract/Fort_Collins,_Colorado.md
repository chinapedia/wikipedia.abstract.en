FORT COLLINS is the Home Rule Municipality that is the county seat[1] and the most populous municipality of Larimer County, Colorado, United States.[2] Situated on the Cache La Poudre River along the Colorado Front Range, Fort Collins is located north of the Colorado State Capitol in Denver. With a 2016 estimated population of 161,000,[3] it is the fourth most populous city in Colorado after Denver, Colorado Springs, and Aurora. Fort Collins is a midsize college city, home to Colorado State University.
