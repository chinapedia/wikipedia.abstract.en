INFORMATION SECURITY, sometimes shortened to INFOSEC, is the practice of protecting information by mitigating information risks. It is part of information risk management. It typically involves preventing or at least reducing the probability of unauthorized/inappropriate access, use, disclosure, disruption, deletion/destruction, corruption, modification, inspection, recording or devaluation, although it may also involve reducing the adverse impacts of incidents. Information may take any form, e.g. electronic or physical.,[1] tangible (e.g. paperwork) or intangible (e.g. knowledge).
