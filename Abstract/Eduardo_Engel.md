EDUARDO ENGEL is the former President and current board member of the think tank Espacio Público and Professor at the Department of Economics of the University of Chile.[1] From 2001 to 2012 he was Professor of Economics at the Yale University.[2] He chaired the Presidential Advisory Council on Conflicts of Interest, Influence Peddling, and Corruption – also known as the Engel Commission – in 2015.[3]
