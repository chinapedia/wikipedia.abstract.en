DENMARK HIGH SCHOOL is a public high school in Forsyth County, Georgia, United States, and its construction was finished in August 2018. Denmark was opened with the main purpose of relieving the issue of overcrowded schools in South Forsyth High School and West Forsyth High School.[1] It is named after the late Leila Daughtry Denmark (1898-2012), who was one of the first female pediatricians in Georgia and retired in 2001 at the age of 103.[2][3]
