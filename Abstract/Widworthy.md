St_Cuthbert's_church,_Widworthy_-_geograph.org.uk_-_443571.jpg Widworthy,_Widworthy_Barton_-_geograph.org.uk_-_290348.jpg WIDWORTHY is a village, parish and former manor in Devon, England. The village is situated 3 1/2 miles east of Honiton. The parish church is dedicated to St Cuthbert. Near the church is Widworthy Barton, the former manor house, which is largely unaltered from its early 17th century form.[1] Widworthy Court is a mansion within the parish built in 1830 by Sir Edward Marwood Elton to the design of G.S. Repton.[2]
