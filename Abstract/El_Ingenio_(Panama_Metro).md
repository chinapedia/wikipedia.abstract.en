EL INGENIO is a Panama Metro station on Line 1. It was originally scheduled to open in August 2014,[1] but opened on 8 May 2015.[2] The station cost US$30 million and was officially opened by ministers Roberto Roy and Álvaro Alemán.[3] It was the thirteenth station opened on the network and is the final subterranean station before the line becomes elevated before 12 de Octubre station.[4]
