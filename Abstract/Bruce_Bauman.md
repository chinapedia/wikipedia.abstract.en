BRUCE BAUMAN is an American writer. He is the author of the novels _Broken Sleep_ (2015) and _And The Word Was_ (2006).[1] His work has appeared in the _Los Angeles Times_, _Salon_, _BOMB_, _Bookforum_, _Dart International Magazine_, and _Black Clock_. He has previously been awarded the City of Los Angeles Award in literature (2008-2009), a Durfee Foundation grant,[2] and an UNESCO/Aschberg award.[3]
