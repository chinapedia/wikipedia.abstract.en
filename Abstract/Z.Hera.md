JI HYE-RAN (born January 3, 1996), better known by her stage name Z.HERA, is a South Korean singer and actress. Z.Hera appeared on the documentary program _Human Theater_ in 2006 as Shaolin Girl when she was 11 years old. She received attention after being the first South Korean singer to appear on the American social site BuzzFeed.[1] She is known for her roles in _Moorim School_ (2016) and _Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo_ (2016).
