_EUCALYPTUS ARGOPHLOIA_, commonly known as the QUEENSLAND WESTERN WHITE GUM,[1] QUEENSLAND WHITE GUM, SCRUB GUM, LAPUNYAH,[2] BURNCLUITH GUM[3] or CHINCHILLA WHITE GUM[4] is a tree that is endemic to a small area of Queensland. It has smooth white bark ageing to other colours, narrow lance-shaped adult leaves, more or less spherical flower buds in groups of seven, white flowers and small, hemispherical to cup-shaped fruit.Eucalyptus_argophloia_buds.jpgEucalyptus_argophloia_bark.jpg]]
