The IMMACULATE CONCEPTION CHURCH[1] ( ) is an old Catholic church in the city of Smolensk in Russia.[2][3] This neo-Gothic building was built from 1884 to 1896 and hosted the Regional Archive of Smolensk. It is in a state of disrepair and is scheduled for complete restoration. It has a large body and remarkable stained glass windows. Religious services are now partial. The number of parishioners is around 9,000.
