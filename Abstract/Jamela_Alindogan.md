JAMELA AISHA ALINDOGAN (Born Approx 1986- ) is a journalist born in the (Sorsogon ?) Philippines and an Al Jazeera producer who reports from Asia. She attended Far Eastern University from 2002 through 2006 and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Communications with a major in Broadcast Journalism. She worked as a news reporter for ABS-CBN for 2 years before going to work at Al Jazeera English in August 2008.
