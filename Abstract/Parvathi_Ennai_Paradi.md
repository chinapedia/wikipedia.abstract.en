_PARVATHI ENNAI PARADI_ (English: Parvathi, look at me!) is a 1993 Tamil romantic drama film directed by V. Sekhar. The film features Saravanan and newcomer Sri Parvathi in the lead roles, with Janagaraj, Vijayakumar, Srividya, Lalitha Kumari, Charle and Vasu Vikram playing supporting roles. The film, produced by K. R. Gangadharan, had musical score by Ilaiyaraaja and was released on 23 July 1993.[1][2][3][4]
