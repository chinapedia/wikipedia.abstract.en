_THE PRINCESS WEIYOUNG_ () is a 2016 Chinese television series starring Tiffany Tang in the title role, alongside Luo Jin, Vanness Wu, Mao Xiaotong and Li Xinai. It is adapted from the novel _The Poisonous Daughter_ () by Qin Jian and is a fictionalized account of Emperor Wencheng of Northern Wei's reign and Empress Feng (Wencheng)'s regency.[1] The series aired on Dragon TV and Beijing TV from 11 November to 9 December 2016.[2]
