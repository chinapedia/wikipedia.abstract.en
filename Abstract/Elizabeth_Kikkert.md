ELIZABETH FALEMAKA KIKKERT (born 3 July 1980)[1] is an Australian politician. She has been a Liberal member of the Australian Capital Territory Legislative Assembly since 2016, representing the electorate of Ginninderra. She was born in Tonga and was a full-time mother and volunteer before entering politics. She is also a practising member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and she and her husband have five children.[2]
