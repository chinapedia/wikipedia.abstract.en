Joseph_Onasakenrat.jpg, chief of the Mohawks of Kanesatake from 1868 to 1881]] MOHAWKS OF KANESATAKE or KANEHSATÀ:KEHRÓ:NON are a Mohawk First Nation in Quebec, Canada. In 2016 the band has a registered population of 2,508 members. Its main reserve is Kanesatake Lands located west of Montreal. It also shares the uninhabited reserve of Doncaster 17 with the Mohawks of Kahnawá:ke for hunting and fishing.
