TEATR DEMONA (, lit. _Demon's Theatre_) is the tenth studio album of the Russian punk-rock band "Korol i Shut", released in 2010. It was the last album of the band featuring the band's long-time main lyric writer, the second vocalist and the founding member Andrei Knyazev, who left the band in 2011. The entire album was recorded with the acoustic instruments only, in an unusual for the band genre of the Art punk.
