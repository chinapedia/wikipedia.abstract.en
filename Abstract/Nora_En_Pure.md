DANIELA NIEDERER (born July 20, 1990),[1] better known by her stage name NORA EN PURE, is a South African-Swiss DJ and deep house producer.[2] She first received recognition for her 2013 single "Come With Me". Further popular releases are "Morning Dew", "Lake Arrowhead" and "Tears In Your Eyes". In July 2016, she was invited to put together a two-hour long mix for Pete Tong's radio show: "BBC's Essential Mix".[3]
