HINDHEDE NATURE PARK is a park located next to Bukit Timah Nature Reserve, in Singapore. A short trail leads into the park starting from the visitor centre and looping to the Hindhede Quarry 90 metres away. Animals that can be found in the park include the Banded Woodpecker, Clouded Monitor Lizard, Greater Racket-tailed Drongo and Plaintain Squirrel.[1]
