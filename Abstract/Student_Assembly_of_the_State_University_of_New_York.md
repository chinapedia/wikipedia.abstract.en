SUNYSALogo.jpg The STUDENT ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE UNIVERSITY OF NEW YORK (SUNYSA) is the university-wide student government for the 64 institutions of the State University of New York (SUNY).[1] It is empowered by Article XVII of the policies of the SUNY Board of Trustees to represent student concerns, elect the student member of the Board, and to act as a communications network between campus student leaders.[2][3][4][5][6]
