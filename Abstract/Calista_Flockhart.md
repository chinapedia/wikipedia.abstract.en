CALISTA KAY FLOCKHART (born November 11, 1964)[1][2] is an American actress. She starred as the title character in the legal comedy-drama series _Ally McBeal_ (1997–2002), Kitty Walker in the drama series _Brothers & Sisters_ (2006–2011) and Cat Grant in the superhero drama series _Supergirl_ (2015–2019). She has also been featured in a number of films, including the comedy film _The Birdcage_ (1996), the romantic comedy film _A Midsummer Night's Dream_ (1999), and the drama film _Things You Can Tell Just by Looking at Her_ (2000).
