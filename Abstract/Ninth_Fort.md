IX_Fort_(2008-09-20)01.jpg Kaunas_Fortress.Ninth_Fort_3.jpg The NINTH FORT () is a stronghold in the northern part of Å ilainiai elderate, Kaunas, Lithuania. It is a part of the Kaunas Fortress, which was constructed in the late 19th century. During the occupation of Kaunas and the rest of Lithuania by the Soviet Union, the fort was used as a prison and way-station for prisoners being transported to labour camps. After the occupation of Lithuania by Nazi Germany, the fort was used as a place of execution for Jews, captured Soviets, and others.[1]
