VIKAS YADAV (born 20 July 1996) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Services in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 6 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Services in the 2017–18 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 5 February 2018.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Services in the 2018–19 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy on 21 February 2019.[4]
