_PACHNEPHORUS FASCIATUS OCCIDENTALIS_ is a subspecies of _Pachnephorus fasciatus_, a species of leaf beetle. It is found in Senegal,[1] Gambia, Guinea Bissau, Sierra Leone, Nigeria, and was described by Stefano Zoia in 2007. The subspecies name refers to the subspecies occurring in an occidental (western) region, compared to the area the nominal subspecies is found in.[2]
