The KOREAN PEOPLE'S ARMY (KPA; , ) is an institution of the Workers' Party of Korea, and constitutes the _de facto_ military forces of North Korea. Under the _Songun_ policy, it is the central institution of North Korean community. Kim Jong-un is its Supreme Commander and the Chairman of the Central Military Commission. The KPA consists of five branches: Ground Force, the Navy, the Air Force, the Strategic Rocket Forces, and the Special Operation Force.
