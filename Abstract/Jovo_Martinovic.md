Jovo_martinovic_660x330.jpg JOVO MARTINOVIC is a freelance investigative journalist in Montenegro, known for his reporting on organized crime in Europe and war criminals in the Balkans. His reporting has appeared in international news outlets, including _The Economist_, _Time_, _Newsday_ and the _Financial Times_. Among his noteworthy projects was helping produce a Vice News documentary on a gang of jewel thieves known as the Pink Panthers.[1] Since October 22, 2015 he has been in prison in Montenegro and has been charged with involvement in a drug trafficking ring.
