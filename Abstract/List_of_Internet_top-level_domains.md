This LIST OF INTERNET TOP-LEVEL DOMAINS (TLD) contains top-level domains, which are those domains in the DNS root zone of the Domain Name System of the Internet. The official list of all top-level domains is maintained by the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA) at the Root Zone Database.[1][2] IANA also oversees the approval process for new proposed top-level domains. , the root domain contains 1530 top-level domains,[3] while a few have been retired and are no longer functional.
