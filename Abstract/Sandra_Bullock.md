SANDRA ANNETTE BULLOCK (; born July 26, 1964) is an American actress, producer, and philanthropist. She was the highest paid actress in the world in 2010 and 2014.[1][2][3] In 2015, Bullock was chosen as _People_{{'s}} Most Beautiful Woman[4] and was included in _Time_{{'s}} 100 most influential people in the world in 2010. Bullock is the recipient of several accolades, including an Academy Award and a Golden Globe Award.
