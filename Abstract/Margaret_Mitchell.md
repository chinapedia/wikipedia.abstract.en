Mitchell wrote only one novel, published during her lifetime, the American Civil War-era novel _Gone with the Wind_, for which she won the National Book Award for Most Distinguished Novel of 1936[2] and the Pulitzer Prize for Fiction in 1937. In more recent years, a collection of Mitchell's girlhood writings and a novella she wrote as a teenager, _Lost Laysen_, have been published. A collection of articles written by Mitchell for _The Atlanta Journal_ was republished in book form.
