O'BANNON HOMESTEAD, also known as SCHUYLER STOCK FARM, STEEPLE HOUSE, and FOUR LEAF CLOVER FARM, is a historic home and farm complex located near Garden City, Cass County, Missouri. The farmhouse was built in 1893, and is a two-story, rectangular, frame dwelling with Queen Anne style embellishments. It features stained glass, an encircling verandah, an octagonal tower and decorative spindle, spool and shingle work. Also on the property are six contributing outbuildings: a wash house / smokehouse, work house, pump house, chicken house, outhouse, and barn.[1]
