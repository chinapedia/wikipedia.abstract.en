_DEN SOM HENGER I EN TRÃ…D_ (Those Hanging by a Thread) is a 1935 novel by the Norwegian new realist author Nini Roll Anker.[1] The book portrays a women's working atmosphere in a group of seamstresses at a clothing factory and the poor conditions they worked under.[2] The book discusses topics such as abortion, religion, and the position of women. The book is one of the most important contributions to Norwegian women's literature.
