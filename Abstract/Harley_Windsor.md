HARLEY WINDSOR (born HARLEY DAHLSTROM-WINSOR; 22 October 1996) is an Australian pair skater. With his skating partner, Ekaterina Alexandrovskaya, he is the 2017 CS Tallinn Trophy champion, the 2017 CS Nebelhorn Trophy bronze medalist and a two-time Australian national champion (2016, 2018). On the junior level, he is the 2017 World Junior champion, the 2017 Junior Grand Prix Final champion, the 2016 JGP Estonia champion and the 2017 JGP Latvia champion.
