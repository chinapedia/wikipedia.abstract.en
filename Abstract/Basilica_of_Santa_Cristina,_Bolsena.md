SANTA CRISTINA is a Roman Catholic basilica church in Bolsena, province of Viterbo, region of Lazio, Italy. The church is best known for being the site of a Eucharistic Miracle in 1263, immortalized by _The Mass at Bolsena_ by Raphael in the Vatican palace.
