JAMES FRIDMAN is a British graphic designer known for taking requests on Twitter for alterations to photographs, but reliably responding with an image edited to slightly different specifications, to comedic effect. Many fans submit photos of themselves with a request to alter the image, expecting a humorous result, to which Fridman replies. He and his work have been featured in various publications, such as _Cosmopolitan_,[1] _The Daily Telegraph_,[2] _Daily Mail_,[3] MTV,[4] _Huffington Post_,[5] _Metro_,[6] BuzzFeed,[7] Mashable[8] and others regularly featuring articles on his latest work.
