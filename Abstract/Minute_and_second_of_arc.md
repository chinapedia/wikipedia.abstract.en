A MINUTE OF ARC, ARCMINUTE (arcmin), ARC MINUTE, or MINUTE ARC is a unit of angular measurement equal to of one degree. Since one degree is of a turn (or complete rotation), one minute of arc is of a turn. The nautical mile was originally defined as a minute of latitude on a hypothetical spherical Earth so the actual Earth circumference is very near 21 600 nautical miles. A minute of arc is of a radian.
