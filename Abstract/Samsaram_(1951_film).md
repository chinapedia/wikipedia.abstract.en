_SAMSARAM_ () is a 1951 Indian Tamil-language drama film directed by Chandru and produced by S. S. Vasan. A remake of the 1950 Telugu film of the same name, it features M. K. Radha, Pushpavalli, Kumari Vanaja, Sriram, M. S. Sundari Bai, T. R. Ramachandran, D. Balasubramaniam, R. Balasubramaniam, Master Sethu and Ratnapapa. It was simultaneously filmed in Hindi as _SANSAAR_, which Vasan directed.[1]
