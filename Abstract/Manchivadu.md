_MANCHIVADU_ (English: GOOD MAN) is a 1973 Telugu drama film, produced by T. Govindarajan under the Venus Combines banner and directed by V. Madhusudhan Rao.[1] It stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Vanisri, Kanchana in the lead roles and music composed by K. V. Mahadevan.[2] The film is a remake of the Kannada movie _Baalu Belagithu_ (1970), which was also remade in Hindi as _Humshakal_ (1974) and in Tamil as _Oorukku Uzhaippavan_ (1976).
