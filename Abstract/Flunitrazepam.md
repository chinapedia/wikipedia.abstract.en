FLUNITRAZEPAM, also known as ROHYPNOL among other names,[1] is a benzodiazepine used in some countries to treat severe insomnia and assist with anesthesia.[2] As with other hypnotics, flunitrazepam has been advised to be prescribed only on a short-term basis or by those with chronic insomnia on an occasional basis.[3] It is a schedule I controlled substance in the United States of America.
