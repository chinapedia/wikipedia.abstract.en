_BROKEN ONES_ is the debut extended play recorded by American singer and runner-up of fifth season of _The Voice_, Jacquie Lee. It was released through Atlantic Records and 300 Entertainment on October 21, 2014.[1] A cover of Cyndi Lauper's 1983 hit, "Girls Just Want to Have Fun" is included along with four original songs co-written by popular songwriters such as Sia Furler and Andreas Carlsson. The EP failed to reach the _Billboard_ 200, but did peak at 193 on the Top Current Albums component chart.[2]
