The Maratha community originally organised silent rallies across the state. A few cases of violence in July were noted after 2 years of silent protest. It was a silent protest against the rape and murder in Kopardi[1] (district Ahmednagar), the victim was a minor school girl. The incident took place on 13 July 2016 between 6.45 pm and 7.30 pm at Kopardi, in Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra. The protesters demand death sentence to rapists as early as possible.
