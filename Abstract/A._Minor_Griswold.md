Griswold joined the Cleveland _Plain Dealer_ in 1860 as associate editor, and after nearly five years joined the Cincinnati _Evening Times._[2][3] He toured the country giving humorous lectures, and died of apoplexy in Sheboygan Falls, Wisconsin, while on tour at the age of 57.[4][5]
