MIMIMI PRODUCTIONS is a German video game software development company based in Munich. They are known for developing the platforming game _The Last Tinker: City of Colors_[1] and the real-time tactics game _Shadow Tactics: Blades of the Shogun_,[2] which was released in December 2016. The company is currently developing _Desperados III_, which is set to be released for Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, published by THQ Nordic.[3]
