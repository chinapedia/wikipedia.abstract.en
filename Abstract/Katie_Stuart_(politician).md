KATIE STUART is a Democratic member of the Illinois House of Representatives from the 112th district.[1] The 112th district is located in the Metro East and includes all or parts of Caseyville, Collinsville Edwardsville, Fairview Heights, Glen Carbon, Granite City, Marryville and O'Fallon.[2] Stuart, an Edwardsville resident, taught math at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville prior to taking office in the Illinois House of Representatives.[3]
