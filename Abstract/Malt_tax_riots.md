The riots began in Hamilton on 23 June 1725 and soon spread throughout the country. The fiercest protests, the SHAWFIELD RIOTS, were in Glasgow, but significant disturbances occurred in Edinburgh, Stirling, Dundee, Ayr, Dundee, Elgin and Paisley.[1]
