CHRISTIAN FAGAN (born 23 June 1961)[1] is a former Australian rules footballer who is the senior coach of the Brisbane Lions in the Australian Football League (AFL).[2] He spent his entire playing career in Tasmania, playing 263 senior games with Hobart, Sandy Bay, and Devonport. Before being appointed head coach of Brisbane in October 2016, Fagan had spent long periods as an assistant coach at Melbourne (1999–2007) and Hawthorn (2008–2016).
