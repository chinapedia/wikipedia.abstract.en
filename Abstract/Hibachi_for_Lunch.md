_HIBACHI FOR LUNCH_ is the third extended play (EP)[1] by American rapper 2 Chainz.[2][3][4] It was released on October 28, 2016.[5] The project consists of seven tracks, with guest appearances from Quavo, Gucci Mane, Ty Dolla Sign and Future, with production from Buddah Bless, DJ Paul, FKi 1st, Foreign Teck, K Swisha, Mike Dean, Mike Will Made It, Mondo, Supah Mario and TWhy Xclusive. The project was self-released. Originally the third track on the tape, "Good Drank" was removed from the tracklist and released January 20, 2017, as the lead single from his fourth studio album _Pretty Girls Like Trap Music_.[6]
