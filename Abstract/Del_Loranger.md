DELBERT LORANGER (March 17, 1920 – March 24, 2003) was an American professional basketball player and coach.[1][2] He played in numerous independent and professional leagues, including the National Basketball League for the Detroit Gems, Indianapolis Kautskys, and Detroit Vagabond Kings. As a player, he averaged 6.8 points per game while in the NBL.[3] Loranger also served as the Vagabond Kings' head coach during the 1948–49 season.[4]
