GLADYS HALL (born April 26, 1891, New York - d. Huntington, Long Island, September 18, 1977) was an American journalist. She wrote a syndicated column, "The Diary of a Professional Movie Fan," in the 1920s, and interviewed movie stars for such fan magazines as _Photoplay, Modern Screen,_ and _Screenland. "_The public,” she once said, “don't want their stars torn down, they want to believe in them, like Santa Claus.”[1]
