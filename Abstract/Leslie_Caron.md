LESLIE CLAIRE MARGARET CARON (; born 1 July 1931) is a Franco-American actress and dancer who appeared in 45 films between 1951 and 2003. Her autobiography, _Thank Heaven_, was published in 2010 in the UK and US, and in 2011 in a French version. Veteran documentarian Larry Weinstein's _Leslie Caron: The Reluctant Star_ premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival on 28 June 2016.[1]
