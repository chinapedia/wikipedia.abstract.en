LYNGDAL CHURCH () is a parish church in Lyngdal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the town of Lyngdal. The church is part of the Lyngdal parish in the Lister deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden church was built in 1848 by the architect Gabriel Kirsebom Kielland. The church seats about 1400 people.[1][2]
