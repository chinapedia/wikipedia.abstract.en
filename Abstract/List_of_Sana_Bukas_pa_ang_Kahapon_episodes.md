_SANA BUKAS PA ANG KAHAPON_ (Lit: _I Wish Yesterday Were Still Tomorrow_; English: _Tomorrow Belongs to Me_) is a 2014 Philippine melodrama television series directed by Jerome C. Pobocan and Trina N. Dayrit, starring Bea Alonzo, Paulo Avelino and Albert Martinez, with Maricar Reyes, together with an ensemble cast.[1][2] The drama was named after the 1983 film of the same name, courtesy of Viva Films. The series was aired on ABS-CBN and worldwide on The Filipino Channel from June 16, 2014 to October 10, 2014, replacing _The Legal Wife_.[3][4][5][6][7]
