SERIFATU OLADUNNI ODUGUWA, popularly known by her stagename QUEEN OLADUNNI DECENCY or QUEEN MUMMY JUJU,[1] was a Nigerian singer and guitarist who specialized in the Jùjú genre of music.[2] Regarded as the first female guitarist in Nigeria, she was the founder and leader of a Jùjú music band called Her Majesty Queen Oladunni Decency And Her Unity Orchestra.[3] She recorded many hit songs until her death at the age of 28.[4]
