TERESA REBULL, née TERESA SOLER I PI (1919[1]-2015) was a nurse for the POUM who later became a singer. She was actively involved in the May Days events and punished for that. She later escaped Spain, where she could face danger from both Stalinists and Francoists, for France. In France she would join the Maquis after the Nazi invasion.[2] After the war she became musically associated to Nova Cançó.[3][4]
