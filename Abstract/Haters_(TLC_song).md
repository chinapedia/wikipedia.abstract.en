"HATERS" is a song by American girl group TLC, from their fifth studio album, _TLC_ (2017). It was released on October 26, 2016, in Japan and November 1, 2016 in New Zealand. The song was later released as the second single from the album on May 5, 2017, in other countries.[1] The song was written by Michael Busbee, Daniel Ullman, Bryan Jarett, Maureen McDonald.
