NETLANDSNES CHAPEL () is a parish church in Kvinesdal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Netland along the river Kvina, about northwest of Fjotland. The church is part of the Fjotland parish in the Lister deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden chapel was built in 1886 by the architect H.A. Fedde. The chapel seats about 100 people.[1][2]
