LJOSLAND CHAPEL () is a parish church in Åseral municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Ljosland. The church is part of the Åseral parish in the Mandal deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The brown, wooden chapel was built in 1959 by the architect Olav Erikstad. The chapel seats about 95 people.[1][2]
