_EUCALYPTUS EXILIPES_, commonly known as the FINE-LEAVED IRONBARK,[1] is a species of medium to tall tree and is endemic in Queensland. It has dark grey or black "ironbark", linear to narrow lance-shaped adult leaves, flower buds in groups of seven, white flowers and cup-shaped to shortened spherical fruit. It is similar to _E. crebra_, differing only in the length of the pedicels.
