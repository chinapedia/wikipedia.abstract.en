J. MAPLE AND GRACE SENNE WILSON HOUSE is a historic home located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It built in 1903-1904, and is a 2 1/2-story, Colonial Revival style red brick dwelling. It has a hipped roof, a rooftop balustrade, and a central hipped dormer. It features a one-story full-width porch supported by Tuscan order columns and topped with a balustrade and two-story octagonal corner tower.[1]
