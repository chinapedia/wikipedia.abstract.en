KVÅS CHURCH () is a parish church in Lyngdal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Kvås. The church is part of the Kvås parish in the Lister deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden church was built in 1836 by an unknown architect. The church seats about 300 people.[1][2]
