VERITIV CORPORATION (NYSE: VRTV), headquartered in Atlanta, GA and a Fortune 500 company,[1] is a leading North American business-to-business distributor of packaging, facility solutions, print and publishing products and services; and also a provider of logistics and supply chain management solutions. Serving customers in a wide range of industries, the Company has approximately 170 operating distribution centers throughout the U.S., Canada and Mexico, and employs approximately 8,900 people. Mechanicsburg in Pennsylvania is their largest and most profitable warehouse although employing only about 55 workers.
