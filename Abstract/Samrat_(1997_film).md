_SAMRAT_ is a 1997 Tamil crime-thriller film directed by C. Dhinakaran. The film features Ramki, Vineetha and newcomer Rukma in the lead roles, with Mohan Natarajan, R. Sundarrajan, Thalaivasal Vijay, Sabitha Anand, Vasuki, Rajasekhar and Veera Pandiyan playing supporting roles. The film, produced by Lakshmi Karan, had musical score by Manoj Saran and was released on 10 October 1997. The film is remake of Hindi film _Baazigar_, which based on the novel _A Kiss Before Dying_ written by Ira Levin. The film became a failure at the box-office.[1][2][3][4].
