H3LLB3NT were an electro-industrial supergroup formed in 1995. The original line-up band consisted of Bryan Barton (of haloblack), Charles Levi (of Pigface), Jared Louche (of Chemlab), Jordan Nogood and Eric Powell (of 16Volt). They released three full length albums: _0.01_ (1996), _Helium_ (1998), _Hardcore Vanilla_ (2001)
