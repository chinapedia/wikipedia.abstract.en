MALMÖ (, also ,[1][2][3] ; ) is the largest city of the Swedish county of Skåne County, the third-largest city in Sweden, after Stockholm and Gothenburg, and the sixth-largest city in Scandinavia, with a population of 316,588 inhabitants out of a municipal total of 338,230 in 2018.[4] The Malmö Metropolitan Region is home to over 700,000 people,[5] and the Øresund Region, which includes Malmö, is home to 4 million people.[6]
