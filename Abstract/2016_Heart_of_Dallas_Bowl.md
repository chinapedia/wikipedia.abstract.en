The 2016 HEART OF DALLAS BOWL was a postseason college football bowl game played at the Cotton Bowl in Dallas, Texas, on December 27, 2016.[1] The seventh Heart of Dallas Bowl featured the Army Black Knights versus the North Texas Mean Green of Conference USA.[2] Sponsored by chicken fast food restaurant Zaxby's, the game was officially known as the ZAXBY'S HEART OF DALLAS BOWL.
