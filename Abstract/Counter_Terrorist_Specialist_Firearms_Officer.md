A COUNTER TERRORIST SPECIALIST FIREARMS OFFICER (CTSFO) is a United Kingdom police firearms officer.[1] The CTSFO standard is the highest Authorised Firearms Officer level in the National Police Firearms Training Curriculum (NPFTC) and was established by the Metropolitan Police Service in the lead up to the 2012 Summer Olympics held in London in July 2012.[2][3] The Firearms units of police forces organise CTSFOs into teams to establish a police tactical unit.
