LAND ROVER is a luxury car brand that specialises in four-wheel-drive vehicles, owned by British multinational car manufacturer Jaguar Land Rover, which has been owned by India's Tata Motors since 2008.[1] The Land Rover is regarded as a British icon, and was granted a Royal Warrant by King George VI in 1951.[2][3] In 2001 it received the Queen's Award for Enterprise for outstanding contribution to international trade.[4]
