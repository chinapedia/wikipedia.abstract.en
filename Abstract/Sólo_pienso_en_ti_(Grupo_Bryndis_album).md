_SOLO PIENSO EN TI_ is a 2007 album by Grupo Bryndis, winning best grupero album at the 8th Annual Latin Grammy Awards. It was the group's last album for Disa before moving to EMI.[1] The title track, with lyrics commencing "Ahora que te conocí, tan solo pienso y pienso en ti", is an original composition by Grupo Bryndis, and not a cover of the Víctor Manuel song. The album won a Latin Grammy Award.
