_JEWISH ROOTS IN UKRAINE AND MOLDOVA_ (full title: _Jewish Roots in Ukraine and Moldova: Pages from the Past and Archival Inventories_) is a book created by genealogist Miriam Weiner and co-published by The Miriam Weiner Routes to Roots Foundation and YIVO Institute for Jewish Research.[1] A searchable database of updated archival holdings listed in the book is available in the Archive Database on the Routes to Roots Foundation website.[2]
