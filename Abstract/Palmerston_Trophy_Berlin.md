The PALMERSTON TROPHY BERLIN was a men's senior (over 50) professional golf tournament on the European Seniors Tour, held on the Faldo course at the Sporting Club Berlin in Bad Saarow, 50 km south-west of Berlin, Germany. It was held just once, in June 2001, and was won by Denis O'Sullivan who finished four shots ahead of Seiji Ebihara and Eddie Polland.[1] The total prize fund was €200,000 with the winner receiving €30,500.
