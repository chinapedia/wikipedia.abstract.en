PACIFICITY is an integrated development strategically located in the prime area of Likas, Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia featuring premier residences, signature offices, hotels and a lifestyle shopping mall. PACIFICITY is convenient and highly accessible from 3 surrounding roads connected by 6-entry and exit points and only 15 mins to Kota Kinabalu International Airport.[1][2] The project is due to be fully completed in 2020.[3]
