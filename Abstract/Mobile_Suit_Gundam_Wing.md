_MOBILE SUIT GUNDAM WING_, also known in Japan as , is a 1995 Japanese mecha anime series directed by Masashi Ikeda and written by Katsuyuki Sumizawa. It is the sixth installment in the _Gundam_ franchise, taking place in the "After Colony" timeline. As with the original series, the plot of _Gundam Wing_ centers on a war in the future (specifically the 2220s) between Earth and its orbital colonies in the Earth-Moon system.
