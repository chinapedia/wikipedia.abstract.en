DANGMEI GRACE (born 5 February 1996) is an Indian footballer, who plays as a forward for the India Women's National Football Team. She was part of the team at the 2014 Asian Games[1] and at the 2016 South Asian Games where she scored two goals against Sri Lanka.[2][3] During the 2016 SAFF Women's Championship, she scored a goal in the first half of the final, helping India clinch its fourth consecutive title at the tournament.[4][5] During the 2018 Indian Women's League she was awarded as Emerging Player Award.
