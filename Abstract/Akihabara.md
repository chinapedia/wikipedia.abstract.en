Akhibara_Crossing_(39878694470).jpg Akihabara2019spring.jpg Akiba_denkigai.jpg Daiichhisega.jpg is a common name for the area around Akihabara Station in the Chiyoda ward of Tokyo, Japan. Administratively, the area called Akihabara mainly belongs to the and Kanda-Sakumachō districts in Chiyoda. There exists an administrative district called Akihabara in the Taitō ward further north of Akihabara Station, but it is not the place people generally refer to as Akihabara.
