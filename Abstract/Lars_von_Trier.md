LARS VON TRIER (born LARS TRIER; 30 April 1956)[1] is a Danish film director and screenwriter[2] with a prolific and controversial[3][4] career spanning almost four decades. His work is known for its genre and technical innovation;[5][6] confrontational examination of existential, social,[7][8] and political[9][10] issues; and his treatment of subjects[11] such as mercy,[12] sacrifice, and mental health.[13]
