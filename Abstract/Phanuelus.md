_PHANUELUS_ is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders). The single described species _PHANUELUS GLADSTONE_ is endemic to India. The holotype and paratypes of _P. gladstone_ were all collected from the campus of Madras Christian College in Chennai. Adult spiders are 3–4 mm in length with a short, high carapace and rounded abdomen.[1]
