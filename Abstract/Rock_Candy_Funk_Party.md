ROCK CANDY FUNK PARTY is an American jazz-funk band from Los Angeles, California. It was formed in 2009 by drummer Tal Bergman and guitarist Ron DeJesus, who had previously released _Grooove, Vol. 1_ in 2007. After adding guitarist Joe Bonamassa, bassist Mike Merritt and keyboardist Renato Neto, the band released its debut album _We Want Groove_ in 2013, which reached number 5 on the US _Billboard_ Jazz Albums chart and number 6 on the UK Jazz & Blues Albums Chart. Percussionist Daniel Sadownick joined after the album's release, featuring on the band's first live album _Rock Candy Funk Party Takes New York: Live at The Iridium_ in 2014.
