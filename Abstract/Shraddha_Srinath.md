SHRADDHA SRINATH is an Indian film actress who predominantly appears in Kannada and Tamil films. She has also appeared in a number of Telugu, Hindi and Malayalam films.[1] She gained wide acclaim for her role in the Kannada psychological thriller _U Turn_ (2016) for which she won the Filmfare Award for Best Actress[2] and also for her performances in _Urvi_, _Vikram Vedha_ and Operation Alamelamma.
