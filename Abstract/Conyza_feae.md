_CONYZA FEAE_ is a species of aster flowers that belong to the family _Asteraceae_. The species is endemic to Cape Verde.[1] It is listed as an endangered plant by the IUCN.[2] First described as _Nidorella feae_, it was placed in the genus _Conyza_ by Hiram Wild in 1969. The spceific name _feae_ refers to the Italian naturalist Leonardo Fea. Its local name is _losna-brabo_[3] or _losna-bravo_. The plant plays a role in traditional medicine.[4]
