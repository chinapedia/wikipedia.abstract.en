The ST. ANNE'S CHURCH[1] () It is a Catholic church located in Yekaterinburg,[2] in the Diocese of Novosibirsk in Russia,[3] suffragan of the Archdiocese of the Mother of God in Moscow. This church belongs to the group of parishes of the deanery of the Urals. The parish is run by the Sisters of the Congregation of Missionary Sisters of the Catholic Apostolate, founded by St. Vincent Pallotti (1795-1850).
