JOÃO AGRIPINO DA COSTA DORIA JÚNIOR (born December 16, 1957) is a Brazilian politician, businessman and journalist who is the 37th and current Governor of São Paulo, in office since January 2019. He previously served as the 52nd Mayor of São Paulo[1] from 1 January 2017[2][3] to his resignation on 6 April 2018. He was the first mayor in 24 years to be elected in the first round.[4][5] Doria is a member of the Brazilian Social Democracy Party (PSDB), and is known for having hosted the TV show _O Aprendiz_, a Brazilian version of _The Apprentice_. Doria resigned the office of mayor in April 2018 in order to run for Governor of São Paulo.[6]
