KENCHO TOBGAY is a Bhutanese international footballer, currently playing as a forward for Transport United F.C.. He made his first appearance for the Bhutan national football team in 2016.[1] He has ruled Bhutan National League and Thimphu A league as top scorer in several seasons and helped his team win the title in 2017. He started playing football at the age of 6 and played won more than 5 titles in B division. He has a degree in B.E Civil Engineering from College of Science and Technology, Royal University of Bhutan(graduated in the year 2015). After his education, his focus to fulfill his long waited dream to become a professional football player and made his first appearance for the Bhutan national football team in 2016.
