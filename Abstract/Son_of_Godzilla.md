is a 1967 Japanese _kaiju_ film directed by Jun Fukuda, with special effects by Sadamasa Arikawa, under the supervision by Eiji Tsuburaya. Produced and distributed by Toho Studios, it is the eighth film in the _Godzilla_ franchise. It stars Tadao Takashima, Akira Kubo, Akihiko Hirata, and Beverly Maeda, with Hiroshi Sekita, Seiji Onaka, and Haruo Nakajima as Godzilla, and Marchan the Dwarf as Minilla.
