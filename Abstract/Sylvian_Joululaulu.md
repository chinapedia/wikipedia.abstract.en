SYLVIAN JOULULAULU (Swedish: _Sylvias hälsning från Sicilien_), _Sylvia's Christmas Song_, is a poem by Zachris Topelius from 1853, which was composed to a christmas carol by Karl Collan. The poem has been translated to Finnish by both Elina Vaara and Martti Korpilahti, and the latter one from 1918 is one of the most beloved Finnish christmas carols.
