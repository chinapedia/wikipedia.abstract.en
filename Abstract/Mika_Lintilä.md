MIKA TAPANI LINTILÄ (born 15 April 1966) is a Finnish politician serving as Deputy Prime Minister of Finland and Minister of Finance since 2019. A member of the Centre Party, he has been a Member of Parliament (MP) since 1999.[1] On 27 October 2016, Lintilä was elected within the Centre Party to follow Olli Rehn as a Minister of Economic Affairs starting 29 December 2016.[2] Lintilä is the son of the Centre Party MP Aaro Lintilä.[3]
