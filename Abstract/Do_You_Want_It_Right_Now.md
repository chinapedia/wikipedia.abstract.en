"DO YOU WANT IT RIGHT NOW" is a song by American singer Siedah Garrett from the 1985 film _Fast Forward_. It was a bigger hit for Degrees Of Motion in 1991. It has also been recorded by Taylor Dayne for her debut studio album _Tell It to My Heart_ (1988). A lyric sample of it was also used in Gat Decor's song "Passion", as well as in Armand Van Helden's 2007 hit "I Want Your Soul".
