_RAJA RAMESH_ is a 1977 Telugu thiller drama film, produced by M. Seshagiri Rao under the Suguna Pictures banner[1] and directed by V. Madhusudhan Rao.[2] It stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Vanisri in the lead roles and music composed by K. V. Mahadevan.[3] The film is a remake of the Bengali movie _Sanyasi Raja_ (1975), which is based on the real life incident of the _Bhawal case_.
