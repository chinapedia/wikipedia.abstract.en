ELIJAH CRANE (August 29, 1754[1]-February 21, 1834)[2] was a Canton, Massachusetts farmer, and inn keeper,[3] who served as the first Town Clerk, and on the first Board of Selectmen of Canton, Massachusetts.[4] Crane was also the first representative to the Massachusetts House of Representatives from Canton,[5] and the sheriff of Norfolk County, Massachusetts from May 16, 1810[6] to 1811 and from June 20, 1812[7] to 1834.[8]
