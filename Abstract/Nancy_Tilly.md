NANCY TILLY is an American children's author. She was born on June 8, 1935 in Atlanta. She received a BA and MA from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and an MA from the University of Chicago. Tilly taught English and humanities at the City Colleges of Chicago until 1973. She taught creative writing classes at local elementary schools through 1982, when she took time off to work on her book, _Golden Girl_ from FSG in 1985.
