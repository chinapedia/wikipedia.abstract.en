ERIK BRÄNNSTRÖM (born 2 September 1999) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenseman. He is currently playing with the Belleville Senators of the American Hockey League (AHL) as a prospect to the Ottawa Senators of the National Hockey League (NHL). His older brother, Isac Brännström, is also an SHL player, currently with HV71. Erik was selected 15th overall by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 2017 NHL Entry Draft, Vegas' third pick of the first round.
