_THE WIND OF 666 BLACK HEARTS_ is a compilation album by Norwegian black metal band Darkthrone. It was announced via Darkthrone's official Facebook page on October 22, 2016[1] and released on November 25, 2016. The album is a collection of demos recorded in 1991 and 1992 at the band's rehearsal space in Vinterbro, Norway. The demos would later develop into songs on the band's first and second black metal albums _A Blaze in the Northern Sky_ and _Under a Funeral Moon_.
