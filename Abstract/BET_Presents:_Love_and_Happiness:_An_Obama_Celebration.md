BET PRESENTS: LOVE AND HAPPINESS: AN OBAMA CELEBRATION was held at the White House on the South Lawn on October 26. 2016. It aired November 15, 2016 on BET and Centric. The celebration served as a "love letter to President Obama and First Lady, Michelle Obama." The event was hosted by Terrence J and Regina Hall.
