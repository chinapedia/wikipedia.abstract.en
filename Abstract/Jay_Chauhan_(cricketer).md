JAY CHAUHAN (born 6 October 1992) is an Indian cricketer who plays for Saurashtra.[1] He made his first-class debut for Saurashtra in the 2016-17 Ranji Trophy on 29 November 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Saurashtra in the 2018–19 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 6 October 2018.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Saurashtra in the 2018–19 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy on 24 February 2019.[4]
