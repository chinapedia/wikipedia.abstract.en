Christ_Church_Flats,_Kew_Road_-_geograph.org.uk_-_219419.jpg '''Christ Church ''' is a former Church of England church on Kew Road in Richmond, in the London Borough of Richmond upon Thames. Designed by Arthur Blomfield, it was built between 1862 and 1864 and extended in 1887.[1] The building is Grade II listed,[2] as are the wrought iron railings around the building to the south and west.[3] It was closed in 1986 and has now been converted into residential flats.
