_THONDAN_ (English : Worker) is a 1995 Tamil political drama film directed by Karvannan. The film features Murali and Rohini in the lead roles, with Anandaraj, Manivannan, Srividya, Vinodhini, Vadivukkarasi, Gnanavel and S. Ramadoss playing supporting roles. The film, produced by R. K. Film Makers Circuit, had musical score by Rajan Sarma and was released on 10 March 1995.[1][2][3][4][5]
