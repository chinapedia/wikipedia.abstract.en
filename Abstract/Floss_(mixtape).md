_FLOSS_ is the third mixtape from Arizona hip hop trio Injury Reserve. It was released on December 15, 2016 and since has received abundant critical acclaim. The mixtape features guest appearances from Cakes da Killa and Vic Mensa. The music video for "Oh Shit!!!" was released on July 26, 2016.[1] A second music video for "All This Money" was released on October 20, 2016.[2]
