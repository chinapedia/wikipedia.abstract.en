FRED AARON SAVAGE (born July 9, 1976)[1] is an American actor, director, and producer.[2] He is best known for his role as Kevin Arnold in the American television series _The Wonder Years_, which ran from 1988 to 1993.[3] He has earned several awards and nominations, such as People's Choice Awards and Young Artist Awards. He is also known for playing the Grandson in _The Princess Bride_.
