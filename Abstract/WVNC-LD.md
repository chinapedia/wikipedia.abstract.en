WVNC-LD, virtual and UHF digital channel 45, is a low-powered NBC-affiliated television station licensed to Watertown, New York, United States. The station is owned by SagamoreHill Broadcasting. WVNC-LD's studios are located at Public Square in downtown Watertown, and its transmitter is located along NY 126/State Street on Champion Hill.[1][2] The station can also be seen on Charter Spectrum channel 6.
