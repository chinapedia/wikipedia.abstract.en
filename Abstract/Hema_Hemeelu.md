_HEMA HEMEELU_ (English: ONE GREATER THAN THE OTHER) is a 1979 Telugu Action film, produced by S. Raghunath, presented by Krishna[1] under the Sri Vijaya Krishna Movies banner[2] and directed by Vijaya Nirmala.[3] It stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Krishna, Vijaya Nirmala Zarina Wahab in the lead roles and music composed by Ramesh Naidu.[4]
