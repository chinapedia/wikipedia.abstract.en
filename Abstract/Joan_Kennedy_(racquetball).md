JOAN KENNEDY is an Irish racquetball player.[1] She represented the Republic of Ireland a number of times in international Ladies Racquetball competitions.[2] She was European Racquetball Federation Ladies Singles Champion in 2003, and that year also captained the Ladies team which won the team competition, played in Zoetermeer, the Netherlands. Kennedy has won numerous national and local titles in Ireland, including National Ladies Singles in 1999 and 2002, representing Dolla Racquetball Club and Templederry Racquetball Club. She has won Canon Hayes Sports and Cidona/Tipperary Sports Awards and is now retired.
