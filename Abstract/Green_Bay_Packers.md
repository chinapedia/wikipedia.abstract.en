The GREEN BAY PACKERS are a professional American football team based in Green Bay, Wisconsin. The Packers compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member club of the league's National Football Conference (NFC) North division. It is the third-oldest franchise in the NFL, dating back to 1919,[1][2] and is the only non-profit, community-owned major league professional sports team based in the United States.[3] Home games have been played at Lambeau Field since 1957.
