HARVEY JOHN GOODMAN (born 6 April 1967 in Bolton) is a British born Australian male weightlifter, competing in the 94 kg category and representing Australia at international competitions. He participated at the 1992 Summer Olympics in the 90 kg event and at the 1996 Summer Olympics in the 91 kg event.[1] He competed at world championships, most recently at the 1999 World Weightlifting Championships.[2]
