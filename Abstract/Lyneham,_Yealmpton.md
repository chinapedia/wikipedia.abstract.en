LynehamYealmptonDevonGeograph.jpg LYNEHAM in the parish of Yealmpton in Devon, is an historic estate. The surviving grand mansion house known as _Lyneham House_ is a grade I listed building.[1] It was built c.1699-1703 by Sir Courtenay Croker (died 1740), MP for Plympton Morice in 1699.
