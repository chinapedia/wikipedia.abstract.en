_NALLATHE NADAKKUM_ (English: Positive thing will happen) is a 1993 Tamil mystery drama film directed by K. Shankar. The film features Saravanan, Kaveri and Rohini in the lead roles, with Napoleon, M. N. Nambiar, Janagaraj, Senthil, Silk Smitha and Manorama playing supporting roles. The film, produced by M. G. Vikas and M. G. Vinoth, had musical score by Deva and was released on 30 June 1993.[1][2][3]
