ZDENA HERFORTOVÁ (born 8 October 1945) is a Czech film and stage actress. She has performed over 90 theatre roles and 150 television roles.[1] At the 1995 Thalia Awards she won the category of Best Actress in a Play,[2] for the title role in the production of _Alžběta Anglická_.[3] She performed at the Mahen Theatre in Brno between 1968 and 1992, subsequently transferring to the Brno City Theatre.[4] Since 1976, she has taught at the Janáček Academy of Music and Performing Arts in Brno.[5]
