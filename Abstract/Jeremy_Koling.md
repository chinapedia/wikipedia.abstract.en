JEREMY KOLING is a professional disc golfer from Charlotte, North Carolina. He is a member of the PDGA National Tour, Disc Golf Pro Tour, and Disc Golf World Tour. Among his most notable accomplishments are his 2008 PDGA Amateur World Championship win and his 2016 United States Disc Golf Championship win.[1] Koling is the only male player to win the PDGA Amateur World Championships and earn the PDGA Male Rookie of the Year Award in back-to-back years (2008 and 2009).[2] Additionally, Koling became the first and only player to win a PDGA Major at both the amateur and professional level after his 2016 USDGC win. Koling has also won at every tier available in amateur and professional divisions throughout his career.
