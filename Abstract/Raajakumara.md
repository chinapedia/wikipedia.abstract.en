_RAAJAKUMARA_ (English: _The Prince_) is a 2017 Indian Kannada language action drama film written and directed by Santhosh Ananddram and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under the Hombale Films banner. It stars Puneeth Rajkumar and Priya Anand in lead roles. Apart from them, the film features an ensemble cast of Ananth Nag, Sarath Kumar, Prakash Raj, Chikkanna, Avinash in important roles.[1] V. Harikrishna composed the film's score and soundtrack. The film was released on 24 March 2017, it received positive reviews from critics and it was a blockbuster at the box office. [2]
