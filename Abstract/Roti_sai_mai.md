_ROTI SAI MAI_ (, ; "sai mai" literally means "silk rope")[1] is known as Ayutthaya's cotton candy is a Southeast Asian dessert from Thailand. It is adapted roti from the Indian subcontinent to make this dessert by wrapping floss or cotton candy in sweet roti. _Roti sai mai_ is available at street stalls in Thailand, frequently sold by Muslim vendors.[2] Roti_sai_mai.jpg, Thailand]]
