DIKESH MALHOTRA (born 7 June 1987 ) is a Nepali businessman, philanthropist and the President and CEO of IMS Group.[1][2][3] Malhotra is the only son of Nepali Businessman Deepak Malhotra, Chairman of IMS Group,[4][5] and Sabita Malhotra, Director of Ebisu Jewellers and grandson of Dev Raj Malhotra. Malhotra's elder sister Dr. Deepika Malhotra is an eye doctor and has recently completed her fellowship from Jhapa, Nepal.[6]
