KARL, 8TH PRINCE OF LÖWENSTEIN-WERTHEIM-ROSENBERG (;[1][2] 8 February 1904 in Kleinheubach – 23 August 1990 in Kleinheubach) was a German Roman Catholic nobleman. From 1948 to 1967 he was president of the Central Committee of German Catholics. Born as _Prinz zu Löwenstein-Wertheim-Rosenberg_, he inherited the title of 'Erbrpinz' on his father's death in 1952 and later became _Fürst zu Löwenstein-Wertheim-Rosenberg_.[3]
