WHO IS THE FIRST WIFE OF MY FATHER[1] is an upcoming Indian Hindi movie based on family drama. This movie is directed by Zeeshan Ahmad and produced by Dhiraj Rokad, Amit Rokad and Shiva Sharma. Cast of this movie are Inder Kumar,[2] Aditi Govitrikar, Mohan Kapoor and Vaishnavi Mahant. Ep of this movie is Vishal Manani. The film[3] is scheduled to be released in 2018.
