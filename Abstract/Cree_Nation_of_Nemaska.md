Nemiscau_QC_2006.jpg in 2006]] The CREE NATION OF NEMASKA is a Cree First Nation of Quebec, Canada. It is headquartered at the Cree village of Nemaska and also has a _terre réservée crie_ of the same name, both located in the Eeyou Istchee territory in Northern Quebec. In 2016, it has a registered population of 781 members.[1] The nation is in negotiation with the government of Canada to obtain its self-governance.[2]
