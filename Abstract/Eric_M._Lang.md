ERIC M. LANG is a game designer. He began his career as a playtester for FASA before publishing his first game, _Mystick_ independently in 2000. He has since worked with publishers Fantasy Flight Games, WizKids, and CMON among others.[1] He is the recipient of the 2016 Diana Jones Award.[2] In March 2017, Lang became CMON's director of game design.[3]
