RUAN SADLER (born 3 January 1992) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Northerns in the 2015–16 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 5 January 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Easterns in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 30 October 2016.[3] In September 2018, he was named in Limpopo's squad for the 2018 Africa T20 Cup.[4] He made his Twenty20 debut for Limpopo in the 2018 Africa T20 Cup on 14 September 2018.[5]
