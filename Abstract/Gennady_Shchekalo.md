GENNADY NIKOLAYEVICH SHCHEKALO (Геннадий Николаевич Щекало, born ) is a Belarusian male former weightlifter, who competed in the 108 kg category and represented Belarus at international competitions. He won the bronze medal in the clean & jerk at the 1995 World Weightlifting Championships lifting 220.0 kg. He participated at the 1996 Summer Olympics in the 108 kg event.[1]
