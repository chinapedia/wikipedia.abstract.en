RIPPEE is an historic town in south central Douglas County, Missouri, United States.[1] The Rippee townsite was located on the northwest side of the Bryant Creek floodplain, due east of the confluence of Rippee Creek, which joins the Bryant from the west.[2] The site lies at an elevation of [3] and approximately south of the Missouri Route 14 bridge over the Bryant. The location is currently within the Rippee State Wildlife Management Area overseen by the Missouri Department of Conservation.[4]
