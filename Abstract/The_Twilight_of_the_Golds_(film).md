TwilightOfTheGolds.jpg _THE TWILIGHT OF THE GOLDS_ is a film directed by Ross Kagan Marks, based upon the play _The Twilight of the Golds_ by Jonathan Tolins. The film was screened at the January 1997 Sundance Film Festival prior to its broadcast in March.[1] The cast included Jennifer Beals as Suzanne, Brendan Fraser as David, Garry Marshall and Faye Dunaway as their parents, and Jon Tenney as Suzanne's husband, with John Schlesinger and Rosie O'Donnell in supporting roles created for the film.
