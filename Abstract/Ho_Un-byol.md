'''Ho Un-byol ''' (born 19 January 1992) is a North Korean football defender for the North Korea women's national football team and for the April 25 Sports Club in the DPR Korea Women's League in North Korea. She was part of the team at the 2007 FIFA Women's World Cup and 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup.[1] In December 2013 she was given the title of "Merited Athlete" after becoming top scorer in the 2013 EAFF Women's East Asian Cup, and in February 2014, she was named North Korean Female Footballer of the Year for 2013.[2]
