Eucalyptus_diptera_(7225541888).jpg Eucalyptus_diptera_(7225544640).jpg _EUCALYPTUS DIPTERA_, commonly known as the TWO-WINGED GIMLET,[1] is a mallet that is endemic to the south-west of Western Australia. It has smooth greenish to brownish bark, linear to lance-shaped adult leaves, flower buds in groups of three, each with two wings along the sides, creamy white to pale lemon-coloured flowers and cup-shaped to hemispherical fruit, also with two wings on the sides.
