INDULGE is the debut extended play written and conducted by alternative pop singer and songwriter JONES. It was released on 17 April 2015 through 37 Adventures Records. The lead single and JONES' debut single "Indulge" was released on the day of the EPs release and was re-released officially on 17 October 2015.[1] The song, along with the extended play, was the main project that sparked JONES' major breakthrough into the music industry. It even caught the attention of big music stars, such as Sam Smith, when he commented on the EP as being "f**king beautiful".[2]
