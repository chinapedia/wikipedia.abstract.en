PETER KELLER (born 19 October 1944 in Ballarat, Victoria) is a former Australian professional tennis player. He reached the first with J. Thomson and second round with John Newcombe at the 1962 Australian Championships in the men's singles and again in 1965 with Juan Gisbert, Sr.. He was currently ranked as Australian number two in the 1970s. He became active between early to late 1960s.[1]
