LOUIS J. SCHULTZ SCHOOL, originally known as CENTRAL HIGH SCHOOL, is a historic school building located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri, USA. The central section was built in 1915, and is a three-story (plus basement), flat roofed, red brick, concrete framed, Renaissance Revival style school building. Flanking two-story wings were finished in 1919 and a shop wing added in 1942. It features a glazed terra cotta cornice and limestone coursing.[1]
