DOINA CIOBANU (Дойна Чобану, born 20 April 1994, Chișinău, Moldova) is a London-based fashion blogger turned model.[1] Described as having one of the strongest personal brands in the United Kingdom’s fashion industry,[2] as well as being one of the “biggest influences” upon it,[3] Ciobanu has modeled and collaborated with a list of luxury fashion brands that includes Agent Provocateur, Burberry, and Versace.[4]
