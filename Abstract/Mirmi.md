MIRMI is town located in the western part of Nepal in the Syangja District, near Galang (Nepal).People of Mirmi are very friendly and hearwarming . People of differenet ethnicites like Brahmin,Magar, Limbu live in Mirmi. It is known for being home for biggest Hydro-Power project in Nepal - Kaligandaki A Hydroelectric Power Station. [1][2]
