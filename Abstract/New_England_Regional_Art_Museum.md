The NEW ENGLAND REGIONAL ART MUSEUM, known as NERAM, is a museum of Australian art located in Armidale in the New England region of New South Wales. NERAM's art collections are the second largest and most valuable regional public collection in NSW[1] after the Newcastle Art Gallery.
