_SPRING BAKING CHAMPIONSHIP_ is an American cooking competition television series that airs on Food Network. It was originally presented by chef Bobby Deen; with fellow Food Network chefs Nancy Fuller, Duff Goldman and Lorraine Pascale serving as judges.[1] All four chefs also appeared in the same roles on _Holiday Baking Championship_; and similar to that competition, the grand prize for the winner of this competition is also $50,000.[2]
