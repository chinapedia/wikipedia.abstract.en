KURTIS WALKER (born August 9, 1959),[1] professionally known by his stage name KURTIS BLOW, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record/film producer, b-boy, DJ, public speaker and minister.[2] He is the first commercially successful rapper and the first to sign with a major record label.[3] "The Breaks", a single from his 1980 self-titled debut album, is the first certified gold record rap song for Hip Hop.
