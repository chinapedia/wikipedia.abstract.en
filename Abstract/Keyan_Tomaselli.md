KEYAN GRAY TOMASELLI is a South African communication professor and author, currently Professor Emeritus and Fellow at University of KwaZulu-Natal, where he established and operated its Centre for Communication, Media and Society for 29 years until becoming a Distinguished Professor of Humanities at University of Johannesburg. He is also Editor of the UKZN-UJ journal _Critical Arts: South-North Cultural and Media Studies_ and co-editor at UJ's journal _Journal of African Cinemas_.[1] He is also a published author and has been largely collected by libraries.[2]
