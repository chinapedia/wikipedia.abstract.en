DARTMOUTH is a town and civil parish in the English county of Devon. It is a tourist destination set on the western bank of the estuary of the River Dart, which is a long narrow tidal ria that runs inland as far as Totnes. It lies within the South Devon Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty and South Hams district, and had a population of 5,512 in 2001,[1] reducing to 5,064 at the 2011 census[2] There are two electoral wards in the _Dartmouth_ area (Townstal & Kingswear). Their combined population at the above census was 6,822.[3][4]
