TUNKU ABDUL RAHMAN HASSANAL JEFFRI IBNI SULTAN IBRAHIM (born 5 February 1993) is the prince of Johor.Tunku Abdul Rahman is styled as Tunku Panglima Johor, with conferred Johor royal title of _Tunku Panglima_ of Johor and the 5th child of his parents. He is the son of a Malay Sultan, Sultan Ibrahim Ismail of Johor state [1]. His father is the current head of state of Johor.
