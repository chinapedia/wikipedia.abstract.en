HUZURNAGAR MANDAL is one of the 23 mandals in Suryapet district of the Indian state of Telangana.[1] It is under the administration of Kodad revenue division with its headquarters at Huzurnagar. It is bounded by Chilkur mandal towards North, Mellachervu mandal and Mattampally mandal towards South, Garidepally Mandal towards west, Kodad mandal towards east.
