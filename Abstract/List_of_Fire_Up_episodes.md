_FIRE UP_ (simplified Chinese: 美味下半场) is a Singaporean television drama series produced by Wawa Pictures in 2016, starring Huang Biren and Thomas Ong as the main characters of the series. Huang portrays the drama's main protagonist as a two-time ex-convict, who resolves to turn her life around and sets up a _zi char_ stall, after failing to get a job, following her release from prison. She works hard and tries her best to make amends to her two grown up children while helping other fellow ex-offenders find jobs (by hiring them to work at her stall) and re-integrate back into society. The series began airing on Mediacorp Channel 8 on 4 October 2016 with 20 episodes.
