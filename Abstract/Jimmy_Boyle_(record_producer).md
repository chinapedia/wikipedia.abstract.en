JIMMY BOYLE (born November 4, 1967) is an American record producer, songwriter and musician of Irish descent. He has performed on, engineered or produced records for a diverse range of musical artists including the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Alanis Morissette, Dave Navarro, John Frusciante, Rasputina, Johnny Cash, Hole, Three Amoebas, and Rage Against the Machine.[1][2] His record and album collaborations have sold over 50 million copies worldwide.[3] His work on Alanis Morissette's single, "You Oughta Know"[4] went on to win Grammy's for Best Rock Song and Female Rock Vocal Performance.
