XANDER MOBUS (born July 8, 1992) is an American voice actor, who is known for his work in English-dubbed anime and video games. In 2014, he voiced the announcer and the characters Master Hand and Crazy Hand in _Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Wii U_, and returned for the role in 2018's _Super Smash Bros. Ultimate_.
