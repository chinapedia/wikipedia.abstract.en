SERDAR ÜSTÜNER (born March 1, 1983) is a boxer from Turkey, who participated in the 2004 Summer Olympics for his country. There he was stopped in the first round of the Men's Middleweight (75 kg) division by Uzbekistan's Sherzod Abdurahmonov. He qualified for the Athens Games by ending up in second place at the 3rd AIBA European 2004 Olympic Qualifying Tournament in Gothenburg, Sweden. In the final he lost to Hungary's Károly Balzsay.
