MARAKKA MUDIYUMAA? (English: _Can I Forget?_) is a 1966 Tamil language drama film, directed and produced by Murasoli Maran under the banner of Mekala Pictures.[1] The film script was written by M. Karunanidhi. Music was by T. K. Ramamoorthy.[2] It stars S. S. Rajendran, Devika, R. Muthuraman and Kanchana playing lead, with Cho, S. V. Sahasranamam Major Sundararajan and S. A. Natarajan playing pivotal roles.[3] Story of the Hindi film of 1967 "Baharon ke Sapne" produced and directed by Nazir Hussain starring Rajesh Khanna and Asha Parekh based on this Tamil movie.
