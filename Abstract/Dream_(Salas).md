San_Diego,_2016_-_250.jpg _DREAM_ is an outdoor 2001 public artwork by Roberto Salas, installed along San Diego's Martin Luther King Jr. Promenade, in the U.S. state of California. The work, which includes five bronze hand sculptures, is one of several commemorating Martin Luther King Jr. along the promenade, including Melv Edwards' _Breaking of the Chains_ and _Shedding the Cloak_ by Jerry and Tama Dumlao and Mary Lynn Dominguez.[1][2]
