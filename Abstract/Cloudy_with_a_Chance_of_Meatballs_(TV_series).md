_CLOUDY WITH A CHANCE OF MEATBALLS_ (also called _CLOUDY: THE SERIES_[1]) is a Canadian/American spin-off animated comic sci-fi sitcom television series produced by DHX Media and Sony Pictures Animation in association with Corus Entertainment for Cartoon Network and YTV.[2] Based on the children's book and the film series of the same name,[3] _Cloudy_ is the first television series to be produced by Sony Pictures Animation and was animated using Toon Boom Harmony.[4]
