_MEMETIC_ is an apocalyptic horror comic book series that was created by James Tynion IV, with art by Eryk Donovan and coloring by Adam Guzowski.[1] The series was published monthly by BOOM! Studios as three oversized issues, beginning in October 2014. In October 2015, BOOM! Studios released all three issues, along with extra features from the writer and artist, in trade paperback form.
