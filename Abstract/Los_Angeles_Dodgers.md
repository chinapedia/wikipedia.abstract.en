The Dodgers compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the National League (NL) West division[1]. Established in 1883 in Brooklyn, New York,[2][3] the team moved to Los Angeles before the 1958 season.[4] They played for four seasons at the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum before moving to their current home of Dodger Stadium in [5].
