DOUGLAS MCAUTHUR GOODWIN[1] (March 11, 1942) is a former American football running back who played two seasons in the NFL with two different teams. He spent one season with the Buffalo Bills and one season with the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League. He played college football for the Maryland Eastern Shore Hawks football team. He was drafted by the Green Bay Packers in the 5th round of the 1965 NFL Draft, and drafted by the Buffalo Bills in the eleventh round of the 1965 AFL Draft.
