DUBAI DESIGN WEEK is an annual event held to celebrate and promote design and creativity in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The first Dubai Design Week took place in 2015, and is held every November at venues across the city, with the hub location of Dubai Design District .[1] With a programme of over 250 free-to-attend exhibitions, talks, workshops, the 2018 festival attracted over 75,000 visitors and as such it is considered the largest creative festival in the Middle East.
