KARAM DOSA is a 2016 Telugu Drama-Comedy-Social Film[1] written and directed by Trivikram.G produced on Veenaa Vedika Productions Private Limited[2] It features Shivakumar Ramachandravarapu as a lead role, Surya Sreenivas, Vankayala Satya Narayana a veteran Telugu actor and actor-director Y. Kasi Viswanath in prominent roles.[3] The National Film Award[4] winning Editor Suresh Urs has worked as editor for this movie. The movie released on 30 December 2016.[5] The movie Dialogues need a special mention as they have been written quite cleverly with a lot of fun and good emotions during the climax. [6]
