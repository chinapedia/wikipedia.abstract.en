The 2016 LIBERTY BOWL was a post-season American college football bowl game played on December 30, 2016 at Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium in Memphis, Tennessee. The 58th edition of the Liberty Bowl featured the Georgia Bulldogs of the Southeastern Conference against the TCU Horned Frogs of the Big 12 Conference.
