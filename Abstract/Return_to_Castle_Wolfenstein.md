_RETURN TO CASTLE WOLFENSTEIN_ is a first-person shooter video game published by Activision,[1] released on November 19, 2001 for Microsoft Windows and subsequently for PlayStation 2, Xbox, Linux and Macintosh. The game serves as both a remake and a reboot to the _Wolfenstein_ series. It was developed by Gray Matter Interactive[2][3] and Nerve Software[4][5] developed its multiplayer mode. id Software, the creators of _Wolfenstein 3D_, oversaw the development and were credited as executive producers.[6] The multiplayer side eventually became the most popular part of the game, and was influential in the genre. Splash Damage created some of the maps for the Game of the Year edition.[7] A sequel, titled _Wolfenstein_, was released on August 18, 2009.
