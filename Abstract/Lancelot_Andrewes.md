LANCELOT ANDREWES (155525 September 1626) was an English bishop and scholar, who held high positions in the Church of England during the reigns of Elizabeth I and James I. During the latter's reign, Andrewes served successively as Bishop of Chichester, of Ely, and of Winchester and oversaw the translation of the _King James Version of the Bible_ (or _Authorized Version_). In the Church of England he is commemorated on 25 September with a Lesser Festival.
