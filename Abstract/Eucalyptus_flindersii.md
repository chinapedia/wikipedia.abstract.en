_EUCALYPTUS FLINDERSII_, commonly known as the SOUTH AUSTRALIAN GREY MALLEE, MALLEE RED GUM, or GREY MALLEE,[1] is a species of mallee that is endemic in South Australia. It usually has smooth, pinkish grey bark, lance-shaped to curved adult leaves, flower buds in groups of three or seven and conical or hemispherical fruit with the valves protruding.Eucalyptus_flindersii_buds.jpgEucalyptus_flindersii_fruit.jpg
