KAUNAIN ABBAS (born 7 May 1990) is an Indian cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Karnataka in the 2016–17 Ranji Trophy on 13 October 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Karnataka in the 2018–19 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 30 September 2018.[3] Abbas studied for a MBA degree at Jain University.[4]
