EMRE LALE (born 14 October 1993) is a Turkish badminton player.[1][2] Lale was born in Bursa, and trained at the İstanbul Büyükşehir Belediyespor SK.[3] As a junior player, he represented his country competed at the 2010 Summer Youth Olympics in Singapore.[4] In 2011, he won a bronze medal at the European Junior Championships in the boys' singles event.[5] Lale won his first senior international title at the Iraq International in the men's singles event.[6] He competed at the 2013 and 2018 Mediterranean Games, also at the 2019 European Games.[7]
