JESÚS GUZMÁN GARETA (Madrid, 15 June 1926) is a veteran Spanish actor, known for _La morte sull'alta collina_ (1969)[1] _Crónicas de un pueblo_ (1971),[2] _Cachimba_ (2004) and _Maestros_ (2000). He appeared in many western films shot in Almería such as _Un par de asesinos_ (1970), _Per qualche dollaro in più_ (1965), _The Good, the Bad and the Ugly_ (1966) and _Ocaso de un pistolero_ (1965).[3]
