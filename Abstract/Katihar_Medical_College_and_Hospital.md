KATIHAR MEDICAL COLLEGE & HOSPITAL (KMCH) is a deemed medical college situated in the city of Katihar in the Indian state of Bihar.[1][2][3] Katihar Medical College offers undergraduate courses in Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) and post-graduate courses (MD/MS And PG Diploma) in almost all Clinical and Non-Clinical Subjects. The college was established and is maintained by the Patna-based Al-Karim Educational Trust.[4][5]
