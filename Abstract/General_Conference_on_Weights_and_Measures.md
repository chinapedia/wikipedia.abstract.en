The GENERAL CONFERENCE ON WEIGHTS AND MEASURES (, CGPM) is the supreme authority of the International Bureau of Weights and Measures (, BIPM), the inter-governmental organization established in 1875 under the terms of the Metre Convention () through which Member States act together on matters related to measurement science and measurement standards. The CGPM is made up of delegates of the governments of the Member States and observers from the Associates of the CGPM.
