KAREN BUSCK (born 5 January 1975) is a Danish singer and songwriter who debuted with the album _Hjertet ser_ in 2001, sung with Erann DD. She has been a judge on the Danish reality tv-contest _Stjerne for en aften_ in 2003. She has a degree as a teacher of music from the Royal Academy of Music, Aarhus/Aalborg.[1][2]
