GARY J. TUNNICLIFFE (born 27 February 1968)[1] is a British special make-up effects designer, writer, and director. He has performed make-up effects in all the _Hellraiser_ films since _Hellraiser III: Hell on Earth_, wrote _Hellraiser: Revelations_, and wrote and directed _Hellraiser: Judgment_. His other make-up credits include _Candyman_, _Wishmaster_, the _Dracula 2000_ series, the _Pulse_ series, and _Feast_.
