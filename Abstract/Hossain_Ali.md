HOSSAIN ALI (born 10 September 1998) is a Bangladeshi cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Chittagong Division in the 2016–17 National Cricket League on 25 September 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Gazi Group Cricketers in the 2016–17 Dhaka Premier Division Cricket League on 29 April 2017.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Rajshahi Kings on 7 November 2017 in the 2017–18 Bangladesh Premier League.[4]
