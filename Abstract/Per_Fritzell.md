PER FRITZELL, (born 2 October 1955) is a Swedish comedian and member of the Galenskaparna och After shave comedic group. He was born in Stockholm but grew up in Gothenburg. He studied at Chalmers University of Technology, and he ended up dropping out of the electro-technic class. In May 2012 he released his first solo album _Jag tror inte på Gud, jag tror på Tomten_. He presented an episode of Sveriges Radios show _Sommar i P1_ on 22 July 2014 talking about his life and career.[1]
