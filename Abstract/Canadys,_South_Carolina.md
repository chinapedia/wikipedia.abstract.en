CANADYS is an unincorporated community in Colleton County, South Carolina, United States.[1] The community is located at the intersection of U.S. Route 15 (US 15) and South Carolina Highway 61 (SC 61), north-northeast of Walterboro. Canadys had a post office from April 16, 1926 until January 11, 1992; it still has its own ZIP code, 29433.[2][3]
