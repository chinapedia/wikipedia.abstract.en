_MICROBACTERIUM KITAMIENSE_ is a heterotrophic, strictly aerobic, mesophilic and non-motile bacterium from the genus of Microbacterium which has been isolated from waste water from a sugar-beet factory in Kitami in Japan.[1][2][3][4] _Microbacterium kitamiense_ produces polysaccharide.[5] _Microbacterium kitamiense_ has a high GC-content.[6]
