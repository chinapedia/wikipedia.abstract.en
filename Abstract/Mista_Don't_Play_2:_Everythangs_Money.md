_MISTA DON'T PLAY 2 EVERYTHANGS MONEY_ is the eighth studio album by American rapper Project Pat from Memphis, Tennessee. It was released on March 24, 2015 via Entertainment One Music. It served as a sequel to his sophomore full-length _Mista Don't Play: Everythangs Workin_, released in 2001.[1][2] Production was handled primarily by Pat's younger brother Juicy J, alone with Lil Awree, Joe Simpson, Crazy Mike, J Dizzle on the Beat, Mike Will Made It, K.E. on the Track and Deezy. It features guest appearances from Bankroll Fresh, Doe B, Nasty Mane, Ty Dolla $ign, Wale, Wiz Khalifa and Juicy J. The album peaked at No. 43 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart in the United States, spawning a single "Twerk It".
