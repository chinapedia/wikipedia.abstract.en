FORSTER (WALLIS ISLAND) AIRPORT (IATA: FOT, ICAO: YFST) is a private Australian airfield located on Wallis Island, New South Wales. The airport is the only one on the small island, which is only accessible by boat or plane, landing at the airport itself.[1] There are no commercial or scheduled flights servicing it.
