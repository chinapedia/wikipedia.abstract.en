POPE CELESTINE V (; 1215 – 19 May 1296), born PIETRO ANGELERIO (according to some sources _Angelario_, _Angelieri_, _Angelliero_, or _Angeleri_), also known as PIETRO DA MORRONE, PETER OF MORRONE, and PETER CELESTINE, was pope for five months from 5 July to 13 December 1294, when he resigned. He was also a monk and hermit who founded the order of the Celestines as a branch of the Benedictine order.
