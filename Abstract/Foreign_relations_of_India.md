The Ministry of External Affairs of India (MEA), also known as the Foreign Ministry, is the government agency responsible for the conduct of FOREIGN RELATIONS OF INDIA. With the world's fifth largest military expenditure, second largest armed force, sixth largest economy by nominal rates and third largest economy in terms of purchasing power parity,[1] India is a regional power,[2] a nuclear power, a nascent global power and a potential superpower. India has a growing international influence and a prominent voice in global affairs.
