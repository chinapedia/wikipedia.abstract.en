FLORENCE HARDOUIN (born 26 January 1967 in France)[1][2] is a French football official.[3] Since February 2013, she has been the Deputy General Manager of the French Football Federation (French: Fédération Française de Football, FFF)[4] and in addition, as of 3 May 2016, a member of the UEFA Executive Committee.[5] She was first female executive elected by the UEFA. Her predecessor, Karen Espelund, was appointed but not elected in 2012.[6] Between 2008 and 2013, Florence Hardouin was a Marketing Director at the French Football Federation (FFF).[7]
