LAJEDO[1] (also _Lajedos_ or _Lagedos_) is a settlement in the southcentral part of the island of Santo Antão, Cape Verde. In 2010 its population was 558. It is situated in the valley of Ribeira das Patas, about 11 km west of the island capital Porto Novo. The settlement consists of several localities, including _Ponte Sul_, _Lajedo_ proper and _Vascona_. The road from Porto Novo to Ribeira da Cruz in the north of the island passes through Lajedo.
