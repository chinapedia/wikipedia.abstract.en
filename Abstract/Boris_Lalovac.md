BORIS LALOVAC (born 16 November 1976) is a Croatian economist and politician who served as the 10th Minister of Finance in the center-left Cabinet of Zoran MilanoviÄ‡ from 14 May 2014 until 22 January 2016. Lalovac is a member of the Social Democratic Party of Croatia and serves as one of four party vice-presidents since December 2016. He has been elected to the Croatian Parliament in 2015 and 2016 parliamentary elections, both times representing the 10th electoral district.[1]
