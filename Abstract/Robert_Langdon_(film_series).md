The _ROBERT LANGDON_ films are a series of American action-adventure mystery-thriller movies directed by Ron Howard. The films are based on the novels written by Dan Brown, and center around the fictional character of Robert Langdon. Though based on the book series, the films have a different chronological order, and consist of: _The Da Vinci Code_ (2006), _Angels & Demons_ (2009) and _Inferno_ (2016). The film series as a whole has grossed almost $1.5 billion worldwide.
