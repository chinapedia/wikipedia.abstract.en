SIERRA LEONE (, also , ),[1][2] officially the REPUBLIC OF SIERRA LEONE, informally SALONE,[3] is a country on the southwest coast of West Africa. It is bordered by Liberia to the southeast and Guinea to the northeast. Sierra Leone has a tropical climate, with a diverse environment ranging from savanna to rainforests, and a total area of [4] and a population of 7,075,641 as of the 2015 census. [5] The capital and largest city is Freetown, and the country is divided into five administrative regions, which are further subdivided into sixteen districts. [6][7]
