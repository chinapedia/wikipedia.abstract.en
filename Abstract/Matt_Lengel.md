MATTHEW LENGEL (born December 27, 1990) is an American football tight end who is currently a free agent. He was signed by the Cincinnati Bengals as an undrafted free agent in 2015. He played college football at Eastern Kentucky and Northeastern University.[1] He also played for the New England Patriots, where he was a member of the Super Bowl LI-winning team, as well as the Cleveland Browns and Houston Texans.
