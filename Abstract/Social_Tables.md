SOCIAL TABLES is a global SaaS company headquartered in Washington, DC that was founded in 2011 by Dan Berger[1] and Matthew Tendler.[2] With a focus to “inspire face-to-face experiences by empowering those who plan and attend them,”[3] the company has created a collaborative web-based event planning platform.[4] The technology includes three-dimensional floor plans where host venues and event organizers can build rooms to position items needed for the event at hand.[5]
