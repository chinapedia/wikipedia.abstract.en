JARROD PICKETT (18 August 1996) is a former Australian rules footballer playing for the Carlton Football Club in the Australian Football League (AFL). He was drafted by the Greater Western Sydney Giants with their first selection and fourth overall in the 2014 national draft.[1] After two seasons with the Giants and failing to play a senior match, he was traded to Carlton during the 2016 trade period.[2] He made his debut in the forty-three point loss against in the opening round of the 2017 season at the Melbourne Cricket Ground.[3]
