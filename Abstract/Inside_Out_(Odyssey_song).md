"INSIDE OUT" is a 1982 song by the American group Odyssey from their album _Happy Together_. The bass line in this track was copied from the group Slave's 1980 hit 'Watching You'. It was the group's second highest charting single on the UK Singles Chart, where it peaked at number 3,[1] and was also certified silver in the UK. It was ranked at number 15 among the "Tracks of the Year" for 1982 by _NME_.[2]
