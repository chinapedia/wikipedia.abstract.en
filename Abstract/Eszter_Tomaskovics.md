'''Eszter Anita Tomaskovics ''' (born 23 August 1987)[1] is an Hungarian female water polo player. She was a member of the Hungary Women's National Team. She was a part of the Hungarian team that claimed the gold medal at the 2005 FINA World Championship in Montreal, Quebec, Canada.[2][3] She also competed in the 2007 and 2009 FINA World Championships.[4][5] At club level, she has played most notably for Vasas, Honvéd (2005–2008) and Olympiacos (2008–2010),[6] winning numerous titles.
