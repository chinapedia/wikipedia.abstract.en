The_Cambridge_History_of_Islam_cover.jpg _THE CAMBRIDGE HISTORY OF ISLAM_ is a two volume history of Islam published by Cambridge University Press in 1970[1] and edited by Peter Holt, Ann K.S. Lambton, and Bernard Lewis. It was reprinted in 1977 with amendments and each volume divided into two for ease of use. It was replaced by the six-volume _New Cambridge History of Islam_ in 2010.[2]
