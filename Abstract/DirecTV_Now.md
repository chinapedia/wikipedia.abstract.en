DIRECTV NOW (also known simply as DTV NOW OR DTVN[1]) is a subscription streaming television service owned by AT&T, which allows subscribers in the United States to stream programming from cable channels without the long term commitment. The service was launched on November 30, 2016.[2][3] , the service has 1.6 million subscribers.[4] The data used by the service is zero rated for AT&T Wireless customers while on the AT&T network, as well as T-Mobile US customers who have the carrier's "Binge On" feature enabled.[5]
