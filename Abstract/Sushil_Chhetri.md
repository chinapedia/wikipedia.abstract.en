SUSHIL CHHETRI () is a Nepalese actor who has worked in the Nepalese film industry for more than a decade. He debuted in Kollywood with the film ‘’Aaago’’ which received much hype and sensation. Unfortunately as the story based on the Maoist crisis it was banned before release.[1] He is known for his movies Aago (2000), Gaajal (2001)[2][3] Chhetri acted in dozens of Nepali movies and 11 Bhojpuri movies.[4][5] Recently after a sort of disappearance from Nepali Film Industry, he came back with his new movie ‘’Bich Bato(2015)’’,[6] before entering Kollywood as an actor he started his career as background dancer.[7]
