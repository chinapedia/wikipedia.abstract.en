The LOPATNIC is a long left tributary of the river Prut in western Ukraine and northern Moldova.[1] Its source is near the village Hrubna (Sokyriany Raion), Ukraine. After a few kilometers, it crosses into Moldova. It flows through the villages Grimăncăuți, Briceni, Tabani, Caracușenii Vechi and Corjeuți, and it discharges into the Prut near the village Lopatnic, on the border with Romania.
