_MONOPOLY STAR WARS_ is the 1997 Monopoly franchise Star Wars video game based on the board game and set in the Star Wars fictional universe. It is one of many _Monopoly_ video game adaptions. The game was developed by Artech Digital Entertainment and published by Hasbro Interactive. It was released exclusively for Microsoft Windows computers. The game employs the same basic ruleset of traditional Monopoly gameplay, but the _Star Wars_ theme includes famous characters and locales in place of the original game pieces and properties.
