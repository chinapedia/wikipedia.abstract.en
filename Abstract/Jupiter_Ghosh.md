JUPITER GHOSH (born 22 July 1989)[1] is a Bangladeshi first-class and List A cricketer. A right-handed batsman and right-arm medium. Ghosh is an all-rounder and plays as a right-handed batsman. He currently is playing for Khulna Division.He was born in Bagerhat, Khulna.[2] He has played for Sylhet Royals in the Bangladesh Premier League.[3]
