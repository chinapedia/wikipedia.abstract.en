WILLIAM HENRY AND LILLA LUCE HARRISON HOUSE, also known as the DR. SAMUEL HARRIS HOUSE, is a historic home located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It was built in 1897, and is a 2 1/2-story, free classic Queen Anne style brick dwelling. It has a steeply pitched side-gable roof with projecting dormers. It features a wraparound porch with circular verandah added between 1900 and 1908.[1]
