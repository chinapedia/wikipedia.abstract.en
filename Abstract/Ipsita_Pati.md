IPSITA PATI () (born 18 June 1991[1]) is an Indian film actress from Odisha. She has won multiple beauty pageant contests including that of Indian Princess,[2] Miss Asia and Miss International. Pati has also acted in Hindi film, _Chor Bazaari (2014)_.
