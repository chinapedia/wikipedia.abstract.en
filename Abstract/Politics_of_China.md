The POLITICS OF THE PEOPLE'S REPUBLIC OF CHINA takes place in a framework of a socialist republic run by a single party, the Communist Party of China, headed by General Secretary.[1] State power within the People's Republic of China (PRC) is exercised through the Communist Party, the Central People's Government (State Council) and their provincial and local representation . The state uses , secret documents produced by Xinhua News Agency, similar to US' President's Daily Brief, though delivered to most of its officials according to level of secrecy of the information, a major source of information of the society.
