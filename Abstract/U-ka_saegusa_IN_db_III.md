_U-KA SAEGUSA IN DB III_ is the third studio album by Japanese group U-ka Saegusa in dB.[1] The album includes 7 previously released singles since Tobitatenai Watashi ni Anata ga Tsubasa wo Kureta till Everybody. The album was released on September 20, 2006 under Giza Studio label. It was released in two versions: a limited CD+DVD edition and a regular CD only edition. The album reached #19 rank in Oricon for first week. It charted for 5 weeks and totally sold 19,213 copies.[2]
