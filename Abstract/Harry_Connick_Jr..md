JOSEPH HARRY FOWLER CONNICK JR.[1] (born September 11, 1967)[2] is a Grammy and Emmy-award winning American singer, composer, actor, and television host. He has sold over 28million albums worldwide.[3] Connick is ranked among the top60 best-selling male artists in the United States by the Recording Industry Association of America, with 16million in certified sales.[4] He has had seven top20 US albums, and ten number-one US jazz albums, earning more number-one albums than any other artist in US jazz chart history.[5]
