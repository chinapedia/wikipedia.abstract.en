DULLE FARMSTEAD HISTORIC DISTRICT, also known as PLEASANT HOME FARM, is a historic home and farm and national historic district located near Jefferson City, Cole County, Missouri. It encompasses nine contributing buildings and one contributing structure and include the brick I-house form farmhouse (1902), the multi-purpose barn (c. 1858), the cattle barn (1933), the ice house (c. 1925), the garage (1942), two chicken shelters (c. 1942), two brooder houses (c. 1942), and an oak plank and iron beam bridge (1934).[1]
