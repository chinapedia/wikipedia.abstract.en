_CITY OF LIES_ is a 2018 American crime film directed by Brad Furman and written by Christian Contreras. It is based on the book _LAbyrinth_ by Randall Sullivan, about the murder investigations of Tupac Shakur and The Notorious B.I.G. The film stars Johnny Depp as retired detective LAPD Russell Poole and Forest Whitaker as a journalist Jackson, with Rockmond Dunbar and Neil Brown Jr. also starring.
