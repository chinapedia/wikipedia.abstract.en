_KSHEERA SAGARA_ is a 1992 Indian Kannada romantic drama film directed and written by Singeetham Srinivasa Rao[1] and produced by Babu Ganesh. The film featured Kumar Bangarappa and Amala in the lead roles whilst Shruti, C. R. Simha and Lokesh appeared in key supporting roles.[2] The film's music was composed by Hamsalekha and cinematography is R. Deviprasad.
