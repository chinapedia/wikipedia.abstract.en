PETER KENNETH ESTLIN (born July 1961) is the 691st Lord Mayor of the City of London. He is an alderman of the City of London Corporation where he represents the ward of Coleman Street. Having served as a Sheriff of the City of London for the year 2016-17, he was elected as the Lord Mayor of London on 1 October 2018 and took up the position in a silent ceremony on 9 November 2018.[1]
