The DEATH OF A BACHELOR TOUR was a concert tour by Panic! at the Disco, in support of the group's fifth studio album _Death of a Bachelor_ (2016). The tour began in Uncasville at the Mohegan Sun Arena on February 24, 2017, and concluded on April 15, 2017, in Sunrise at the BB&T Center. Adding gross estimates from those concerts yet to be reported, the overall box office take was about $17 million when the tour closed. About 350,000 fans saw the Death of a Bachelor Tour during its run.[1]
