_LOVE AND..._ () is a 2015 South Korean drama film written and directed by Korean-Chinese filmmaker Zhang Lu and stars Park Hae-il, Ahn Sung-ki, Moon So-ri and Han Ye-ri. Commissioned by Seoul Senior Film Festival's support program, it consists of four parts – "Love," "Film," "Them" and "Love Again" – is a story within a story. It made its premiere as the opening film of the 8th Seoul Senior Film Festival in September 2015.[1][2][3]
