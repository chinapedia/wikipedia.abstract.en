MOHAMMADIEH RAILWAY STATION (_Persian:_ايستگاه راه آهن محمدیه, _Istgah-e Rah Ahan-e Mohammadieh_) is located in Qom, Qom Province. The station is owned by IRI Railway. In order to shorten travel time on passing longer distance trains, most services serving Qom railway station in the city centre were moved to this station located on Qom bypass rail line.
