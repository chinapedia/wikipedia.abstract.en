PARTNERSHIP ON AI (full name PARTNERSHIP ON ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE TO BENEFIT PEOPLE AND SOCIETY) is a technology industry consortium focused on establishing best practices for artificial intelligence systems and to educate the public about AI. Publicly announced September 28, 2016, its founding members are Amazon, Facebook, Google, DeepMind, Microsoft, and IBM, with interim co-chairs Eric Horvitz of Microsoft Research and Mustafa Suleyman of DeepMind.[1][2][3][4] Apple joined the consortium as a founding member in January 2017.[5]
