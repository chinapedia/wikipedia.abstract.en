FREDERICK W. AND MARY KARAU POTT HOUSE is a historic home located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It was built about 1885, and is a 2 1/2-story, Italianate style brick dwelling. It has a side-gabled roof, a projecting pedimented front gable and parapet chimneys. It features an overhanging eave with curvilinear brackets and modillions, tall narrow windows with round and segmental arches, and an ornate central portico supported by groups of chamfered columns.[1]
