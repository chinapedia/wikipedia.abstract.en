_MARAVAN_ is a 1993 Tamil action drama film directed by Manoj Kumar. The film features Prabhu and Kushboo in the lead roles, with Vijayakumar, Sumithra, Napoleon, R. P. Viswam, Chandrasekhar, Thyagu, Vadivelu, Venniradai Moorthy and Dakshayini playing supporting roles. The film, produced by Mohan Natarajan and Tharangai V. Shanmugam, had musical score by Deva and was released on 15 August 1993.[1][2][3]
