Keke Wyatt (born KE'TARA SHAVON WYATT) is an American R&B recording artist. She has written and recorded material for her four studio albums, _Soul Sista_ (2001), _Who Knew?_ (2010), _Unbelievable_ (2011), _Rated Love_ (2016) and one extended play _Ke'Ke'_ (2014). Songs included in this list are from her studio albums, extended play as well as collaborations with other recording artists on duets and featured songs on their respective albums and the unreleased tracks during the recording process the unreleased albums _Emotional Rollercoaster_ (2005) and _Ghetto Rose_ (2007).
