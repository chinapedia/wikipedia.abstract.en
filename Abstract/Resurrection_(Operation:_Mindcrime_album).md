_RESURRECTION_ is the second studio album by progressive metal band Operation: Mindcrime. It is the second in a concept album trilogy on virtual currencies, internet banking and stock trading.[1] It was released on September 23, 2016. The first single, titled "Taking On The World" was released in September 2016, and a video was filmed with the song's guests, Blaze Bayley and Tim "Ripper" Owens.
