RAJESH MAPUSKAR is an Indian film writer, director and producer. He got involved in the world of films at a very young age, as his family owned a cinema hall. He made his writing and directional debut with Ferrari Ki Sawaari (2012). Later, he wrote and directed the acclaimed Marathi language film _Ventilator_ (2016), which won him several accolades, including the National Film Award for Best Director.
