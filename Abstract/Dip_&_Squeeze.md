Dip_&_Squeeze_ketchup.jpg DIP & SQUEEZE is the brand name of a type of packaging for tomato ketchup used by Heinz Tomato Ketchup. The product was announced in 2010 and rolled out to consumers at U.S. fast food restaurants in March, 2011.[1] Later in 2011 it was sold directly to retail consumers at Target and Wal-Mart.[2]
