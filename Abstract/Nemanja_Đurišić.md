NEMANJA ĐURIŠIĆ (; born February 23, 1992) is a Montenegrin professional basketball player for Filou Oostende. Đurišić is also currently a member of Montenegrin national basketball team and during his college years he played for the basketball team at University of Georgia in the United States. He can play both small or power forward position. He is one of the few players from Montenegro who completed all 4 years of basketball scholarship at the university in the United States.
