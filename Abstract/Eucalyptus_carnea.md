_EUCALYPTUS CARNEA_, known as the THICK-LEAVED MAHOGANY[1] or BROAD-LEAVED WHITE MAHOGANY,[2] is species of tree that is endemic to coastal areas of eastern Australia. It has rough, stringy bark from the trunk to the thinnest branches, lance-shaped or curved adult leaves, flower buds in groups of between seven and eleven, white flowers and cup-shaped to hemispherical fruit.[3][4]Eucalyptus_carnea_buds.jpgEucalyptus_carnea_flowers.jpgEucalyptus_carnea_fruit.jpg
