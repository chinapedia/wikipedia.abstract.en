_CALADENIA CAESAREA_ SUBSP. _MARITIMA_, commonly known as the CAPE SPIDER ORCHID, is a plant in the orchid family Orchidaceae and is endemic to the south-west of Western Australia. It has a single spreading, hairy leaf and up to three small mustard-coloured flowers with red stripes.
