_MANI RATHNAM_ is a 1994 Tamil drama film directed by K. Jayabalan. The film features Napoleon, Anand Babu, Mohana and newcomer Chandini in the lead roles, with Vadivelu, Babloo Prithiveeraj, Jafar Azad and Vittal Prasad playing supporting roles. The film, produced by A. Suresh, had musical score by Sirpy and was released on 2 November 1994. The film became a failure at the box office.[1][2][3][4][5]
