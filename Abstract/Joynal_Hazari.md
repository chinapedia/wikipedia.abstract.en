JOYNAL ABEDIN HAZARI[1] is a Bangladesh Awami League politician and former member of Parliament.[2] He was elected to parliament in 1996 from Awami League. He studied in Feni college where he joined politics. In 1982 he became general secretary of the district Awami League.[3] He was accused of torture, extortion and murder during the term he was in power.[4][5] His relative Nizam Hazari is the current member of parliament from his old constituency.[6]
