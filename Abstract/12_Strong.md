_12 STRONG_ (also known as _12 STRONG: THE DECLASSIFIED TRUE STORY OF THE HORSE SOLDIERS_) is a 2018 American action[1] war drama film directed by Nicolai Fuglsig and written by Ted Tally and Peter Craig. The film is based on Doug Stanton's non-fiction book _Horse Soldiers_, which tells the story of U.S. Army Special Forces sent to Afghanistan immediately after the September 11 attacks.
