MORRIS ROSSABI is an American historian, currently Senior Scholar and Adjunct Professor at Columbia University[1] and also Distinguished Professor of History at Queens College,[2] and also a published author.[3] Since he specializes in the history of Mongolia, China and East Asia, he was given an honorary doctorate from the National University of Mongolia. In 2011, he also delivered the W. Allyn Rickett-endowed lecture at University of Pennsylvania about Mongol influence on Ming China.[4]
