_THE CASE OF PROSECUTOR M_ (German: _DER FALL DES STAATSANWALTS M..._) is a 1928 German silent mystery film directed by Rudolf Meinert and Giulio Antamoro and starring Maria Jacobini, Jean Angelo and Gregori Chmara.[1] It was released in the United States in 1930 by Unusual Photoplays Inc. as _THE STRANGE CASE OF THE DISTRICT ATTORNEY_ with English intertitles by Don Bartlett and an added sound effects track.
