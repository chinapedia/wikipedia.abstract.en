Podolfrolovsky.ipg.jpg with Sts Peter and Paul Church]] The OLD CATHEDRAL OF ST. SOPHIA (, ) also called CATHOLIC CATHEDRAL OF ST. SOPHIA is the name given to a Catholic cathedral that was in the city of Kiev, the capital of the European country of Ukraine.[1][2] It was transformed into an Orthodox church and later demolished during the Soviet era. The current main Catholic cathedral is now dedicated to St. Nicholas.
