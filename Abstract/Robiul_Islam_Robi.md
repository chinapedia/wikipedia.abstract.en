ROBIUL ISLAM ROBI (born 15 October 1990) is a Bangladeshi first-class cricketer who plays for Khulna Division.[1] In May 2017, he scored his first century in a List A match, playing for Khelaghar Samaj Kallyan Samity in the 2016–17 Dhaka Premier Division Cricket League.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Khelaghar Samaj Kallyan Samity in the 2018–19 Dhaka Premier Division Twenty20 Cricket League on 25 February 2019.[3]
