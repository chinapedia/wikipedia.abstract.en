Viaduto_do_Corgo_(Corgo_Viaduct),_Vila_Real,_Portugal_(2013-06)_(16801183530).jpg VILA REAL BRIDGE is a road bridge in Portugal. It is located in the Vila Real District, crossing the Corgo River. It is part of the A4 highway. Vila Real Bridge has a concrete deck, with the main span supported by two pylons. It carries four lanes of road traffic on its deck.
