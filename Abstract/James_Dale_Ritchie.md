JAMES DALE RITCHIE (November 4, 1976 â€“ November 12, 2016), also known as THE MIDNIGHT SUN KILLER and THE ANCHORAGE SERIAL KILLER, was an American serial killer. Throughout 2016, Ritchie murdered upwards of five individuals in and around Anchorage, Alaska, most of whom were in parks or along bike paths. He always committed his murders at night, often around midnight or a short time after. Ritchie was killed during a shootout with police officers in downtown Anchorage on November 12, 2016. Following his death, a Colt Python handgun on his person connected him to the string of murders he committed over the course of two months.
