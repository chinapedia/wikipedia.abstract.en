Aerial_of_South_Valley,_Albuquerque,_New_Mexico_02.jpg STATE ROAD 500 (NM 500) is a state highway in the US state of New Mexico. NM 500's western terminus is at NM 45 in Albuquerque, and the northern terminus is at Interstate 25 (I-25) and U.S. Route 85 (US 85) in Albuquerque. From I-25, NM 500 continues east as Rio Bravo Boulevard to University Boulevard.
