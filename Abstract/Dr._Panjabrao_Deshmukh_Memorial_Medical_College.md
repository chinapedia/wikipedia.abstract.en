DR. PANJABRAO DESHMUKH MEMORIAL MEDICAL COLLEGE or BHAUSAHEB DESHMUKH MEMORIAL MEDICAL COLLEGE is a medical college located in Amravati, Maharashtra. The institute has 100 undergraduate seats for MBBS.[1] The institute is named after Panjabrao Deshmukh. The college is recognized by Medical Council of India and is affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik.[2]
