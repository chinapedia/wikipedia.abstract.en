JUMA MUWOWO (born 19 January 1980) is a Zambian male badminton player.[1][2] Muwowo also play for the Central Sport Club in Zambia.[3] In 2010, he competed at the Commonwealth Games in New Delhi, India.[4] In 2015, he reach the final at the Zambia International tournament in the mixed doubles event with Ogar Siamupangila after beat their compatriot Chongo Mulenga and Mary Chilambe in the straight game, but they were defeated by A. Kashkal and Hadia Hosny of Egypt in the final.[5] In 2016, the pair also reach the final in the same tournament and finished as runner-up.[6]
