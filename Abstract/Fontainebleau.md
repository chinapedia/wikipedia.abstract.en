FONTAINEBLEAU (; )[1] is a commune in the metropolitan area of Paris, France. It is located south-southeast of the centre of Paris. Fontainebleau is a sub-prefecture of the Seine-et-Marne department, and it is the seat of the _arrondissement_ of Fontainebleau.
