HIMMELBERGER AND HARRISON BUILDING, also known as the LIBERTY NATIONAL LIFE BUILDING and H & H BUILDING, is a historic commercial building located at Cape Girardeau, Missouri. It built in 1907-1908, and is a five-story red brick, H-shaped structure. It is a steel reinforced concrete building on a poured concrete foundation with a full basement. It features a recessed marble entry flanked by round Tuscan order columns.[1]
