NICOLAS KIM COPPOLA (born January 7, 1964),[1] known professionally as NICOLAS CAGE, is an American actor and filmmaker. During his early career, Cage starred in a variety of films such as _Valley Girl_ (1983), _Racing with the Moon_ (1984), _Birdy_ (1984), _Peggy Sue Got Married_ (1986), _Raising Arizona_ (1987), _Moonstruck_ (1987), _Vampire's Kiss_ (1989), _Wild at Heart_ (1990), _Fire Birds_ (1990), _Honeymoon in Vegas_ (1992), and _Red Rock West_ (1993).
