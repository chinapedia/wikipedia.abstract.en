Frontispiz,_Castell.jpg WOLFGANG DIETRICH OF CASTELL-REMLINGEN (German - _Wolfgang Dietrich zu Castell-Remlingen_) (6 January 1641, Remlingen - 8 April 1709, Castell) was a German nobleman. From 1668 until his death he was the ruler of the county of Castell-Remlingen, sharing power with his brother Friedrich Magnus of Castell-Remlingen. He also held other offices in the Margraviate of Ansbach and the Electoral Palatinate.
