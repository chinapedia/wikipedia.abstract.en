MEK PEK (born 4 January 1964) is a Danish singer and actor from Aarhus. Mek started his career in 1979 in the punk band _Lost Kids_ in which he played the bass. _Lost Kids_ eventually dissolved as a band and in 1982 he started his own band called _Mek Pek Party Band_. In 1995 he started The Allrights which he performed with under the name Mek Pek & The Allrights. Between 1990 and 2000 he worked with Kim Larsen and Peter A.G. Nielsen from the band Gnags.
