The LANDSKRONE in the borough of Bad Neuenahr-Ahrweiler in the German state of Rhineland-Palatinate is a hill, , in the Middle Rhine area. It used to be called the _Gimmiger Berg_ and _Gymmicher Kupp_, but is now named after the ruins of the imperial castle of which are found on its heights. On the western hillside of the Landskrone is the Chapel of Our Lady of Perpetual Succour (_Maria-Hilf-Kapelle_) or St. Mary's Chapel (_Marienkapelle_).
