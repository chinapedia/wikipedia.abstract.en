HAMZA GHANCHI (born 17 December 1994) is a Pakistani cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Karachi Whites in the 2013–14 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy on 5 December 2013.[2] On 8 October 2016 in the 2016–17 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy match against National Bank of Pakistan, he scored a triple century in the first innings, finishing 300 not out.[3] He became the third-youngest Pakistani cricketer to make a triple century in a first-class match.[4]
