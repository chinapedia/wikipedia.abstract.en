LORRAINE LOOTS is a South African artist and miniaturist from Cape Town. She has created miniature depictions of circular landscapes and everyday objects on a white background. Lorraine first rose to notoriety during 2013 when she began a project called 365 Paintings for ants and later in 2014 with 365 Postcards for ants where she painted one painting every day of the year and posted it on social media platforms for two consecutive years. This project gained her lots of media attention where she was featured on CBS, CNN, _Charlie Rose_, _Huffington Post_ and Colossal to name a few.[1][2][3][4]
