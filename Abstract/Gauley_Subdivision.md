The GAULEY SUBDIVISION is a railroad line owned by CSX Transportation in the U.S. states of West Virginia and Virginia. It was formerly part of the CSX HUNTINGTON EAST DIVISION.[1] It became part of the CSX Florence Division on June 20, 2016. The line runs from Gauley, West Virginia to Fola, West Virginia for a total of 22.0 miles. At its east end the line continues west from the New River Subdivision and at its west end the track comes to an end.[2]
