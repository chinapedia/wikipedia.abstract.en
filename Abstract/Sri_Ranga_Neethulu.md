_SRI RANGA NEETHULU_ is a 1983 Telugu comedy film, produced by Venkat Akkineni, Nagarjuna Akkineni under the Annapurna Studios banner[1] and directed by A. Kodandarami Reddy.[2] It stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Sridevi in the lead roles and music composed by Chakravarthy. The film is a remake of the Tamil film _Ooty Varai Uravu_ (1967). The film was recorded as a _Super Hit_ at the box office.[3]
