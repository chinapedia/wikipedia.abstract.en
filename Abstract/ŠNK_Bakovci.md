ŠPORTNO NOGOMETNI KLUB BAKOVCI (), commonly referred to as ŠNK BAKOVCI or simply BAKOVCI, is a Slovenian football club from the village of Bakovci. Their main colours are red and white. They currently play in the 1. MNL, the fifth tier of the Slovenian football pyramid. The club was established in 1953. Their home ground is ŠRC Bakovci (__) with a seating capacity for 500 spectators.
