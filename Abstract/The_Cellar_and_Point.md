THE CELLAR AND POINT is an American "garage-chamber'" septet based in New York City.[1] Formed by drummer/producer/composer Joseph Branciforte and guitarist/composer Christopher Botta in 2010, the group's music draws from jazz, contemporary classical music, post-rock, electronica, and ambient music. Their debut record, _Ambit_, was released in 2014 on Cuneiform Records.
