MURDER OF PENTECOSTALS IN SLAVIANSK is the crime committed in June 2014 in the city of Slaviansk, Donetsk Oblast, in Ukraine during which four members of Pentecostal church, Transfiguration of The Lord, () were taken and killed by militants of the Russian Orthodox Army.[1][2] Occurring during a period of religious persecution that began in April 2014, the crime caused a great public outcry.
