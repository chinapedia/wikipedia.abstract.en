The ECONOMY OF SAUDI ARABIA is one of the top twenty economies in the world (G20).[1] It is dependent on oil[2] as the country has the second-largest proven petroleum reserves,[3] and the country is the largest exporter of petroleum in the world.[4] It also has the fifth-largest proven natural gas reserves[5] and is considered an "Energy Superpower". With a total worth of US$34.4 trillion, Saudi Arabia has the second most valuable natural resources in the world.[6]
