"THE ONE YOU'VE BEEN WAITING FOR" is the fifth episode of the paranormal drama television series _Supernatural_{{'}}s season 12, and the 246th overall. The episode was written by Meredith Glynn and directed by Nina Lopez-Corrado. It was first broadcast on November 10, 2016, on The CW. In the episode, Sam and Dean must stop a group of Nazi necromancers, who have control of a pocket watch which holds the soul of Adolf Hitler.
