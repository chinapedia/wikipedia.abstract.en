LAUDAL CHURCH () is a parish church in Marnardal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Laudal. The church is part of the Laudal parish in the Mandal deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden, cruciform church was built in 1826 by the architect Leg Askildsen Hallingskaar. The church seats about 300 people.[1]
