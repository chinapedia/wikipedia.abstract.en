The first season of _Superstore_, the U.S. television series, was ordered on January 14, 2015.[1] The series was picked up by NBC on May 7, 2015.[2] It debuted on November 30, 2015, with a two-episode premiere.[3] The series was created by Justin Spitzer, who also serves as an executive producer.[4] The season ended on February 22, 2016. The first season originally had an episode order of 13 episodes, but it was later trimmed by NBC to 11 episodes due to scheduling, and _The Voice_ returning to schedule.[5]
