TERRY HEE YONG KAI (; born 6 July 1995) is a badminton player from Singapore.[1] He won the mixed team bronze medal at the 2014 Commonwealth Games in Glasgow, Scotland.[2] He also part of the national team that won the men's team bronze medals at the 2015 and 2017 Southeast Asian Games.[3] He made his second appearance at the Commonwealth Games in Gold Coast 2018.[4]
