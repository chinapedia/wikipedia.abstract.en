_PAATTU PADAVA_ (English: Shall I sing a song) is a 1995 Tamil romantic drama film directed by B. R. Vijayalakshmi making her directorial debut and remains her only film directed so far. The film features S. P. Balasubrahmanyam, Rahman and newcomer Lavanya in the lead roles, with Janagaraj, Kalyan Kumar, Mohan Natarajan, Chinni Jayanth, Srividya, C. R. Saraswathi and Sabitha Anand playing supporting roles. The film, produced by P. Mohanraj and P. Dhanaraj, had musical score by Ilaiyaraaja and was released on 10 February 1995. The film was an average grosser at the box office.[1][2][3][4][5][6][7]
