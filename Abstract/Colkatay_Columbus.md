_COLKATAY COLUMBUS_ is a 2016 Bengali film directed by Saurav Palodhi and produced by Sony AATH & Joy B Ganguly.The film's music has been composed by Neel Dutt. The film starring Mir, Anirban Bhattacharya, Gaurav Chakrabarty, Suchandra Vaaniya, Ritabhari Chakraborty and Tanushree Chakraborty in the lead roles. The background score is by Dipankar Chaki while the lyrics are penned by [1] Souvik Misra.[2] This is a satirical film set in the present day at Kolkata.[3][4]
