CAROLINA GAITÁN (born CAROLINA DEL PILAR GAITÁN LOZANO on April 4, 1984), is a Colombian singer, model, and television actress.[1] She has been working on television since 2002, becoming known for the television program, _Popstars: Colombia_.[2] She is best known for telenovelas such as _Isa TK+_ and _Flor Salvaje_ in secondary roles.[3][4] In 2015, Gaitán starred in the telenovela titled, _Hermanitas Calle_ as Nelly Calle,[5] and is currently part of the series _Sin senos sí hay paraíso_ as the main protagonist.[6]
