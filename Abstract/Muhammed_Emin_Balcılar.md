MUHAMMED EMIN BALCILAR (born 15 April 1996) is a Turkish professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for TFF Third League club Pazarspor on loan from Turkish Süper Lig club Çaykur Rizespor. Muhammed Emin Balcılar made his debut for Çaykur Rizespor in a 1-1 tie with Galatasaray on 5 May 2015.[1]
