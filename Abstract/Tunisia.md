TUNISIA, officially the REPUBLIC OF TUNISIA, is a country in the Maghreb region of North Africa, covering . Its northernmost point, Cape Angela, is the northernmost point on the African continent. It is bordered by Algeria to the west and southwest, Libya to the southeast, and the Mediterranean Sea to the north and east. Tunisia's population was 11.435 million in 2017.[1] Tunisia's name is derived from its capital city, Tunis, which is located on its northeast coast.
