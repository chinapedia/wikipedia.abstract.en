TOPSY PHIRI (born 11 August 1980) is a Zambian male badminton player.[1][2] In 2015, he placed second at the Chinese Ambassadors Badminton Championship in the men's doubles event with Donald Mabo.[3] He was the men's doubles runner-up at Top 16 Badminton Championship in Lusaka.[4] In the mixed doubles, he also the runner-up at the Ethiopia International tournament with Elizaberth Chipeleme.[5][6]
