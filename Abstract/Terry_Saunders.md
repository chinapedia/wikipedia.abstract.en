TERRY SAUNDERS was a film and Broadway performer. She is most widely known for playing Lady Thiang in the film and stage versions of _The King And I_, first as a replacement on Broadway,[1] then in the film,[2] performing the song "Something Wonderful" in both. Saunders had five other roles on Broadway [3] and guest starred on an episode of _Make Room For Daddy_.[4] She died in 2012.[5]
