HICKIWAN is a populated place situated in Pima County, Arizona.[1] It has been known by a plethora of names over the years, such as HIKIBON, HIKIRO, HIKJORN, HIKUWAN, JIQUIBO, KOKULI, MILPITAS, PERIGUA, PERIQUA, PIRIQUA, and TACHITOA. The name was officially recognized as Hickiwan by a decision of the Board on Geographic Names in 1941. It has an estimated elevation of above sea level.[2]
