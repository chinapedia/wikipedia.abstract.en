St_Mary's,_the_Taranaki_Cathedral_(11045355415).jpg THE TARANAKI CATHEDRAL, THE CHURCH OF ST MARY, in New Plymouth, is the newest cathedral in the Anglican Communion:[1] a parish church from 1842 to 2010, it was consecrated as a cathedral in the Diocese of Waikato and Taranaki on 6 March that year.[2] On 28 June 1984, the building was registered as a Category I heritage structure by the New Zealand Historic Places Trust (now Heritage New Zealand), with registration number 148.[3]
