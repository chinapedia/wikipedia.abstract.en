_MEESAYA MURUKKU_ () is a 2017 Indian Tamil language romantic comedy film written and directed by Adhi of the musical duo Hiphop Tamizha, in his directorial debut. He also stars as the lead along with Aathmika as the female lead, while Vivek, RJ Vigneshkanth, and Vijayalakshmi play supporting roles. The film, a partly fictionalised biopic of Adhi, was released on 21 July 2017. It became a blockbuster. The movie was remade in Kannada in 2019 as _Padde Huli_.[1]
