LYSERGIC ACID DIETHYLAMIDE (LSD), also known as ACID, is a hallucinogenic drug.[1] Effects typically include altered thoughts, feelings, and awareness of one's surroundings.[2] Many users see or hear things that do not exist.[3] Dilated pupils, increased blood pressure, and increased body temperature are typical.[4] Effects typically begin within half an hour and can last for up to 12 hours.[5] It is used mainly as a recreational drug and for spiritual reasons.[6][7]
