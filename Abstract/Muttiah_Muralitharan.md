Deshabandu MUTTIAH MURALITHARAN (, ; also spelt MURALIDARAN; born 1972) is a Sri Lankan cricket coach and former cricketer who was rated the greatest Test match bowler ever by _Wisden Cricketers' Almanack_ in 2002. He retired from Test cricket in 2010, registering his 800th and final wicket on 22 July 2010 from his final ball in his last Test match.[1] Muralitharan holds the world record for the most wickets in both test and one-day cricket. In 2017, he became the only Sri Lankan to be inducted into the ICC Hall of Fame.[2] He won the Ada Derana Sri Lankan of the Year in 2017.[3]
