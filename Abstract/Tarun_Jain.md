TARUN JAIN (born May 18, 1985) is an Indian writer, actor and entrepreneur. He is best known for writing Pyaar Ka Punchnama 2 and De De Pyaar De, as well as for creating, writing and acting in the Digital Sitcom _Life Sahi Hai._ He is also the founder of iForIndia.org, a social non-profit organisation created to push to greater self-governance and political accountability.[1][2][3][4]
