CLIVE STUART ANDERSON (born 10 December 1952 in Stanmore, Middlesex[1]) is an English television and radio presenter, comedy writer and former barrister. Winner of a British Comedy Award in 1991,[2] Anderson began experimenting with comedy and writing comedic scripts during his 15-year legal career, before starring in _Whose Line Is It Anyway?_ on BBC Radio 4, then later Channel 4. He has also hosted a number of radio programmes, and made guest appearances on _Have I Got News for You_, _Mock the Week_ and _QI_.[3]
