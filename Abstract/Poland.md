POLAND ( ), officially the REPUBLIC OF POLAND ( ), is a country located in Central Europe.[1] It is divided into 16 administrative subdivisions, covering an area of , and has a largely temperate seasonal climate.[2] With a population of approximately 38.5 million people, Poland is the sixth most populous member state of the European Union.[3] Poland's capital and largest metropolis is Warsaw. Other major cities include Kraków, Łódź, Wrocław, Poznań, Gdańsk, and Szczecin.
