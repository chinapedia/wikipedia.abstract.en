The 2016 SUMMIT LEAGUE WOMEN'S SOCCER TOURNAMENT is the postseason women's soccer tournament for the Summit League to be held from November 3 to 5, 2016. The three match tournament was be held at Dacotah Field in Fargo, North Dakota. The four team single-elimination tournament will consist of two rounds based on seeding from regular season conference play.
