LAWLER MOTOR COMPANY BUILDING, also known as the FARMER AUTOMOBILE COMPANY BUILDING, is a historic commercial building located at St. Joseph, Missouri. It was built in 1913, and is a two-story, trapezoidal shaped small-scale brick commercial building. It was designed as an automobile showroom and service center, with a second-story apartment. The building housed a Ford dealership from 1917 to 1928.[1]
