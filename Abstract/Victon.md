VICTON (; stylized as VICTON, an acronym for VOICE TO NEW WORLD) is a South Korean boy group formed in 2016. The group is composed of seven members: Han Seung-woo, Kang Seung-sik, Heo Chan, Lim Se-jun, Do Han-se, Choi Byung-chan and Jung Su-bin. The group debuted on November 9, 2016, with their extended play _Voice to New World_. On February 16, 2017, Victon announced Alice as their official fandom name.
