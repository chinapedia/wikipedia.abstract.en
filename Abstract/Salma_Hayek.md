SALMA HAYEK PINAULT (,[1] ; born SALMA VALGARMA HAYEK JIMÉNEZ; September 2, 1966)[2][3][4] is a Mexican and American film actress and producer. She began her career in Mexico starring in the telenovela _Teresa_ and starred in the film _El Callejón de los Milagros_ (_Miracle Alley_) for which she was nominated for an Ariel Award. In 1991 Hayek moved to Hollywood and came to prominence with roles in films such as _Desperado_ (1995),[5] _From Dusk till Dawn_ (1996), _Wild Wild West_ and _Dogma_ (both 1999).
