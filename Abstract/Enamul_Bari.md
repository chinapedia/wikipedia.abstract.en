MUHAMMAD ENAMUL BARI is a former Bangladesh Air Force officer whose last assignment before retiring was the 13th Chief of Air Staff of Bangladesh Air Force.[1][2] He retired on June 12, 2015 after over 30 years of service.[3][4] He is the present Chairman of Biman Bangladesh Airlines, the flag carrier of Bangladesh.[5]
