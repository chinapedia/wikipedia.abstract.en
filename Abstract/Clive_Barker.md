CLIVE BARKER (born 5 October 1952) is an English writer, film director, and visual artist. Barker came to prominence in the mid-1980s with a series of short stories, the _Books of Blood_, which established him as a leading horror writer. He has since written many novels and other works, and his fiction has been adapted into films, notably the _Hellraiser_ and _Candyman_ series. He was also the executive producer of the film _Gods and Monsters_.
