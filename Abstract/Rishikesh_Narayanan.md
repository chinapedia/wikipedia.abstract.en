RISHIKESH NARAYANAN (born 1974) is an Indian neuroscientist, computer engineer and a professor at the Molecular Biophysics Unit (MBU) of the Indian Institute of Science.[1] He is the principal investigator at the Cellular Neurophysiology Laboratory of MBU[2] where his team is engaged in researches on _experimental and theoretical aspects of information processing in single neurons and their networks_.[3] The Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, the apex agency of the Government of India for scientific research, awarded him the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize for Science and Technology, one of the highest Indian science awards, in 2016, for his contributions to biological sciences.[4]
