WILLIAM GARY BUSEY (; born June 29, 1944)[1] is an American actor. A prolific character actor, Busey has appeared in over 150 films, including _Lethal Weapon_ (1987), _Predator 2_ (1990), _Point Break_ (1991), _Under Siege_ (1992), _The Firm_ (1993), _Carried Away_ (1996), _Black Sheep_ (1996), _Lost Highway_ (1997), _Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas_ (1998), _The Gingerdead Man_ (2005), _Quigley_ (2003), and _Piranha 3DD_ (2012). Gary also made guest appearances on television shows such as _Gunsmoke_, _Walker, Texas Ranger_, _Law & Order_, _Scrubs_, and _Entourage_.
