MARK VAN DER SCHOOT (born 4 October 1990), known by his stage name MADUK, is a Dutch drum and bass music producer and DJ from Amsterdam. He has released music on Hospital Records, Liquicity Records, Viper Recordings as well as Monstercat[1] and Fokuz Recordings. He released his first album _Never Give Up_ at Hospital Records on 29 April 2016.[2] Together with Maris Goudzwaard, he founded Liquicity.[3]
