MANIPAYAL (English: _Good Boy_) is a 1973 Tamil Family - Children's film, directed by A. Jaganathan and Produced and Screenplay by R. M. Veerappan. The film dialogue was written by Jagadeesan and the story was written by Poovai Krishan respectively. Music was by M. S. Viswanathan. It stars Master Sekhar and Baby Indra, playing lead, with A. V. M. Rajan, Jayanthi, V. K. Ramasamy and S. V. Subbaiah playing pivotal roles.[1][2]
