_FOOD POLICY_ is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering food policy. It was established in 1975 and is published bimonthly by Elsevier. The editors-in-chief are Mario Mazzocchi (University of Bologna) and Marc F. Bellemare (University of Minnesota). According to the _Journal Citation Reports_, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 3.086.[1]
