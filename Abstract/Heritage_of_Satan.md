_HERITAGE OF SATAN_ is the ninth studio album by Czech black/heavy metal band Root, released on 25 October 2011 through Agonia Records.[1] It was their first album to be released by Agonia, as well as their last one with guitarist Marek "Ashok" Å merda, who left the band three years later to join Cradle of Filth. The album counts with many guest musicians, such as Erik Danielsson from Watain (who also provided its cover art), Nergal from Behemoth, and Blasphemer from Mayhem, Aura Noir and Ava Inferi.[2]
