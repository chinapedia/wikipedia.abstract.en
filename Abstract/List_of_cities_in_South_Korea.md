South_Korea_cities.svg The largest CITIES OF SOUTH KOREA have an autonomous status equivalent to that of provinces. Seoul, the largest city and capital, is classified as a _TEUKBYEOLSI_ (Special City), while the next 6 largest cities (see the list below) are classified as _GWANGYEOKSI_ (Metropolitan Cities; see Special cities of South Korea). Smaller cities are classified as _SI_ ("cities") and are under provincial jurisdiction, at the same level as counties (see Administrative divisions of South Korea).
