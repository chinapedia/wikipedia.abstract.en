_SID MEIER'S ALPHA CENTAURI_ is a 4X video game, considered a spiritual sequel to the _Civilization_ series. Set in a science fiction depiction of the 22nd century, the game begins as seven competing ideological factions land on the planet Chiron ("Planet") in the Alpha Centauri star system. As the game progresses, Planet's growing sentience becomes a formidable obstacle to the human colonists.
