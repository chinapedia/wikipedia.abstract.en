MARIYA MITSOVA (; born 21 November 1996) is a Bulgarian badminton player.[1] Mitsova represented her country at the 2014 Summer Youth Olympics in Nanjing, China.[2] In 2016, she became the runner-up at the Croatian International tournament in the mixed doubles event partnered with Alex Vlaar.[3] She won two titles at the Hellas International tournament in the women's singles and doubles event.[4] She was part of the national team that won the silver medal at the 2016 European Women's Team Championships in Kazan, Russia.[5]
