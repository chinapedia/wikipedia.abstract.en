LOIS LANE is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. Created by writer Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster, she first appeared in _Action Comics_ #1 (June 1938). Lois is an award-winning journalist for the Metropolis newspaper the _Daily Planet_ and the primary love interest of the superhero Superman and his alter-ego, Clark Kent. In DC continuity, she is also his wife and the mother of their son, Jon Kent, the current Superboy in the DC Universe.
