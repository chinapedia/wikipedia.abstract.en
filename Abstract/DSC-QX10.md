The SONY CYBER-SHOT DSC-QX10 is an ultrazoom, mobile device-mountable, Wi-Fi-controlled camera module announced by Sony on September 3, 2013. It can be controlled using an Android or iOS device.This super shooter facilitate an optical zoom of 10x. In this line some other models are Sony DSC-QX30, DSC-QX100, ILCE-QX1.
