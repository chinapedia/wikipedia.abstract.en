_EUCALYPTUS CERATOCORYS_, also known as the HORN-CAPPED MALLEE,[1] is a mallee that is native to South Australia and Western Australia. It has rough, ribbony bark at the base of its trunk, smooth greyish bark above, lance-shaped adult leaves, flower buds in groups of seven or nine with ridges along the sides, white to cream-coloured flowers and cylindrical fruit.Eucalyptus_ceratocorys_bark.jpg]]
