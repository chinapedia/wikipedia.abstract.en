THANASIS LASKARIDIS (Greek: Θανάσης Λασκαρίδης; born 1953) is a Greek shipowner and businessman[1][2][3] based in the United Kingdom.[4] He is the president and CEO of _Lavinia Corporation._[5][6][7] He is also founder of _The People's Trust_ in Greece[8] and co-founder of _Aikaterini Laskaridis Foundation._ He has six children.[9][10][11]
