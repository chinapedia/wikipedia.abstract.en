_CRITICAL PUBLIC HEALTH_ is a quarterly peer-reviewed public health journal. It was established in 1979 as _RADICAL COMMUNITY MEDICINE_, obtaining its current name in 1990.[1] It is published by Routledge and the editors-in-chief are Judith Green (King's College London) and Lindsay McLaren (University of Calgary). According to the _Journal Citation Reports_, the journal has a 2017 impact factor of 2.412.[2]
