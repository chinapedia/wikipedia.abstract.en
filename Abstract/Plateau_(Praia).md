Praia1x2.jpg]] _PLATEAU_ is the historic centre of the capital city of Praia in the island of Santiago, Cape Verde. It gets its name (from French “plateau”) because it is situated on a plateau overlooking the port of Praia. Its average elevation is 37 meters above sea level.[1] The population of _Plateau_ was 1,019 at the 2010 census.[2] Many public buildings are situated in _Plateau_, notably the Presidential Palace, the cathedral, the city hall and the Ethnographic Museum.
