They are located in all 25 geographic townships comprising this municipality. The largest, by both area and volume, is Lake Temagami. It contains more than 1,200 islands and of shoreline.[1] Other significant lakes include Cross Lake, Gull Lake, Net Lake, Obabika Lake and Rabbit Lake.
