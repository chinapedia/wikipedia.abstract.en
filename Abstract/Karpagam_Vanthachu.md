_KARPAGAM VANTHACHU_ (English: Karpagam has arrived) is a 1993 Tamil comedy film directed by R. Krishnamurthy, who is best known for directing _Billa_ and _Thee_. The film features Arjun Sarja and Raadhika in the lead roles, with S. S. Chandran, Vinu Chakravarthy, Venniradai Moorthy, Y. G. Mahendra, Charle, Gowtham Sundararajan, Vidyasri and Soorya playing supporting roles. The film, produced by Major Sundarrajan's wife S. Shyamala, had musical score by Sankar Ganesh and was released on 15 October 1993. The film was an adaptation of the stage play _Delhi Maamiyar_ written by Komal Swaminathan. The film was dubbed into Telugu as _Bejawada Rowdy_.[1][2][3][4]
