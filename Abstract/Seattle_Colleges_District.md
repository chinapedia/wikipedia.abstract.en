The SEATTLE COLLEGES DISTRICT (previously SEATTLE COMMUNITY COLLEGES DISTRICT) is a group of colleges located in Seattle, Washington. It consists of three colleges—North Seattle College, Seattle Central College (including the Wood Technology Center[1] and Seattle Maritime Academy[2]), South Seattle College (including the Georgetown Campus)—and the Seattle Vocational Institute.[3] Together the colleges form the second largest institution of higher education in the state,[4] behind the University of Washington, to which many of their graduates transfer.
