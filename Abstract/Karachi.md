KARACHI (; ALA-LC: , ; ) is the capital of the Pakistani province of Sindh. It is the most populous city in Pakistan,[1][2] and sixth-most-populous city proper in the world.[3] Ranked as a beta-global city,[4][5] the city is Pakistan's premier industrial and financial centre[6] and is considered as the cultural, economic, philanthropic, educational, and political hub of the country.[7] Karachi is also Pakistan's most cosmopolitan city.[8] Situated on the Arabian Sea, Karachi serves as a transport hub, and is home to Pakistan's two largest seaports, the Port of Karachi and Port Bin Qasim, as well as Pakistan's busiest airport, Jinnah International Airport.
