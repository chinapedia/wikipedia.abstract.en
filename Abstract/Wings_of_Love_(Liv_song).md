"WINGS OF LOVE" is the debut single by Swedish supergroup Liv. It was released on 30 September 2016. The music video for "Wings of Love" was shot on 3 July 2016 and directed by Lykke Li. On 1 July, Li asked people to participate in the video by being naked. On 4 November 2016, an alternate "tantra" version of the song was released, featuring Punjabi singer Jasbir Jassi.[1][2][3][4][5]
