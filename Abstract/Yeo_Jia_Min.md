YEO JIA MIN (born 1 February 1999) is a Singaporean badminton player.[1] Yeo was born and bred in Singapore to Malaysian parents who are Singapore permanent residents. The teenager received her Singapore citizenship in the last couple of years.[2] In 2015, she clinched two titles at the Badminton Asia U17 & U15 Junior Championships in Kudus, Indonesia.[3] She won the bronze medal at the 2017 Asian Junior Championships, and also part of the national team that won the women's team bronze at the 2015 and 2017 Southeast Asian Games.[4] Yeo was ranked World Junior No.1 in the girls' singles event in June 2017.[5] She competed at the 2018 Commonwealth Games in Gold Coast.[6]
