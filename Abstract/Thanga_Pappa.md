_THANGA PAPPA_ (English : Golden Baby) is a 1993 Tamil supernatural horror film directed by R. Aravindraj. The film features Shamili, Ramki, M. N. Nambiar, Thalaivasal Vijay, Aishwarya and Sreeja in the lead roles, with Chandrasekhar, Keerikkadan Jose, Thyagu, Raviraj, Murali Kumar and Prabhakaran playing supporting roles. The film, produced by the film editor G. Jayachandran, had musical score by Deva and was released on 30 June 1993.[1][2][3][4]
