The show premiered on February 12, 2017, joining _Kamen Rider Ex-Aid_ and later, _Kamen Rider Build_ in the _Super Hero Time_ line-up on TV Asahi affiliate stations until _Kyuranger_ concluded on February 4, 2018. _Kyuranger's_ main motifs are constellations and Greco-Roman mythology and it is the first Super Sentai series that introduced nine regular members right at the beginning, more than any of the previous installments which have typically started with five or fewer.
