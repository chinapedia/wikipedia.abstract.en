KORSHAMN CHAPEL () is a parish church in Lyngdal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Korshamn. The church is part of the Austad parish in the Lister deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden church was built in 1906 by the architect Arne Abrahamsen. The church seats about 100 people.[1][2]
