MENOMINEE–MARINETTE TWIN COUNTY AIRPORT, is a public use airport located 1 mile (2 km) northwest of the central business district of Menominee, Michigan, a city in Menominee County, Michigan, United States.[1] It is included in the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) National Plan of Integrated Airport Systems for 2017–2021, in which it is categorized as a local general aviation facility.[2]
