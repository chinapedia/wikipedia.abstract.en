VALERIE VELAZQUEZ (born July 6, 1985) is an American National Top 5 Finalist in the Miss Latina US Pageant, singer, philanthropist and creative entrepreneur. Velazquez represented Texas as a two time Miss Texas in 2008 & 2009 and finished 3rd Runner-Up in the National beauty competition representing her Mexican, Spanish, and Italian heritage.[1] Velazquez is on the Board Of Directors of 501 (c) (3) Non-Profit Beauty for Freedom in New York City where she campaigns to end human trafficking.[2] She's worked with Grammy Nominated Producer Mack Damon on a track for action film Machete directed by Robert Rodriguez and Ethan Maniquis, however the song was not chosen for final film release.[3] In July 2016 Velazquez was asked to ring the Nasdaq Opening Bell at the Opening Bell Ceremony [4] to raise awareness for her ongoing efforts in philanthropy and causes.[5] Velazquez now resides in Manhattan.
