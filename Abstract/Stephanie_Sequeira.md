STEPHANIE SEQUEIRA (born June 30, 1993) is an American professional bodybuilder, model, and personal trainer. She was born to Cuban parents in Colombia, and moved to Venezuela shortly after. She became a professional IFBB Bikini athlete in November 2015 at the NPC National Championships in Miami. She trained at LA Fitness, XSport, and the Quads Gym in Chicago, IL while preparing for this contest. She has become Blackstone Labs sponsored athlete and is a signed model with the Denise Carol Modeling Agency.
