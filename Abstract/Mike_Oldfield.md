MICHAEL GORDON OLDFIELD (born 15 May 1953)[1] is an English multi-instrumentalist and composer. His work blends progressive rock with world, folk, classical, electronic, ambient, and new-age music. His biggest commercial success is the 1973 album _Tubular Bells_which launched Virgin Records and became a hit in America after its opening was used as the theme for the film _The Exorcist_. He recorded the 1983 hit single "Moonlight Shadow"[2] and a rendition of the Christmas piece "In Dulci Jubilo".
