_STILL PROCESSING_ is a _New York Times_ culture podcast hosted by Jenna Wortham, who works for the New York Times Magazine, and Wesley Morris, the paper's critic at large.[1] The show debuted on September 8, 2016. _Still Processin_g won a 2017 Webby Award in the Podcast & Digital Audio category, and was nominated for a 2019 Shorty Award.
