CIARÁN SHEEHAN is an Irish-born actor who has appeared in numerous Off-Broadway and Broadway theatre productions, including _Les Misérables_, playing both Babet and Marius, and _Phantom of the Opera_, where he has played both Raul and the title character. He has worked extensively with the Irish Repertory Theatre, both as a producer and actor, appearing in Frank McCourt's _The Irish… and How They Got That Way_, _Finian's Rainbow_, and his own production of _The Molly Maguires_.
