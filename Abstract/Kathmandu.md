KATHMANDU (;[1] , _Yei_, Nepali pronunciation: ) is the capital city and largest city of Nepal with a population of around 3 million. Kathmandu metropolitan including Lalitpur and Bhaktapur has a population of around 5-6 million people. Kathmandu is also the largest metropolis in the Himalayan hill region. Nepali is the most spoken language in the city.
