JITKA SCHNEIDEROVÁ (born 23 March 1973) is a Czech film, television and stage actress. She studied at the Janáček Academy of Music and Performing Arts in Brno.[1] She acted at Studio Ypsilon in Prague between 1996 and 2003.[2] Schneiderová took part in the Czech television series _StarDance_ in 2015, finishing second with dancing partner Marek Dědík behind winners Marie Doležalová and Marek Zelinka.[3]
