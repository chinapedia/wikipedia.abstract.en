is a horizontal-scrolling shooter arcade game developed and released by Irem in 1987. The player controls a star ship, the R-9 "Arrowhead", in its efforts to destroy the Bydo, a powerful alien race bent on wiping out all of mankind. The R-9 can acquire a glowing orb-like device called a "Force", protecting it from enemy fire and providing additional firepower. In North America, it was distributed by Nintendo.
