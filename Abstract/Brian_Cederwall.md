BRIAN WILLIAM CEDERWALL (born 24 February 1952, Christchurch, New Zealand) represented Wellington in rugby union between 1972 and 1983 playing 103 first class matches.[1] Cederwall also played 52 first-class cricket matches for Wellington. He is the older brother of Grant Cederwall.[2] He played four All Black trials also making the New Zealand juniors in 1973 and 1974.[3][4][5][6][7][8]
