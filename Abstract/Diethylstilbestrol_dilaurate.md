DIETHYLSTILBESTROL DILAURATE (brand name ACNESTROL-LOTION) is a synthetic, nonsteroidal estrogen of the stilbestrol group and an ester of diethylstilbestrol (DES) that was previously marketed but is now no longer available.[1][2] It was formulated and used as a micronized topical medication to treat acne in adolescent boys and young men.[3][4] The drug was marketed as early as 1951.[5]
