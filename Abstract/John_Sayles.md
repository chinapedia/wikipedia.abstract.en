JOHN THOMAS SAYLES (born September 28, 1950) is an American independent film director, screenwriter, editor, actor and novelist. He has twice been nominated for the Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay, for _Passion Fish_ (1992) and _Lone Star_ (1996). His film _Men with Guns_ (1997) was nominated for the Golden Globe for Best Foreign Language Film. His directorial debut, _Return of the Secaucus 7_ (1980), has been added to the National Film Registry.
