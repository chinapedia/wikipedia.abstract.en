Fry|and|Fyr (disambiguation){{!}}Fyr}} SERBIA AND MONTENEGRO (Serbian: Србија и Црна Гора, СЦГ / _Srbija i Crna Gora_, SCG), also known as the STATE UNION OF SERBIA AND MONTENEGRO (), and known as the FEDERAL REPUBLIC OF YUGOSLAVIA from 1992 to 2003, was a country in Southeast Europe from 2003 to 2006, created from the two remaining federal republics of Yugoslavia after its breakup in 1992. The republics of Serbia and Montenegro together established a federation in 1992 as the FEDERAL REPUBLIC OF YUGOSLAVIA (FR YUGOSLAVIA or FRY; Савезна Република Југославија) or YUGOSLAVIA for short.
