CACHE ALTERNATIVE HIGH SCHOOL in Logan, Utah, United States is an alternative high school that serves approximately 145 students who are in need of services offered outside of a traditional high school setting. Cache High provides more individualized guidance, support, and resources for students' academic and social development. The school works in close partnership with Bridgerland Technical College to ensure students have access to training that will help them secure lucrative trade work upon graduation. The student teacher ratio is 19:1, significantly lower than the Utah average of 24:1.[1] Cache Alternative High is one of three high schools in the Cache County School District.
