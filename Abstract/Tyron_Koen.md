TYRON KOEN (born 25 October 1997) is a South African cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for KwaZulu-Natal in the 2016–17 Sunfoil 3-Day Cup on 17 November 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for KwaZulu-Natal in the 2016–17 CSA Provincial One-Day Challenge on 20 November 2016.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for KwaZulu-Natal in the 2017 Africa T20 Cup on 8 September 2017.[4]
