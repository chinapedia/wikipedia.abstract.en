CHAIM TOPOL (, born September 9, 1935), also spelled HAYM TOPOL, mononymously known as TOPOL,[1] is an Israeli actor, singer, comedian, voice artist, film producer, author, and illustrator. He is best known for his portrayal of Tevye the Dairyman, the lead role in the musical _Fiddler on the Roof_, on both stage and screen, having performed this role more than 3,500 times in shows and revivals from the late 1960s through to 2009.[2]
