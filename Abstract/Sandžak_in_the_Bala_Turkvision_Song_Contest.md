The participation of SANDŽAK IN THE BALA TURKVISION SONG CONTEST was originally planned to begin in Turkey, at the Bala Turkvision Song Contest in 2016. Radio Television of Novi Pazar (RTV Novi Pazar) would have been responsible for the selection process of their participant, for their debut in 2016. However, it was confirmed that both the and contests had been cancelled due to the December 2016 Istanbul bombings. Sandžak have yet to announce their plans to make their debut at the Bala Turkvision Song Contest 2017.
