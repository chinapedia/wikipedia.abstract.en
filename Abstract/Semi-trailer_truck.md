Freightliner_CL_120_Columbia_2010.jpg]] Mercedes-Benz_Actros_truck.jpg semi-trailer]] End_dump_2005-11-28.km.jpg FAW_truck.jpg semi-trailer truck in China]] A SEMI-TRAILER TRUCK (more commonly SEMI TRUCK or simply "SEMI") is the combination of a tractor unit and one or more semi-trailers to carry freight. A semi-trailer attaches to the tractor with a fifth-wheel coupling (hitch), with much of its weight borne by the tractor. The result is that both the tractor and semi-trailer will have a design distinctly different from that of a rigid truck and trailer.
