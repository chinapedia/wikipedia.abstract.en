PRISM VIDEO CONVERTER SOFTWARE (trademarked) is a video conversion software published by NCH Software available[1] for Windows and Macintosh Operating Systems. It offers users the ability to convert AVI, ASF, DVD, FLV, QuickTime, MP4, MPEG-PS and any other video formats[2] using Microsoft DirectShow to another video format. It allows users to set the compression/encoder rates, resolution and frame rates of output files. "Supports all of the popular multimedia devices including PSP, iPod, MP4 players and mobile phones",[3] and has been referenced by _The New York Times_ as a “commercial software with faster conversion speeds and more features” when used to convert video files.[4]
