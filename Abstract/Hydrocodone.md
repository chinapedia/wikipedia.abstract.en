HYDROCODONE, sold under the brand name HYSINGLA among others, is an opioid used to treat severe pain of a prolonged duration, if other measures are not sufficient.[1][2] It is also used as a cough suppressant in adults.[3] It is taken by mouth.[4] Typically it is sold as the combinations acetaminophen/hydrocodone or ibuprofen/hydrocodone.[5][6] By itself it is sold in a long-acting formulation.[7]
