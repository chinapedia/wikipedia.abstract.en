STEVEN "STEVE" FRENCH is an American actor, television announcer and film and television voice-over actor. French is the recipient of a Drama Desk Award for the musical In Transit;[1] and known for his voice-over work in such television shows as Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt and announcer for Match Game with host Alec Baldwin, along with films Into the Woods, among others. He has appeared On- and Off- Broadway.
