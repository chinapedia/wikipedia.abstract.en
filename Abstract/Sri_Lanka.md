SRI LANKA (, ;[1][2] __; _Ilaá¹…kai_), officially the DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA, is an island country in South Asia, located in the Indian Ocean to the southwest of the Bay of Bengal and to the southeast of the Arabian Sea. The island is geographically separated from the Indian subcontinent by the Gulf of Mannar and the Palk Strait. The legislative capital, Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte, is a suburb of the commercial capital and largest city, Colombo.
