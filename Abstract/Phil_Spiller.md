PHILIP ALAN SPILLER is a former National Football League player. He was drafted in the 16th round (No. 410 overall) of the 1967 NFL Draft by the St. Louis Football Cardinals as a Defensive Back (DB). He played the 1967 and part of the 1968 seasons for St. Louis, but was traded in 1968 to the 1st year fledgling expansion team the AFC Cincinnati Bengals and to the 2nd year NFC Atlanta Falcons.[1]
