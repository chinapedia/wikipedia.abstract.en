PETER YOUNGHUSBAND (born 17 February 1990) is a New Zealand cricketer.[1] He made his List A debut for Wellington on 21 January 2015 in the 2014–15 Ford Trophy.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Wellington on 30 December 2016 in the 2016–17 Super Smash.[3] He made his first-class debut for Wellington on 25 February 2017 in the 2016–17 Plunket Shield season.[4] In June 2018, he was awarded a contract with Wellington for the 2018–19 season.[5]
