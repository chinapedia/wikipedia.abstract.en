RICHARD ROLAND HAWLEY (born 13 August 1955 in Bradford, West Yorkshire) is a British actor, best known for his work in _Grushko_, _Prime Suspect_, _Family Affairs_, '' Love Actually'' and _Coronation Street_.[1] He has done voice work in the video game _The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt_ portraying the character Sigi Reuven, known better as Sigismund Dijskra.[2]
