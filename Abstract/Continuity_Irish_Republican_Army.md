The CONTINUITY IRISH REPUBLICAN ARMY, usually known as the CONTINUITY IRA (CIRA; )[1][2][3] is an Irish republican paramilitary group that claims to be the armed forces of the Irish Republic that was proclaimed in 1916. It aims to bring about a united Ireland. It emerged from a split in the Provisional IRA in 1986 but did not become active until the Provisional IRA ceasefire of 1994.
