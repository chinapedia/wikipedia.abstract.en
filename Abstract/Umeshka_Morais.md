UMESHKA MORAIS (born 18 August 1995) is a Sri Lankan cricketer.[1] He made his first-class debut for Moors Sports Club in the 2016–17 Premier League Tournament on 29 November 2016.[2] He made his List A debut for Sri Lanka Police Sports Club in the 2017–18 Premier Limited Overs Tournament on 18 March 2018.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut for Panadura Sports Club in the 2018–19 SLC Twenty20 Tournament on 18 February 2019.[4]
