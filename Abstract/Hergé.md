GEORGES PROSPER REMI (; 22 May 1907 – 3 March 1983), known by the pen name HERGÉ (;[1] ), was a Belgian cartoonist. He is best known for creating _The Adventures of Tintin_, the series of comic albums which are considered one of the most popular European comics of the 20th century. He was also responsible for two other well-known series, _Quick & Flupke_ (1930–1940) and _The Adventures of Jo, Zette and Jocko_ (1936–1957). His works were executed in his distinct _ligne claire_ drawing style.
