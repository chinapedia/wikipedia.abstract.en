The game contains key staff from Bandai Namco's _Tales_ series of video games, including character designer Kosuke Fujishima, and scenario writer Takumi Miyajima. The game was released for PlayStation 4 in Japan in July 2016 and in North American and Europe in June 2017.[1] A Windows version was released worldwide in April 2018.
