MORGAN FREEMAN[1] (born June 1, 1937)[2] is an American actor, film director, and film narrator. Freeman won an Academy Award in 2005 for Best Supporting Actor with _Million Dollar Baby_ (2004), and he has received Oscar nominations for his performances in _Street Smart_ (1987), _Driving Miss Daisy_ (1989), _The Shawshank Redemption_ (1994), and _Invictus_ (2009). He has also won a Golden Globe Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award.
