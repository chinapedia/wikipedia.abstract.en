The LATVIA MEN'S NATIONAL UNDER-18 AND UNDER-19 BASKETBALL TEAM is the representative for Latvia in international basketball competitions, and it is formed and run by the Latvia Basketball Association. The LATVIA MEN'S NATIONAL UNDER-18 BASKETBALL TEAM represents Latvia at the FIBA Europe Under-18 Championship[1] and the LATVIA MEN'S NATIONAL UNDER-19 BASKETBALL TEAM at the FIBA Under-19 Basketball World Cup.
