PALLAB LOCHAN DAS (born December 31, 1978 in Biswanath Chariali, Assam) is a Bharatiya Janata Party politician from Assam. He was elected in Assam Legislative Assembly election in 2011 and 2016 from Rangapara constituency. He is the current Member of Parliament from Tezpur (Lok Sabha constituency).[1][2] He became a minister in the Sarbananda Sonowal-led government in 2016. Formerly, he was with Indian National Congress.[3][4]
