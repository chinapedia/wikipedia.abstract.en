VANESSA HUA is a journalist and writer based in San Francisco. She is the author of _Deceit and Other Possibilities_ (Willow Books, 2016) and _A River of Stars_ and _The Sea Places_, which are forthcoming from Ballantine Books. She is a columnist for the _San Francisco Chronicle_ [1] and a member of the San Francisco Writers' Grotto.[2] Her fiction has appeared in _The Atlantic_, _ZYZZYVA_, _Guernica_, and other publications.
