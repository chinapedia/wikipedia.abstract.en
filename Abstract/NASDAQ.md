The NASDAQ STOCK MARKET (, also known as NASDAQ) is an American stock exchange. It is the second-largest stock exchange in the world by market capitalization, behind only the New York Stock Exchange located in the same city.[1] The exchange platform is owned by Nasdaq, Inc.,[2] which also owns the Nasdaq Nordic (formerly known as OMX) and Nasdaq Baltic stock market network and several U.S. stock and options exchanges.
