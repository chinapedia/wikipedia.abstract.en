SOPHIE SHIRLEY (born June 30, 1999) is a Canadian-born women’s ice hockey player from Saskatoon. She was named to the Canada women's national ice hockey team roster [1] that shall participate in a pair of games against the United States on December 13 (in Plymouth, Michigan) and December 20, 2016 (in Sarnia, Ontario). Said series against the US shall mark Shirley’s debut with the national team.[2]
