The US + THEM TOUR was a concert tour by Roger Waters, formerly of Pink Floyd. The tour visited the United States, Canada, New Zealand, Australia and countries in Europe and Latin America, showcasing songs from Waters' career with Pink Floyd and from his most recent album as a solo artist, _Is This the Life We Really Want?_ It opened on 26 May 2017 in Kansas City, Missouri[1][2] and ended on 9 December 2018 in Monterrey, Mexico.
