WILLIAM VILLAFAÑE is a former advisor and Puerto Rico Chief of Staff for the governorship of Ricardo Rosselló.[1] Villafañe holds a bachelor's degree in accounting from the University of Puerto Rico and a juris doctor from the Interamerican University of Puerto Rico School of Law.[2] It is unclear, however, if Villafañe is a certified public accountant or an attorney-at-law. Regardless, Villafañe worked in the past as a legislative advisor in the Legislative Assembly of Puerto Rico, as an advisor in the Puerto Rico Commission on Public Service, and as secretary general of the New Progressive Party (PNP in Spanish).
