HEZEKIAH (; ) was, according to the Hebrew Bible, the son of Ahaz and the 13th king of Judah.[1] Edwin Thiele concluded that his reign was between c. 715 and 686 BCE.[2] He is considered a very righteous king by the author of the Books of Kings.[3] He is also one of the most prominent kings of Judah mentioned in the Bible and is one of the kings mentioned in the genealogy of Jesus in the Gospel of Matthew.[4]
