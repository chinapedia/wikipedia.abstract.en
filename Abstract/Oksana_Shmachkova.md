OKSANA SHMACHKOVA (; born 20 June 1981 in Rayevskaya) is a Russian women's international footballer who plays as a defender. She is a member of the Russia women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2003 FIFA Women's World Cup[1] as well as UEFA Women's Euro 2001 and 2009. On club level she plays for WFC Rossiyanka in Russia.[2]
