NADIA ROSE (born 11 June 1993), is an English rapper from Croydon, London.[1] Rose released her debut EP _Highly Flammable_ on 13 January 2017[2] following the successful singles "Station", "BOOM", and "D.F.W.T". She studied Music and Music Management at university.[3] Rose is represented by Sony Music Group under the subsidiary Relentless Records. Nadia Rose is also the cousin of Michael "Stormzy" Omari. She came fifth in the BBC's 'Sound of 2017' shortlist.[4]
