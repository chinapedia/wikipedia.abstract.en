JAMKARAN RAILWAY STATION (_Persian:_ايستگاه راه آهن جمکران, _Istgah-e Rah Ahan-e Jamkaran_) is located in Jamkaran, in the outskirts of Qom, Qom Province. The station is owned by IRI Railway. There are weekly commuter trains from Tehran on Tuesday evenings and back to Tehran early Wednesday morning as Tuesday evenings are the Jamkaran Mosque's main activity and pilgrimage day.
