_WEDNESDAY MORNING, 3 A.M._ is the debut studio album by American folk rock duo Simon & Garfunkel. Following their early gig as "Tom and Jerry", Columbia Records signed the two in late 1963. It was produced by Tom Wilson and engineered by Roy Halee. The cover and the label include the subtitle _exciting new sounds in the folk tradition_. Recorded in March 1964, the album was released on October 19.
