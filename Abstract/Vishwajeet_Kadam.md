VISHWAJEET KADAM (born 13 January 1980) is a politician from the Indian state of Maharashtra. Son of late congress leader Patangrao Kadam, Vishwajeet was born and brought up in Pune. He is currently an MLA from Palus-Kadegaon Vidhan Sabha constituency after the death of his father late Shri Patangrao Kadam. He was elected unopposed.[1][2][3]
