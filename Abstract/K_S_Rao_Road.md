City_Center_Premises_Mangalore.jpg decorated during Mangalore Dasara]] KARNAD SADASHIVA RAO ROAD or popularly known as K S RAO ROAD is one of the busiest roads in Mangalore, Karnataka, India. It runs from PVS Circle at one end to join Balmatta Road at the other. This road is named after the indian freedom fighter Karnad Sadashiva Rao.[1]
