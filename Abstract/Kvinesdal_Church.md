KVINESDAL CHURCH () is a parish church in Kvinesdal municipality in Vest-Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Liknes. The church is part of the Kvinesdal parish in the Lister deanery in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, octagonal, wooden church was built in 1837 using plans by the architect Hans Linstow. The church seats about 500 people.[1][2]
