_SPOTLIGHT ON A MURDERER_ (_Pleins feux sur l'assassin_ in its original French title, which translates to _Spotlights on the Murderer_) is a 1961 French psychodrama/murder-mystery film directed by Georges Franju. It was released on Blu-ray and DVD by Arrow Films in August 2017 in the UK and USA regions in an open-matte 1.37:1 transfer by Gaumont of the film's original 1.66:1 aspect ratio.
