WŁADYSŁAW PUŚLECKI (born 11 May 1933 in Bartoszewice, Poland, died 8 December 2008 in Opole, Poland) [1][2] – was a Polish pedagogist, professor of Humanities, specialist in the field of general and alternative didactic methods, propagator of student subjectivity (Polish: „pełnomocność ucznia”), researcher of school of the future, educator of few teachers generations.
