Friedrich-Johann-Justin-Bertuch_Mythical-Creature-Dragon_1806.jpg from 1806]] Ninedragonwallpic1.jpg, Beihai Park, Beijing ]] A DRAGON is a large, lizard-like legendary creature that appears in the folklore of many cultures around the world. Beliefs about dragons vary drastically by region, but dragons in western cultures since the High Middle Ages have often been depicted as winged, horned and capable of breathing fire. Dragons in eastern cultures are usually depicted as wingless, serpentine lizards with above-average intelligence.
