SUJON HAWLADER (born 23 June 1995) is a Bangladeshi first-class cricketer who plays for Rajshahi Division.[1] He made his List A debut for Abahani Limited in the 2016–17 Dhaka Premier Division Cricket League on 29 April 2017.[2] He made his Twenty20 debut for Shinepukur Cricket Club in the 2018–19 Dhaka Premier Division Twenty20 Cricket League on 25 February 2019.[3]
