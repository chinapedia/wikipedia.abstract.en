Lieutenant Commander GEORDI LA FORGE is a fictional character who appeared in all seven seasons of the American science fiction television series _Star Trek: The Next Generation_ and its four feature films. Portrayed by LeVar Burton, he served as helmsman of the USS _Enterprise_-D in the first season, then occupied the role of the chief engineer for the rest of the series and in the films.
