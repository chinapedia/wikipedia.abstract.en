_CALADENIA CHRISTINEAE_, commonly known as CHRISTINE'S SPIDER ORCHID, is a plant in the orchid family Orchidaceae and is endemic to the south-west of Western Australia. It has a single erect, hairy leaf and up to four small, white to cream-coloured flowers. It is distinguished from similar spider orchids by its small, odourless flowers with their stiffly held sepals and petals.Caladenia_christineae_labellum.jpg
