_EDURMANELI GANDA PAKKADMANELI HENDTHI_ (English: OPPOSITE HOUSE HUSBAND-SIDE HOUSE WIFE) is a 1992 Indian Kannada comedy drama film directed by Relangi Narasimha Rao and produced by P. Balaram. A remake of Rao's Telugu film, _Edurinti Mogudu Pakkinti Pellam_ (1991), the film featured Shashikumar, Shruti and Master Adithya in the lead roles.[1] The film's music was composed by Raj-Koti and cinematography is by Sharath.
