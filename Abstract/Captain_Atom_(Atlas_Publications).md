_CAPTAIN ATOM_ was an Australian comic book series created and written by Jack Bellew with illustrations by Arthur Mather.[1] It was published from 1948 to 1954, with 64 issues[2] and it also appeared as strips in a number of Australian newspapers. The protagonist, the first to use the name, has no relation to the later American superhero Captain Atom,[3] published by Charlton Comics in 1965[4] and subsequently by DC Comics in 1987.[5]
