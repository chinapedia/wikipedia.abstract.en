_ENTEDE BHANTA_ is a 1992 Indian Kannada action drama film directed by D. Rajendra Babu and produced by H. N. Maruthi. Based on a novel by Sudarshan Desai, the film featured Ambarish, Rajani and Vanitha Vasu in the lead roles.[1] The film's music was composed by Hamsalekha and cinematography was by H. G. Raju.
