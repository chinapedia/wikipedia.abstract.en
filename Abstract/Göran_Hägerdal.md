KARL GÖRAN HÄGERDAL (born March 11, 1966 in Kristdala parish, Kalmar County, Sweden[1]) is the Global Director of Scouting Development at the World Scout Bureau headquarters of the World Organization of the Scout Movement in Kuala Lumpur, and served as one of three camp chiefs of the 22nd World Scout Jamboree at Rinkaby, Kristianstad, Skåne in southern Sweden, 27 July to 7 August 2011. Between 2004 and 2012 he was Deputy Secretary General of the Svenska Scoutrådet, the former umbrella organization for Swedish Scouting.
