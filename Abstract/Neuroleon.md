Neuroleon_sansibaricus_type.JPG'' type specimen from the Bavarian State Collection of Zoology]] _NEUROLEON_ is a large and complex genus of antlions in the subfamily Myrmeleontinae. There are more than 150 described species from Europe, Asia and Africa, and there are many undescribed taxa. Where the biology of the larvae is known, they live freely in sand or in sheltered rock crevices.[1][2]
