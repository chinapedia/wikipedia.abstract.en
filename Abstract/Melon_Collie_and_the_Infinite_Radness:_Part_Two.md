_MELON COLLIE AND THE INFINITE RADNESS: PART TWO_ is a five-track EP released by Canadian indie group Tokyo Police Club on September 23, 2016, following the release of part one five months earlier on April 8. The band released the single "My House" on September 2, 2016, to promote the EP.[1] Like its predecessor, the title is a reference to the 1995 Smashing Pumpkins album _Mellon Collie and the Infinite Sadness_.
