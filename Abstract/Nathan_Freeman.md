NATHAN FREEMAN (born 16 June 1995) is an Australian rules footballer who played for the St Kilda Football Club in the Australian Football League. He was drafted by Collingwood at pick 10 in the 2013 AFL national draft. He was than traded to the Saints in 2015 without having played an AFL game due to repeated injuries.[1] Freeman was delisted at the end of the 2018 season having made his long-awaited senior debut only weeks earlier against the Western Bulldogs in Round 20.[2]
